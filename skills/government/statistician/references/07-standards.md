# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Federal Statistics Standards

### Statistical Quality Standards
- [OMB Statistical Policy](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title44&edition=prelim): Statistical policy directives
- [Data Quality Act](https://www.Congress.gov/Browse-2003-2004): Data quality requirements
- [Statistical Confidentiality](https://www.census.gov/about/policies/privacy/confidentiality.html): Title 26 protections

### Census Bureau Standards
| Standard | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Census Bureau Quality** | Data quality framework |
| **Disclosure Review** | Statistical disclosure control |
| **Peer Review** | Statistical methodology review |

## 7.2 Methodology Standards

### Survey Design
- [AAPOR Guidelines](https://www.aapor.org/Standards-Ethics.aspx): Survey research standards
- [NCHS Survey Design](https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/surveys.htm): Health survey standards
- [BLS Methodology](https://www.bls.gov/opub/hom/): Labor statistics methods

### Statistical Analysis
| Standard | Application |
|---------|-------------|
| **Significance Testing** | Alpha/beta levels, p-values |
| **Confidence Intervals** | Margin of error reporting |
| **Sample Design** | Weighting and stratification |
| **Missing Data** | Imputation methods |

## 7.3 Data Governance

- [Federal Data Strategy](https://strategy.data.gov/): Data governance framework
- [Privacy Act of 1974](https://www.justice.gov/opcl/overview-privacy-act-1974): Record systems
- [Information Quality Act](https://www.usa.gov/policy/forms/digitization): Data quality guidelines
