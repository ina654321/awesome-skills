# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Water Resource Standards

### Federal Regulations
- [Clean Water Act](https://www.epa.gov/laws-regulations/summary-clean-water-act): 33 USC 1251
- [Safe Drinking Water Act](https://www.epa.gov/sdwa): 42 USC 300f
- [40 CFR Part 122](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-40/part122): NPDES permit program
- [USGS Water Data](https://waterdata.usgs.gov/nwis): Real-time streamflow

### EPA Standards
| Standard | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **NPDES** | Discharge permits |
| **WQS** | Water quality standards |
| **MCL** | Drinking water maximums |
| **TMDL** | Total maximum daily loads |

## 7.2 Technical Standards

### USGS Protocols
- [Streamgaging](https://pubs.usgs.gov/tm/04/a09/): Discharge measurement
- [Water Quality](https://www.usgs.gov/mission-areas/water/resources): Sampling protocols
- [Groundwater](https://pubs.usgs.gov/circ/circ1278): Aquifer testing
