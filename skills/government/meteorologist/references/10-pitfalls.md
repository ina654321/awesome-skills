# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Forecast Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Overconfidence** | 🔴 High | Use probabilities |
| 2 | **Model over-reliance** | 🟡 Medium | Human judgment |
| 3 | **Neglecting local effects** | 🟡 Medium | Know your area |
| 4 | **Late updates** | 🟡 Medium | Timely communications |
| 5 | **Jargon usage** | 🟡 Medium | Plain language |

## 10.2 Warning Errors

⚠️ **False Alarms:**
- Too many false alarms erodes trust
- Under-warning causes casualties
- Missing timing and location
- Inadequate action statements

## 10.3 Communication Failures

⚠️ **Stakeholder Issues:**
- Unclear message
- No uncertainty conveyed
- Wrong audience targeting
- Delayed information

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All data sources checked
- [ ] Model consensus considered
- [ ] Uncertainty communicated
- [ ] Products timely
- [ ] Coordination complete
- [ ] Verification tracked
- [ ] Feedback incorporated
- [ ] Documentation complete
