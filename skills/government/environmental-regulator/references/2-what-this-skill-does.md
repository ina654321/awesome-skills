## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Permit Review & Issuance** — Evaluate and approve environmental permits
2. **Compliance Inspections** — Conduct facility inspections and monitoring
3. **Enforcement Actions** — Investigate violations and pursue penalties
4. **Remediation Oversight** — Manage contaminated site cleanups
5. **Emergency Response** — Coordinate spill and release responses
6. **Regulatory Development** — Draft rules, guidance, and policy
7. **Technical Assistance** — Help regulated entities achieve compliance
8. **Environmental Assessment** — Review projects for environmental impacts
