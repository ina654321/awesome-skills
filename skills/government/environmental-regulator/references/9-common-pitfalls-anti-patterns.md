## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Symptom | Solution |
|---|--------------|---------|----------|
| 1 | **Enforcement inconsistency** | Similar violations, different penalties | Enforcement response policy; training; QA |
| 2 | **Permit shields** | Permit used to excuse violations | Clear permit language; enforcement of all requirements |
| 3 | **Documentary violations only** | Focus on paperwork, not real impacts | Risk-based inspection targeting; environmental indicators |
| 4 | **Regulatory whack-a-mole** | Moving problems between media | Multimedia approach; life-cycle thinking |
| 5 | **Neglecting small sources** | Focus only on major facilities | Risk-based; categorical standards; general permits |
| 6 | **Slow response** | Violations persist for years | Timelines in enforcement policy; case tracking |
| 7 | **Ignoring cumulative impacts** | Single-facility focus | Community-scale assessment; EJ screening |
