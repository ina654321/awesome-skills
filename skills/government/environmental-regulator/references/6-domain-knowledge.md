## § 6 · Domain Knowledge

### 6.1 Major Environmental Statutes

| Statute | Media | Key Requirements | Penalties |
|---------|-------|------------------|-----------|
| **Clean Air Act** | Air | NAAQS, permits, MACT standards | $50K/day civil; criminal for knowing |
| **Clean Water Act** | Water | NPDES permits, discharge limits | $66K/day civil; criminal for negligent |
| **RCRA** | Hazardous waste | Cradle-to-grave tracking, TSD permits | $50K/day; criminal for knowing |
| **CERCLA** | Contaminated sites | Cleanup liability, reporting | Strict liability; joint & several |
| **TSCA** | Chemicals | Testing, reporting, restrictions | $50K/day civil |
| **EPCRA** | Emergencies | Reporting, planning, notifications | $50K/day civil |

### 6.2 Penalty Calculation Framework

```
Total Penalty = Economic Benefit + Gravity + Adjustments

Economic Benefit:
- Delayed compliance costs
- Avoided pollution control
- Competitive advantage

Gravity Factors:
- Seriousness of violation
- Size of violator
- History of non-compliance
- Good faith efforts

Adjustments:
+ Litigation costs avoided (settlement)
+ Ability to pay
+ Other unique factors
```

### 6.3 Inspection Types and Frequency

| Type | Focus | Frequency | Resource Intensity |
|------|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Full Compliance** | All requirements | Risk-based (1-5 years) | High |
| **Targeted** | Specific issue | As needed | Medium |
| **Verification** | Follow-up on violations | Post-violation | Low |
| **Multi-media** | Air, water, waste together | Major facilities | High |
| **Emergency** | Incident response | Immediate | Variable |
