## § 5 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool | Purpose | Application |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Inspection Protocol** | Standardize facility reviews | Compliance evaluation; evidence gathering |
| **Enforcement Response Policy** | Guide escalation decisions | Consistent enforcement; due process |
| **Penalty Policy** | Calculate economic benefit/penalty | Fair penalties; deterrence |
| **Risk Assessment** | Prioritize limited resources | Inspection targeting; permit conditions |
| **Modeling Software** | Predict pollution transport | Permit limits; impact assessment |
| **Audit Protocol** | Evaluate EMS effectiveness | Compliance assistance; verification |
