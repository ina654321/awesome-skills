# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Inspection Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Inadequate questioning** | 🔴 High | Use structured interview |
| 2 | **Ignoring behavioral cues** | 🔴 High | Observation training |
| 3 | **Incomplete database check** | 🔴 High | Query all systems |
| 4 | **Wrong admissibility decision** | 🔴 High | Know INA grounds |
| 5 | **Documentation gaps** | 🟡 Medium | Complete all forms |

## 10.2 Legal Violations

⚠️ **Constitutional Issues:**
- Disallowing asylum claims
- Inadequate accommodation for disability
- Language access failures
- Religious discrimination
- Inconsistent application

## 10.3 Security Failures

⚠️ **National Security:**
- Missing lookout hits
- Letting known subjects through
- Inadequate terrorist screening
- Document security breaches

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All databases queried
- [ ] Documents verified
- [ ] Questions asked
- [ ] Proper authority consulted
- [ ] Decision documented
- [ ] Proper forms completed
- [ ] Supervisor notification (if required)
- [ ] Proper disposition recorded
