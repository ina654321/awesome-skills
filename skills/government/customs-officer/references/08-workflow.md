# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Primary Inspection Workflow

```
Phase 1: Document Review
├── Verify traveler identity
├── Review customs declaration (6059B)
├── Check visa/entry status
├── Verify supporting documents
└── Query lookout databases

Phase 2: Risk Assessment
├── NCATS score evaluation
├── Target selection criteria
├── Secondary referral factors
├── Currency reporting requirements
└── Agricultural alerts

Phase 3: Disposition
├── Primary clearance
├── Referral to secondary
├── Penalty assessment
├── Seizure processing
└── Referral to another agency
```

## 8.2 Cargo Examination Process

1. **Documentary Review** (30 min): Bill of lading, commercial invoice, packing list
2. **Manifest Reconciliation** (15 min): Confirm cargo matches declaration
3. **Physical Inspection** (variable): Full or targeted examination
4. **Sample Collection** (as needed): Lab testing for compliance
5. **Classification/Duty Assessment** (45 min): HTS determination, duty calculation

## 8.3 Seizure Procedures

| Violation | Authority | Process |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| **Narcotics** | 21 USC 801 | Release to DEA |
| **Counterfeit** | 19 CFR 133.23 | Trademark seizure |
| **Currency** | 31 USC 5316 | Currency seizure |
| **Endangered Species** | ESA | CITES referral |
