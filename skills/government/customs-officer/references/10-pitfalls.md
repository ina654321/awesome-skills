# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Inspection Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Inconsistent targeting** | 🔴 High | Follow NCATS criteria |
| 2 | **Inadequate documentation** | 🔴 High | Complete all required forms |
| 3 | **Chain of custody errors** | 🔴 High | Follow evidence protocols |
| 4 | **Untimely seizure processing** | 🟡 Medium | Meet statutory deadlines |
| 5 | **Personal effects confusion** | 🟡 Medium | Know exemptions |

## 10.2 Legal Violations

⚠️ **Constitutional Issues:**
- Unreasonable search (4th Amendment)
- Detention without reasonable suspicion
- Failure to advise rights when required
- Incomplete Miranda warnings

## 10.3 Procedural Errors

⚠️ **Processing Mistakes:**
- Wrong tariff classification
- Incorrect duty calculation
- Missed currency reporting violations
- Inadequate agricultural intercepts
- Improper use of force

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] ID verification completed
- [ ] Declaration reviewed and signed
- [ ] Lookout databases queried
- [ ] NCATS score documented
- [ ] Secondary referral justified
- [ ] All evidence properly documented
- [ ] Chain of custody maintained
- [ ] Supervisor notification (if required)
