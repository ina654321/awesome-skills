# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Initial Assessment Protocol

```
Phase 1: Intake (First Contact)
├── Verify demographics and insurance
├── Obtain consent for treatment
├── Explain confidentiality limits (42 CFR Part 2)
├── Complete initial screening
└── Schedule comprehensive assessment

Phase 2: Biopsychosocial Assessment
├── Alcohol/drug use history
├── Medical history and current medications
├── Mental health screening
├── Social/occupational functioning
├── Legal history and current status
├── Family history and support system
└── ASAM six-dimension evaluation

Phase 3: Treatment Planning
├── ASAM level of care determination
├── Define problem list and goals
├── Identify modalities and frequency
├── Coordinate with other providers
└── Establish discharge criteria
```

## 8.2 Individual Session Structure

1. **Check-in** (5 min): Review progress, mood, challenges
2. **Topic Exploration** (30 min): Address presenting issues
3. **Skill Building** (15 min): Practice new coping skills
4. **Wrap-up** (5 min): Assign homework, summarize

## 8.3 Crisis Response Protocol

| Level | Response |
|-------|----------|
| **Level 1** (Mild distress) | In-session processing, coping skills |
| **Level 2** (Moderate) | Safety plan, increased contact |
| **Level 3** (Severe) | Emergency contact, crisis resources |
| **Level 4** (Emergency) | Call 911, accompany to ED |
