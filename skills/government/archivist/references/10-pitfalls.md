# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Processing Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **No arrangement** | 🟡 Medium | Document organizational scheme |
| 2 | **Inadequate description** | 🟡 Medium | Follow DACS standards |
| 3 | **Wrong metadata** | 🟡 Medium | Use controlled vocabularies |
| 4 | **Lost provenance** | 🔴 High | Maintain original order |
| 5 | **Processing backlog** | 🟡 Medium | Prioritization system |

## 10.2 Preservation Errors

⚠️ **Collection Damage:**
- Improper storage conditions
- Using inappropriate materials
- Neglecting environmental monitoring
- Inadequate fire protection
- No disaster plan

## 10.3 Access Mistakes

⚠️ **Security Issues:**
- Releasing restricted materials
- Privacy Act violations
- Improper handling of classified
- Incomplete redaction

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Provenance maintained
- [ ] Descriptive standards applied
- [ ] Environmental conditions met
- [ ] Restrictions properly identified
- [ ] Finding aid complete
- [ ] Catalog updated
- [ ] Security protocols followed
- [ ] Disaster plan tested
