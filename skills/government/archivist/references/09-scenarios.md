# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Restricted Records

**User:** "Researcher requesting access to personnel files from 1975. What's the process?"

**Expert:**
> **Access Review:**
> 
> 1. **Restriction Identification**:
>    - Privacy Act (5 U.S.C. 552a)
>    - Freedom of Information Act exemptions
>    - Donor restrictions
>    - Presidential records restrictions
> 
> 2. **Review Process**:
>    - Examine for PII
>    - Assess law enforcement exemptions
>    - Consult with legal
>    - Determine redaction needs
> 
> 3. **Response Options**:
>    - Full access
>    - Partial access with redactions
>    - Redirect to alternative sources
>    - Still restricted (explain why)
> 
> 4. **Documentation**:
>    - Document review decision
>    - Retain copy of released material

## 9.2 Damaged Collection

**User:** "Water damage discovered in collection stored in basement. What are the next steps?"

**Expert:**
> **Emergency Response:**
> 
> 1. **Immediate Actions**:
>    - Remove from water source
>    - Assess extent of damage
>    - Document with photos
>    - Alert supervisor
> 
> 2. **Salvage**:
>    - Freeze-drying for wet materials
>    - Air drying for paper
>    - Separate wet from dry
>    - Mold prevention
> 
> 3. **Assessment**:
>    - Conservation consultation
>    - Determine restoration feasibility
>    - Prioritize items for treatment
> 
> 4. **Prevention**:
>    - Environmental system upgrade
>    - Regular monitoring
>    - Emergency plan

## 9.3 Digital Migration

**User:** "Agency has 20-year-old digital records on obsolete media. How to preserve?"

**Expert:**
> **Digital Preservation:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Identify media types
>    - Inventory file formats
>    - Assess obsolescence risk
> 
> 2. **Migration Strategy**:
>    - Transfer to current media
>    - Convert to open formats (PDF/A, TIFF)
>    - Validate integrity
>    - Add preservation metadata
> 
> 3. **Storage**:
>    - Multiple copies (LTO, cloud)
>    - Checksum verification
>    - Bit-level preservation
> 
> 4. **Access**:
>    - Create access copies
>    - Provide metadata for discovery
>    - Maintain migration path
