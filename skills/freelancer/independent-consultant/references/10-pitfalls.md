# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Not having a written contract** | High | Always use SOW and engagement contract |
| 2 | **Working without deposit** | High | 25-50% deposit before starting |
| 3 | **Scope creep without renegotiation** | High | Document change orders; renegotiate |
| 4 | **Underpricing to win business** | High | Price for value delivered, not time spent |
| 5 | **Not tracking billable hours** | High | Use time tracking software; bill all hours |
| 6 | **Missing invoicing deadlines** | Medium | Monthly invoicing; automated reminders |
| 7 | **Over-promising results** | High | Be specific about what you control |
| 8 | **Taking on any client who pays** | Medium | Vet for culture fit and engagement viability |
| 9 | **Not building pipeline** | High | Always have 3-6 months of prospects |
| 10 | **Ignoring client warning signs** | High | Silence, scope creep, payment delays |

## 10.2 Business Operations Mistakes

- **No separate business account**: Personal and business finances must separate
- **Not saving for taxes**: Set aside 30% of income quarterly
- **No liability insurance**: E&O (errors & omissions) critical
- **No retirement plan**: SEP-IRA or Solo 401(k) for self-employed
- **Not tracking expenses**: Business deductions reduce tax burden
- **No business entity**: LLC protects personal assets

## 10.3 Client Relationship Mistakes

- **Befriending clients**: Maintain professional boundaries
- **Being too available**: Set clear response time expectations
- **Taking sides in internal politics**: Stay neutral
- **Gossiping about other clients**: Confidentiality is paramount
- **Blame assignment**: Focus on solutions, not causes
- **Over-delivering on undeliverable scope**: Don't create unsustainable expectations

## 10.4 Deliverable Quality Mistakes

- **Late submissions**: Build in buffer time
- **Unpolished work**: Always quality-check before delivery
- **Not testing recommendations**: Ensure feasibility before proposing
- **Generic frameworks**: Customize to client context
- **Too much information**: Executive summary first, detail second
- **No visual communication**: Data visualization improves comprehension

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Written SOW/contract before any work
- [ ] 25-50% deposit received
- [ ] Clear scope with boundaries
- [ ] Stakeholder map documented
- [ ] Discovery completed before solution development
- [ ] Weekly status updates sent
- [ ] All hours tracked and billed
- [ ] Deliverables reviewed before sending
- [ ] Project closeout meeting conducted
- [ ] Testimonial/references requested
- [ ] Client permission for case study
- [ ] Final invoice sent within 30 days
- [ ] Follow-up scheduled or retainer negotiated
- [ ] Pipeline updated with new prospects
