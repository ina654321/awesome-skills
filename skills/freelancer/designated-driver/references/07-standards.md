## 7.2 Legal Requirements by State

| Requirement | Common Standard | Notes |
|-------------|-----------------|-------|
| Age Minimum | 21 years | Some states allow 18+ with restrictions |
| License Type | Valid driver's license | Commercial may be required for larger vehicles |
| Insurance | $100k/$300k/$50k liability minimum | Many apps require more |
| Background Check | Clean MVR (3-5 year lookback) | DUI/DWI disqualifies permanently |
| Vehicle Inspection | Annual state inspection | Varies by state |

## 7.3 DUI/DWI Laws Reference

| Offense Level | BAC Threshold | License Suspension | Criminal Charge |
|---------------|---------------|---------------------|----------------|
| First (Misdemeanor) | 0.08% | 90-180 days | Up to $2,000 fine + jail |
| Second (Misdemeanor) | 0.08% | 1-2 years | Up to $4,000 fine + jail |
| Aggravated (Felony) | 0.15%+ | Up to permanent revocation | Felony charges possible |
| Under 21 (Zero Tolerance) | 0.02%+ | 30-90 days | Varies by state |

## 7.4 Vehicle Safety Standards

### Pre-Trip Vehicle Inspection Checklist

- Headlights, brake lights, turn signals functional
- Tires: tread depth minimum 2/32", no visible damage
- Brakes: responsive, no unusual sounds
- Mirrors: all mirrors present and adjustable
- Seat belts: all seats functional
- First aid kit: stocked and accessible
- Emergency supplies: flashlight, flares, phone charger
- Vehicle cleanliness: professional appearance required

## 7.5 Insurance Coverage Types

| Coverage Type | Recommended Minimum | Purpose |
|---------------|---------------------|---------|
| Liability | $100k/$300k/$50k | Covers injuries to passengers/third parties |
| Collision | Actual cash value | Covers your vehicle damage |
| Comprehensive | Actual cash value | Covers theft, vandalism, weather |
| Uninsured Motorist | State minimum | Protects against uninsured drivers |
| Rideshare Gap | Per-trip coverage | Covers gap between personal and rideshare insurance |
