# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Client Booking Flow

```
Booking Process
├── 1. Initial Request
│   ├── Confirm pickup location and destination
│   ├── Note number of passengers and party size
│   ├── Inquire about vehicle type needs
│   └── Provide ETA and rate quote
│
├── 2. Pre-Service Communication
│   ├── Send confirmation with driver's credentials
│   ├── Provide vehicle description and license plate
│   ├── Confirm parking/loading instructions
│   └── Share estimated arrival time
│
└── 3. Day-of Service
    ├── Arrive 5-10 minutes early
    ├── Confirm client identity discreetly
    ├── Inspect vehicle for safety before loading
    └── Begin transport
```

## 8.2 Safety Protocol

### Assessing Inebriated Passengers

1. **Visual Assessment** (from parking lot before approach)
   - Observe gait, slurred speech indicators
   - Note if assistance is needed to stand/walk
   - Check for visible injury or distress

2. **Capacity Evaluation**
   - Can passenger follow basic instructions?
   - Can they state their destination?
   - Are they violent or aggressive?
   - Do they have a valid payment method?

3. **Accommodation Protocol**
   - Ensure passenger enters on curbside, not traffic side
   - Assist with seatbelt if needed (don't force)
   - Confirm destination one final time before departure
   - Drive smoothly (avoid sudden stops, sharp turns)

### Special Considerations

- **Intoxicated but mobile:** Safe to transport
- **Unconscious/unresponsive:** Call 911, do not transport
- **Violent or threatening:** Refuse service, call law enforcement
- **Injured party:** Recommend medical attention, assist if able

## 8.3 Emergency Response

```
Incident Response
├── Vehicle Breakdown
│   ├── Pull over safely, turn on hazard lights
│   ├── Contact dispatch/backup driver
│   ├── Keep passengers calm in locked vehicle
│   └── Arrange alternative transport if needed
│
├── Passenger Medical Emergency
│   ├── Pull over immediately
│   ├── Call 911
│   ├── Administer first aid if trained
│   └── Remain with passenger until EMS arrives
│
└── Accident Response
    ├── Check all passengers for injuries
    ├── Call 911 if any injuries
    ├── Secure scene, exchange information
    └── Document incident thoroughly
```

## 8.4 Post-Service Protocol

1. Assist passenger to door if needed
2. Confirm passenger is with responsible party or in safe location
3. Collect payment (tip awareness - don't pressure)
4. Thank passenger and wish them safety
5. Log ride details: time, distance, route, observations
6. Refuel/reset vehicle for next service

## 8.5 Vehicle Maintenance Workflow

- Daily: Pre-trip inspection (10 min)
- Weekly: Fluid levels, tire pressure check
- Monthly: Interior cleaning, exterior wash
- Quarterly: Oil change, brake inspection
- Annually: Full mechanical inspection, insurance review
