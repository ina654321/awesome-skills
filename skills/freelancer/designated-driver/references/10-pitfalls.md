# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Transporting visibly unconscious person** | High | Call 911 immediately; do not transport |
| 2 | **Overloading vehicle** | High | Know your capacity, schedule backup or multi-trip |
| 3 | **Driving without proper insurance** | High | Verify commercial/personal ride insurance gap coverage |
| 4 | **Skipping vehicle inspection** | High | Daily checklist prevents breakdowns and liability |
| 5 | **Leaving intoxicated person at dropoff alone** | High | Confirm they're with someone or entering safe building |
| 6 | **Engaging with aggressive passengers** | Medium | Stay calm, pull over in safe location if needed |
| 7 | **Not verifying passenger identity** | Medium | Confirm you're picking up the right party |
| 8 | **Speeding or aggressive driving** | High | Smooth, defensive driving protects everyone |
| 9 | **No documentation of rides** | Medium | Log all trips for liability protection |
| 10 | **Accepting cash outside platform** | Medium | Platform payment protects both parties |

## 10.2 Legal & Insurance Red Flags

- Operating without proper commercial insurance (personal policies often void)
- DUI/DWI on driving record discovered after hire
- Vehicle registration lapses
- Passengers in cargo areas of SUVs/vans
- Refusing to provide receipt/invoice for business expenses
- No written service agreement with regular clients

## 10.3 Safety Anti-Patterns

- Picking up from private residences (use designated meeting points)
- Not checking mirrors before opening doors
- Driving while fatigued
- Not maintaining emergency contact info accessible
- Wearing distracting clothing/accessories
- Not knowing local traffic laws for the area you're operating in

## 10.4 Business Mistakes

- **Underpricing:** Minimum $50 for short rides, $100+ for hour-plus distances
- **No cancellation policy:** Charge 50% for <2hr notice cancellations
- **No backup plan:** Always have 1-2 backup driver contacts
- **Poor record keeping:** Track all miles, expenses, income for taxes
- **Ignoring MVR requirements:** Check your own driving record quarterly

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Valid driver's license with clean record
- [ ] Commercial or rideshare-gap insurance active
- [ ] Vehicle registration and inspection current
- [ ] Daily pre-trip inspection completed
- [ ] First aid kit stocked
- [ ] Emergency contact list accessible
- [ ] GPS tracking enabled (share with dispatch/family)
- [ ] No weapons in vehicle
- [ ] Professional appearance maintained
- [ ] Service agreement template available
- [ ] Mileage tracking system in place
- [ ] Clean, odor-free vehicle maintained
