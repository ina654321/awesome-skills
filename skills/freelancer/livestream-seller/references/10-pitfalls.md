# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Not disclosing sponsored content** | High | Always use #ad, #sponsored, or "Ad:" label |
| 2 | **Selling counterfeit or unauthorized goods** | High | Only sell authenticated, authorized products |
| 3 | **Fake follower purchases** | High | Build organically; bots destroy engagement rate |
| 4 | **Ignoring copyright music** | High | Use royalty-free libraries exclusively |
| 5 | **Poor audio quality** | High | Invest in lavalier or condenser mic; test levels |
| 6 | **Inconsistent streaming schedule** | Medium | Set and announce regular schedule |
| 7 | **No backup internet** | High | Always have mobile hotspot as backup |
| 8 | **Overpricing vs. competitors** | Medium | Research competitor pricing before each stream |
| 9 | **Not fulfilling orders on time** | High | Platforms penalize slow fulfillment |
| 10 | **Ignoring negative feedback** | Medium | Respond professionally to all reviews |

## 10.2 FTC & Legal Violations

- **FTC endorsement guidelines**: Must disclose material connections clearly
- **False advertising**: Never exaggerate product capabilities
- **Misrepresentation**: Authenticity claims must be verifiable
- **Privacy violations**: Do not share customer data across platforms
- **Scam alerts**: Never ask viewers for payment outside platform

## 10.3 Platform-Specific Mistakes

### TikTok
- Posting low-quality vertical video
- Ignoring trending sounds and formats
- Not using TikTok Shop features during streams
- Removing duets/stitches that drive traffic

### Instagram
- Posting only sales content (no value posts)
- Ignoring Reels algorithm (mandatory for reach)
- Not utilizing Stories for behind-the-scenes
- Blocking DMs from potential customers

### YouTube
- Uploading horizontal video to Shorts
- Not enabling end screens and cards
- Ignoring YouTube Shopping integration
- Posting inconsistently

## 10.4 Technical Mistakes

- **Insufficient bandwidth**: Below 10 Mbps upload causes buffering
- **Wrong aspect ratio**: Posting 16:9 to TikTok/Instagram results in letterboxing
- **No closed captions**: Required for accessibility and algorithm boost
- **Cluttered background**: Distracting from products
- **Backlighting**: Product silhouettes unreadable

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All sponsored content properly disclosed
- [ ] Product inventory verified before stream
- [ ] Technical equipment tested (audio, video, internet)
- [ ] Backup internet connection available
- [ ] All products legally authorized for sale
- [ ] Copyright-free music library prepared
- [ ] Pricing researched against competitors
- [ ] Community guidelines reviewed and followed
- [ ] Order fulfillment SLA met (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Tax obligations tracked (25-30% set aside)
- [ ] Analytics reviewed after each stream
- [ ] Follower growth and engagement tracked
