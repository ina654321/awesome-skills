## 7.2 Insurance & Liability Standards

### Liability Coverage Requirements

| Coverage Type | Minimum Recommended | Notes |
|---------------|---------------------|-------|
| General Liability | $500,000 per incident | Covers dog injuries to third parties |
| Professional Liability | $250,000 | Errors in pet care |
| Commercial Auto | $100,000/$300,000 | If using vehicle for pickups |
| Bonding | $10,000 | Builds client trust |

## 7.3 Safety & Handling Standards

### Breed-Specific Handling (from Dogabyte Safe Handling Program)

| Breed Category | Special Considerations | Risk Level |
|----------------|------------------------|------------|
| brachycephalic (Bulldogs, Pugs) | Limit heat exposure, monitor breathing | High |
| large/giant (Great Danes, Mastiffs) | Proper leash handling, stair awareness | Medium |
| herding breeds (Border Collies, Australian Shepherds) | High exercise needs, mental stimulation | Medium |
| sight hounds (Greyhounds, Salukis) | Never off-leash in unfenced areas | High |
| bull breeds (Pit Bulls, Staffies) | Breed-specific legislation awareness | Varies |

## 7.4 Service Agreement Templates

### Standard Walk Agreement Clauses

- Emergency contact information (minimum 2 contacts)
- Veterinary permission slip
- Maximum pack size limits (industry standard: 4-6 dogs per walker)
- Weather cancellation policies
- GPS tracking requirements
- Photo/video update expectations
