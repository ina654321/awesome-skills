## 7.2 Patient Transport Classifications

| Transport Type | Requirements | Typical Use |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|
| Basic Life Support (BLS) | CPR cert, first aid, wheelchair handling | Non-emergency, ambulatory patients |
| Advanced Life Support (ALS) | EMT-B or higher certification | Patients requiring monitoring |
| Stretcher Transport | Two-person team, proper immobilization | Bed-bound patients |
| Wheelchair Van | ADA-compliant vehicle, securement training | Outpatient appointments |

## 7.3 Required Certifications

| Certification | Validity | Renewal | Provider |
|---------------|----------|---------|----------|
| CPR/AED (Healthcare Provider) | 2 years | Every 2 years | American Heart Association, Red Cross |
| First Aid | 2 years | Every 2 years | OSHA-approved provider |
| Bloodborne Pathogens | 1 year | Annual | OSHA-approved provider |
| Wheelchair Securement | Varies | Varies | Manufacturer or third-party |
| HIPAA Privacy Training | Varies | Annual | HHS-certified programs |

## 7.4 Documentation Requirements

### Pre-Transport Assessment Checklist

- Patient name and date of birth verified (two identifiers)
- Current medications list obtained
- Special needs documented (oxygen, mobility aids, sensory impairments)
- Emergency contact confirmed
- Attending physician or facility authorization on file
- Insurance/authorization number verified
- Special instructions noted (NPO status, IV fluids, etc.)

## 7.5 Equipment Standards

| Equipment | Standard | Inspection Frequency |
|-----------|----------|----------------------|
| Wheelchair | ADA-compliant, weight capacity verified | Pre-shift |
| Stretchers | Proper functioning, clean linens | Pre-shift |
| Oxygen (if carried) | Tank levels checked, regulator functional | Daily |
| First aid kit | Stocked per OSHA standards | Weekly |
| Vehicle | Clean, temperature-controlled | Pre-trip |
