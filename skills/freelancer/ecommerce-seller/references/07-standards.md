## 7.3 Product Photography Standards

### Minimum Image Requirements by Platform

| Platform | Minimum Images | Video Required | White Background |
|----------|---------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Amazon | 6 | Recommended | Yes (main image) |
| eBay | 1 | Optional | No (but recommended) |
| Etsy | 1 | Optional | No |
| Poshmark | 4 | Optional | No |
| Shopify Store | 1 | Recommended | Yes (main image) |

### Lighting Standards

- **Color accuracy**: Realistic, not filtered
- **Minimum resolution**: 1000x1000 pixels (Amazon: 1600x1600)
- **File format**: JPEG preferred, PNG accepted
- **File size**: Under 15MB per image
- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 or 4:3, no distortion
