# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Selling counterfeit/knockoff goods** | High | Only source from authorized distributors |
| 2 | **Poor product photography** | High | Invest in $100-300 lighting setup and tripod |
| 3 | **Ignoring return requests** | High | Respond within 24 hours always |
| 4 | **Underpricing to undercut competitors** | Medium | Price for profit margin, not just sales volume |
| 5 | **Not tracking expenses for taxes** | High | Use Wave (free) or QuickBooks for expense tracking |
| 6 | **Shipping without tracking** | High | Always use tracked shipping for items $25+ |
| 7 | **Keyword stuffing titles** | Medium | Natural keyword integration > quantity |
| 8 | **Listing without researching competition** | Medium | Always review top 10 similar listings before listing |
| 9 | **Ignoring platform policy changes** | Medium | Review seller updates monthly |
| 10 | **Mixing personal and business finances** | High | Separate business bank account required |

## 10.2 Listing Mistakes

- **Misleading titles**: Exaggerating features or compatibility
- **Incomplete descriptions**: Missing dimensions, materials, or inclusions
- **No size reference**: Coins, rulers, or models without scale reference
- **Stock photos used**: Always photograph actual items when possible
- **Over-editing photos**: Color correction that misrepresents the product

## 10.3 Shipping Mistakes

- **Wrong address entered**: Always copy-paste from buyer confirmation
- **Unprotected packaging**: Items moving freely in box
- **Shipping to old address**: Check for address updates before shipping
- **No insurance on valuable items**: Items over $100 should have insurance
- **Late handling**: Missing the stated handling window

## 10.4 Legal & Compliance Issues

- **No business license**: May be required depending on volume
- **Unreported income**: 1099 forms are reported to IRS
- **False claims**: Health/medical claims on non-medical products
- **Import restriction violations**: Some products banned from import
- **Product safety non-compliance**: CPSC requirements for certain categories

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All items accurately described with measurements
- [ ] High-quality photos (minimum 1000x1000px)
- [ ] White background main image
- [ ] Competitor pricing researched
- [ ] All applicable fees calculated into price
- [ ] Tracking provided within handling window
- [ ] Adequate packaging for shipping conditions
- [ ] Customer inquiries responded to within 24 hours
- [ ] Return requests handled professionally
- [ ] Business finances separated from personal
- [ ] Quarterly estimated taxes set aside
- [ ] Inventory levels updated after each sale
