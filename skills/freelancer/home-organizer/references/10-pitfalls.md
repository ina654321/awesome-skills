# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Taking on hoarding cases without training** | High | Get specialized certification; refer complex cases |
| 2 | **Pushing clients to discard faster than comfortable** | High | Let client lead the pace |
| 3 | **Working without signed service agreement** | High | Always document scope and policies |
| 4 | **Not disclosing all fees upfront** | High | Provide complete fee schedule before first session |
| 5 | **Making decisions for the client** | High | Guide but client decides what stays |
| 6 | **Not photographing before/during work** | Medium | Documentation protects everyone |
| 7 | **Underestimating time required** | Medium | Build in buffer time; over-quote slightly |
| 8 | **Not wearing appropriate clothing/footwear** | Medium | Closed-toe shoes; comfortable clothes |
| 9 | **Organizing without understanding lifestyle** | Medium | Always ask how space is actually used |
| 10 | **Not referring to mental health professionals when needed** | High | Know your scope of practice |

## 10.2 Client Relationship Mistakes

- **Judging the client**: "How did you let it get this bad?"
- **Labeling items**: Calling possessions "junk" or "clutter"
- **Comparing to others**: "My closet is nothing like this"
- **Making promises**: "We'll finish this room today"
- **Overstepping boundaries**: Taking charge of decisions
- **Not respecting privacy**: Sharing before/after photos without consent
- **Pushing religious/moral values**: Items are functional, not "wasteful"

## 10.3 Safety Mistakes

- **Not asking about pets before arriving**: Pet waste is biohazard
- **Working in unsafe structural areas**: Watch for rot, weakness
- **Lifting heavy items without help**: Risk of injury
- **Not washing hands**: Sanitation is essential
- **Leaving hazardous materials unsecured**: Chemicals, sharp objects
- **Working alone in isolated areas**: Safety check-ins required

## 10.4 Business Mistakes

- **Not getting deposit for large projects**: 25-50% deposit required
- **No cancellation policy**: Charge for cancelled sessions
- **No liability insurance**: E&O and general liability essential
- **Not tracking mileage**: Tax deductible for business travel
- **Mixing personal motivation with professional service**: Maintain boundaries
- **Not saving for taxes**: 25-30% of income

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Initial assessment completed
- [ ] Client categorized correctly (chronic disorg vs. hoarding)
- [ ] Safety hazards identified and addressed
- [ ] Service agreement signed
- [ ] Before photos taken (with consent)
- [ ] Goals and priorities documented
- [ ] Decision-making process clarified (client leads)
- [ ] Sorting categories clearly defined
- [ ] All items processed, not just relocated
- [ ] After photos taken (with consent)
- [ ] Maintenance plan provided
- [ ] Follow-up scheduled or check-in completed
- [ ] All payments collected
- [ ] Referrals made if needed
