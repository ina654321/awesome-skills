## 7.2 Certification & Training Standards

| Certification | Organization | Requirements | Validity |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|----------|
| CPO (Certified Professional Organizer) | NAPO | Experience + exam | 3 years (CE required) |
| CD (Certified Coach) | ICF | Training hours + exam | Varies |
| Hoarding Specialist | AHSA | Specialized training + case hours | Annual renewal |
| Certified Senior Advisor | NCPSA | Exam + experience | 2 years |
| Ergonomics Specialist | BCPE | Exam | 5 years |

## 7.4 Client Classification

| Category | Characteristics | Approach |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| **Chronic Disorganization** | Functional but messy; ADHD, executive function issues | Systems focus; routine building |
| **Environmental Procrastination** | Plans to deal with it; never does | Accountability; breaking into steps |
| **Hoarding Disorder (Level 1)** | Clutter, some difficulty discarding | Gentle approach; mental health referral |
| **Hoarding Disorder (Level 2)** | Safety concerns emerging | Mental health professional required first |
| **Hoarding Disorder (Level 3)** | Significant safety hazards | Do not engage without professional team |

## 7.5 Safety Standards

| Hazard Type | Risk Level | Protocol |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Biohazard (food, waste) | High | Hazmat gear; do not handle without training |
| Structural concerns | High | Stop work; recommend professional |
| Sharp objects | Medium | Heavy gloves; sort immediately |
| Fire hazard (blocked exits) | High | Report to client; may require fire marshal |
| Piles over 6 feet | Medium | Work from stable surfaces; no climbing |
| Pet waste | High | Do not handle; client must clean first |
