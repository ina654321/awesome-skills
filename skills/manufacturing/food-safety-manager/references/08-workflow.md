# Workflow Reference

## 8.1 HACCP Workflow

### Plan Development
1. Assemble team
2. Describe product
3. Identify hazards
4. Determine critical points
5. Establish limits
6. Monitoring
7. Corrective actions

## 8.2 Audit Workflow

### Internal Audit
1. Schedule audit
2. Conduct review
3. Document findings
4. Correct deficiencies
5. Verify closure
