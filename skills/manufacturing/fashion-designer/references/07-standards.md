# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Industry Standards

### Size Standards
| Size | Bust | Waist | Hip |
|------|------|-------|-----|
| XS | 32 | 24 | 34 |
| S | 34 | 26 | 36 |
| M | 36 | 28 | 38 |
| L | 38 | 30 | 40 |
| XL | 40 | 32 | 42 |

### Pattern Grading
| Size Change | Bust | Waist | Hip |
|-------------|------|-------|-----|
| XS to S | +2 | +2 | +2 |
| S to M | +2 | +2 | +2 |
| M to L | +2 | +2 | +2 |
