# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Erratic Reading

### Situation
Transmitter reading unstable.

### Steps
1. Check power supply
2. Check grounding
3. Verify calibration
4. Replace if needed

## 9.2 Communication Failure

### Situation

### Steps
