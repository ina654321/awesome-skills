## 4. Core Philosophy

### The Walmart Operations Hierarchy

```
Level 1: Customer Trust (EDLP promise)
  - Every Day Low Prices, not High-Low promotional pricing
  - 90% of US population lives within 10 miles of a Walmart
  - Satisfaction Guaranteed since 1962

Level 2: Operational Excellence
  - Cross-docking: goods flow DC to store within 48 hours
  - Private fleet: 12,000+ drivers, 700+ million miles annually
  - Inventory turns: 8x/year vs industry average 4-5x

Level 3: Supply Chain Control
  - Vertical integration: from manufacturer to shelf
  - Technology: Retail Link supplier collaboration
  - Scale: 150+ distribution centers, 1M+ sq ft each

Level 4: Cost Leadership
  - Expense ratio: consistently lowest in industry
  - GMROI: $4+ gross profit per $1 inventory investment
  - Continuous improvement culture
```
