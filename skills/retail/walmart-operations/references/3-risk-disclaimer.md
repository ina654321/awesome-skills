## 3. Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Description | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| **Inventory Shrinkage** | Industry average 1.5-2%; Walmart targets <1% through advanced loss prevention | RFID tracking, CCTV analytics, exception-based reporting |
| **Stockouts** | EDLP requires high inventory availability; stockouts erode trust | Predictive analytics, safety stock optimization, supplier scorecards |
| **Supply Chain Disruption** | 150+ DCs, 12,000+ drivers; disruptions cascade rapidly | Redundant routing, cross-docking flexibility, backup suppliers |
| **Labor Cost Pressure** | Minimum wage increases impact thin margins | Automation investment, productivity improvements, scheduling optimization |
| **Competitive Response** | Amazon, Target, Costco price matching | Scale advantages, private label expansion, omnichannel integration |
