## Phase 1: Inventory Management Excellence

```
Weekly Cycle Count Process:
  1. Generate picklist by velocity (A-items weekly, B-biweekly, C-monthly)
  2. Two associates count independently (no communicating counts)
  3. Reconcile: match = pass; mismatch = recount within 24 hrs
  4. Investigate variance >$100 or >2% of category total
  5. Adjust inventory records in system with manager approval
  6. Report: accuracy %, variance $, top shrinkage categories

Target Metrics:
  - Inventory accuracy: >=98%
  - Cycle count completion: 100% on schedule
  - Shrink: <1.0% of sales (Walmart industry-leading target)
  - Dead stock (90+ days): <3% of inventory value
```

### Phase 2: Cross-Docking Implementation

```
Cross-Docking Types at Walmart:
  
  1. Standard Cross-Docking
     - Direct flow from receiving to shipping
     - Minimal handling and storage
     - Perishables, high-velocity items

  2. Consolidation Cross-Docking
     - Combine multiple supplier shipments
     - Full truckload optimization
     - Reduces transportation costs

  3. Deconsolidation Cross-Docking
     - Break bulk shipments for store allocation
     - Customize quantities per store
     - Improves inventory positioning

  4. Hybrid Cross-Docking
     - Mix of storage and direct flow
     - Seasonal items, promotional goods
     - Flexible based on demand signals

  5. Retail Cross-Docking
     - Direct to store floor
     - Bypasses backroom entirely
     - Furniture, appliances, seasonal displays

Implementation Checklist:
  - Supplier coordination and scheduling
  - DC staging area design (19km conveyor belts avg)
  - Real-time inventory visibility
  - Quality control checkpoints
  - Store receiving process alignment
```

Route Optimization:
