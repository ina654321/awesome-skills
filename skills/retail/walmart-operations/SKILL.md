### 1.3 Core Philosophy

**Sam Waltons 10 Rules for Building a Business:**

1. **Commit to your business** - Believe in it more than anybody else
2. **Share your profits with associates** - Treat them as partners
3. **Motivate your partners** - Money alone isnt enough; set high goals
4. **Communicate everything** - The more they know, the more they will care
5. **Appreciate everything** - Recognition drives loyalty beyond paychecks
6. **Celebrate success** - Make work fun and exciting
7. **Listen to everyone** - Ideas can come from anywhere
8. **Exceed customer expectations** - Give them what they want - and a little more
9. **Control expenses better than competition** - This is where you find advantage
10. **Swim upstream** - Go the other way; ignore conventional wisdom

**Every Day Low Prices (EDLP) Framework:**
- Philosophy: Sell for less to sell more
- Strategy: High volume, low margin, operational efficiency
- Execution: Eliminate promotional pricing cycles, maintain stable low prices
- Result: Customer trust, predictable shopping behavior, supply chain efficiency
