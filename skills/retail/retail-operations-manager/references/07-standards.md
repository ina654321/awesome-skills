# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Loss Prevention Standards

- [Retail Industry Leaders Association (RILA)](https://www.rila.org/) - Loss Prevention Council, SHRINK awareness initiatives
- [National Retail Federation (NRF) Loss Prevention](https://nrf.com/topics/loss-prevention) - Retail theft statistics, LP standards, and APC (Average Percentage Change) benchmarks
- [EAS (Electronic Article Surveillance)](https://www.nrf.org/topics/loss-prevention/eas-systems) - Industry standards for RF/AM/RFID tags and deactivation systems
- [International Association of Professional Security Consultants (IAPSC)](https://www.iapsc.org/) - Security consulting standards and certifications
- [Checkpoint Systems](https://www.checkpointsystems.com/) - RFID and EAS product standards, shrink management

## 7.2 Inventory & Supply Chain Standards

- [GS1](https://www.gs1.org/) - Barcode standards (UPC/EAN), RFID standards (EPC), GPC product classification
- [GSCP (Global Standard Collaboration Process)](https://www.gs1.org/standards/gpc) - Product data synchronization standards
- [RFID Standards (ISO/IEC 18000 series)](https://www.iso.org/standard/53442.html) - RFID air interface protocol for item management
- [VICS (Voluntary Interindustry Commerce Solutions)](https://vics.org/) - EDI standards for retail supply chain
- [WMSI (Warehouse Management Systems Institute)](https://www.wmsi.org/) - WMS implementation standards and benchmarks

## 7.3 Safety & Compliance

- [OSHA Retail Safety Standards](https://www.osha.gov/retail-industry) - Workplace safety regulations for retail environments
- [NFPA (National Fire Protection Association)](https://www.nfpa.org/) - Fire safety codes for commercial retail spaces
- [ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) Compliance for Retail](https://www.ada.gov/resources/ada-guidance/) - Accessibility standards for store design and checkout
- [FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)](https://www.fda.gov/food/food-safety-modernization-act-fsma) - Food handling standards for grocery and food retail

## 7.4 Omnichannel Standards

- [NRF Omnichannel Research](https://nrf.com/topics/omnichannel) - BOPIS, SFS, curbside pickup benchmarks and best practices
- [CIC (Checkout Institute)](https://checkoutinstitute.com/) - Contactless checkout and payment standards
- [PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/) - Payment security compliance for POS systems
- [Curbside Pickup Standards - IHL Group](https://www.ihlg1.com/) - Omnichannel fulfillment technology benchmarks

## 7.5 Labor & Scheduling Standards

- [ILEA (Institute for Supply Management)](https://www.ismworld.org/) - Labor standards and procurement guidelines
- [ASTD / ATD (Association for Talent Development)](https://www.td.org/) - Training standards for retail associates
- [EEOC (Equal Employment Opportunity)](https://www.eeoc.gov/) - Employment compliance standards
- [Predictive Scheduling Laws](https://www.shrm.org/topics/legal-trends/pages/predictable-scheduling-laws.aspx) - State-level scheduling regulations (CA, OR, NY)
