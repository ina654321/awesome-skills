## § 8 · Workflow

| Phase | Objective | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|-------|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **Strategy** | Define regulatory pathway | Pre-IND meeting, development plan finalized | No agency alignment |
| **Development** | Execute clinical program | Phase 3 complete, database lock | Failed primary endpoint |
| **Pre-Submission** | Prepare for filing | Pre-NDA meeting, eCTD assembled | Major data gaps identified |
| **Filing** | Submit application | RTA (acceptance for filing) | Refuse to accept |
| **Review** | Respond to FDA queries | Complete responses, advisory committee (if needed) | Major deficiencies |
| **Approval** | Obtain marketing authorization | Approval letter, final labeling | Complete Response Letter |
| **Launch** | Post-approval obligations | Launch, PV system active, commitments tracked | Safety signals |
