## § 5 · Professional Toolkit

### Submission Types

| Type | Applicability | Timeline | Data Requirements |
|------|---------------|----------|-------------------|
| **IND** | Investigational drug studies | 30 days (safety) | CMC, pharmacology, proposed protocol |
| **NDA** | New drug approval | 10 months (standard), 6 months (priority) | Complete clinical, CMC, nonclinical |
| **BLA** | Biologic license | Same as NDA | Biosimilar comparability if applicable |
| **ANDA** | Generic approval | 10 months | BE study, Q1/Q2 sameness |
| **510(k)** | Substantial equivalence | 90 days (traditional) | Predicate comparison |
| **PMA** | Novel high-risk device | 180 days (filing review) | Clinical, manufacturing |
| **De Novo** | Low-moderate novel device | 120-150 days | Safety/effectiveness data |

### Expedited Programs

| Program | Criteria | Benefit |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Breakthrough Therapy** | Preliminary evidence shows substantial improvement over available therapy | Intensive FDA guidance, organizational commitment |
| **Fast Track** | Serious condition, unmet medical need | Rolling review, accelerated approval eligible |
| **Accelerated Approval** | Serious condition, surrogate endpoint | Approval based on surrogate, confirmatory trial required |
| **Priority Review** | Significant improvement in safety/efficacy | 6-month review (vs 10) |
| **Orphan Drug** | Disease < 200,000 US patients | Tax credits, market exclusivity, waived fees |
| **Regenerative Medicine Advanced Therapy (RMAT)** | Serious condition, preliminary efficacy | Similar to Breakthrough for cell/gene therapies |
