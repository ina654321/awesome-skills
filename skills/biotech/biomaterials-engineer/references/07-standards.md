## 7.3 Degradation Kinetics Reference

### PLGA Hydrolysis Rate Constants

| Grade | LA:GA | Mn0 (kDa) | k_deg (wk⁻⁰·⁶) | β (shape) | t_50% (weeks) | t_90% Mn (weeks) |
|-------|-------|-----------|----------------|-----------|----------------|------------------|
| 50:50 | 50:50 | 80 | 0.12–0.15 | 0.5–0.6 | 3–4 | 6–8 |
| 65:35 | 65:35 | 80 | 0.08–0.10 | 0.5–0.6 | 5–7 | 9–12 |
| 75:25 | 75:25 | 80 | 0.06–0.08 | 0.5–0.6 | 8–10 | 16–20 |
| 85:15 | 85:15 | 80 | 0.04–0.06 | 0.5–0.6 | 14–18 | 28–36 |

Formula: Mn(t) = Mn0 × exp(−k_deg × t^β)

### Sterilization Effects on Molecular Weight

| Method | Typical Mn Reduction | Notes |
|--------|---------------------|-------|
| Gamma (25 kGy) | 20–40% | Chain scission; may accelerate degradation |
| EtO | 0–5% | Preferred for polymers; no chain scission |
| e-beam (15 kGy) | 10–20% | Lower dose = less degradation |
| Autoclave | 50–80% | NOT recommended for PLGA/PLA |

## 7.5 ISO 10993 Biocompatibility Test Selection Guide

Based on ISO 10993-1:2018 risk-based approach:

| Device Contact | Contact Duration | Cytotoxicity | Sensitization | Irritation | Implantation | Genotoxicity |
|---------------|-----------------|--------------|---------------|------------|--------------|---------------|
| Surface (intact skin) | < 24h | Required | Consider | Consider | — | — |
| Surface (mucosa) | < 24h | Required | Required | Required | — | — |
| Surface (broken skin) | ≤ 30d | Required | Required | Required | Consider | — |
| External communicating (blood path) | ≤ 24h | Required | Required | Required | — | Required |
| Implant (bone) | > 30d | Required | Required | Required | Required | Required |
| Implant (soft tissue) | > 30d | Required | Required | Required | Required | Consider |

## 7.6 Key Software & Characterization Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Key Output |
|------|---------|------------|
| **MATLAB / Python (SciPy)** | Degradation kinetics fitting, drug release modeling | Mn(t), drug release curves |
| **COMSOL Multiphysics** | Diffusion-reaction, scaffold FEA | Concentration profiles, stress distribution |
| **Abaqus** | Structural FEA for implants | Von Mises stress, fatigue life |
| **ImageJ / Fiji** | Porosity, pore size from SEM/microCT | φ%, pore size distribution |
| **GraphPad Prism** | Cytotoxicity statistics | IC50, viability curves |
| **Minitab / JMP** | DOE, parameter optimization | Response surface models |
| **Micro-CT (SkyScan 1272)** | 3D pore architecture | Porosity, interconnectivity, pore size |
| **DMA (TA Q800)** | Viscoelastic properties | E', E'', tan δ vs. T/frequency |
| **GPC / SEC** | Molecular weight distribution | Mn, Mw, PDI |
| **ICP-MS** | Metal ion release quantification | μg/L of Ti, Co, Cr, Al, V |
| **XPS** | Surface chemistry analysis | Atomic composition, bonding states |
| **SEM / TEM** | Morphology, coating uniformity | Particle size, film thickness |
