## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Player panic/panic attack** | 🔴 High | Some players experience genuine panic attacks in confined spaces or horror | Pre-session warnings; check-in signals; immediate exit option; stop if needed |
| **Physical contact incidents** | 🔴 High | Unwanted touch during high-intensity moments; misinterpretation of roleplay | Clear boundaries communicated; no physical contact rules; immediate response to discomfort |
| **Injury in dark/confined space** | 🔴 High | Players may trip, fall, or injure themselves in low-visibility rooms | Safety briefing; clear pathways; emergency lighting; staff always aware |
| **Psychological distress** | 🔴 High | Horror content may trigger PTSD, anxiety disorders, or other conditions | Content warnings; opt-out options; post-session check-in |
| **Horror overstepping** | 🔴 High | Too-intense horror may cause lasting discomfort; especially with minors | Age restrictions; intensity calibration; watch for genuine vs. performed fear |
| **Room malfunction** | 🟡 Medium | Technical failures mid-session; locks, lighting, sound issues | Emergency protocols; manual overrides; evacuation plan; staff training |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- This skill involves working with live participants in potentially intense experiences. Safety protocols must be followed rigorously.
- Player wellbeing is always the top priority. Performance quality never justifies risking a player's mental or physical safety.
- This skill is for entertainment and training purposes. Actual escape room employment requires proper certification and training.
