## 7. Standards & Reference

### 7.1 剧本杀 Genres & Hosting Approaches

| Genre | Duration | Player Count | Key DM Skills | Difficulty Calibration |
|-----------------|----------------------|-------------------|------------------------|---------------------|
| **硬核推理** | 6-10 hours | 6-10 | Complex clue design, logical consistency, fair play | High — solvable with attention |
| **情感本** | 4-6 hours | 6-8 | Emotional engineering, trigger awareness, catharsis facilitation | Medium — story over logic |
| **机制本** | 4-6 hours | 6-12 | Game mechanics mastery, rule adjudication, fun calibration | Medium — engagement over depth |
| **欢乐本** | 3-5 hours | 6-12 | Comedic timing, improv flexibility, energy management | Low — pure entertainment |
| **恐怖本** | 3-5 hours | 4-8 | Atmosphere control, jump timing, fear management | Medium — thrills over logic |
| **阵营本** | 4-6 hours | 8-12 | Group dynamics, secret management, betrayal handling | Medium — social over plot |
| **沉浸本** | 5-8 hours | 4-8 | NPC performance, multi-character arcs, emotional depth | High — theatrical excellence |

### 7.2 Session Quality Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Player Engagement** | Active participation time
| **Clue Solvability** | Players who found key clues
| **Emotional Peak Rating** | Post-session survey (1-10) | >8 |
| **Pacing Flow Score** | DM self-assessment (1-10) | >7 |
| **Return Rate** | Players who rebook

### 7.3 Script Evaluation Framework (8 Dimensions)

| Dimension | Weight | Key Questions |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| 人物弧光 | 15% | Do characters grow? Is their arc satisfying? |
| 动机合理性 | 20% | Can you trace why each person did what they did? |
| 证据链完整度 | 20% | Is there a unique solution? Are all clues necessary? |
| 节奏设计 | 15% | Are there peaks and valleys? Does it drag? |
| 核诡创新性 | 10% | Is the twist surprising but fair? |
| 情感共鸣点 | 10% | Do players connect emotionally? |
| 机制趣味性 | 5% | Are mini-games engaging? |
| 商业适配性 | 5% | Does it work for venue capacity and turnover? |
