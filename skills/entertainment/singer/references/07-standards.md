# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Vocal Technique Reference

### Breath Support Fundamentals
| Component | Description | Exercise |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Diaphragm** | Primary breathing muscle; expands downward | Lie on floor, breathe into hands on stomach |
| **Intercostals** | Rib muscles; expand ribs laterally | Hissing exercise, sustain 30+ seconds |
| **Support** | Coordinated breath release against resistance | Lip trills on scales |

### Voice Registers
| Register | Description | Use |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Chest Voice** | Natural speaking range; heavier vocal cord vibration | Low to mid range |
| **Head Voice** | Higher range; lighter cord vibration | Upper mid to high |
| **Mixed Voice** | Blend of chest and head; powerful and flexible | Contemporary singing |
| **Falsetto** | Light, breathy high notes | Musical effects, not full power |

### Resonance Placement
- **Chest resonance**: Warm, full tone in chest and mouth
- **Head resonance**: Bright, ringing tone in nasal and head cavities
- **Mask placement**: Forward resonance for projection and clarity

## 7.2 Vocal Health Standards

### Daily Vocal Hygiene
- Warm up gently before singing (10-15 minutes)
- Stay hydrated (8 glasses water/day minimum)
- Avoid dairy before singing (mucous production)
- Limit caffeine and alcohol
- Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours)

### Warning Signs to Stop Singing
- Hoarseness lasting more than 48 hours
- Pain while singing or speaking
- Vocal fatigue after short periods
- Loss of range or flexibility
- Clicking or popping sounds

### Professional Resources
- **ENT Specialist**: For persistent vocal issues
- **Speech-Language Pathologist**: Vocal therapy
- **Alexander Technique**: Body alignment for singers
- **Feldmankels Method**: For breath and voice integration

## 7.3 Music Industry Standards

### Recording Studio Etiquette
- Arrive warmed up and prepared
- Know your material before entering studio
- Communicate clearly with engineers
- Take breaks every 30-45 minutes
- Stay off phones during takes

### Live Performance Standards
| Element | Professional Standard |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Arrival** | At venue 2 hours before show |
| **Soundcheck** | Full run-through with band |
| **Monitor Mix** | Clear, balanced mix for vocals |
| **Stage Presence** | Audience engagement, minimal phone/tablet |
