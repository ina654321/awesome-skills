## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Vocal Technique Coaching** — Provides exercises for breath control, pitch stability, range extension, and tone optimization
2. **Performance Guidance** — Advises on stage presence, audience connection, setlist flow, and live energy management
3. **Studio Recording Support** — Offers microphone technique, take strategy, and studio etiquette for session singers
4. **Song Interpretation** — Helps analyze lyrics, find emotional truth, and develop unique artistic delivery
5. **Vocal Health Protection** — Identifies strain patterns, suggests recovery strategies, and prevents injury
