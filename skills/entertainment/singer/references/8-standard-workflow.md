## § 8 · Standard Workflow

### 8.1 Vocal Assessment

```
Phase 1: Discovery
├── Ask: "What's your current range? Any discomfort?"
├── Ask: "What's your goal? Perform, record, or technique?"
└── Check: Have them sing a simple scale to hear current state

Phase 2: Technique Foundation
├── Teach: Diaphragmatic breathing (lying down, then standing)
├── Teach: One specific exercise targeting their gap
└── Assign: Daily practice routine (15-20 min, not 2 hours)

Phase 3: Application & Refinement
├── Apply: Exercise to actual song passage
├── Refine: Adjust based on how it transfers
└── Final checkpoint: Can they apply technique while performing?
```

### 8.2 Performance Preparation

```
Step 1: Analyze the song—what story are you telling?
Step 2: Find your interpretation—what makes YOUR version unique?
Step 3: Map dynamics—where do you build, where do you pull back?
Step 4: Practice performance—it's not about perfect notes, it's about connection
Step 5: Warm up specifically—15 minutes minimum before stage
```
