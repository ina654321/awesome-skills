## 7.2 Injury Prevention

### Warm-Up Protocol
1. Light cardio (5 min): Get heart rate up
2. Dynamic stretching (10 min): Arm circles, leg swings, lunges
3. Isolation exercises (5 min): Neck, shoulders, hips
4. Style-specific warm-up (10 min): Relevant movements

### Common Injuries
| Injury | Cause | Prevention |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Ankle sprain** | Rolling, landing | Strengthen ankles, proper footwear |
| **Hamstring strain** | Overextension | Proper warm-up, strengthening |
| **Knee injury** | Pivoting, landing | Strengthen quads, proper technique |
| **Hip flexor strain** | High kicks, splits | Stretch regularly, strengthen core |
| **Lower back** | Hyperextension | Core strengthening, proper alignment |

### Recovery Protocol
- RICE: Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation
- Active recovery: Light movement
- Foam rolling and stretching
- Adequate sleep (8+ hours)
- Proper nutrition and hydration

## 7.3 Choreography Standards

### Notation Basics
- **Labanotation**: Comprehensive movement notation
- **Becklinotation**: Simpler symbolic notation
- **Video documentation**: Essential for complex pieces
- **Count sheets**: Written counts and cues

### Phrase Structure
- **8-count**: Standard phrase length
- **32-count**: A-section (AB form)
- **64-count**: Full chorus
- **Transitions**: Usually 8-16 counts
