## 7.2 Animal Welfare & Care Standards

| Standard | Scope | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------|------------------|
| **FASS Ag Guide** | Farm animal care | Pain management, euthanasia, humane handling |
| **American Humane Certified** | Third-party audit | Space, feed, water, health, environment |
| **Global Animal Partnership** | Step-rated welfare | 5 freedoms + enhanced welfare criteria |
| **USDA Organic** | Pasture access | Ruminants must have access to pasture |
| **Beef Quality Assurance (BQA)** | Beef cattle handling | Stockmanship, injection site guidelines |
| **National Dairy FARM Program** | Dairy animal care | SOPs for every stage of production |
| **Pork Quality Assurance (PQA)** | Swine handling | Herd health, food safety, animal welfare |
| **Lancaster-agree Ag Guide** | Welfare audit | Pre-slaughter, handling, transport |

## 7.3 Environmental & Regulatory Standards

| Requirement | Standard | Compliance |
|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Manure management plan** | State law | Annual nutrient management plan required |
| **CAFO permit** | EPA 40 CFR 122 | Required if >1,000 animal units |
| **Mortality disposal** | State law | Incineration, composting, or rendering |
| **Groundwater protection** | EPA/State | Buffer zones, wellhead protection |
| **Pest management** | EPA FIFRA | Restricted use pesticide applicator license |
| **Recordkeeping** | FDA/State | Animal treatment, feed, withdrawals |
| **Biosecurity** | State/Industry | Visitor log, isolation protocols, sanitation |

## 7.4 Nutritional & Health Metrics

| Species | Water Intake | Dry Matter Intake | Space Requirement |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Beef cattle (1000 lb)** | 8–12 gal/day | 2% BW | 100–150 sq ft/head (lot) |
| **Dairy cow (1500 lb)** | 30–50 gal/day | 3–4% BW | 100–150 sq ft/head |
| **Sow (nursery 30 lb)** | 0.5–1 gal/day | 3–4% BW | 4–5 sq ft/head |
| **Sow (farrowing)** | 5–8 gal/day | 5–6% BW | 8×8 ft farrowing crate |
| **Poultry (broiler)** | 0.1–0.2 gal/bird | 1.5–2% BW | 0.6–1.0 sq ft/bird |
| **Sheep (ewe)** | 2–4 gal/day | 2–3% BW | 12–20 sq ft/ewe |
