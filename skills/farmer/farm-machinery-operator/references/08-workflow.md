# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Pre-Operation Equipment Inspection (Daily)

```
Step 1: Walk-Around Inspection
├── Check all fluid levels: engine oil, coolant, hydraulic fluid, fuel
├── Inspect tires/tracks: inflation/condition, lug nuts tight
├── Check lights and signals: headlights, turn signals, hazard lights
├── Inspect mirrors and visibility: clean, unobstructed
├── Verify ROPS: roll bar locked in place, seatbelt functional
└── Check safety devices: master shield on PTO, guards in place

Step 2: Engine Start Check
├── Depress clutch (transmission) fully before starting
├── Start engine; listen for abnormal noises
├── Check gauges: oil pressure, temperature, battery charge
├── Test brakes: both pedals together and individually
├── Test PTO engagement: engage and disengage smoothly
└── Verify all controls: 3-point hitch, remote hydraulics

Step 3: Field/Operation Start
├── Warm up engine for 5 minutes at partial throttle
├── Operate all hydraulics through full range of motion
├── Test emergency shut-off
└── Verify backup alarm (if equipped) and rear-view camera
```

## 8.2 Harvesting Workflow (Grain Combine)

```
Phase 1: Pre-Harvest Setup
├── Adjust combine settings: concave clearance, rotor speed, sieve settings
├── Install appropriate head: corn head vs. grain platform
├── Check belt tension on all drives: header, separator, cleaning shoe
├── Verify grain tank auger and unload tube operational
├── Calibrate yield monitor with test weight
└── Confirm grain moisture readings: harvest at correct moisture content

Phase 2: Harvest Operations
├── Cut at proper height: 6–12 inches depending on crop
├── Adjust forward speed to maintain recommended RPM: 2,200–2,400 (John Deere)
├── Monitor grain loss: < 1% loss behind combine acceptable
├── Check grain tank level; plan unload before overfilling
├── Communicate with grain cart operator via radio/hand signals
└── Monitor engine temperature; shut down if overheating

Phase 3: End-of-Day Procedures
├── Clean combine: air pressure blow-off, remove debris from radiator
├── Lubricate all grease points per operator manual
├── Check and tighten belts; replace any glazed or cracked belts
├── Inspect cutter bar: knife sections, guards, wear
├── Document hours, fuel used, yield for record-keeping
└── Park on level ground; lower header to ground or remove and store
```

## 8.3 Tractor Safety & Field Navigation

1. **Slope operation:** Never operate on slopes > 15° without evaluating stability; use 4WD; slow speed
2. **Passenger policy:** One person only — no extra riders on fenders or toolbox
3. **Hitching equipment:** Use the drawbar pin with proper rating; never hitch to axle or top link
4. **PTO operation:** Keep master shield in place; disengage PTO before dismounting
5. **Transport:** Reduce speed on roads; use SMV sign; lock brake pedals together; ensure lights working
6. **Emergency stop:** Know location of emergency shut-off, fire extinguisher, first aid kit
