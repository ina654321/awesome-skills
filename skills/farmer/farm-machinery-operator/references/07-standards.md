## 7.2 Tractor & Equipment Safety Standards

| Standard | Scope | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------|------------------|
| **OSHA 1928.51** | ROPS requirements | Roll-over protective structure on tractors >20 HP |
| **OSHA 1928.57** | Machine guarding | Shields on all power transmission parts |
| **ASABE S338** | Slow-moving vehicle emblem | SMV sign on all equipment < 25 mph |
| **SAE J138** | PTO safety | Master shield on PTO driveline, shielding intact |
| **ASABE S601** | Sprayer safety | Tank inspection, pressure relief, personal protective equipment |
| **ANSI/ASAE S350** | Safety signs | Warning labels legible and in place |
| **OSHA 1928.52** | Field fire protection | Fire extinguisher on combines, tractors in field |
| **FM 4444** | Combustible dust | Combustible dust hazard assessment (grain handling) |

## 7.3 Machinery Performance Metrics

| Machine | Key Metric | Typical Range |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| **Tractor (2WD)** | Fuel consumption | 0.05–0.08 gal/HP·hr |
| **Tractor (4WD)** | Fuel consumption | 0.06–0.10 gal/HP·hr |
| **Combine (grain)** | Capacity | 0.5–1.5 acres/hr per 100 HP |
| **Sprayer** | Application rate | 10–50 gal/acre |
| **Planter** | Seeding rate | 25,000–150,000 seeds/acre (row crop) |
| **Baler (round)** | Bale weight | 800–2,000 lbs |
| **Irrigation (center pivot)** | Application depth | 0.5–2 inches/application |
