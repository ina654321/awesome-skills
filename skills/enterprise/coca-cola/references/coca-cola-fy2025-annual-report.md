# Coca-Cola FY2025 Annual Report Summary

## Executive Summary

The Coca-Cola Company delivered solid results in FY2025, demonstrating resilience in a complex global environment. Net revenues grew 2% to $47.9 billion, with organic revenues (non-GAAP) growing 5%.

## Financial Highlights

### Revenue Performance

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Net Revenues | $47.9B | $46.9B | +2% |
| Organic Revenues (Non-GAAP) | - | - | +5% |
| Unit Case Volume | Even | Base | Flat |
| Price/Mix | - | - | +4% |

### Earnings Performance

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Operating Income | $13.8B | $10.0B | +38% |
| Comparable Operating Margin | 31.2% | 30.0% | +120bps |
| EPS (Reported) | $3.04 | $2.47 | +23% |
| Comparable EPS (Non-GAAP) | $3.00 | $2.88 | +4% |

### Cash Flow

| Metric | FY2025 |
|--------|--------|
| Cash Flow from Operations | $7.4B |
| Free Cash Flow (Non-GAAP) | $5.3B |
| Free Cash Flow (excl. one-time) | $11.4B |

## Operating Segments

### Regional Performance

| Region | Performance |
|--------|-------------|
| Europe, Middle East & Africa | Operating Income +1% |
| Latin America | Operating Income -1% (strong organic growth, currency headwinds) |
| North America | Operating Income +11% |
| Asia Pacific | Operating Income -5% |
| Bottling Investments | Operating Income -14% |

### Key Market Insights

- **Brazil**: Volume growth leader in Q4
- **United States**: Volume growth supported by strong execution
- **Japan**: Volume growth in Asia Pacific
- **Mexico**: Full-year volume decline
- **Thailand**: Volume decline

## Strategic Initiatives

### Portfolio Transformation

- Continued shift toward zero-sugar offerings
- Costa Coffee integration progressing
- BODYARMOR scaling (with $960M impairment charge in Q4)
- Premium water growth (smartwater, Topo Chico)

### Marketing & Innovation

- Investment in marketing increased to drive brand health
- Freestyle platform expansion continuing
- Digital engagement and personalization

## CEO Statement

> "I'm encouraged by our performance in 2025 which showed both the resilience and momentum that define our business. Looking ahead, we will focus on executing our strategy even better and positioning our system for long-term success."
> — James Quincey, Chairman and CEO

## 2026 Financial Outlook

| Metric | Guidance |
|--------|----------|

## Key Risks & Challenges

1. **Currency Headwinds**: 5-9 point impact on various metrics
2. **Input Costs**: Higher marketing expenses and input costs
3. **BODYARMOR Impairment**: $960M non-cash charge related to trademark
4. **Market Share**: Competition in all beverage categories
