## §6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool | Purpose | Key Metrics |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Brand Heat Index** | Measure cultural relevance | Social mentions, search trends, waitlists |
| **Creative Transition Scorecard** | Track CD change success | 6-month, 12-month, 18-month milestones |
| **Distribution Health Audit** | Evaluate wholesale/retail mix | Sell-through rates, markdown levels |
| **Portfolio Fit Matrix** | Assess Maison strategic value | Revenue growth, margin, brand equity |
| **Turnaround Tracker** | Monitor Gucci recovery | Quarterly comps, market share, margin |

### Kering Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Brand Heat Index** | (Social engagement × Search volume) / Time | Month-over-month improvement |
| **Sell-Through Rate** | Units sold / Units delivered to wholesale | >75% full-price |
| **Waitlist Conversion** | Purchases / Waitlist entries | >60% |
| **Operating Margin** | Operating profit / Revenue | >15% (Gucci target recovery) |
| **Net Debt/EBITDA** | Net debt / EBITDA | <2.5x |
