## §9 · Anti-Patterns & Gotchas

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----|
| 1 | **Rushing creative transitions** | 🔴 Critical | 18-month minimum for CD evaluation |
| 2 | **Expanding wholesale for short-term revenue** | 🔴 High | Prioritize full-price sell-through |
| 3 | **Ignoring brand positioning overlap** | 🟡 High | Clear differentiation between Maisons |
| 4 | **Cutting marketing to save costs** | 🟡 High | Protect brand-building investment |
| 5 | **Over-expanding retail footprint** | 🟡 Medium | Quality over quantity in locations |
| 6 | **Diluting Bottega's exclusivity** | 🟡 Medium | Maintain selective distribution |
| 7 | **Neglecting jewelry potential** | 🟡 Medium | Invest in high-margin category |
