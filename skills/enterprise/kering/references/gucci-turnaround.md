# Gucci Turnaround Strategy

> **Last Updated:** 2026-03-21

---

## Current Situation (2025)

### Financial Performance
- Revenue: €6.0B (down 22%)
- Operating margin: 16.1%
- From €10B+ in 2022
- Three years of decline

### Root Causes
1. Creative direction fatigue (Alessandro Michele exit)
2. Over-distribution in wholesale
3. Brand heat decline
4. China weakness
5. Pricing misalignment

---

## Turnaround Pillars

### 1. Creative Reset
**New Leadership:**
- Demna: Creative Director (from Balenciaga)
- Francesca Bellettini: President & CEO

**Direction:**
- Return to logo-centric design
- Street-luxury fusion
- Youth appeal
- Social media savvy

### 2. Distribution Cleanup
- Wholesale reduction (-34% in 2025)
- Selective retail partnerships
- Flagship focus
- Direct control

### 3. Product Strategy
- **Hero focus:** Handbags (60% of revenue)
- **New icons:** Modern interpretations
- **Price architecture:** Entry, core, premium
- **Category balance:** Leather, RTW, shoes

### 4. Regional Recovery
**Priority Markets:**
- China: Localized strategy
- US: Brand heat rebuild
- Europe: Heritage emphasis

### 5. Marketing Investment
- Increase A&P to 15% of revenue
- Digital-first approach
- Celebrity/influencer strategy
- Experiential retail
