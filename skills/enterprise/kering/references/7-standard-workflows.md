## §7 · Standard Workflows

### §7.1 · Creative Director Transition Management

```
Month 0-3: Announcement & Assessment
├── Communicate vision to internal teams
├── Assess atelier capabilities and needs
├── Review archive for inspiration
├── Meet key wholesale partners
└── ✓ Output: 100-day plan

Month 3-9: First Collection Development
├── Design development with creative freedom
├── Limited preview to top clients
├── Production capacity planning
├── Marketing narrative development
└── ✓ Output: Debut collection ready

Month 9-12: Launch & Evaluation
├── Runway show execution
├── Buyer feedback collection
├── Pre-order analysis
├── Press and influencer response
└── ✓ Output: Launch performance report

Year 2: Commercial Scaling
├── Full collection rollout
├── Retail expansion plan
├── Wholesale re-engagement
├── Accessory category focus
└── ✓ Output: Year 2 strategy
```

### §7.2 · Gucci Turnaround Playbook

```
Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)

├── Atelier capability assessment
└── ✓ Output: Operational baseline established

Phase 2: Creative Reset (Months 6-12)

├── Archive-inspired modern designs

├── Limited edition drops for buzz
└── ✓ Output: Market response to new creative

Phase 3: Distribution Optimization (Months 12-18)
├── Selective wholesale partnerships
├── Flagship store renovations
├── Digital experience upgrade
├── VIP clienteling program
└── ✓ Output: Improved sell-through rates

Phase 4: Growth Acceleration (Months 18-24)
├── Full collection breadth
├── Geographic expansion
├── Accessory category growth
├── Marketing investment increase
└── ✓ Output: Return to growth trajectory
```
