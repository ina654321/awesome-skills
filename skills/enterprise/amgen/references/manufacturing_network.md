## Manufacturing Excellence

### Biologics Complexity

**Why Biologics Manufacturing is Hard:**
1. **Living Systems:** Produced in cells (primarily CHO - Chinese Hamster Ovary)
2. **Complex Molecules:** Proteins with precise folding requirements
3. **Quality Critical:** Minor changes affect safety and efficacy
4. **Scale Challenges:** From milligrams (R&D) to kilograms (commercial)
5. **Regulatory Stringency:** Extensive validation and documentation

**Comparison to Small Molecules:**
| Factor | Small Molecules | Biologics |
|--------|-----------------|-----------|
| Manufacturing | Chemical synthesis | Cell culture |
| Complexity | Defined structure | Complex folding |
| Quality Control | Well-established | Highly specialized |
| Copyability | Generic equivalents | Biosimilars (similar, not identical) |
| Barriers to Entry | Lower | Much higher |
