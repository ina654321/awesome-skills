- Cloud and security services
- Professional services

## Network Infrastructure

### Wireless Network
- **4G LTE**: Covers 99% of US population
- **5G Nationwide**: Low-band coverage nationwide
- **5G Ultra Wideband**: C-band (n77) + mmWave (n260/n261)
- **Spectrum Holdings**: C-band (161 MHz average nationwide), mmWave, CBRS, 600 MHz

### Wireline Network
- **Fios Fiber**: ~17M+ homes passed (legacy), ~30M with Frontier acquisition
- **Copper**: Legacy DSL (being phased out)
- **Backbone**: 850,000+ route miles of fiber
