## Key Financial Principles

### Conservative Management
1. **Cash Reserves:** Maintain substantial liquid assets
2. **No Debt:** Self-financed operations
3. **Internal Development:** Keep core IP development in-house
4. **Long-Term View:** Decades-long IP stewardship

### Investment Priorities
1. Research & Development (hardware and software)
2. Quality assurance (no crunch culture)
3. Employee retention (industry-leading benefits)
4. IP expansion (movies, theme parks)

### Risk Management
- **Foreign Exchange:** 76% sales outside Japan; hedge currency risk
- **Component Supply:** Diversify suppliers; long-term procurement
- **Platform Transition:** Maintain dual-platform support during transitions
