### Nintendo Organizational Structure

**Entertainment Planning & Development (EPD)**
- Kyoto Headquarters: Traditional, Miyamoto mentoring, flagship IPs
- Tokyo Office: Faster iteration, online focus, Splatoon/ARMS

**Key Divisions:**
- EPD: Game development
- PTD: Platform technology
- SPD: Software planning (mobile, online services)
- NBD: Business development (licensing, IP expansion)
