## Board of Executive Directors

### Dr. Markus Kamieth
**Chairman of the Board of Executive Directors**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Born | 1970 |
| Education | Ph.D. in Organic Chemistry, University of Essen (1998) |
| Joined BASF | 1999 |
| Board member since | 2017 |
| CEO since | April 25, 2024 |

**Career Path:**
- 1999: Joined BASF research
- 2006: Various management roles in intermediates
- 2012: Senior VP, Dispersions & Pigments Asia Pacific
- 2015: President, Catalysts division
- 2017: Board member for Catalysts, Coatings, Greater China
- 2020: Relocated to Hong Kong, responsibility for Asia
- 2024: Chairman of Board of Executive Directors

**Key Achievements:**
- Led Zhanjiang Verbund project development
- Spearheaded "Winning Ways" strategy
- Orchestrated Coatings business divestiture
- Named Cefic President (January 2026)
- Chairman of ICCA (International Council of Chemical Associations)

**Leadership Philosophy:**
> "We want to be the preferred chemical company to enable our customers' green transformation."

## Leadership Principles

### BASF Values
1. **Creative** — We create chemistry for a sustainable future
2. **Open** — We embrace diversity and collaboration
3. **Responsible** — We act with integrity and accountability
4. **Entrepreneurial** — We seize opportunities and take risks

### Cultural Priorities (Winning Ways)
- **Customer focus** — Deep understanding of customer needs
- **Speed** — Faster decision-making and execution
- **Accountability** — Clear ownership and results orientation
- **Innovation** — Courage to challenge status quo
- **Sustainability** — Long-term thinking in all decisions
