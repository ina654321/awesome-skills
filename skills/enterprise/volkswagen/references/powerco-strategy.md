# PowerCo Battery Strategy

**Company:** PowerCo SE (Volkswagen Group Battery Company)  
**Founded:** 2022  
**Headquarters:** Salzgitter, Germany  
**CEO:** Frank Blome

---

## Mission & Vision

**Mission:** Secure Volkswagen Group's battery supply through in-house production, vertical integration, and sustainable manufacturing.

**Vision:** Become a leading global battery producer with standardized, cost-competitive, and sustainable cells.

---

## Unified Cell Concept

### Design Philosophy
- **Standardization:** Single prismatic cell format for all applications
- **Scalability:** Adaptable capacity through module/pack configuration
- **Cost Leadership:** Target 50% cost reduction by 2030
- **Sustainability:** Full lifecycle carbon footprint optimization

### Cell Specifications
| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Format | Prismatic |
| Dimensions | Unified standard (TBD) |
| Chemistry variants | LFP, NMC 622, NMC 811, Silicon-anode |
| Energy density | 250-300 Wh/kg (cell level) |
| Production | Dry electrode coating |

### Chemistry Roadmap
| Generation | Chemistry | Energy Density | Application |
|------------|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| Gen 1 (2023) | NMC 811 | 250 Wh/kg | Premium, performance |
| Gen 2 (2025) | Silicon-anode | 280 Wh/kg | Extended range |
| Gen 3 (2027) | Solid-state | 400+ Wh/kg | Next-gen platforms |
| LFP Variant | Lithium Iron Phosphate | 160 Wh/kg | Entry-level, stationary |

## Recycling Strategy

### Closed-Loop Target
- **Goal:** 95% material recovery by 2030
- **Approach:** Battery passport, traceability
- **Partnership:** Blackstone Technology, Redwood Materials

### Recycling Process
1. **Collection:** Dealer network, end-of-life
2. **Disassembly:** Automated cell extraction
3. **Processing:** Hydrometallurgical recovery
4. **Refining:** Battery-grade material output
5. **Reuse:** Direct integration into production

### Pilot Facility
- **Location:** Salzgitter
- **Capacity:** 1,500 tons/year initial
- **Expansion:** Scaling to 10,000+ tons
