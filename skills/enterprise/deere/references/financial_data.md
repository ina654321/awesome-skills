# John Deere Financial Data Reference

## Annual Financial Summary (FY2025)

### Consolidated Results

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Total Net Sales & Revenues** | $45.684B | $51.716B | -11.7% |
| **Equipment Operations Net Sales** | $38.917B | $44.759B | -13.1% |
| **Net Income** | $5.027B | $7.100B | -29.2% |
| **Diluted EPS** | $18.50 | $25.62 | -27.8% |
| **Operating Margin** | 15.6% | 19.8% | -420 bps |

### Segment Performance

#### Production & Precision Agriculture (PPA)
| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Net Sales | $26.450B | $30.186B | -12.4% |
| Operating Profit | $5.482B | $7.089B | -22.7% |
| Operating Margin | 20.7% | 23.5% | -280 bps |

**Key Drivers:**
- Sales volumes decreased 30% in U.S./Canada
- Brazil sales increased 22% (strong grower production)
- Price realization: +1% U.S./Canada, +4% Brazil

#### Small Agriculture & Turf (SAT)
| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Net Sales | $10.224B | $10.969B | -6.8% |
| Operating Profit | $1.207B | $1.627B | -25.8% |
| Operating Margin | 11.8% | 14.8% | -300 bps |

**Key Drivers:**
- Sales volumes decreased 17% in U.S./Canada
- India sales increased 26%
- Europe sales increased 5%

#### Construction & Forestry (CF)
| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Net Sales | $9.875B | $12.076B | -18.2% |
| Operating Profit | $1.142B | $1.795B | -36.4% |
| Operating Margin | 11.6% | 14.9% | -330 bps |

**Key Drivers:**
- Sales volumes decreased 15% in U.S./Canada
- Rental purchase decreases, lower construction activity
- Trade uncertainty impact

#### Financial Services (FS)
| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Revenue | $6.289B | $6.493B | -3.1% |
| Net Income | $890M | $696M | +27.9% |

**Key Drivers:**
- Net income increase due to special items
- Lower average portfolio balance (BJD deconsolidation)
- Favorable financing spreads
