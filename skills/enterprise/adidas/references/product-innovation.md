# Adidas Product Innovation Framework

## Innovation Architecture

### Core Technology Platforms

#### 1. Boost Technology
**Overview**: Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) capsules fused together for energy return

| Specification | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Energy Return** | 70%+ (industry-leading) |
| **Durability** | 1000+ km lifespan |
| **Temperature Stability** | -20°C to +40°C |
| **Key Products** | Ultraboost, NMD, Yeezy |

**Applications:**
- Running: Ultraboost Light (30% lighter)
- Basketball: Harden Vol. 7
- Lifestyle: NMD, Yeezy Boost

#### 2. Lightstrike
**Overview**: Lightweight, responsive foam for speed and agility

| Specification | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Weight** | 30% lighter than Boost |
| **Responsiveness** | High energy return, firm feel |
| **Key Products** | Adizero Adios Pro, Dame 8 |

**Applications:**
- Running: Adizero racing line
- Basketball: Dame signature line
- Training: Speed-focused workouts

#### 3. Primeknit
**Overview**: Seamless, sock-like upper construction

| Specification | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Material** | Digital knitting technology |
| **Benefits** | Zero waste, precise fit |
| **Variants** | Primeknit+, Primeblue |

**Sustainability Integration:**
- Primeblue: 75% Parley Ocean Plastic
- Zero waste manufacturing
- Recycled content variants

#### 4. 4D Technology
**Overview**: 3D-printed midsole using Digital Light Synthesis

| Specification | Detail |
|---------------|--------|
| **Manufacturing** | Digital Light Synthesis |
| **Customization** | Lattice structure tuning |
| **Key Products** | 4D FWD, 4D Run |

**Strategic Position:**
- Premium positioning (€200+ price point)
- Limited production runs
- Innovation showcase

- **Goal**: 100M pairs/year by 2030

- Reduce overproduction waste
- Customer-specific fit options

## Product Development Process

### Stage-Gate Framework

```
Stage 1: Insight & Ideation (8 weeks)
├── Consumer research
├── Trend analysis
└── Technology scouting

Stage 2: Concept Development (12 weeks)
├── Design concepts
├── Prototype development
└── Initial costing

Stage 3: Technical Development (16 weeks)
├── Engineering
├── Material sourcing
└── Wear testing

Stage 4: Commercialization (20 weeks)
├── Production ramp
├── Marketing development
└── Retail preparation

Stage 5: Launch & Scale (Ongoing)
├── Market launch
├── Performance monitoring
└── Iteration
```
