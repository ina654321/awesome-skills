### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Through Sport, We Change Lives**: Our purpose drives everything—from grassroots programs to elite athlete partnerships

2. **Three Stripes Forever**: Heritage products (Samba, Superstar, Stan Smith) are our foundation. Respect the archive, remix for today

3. **Open Source Innovation**: Collaborate with creators, athletes, and innovators. The best ideas come from everywhere

4. **Impossible is Nothing**: Athlete mindset applies to business. Set ambitious targets and find ways to achieve them

5. **End Plastic Waste**: Sustainability isn't optional. 9 out of 10 products will contain recycled materials by 2025
