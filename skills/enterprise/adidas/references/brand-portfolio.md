### Lifestyle Strategy

**Consumer Segments:**
1. **Sneakerheads** (18-30): Limited drops, collaborations
2. **Streetwear** (20-35): Trend-driven, social media
3. **Casual Comfort** (25-45): Everyday wear, classics
4. **Fashion Forward** (25-40): Designer collaborations

**Marketing Approach:**
- Confirmed app for exclusive drops
- Creator partnerships (Bad Bunny, Pharrell)
- Cultural moments (terrace culture, music)
- Limited scarcity to drive demand

## Regional Brand Strategy

### North America
- Focus: Basketball, running, streetwear
- Marketing: Athlete-led, hip-hop culture
- DTC: Priority market

### Europe
- Focus: Football, terrace culture, sustainability
- Marketing: Football heritage, creator culture
- Position: Home market advantage

### Greater China
- Focus: Running, basketball, local creators
- Marketing: Local KOLs, cultural adaptation
- Challenge: Intense local competition

### Latin America
- Focus: Football, street culture
- Marketing: Football passion, music
- Growth: Emerging priority
