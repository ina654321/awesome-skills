## 2025 Outlook

### Guidance

| Metric | Range | Commentary |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Revenue Growth** | High single-digit | Continued brand momentum |
| **Operating Profit** | €1.7-1.8B | Margin expansion continues |
| **Gross Margin** | 51-52% | DTC mix improvement |
| **Marketing Spend** | 11-12% of sales | Brand investment priority |

### Strategic Priorities (2025)

1. **Brand Heat**: Samba, Gazelle, Spezial momentum
2. **DTC Growth**: App ecosystem, membership program
3. **Sustainability**: 9/10 products with recycled materials
4. **Local for Local**: Regional product development
5. **Innovation**: Lightstrike, 4D, bio-based materials
