### Marketing Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use specific, measurable claims
- Reference third-party verification
- Show progress over time
- Acknowledge challenges

**Don't:**
- Make vague "green" claims
- Exaggerate environmental benefits
- Use misleading imagery
- Ignore supply chain impacts
