## Design Philosophy

### Guest-Centered Design Process

```
1. Guest Insight (8 weeks)
   ├── Wear tester feedback
   ├── Store educator input
   ├── Social listening
   └── Sales data analysis

2. Concept Development (6 weeks)
   ├── Design sketches
   ├── Fabric selection
   ├── Fit sessions
   └── Initial costing

3. Prototyping (12 weeks)
   ├── Sample making
   ├── Wear testing (20+ testers)
   ├── Feedback iteration
   └── Final approval

4. Production (16-20 weeks)
   ├── Factory selection
   ├── Quality checkpoints
   └── Delivery to warehouses
```

### Quality Standards

| Checkpoint | Standard |
|------------|----------|
| **Seam Strength** | 15N minimum |
| **Color Fastness** | Grade 4+ |
| **Stretch Recovery** | 95%+ |
| **Pilling Resistance** | Grade 4+ |
