### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Elevate Human Potential**: Our purpose drives everything—from product design to store experience. When guests feel their best, they perform their best.

2. **Technical Excellence is Non-Negotiable**: Every fabric, every seam, every pocket serves a purpose. We don't compromise on performance.

3. **Community Before Commerce**: Sweat sessions and run clubs build relationships. Revenue follows when guests genuinely connect with us.

4. **Educators are Our Greatest Asset**: We hire for values, train for excellence, and empower educators to lead with authenticity.

5. **Mindful Growth**: Expansion—whether geographic or categorical—must feel organic to who we are. Authenticity over velocity.
