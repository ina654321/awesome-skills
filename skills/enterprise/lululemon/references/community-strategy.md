# Lululemon Community Strategy

## Philosophy: Store as Community Hub

### Core Belief
"Our stores are not places to sell things. They are places to connect, sweat, and grow. Revenue is the result of authentic community building, not the goal."

## Community Pillars

### 1. Store Ambassador Program

#### Ambassador Profile
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Role** | Local fitness/wellness leaders |
| **Count** | 8-12 per store |
| **Compensation** | Free product, event collaboration, exposure |
| **Commitment** | 1-year partnership, renewable |

#### Selection Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Assessment |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Community Following** | 30% | Social proof, class attendance |
| **Values Alignment** | 25% | Mindfulness, inclusivity, growth |
| **Teaching Excellence** | 25% | Skill, safety, engagement |
| **Authentic Connection** | 20% | Genuine passion, not transactional |

#### Ambassador Benefits
- $1,000+ annual product allowance
- Exclusive preview access
- Social media features
- Event collaboration opportunities
- Professional development

### 2. Sweat Collective

#### Program Overview
Community of fitness professionals who receive:
- 25% discount on purchases
- Early access to product
- Exclusive events
- Feedback opportunities

#### Membership Requirements
- Active fitness professional certification
- Teaching minimum 5 classes/week
- Values alignment with Lululemon

#### Program Scale
- Global members: 50,000+
- Annual spend: $200M+
- Ambassador pipeline: Primary source

## Local Market Activation

### New Store Opening Playbook

#### Pre-Opening (12 weeks)
1. **Ambassador Recruitment** (Week 1-4)
   - Local fitness leader identification
   - Interview and selection
   - Partnership agreement

2. **Community Seeding** (Week 5-8)
   - Pop-up sweat sessions
   - Studio partnerships
   - Social presence building

3. **Hype Building** (Week 9-12)
   - Exclusive preview events
   - Influencer seeding
   - PR and media

#### Opening Week
- Daily complimentary classes
- Ambassador takeovers
- Community meet-and-greets
- Limited product drops

#### First 90 Days
- Weekly programming establishment
- Feedback collection and iteration
- Ambassador relationship deepening
- Local partnership development
