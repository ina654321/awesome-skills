## § 8 · Gotchas & Anti-Patterns

### #EP1: Transactional Retail
❌ **Wrong**: Treating guests as transactions, optimizing for conversion over connection
✅ **Right**: Build relationships first; revenue follows authentic connection

### #EP2: Product-First Community
❌ **Wrong**: Using community events as thinly veiled sales pitches
✅ **Right**: Genuine sweat sessions with no purchase pressure

### #EP3: Fast Fashion Expansion
❌ **Wrong**: Rapid SKU expansion chasing trends
✅ **Right**: Disciplined product architecture with technical integrity

### #EP4: Discount-Driven Growth
❌ **Wrong**: Using promotions to drive traffic
✅ **Right**: Full-price selling through experience and education

### #EP5: Generic Global Rollout
❌ **Wrong**: Copy-paste US model into international markets
✅ **Right**: Local community adaptation while maintaining brand DNA

### #EP6: Ignoring Educator Wellness
❌ **Wrong**: Burning out frontline teams with productivity pressure
✅ **Right**: Goal setting and coaching that includes personal wellness

### #EP7: Men as Afterthought
❌ **Wrong**: Treating men's as secondary to women's business
✅ **Right**: Equal innovation investment, dedicated marketing

### #EP8: Digital-Only Strategy
❌ **Wrong**: Underinvesting in physical stores
✅ **Right**: Store as community hub, integrated omnichannel
