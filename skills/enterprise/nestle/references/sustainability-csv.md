## Regenerative Agriculture

### Definition (Nestlé)

> "An outcome-based farming approach that protects and improves soil health, biodiversity, climate, and water resources while enhancing farming productivity and profitability."

### Goals

| Milestone | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| **2025** | 20% of key ingredients from regenerative agriculture |
| **2030** | 50% of key ingredients from regenerative agriculture |

### Key Ingredients in Scope

| Category | Key Crops | Practices |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Coffee** | Arabica, Robusta | Shade trees, intercropping |
| **Cocoa** | Cocoa beans | Agroforestry, biodiversity |
| **Dairy** | Milk | Sustainable feed, manure management |
| **Cereals** | Wheat, corn | Cover crops, reduced tillage |
| **Vegetables** | Onions, tomatoes | Crop rotation, organic fertilizers |

### Regenerative Practices

| Practice | Description | Benefits |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Cover Cropping** | Planting between main crops | Soil health, carbon sequestration |
| **Reduced Tillage** | Minimal soil disturbance | Water retention, soil structure |
| **Agroforestry** | Trees integrated with crops | Shade, biodiversity, carbon |
| **Organic Fertilizers** | Natural nutrient sources | Soil biology, reduced synthetic input |
| **Sustainable Feed** | Alternative protein sources | Lower methane, deforestation-free |

### Farmer Support Programs

| Program | Description | Scale |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Nescafé Plan** | Coffee farmer support | Millions of farmers |
| **Nestlé Cocoa Plan** | Cocoa sustainability | 140K+ farmers |
| **Nespresso AAA** | Premium coffee sourcing | 110K+ farmers |
| **Dairy Net Zero** | US dairy farms | Pilot expansion |
