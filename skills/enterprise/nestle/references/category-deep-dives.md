# Category Deep Dives

## 1. COFFEE

### Overview
Coffee is Nestlé's largest and most profitable category, generating ~25% of total sales.

**2024 Performance:**
- Organic Growth: Mid single-digit
- Key drivers: Nescafé, Nespresso, Starbucks
- UTOP Margin: Among highest in portfolio

### Brand Portfolio

| Brand | Positioning | Key Markets | Formats |
|-------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| **Nescafé** | World's favorite coffee | Global | Soluble, RTD, Dolce Gusto |
| **Nespresso** | Premium coffee experience | Premium markets | Original, Vertuo, Professional |
| **Starbucks** | Premium retail coffee | Global (licensed) | CPG retail products |

### Nescafé Sub-Brands

| Sub-Brand | Target | Positioning | Caffeine Content |
|-----------|--------|-------------|------------------|
| Nescafé Classic | Mass market | Everyday coffee | 50-90mg/serving |
| Nescafé Gold | Premium mass | Richer taste | 90mg/serving |
| Nescafé Azera | Premium instant | Barista-style | Varies |
| Nescafé 3-in-1 | Convenience | All-in-one | 45-65mg/serving |
| Nescafé Dolce Gusto | System users | Multi-beverage | Varies by capsule |

### Nespresso Systems

**Original Line:**
- 19-bar pressure espresso
- 25+ capsule varieties
- Compact machines
- Premium pricing ($0.60-0.80/capsule)

**Vertuo:**
- Centrifusion technology
- Multiple cup sizes (espresso to mug)
- US market focus
- Rapid growth driver

**Professional:**
- Momento system (office/hospitality)
- Aguila (high-volume café)
- Zenius/Gemini (small business)

### Coffee Sustainability: Nescafé Plan

| Commitment | Target | Progress |
|------------|--------|----------|
| Responsibly sourced coffee | 100% by 2025 | On track |
| Farmer support programs | Millions reached | Ongoing |
| Regenerative agriculture | Scaling | 20% by 2025 |
| Deforestation-free | 100% | 95%+ achieved |

## 2. PETCARE (PURINA)

### Overview
PetCare is Nestlé's second-largest category, with strong growth and margins.

**2024 Performance:**
- Organic Growth: Low single-digit
- Revenue: ~$18B
- Key brands: Purina ProPlan, Purina ONE, Friskies, Fancy Feast

### Brand Architecture

| Tier | Brands | Price Position | Key Attributes |
|------|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **Ultra-Premium** | ProPlan Veterinary Diets, LiveClear | $15+/kg | Veterinary channel, science-backed |
| **Premium** | ProPlan, Purina ONE, Beneful | $10-15/kg | Pet specialty, natural ingredients |
| **Mass Premium** | Friskies, Fancy Feast | $6-10/kg | Grocery, taste preference |
| **Economy** | Alpo, Dog Chow, Cat Chow | <$6/kg | Mass market, value |

### Science-Based Nutrition

| Innovation Platform | Description |
|---------------------|-------------|
| **LiveClear** | Allergen-reducing cat food |
| **ProPlan Veterinary Diets** | Prescription nutrition |
| **Bionic Bead** | Dental health technology |
| **OptiStart** | Puppy/kitten development |

### Key Markets

| Market | Position | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| USA | #1 or #2 in most segments | Market leader |
| Brazil | Leading | Emerging market growth |
| Europe | Strong | Premiumization focus |
| China | Growing | Rapid expansion |

### PetCare Sustainability

- Packaging: 100% recyclable/reusable target by 2025
- Sourcing: Responsibly sourced proteins
- Manufacturing: Renewable energy transition

## 3. INFANT NUTRITION

### Overview
Critical category with high regulation and consumer trust requirements.

**2024 Performance:**
- Organic Growth: Low single-digit
- Key markets: China, emerging markets
- Innovation: HMOs (human milk oligosaccharides)

### Brand Portfolio

| Brand | Positioning | Key Markets |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **NAN** | Premium infant formula | Global |
| **Gerber** | Baby food & formula | Americas |
| **Lactogen** | Affordable nutrition | Emerging markets |
| **S-26** | Premium formula | Asia, LatAm |
| **Wyeth** | Premium formula | China |

### Key Innovations

| Innovation | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **HMOs** | 5 HMOs in premium formulas |
| **NAN with Sinergity** | 5 HMOs + probiotics |
| **Organic lines** | Growing segment |
| **Hypoallergenic** | Specialized formulas |

### Regulatory Environment

- WHO Code compliance required globally
- China: Strict registration requirements
- EU: Recent DHA/ARA regulation changes
- Marketing restrictions in most markets
