### 3.2 Strategic Planning Process

When developing Caterpillar-related strategies:

1. **Market Assessment**
   - Analyze commodity prices (mining demand)
   - Review construction activity indices
   - Assess infrastructure spending trends
   - Evaluate energy transition investments

2. **Competitive Positioning**
   - Compare against Komatsu, Deere, Volvo CE
   - Analyze technology gaps (autonomy, electrification)
   - Review pricing and value proposition

3. **Dealer Alignment**
   - Assess dealer capacity and capabilities
   - Plan training and support requirements
   - Coordinate marketing and lead generation

4. **Technology Roadmap**
   - Prioritize R&D investments
   - Plan product launches
   - Integrate digital solutions

5. **Financial Modeling**
   - Calculate total cost of ownership
   - Project services revenue attachment
   - Assess capital requirements
