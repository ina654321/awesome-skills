## Segment Financials (2024)

### Construction Industries
- **Sales**: ~$26.1B
- **Primary Products**: Excavators, dozers, loaders, motor graders
- **Geographic Mix**: North America (strong), Europe (weak), Asia Pacific (soft China)
- **Key Trends**: Dealer inventory reduction, price realization favorable

### Resource Industries
- **Sales**: ~$12.2B
- **Primary Products**: Mining trucks, hydraulic shovels, underground equipment
- **Market Conditions**: Mining capital discipline, high fleet utilization
- **Autonomy Growth**: 690+ autonomous trucks operational

### Energy & Transportation
- **Sales**: ~$22.9B
- **Primary Products**: Reciprocating engines, turbines, locomotives
- **Growth Drivers**: Data center power demand, LNG infrastructure
- **Backlog**: Elevated at $30B (increased $2.5B YoY)

### Financial Products
- **Revenues**: $3.4B
- **Segment Profit**: $766M
- **Past Dues**: 1.56% (improved from 1.79%)
- **Write-offs**: $115M (vs. $65M prior year)

## Key Financial Risks

1. **Commodity Price Volatility**: Mining capex tied to commodity cycles
2. **Construction Cyclicality**: Exposure to infrastructure spending
3. **Currency Fluctuations**: 50%+ revenue outside North America
4. **Interest Rate Sensitivity**: Financial products segment
5. **Geopolitical Risk**: Operations in 192 countries

## Investment Highlights

- **Dividend Aristocrat**: 55 consecutive years of dividend payments
- **Services Moat**: High-margin recurring revenue growing to 50% target
- **Technology Leadership**: Autonomy, electrification, digital solutions
- **Dealer Network**: Unmatched global distribution and service capability
- **Brand Equity**: "Cat Yellow" synonymous with quality and reliability
