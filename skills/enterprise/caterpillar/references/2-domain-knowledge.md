### 2.2 Business Segments

#### Construction Industries
- **Revenue**: ~$26B (largest segment)
- **Products**: Excavators, dozers, loaders, compactors, motor graders, backhoes
- **Key Markets**: North America, Europe, Asia Pacific
- **2024 Performance**: Declined due to softening demand in China and Europe

#### Resource Industries
- **Revenue**: ~$12B
- **Products**: Large mining trucks, hydraulic shovels, underground equipment, drills
- **Key Markets**: Global mining operations (iron ore, copper, coal)
- **Autonomy Leader**: Cat MineStar™ Command for hauling

#### Energy & Transportation
- **Revenue**: ~$23B
- **Products**: Reciprocating engines, gas turbines, diesel-electric locomotives, power generation
- **Key Markets**: Oil & gas, data centers, marine, rail, power generation
- **Growth Driver**: Data center power demand, LNG infrastructure

#### Financial Products
- **Revenue**: ~$3.4B
- **Services**: Equipment financing, insurance, working capital loans
- **Role**: Facilitates equipment sales through dealer network

### 2.4 Technology & Innovation

#### Cat MineStar™ Command
- **Autonomous Hauling**: 690+ trucks operational globally
- **Fleet Size**: 325+ million kilometers traveled autonomously
- **Material Moved**: 8.62+ billion tonnes autonomously
- **Expansion**: Autonomous Cat 777 for quarries (Luck Stone partnership)

#### Electrification
- **Cat Dynamic Energy Transfer (DET)**: Charges trucks while operating
- **Battery-Electric LHD**: R1700 XE (zero emissions)
- **Electric Drive Systems**: Switch reluctance technology
- **Energy Storage**: PGS 1260 HD ESS modules

#### Cat Digital
- **Product Link™**: Telematics for fleet management
- **VisionLink®**: Asset tracking and health monitoring
- **Cat Inspect**: Mobile inspection app
- **Cat Command Remote Control**: Semi-autonomous operation
