# Apple Supply Chain Strategy

## Overview

Apple operates one of the most complex and efficient supply chains in the world, managing:
- 200+ suppliers across 50+ countries
- 164,000 employees (50% retail)
- Production of 200M+ iPhones annually
- Component sourcing from 3,000+ facilities

## Geographic Diversification Strategy

### "China Plus One" Approach

**Historical Concentration (2019):**
- ~90% of iPhone assembly in China
- Foxconn Zhengzhou: "iPhone City" (200,000+ workers)

**Current State (2024-2025):**
| Region | Role | Share |
|--------|------|-------|
| China | iPhone assembly, components | ~75% (down from 90%) |
| India | iPhone assembly (growing) | ~14% (target 25% by 2025) |
| Vietnam | iPad, Mac, Watch, AirPods | Growing significantly |

### India Strategy

**Timeline:**
- 2017: First iPhone SE assembly
- 2020: iPhone 11 production
- 2022: iPhone 14 at launch
- 2023: iPhone 15 at launch
- 2024: iPhone 16 at launch
- 2025: Target 25% of global iPhone production

**Key Facilities:**
- Foxconn Chennai (Tamil Nadu)
- Tata Electronics (acquired Wistron Karnataka)
- Pegatron Chennai

**Drivers:**
- Government Production Linked Incentive (PLI) scheme
- Avoiding US tariffs on Chinese goods
- Growing Indian domestic market
- Geopolitical risk mitigation

### Vietnam Strategy

**Products:**
- iPad: Majority of production
- Mac: Growing share
- Apple Watch: Significant portion
- AirPods: Majority of production

**Key Facilities:**
- Foxconn, Luxshare, Goertek facilities
- North Vietnam concentration

## Supply Chain Resilience

### Risk Management

**COVID-19 Lessons:**
- 2022 Zhengzhou lockdown disrupted iPhone 14 Pro supply
- Accelerated diversification push
- Built in more buffer inventory

**Geopolitical Risks:**
- US-China trade tensions
- Taiwan/China tensions (TSMC exposure)
- Tariff exposure mitigation

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Dual Sourcing:** Multiple suppliers for critical components
2. **Regional Production:** Produce for region in that region
3. **Strategic Inventory:** Pre-position components
4. **Supplier Development:** Invest in supplier capabilities
5. **Advanced Manufacturing Fund:** $5B invested in US manufacturing

## Environmental Initiatives

**Carbon Neutral Goal:** 2030 for entire product lifecycle

**Progress:**
- 100% renewable energy for corporate operations (achieved)
- 60% of manufacturing powered by renewable energy
- 300+ suppliers committed to 100% renewable energy
- Recycled materials: Aluminum, rare earth elements, tungsten

**Packaging:**
- Eliminated plastic wrap from iPhone boxes
- 100% fiber-based packaging (except inks/labels)
- Smaller boxes = more devices per shipment

## Labor and Ethics

**Supplier Responsibility:**
- Code of Conduct for all suppliers
- Annual audits (800+ conducted in 2023)
- Focus on: Working hours, health/safety, environmental compliance

**Challenges:**
- Foxconn labor practices (under ongoing scrutiny)
- Xinjiang supply chain concerns
- Conflict minerals sourcing

**Transparency:**
- Annual Supplier Responsibility Report
- Conflict Minerals Report
- Progress reports on supplier compliance

## Financial Impact

**Cost Structure:**
- Manufacturing cost: ~60% of iPhone BOM
- Labor costs in India: 20-30% lower than China
- Tariff savings from India production: Significant for US market

**Investment:**
- India: Foxconn investing $1.6B+
- Vietnam: Luxshare expanding facilities
- US: Advanced Manufacturing Fund ($5B)
