# Zoom Company Overview

## Company Profile

**Zoom Communications, Inc.** (NASDAQ: ZM) is an AI-first work platform for human connection, founded in 2011 by Eric Yuan, a former Cisco WebEx engineering leader. The company went public in April 2019 and was one of the rare tech companies profitable at IPO.

### Headquarters & Global Presence
- **Headquarters:** San Jose, California
- **Data Centers:** 13+ co-located facilities globally
- **Countries Served:** 190+
- **Employees:** ~7,400 worldwide (as of 2025)

### Core Value: "Deliver Happiness"

Zoom's culture centers on one word: **Care**

Eric Yuan's philosophy:

> "If you do not have a great culture, you really cannot scale"

## COVID-19 Growth Story

The most remarkable scaling story in SaaS history:

| Metric | Pre-COVID (Dec 2019) | Peak COVID (Apr 2020) | Growth |
|--------|---------------------|----------------------|--------|
| Daily Participants | 10M | 300M+ | **30x** |
| Employees | 2,000 | 6,000+ | **3x** |
| Revenue Run Rate | <$1B | $4B+ | **4x** |

### How Zoom Handled 30x Growth Without Downtime

1. **Pre-built Capacity:** Data centers running at 50% utilization pre-COVID
2. **Cloud Burst Agreements:** Overflow capacity with AWS and Oracle Cloud
3. **Scalable Architecture:** No code changes required for massive growth
4. **Stateless Design:** Horizontal scaling by adding servers
5. **Culture of Care:** Employees worked relentlessly for the mission

## Post-Pandemic Transformation

### Challenges
- Growth normalization after COVID surge
- Increased competition from Teams
- Return-to-office reducing pure remote work

### Strategic Response
1. **AI-First Transformation:** Zoom AI Companion across all products
2. **Platform Expansion:** From meetings to full workplace suite
3. **Enterprise Focus:** Upmarket with Contact Center, Phone
4. **Profitability Focus:** Operating margin expansion (17.4% GAAP, 39.4% non-GAAP)
