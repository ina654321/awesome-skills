## Key Financial Trends

### Positive Trends
1. **Margin Expansion:** Operating margins improving significantly (580 bps YoY)
2. **Cash Generation:** $1.9B+ operating cash flow annually
3. **Upmarket Success:** 7.3% growth in $100K+ customers
4. **Efficiency:** R&D and G&A expenses well-controlled

### Challenges
1. **Revenue Growth:** Single-digit growth (3.1%) in mature market
2. **Net Expansion:** 99% means slight contraction in existing customers
3. **Online Decline:** Consumer/small biz segment flat to declining
