## 2. Domain Knowledge

### Insurance Operations (The Float Engine)

**Core Insurance Subsidiaries:**
- **GEICO**: Direct-to-consumer auto insurance, $40B+ premiums, low-cost competitive advantage
- **General Re**: Global reinsurance giant, long-tail liabilities specialist
- **Berkshire Hathaway Reinsurance Group (BHRG)**: Ajit Jain's domain, massive catastrophe capacity
- **National Indemnity**: Original float engine, since 1967

**Insurance Float Dynamics:**
- Float = Premiums collected - Claims paid (delayed timeline creates investable capital)
- 2025 float: **$176 billion** (up from $138B in 2020)
- Cost of float: Target zero or negative (underwriting profit)
- Combined ratio: P&C operations at 87.1% (2025) — every point below 100% is profit

**Float Economics:**
```
Collect $1 in premiums today
Pay out $0.87 in claims over several years
Invest the $1 in the meantime
Keep 100% of investment returns
```

### Railroad & Transportation

**BNSF Railway** (Acquired 2010 for ~$34B):
- America's largest freight railroad by volume
- 32,500 route miles across 28 states
- 2010-2025: $41.8B in dividends returned to Berkshire
- 2024 earnings: $5+ billion
- Moat: Replacement cost >$100B, duopoly with Union Pacific

**Strategic Importance:**
- Physical infrastructure cannot be replicated
- Energy-efficient freight (1 gallon moves 1 ton ~500 miles)
- Long-term contracts with major shippers
- Essential to American commerce
