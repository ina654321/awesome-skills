## § 4 · Workflow: Strategic Planning & Execution

### § 4.1 · Strategic Planning Process

**Annual Strategy Cycle:**

```
PHASE 1: MARKET INTELLIGENCE (Q3)
├── Customer needs assessment (voice of customer)
├── Competitive landscape analysis
├── Technology trend evaluation
└── Market sizing and growth projections

PHASE 2: STRATEGY FORMULATION (Q4)
├── Portfolio prioritization (growth/share matrix)
├── M&A target identification
├── R&D resource allocation
├── Geographic expansion priorities
└── Financial planning (5-year model)

PHASE 3: EXECUTION PLANNING (Q1)
├── Hoshin deployment (strategy deployment)
├── KPI cascade to business units
├── Initiative prioritization
└── Resource allocation

PHASE 4: OPERATIONAL EXECUTION (Ongoing)
├── PPI Business System deployment
├── Monthly operating reviews
├── Quarterly strategic reviews
└── Continuous improvement cycles
```

### § 4.2 · Product Development Workflow

**High-Impact Innovation Process:**

| Stage | Activity | Decision Gate |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Discovery | Voice of customer, unmet needs | Opportunity validation |
| Concept | Technical feasibility, business case | Concept approval |
| Development | R&D execution, prototyping | Design freeze |
| Validation | Customer testing, regulatory | Launch approval |
| Commercialization | Market launch, scale-up | Post-launch review |

**R&D Investment Priorities:**
1. Next-generation analytical instruments (MS, EM)
2. Bioproduction technologies (single-use, continuous)
3. Clinical diagnostics (precision medicine)
4. Digital/software solutions (connectivity, AI)

### § 4.3 · M&A Integration Playbook

**Integration Framework (First 100 Days):**

```
WEEK 1-2: STABILIZATION
├── Leadership alignment
├── Communication plan execution
├── Employee retention actions
└── Business continuity assurance

WEEK 3-8: FOUNDATION
├── Functional integration planning
├── Synergy identification (cost + revenue)
├── Cultural assessment and integration
└── Customer communication

WEEK 9-12: ACCELERATION
├── Quick wins implementation
├── Best practice sharing
├── Commercial integration
└── PPI Business System introduction

MONTH 4-12: OPTIMIZATION
├── Synergy realization tracking
├── Process standardization
├── Systems integration
└── Strategic growth initiatives
```
