# Thermo Fisher Scientific — Business Segments

## Overview

Thermo Fisher Scientific operates through four reportable segments, each serving different aspects of the life sciences workflow from research to commercial manufacturing.

---

## Life Sciences Solutions

**FY2025 Revenue: $10.3B (23% of total)**

### Description
Provides reagents, instruments, and consumables for biological and medical research, drug discovery, and bioproduction.

### Key Product Categories

| Category | Products | Brands |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Genetic Analysis | qPCR, sequencing, microarrays | Applied Biosystems, Ion Torrent |
| Biosciences | Antibodies, cell culture, proteins | Invitrogen, Gibco |
| Bioproduction | Single-use technologies, filtration | HyPerforma, DynaDrive |

### Market Position
- #1 in quantitative PCR (qPCR)
- #1 in cell culture media and reagents
- #1 in NGS sample preparation
- Leading position in single-use bioreactors

### Key Customers
- Academic research institutions
- Pharmaceutical R&D
- Biotechnology companies
- Contract manufacturing organizations

### Growth Drivers
- Genomics and personalized medicine
- Cell and gene therapy manufacturing
- mRNA therapeutics
- Biosimilars development

## Analytical Instruments

**FY2025 Revenue: $8.8B (20% of total)**

### Description
Provides instruments, software, and services for analysis in laboratories across life sciences, environmental, and industrial markets.

### Key Product Categories

| Category | Products | Market Position |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Mass Spectrometry | Orbitrap, triple quadrupole, ICP-MS | #1 in life sciences MS |
| Electron Microscopy | Cryo-TEM, FIB-SEM, SEM | #1 in structural biology EM |
| Chromatography | HPLC, ion chromatography, GC | Top 3 globally |
| Materials Characterization | XRD, XRF, Raman | Leading positions |

### Key Brands
- Thermo Scientific
- FEI (acquired 2016)
- Dionex

### Applications
- Proteomics and metabolomics research
- Structural biology (cryo-EM)
- Environmental testing
- Food safety
- Pharmaceutical QA/QC

### Growth Drivers
- Cryo-EM for drug discovery
- Proteomics for precision medicine
- Environmental and food safety regulations
- Advanced materials research

## Specialty Diagnostics

### Description

### Key Product Categories

| Category | Products | Brands |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Clinical Diagnostics | Immunoassays, clinical chemistry | Thermo Fisher |
| Microbiology | ID/AST systems, blood culture | Thermo Scientific |
| Transplant Diagnostics | HLA typing, sequencing | One Lambda |
| ImmunoDiagnostics | Allergy, autoimmune testing | Phadia |

### Market Position
- Leading position in transplant diagnostics
- Strong presence in clinical microbiology
- Significant allergy and autoimmune testing

### Healthcare Market Channel
- Fisher Scientific channel for clinical labs
- #1 in North American lab distribution

### Growth Drivers
- Precision medicine and companion diagnostics
- Hospital-acquired infection control
- Transplant volume growth
- Emerging market expansion

## Laboratory Products and Biopharma Services

**FY2025 Revenue: $23.2B (52% of total)**

### Description
The largest and most diversified segment, providing lab products, clinical research services, and pharmaceutical manufacturing services.

### Sub-Segments

#### 1. Laboratory Products
- Lab consumables, chemicals, safety products
- Fisher Scientific channel (#1 in North America)
- Unity Lab Services (service and support)

#### 2. Clinical Research (PPD)
- Phase I-IV clinical trial management
- Laboratory services (central labs, biomarkers)
- Real-world evidence and post-market studies
- 26,000+ employees, operations in 50+ countries

#### 3. Pharma Services (Patheon)
- Drug substance manufacturing (small molecule, biologics)
- Drug product manufacturing
- Formulation development
- Clinical trial materials

#### 4. Clinical Trials Logistics
- Packaging and labeling
- Distribution and cold chain
- Comparator sourcing
- Returns and destruction

### Market Position
- #1 in lab distribution globally
- Top 5 global CRO (PPD)
- Top 5 global CDMO (Patheon)
- Leading clinical trial logistics

### Integrated Offering: Accelerator Drug Development
Combines CRO (PPD) and CDMO (Patheon) capabilities for seamless drug development:
- Streamlined clinical-to-commercial transition
- Reduced development timelines
- Simplified vendor management

### Growth Drivers
- Biopharma outsourcing trend
- Cell and gene therapy services
- mRNA manufacturing
- Emerging biotech funding
