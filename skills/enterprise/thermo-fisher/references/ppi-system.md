# Thermo Fisher Scientific вҖ” PPI Business System

## Overview

The **PPI Business System** (Practical Process Improvement) is Thermo Fisher Scientific's operational excellence framework, adapted from lean manufacturing principles and deployed across all business segments and functions.

## Core Philosophy

> "The PPI Business System is our competitive advantage вҖ” it enables us to adapt and deliver."
> вҖ” Marc N. Casper, Chairman and CEO

The PPI Business System enables Thermo Fisher to:
- Drive continuous improvement across all operations
- Respond rapidly to market changes
- Deliver consistent operational performance
- Generate productivity gains (2-3% annually)
- Support integration of acquisitions

## Key Elements

### 1. Strategy Deployment (Hoshin Kanri)

**Purpose:** Align organization around strategic priorities

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Annual Planning | Top-down strategic objectives |
| Catchball Process | Two-way dialogue for goal alignment |
| Breakthrough Objectives | 3-5 year strategic goals |
| Annual Objectives | Specific, measurable 1-year targets |
| Deployment Matrix | Cascading KPIs to all levels |

### 2. Daily Management

**Purpose:** Monitor and control daily operations

| Element | Practice |
|---------|----------|
| Tiered Meetings | Daily huddles at team, department, site levels |
| Visual Boards | KPI visibility, trend tracking |
| Standard Work | Documented best practices |
| Abnormality Response | Rapid escalation and problem-solving |

**Tier Structure:**
- **Tier 1:** Team/Cell level (15 min daily)
- **Tier 2:** Department level (30 min daily)
- **Tier 3:** Site level (1 hour weekly)
- **Tier 4:** Business unit level (monthly)

**Purpose:** Engage all employees in improvement

| Activity | Frequency | Scope |
|----------|-----------|-------|

| Idea Systems | Continuous | Employee suggestions |

| Performance Centers | Site-level visual management |

## PPI in Action: Acquisition Integration

When Thermo Fisher acquires a company, PPI deployment follows this pattern:

| Phase | Timeline | PPI Activities |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| Stabilization | Weeks 1-4 | Assess current state, quick wins |
| Foundation | Months 2-6 | Daily management implementation |
| Acceleration | Months 6-18 | Problem-solving training, Kaizen |
| Optimization | Year 2+ | Full PPI maturity, self-sustaining |

## Key Principles

1. **Respect for People**
   - Engage all employees in improvement
   - Develop problem-solving capabilities

2. **Focus on Process**
   - Fix processes, not blame
   - Go to the gemba (actual place)

3. **Scientific Thinking**
   - Hypothesis-driven problem-solving
   - Experiment and learn

4. **Customer First**
   - All improvement serves customer value
   - Quality at the source
