**Acquisition Discipline:**

```
THERMO FISHER M&A FRAMEWORK:

1. Strategic Fit Assessment
   ├── Extends customer value proposition?
   ├── Fills capability gaps?
   ├── Leverages existing commercial infrastructure?
   └── Strengthens market leadership?

2. Financial Evaluation
   ├── Accretive to adjusted EPS within 12 months?
   ├── ROIC > WACC by year 3?
   ├── Synergies achievable ($75M+ typical target)?
   └── Revenue synergies from cross-selling?

3. Integration Excellence
   ├── PPI Business System deployment
   ├── Cultural alignment assessment
   ├── Commercial integration (leverage TMO scale)
   └── Talent retention strategy
```
