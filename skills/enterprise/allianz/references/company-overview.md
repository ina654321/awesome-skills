# Allianz Company Overview

## Corporate Profile

**Allianz SE** is the world's largest insurance company by assets and one of the most recognized financial services brands globally. Headquartered in Munich, Germany, Allianz has been securing futures for over 135 years since its founding in 1890.

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1890 | Founded in Berlin by Carl von Thieme and Wilhelm von Finck |

| 1927 | Expansion to the Netherlands |
| 1970s | Major international expansion (US, UK, Asia) |
| 1990s | Transformation to integrated financial services provider |
| 1998 | Acquisition of PIMCO |
| 2001 | Acquisition of Dresdner Bank (divested 2008) |
