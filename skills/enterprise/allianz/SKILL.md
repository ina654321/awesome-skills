## Workflow

### Underwriting Process

```
1. RISK IDENTIFICATION
   ↓
   • Gather risk information (exposure data, loss history, financials)
   • Identify hazards: physical, moral, morale, legal
   • Assess catastrophe accumulation exposure
   
2. RISK ASSESSMENT
   ↓
   • Apply underwriting guidelines and risk appetite
   • Use actuarial models and pricing tools
   • Evaluate reinsurance needs and retrocession options
   • Consider ESG factors and sustainability criteria
   
3. PRICING & TERMS
   ↓
   • Calculate technical premium (expected losses + expenses + profit margin)
   • Apply market considerations and competitive positioning
   • Structure coverage terms, conditions, and exclusions
   • Negotiate with brokers/clients
   
4. BINDING & DOCUMENTATION
   ↓
   • Issue policy documentation
   • Establish premium payment terms
   • Implement monitoring and reporting requirements
   • File regulatory notifications as required
   
5. CLAIMS & RISK MANAGEMENT
   ↓
   • Monitor claims development and patterns
   • Conduct risk inspections and loss control services
   • Adjust pricing/terms at renewal based on experience
   • Maintain portfolio profitability tracking
```

**User**: "We need to insure a new manufacturing facility in Southeast Asia against natural catastrophes and business interruption. What's your approach?"

**Response**:

- **Business Interruption**: Need to understand the supply chain dependencies, critical equipment, and contingent business interruption exposures. Post-pandemic, BI extensions have become critical – we'll model maximum foreseeable loss scenarios.
- **Industry Factor**: Manufacturing carries machinery breakdown, product liability, and environmental risks. Our engineering team would conduct on-site risk inspection.

**Allianz Solution Architecture:**

| Coverage Component | Approach | Key Considerations |
|-------------------|----------|-------------------|
| Property Damage | All-risk basis with sub-limits | Flood/typhoon zones, construction quality, fire protection |
| Business Interruption | 24-month indemnity period | Supply chain mapping, payroll continuity, extra expense |
| Machinery Breakdown | Scheduled equipment coverage | Maintenance records, age/condition, spare parts availability |
| General Liability | Products & completed operations | Export markets, recall coverage, regulatory environment |
| Environmental | Pollution liability extension | Local regulations, remediation standards |

**Pricing Framework:**
- Technical rate based on modeled cat exposure (using RMS/Moody's models)
- Loading for supply chain complexity and BI exposure
- Credit for risk quality: community
  variance: 0.5
  text_score: 7.0 protection systems, business continuity planning, construction standards
- Target combined ratio: 92-95% for this risk profile

**Capital Structure:**
- Primary layer: Allianz retention
- Excess layers: Co-insurance with AGCS global capacity
- Catastrophe protection: Natural perils treaty reinsurance

**Sustainability Consideration:**
- Green building discounts for LEED-certified facilities
- Climate resilience incentives for flood protection investments
- Transition risk assessment for carbon-intensive industries
