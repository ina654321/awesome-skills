## Incident Analysis: October 2023

### The Pedestrian Incident

**Sequence of Events:**
1. Human-driven vehicle strikes pedestrian in San Francisco
2. Pedestrian falls into Cruise AV lane
3. Cruise AV strikes pedestrian, dragging them 20 feet
4. Initial Cruise report: "Vehicle braked aggressively"
5. Full video revealed: AV continued moving post-collision

### Regulatory Response

| Agency | Action | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| CA DMV | Suspended driverless permits | Immediate halt |
| CA PUC | Investigated safety report | Fined for delay |
| NHTSA | Recall investigation | Bolt AV modifications |
| Federal | Congressional inquiry | Reputational damage |

### Internal Fallout

**Leadership Changes:**
- Kyle Vogt (CEO): Resigned November 2023
- Dan Kan (CPO): Resigned
- GM-appointed interim leadership

**Cultural Issues Alleged:**
- "Move fast and break things" mentality
- Pressure to hit deployment targets
- Safety reporting concerns

## Strategic Rationale for Shutdown

### GM's December 2024 Announcement

**Stated Reasons:**
1. "Considerable time and resources needed to scale"
2. "Increasingly competitive robotaxi market"
3. Capital allocation to core business priorities

**Implicit Factors:**
- $10B+ losses without profitability path
- Waymo's insurmountable lead (5+ years)
- Regulatory environment post-incident
- Reputational risk to GM brand
- Personal autonomy opportunity lower risk

### Alternative Paths Considered

| Option | Pros | Cons | Decision |
|--------|------|------|----------|
| Continue with reduced scale | Preserve optionality | Continued losses | Rejected |
| Partner with Waymo | Share costs | Lose control | Not pursued |
| Sell Cruise | Recover value | Low buyer interest | Failed |
| Focus on trucking | Less competition | Different tech stack | Rejected |
| **Shut down, absorb tech** | Cost savings, IP preservation | Write-off, layoffs | **Selected** |

## Lessons Learned

### Industry Implications

**For AV Development:**
1. L4 autonomy harder than projected (5-10 year delay from 2016 predictions)
2. Robotaxi unit economics challenging without massive scale
3. Safety incidents have outsized regulatory impact
4. Waymo's technical lead creates winner-take-most dynamics

**For GM Specifically:**
1. Disciplined capital allocation: Cut losses, pivot strategy
2. Personal autonomy lower risk than robotaxi
3. Super Cruise path offers immediate monetization
4. Avoid moonshots without clear ROI timeline

### Competitive Impact

| Company | Reaction to Cruise Shutdown |
