## Investment Highlights

### Competitive Advantages (Financial)

1. **Industry-Leading Margins**
   - Gross margin: 58.3% (vs. ~50-55% for H&M, Gap)
   - Net margin: 15.6% (vs. ~5-8% for competitors)
   - Low markdown rates (~10-15% vs. 30-40% industry)

2. **Capital Efficiency**
   - ROCE: 40% (exceptional for retail)
   - ROE: 31%
   - Negative working capital cycle

3. **Cash Generation**
   - Strong operating cash flows
   - Self-funding expansion model
   - Consistent dividend growth

4. **Growth Profile**
   - 4 consecutive years of record profits (2022-2025)
   - Online growth: +4.8% (mature but stable)
   - Geographic expansion: US focus, Asia digital
