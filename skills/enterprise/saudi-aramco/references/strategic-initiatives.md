# Saudi Aramco Strategic Initiatives

## Vision 2030 Alignment

Saudi Aramco is the primary funding engine for Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 economic diversification, contributing through:

- **PIF Funding**: Share transfers and dividends feed sovereign wealth
- **SABIC Integration**: $69B acquisition enabled PIF gigaproject funding
- **Localization**: IKTVA program (70% local content target)
- **Employment**: 75,000+ direct employees, 200,000+ indirect

## Crude-to-Chemicals Strategy

### Objective
Convert 2-3 MMBPD of crude oil directly to chemicals, bypassing fuels refining.

### Rationale
| Factor | Trend |
|--------|-------|
| Transport fuel demand | Declining (EV adoption) |
| Petrochemical demand | Growing (plastics, materials) |
| Margin capture | Higher in chemicals |
| Reserve longevity | Extends economic relevance |

### Key Projects
- **SABIC Integration**: Full feedstock/chemicals optimization
- **Global JVs**: China (Fujian 320K BPD refinery), India, Korea
- **Technology**: Next-gen refining for direct crude-to-chemicals conversion

## Carbon Management Strategy

### Net-Zero Commitment
- **Target**: Scope 1 & 2 net-zero by 2050
- **Interim**: 15% carbon intensity reduction by 2035
- **Current**: 0.05% methane intensity (vs OGCI 0.2% target)

### CCS Hub Development
| Phase | Capacity | Timeline |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Hawiyah (operating) | 0.8 MM tpa | Active |
| Jubail Phase 1 | 9 MM tpa | 2027-2028 |
| Future expansion | 44 MM tpa | By 2035 |

### Carbon-EOR Integration
- Captured CO₂ injected for enhanced oil recovery
- Uthmaniyah field: Proven Carbon-EOR operations
- Revenue stream: Incremental oil + carbon credits

## Hydrogen & New Energies

### Blue Hydrogen
- **Feedstock**: Jafurah natural gas
- **Process**: Steam methane reforming + CCS
- **Target**: 2.5 MM tpa low-carbon ammonia by 2030
- **Exports**: Pilot shipments to Japan, Korea completed

### Green Hydrogen
- **Location**: NEOM
- **Capacity**: 1.2 MM tpa ammonia
- **Power**: Solar + wind (renewable)
- **Partners**: ACWA Power, Air Products

### Ammonia as Carrier
- Easier to transport/store than pure H₂
- Existing infrastructure leverage
- Marine fuel application (IMO 2050 target)
