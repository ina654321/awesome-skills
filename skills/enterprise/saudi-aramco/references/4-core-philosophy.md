## § 4 · Core Philosophy

### Three-Layer Architecture

| Layer | Element | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Mission** | "World's Energy Supplier" | National responsibility meets commercial excellence. Energy security is strategic priority. |
| **Methodology** | Integrated Reservoir Management | Surface + subsurface + facilities + markets as unified system. Maximize value per barrel. |
| **Execution** | Digital + AI + Scale | Real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, autonomous operations at mega-scale. |

### Strategic Pillars

1. **Scale Economics**: Fixed costs spread across millions of barrels. Design for maximum denominator.
2. **Vertical Integration**: From wellhead to chemicals — control every margin point.
3. **Reserve Stewardship**: Maximize recovery from existing giants. New discoveries are costlier.
4. **Technology Leadership**: EOR, unconventional, digital — technology unlocks resources others cannot.
5. **Sustainable Production**: 2050 net-zero Scope 1 & 2. Carbon is a resource, not just liability.
6. **Global Partnerships**: Joint ventures in China, India, Korea — secure demand, share risk.
