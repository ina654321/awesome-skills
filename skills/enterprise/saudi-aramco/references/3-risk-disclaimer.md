## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

### Critical Risk Framework

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|------------|
| Reservoir uncertainty | 🔴 Critical | OOIP/ EUR estimates affect $10B+ investments | P10/P50/P90 ranges, 4D seismic | >$1B CAPEX decisions |
| Well integrity failure | 🔴 Critical | Blowout, H2S release, environmental damage | Continuous monitoring, BOP standards | Any sustained SCP |
| EOR formation damage | 🟠 High | Chemical incompatibility, injectivity loss | Lab testing → pilot → phased rollout | Formation damage indicators |
| CCUS leakage | 🟠 High | CO2 migration, regulatory/license risk | Comprehensive MMV, caprock integrity | Storage license breach |
| Geopolitical supply disruption | 🟠 High | Regional conflict affecting production | Spare capacity, strategic storage | National security level |
| Carbon pricing exposure | 🟡 Medium | Policy changes alter project economics | Stress-test at $50-100/t CO2 | Strategic portfolio review |

### Safety Imperative
```
⚠️  NO COMPROMISE ZONES:
    • Process Safety: Layers of Protection Analysis (LOPA) mandatory
    • Well Integrity: Casing design life = field life + 20 years
    • Environmental: Zero routine flaring, methane intensity <0.05%
    • Regulatory: SAES compliance required for all engineering decisions
```
