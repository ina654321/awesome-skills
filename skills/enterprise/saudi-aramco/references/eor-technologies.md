# Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) Technologies

## EOR at Saudi Aramco

Saudi Aramco operates some of the world's largest EOR projects, targeting recovery factors >70% in giant carbonate reservoirs.

## EOR Methods Deployed

### 1. Miscible Gas Injection
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Mechanism** | CO₂ or hydrocarbon gas miscible with oil reduces viscosity, improves sweep |
| **Application** | Light oil (>35°API), deep reservoirs (>3,000 ft) |
| **Aramco Experience** | Ghawar — world's largest miscible gas injection project |
| **Incremental RF** | 15-25% of OOIP |
| **Key Success Factor** | Vertical conformance, gravity-stable injection |

### 2. Water-Alternating-Gas (WAG)
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Mechanism** | Alternating water and gas slugs improve mobility control |
| **Application** | Carbonate reservoirs with high heterogeneity |
| **Advantage** | Better sweep efficiency than continuous gas |
| **Design Variables** | WAG ratio, cycle volume, injection rate |

### 3. Chemical EOR
| Method | Mechanism | Aramco Status |
|--------|-----------|---------------|
| Polymer flooding | Increase water viscosity, improve mobility ratio | Pilot testing |
| Surfactant flooding | Reduce interfacial tension, mobilize residual oil | R&D phase |
| Alkaline flooding | In-situ soap generation, wettability alteration | Screening |

### 4. Thermal EOR
| Method | Application | Status |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Steam injection | Heavy oil | Limited (mostly light oil) |
| In-situ combustion | Heavy oil | Not deployed |

## EOR Screening Criteria

| Parameter | Miscible Gas | WAG | Chemical | Thermal |
|-----------|-------------|-----|----------|---------|
| Oil Gravity (°API) | >30 | >25 | 20-40 | <20 |
| Viscosity (cp) | <10 | <50 | <200 | >1000 |
| Depth (ft) | >3,000 | >2,000 | >2,000 | Variable |
| Reservoir Type | Carbonate/sandstone | Carbonate preferred | Sandstone preferred | Heavy oil |
| Aramco Fit | Excellent | Excellent | Limited | N/A |

## Key Aramco EOR Projects

### Ghawar Field EOR
- **Scale**: World's largest EOR operation
- **Method**: Miscible CO₂ and WAG
- **Injection**: Millions of cubic feet per day
- **Result**: Maintaining 5+ MMBPD from 70+ year-old field

### Khurais Complex EOR
- **Challenge**: Complex geology, lower permeability
- **Method**: Miscible gas injection
- **Target**: 1.2 MMBPD sustained production

### Uthmaniyah Carbon-EOR
- **Innovation**: CO₂ from Hawiyah gas plant
- **Integration**: CCS + EOR synergy
- **Capacity**: 0.8 MM tpa CO₂ injection

## EOR Best Practices

1. **Pilot Before Scale**: Pattern pilots mandatory before full-field
2. **4D Seismic**: Monitor flood front, identify bypassed oil
3. **Wells as Sensors**: Real-time monitoring, intelligent completions
4. **Conformance Control**: Address thief zones, improve sweep
5. **Integrated Modeling**: Coupled reservoir-surface-facilities optimization

## Future EOR Directions

- **Low-salinity waterflooding**: Wettability modification
- **Smart water**: Ion-tuned injection water
- **Nanofluids**: Enhanced displacement at pore scale
- **Microbial EOR**: Bio-surfactant production
