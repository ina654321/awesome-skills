# Riot Esports Manager — Player Welfare Checklist

Full reference for §3 (Player Burnout Risk) and §8.3. Riot's comprehensive player welfare program.

## Physical Health

### RSI & Ergonomics Prevention
- [ ] Mandatory ergonomic assessments (quarterly)
- [ ] Wrist braces provided for high-volume practice days
- [ ] Physical therapy sessions available (bi-weekly minimum)
- [ ] Practice station ergonomics audit (per team facility)

### General Health
- [ ] Team doctor on-site at all live events
- [ ] Annual comprehensive health checkup (blood work, cardiovascular)
- [ ] Sleep quality monitoring (travel schedule management)
- [ ] Nutrition program (team chef or meal stipend)

## Mental Health

### Support Infrastructure
- [ ] Dedicated sports psychologist (not HR — confidential)
- [ ] 24/7 crisis hotline (separate from team/management)
- [ ] Mandatory off-seasons (minimum 4 consecutive weeks with zero practice requirement)
- [ ] Mental health days (3 per season, no questions asked)

### Warning Signs Monitoring
| Indicator | Threshold | Action |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Practice hours >60/week | Triggers wellness check | Manager + psychologist |
| Performance decline >15% over 4 weeks | Flag for review | Analyze root cause |
| Social media engagement changes | Pattern analysis | Check for signs of distress |
| Team conflict frequency | >3 formal mediations/season | Full team wellness review |

## Career Transition

### Active Career Support
- [ ] Academic tuition reimbursement (part-time studies encouraged)
- [ ] Financial literacy program (mandatory for rookies)
- [ ] Content creation training (streaming, YouTube, brand building)
- [ ] Coaching certification pathways (while still playing)

### Post-Retirement Support
- [ ] Career counseling sessions (2 years pre/post retirement)
- [ ] Networking introductions to gaming industry roles
- [ ] Mental health support extended 2 years post-retirement
- [ ] Alumni network (former players mentoring current)

## Practice Hour Regulations (League Rules)

| League | Max Practice Hours/Week | Scrim Hours Included | Rest Day Required |
|--------|------------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| LCS | 60 | Yes | 1 full day |
| LEC | 55 | Yes | 1 full day |
| LCK | 50 | Yes | 1 full day |
| LPL | 65 | Yes | 1 full day |
| VCT | 50 | Yes | 1 full day |

**Emergency clause:** During live event weeks, practice hour limits reduced by 40%.
