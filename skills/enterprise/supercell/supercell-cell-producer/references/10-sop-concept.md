# SOP 1: Starting a New Game Concept

## Purpose

Validate a game idea before investing in prototype development.

## Input

Game idea or market opportunity

## Output

Go/No-Go decision for prototype phase

## Process

### Step 1: Concept Pitch (Day 1)

**Write 1-Page Concept Document**:
- Core gameplay loop (1 sentence)
- Target audience
- Differentiation from competitors
- Monetization hypothesis
- Why this, why now

**Present to Potential Cell Members**:
- Gauge initial excitement
- Identify who wants to work on it
- Collect early feedback
- Refine based on input

**Deliverable**: Concept document

### Step 2: Market Validation (Days 2-3)

**Research Competitive Landscape**:
- Identify direct competitors
- Identify indirect competitors
- Analyze their strengths and weaknesses
- Find gaps and opportunities

**Analyze Market Size and Trends**:
- Total addressable market (TAM)
- Serviceable addressable market (SAM)
- Market growth trends
- Platform trends

**Identify Similar Successes/Failures**:
- Games that succeeded in this genre
- Games that failed and why
- Lessons learned
- Timing factors

**Interview 5-10 Potential Players**:
- Would they play this?
- What do they like/dislike about similar games?
- What's missing in current offerings?
- Price sensitivity

**Deliverable**: Market validation report

### Step 3: Team Assembly (Days 4-7)

**Recruit Cell Members (Voluntary)**:
- Game Lead (if not already)
- Developers (2-6)
- Artists (2-4)
- Game Designer(s) (1-2)
- Optional roles

**Key Principle**: Team members must CHOOSE to join. No assignments.

**Define Roles and Responsibilities**:
- Who does what?
- Decision-making process
- Communication norms

**Set Up Collaboration Tools**:
- Project management (Jira, Trello, etc.)
- Communication (Slack, Discord)
- File sharing (Google Drive, Dropbox)
- Version control (Git)

**Schedule Regular Meetings**:
- Daily standup
- Weekly playtest
- Monthly planning

**Deliverable**: Team charter

### Step 4: Go/No-Go Decision (Day 7)

**Review Market Validation**:
- Is there a real opportunity?
- Is the market big enough?
- Is timing right?

**Confirm Team Commitment**:
- Is the team excited?
- Do we have the right skills?
- Is everyone committed?

**Set Prototype Timeline (2-6 weeks)**:
- What will we build?
- What's the definition of done?
- What's the success criteria?

**Define Prototype Success Criteria**:
- "One more game" feeling?
- Team plays voluntarily?
- Clear differentiation?

**Deliverables**:
- Go/No-Go decision
- Prototype roadmap
- Success criteria

## Success Criteria

**Go**:
- Clear concept with differentiation
- Market opportunity validated
- Committed team of 5-15
- 2-6 week prototype plan

**No-Go**:
- Generic concept without unique angle
- Market too small or saturated
- Can't assemble committed team
- No clear path to prototype
