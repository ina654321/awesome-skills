# SOP 3: Soft Launch Decision

## Purpose

Use real player data to make kill, iterate, or global launch decision.

## Input

2-6 months of soft launch data

## Output

Kill, iterate, or global launch decision

## Process

### Data Collection

**Retention Metrics**:
- D1, D7, D30 retention
- Cohort curves
- Retention by source

**Monetization Metrics**:
- ARPDAU (Average Revenue Per Daily Active User)
- Conversion rate (free → paying)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- CPI (Cost Per Install)

**Engagement Metrics**:
- Sessions per player per day
- Average session length
- DAU/MAU ratio
- Feature usage

**Technical Metrics**:
- Crash rate
- Load times
- Error rates
- Device performance

**Qualitative Feedback**:
- App Store reviews
- Support tickets
- Social media sentiment
- Community feedback

**Competitive Landscape**:
- New competitor launches
- Market changes
- Platform policy updates

### Analysis Framework

#### Retention Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | World Class |
|--------|------|-------|-------------|
| D1 Retention | 40% | 50% | 60%+ |
| D7 Retention | 15% | 20% | 25%+ |
| D30 Retention | 8% | 10% | 15%+ |

#### Monetization Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | World Class |
|--------|------|-------|-------------|
| ARPDAU | $0.05 | $0.10 | $0.20+ |
| Conversion Rate | 2% | 5% | 10%+ |
| CPI | <$1 | <$0.50 | <$0.20 |

#### Trend Analysis

**Improving**:
- Retention increasing week-over-week
- Monetization improving
- Engagement growing
→ Continue iterating

**Stable**:
- Metrics flat
- No clear improvement path
→ Consider kill if below thresholds

**Declining**:
- Retention dropping
- Engagement falling
→ Likely kill

### Decision Matrix

**Global Launch**:
- Metrics GREAT across the board
- Clear path to profitability
- Team passionate
- Market timing right

**Continue Iteration**:
- Metrics GOOD with clear improvement path
- Team sees opportunities
- Some metrics not yet GREAT

**Kill**:
- Metrics POOR after 2+ iterations
- No clear improvement path
- Team has lost passion
- Market changed significantly

### Decision Meeting

**Present Data Objectively**:
- Show metrics without spin
- Compare to benchmarks
- Present trends

**Gather Cell Sentiment**:
- Does the team still believe?
- Are people playing daily?
- Is the team excited?

**Get External Perspectives**:
- Show game to other cells
- Get honest feedback
- "Should we kill this?"

**Game Lead Makes Final Decision**:
- Based on data and sentiment
- No consensus required
- Decide and own it

**Announce and Execute Immediately**:
- No delays
- Clear communication
- Execute without hesitation

## Deliverables

- Soft launch report
- Recommendation document
- Decision rationale
- Next steps plan
