# MUJI Product Development Framework

## Design Philosophy

### "No-Brand Quality Goods"

**Core Principles:**
1. **No excess decoration**: Function over form
2. **Material honesty**: Natural textures, unbleached
3. **Rational design**: Every element serves purpose
4. **Appropriate price**: Accessible to many
5. **Environmental consciousness**: Sustainability integrated

## Development Process

### Stage 1: Need Identification
- Consumer research (no focus groups—observation)
- Lifestyle trend analysis
- Material innovation scouting

### Stage 2: Concept Design
- Sketch concepts
- Material specification
- Cost estimation
- Sustainability review

### Stage 3: Prototyping
- Sample production
- User testing (internal)
- Durability testing
- Packaging design

### Stage 4: Production
- Supplier selection
- Quality protocols
- Environmental audit
- Pilot production

### Stage 5: Launch
- Store display design
- Staff education
- Customer feedback
- Continuous improvement
