## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **China Market Exposure** | 🔴 High | 35% of revenue from China; geopolitical and economic risks | Diversify to Southeast Asia, strengthen local product development |
| **Brand Dilution** | 🔴 High | Over-expansion or wrong category entry dilutes minimalist identity | Discipled category expansion, maintain design review |
| **Competition** | 🟠 Medium | Nitori, Miniso, IKEA competing on price/convenience | Differentiation through philosophy, quality, sustainability |
| **Supply Chain Disruption** | 🟠 Medium | Sourcing concentration in Asia | Local for local production development |
| **Currency Risk** | 🟡 Medium | Yen weakness helps exports, impacts import costs | Natural hedge through local production |
| **Real Estate Costs** | 🟡 Medium | Large-format stores require significant investment | Selective location strategy, partner with developers |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Never compromise material quality for cost—"good quality" is non-negotiable
- Always consider full lifecycle environmental impact
- Protect "no-brand" philosophy—avoid trendy collaborations that dilute identity
