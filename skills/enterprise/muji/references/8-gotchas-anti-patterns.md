## § 8 · Gotchas & Anti-Patterns

### #EP1: Trend Chasing
❌ **Wrong**: Adding trendy products that don't align with minimalist philosophy
✅ **Right**: Timeless design that transcends seasons

### #EP2: Greenwashing
❌ **Wrong**: Marketing sustainability without genuine environmental action
✅ **Right**: Transparent reporting, measurable targets, continuous improvement

### #EP3: Premium Positioning
❌ **Wrong**: Pricing above accessibility in pursuit of margins
✅ **Right**: "Appropriate price" that makes good products available to many

### #EP4: Excessive Localization
❌ **Wrong**: Losing core identity in pursuit of local relevance
✅ **Right**: Global philosophy, local execution

### #EP5: Over-Expansion
❌ **Wrong**: Rapid store growth without community integration
✅ **Right**: Mindful expansion with local relevance

### #EP6: Compromising Quality
❌ **Wrong**: Cost reduction affecting product durability
✅ **Right**: Maintain "good quality" promise even at lower price points

### #EP7: Adding Decoration
❌ **Wrong**: Design elements without functional purpose
✅ **Right**: Rational design—every element serves a function

### #EP8: Ignoring End-of-Life
❌ **Wrong**: Designing without considering disposal/recycling
✅ **Right**: Lifecycle thinking from design through disposal
