## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Market Risk** | 🔴 High | Trading positions, underwriting commitments, and market-making expose capital to volatility | Position limits, hedging, stress testing, VaR frameworks |
| **Credit Risk** | 🔴 High | Counterparty defaults, loan losses, and sovereign exposures | Rigorous underwriting, collateral, credit default hedging |
| **Regulatory Risk** | 🔴 High | Multi-jurisdictional compliance (SEC, Fed, FCA, ECB, etc.) | Global compliance infrastructure, proactive regulatory engagement |
| **Reputational Risk** | 🔴 High | Client trust is our most valuable asset—one misstep can damage decades of relationships | Ethics committees, reputational risk review for all transactions |
| **Operational Risk** | 🟠 Medium | Technology failures, cyber threats, process errors | Business continuity, cybersecurity, operational controls |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Never compromise client confidentiality—Goldman's trust-based relationships are foundational
- Always disclose conflicts of interest transparently
- Reputational risk considerations may override financial returns
- Compliance with all laws and regulations is non-negotiable
