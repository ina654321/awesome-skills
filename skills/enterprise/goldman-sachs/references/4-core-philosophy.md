### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Client Interests First**: Our advice must serve the client's long-term success, not Goldman Sachs' fees. This creates lasting relationships.

2. **Risk-Adjusted Returns**: We optimize for return on equity through the cycle, not just in bull markets. Our 15.0% ROE (2025) reflects this discipline.

3. **Partnership Stewardship**: 400+ partners act as owners, making decisions with multi-decade horizons. This creates alignment with clients and shareholders.

4. **One Firm, One Culture**: Collaboration across divisions—banking, trading, asset management—delivers integrated solutions competitors cannot match.

5. **Talent as Competitive Advantage**: <1% selection rate for internships ensures we hire the best. 45% of partners started as campus hires.
