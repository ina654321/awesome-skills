# Danaher Acquisition Playbook

## Overview

Danaher has completed 50+ acquisitions since 2001, transforming from an industrial conglomerate into a life sciences and diagnostics leader. This playbook documents the proven methodology.

## Phase 3: Negotiation (Months -3 to -1)

### Valuation Framework

| Method | Weight | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| DCF | 40% | Intrinsic value based on DBS improvements |
| Comparable Transactions | 35% | Market reality |
| Trading Multiples | 25% | Public market sanity check |

**Danaher Typical Multiples:**
- Strong target (market leader, high growth): 8-12x EBITDA
- Average target: 6-8x EBITDA
- Turnaround (high DBS opportunity): 4-6x EBITDA

### Deal Structure Considerations

| Element | Danaher Preference | Rationale |
|---------|-------------------|-----------|
| Form | Cash preferred | Speed, certainty |
| Timing | Quick close | Minimize disruption |
| Reps & Warranties | Standard | Market terms |
| Escrow | 10-15% | Retention/recovery |
| Key Person Retention | Critical | DBS deployment |

### Negotiation Priorities

1. **Price** — disciplined, walk if price exceeds DBS-adjusted value
2. **Speed** — minimize process length to reduce uncertainty
3. **Certainty** — secure financing, antitrust clearance path
4. **Retention** — lock in key leaders pre-close

## Lessons Learned

### Success Factors

1. **Speed of DBS deployment** — Faster deployment = faster value creation
2. **Retention of key talent** — Especially in technical/sales roles
3. **Clear communication** — Over-communicate to reduce uncertainty
4. **Quick wins** — Early momentum builds confidence
5. **Cultural sensitivity** — Respect what made target successful

### Common Pitfalls

1. **Integration drift** — Without dedicated focus, integration slows
2. **Customer neglect** — Internal focus causes customer attrition
3. **Talent flight** — Uncertainty drives key people to leave
4. **Process imposition** — Forcing Danaher ways without understanding
5. **Synergy overstatement** — Assuming improvements without DBS rigor
