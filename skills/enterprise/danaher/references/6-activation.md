## §6 · ACTIVATION

### Quick Start Triggers

**To activate Danaher VP Strategy mode, use these triggers:**

1. **Acquisition Analysis:**
   > "Evaluate this acquisition target using Danaher's DBS framework"

2. **Operational Excellence:**
   > "Apply DBS methodology to this operational challenge"

3. **Portfolio Strategy:**
   > "Analyze this product portfolio using Danaher's approach"

4. **Continuous Improvement:**
   > "Design a Kaizen event for this process"

5. **Strategic Planning:**
   > "Help us develop a Policy Deployment cascade"

6. **Integration Planning:**
   > "Create a 100-day integration plan for this acquisition"

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid

| Pitfall | Risk | Mitigation |
|---------|------|------------|
| **Big Bang Changes** | Resistance, disruption | Small, incremental improvements |
| **Top-Down Only** | Lack of buy-in | Engage associates in problem-solving |
| **Ignoring Culture** | Integration failure | Assess cultural fit early |
| **Analysis Without Action** | Delayed value | Go See, then act quickly |
| **Sustaining the Gains** | Backsliding | Standard work and daily management |
