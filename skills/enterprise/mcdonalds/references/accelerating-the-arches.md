# Accelerating the Arches Strategy

## Overview

**Accelerating the Arches** is McDonald's growth strategy launched in November 2020 and enhanced with **Accelerating the Arches 2.0** in January 2023. The strategy is built around the M-C-D framework and the 4Ds.

## The M-C-D Framework

### M: Maximize Marketing

**Objective:** Leverage McDonald's unmatched brand power to create cultural moments that drive engagement and sales.

**Key Initiatives:**
- **Famous Orders**: Celebrity meal collaborations (Travis Scott, BTS, Cardi B, Saweetie)
- **Cultural Partnerships**: Minecraft Movie, The Grinch, Monopoly
- **Localized Marketing**: Region-specific campaigns that resonate locally
- **Digital Marketing**: Social media, influencer partnerships, app engagement

**2025 Highlights:**
- Monopoly promotion: One of largest digital customer acquisition events ever (46 million U.S. app users)
- Grinch Meal: Highest single sales day in company history
- Minecraft collaboration: Record engagement with younger demographics

**Measurement:**
- Brand awareness and relevance scores
- Social media engagement
- Marketing-driven sales lift

### C: Commit to the Core

**Objective:** Focus investment and innovation on the menu categories that matter most: Burgers, Chicken, and Coffee.

**Burgers:**
- Big Mac quality improvements
- Quarter Pounder enhancements
- Cheeseburger consistency
- **Big Arch**: New premium platform introduced 2025

**Chicken:**
- McNuggets (original recipe)
- McCrispy (launched 2022, expanded globally)
- McWings (Australia debut 2025)
- **Strategy**: Chicken is the fastest-growing category globally

**Coffee:**
- McCafé espresso-based drinks
- Iced coffee platform
- Premium coffee upgrades

**Menu Innovation Principles:**
1. Must improve or replace, not just add
2. Must not slow down operations
3. Must have strong consumer demand
4. Must be profitable for franchisees

### D: The 4Ds

#### 1. Digital

**Objective:** Create seamless digital experiences that drive loyalty and increase frequency.

**Components:**
- **Mobile App**: Ordering, payment, loyalty, offers
- **Self-Order Kiosks**: In-store digital ordering (90%+ U.S. deployment)
- **Dynamic Menu Boards**: AI-powered pricing and promotion
- **Kitchen Technology**: Automated production systems

**2025 Performance:**
- Digital sales: 28% of system sales
- Loyalty members: 210 million (90-day active)
- Loyalty-driven sales: ~$37 billion

**2027 Targets:**
- 250 million loyalty members
- $45 billion loyalty-driven sales

**Key Technologies:**
- **Dynamic Yield**: Personalization engine (acquired 2019)
- **Google Cloud Partnership**: AI and data analytics
- **IBM Partnership**: Drive-thru voice AI

#### 2. Delivery

**Objective:** Make McDonald's available wherever customers want it.

**Partnerships:**
- Uber Eats (global)
- DoorDash (primarily U.S.)
- Just Eat Takeaway (Europe)
- Local delivery partners (key markets)

**Coverage:**
- 90%+ of restaurants offer delivery
- Average delivery time: 30 minutes
- Average delivery check: Higher than in-store

**Operational Model:**
- "McDelivery" branded experience
- Dedicated delivery packaging
- Kitchen workflow optimization
- Delivery-only menu options in some markets

#### 3. Drive-Thru

**Objective:** Maintain leadership in the convenience channel that represents 70% of U.S. sales.

**Enhancements:**
- Dual drive-thru lanes
- Express lanes for mobile orders
- AI-powered voice ordering (pilot)
- Dynamic menu boards with personalization
- Dedicated fulfillment teams

**Performance Standards:**
- Target service time: Under 90 seconds
- Order accuracy: 95%+
- Customer satisfaction: Measured continuously

**Future Innovation:**
- Automated order taking
- Predictive preparation based on customer identification
- License plate recognition for loyalty

#### 4. Development

**Objective:** Accelerate restaurant expansion to reach 50,000 locations by 2027.

**Growth Targets:**
| Year | Gross Openings | Net Openings | Total Restaurants |
|------|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| 2024 | 2,000+ | 1,600+ | 41,822 |
| 2025 | 2,275 | 1,880 | 43,477 |
| 2026 | ~2,600 | ~2,100 | 45,500+ |
| 2027 | ~2,800 | ~2,200 | 50,000 |

**Expansion by Segment:**
- **U.S.**: ~900 new restaurants (2023-2027)
- **International Operated Markets**: ~1,900 new restaurants
- **International Developmental Licensed**: ~7,000 new restaurants

**Restaurant Formats:**
- **Traditional**: Freestanding with drive-thru
- **Urban**: Smaller footprint, walk-up/delivery-focused
- **Accelerated**: Modern, efficient design
- **Drive-Thru Only**: Post-COVID format

## The "3 for 3" Formula

Beyond the M-C-D framework, McDonald's executes on three core elements:

### 1. Compelling Value

**Strategy:** Win on affordability without compromising quality or profitability.

**Offerings:**
- **McValue**: Everyday value platform
- **EVM (Extra Value Meals)**: Bundle deals
- **$1 $2 $3 Menu**: Tiered value options
- **App-Exclusive Deals**: Loyalty member benefits

**2025 Results:**
- Gained share with low-income consumers
- Highest quarterly comparable guest count gap vs. competitors
- Improved affordability scores

### 2. Breakthrough Marketing

**Strategy:** Create cultural moments that drive brand relevance and engagement.

**Approaches:**
- Celebrity partnerships
- Nostalgia-driven campaigns
- Gamification (Monopoly)
- Local cultural relevance

### 3. Menu Innovation

**Strategy:** Introduce craveable new items that bring excitement without complexity.

**2025 Innovations:**
- Snack Wraps (U.S. return)
- McWings (Australia)
- Big Arch (multiple markets)
- New McCafé beverages

## Strategic Pillars for Success

### 1. Franchisee Alignment

Every strategic initiative must work for franchisees:
- Transparent communication
- Proven ROI
- Implementation support
- Financial assistance where needed

### 2. Operational Excellence

Speed and accuracy remain paramount:
- QSC (Quality, Service, Cleanliness) standards
- Crew training and development
- Technology-enabled efficiency
- Continuous improvement culture

### 3. Customer Obsession

Understanding and serving customer needs:
- Value across income segments
- Convenience in all channels
- Quality food and experience
- Cultural relevance

## 2026 Priorities

Based on FY2025 performance and learnings, McDonald's 2026 focus areas:

1. **Accelerate Digital**: New app launch, enhanced loyalty program
2. **Value Leadership**: Maintain momentum with low-income consumers
3. **Menu Innovation**: Protein-forward options, global platform scaling
4. **Development Pace**: 2,600+ new restaurants
5. **Franchisee Health**: Support profitability through efficiency
