# Roche Diagnostics Strategy

## Overview

Roche Diagnostics is the **global market leader in in-vitro diagnostics (IVD)** with a unique strategic position as the only major healthcare company with world-leading divisions in both **diagnostics and pharmaceuticals**.

**2024 Performance:**
- Sales: CHF 14.3 billion (+4% CER)
- Base business growth (ex-COVID): +8%
- Core operating profit margin: 16.8%
- Global installed base: 100,000+ analyzers

## Strategic Positioning

### The Roche Differentiation

```
Competitor Positioning:
├── Abbott: Strong in point-of-care, diabetes, rapid diagnostics
├── Siemens Healthineers: Imaging + lab diagnostics integration
├── Danaher (Beckman): Clinical chemistry, automation
├── Thermo Fisher: Life sciences + specialty diagnostics
└── Roche: ONLY company with world-leading Pharma + Diagnostics
```

**Competitive Moats:**
1. **Installed Base Lock-in**: cobas platform creates high switching costs
2. **Test Menu Depth**: 2,000+ assays across platforms
3. **Companion Diagnostics**: Exclusive integration with Roche therapeutics
4. **Global Reach**: Present in 100+ countries with local support
5. **Pharma Synergies**: NAVIFY platform, biomarker discovery, real-world data

- **cobas Liat**: PCR testing in physician offices

**Challenge**: Volume-Based Procurement (VBP) pricing pressure

**Response:**
- Focus on HMV assays less subject to commoditization
- NAVIFY digital solutions as differentiator
- Local manufacturing partnerships
- Companion diagnostics for Roche drugs
