# Roche Annual Report 2024 — Key Highlights

## Chairman's Letter — Dr. Severin Schwan

> "Preventing, stopping and curing diseases - this is what Roche is all about. Our mission is to improve people's quality of life and help them live longer through scientific excellence."

### 2024 Performance Summary

| Metric | 2024 | 2023 | Change (CER) |
|--------|------|------|--------------|
| **Group Sales** | CHF 60.5B | CHF 56.6B | +7% |
| **Pharmaceuticals** | CHF 46.2B | CHF 43.7B | +8% |
| **Diagnostics** | CHF 14.3B | CHF 13.7B | +4% |
| **Core Operating Profit** | CHF 20.8B | CHF 18.2B | +14% |
| **Core EPS** | CHF 21.44 | CHF 20.20 | +7% |
| **IFRS Net Income** | CHF 9.2B | CHF 12.0B | -19%* |

*IFRS net income impacted by impairment charges and higher interest costs

### Dividend

- **Proposed Dividend**: CHF 9.70 per share
- **Significance**: Would be the **38th consecutive annual increase**
- **Dividend Yield**: ~3.2% at current share price

### Strategic Priorities 2024-2025

1. **Personalized Healthcare Integration**
   - Companion diagnostics for 20+ medicines
   - Foundation Medicine genomic profiling
   - NAVIFY digital health platform expansion

2. **Innovation Leadership**
   - 17 potential blockbuster medicines in pipeline
   - 80+ clinical-stage programs
   - R&D investment: CHF 13+ billion annually

3. **Operational Excellence**
   - Core operating margin: 34.4% (+2.1pp)
   - Operating free cash flow: CHF 21.2B (+34%)
   - Net debt reduced by CHF 1.4B to CHF 17.3B

4. **Sustainability & Access**
   - Global access programs in 95+ countries
   - 16 million patients reached through access programs
   - Carbon neutrality targets on track

## CEO Commentary — Thomas Schinecker

> "2024 was a strong year for Roche. In the fourth quarter, we continued our very positive momentum for the third consecutive quarter with Group sales growth of 9% (CER). Core earnings per share exceeded the guidance raised at half year."

### Key Achievements

**Pharmaceuticals Division:**
- Strong growth across oncology, immunology, neuroscience
- New drug approvals: Itovebi (breast cancer), Ocrevus Zunovo (MS)
- 9% growth excluding COVID-19 impact and Ronapreve

**Diagnostics Division:**
- Base business growth of 8% (excluding COVID-19 products)
- New launches: Elecsys PRO-C3, cobas i601 mass spectrometry
- NAVIFY Digital Pathology 3.0 release
