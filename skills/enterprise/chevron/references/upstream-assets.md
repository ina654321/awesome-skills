## International

### Kazakhstan — Tengizchevroil (TCO)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Working Interest** | 50% (operator) |
| **Partners** | ExxonMobil (25%), KazMunayGas (20%), LukArco (5%) |
| **Production** | ~600,000+ b/d (with FGP) |
| **Reserves** | 25+ billion barrels original oil in place |
| **FGP Expansion** | Completed 2024; +260,000 b/d capacity |

**Tengiz Field Characteristics**:
- One of world's deepest supergiant fields (12,000+ ft)
- High pressure, high H2S content
- Complex reservoir: Carbonate platform
- 30+ year production history

**Future Growth Project (FGP)**:
- Wellhead pressure management project (WPMP)
- Sour gas injection
- Third generation plant
- $45B+ total investment
