## The 10-Foot Rule

> "I promised myself that if a customer came within 10 feet of me, I would look them in the eye, greet them, and ask if I could help them." — Sam Walton

**Execution:**
1. Acknowledge customer presence within 10 feet
2. Make eye contact and smile
3. Greet with warmth
4. Offer assistance
5. Follow through on requests

## The Sundown Rule

> "Sundown Rule: Respond to requests or issues before the end of the business day."

**Application:**
- Customer complaints resolved same day
- Operational issues escalated within 2 hours
- Supplier issues addressed within 24 hours
- Emergency response immediate
