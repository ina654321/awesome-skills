# EDLP (Every Day Low Prices) Framework

## Core Philosophy

> "We price items at a low price every day so our customers trust that our prices will not change under frequent promotional activity." — Walmart SEC Filing

## EDLP vs. Hi-Lo Comparison

| Dimension | EDLP (Walmart) | Hi-Lo (Traditional) |
|-----------|----------------|---------------------|
| Pricing | Stable, predictable | Volatile, promotional |
| Customer Trust | High | Lower (wait for sales) |
| Inventory Management | Smoother demand | Spike-and-crash cycles |
| Advertising Cost | Lower | Higher (promotions) |
| Margin Stability | More stable | Volatile |
| Labor Efficiency | Higher (steady flow) | Lower (promotion rushes) |

## EDLP Mechanics

### Price Calculation Framework

```
EDLP Price = Cost + Operating Margin - Scale Efficiency

Where:
- Cost = Product cost + Supply chain cost
- Operating Margin = Minimum viable margin (typically 20-25% gross)
- Scale Efficiency = Volume-driven cost reductions
```

### The EDLP-EDLC Flywheel

```
       Lower Prices
            ↓
    ┌───────┴───────┐
    ↓               ↓
Higher Volume   Lower Costs
    ↓               ↓
    └───────┬───────┘
            ↓
    Reinvestment in Price
            ↓
    Customer Trust/Traffic
            ↓
       Lower Prices (repeat)
```

## Pricing Decisions

### When to Accept Cost Increases

| Factor | Accept | Negotiate | Reject |
|--------|--------|-----------|--------|
| Commodity costs | Temporary spikes | Persistent increases | Artificial inflation |
| Supplier margin | Reasonable | Excessive | Unjustified |
| Competitive position | Maintain gap | Close gap temporarily | Lose leadership |
| Customer impact | Minimal | Moderate | Severe |

### Rollback Strategy

Rollbacks are temporary price reductions below EDLP levels.

| Scenario | Rollback Depth | Duration |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| New customer acquisition | 10-15% | 4-6 weeks |
| Competitive defense | Match competitor | As needed |
| Inventory clearance | 20-30% | Until cleared |
| Seasonal drive | 15-20% | Season peak |
| Event support | 10-15% | Event duration |

| Apparel | Target | Amazon, TJX |
| Home | Target | Home Depot, Lowe's |

### Price Gap Management

| Gap Level | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| >5% below | Monitor only |
| 0-5% below | Maintain |
| 0-5% above | Assess trade-off |
| >5% above | Immediate response |
