# Walmart Supply Chain Excellence

## Overview

Walmart operates one of the world's most efficient supply chains, consistently ranked in Gartner's Top 20 Supply Chains. The foundation of this excellence is **cross-docking** combined with **private fleet operations** and **vendor collaboration**.

## Cross-Docking Deep Dive

### What is Cross-Docking?

Cross-docking is a logistics procedure where products from a supplier or manufacturing plant are distributed directly to a customer or retail chain with minimal to no handling or storage time.

### Cross-Dock Process Flow

```
SUPPLIER → RECEIVING → SORT/STAGE → SHIPPING → STORE
              ↓            ↓            ↓
           2-4 hrs      1-2 hrs      Same day
           (RFID scan)  (By destination) (Direct route)
```

### Cross-Dock Benefits

| Benefit | Impact | Measurement |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Reduced storage costs | -60% vs. traditional | Cost per case |
| Faster inventory turns | 8-10x annually | Turn rate |
| Fresher products | Same-day delivery | Sell-by dates |
| Lower handling | Minimal touch points | Labor cost/case |
| Reduced shrink | Less warehouse time | Shrink % |
| Faster restock | 24-48 hours | Lead time |

### Types of Cross-Docking

| Type | Description | Use Case |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Manufacturing** | Direct from production | High-volume goods |
| **Distributor** | Consolidation point | Multi-supplier items |
| **Transportation** | Mode switching | Import containers |
| **Retail** | Store allocation | Mixed SKU pallets |

## Distribution Network

### U.S. Distribution Infrastructure

| Facility Type | Count | Function |
|---------------|-------|----------|
| Regional DCs | 120+ | General merchandise |
| Grocery DCs | 44 | Food distribution |
| eCommerce FCs | 29 | Online order fulfillment |
| Consolidation Centers | 3+ | Import/High-volume |
| Specialty DCs | Multiple | Pharmacy, optical, etc. |

### DC Network Design Principles

1. **130-mile radius** — Each store within 130 miles of a DC
2. **Temperature zones** — Separate ambient, refrigerated, frozen
3. **High-velocity lanes** — Fast movers bypass staging
4. **Store-specific staging** — Pre-sorted by delivery route
5. **RFID-enabled** — Real-time inventory visibility

## Vendor Collaboration

### Retail Link System

Retail Link is Walmart's proprietary supplier portal providing:

- Real-time sales data
- Inventory levels
- Forecast collaboration
- Order management
- Performance metrics

### VMI (Vendor Managed Inventory)

**How it works:**
1. Supplier monitors store inventory via Retail Link
2. Supplier generates purchase orders based on agreed parameters
3. Walmart receives and cross-docks shipments
4. Supplier responsible for in-stock performance

**Benefits:**
| For Walmart | For Supplier |
|-------------|--------------|
| Reduced inventory carrying cost | Demand visibility |
| Lower administrative cost | Production planning |
| Improved in-stock | Relationship depth |

### OTIF (On-Time In-Full)

OTIF measures supplier delivery performance.

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| On-Time | Arrives within delivery window | 95% |
| In-Full | Complete order quantity | 95% |
| Combined OTIF | Both on-time AND in-full | 95% |

**Consequences of Poor OTIF:**
- Supplier fines (2-3% of COGS)
- Reduced purchase orders
- Relationship escalation
- Potential removal
