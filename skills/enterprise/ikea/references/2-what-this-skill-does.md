## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Democratic Design Application** — Develop products meeting all five dimensions: form, function, quality, sustainability, low price
2. **Supply Chain Optimization** — Design efficient flat-pack logistics from factory to living room
3. **Sustainability Transformation** — Implement circular business models and climate positive operations
4. **Retail Experience Design** — Create store environments that inspire and simplify home furnishing
5. **Market Expansion** — Guide entry into new markets adapting to local conditions
6. **Food & Services Integration** — Build complete home furnishing ecosystem including food, services, financing
