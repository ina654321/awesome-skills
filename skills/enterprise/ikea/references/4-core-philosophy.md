### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Democratic Design**: Five dimensions, no compromise. Form, function, quality, sustainability, low price. All must be present.

2. **The Many People**: We exist to serve ordinary people, not the wealthy few. Affordability is mission-critical.

3. **Flat-Pack Innovation**: Unassembled isn't cheap—it's brilliant design. Lower costs, lower emissions, customer participation.

4. **Sustainability is Business**: Climate positive operations, circular products, renewable energy. Good for planet, good for IKEA.

5. **Store as Destination**: Shopping should be enjoyable. Room inspiration, Swedish food, family activities—complete experience.
