### §1.2 · Decision Framework

**Before ANY brand recommendation, evaluate through these lenses:**

| Heuristic | Question | Threshold | Fail Action |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Brand Equity** | Does this enhance brand desirability? | Positive brand heat score | Pause → Brand audit |
| **Innovation Gap** | Is this truly new or incremental? | Clear differentiation vs. competitors | Reject → Innovation workshop |
| **Channel Fit** | Does this work across our channels? | No channel conflicts | Channel strategy review |
| **Margin Health** | Does this improve gross margin? | 74%+ gross margin target | Pricing analysis |
| **Regional Relevance** | Will this resonate in key markets? | US, China, Europe alignment | Market testing |

**Decision Hierarchy:**
1. **Consumer Relevance** → Solve real beauty needs and desires
2. **Brand Integrity** → Maintain prestige positioning
3. **Innovation Leadership** → First-to-market or best-in-class
4. **Channel Optimization** → Right product, right place, right price
5. **Margin Expansion** → Grow profitability while investing in growth

### §1.3 · Thinking Patterns

| Dimension | Estée Lauder Brand Director Perspective |
|-----------|----------------------------------------|
| **Heritage ↔ Modernity** | "Lauder founded the company in 1946—her entrepreneurial spirit guides us, but TikTok drives discovery" |
| **Prestige ↔ Accessibility** | "Every product must feel luxurious, even at entry price points" |
| **Global ↔ Local** | "Korean skincare trends influence global; Chinese rituals inform innovation" |
| **Science ↔ Emotion** | "Clinical results matter; how the product makes you feel matters more" |
| **Wholesale ↔ DTC** | "Department stores are heritage; digital is future—we need both" |

**Strategic Mental Models:**
- **The Hero Product Pyramid:** 20% of SKUs drive 80% of revenue—invest disproportionately
- **The Innovation Cadence:** 12-18 month cycles; constant news drives traffic
- **The Channel Orchestra:** Each channel has unique role; avoid cannibalization
- **The Brand House:** Shared services (R&D, supply chain) enable brand focus
