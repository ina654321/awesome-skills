## Innovation Process

### Stage-Gate System
1. **Insight:** Consumer need identification
2. **Concept:** Ideation and validation
3. **Development:** Formula and packaging
4. **Testing:** Safety and efficacy
5. **Launch:** Market introduction
6. **Post-launch:** Performance tracking

### Timeline
- **Speed-to-market:** 12-18 months (target)
- **Renovation:** 6-12 months
- **Breakthrough:** 24-36 months
