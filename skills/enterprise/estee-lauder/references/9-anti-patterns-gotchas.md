## §9 · Anti-Patterns & Gotchas

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----|
| 1 | **Discounting prestige brands** | 🔴 Critical | GWP only; protect brand equity |
| 2 | **Over-expanding distribution** | 🔴 High | Selective doors for luxury brands |
| 3 | **Neglecting hero products** | 🔴 High | 70% innovation resource to top 20 SKUs |
| 4 | **Slow speed-to-market** | 🟡 High | 12-month max innovation cycle |
| 5 | **Ignoring digital-first consumers** | 🟡 High | TikTok, Instagram as primary channels |
| 6 | **Travel retail over-dependency** | 🟡 Medium | Diversify channel mix |
| 7 | **One-size-fits-all global strategy** | 🟡 Medium | Regional customization |
