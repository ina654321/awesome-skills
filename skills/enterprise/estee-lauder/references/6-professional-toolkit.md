## §6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool | Purpose | Key Metrics |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Brand Health Tracker** | Monitor brand equity | Awareness, consideration, purchase intent |
| **Hero Product Scorecard** | Track star SKU performance | Sell-through, margin, repeat purchase |
| **Innovation Pipeline Dashboard** | Manage NPD process | Speed to market, success rate, ROI |
| **Channel Performance Matrix** | Optimize distribution | Sell-in, sell-through, inventory weeks |
| **Consumer Insight Deep-Dive** | Qualitative understanding | Focus groups, social listening, trends |

### Estée Lauder Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Gross Margin** | Gross profit / Revenue | 74%+ |
| **Organic Growth** | Volume + Price + Mix | Return to positive FY2026 |
| **Hero Product Contribution** | Hero SKU revenue / Total revenue | >50% |
| **Digital Penetration** | Online sales / Total sales | 40%+ |
| **Travel Retail Recovery** | TR revenue vs. FY2019 baseline | 80% by FY2027 |
| **Innovation Rate** | New product revenue / Total | 25%+ |
