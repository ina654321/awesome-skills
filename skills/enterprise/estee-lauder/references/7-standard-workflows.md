## §7 · Standard Workflows

### §7.1 · Product Innovation Process

```
Phase 0: Consumer Insight (Months -24 to -18)
├── Trend scouting (WGSN, social listening)
├── Consumer need gaps analysis
├── Competitive landscape review
├── Regional input (US, China, Europe)
└── ✓ Output: Innovation brief

Phase 1: Concept Development (Months -18 to -12)
├── Formula development (R&D)
├── Packaging design concepts
├── Naming and positioning exploration
├── Concept testing (qual + quant)
└── ✓ Output: Validated concept

Phase 2: Product Development (Months -12 to -6)
├── Final formula lock
├── Regulatory approval pathway
├── Manufacturing scale-up plan
├── Pre-launch sampling program
└── ✓ Output: Production-ready product

Phase 3: Launch Preparation (Months -6 to 0)
├── Marketing campaign development
├── Retailer education and seeding
├── Influencer and press seeding
├── E-commerce content creation
└── ✓ Output: Go-to-market plan

Phase 4: Launch & Optimization (Months 0-12)
├── Retail rollout
├── Performance tracking
├── Post-launch consumer research
├── Line extension planning
└── ✓ Output: Launch performance report
```

### §7.2 · Brand Turnaround Playbook

```
Month 0-6: Diagnostic & Foundation
├── Brand equity assessment
├── Portfolio SKU rationalization
├── Consumer perception research
├── Competitive positioning audit
└── ✓ Output: Turnaround strategy

Month 6-12: Quick Wins
├── Hero product renovation
├── Digital content refresh
├── Influencer partnership reboot
├── Pricing optimization
└── ✓ Output: Momentum indicators

Month 12-18: Innovation Acceleration
├── Breakthrough NPD launch
├── Channel expansion
├── Marketing investment increase
├── Regional customization
└── ✓ Output: Growth trajectory

Month 18-24: Scale & Sustain
├── Full portfolio strength
├── Market share recovery
├── Margin expansion
├── Long-term growth platform
└── ✓ Output: Sustainable growth model
```
