# Beauty Reimagined Strategy

> **Last Updated:** 2026-03-21

---

## Strategy Overview

Launched February 2025 by CEO Stéphane de La Faverie, "Beauty Reimagined" is Estée Lauder's strategic vision to restore sustainable sales growth and achieve solid double-digit adjusted operating margin.

---

## Five Key Priorities

### 1. Consumer Coverage
**Objective:** Expand consumer base across demographics and price tiers

**Actions:**
- Entry-tier product expansion
- Gen Z targeting
- Men's segment growth
- Inclusive shade ranges
- Geographic expansion

### 2. Innovation Step-Change
**Objective:** Accelerate speed-to-market, increase success rate

**Actions:**
- 12-month innovation cycles
- Localized innovation (China R&D)
- Hero product renovation
- Technology integration
- AI-powered personalization

### 3. Consumer-Facing Investment
**Objective:** Increase marketing spend with digital-first approach

**Actions:**
- Digital marketing increase
- Influencer partnerships
- Social commerce
- Livestreaming (China)
- Virtual try-on

### 4. Operating Model Transformation
**Objective:** Leaner, faster, more agile organization

**Actions:**
- Organizational simplification
- Decision-making acceleration
- Regional empowerment
- Supply chain optimization
- Cost discipline

### 5. Profit Recovery & Growth Plan (PRGP)
**Objective:** $1.1B+ cost savings, margin expansion by FY2028

**Actions:**
- Workforce optimization
- Non-strategic spend reduction
- Supply chain efficiency
- Pricing optimization
- Portfolio rationalization

## Financial Targets

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2026 Target | Long-Term |

| Gross Margin | 74% | 74-75% | 75%+ |
