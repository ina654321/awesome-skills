# IBM FY2025 Financial Reference

## Executive Summary

IBM delivered strong performance in FY2025, exceeding expectations for revenue, profit, and free cash flow as the company continued its transformation to a hybrid cloud and AI platform.

## Financial Highlights (FY2025)

| Metric | Value | Change YoY |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Revenue** | $67.5B | +8% (+6% constant currency) |
| **Gross Profit Margin (GAAP)** | 58.2% | +150 basis points |
| **Operating Gross Margin** | 59.5% | +170 basis points |
| **Net Income** | $10.6B | +76% |
| **Free Cash Flow** | $14.7B | +9% |
| **Cash & Marketable Securities** | $14.5B | — |
| **Total Debt** | $61.3B | — |

## Key Growth Metrics

### Generative AI Business
- **Book of Business**: $12.5B+ (as of Q4 2025)
- **Growth**: Multi-billion dollar scale in <2 years
- **Mix**: ~75% Software and Consulting

### Hybrid Cloud Revenue
- **Total**: ~$23B+ (estimated, consistent with prior trends)
- **Growth**: Double digits
- **Red Hat Contribution**: $6.5B+ ARR
