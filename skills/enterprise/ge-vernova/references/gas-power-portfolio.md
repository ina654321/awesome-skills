# GE Vernova Gas Power Portfolio

## Overview

GE Vernova's Gas Power business is the global leader in gas turbine technology, with the largest installed base (~7,000 units) and most operating experience. The business spans heavy-duty and aeroderivative turbines for utility, industrial, and oil & gas applications.

**Key Metrics:**
- ~175 GW total installed capacity
- 7,000+ units in 150+ countries
- 99%+ fleet reliability
- 50%+ hydrogen capability (pathway to 100%)
