# GE Vernova Services & Digital Solutions

## Overview

GE Vernova's Services business is a major profit and cash flow driver, built on one of the largest installed bases in the energy industry. Combined with advanced digital solutions, the company delivers lifecycle value through predictive maintenance, performance optimization, and outcome-based agreements.

**Key Metrics:**
- Services represent ~60% of revenue across segments
- $119B+ total backlog (heavily services-weighted)
- 20-30 year asset lifecycles
- 7,000+ gas turbines under service agreements
- 95% of transmission utilities in service network

## Outcome-Based Agreements

### Performance Guarantees

**Availability Guarantees:**
- Typical: 95-98% availability
- Penalty/incentive structures
- Force majeure provisions
- Measurement protocols

**Efficiency Guarantees:**
- Heat rate commitments
- Power output guarantees
- Degradation management

**Emissions Guarantees:**
- NOx compliance
- CO2 intensity targets
- Hydrogen blending milestones

### Risk Sharing Models

**Traditional:**
- Customer bears performance risk
- Time and materials billing

**Outcome-Based:**
- Shared performance risk
- Fixed fees with upside sharing
- GE Vernova assumes operational risk

**Power Purchase:**
- GE Vernova operates asset
- Customer pays per MWh
- Full risk transfer option
