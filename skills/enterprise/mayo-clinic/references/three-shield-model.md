# The Three-Shield Model

## Overview

The Three-Shield Model is Mayo Clinic's foundational organizational framework, representing the integration of three core missions: **Practice**, **Education**, and **Research**. This model, established by the Mayo brothers in the early 1900s, ensures that every clinical decision strengthens all three areas simultaneously.

```
         ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
         │      MAYO CLINIC THREE-SHIELD MODEL     │
         │     "The Needs of the Patient Come First"│
         └─────────────────────────────────────────┘
                           │
        ┌──────────────────┼──────────────────┐
        │                  │                  │
        ▼                  ▼                  ▼
   ┌─────────┐      ┌─────────┐        ┌─────────┐
   │PRACTICE │      │EDUCATION│        │RESEARCH │
```

## Integration: How Shields Reinforce Each Other

### Practice ↔ Education
- Clinical care provides training ground for students/residents
- Educators bring latest knowledge to patient care
- Students ask questions that challenge conventional thinking

### Practice ↔ Research
- Clinical questions drive research priorities
- Research discoveries improve patient outcomes
- Practice data generates research hypotheses

### Education ↔ Research
- Trainees conduct research under mentorship
- Research training prepares next generation of physician-scientists
- Educational innovations spread through research dissemination

### All Three → Patient Benefit
```
Excellent Care
      ↓
Trains Future Physicians
      ↓
Generates Research Questions
      ↓
Research Discoveries
      ↓
Improve Practice
      ↓
[BACK TO TOP]
```

## The Primary Value: Patient First

> "The best interest of the patient is the only interest to be considered, and in order that the sick may have the benefit of advancing knowledge, union of forces is necessary." — Dr. William J. Mayo, 1910

All three shields exist to serve the patient:
- **Practice** delivers immediate care
- **Education** ensures future care quality
- **Research** discovers better treatments

### Sustainability
The Three-Shield Model ensures:
1. **Current patients** receive best available care
2. **Future patients** will have trained physicians
3. **Tomorrow's treatments** are being discovered today
