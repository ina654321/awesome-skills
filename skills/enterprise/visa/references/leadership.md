# Visa Leadership & Culture

## Executive Leadership

### Ryan McInerney — Chief Executive Officer

**Background:**
- Joined Visa in 2013 as President
- Became CEO in February 2023
- Previously at JPMorgan Chase (Consumer Banking)
- Compensation: $31.56M (FY2025)

**Leadership Priorities:**
1. **Tap to Everything**: Expand contactless to all use cases
2. **Value-Added Services**: Grow high-margin revenue streams
3. **New Flows**: B2B, P2P, stablecoins, real-time payments
4. **AI-Driven Commerce**: Intelligence in every transaction
5. **Global Financial Inclusion**: Expand access to digital payments

**Key Quotes:**
> *"We are witnessing a powerful convergence of technology trends: the rise of AI-driven commerce, the broad adoption of tokenized payments and data, the growing role of stablecoins and the digitization of identity. This isn't an incremental change; it's a fundamental rewiring of how money moves around the world."*

> *"We are entering a once-in-a-lifetime inflection point in payments, a shift where payments are simultaneously becoming more intelligent and fully integrated into the fabric of commerce."*

### Operating Principles

#### 1. Ecosystem Enablement
Build platforms that empower partners rather than compete with them.

> *"Our clients and partners are at the center of everything we do."*

#### 2. Security as Foundation
Tokenization and fraud prevention as competitive moats.

> *"Security is not a feature—it's the foundation."*

#### 3. Open Architecture
Support multiple form factors, rails, and currencies.

> *"Be the network of networks."*

#### 4. Data-Driven Decision Making
Use Visa's unique transaction data for insights.

> *"We have an unparalleled real-time view of global commerce."*

#### 5. Regulatory Partnership
Work with governments, not around them.

> *"Compliance is a competitive advantage."*
