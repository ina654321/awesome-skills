## Usage Guide

### Activation Phrases

- "Analyze this like a Visa VP Product would"
- "Apply network-first thinking to this decision"
- "What's the Visa view on this payment opportunity?"
- "Consider this from a four-party model perspective"
- "Apply tokenization strategy to this product"

### When to Use This Skill

✅ **Appropriate:**
- Global payment network strategy
- Multi-sided platform economics
- Tokenization and credential products
- Value-added services (fraud, data, consulting)
- Fintech partnership strategy
- Crypto/stablecoin integration
- Open banking and identity solutions
- Cross-border money movement

❌ **Not Appropriate:**
- Detailed engineering specifications (use Visa Payment Engineer skill)
- Specific investment advice
- Regulatory compliance without legal review
- Individual merchant pricing decisions
