# BMS Loss of Exclusivity (LOE) Strategy

## Overview
BMS faces a multi-year portfolio transition as key products face patent expiry. The company is executing a comprehensive strategy to navigate LOE while building sustainable growth.

## LOE Timeline

### Current LOE: Revlimid (2022-ongoing)
| Aspect | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Generic entry | Began 2022 |
| 2025 decline | -44% YoY |
| Strategy | Cash flow optimization, minimize SG&A |
| Outlook | Continued decline through 2026+ |

### Near-Term LOE: Eliquis (2027-2028)
| Aspect | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Medicare negotiation | Effective 2026 (IRA) |
| Generic entry | Expected 2027-2028 |
| 2026 guidance | +10-15% growth (volume offset) |
| Strategy | Maximize pre-LOE value, DTC campaign |

### Mid-Term LOE: Opdivo (Late 2020s)
| Aspect | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Biosimilar preparation | Ongoing |
| Strategy | Qvantig launch, Opdualag transition |
| Next-gen IO | Pumitamig, relatlimab HD |

## LOE Navigation Framework

### 1. Growth Portfolio Expansion
**Target**: $25B+ non-LOE revenue by 2029

| Product Category | Strategy |
|------------------|----------|
| New Products | Camzyos, Sotyktu, Reblozyl, Breyanzi scale to blockbuster |
| Pipeline | Iberdomide, pumitamig, Cobenfy multi-billion potential |
| M&A | Bolt-on acquisitions to fill gaps |

### 2. Lifecycle Management
| Product | Tactic |
|---------|--------|
| Opdivo | Subcutaneous Qvantig, Opdualag transition |
| Eliquis | Real-world evidence, patient support |
| Revlimid | Volume growth in limited generic markets |

### 3. Productivity & Cost Management
- $2B cost savings by 2027
- Legacy Portfolio SG&A reduction
- Growth Portfolio investment protection

### 4. Geographic Expansion
- International growth offsetting U.S. LOE
- Emerging markets opportunity
- China market development

## Financial Impact Scenarios

### Base Case
- Growth Portfolio: +15%+ annually through 2029
- Legacy Portfolio: Manage decline to minimize impact
- Total revenue: Transition through 2027-2028 trough
- Return to growth: 2028-2029

### Revenue Bridge to 2029
| Source | Contribution |
|--------|--------------|

| Pipeline approvals | Iberdomide, pumitamig, etc. |
| M&A | Bolt-on acquisitions |
