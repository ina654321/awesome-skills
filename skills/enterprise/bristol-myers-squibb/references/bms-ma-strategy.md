# BMS M&A Strategy & History

## Strategic Framework

### Acquisition Criteria
| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Stage | Late Phase 2/3 or approved |
| Peak sales potential | >$1B per asset |
| Strategic fit | Therapeutic area or modality expansion |
| Risk profile | De-risked development |
| Cultural fit | Aligns with BMS values |

### Annual BD Capacity
- **Target**: $3-5 billion annually for bolt-on deals
- **Focus**: Late-stage assets, partnerships, licensing
