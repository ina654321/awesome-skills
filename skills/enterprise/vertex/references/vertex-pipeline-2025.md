# Vertex Pipeline 2025-2026

## Phase 3 Programs

### 1. VX-880 (zimislecel) — Type 1 Diabetes
- **Indication**: T1D with severe hypoglycemia and hypoglycemic unawareness
- **Mechanism**: Allogeneic stem cell-derived fully differentiated islets
- **Study**: FORWARD Phase 1/2/3
- **Key Data (Phase 1/2)**:
  - 10/12 full-dose patients: Insulin independent at Day 365
  - 100% elimination of severe hypoglycemic events
  - >70% time in target glucose range
- **Enrollment**: ~50 patients (Phase 3 portion)
- **Timeline**: Complete enrollment 2025; regulatory submissions 2026
- **Market**: ~60,000 US patients initially; $5B+ peak potential
- **Manufacturing**: Memphis cell therapy facility

### 2. Povetacicept — IgA Nephropathy
- **Indication**: IgA nephropathy (IgAN)
- **Mechanism**: Dual BAFF/APRIL antagonist fusion protein
- **Study**: RAINER Phase 3
- **Origin**: Alpine Immune Sciences acquisition ($4.9B)
- **Key Data (Phase 1b/2a)**:
  - 64.1% proteinuria reduction at 36 weeks
  - 67% disease remission rate
  - Stable renal function
- **Dosing**: Subcutaneous every 4 weeks
- **Timeline**: Interim data H1 2026; potential approval 2026-2027
- **Market**: ~130,000 US patients; $3-5B peak potential
- **Expansion**: Membranous nephropathy, lupus nephritis (Phase 2)

### 3. Inaxaplin — APOL1-Mediated Kidney Disease
- **Indication**: AMKD (patients with two APOL1 risk variants)
- **Mechanism**: APOL1 inhibitor
- **Study**: AMPLITUDE Phase 3
- **Status**: Ongoing enrollment
- **Market**: Disproportionately affects African American population

### 4. Suzetrigine — Diabetic Peripheral Neuropathy
- **Indication**: Painful DPN
- **Mechanism**: NaV1.8 inhibitor (same as Journavx)
- **Study**: VX24-548-110 Phase 3
- **Timeline**: Ongoing
- **Market**: Large chronic pain opportunity

## Research Programs

### Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD)
- **Mechanism**: SERPINA1 correction
- **Approach**: Small molecule correctors
- **Status**: Research

### Next-Generation Pain

- **Stage**: Discovery/Early development
