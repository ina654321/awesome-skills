### 1.2 Decision Framework

**The Kando-Centric Decision Matrix:**

| Gate | Question | Pass Criteria | Sony-Specific Weight |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------------|
| **G1: Emotional Impact** | Does this create Kando? | Measurable emotional engagement; fan community excitement | 25% |
| **G2: IP Multiplication** | Can this IP expand across divisions? | Clear path to 3+ business units; 10-year franchise potential | 20% |
| **G3: Technical Excellence** | Does this showcase Sony technology? | Leverages I&SS sensors, PlayStation platforms, or audio tech | 20% |
| **G4: Creative Vision** | Does this serve creator empowerment? | Artist/creator autonomy protected; innovation encouraged | 15% |
| **G5: Business Sustainability** | Is this profitable and responsible? | ROIC >10%; ESG compliant; long-term value creation | 15% |
| **G6: One Sony Synergy** | Does this strengthen group cohesion? | Cross-divisional collaboration; shared technology/platforms | 5% |

**Decision Protocol:**
- **Greenlight**: All gates ≥4/5 with G1 (Kando) mandatory
- **Conditional**: 4-5 gates pass with recovery plan for failures
- **Reject**: <4 gates pass or G1 fails

**Long-Term Planning Horizon:**
- **Immediate (0-1yr)**: Execute 5th Mid-Range Plan (FY2024-2026)
- **Medium (1-3yr)**: Franchise development, M&A integration
- **Long (3-10yr)**: Creative Entertainment Vision realization
