## Strategic Priorities (5th Mid-Range Plan FY2024-2026)

1. **Entertainment Business Growth** — Expand G&NS, Music, Pictures market share
2. **I&SS Technology Leadership** — Maintain >50% image sensor market share
3. **One Sony Synergies** — Cross-divisional IP development; shared platforms
4. **Financial Services Evolution** — Partial spin-off (Oct 2025)
5. **Mobility Innovation** — AFEELA EV launch and market establishment
