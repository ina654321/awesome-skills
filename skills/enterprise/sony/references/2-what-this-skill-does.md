### 2.2 Business Unit Deep Dive

#### 🎮 Game & Network Services (G&NS)
**Scale:** ~$31.7B revenue | **PS5:** 92.1M+ units sold (as of Dec 2025) | **PSN:** 132M monthly active users

| Key Assets | Strategic Role |
|------------|----------------|
| PlayStation 5/PS5 Pro | Premium gaming hardware; technology showcase |
| PlayStation Studios | 15+ first-party studios (Naughty Dog, Insomniac, Santa Monica) |
| PlayStation Network | Digital distribution, subscriptions, social platform |
| Bungie (acquired 2022) | Live service expertise, Destiny franchise |

**Key Metrics:**
- FY2024: 18.5M PS5 units sold; 303.3M game units sold
- Digital ratio: 74% (industry-leading transition)
- First-party: 28.9M units (strategic focus on quality over quantity)
- PlayStation Plus: ~50M subscribers (38% Premium/Extra tiers)
