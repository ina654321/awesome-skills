# The Kando Philosophy & Creative Entertainment Vision

## Origins of Kando (感動)

**Definition**: Kando refers to the profound excitement and gratification derived from experiencing supreme quality and performance. It's the feeling of awe when encountering something that moves you emotionally.

**Etymology**:
- 感 (kan): Feeling, sensation, emotion
- 動 (dō): Move, touch, stir
- Combined: To be deeply moved; profound emotional connection

**Historical Context**:
Sony's founders, Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita, established the company with a vision to create products that would enrich people's lives. The Kando concept was formalized under Kenichiro Yoshida's leadership as the unifying principle for Sony's transformation into a creative entertainment company.

## Kando in Sony's Corporate Philosophy

### Purpose
> **"Fill the world with emotion, through the power of creativity and technology"**

This purpose statement encapsulates the Kando philosophy:
- **Emotion** is the outcome (Kando)
- **Creativity** comes from artists and creators
- **Technology** is Sony's tool to enable that creativity

| **Diversity** | Harness varied viewpoints | Global talent; inclusive content |

## The Creative Entertainment Vision (10-Year Horizon)

### Overview
Announced in 2024, the Creative Entertainment Vision outlines Sony's ambition for 2034 and beyond.

**Key Message**:
> **"Create Infinite Realities"**

Sony aims to seamlessly connect multi-layered worlds where physical and virtual realities overlap, delivering limitless Kando through creativity and technology.

### Three Pillars

#### 1. Creativity Unleashed (創造の解放)
**Vision**: Transcend dimensions to unleash the creativity of creators around the world

**Implementation Areas**:
| Technology | Application | Sony Asset |
|------------|-------------|------------|
| AI-assisted creation | Music production, animation | SME AI tools; Aniplex pipeline |
| Cloud production | Remote collaboration | PlayStation Network infrastructure |
| Virtual production | Real-time filmmaking | Crystal LED volumes |
| Spatial computing | 3D content creation | Sony imaging sensors |

**Key Initiatives**:
- Creator tools democratization
- AI as creative assistant (not replacement)
- Global talent access through technology

#### 2. Boundaries Transcended (境界の超越)
**Vision**: Transcend boundaries to connect diverse people and values, fostering communities

**Implementation Areas**:
| Boundary | Sony Solution | Example |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| Physical/Virtual | VR/AR experiences | PS VR2; spatial entertainment |
| Geographic | Global streaming | Crunchyroll worldwide |
| Temporal | Time-shifted content | Game streaming; cloud saves |
| Cultural | Localized experiences | Multi-language anime; region-specific music |

**Key Initiatives**:
- Metaverse/3D social spaces
- Cross-platform communities
- Fan-creator direct connection

#### 3. Narratives Everywhere (物語の拡張)
**Vision**: Expand exciting experiences rich in narrative beyond imagination and across the world

**Implementation Areas**:
| Narrative Form | Sony Capability | Example |
|----------------|-----------------|---------|
| Games | PlayStation Studios | The Last of Us franchise |
| Film/TV | Sony Pictures; PlayStation Productions | Uncharted film; TLOU HBO |
| Anime | Aniplex; Crunchyroll | Demon Slayer universe |
| Music | SME artists; soundtracks | Beyoncé visual albums |
| Experiences | Location-based; live events | Immersive exhibitions |

**Key Initiatives**:
- Transmedia storytelling
- IP 360° expansion
- Persistent worlds across media

## Kando Measurement Framework

While Kando is inherently subjective, Sony employs several proxies:

### Quantitative Indicators
| Metric | Measurement | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Fan engagement | Time spent, repeat consumption | Industry-leading retention |
| Community growth | Social following, event attendance | 20%+ YoY growth |
| Critical acclaim | Awards, reviews | 90+ Metacritic (games); Grammys (music) |
| Cultural impact | Meme penetration, mainstream recognition | Breakthrough to non-core audiences |
| Creator satisfaction | Retention, exclusivity deals | 90%+ renewal rates |

### Qualitative Indicators
| Indicator | Assessment Method |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Emotional response | Focus groups, sentiment analysis |
| Social sharing | Organic advocacy, user-generated content |
| Longevity | Classic status; continued relevance |
| Cross-generational appeal | Parent-child shared experiences |

## Kando vs. Business Metrics

Sony balances Kando-focused creative excellence with business performance:

| Kando Priority | Business Counterpart | Balanced Outcome |
|----------------|---------------------|------------------|
| Creator vision | Budget constraints | Sustainable creativity |
| Quality excellence | Time-to-market | Polished products with reasonable timelines |
| Fan satisfaction | Monetization | Long-term relationship over short-term revenue |
| Innovation risk | ROI requirements | Portfolio approach to R&D |

**Core Principle**: 
> "Technology exists for the purpose of realizing a creative vision, not the other way around."
> — Kenichiro Yoshida
