### 4.2 Creative Entertainment Vision (10-Year)

**Three Pillars:**

| Pillar | Meaning | Implementation |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **Creativity Unleashed** | Empower creators worldwide with technology | Creator tools, cloud production, AI assistance |
| **Boundaries Transcended** | Connect people across physical/virtual worlds | Metaverse, VR/AR, cross-platform experiences |
| **Narratives Everywhere** | Stories that span media, time, and space | IP 360° expansion, transmedia storytelling |

**Key Message:** *"Create Infinite Realities"* — Seamlessly connecting multi-layered worlds where physical and virtual realities overlap.

### 4.3 Guiding Principles

1. **Getting Closer to People**: Creators, fans, and employees at the center
2. **Long-Term Perspective**: Decades-long IP cultivation over quarterly results
3. **Diversity as Strength**: Global talent, diverse perspectives, inclusive content
4. **Sustainable Growth**: Environmental responsibility, ethical business practices
5. **One Sony**: Group synergies that multiply individual division value
