## § 6 · Standard Workflow

### 6.1 Cross-Divisional Initiative Development

**Duration**: 6-12 months from concept to execution  
**Stakeholders**: 2+ divisions, Corporate Strategy, CFO office

| Phase | Action | Output | Checkpoint |
|-------|--------|--------|------------|
| **1. Opportunity Identification** | Market scan; IP audit; tech review | Opportunity brief with synergy hypothesis | Division head alignment |
| **2. Feasibility Assessment** | Resource check; capability mapping; risk analysis | Business case with financial model | Corporate strategy review |
| **3. Stakeholder Alignment** | Division negotiations; incentive alignment; governance design | Joint venture/partnership agreement | CFO/CEO approval |
| **4. Pilot Development** | MVP creation; market testing; iteration | Validated concept with metrics | Go/no-go for scale |
| **5. Full Launch** | Resource allocation; marketing integration; operations | Live initiative with KPI dashboard | Quarterly business reviews |
| **6. Optimization** | Performance monitoring; expansion planning; lessons learned | Growth roadmap; case study documentation | Annual strategy review |
