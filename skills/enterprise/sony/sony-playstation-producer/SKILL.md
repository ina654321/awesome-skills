## Risk Management Framework

### Risk Matrix

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Development Delay** | High | High | Buffer time, scope management, early warning systems |
| **Budget Overrun** | High | Medium | Contingency reserves, regular financial reviews |
| **Technical Blocker** | Medium | Critical | Prototype early, technical directors, external consulting |
| **Key Talent Departure** | Medium | High | Knowledge documentation, succession planning, culture |
| **Creative Pivot** | Medium | High | Regular playtests, clear vision documentation |
| **Market Shifts** | Medium | Medium | Player research, competitive monitoring, pivot capability |
| **Crunch/Team Burnout** | High | High | Sustainable planning, mental health support, flexible work |
| **Hardware Shortages** | Low | High | PC ports, cloud streaming alternatives |

### Crisis Escalation

**Level 1: Schedule Slip**
- Trigger: >2 week delay detected
- Response: Scope reduction, resource reallocation

**Level 2: Budget Crisis**
- Trigger: >20% budget overrun
- Response: Executive review, potential scope cuts, timeline extension

**Level 3: Technical Disaster**
- Trigger: Core system failure, unfixable bugs
- Response: Emergency team, external experts, potential cancellation

**Level 4: Team Crisis**
- Trigger: Mass departures, burnout epidemic
- Response: HR intervention, work stoppage, culture reset

## Anti-Patterns

### Production Anti-Patterns

**1. Death March Development**
- ❌ Months of mandatory crunch, team burnout
- ✅ Sustainable pace, occasional targeted crunch with compensation

**2. Feature Creep Without Validation**
- ❌ Adding features because they're cool, not because they serve the game
- ✅ Strict scope management, player-validated features only

**3. Siloed Development**
- ❌ Departments working without communication
- ✅ Cross-functional teams, daily standups, integrated development

**4. Ignoring Player Feedback**
- ❌ Dismissing beta tester concerns as "they don't get it"
- ✅ Structured feedback integration, data-driven decisions

### Business Anti-Patterns

**5. Short-term Revenue Over Long-term IP**
- ❌ Rushed annual releases damaging franchise reputation
- ✅ Quality over quantity, protecting brand value

**6. Exclusivity at All Costs**
- ❌ Refusing PC ports when console sales have saturated
- ✅ Strategic exclusivity windows, then platform expansion

**7. Homogenized Portfolio**
- ❌ All games following the same template
- ✅ Diverse experiences (indie to AAA, different genres)
