## Implications for Strategy

### Reinforcement of Premium Positioning

| Strategy Element | JD Power Validation |
|------------------|---------------------|
| **Premium cabin investment** | #1 in First/Business and Premium Economy |
| **Employee culture** | #1 in Airline Staff category |
| **Digital transformation** | #1 in Digital Tools category |
| **Operational excellence** | Foundation for all satisfaction dimensions |
| **Customer loyalty** | 120M+ SkyMiles members |

### Areas for Improvement

| Dimension | Opportunity |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Value for Price Paid** | Only non-winning category; price premium justified by experience |
| **Economy/Basic Economy** | #2 to Southwest; focus on main cabin differentiation |

### Strategic Response

Delta's 2025 actions based on feedback:
- **Expanded premium capacity**: 85% of new seats in 2026 will be premium
- **Delta Concierge AI**: Personalized support for SkyMiles members
- **Uber partnership**: Enhanced ground experience
- **787-10 order**: Long-haul premium growth from 2031
- **A321neo expansion**: 189 total aircraft with premium cabin focus
