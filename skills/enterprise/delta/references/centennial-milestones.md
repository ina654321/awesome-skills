# Delta Air Lines Centennial Milestones (1925-2025)

## Overview

Delta Air Lines celebrated its **100th anniversary** in 2025, marking a century of aviation excellence from a small crop-dusting operation in Louisiana to the world's most valuable airline.

---

## The First 100 Years: Key Milestones

### Founding Era (1925-1940s)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1925** | Founded as **Huff Daland Dusters** in Macon, Georgia (May 30) — first aerial crop-dusting company |
| **1925** | Moved headquarters to Monroe, Louisiana |
| **1928** | Collett E. Woolman purchased company, renamed **Delta Air Service** (named for Mississippi Delta region) |
| **1929** | Began passenger service (Dallas to Jackson, Mississippi via Shreveport and Monroe) |
| **1930** | Suspend passenger service (returned to crop dusting during Great Depression) |
| **1934** | Resumed passenger service as **Delta Air Lines** (July 4) |
| **1941** | Headquarters moved to **Atlanta, Georgia** — establishing the hub that would define the airline |
| **1945** | Began international service to Caribbean and Venezuela |

### Growth & Expansion Era (1950s-1980s)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1953** | Merged with **Chicago and Southern Air Lines** — first major merger, gained routes to Caribbean and South America |
| **1955** | Introduced "Delta Air Express" freight service |
| **1959** | First airline to launch **DC-8 jet service** |
| **1961** | Merged with **Northeast Airlines** — gained Boston hub and New England markets |
| **1970** | Entered widebody era with **Boeing 747** service |
| **1972** | Merged with **Northeast Airlines** (second merger with this name) |
| **1979** | Began **Atlanta-London** service — first transatlantic route |

### Transformative Mergers Era (1980s-2000s)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1987** | Merged with **Western Airlines** — gained Salt Lake City hub, became 4th largest U.S. airline |
| **1991** | Acquired **Pan Am's transatlantic routes** and Frankfurt hub — major European expansion |
| **1995** | Launched **SkyMiles** loyalty program (evolved from previous programs) |
| **1997** | First airline to board 100 million passengers in a year |
| **2000** | Named **Air Transport World Airline of the Year** |
| **2005** | Filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy reorganization (September) — emerged in 2007 |
| **2008** | Merged with **Northwest Airlines** — became **world's largest airline**, gained Detroit and Minneapolis hubs, Tokyo access |

### Modern Era (2010s-2020s)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **2010** | Completed integration with Northwest |
| **2012** | Invested in **Virgin Atlantic** (49% stake) — strengthened transatlantic partnership |
| **2013** | Became first U.S. airline to invest in **SAF (Sustainable Aviation Fuel)** |
| **2014** | Launched industry-leading profit sharing program ($1B+ annually to employees) |
| **2015** | Completed acquisition of **Penair** stake, expanded regional network |
| **2016** | **Ed Bastian became CEO** — current leader of Delta's transformation |
| **2018** | Named **Fortune's Most Admired Airline** |
| **2019** | Opened **First Delta Sky Club at Atlanta** (new international terminal) |
| **2019** | Joined **Pratt & Whitney GTF MRO network** with Delta TechOps |

### Pandemic & Recovery Era (2020-2025)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **2020** | COVID-19 pandemic devastated airline industry; Delta pivoted to cargo and maintained operations |
| **2021** | #1 in JD Power Customer Satisfaction |
| **2022** | Most on-time North American airline (Cirium) |
| **2023** | #1 Premium Economy satisfaction (JD Power) |
| **2023** | Launched **Delta Sync** free Wi-Fi on 700+ aircraft |
| **2024** | **Air Transport World Airline of the Year** |
| **2024** | #1 First/Business and Premium Economy satisfaction (JD Power) |
| **2024** | 200 million customers served |
| **2025** | **CENTENNIAL YEAR** — celebrated 100 years of operation |
| **2025** | Record revenue of **$63.4 billion** |
| **2025** | Named **Best U.S. Airline** by The Points Guy (8th consecutive year) |
| **2025** | Announced **30 Boeing 787-10** order for long-haul growth |

## Fleet Evolution

### Historic Aircraft Types

| Era | Aircraft | Significance |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| **1940s-1950s** | Douglas DC-3, DC-4, DC-6 | Post-war expansion |
| **1950s-1960s** | Convair 340, 440 | Regional service |
| **1960s-1970s** | Douglas DC-8, DC-9 | Jet age entry |
| **1970s-1980s** | Boeing 747, L-1011 TriStar | Widebody era |
| **1980s-2000s** | Boeing 727, 737, 757, 767 | Fleet modernization |
| **1980s-2020s** | MD-88, MD-90 | Regional jet replacement |
| **2010s-2020s** | Airbus A220, A320 family, A330, A350 | Current fleet backbone |
| **2020s-2030s** | Airbus A321neo, Boeing 787-10 | Future fleet |
