### Fleet Strategy Highlights

- **A220 Deployment**: Replacing regional jets and older 717s; optimal for 100-150 seat markets
- **A321neo Order**: 100+ aircraft for domestic growth and A320 replacement
- **A350 Flagship**: Premium long-haul service with Delta One suites
- **767 Retirement**: Phasing out by 2030; replaced by A330neo and A350
- **757 Retirement**: Limited replacements; some routes upgauged to A321neo
