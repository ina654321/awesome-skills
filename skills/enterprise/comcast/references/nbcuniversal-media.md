# NBCUniversal Media

## Overview

**NBCUniversal** is one of the world's leading media and entertainment companies, encompassing broadcast and cable television networks, film studios, streaming services, and news operations. Acquired by Comcast in 2011 (majority) and 2013 (full ownership), NBCUniversal represents the content and entertainment pillar of Comcast's integrated strategy.
