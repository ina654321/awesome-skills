# Sky Group

## Overview

**Sky Group** (formerly British Sky Broadcasting/BSkyB) is Europe's leading media and entertainment company, acquired by Comcast in November 2018 for £17.28 per share ($40B total). Headquartered in Isleworth, London, Sky serves approximately 23 million customers across the UK, Ireland, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, and Italy.

## Corporate History
