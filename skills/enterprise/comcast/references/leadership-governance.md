## Governance Structure

### Shareholder Structure

**Class A Common Stock (CMCSA):**
- Publicly traded
- One vote per share
- ~3.9B shares outstanding

**Class B Common Stock:**
- Controlled by Roberts family
- Super-voting rights (20 votes per share)
- ~9% economic ownership, ~33% voting control

**Roberts Family Control:**
- Ralph Roberts founded company (1963)
- Brian Roberts continues family leadership
- Long-term strategic focus
- Alignment with shareholder interests
