### Revenue Projections

| Scenario | Annual Revenue | Gross Margin |
|----------|----------------|--------------|
| Low | €44 billion | 56% |
| High | €60 billion | 60% |

### Growth Drivers

1. **AI-Driven Demand**
   - Semiconductor market >$1T by 2030
   - 9% CAGR (2025-2030)
   - Advanced logic and DRAM acceleration

2. **EUV Lithography Spending CAGR (2025-2030)**
   - Advanced Logic: 10-20%
   - DRAM: 15-25%

3. **High-NA EUV Ramp**
   - Volume production by 2026
   - Full industry adoption for 2nm nodes

## Key Strategic Priorities

### Technology Leadership
- Scale EUV technology into next decade
- Extend holistic lithography portfolio
- Enable 3D front-end integration

### Capacity Expansion
- EUV: 90 systems/year capacity by 2025-2026
- DUV: 600 systems/year by 2025-2026
- High-NA: 20 systems/year by 2027-2028

### Capital Returns
- Growing dividends (2025: €7.50/share proposed)
- Share buybacks (€5.9B in 2025)
- Balanced capital allocation

---

## Risk Factors

### Export Controls
- US restrictions on advanced equipment
- Dutch government licensing requirements
- Potential expansion to additional products

### Supply Chain
- Rare earth element dependencies
- Single-source components
- Geopolitical trade tensions

### Market Concentration
- Top 3 customers drive majority of revenue
- Semiconductor cyclicality
- Customer consolidation
