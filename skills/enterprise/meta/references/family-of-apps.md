# Meta Family of Apps

## Overview

Meta's Family of Apps generates 98%+ of company revenue through advertising. Together they serve 3.35+ billion daily active people.

## Products

### Facebook (3.03B Monthly Active Users)
- **Founded**: 2004
- **Core Purpose**: Community building and connection
- **Key Features**:
  - News Feed (AI-ranked)
  - Groups (1.8B+ monthly users in groups)
  - Marketplace (1B+ monthly users)
  - Events
  - Watch (video)
- **Revenue Model**: Advertising (98% of FB revenue)
- **Demographics**: Broad appeal, strong with 25-54 age group

### Instagram (2B+ Monthly Active Users)
- **Acquired**: 2012 ($1B)
- **Core Purpose**: Visual expression and discovery
- **Key Features**:
  - Reels (TikTok competitor) — primary growth driver
  - Stories (ephemeral content)
  - Direct messaging
  - Shopping/Commerce
  - Creator tools
- **Revenue Model**: Advertising
- **Demographics**: Strong with 18-34 age group

### WhatsApp (2B+ Monthly Active Users)
- **Acquired**: 2014 ($19B)
- **Core Purpose**: Private messaging globally
- **Key Features**:
  - End-to-end encryption
  - Voice/video calls
  - Status (Stories)
  - Business API
  - Communities
- **Revenue Model**: Business API, future commerce
- **Demographics**: Dominant outside US (India, Brazil, Europe)

### Messenger (1B+ Monthly Active Users)
- **Spun out**: 2011
- **Core Purpose**: Instant communication
- **Key Features**:
  - Meta AI integration
  - Games
  - Payments
  - Business messaging
- **Revenue Model**: Business use, potential future ads

### Threads (400M+ Users)
- **Launched**: July 2023
- **Core Purpose**: Public conversations
- **Key Features**:
  - Fediverse integration (ActivityPub)
  - Instagram connection
  - Real-time discussions
- **Revenue Model**: None yet (planned for 2026)
- **Position**: Twitter/X competitor

## Competitive Position

### vs. TikTok
- Reels as primary competitor to TikTok
- Instagram captures more time spent among 18-34 demographic
- TikTok Shop ($16.4B) vs. Instagram Shopping ($32.1B)

### vs. X/Twitter
- Threads positioned as open alternative
- 400M+ users vs. X's ~250M (estimated)
- Fediverse integration differentiates from closed platform

### vs. Snap
- Instagram Stories vs. Snapchat Stories
- Stronger monetization on Instagram
- Reels outperforming Spotlight
