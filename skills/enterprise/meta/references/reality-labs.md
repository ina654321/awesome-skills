# Reality Labs (VR/AR/Metaverse)

## Overview

Reality Labs is Meta's division for virtual reality, augmented reality, and metaverse technologies. It's a long-term bet on the next computing platform.

## Product Portfolio

### Quest VR Headsets

#### Quest 3 (Released October 2023)
- **Price**: $499 (256GB), $649 originally
- **Features**: Mixed reality, pancake lenses, Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2
- **Market Position**: Premium consumer VR

#### Quest 3S (Released October 2024)
- **Price**: $299 (128GB), $399 (256GB)
- **Features**: Budget-friendly Quest 3 experience
- **Strategy**: Lower entry price to drive adoption

#### Quest Pro (Discontinued 2024)
- **Original Price**: $1,499
- **Lesson**: Enterprise market not ready for premium pricing

#### Market Position
- **Market Share**: ~70% of VR headset shipments (Q3 2024)
- **Total Sold**: 20M+ Quest units
- **Content Revenue**: $2B+ spent in Quest Store
- **SteamVR Usage**: 60%+ of VR headsets on Steam

### Horizon Worlds

#### Original Vision
- Social VR platform
- User-generated worlds
- Metaverse centerpiece
- 1B user goal (Zuckerberg, 2021)

#### Reality
- MAU never exceeded a few hundred thousand
- Creator economy struggled
- User retention challenges

#### 2026 Pivot
- Originally announced VR shutdown (June 15, 2026)
- Reversed after user feedback
- **Current Strategy**:
  - Mobile/web as primary platform
  - VR maintained for existing content
  - No new VR games
  - Focus on mobile gaming

### Ray-Ban Meta Smart Glasses

#### Features
- Photo/video capture
- Meta AI integration (voice)
- Music/audio
- Lightweight design
- Stylish frames

#### Success Factors
- No display = no social stigma
- Actually wearable all day
- AI assistant adds utility
- Strong partnership with Luxottica

#### Market Reception
- Strong sales momentum
- Proof point for AI + wearables
- Path to AR glasses

### Horizon OS

#### Strategy
- Opening Quest's operating system to third parties
- ASUS, Lenovo building Horizon OS headsets
- Similar to Android strategy for mobile

## Strategic Assessment

### What's Working
- Quest market leadership
- Ray-Ban Meta product-market fit
- Content ecosystem ($2B+ spent)
- Developer community

### Challenges
- Mainstream VR adoption slower than expected
- Horizon Worlds underperformance
- Massive losses unsustainable
- "Metaverse" term has negative perception

### Future Path
1. **2025-2026**: AI-first wearables (Ray-Ban Meta evolution)
2. **2026-2027**: Budget cuts, focus on proven products
3. **2027-2030**: True AR glasses (display + AI)
4. **2030+**: Widespread spatial computing
