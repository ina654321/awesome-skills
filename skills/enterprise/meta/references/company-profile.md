### "Year of Efficiency" (2023)
- 21,000 jobs eliminated
- Flattened organizational structure
- Reduced layers of management
- Focus on "do more with less"
- Result: Improved profitability, leaner operations

### Culture Principles
1. **Move Fast**: Speed of iteration matters
2. **Be Bold**: Take ambitious bets
3. **Focus on Impact**: Prioritize high-value work
4. **Be Open**: Share information freely
5. **Build Social Value**: Products should help people

## Key Milestones

### Early Years (2004-2010)
- 2004: Facebook launched at Harvard
- 2006: Opened to public
- 2007: Facebook Platform launched
- 2008: 100M users

### Growth Era (2010-2016)
- 2012: IPO, Instagram acquisition ($1B)
- 2013: 1B users
- 2014: WhatsApp ($19B), Oculus ($2B) acquisitions
- 2016: 2B users

### Challenges & Pivot (2016-2021)
- 2016: Election interference concerns
- 2018: Cambridge Analytica scandal
- 2019: Privacy-focused vision announced
- 2021: Rebranded to Meta, 3B users

### AI & Efficiency Era (2022-Present)
- 2022: First revenue decline
- 2023: "Year of Efficiency", Llama 2 released
- 2024: Strong recovery, Llama 3, AI investment
- 2025: AI-first strategy, $60B+ AI investment
