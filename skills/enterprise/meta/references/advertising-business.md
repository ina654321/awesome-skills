## Advertising Model

### Auction System
- Second-price auction (with modifications)
- Bid × Estimated Action Rate × Ad Quality = Total Value
- Advertisers bid for outcomes (clicks, impressions, conversions)

### Targeting

#### Traditional Targeting
- Demographics (age, gender, location)
- Interests
- Behaviors
- Custom audiences (website visitors, email lists)
- Lookalike audiences

#### AI-Powered Targeting (Advantage+)
- Automated audience expansion
- Predictive modeling
- Cross-platform signals
- Privacy-safe aggregation

### Measurement

#### Privacy Challenges
- iOS 14.5+ ATT impact (2021)
- Cookie deprecation
- Regulatory restrictions
- **Response**: AI modeling, first-party data

#### Solutions
- Aggregated Event Measurement
- Conversions API (server-side)
- SKAdNetwork integration
- Modeled conversions

### vs. Google
- Meta: Interest-based, social discovery
- Google: Intent-based, search
- Complementary platforms for most advertisers

### vs. TikTok
- TikTok: Younger demos, viral potential
- Meta: Broader reach, mature tools
- Both competing for video ad dollars

### vs. Amazon
- Amazon: Bottom-funnel, purchase intent
- Meta: Top/mid-funnel, discovery
- Increasing competition in commerce
