# Marriott International Operations Guide

## Company Overview

**Marriott International, Inc.** (NASDAQ: MAR) is the world's largest hospitality company, headquartered in Bethesda, Maryland. Founded in 1927 by J. Willard Marriott as a root beer stand, the company has grown to operate, franchise, and license hotel, residential, timeshare, and other lodging properties worldwide.

## Historical Timeline

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1927** | J. Willard Marriott opens The Hot Shoppe root beer stand |
| **1957** | First hotel opens: Twin Bridges Marriott Motor Hotel |
| **1967** | Marriott International incorporated |
| **1993** | Marriott International splits from Host Marriott (REIT) |
| **1995** | Enters timeshare with Marriott Vacation Club |
| **2016** | Acquires Starwood Hotels & Resorts for $13.6B |
| **2018** | Launches unified Marriott Bonvoy loyalty program |
| **2019** | Launches Homes & Villas by Marriott |
| **2021** | Anthony Capuano becomes CEO after Arne Sorenson's passing |
| **2024** | Opens 9,000th property; acquires citizenM for $355M |
| **2025** | Record 100,000 gross rooms added; $26.2B revenue |
