# BMW i-Series Electric Vehicles

## Overview

The **BMW i** sub-brand represents BMW's dedicated electric vehicle lineup, combining zero-emission powertrains with BMW's signature driving dynamics.

## Technology Features

### eDrive Technology

| Generation | Features | Models |
|------------|----------|--------|
| **Gen 5** | Integrated motor/transmission, no rare earths | iX3, i4, iX, i7, i5 |
| **Gen 6** | 800V, 400kW charging, 20% more density | Neue Klasse (2025+) |

### Efficiency Technologies

**Heat Pump:**
- Standard on all i-models
- Reduces winter range loss by up to 30%
- Uses waste heat from battery/motor

**Regenerative Braking:**
- Adaptive recuperation
- One-pedal driving capability
- Optimized for efficiency

**Aerodynamics:**
- Active grille shutters
- Air curtain technology
- Underbody optimization

### Charging Infrastructure

**BMW Charging Network:**
- 11,000+ public charging points (UK)

- Plug & Charge capability
- BMW Charging app integration
