## § 4 — Workflow

### § 4.1 BMW Product Development Lifecycle

#### Phase 1: Concept & Strategy (Months 0-12)

| Task | ✓ Done When | ✗ FAIL If | Output |
|------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Market analysis | Segment dynamics quantified | Assumptions unvalidated | Market requirements doc |
| Customer insight | 3+ personas validated | Internal opinion only | Customer journey maps |
| Competitive audit | Feature/price gaps identified | Incomplete competitor set | Competitive positioning |
| Brand fit assessment | Heritage elements identified | Dilutes brand DNA | Brand alignment memo |
| Business case | NPV positive, risk adjusted | <15% IRR hurdle | Approved business case |

#### Phase 2: Design & Engineering (Months 12-36)

| Task | ✓ Done When | ✗ FAIL If | Output |
|------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Design freeze | Exterior/interior approved | Design committee rejects | Design sign-off |
| Platform selection | Neue Klasse/CLAR/other chosen | Limits future powertrains | Platform decision doc |
| Technical specs | Performance targets defined | Below competitive benchmarks | Technical requirements |
| Supplier selection | Critical suppliers contracted | Single-source dependencies | Supplier agreements |
| Sustainability review | CO2 targets validated | Lifecycle increase vs current | Sustainability approval |

#### Phase 3: Validation & Launch (Months 36-48)

| Task | ✓ Done When | ✗ FAIL If | Output |
|------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Prototype testing | All validation gates passed | Critical failure in durability | Test reports |
| Production readiness | Quality rate targets met | PPM > industry benchmark | Production approval |
| Marketing readiness | Positioning, pricing finalized | Value proposition unclear | Launch plan |
| Dealer training | Sales/service certified | Knowledge gaps identified | Certification records |
| Launch execution | Volume ramp on plan | Quality issues emerge | Market introduction |
