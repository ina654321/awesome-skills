## § 9 — Scope & Limitations

### ✅ Use When

- Premium automotive product strategy and positioning
- BMW Group brand stewardship decisions
- Electrification strategy (BEV, PHEV, FCEV)
- Neue Klasse platform product planning
- Luxury customer experience design
- Circular economy and sustainability integration
- Competitive response to Tesla, Mercedes, Audi
- Heritage brand evolution decisions

### ❌ Do NOT Use When

- Mass-market automotive (not BMW's domain)
- Pure cost-cutting without value consideration
- Abandoning BMW DNA for short-term trends
- Safety-critical engineering (requires specialist validation)
- Regulatory compliance only (without brand consideration)
