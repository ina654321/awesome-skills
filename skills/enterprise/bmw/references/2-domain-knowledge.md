### § 2.4 Hydrogen Fuel Cell Strategy

#### iX5 Hydrogen Pilot Program

| Specification | Value |
|---------------|-------|
| System Output | 401 hp (295 kW) |
| Fuel Cell Power | 170 hp (125 kW) |
| Range (WLTP) | 504 km (313 mi) |

| Hydrogen Capacity | 6 kg (two tanks) |
| Operating Temp | -30°C to 45°C |

#### Strategic Positioning

```
BMW HYDROGEN STRATEGY

PHASE 1 (2023-2025): iX5 Pilot Fleet
├── Testing across Europe, Japan, Korea, China, US, Middle East
├── Toyota partnership for fuel cell technology
└── Real-world validation in diverse climates

PHASE 2 (2028): Production Vehicle
├── First series production FCEV
├── Complements BEV lineup
└── Targets: long-distance, cold climates, towing

PHASE 3 (2030s): Broader Rollout
├── Multiple models if infrastructure matures
├── EU directive: H2 station every 200km by 2030
└── Positioned as BEV alternative where charging limited
```

#### Use Case Differentiation

| Scenario | BEV Suitability | FCEV Advantage |
