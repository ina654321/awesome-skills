### §2.2 Business Segments

#### Ford Pro (Commercial Business)
- **Revenue (2025)**: $66+ billion
- **EBIT (2025)**: $6.8 billion (double-digit margin)
- **US Market Share**: 42%+ in commercial vehicles
- **Software Subscriptions**: 840,000+ globally (30% YoY growth)
- **Connected Vehicles**: 5.2+ million in operation
- **Key Products**: F-Series Super Duty, Transit vans, E-Transit
- **Services**: Ford Pro Intelligence, charging, financing, telematics

#### Ford Blue (ICE & Hybrid)
- **Revenue (2025)**: $101 billion
- **EBIT (2025)**: $3.0 billion
- **Focus**: Iconic gas-powered and hybrid vehicles
- **Key Products**: F-150, Bronco, Explorer, Maverick
- **Hybrid Leadership**: 77% of US hybrid truck market
- **Notable**: F-150 and Maverick are #1 and #2 best-selling hybrid pickups

#### Ford Model e (EV Division)
- **Status**: Restructuring for profitability by 2029
- **EBIT Loss (2025)**: $4.8 billion (improved from $5.1B in 2024)
- **Key Products**: Mustang Mach-E, F-150 Lightning, E-Transit
- **Strategy Pivot**: Focus on affordable, high-volume EVs via Universal EV Platform
- **Challenges**: Industry pricing pressure, demand fluctuations, cost structure

#### Ford Credit
- **EBT (2025)**: $2.6 billion (55% increase YoY)

### §2.4 Strategic Priorities (2026+)

1. **EV Transformation**
   - Path to Model e profitability by 2029
   - Universal EV Platform for affordable models
   - $9.5-10.5B capital expenditures (incl. $1.5B for Ford Energy)
   - Battery energy storage business launch (20 GWh capacity by 2027)

2. **Hybrid Expansion**
   - Target: 50% of global volume electrified (hybrid/PHEV/EV) by 2030 (vs 17% today)
   - Expand hybrid offerings across truck and SUV lineup

3. **Ford Pro Growth**
   - Continue software/services expansion (targeting 50% gross margin)
   - International expansion
   - AI-powered fleet optimization

4. **Cost & Quality**
   - Target: 8% adjusted EBIT margin by 2029
   - Reduce warranty costs and material expenses
   - Manufacturing efficiency improvements

5. **Capital Discipline**
   - 2026 Adjusted EBIT guidance: $8-10 billion
   - 2026 Free Cash Flow guidance: $5-6 billion
