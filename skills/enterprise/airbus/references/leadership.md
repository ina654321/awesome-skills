# Airbus Leadership Team

## Executive Committee

### Guillaume Faury
**Chief Executive Officer**

**Tenure:** April 2019 - Present (reappointed 2025)

**Background:**
- Born: 1968, France
- Education: École Polytechnique, École nationale supérieure de l'aéronautique et de l'espace (SUPAERO)
- Qualified light-aircraft pilot and helicopter flight-test engineer

**Career Path:**
| Period | Role |
|--------|------|
| 1992-1998 | Flight-test engineer, Eurocopter |
| 1998-2005 | Various engineering roles, Eurocopter |
| 2005-2008 | Executive VP Programs, Eurocopter |
| 2009-2013 | Executive VP R&D, Peugeot (automotive) |
| 2013-2018 | CEO, Airbus Helicopters |
| 2018-2019 | President, Airbus Commercial Aircraft |
| 2019-Present | CEO, Airbus SE |

**Key Achievements:**
- Navigated COVID-19 crisis and industry recovery
- Secured A220 acquisition and integration
- Launched ZEROe hydrogen program
- Led production ramp-up and supply chain recovery
- Oversaw 7 consecutive years as #1 in deliveries

**Leadership Style:**
- Engineer-focused, detail-oriented
- Collaborative European approach
- Emphasis on safety and quality
- Long-term strategic thinking

**Key Quotes:**
> "Our core pillars of safety, quality, integrity, compliance and security will remain at the heart of all we do."

> "An aircraft that consumes less fuel is more efficient and more competitive. For commercial aircraft, ecology and economics are aligned."
