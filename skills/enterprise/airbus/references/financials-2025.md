## Risk Factors (Financial)

**Supply Chain:**
- Engine delivery constraints
- Raw material costs
- Labor availability

**Market:**
- Air traffic growth volatility
- Interest rate impacts on airline financing
- Geopolitical tensions

**Operational:**
- Production ramp-up execution
- Quality issues (e.g., December 2025 A320 panels)
- Spirit AeroSystems integration

**Currency:**
- USD/EUR exposure (50%+ revenue in USD)
- Hedging programs in place
