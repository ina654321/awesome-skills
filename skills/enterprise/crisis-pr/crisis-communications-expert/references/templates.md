# Holding Statement & Statement Templates

## Holding Statement Template

A holding statement is issued when facts are limited but the crisis is public. Its purpose is to acknowledge, demonstrate action, and buy time for investigation.

```
[HOLDING STATEMENT — [DATE]]

[Company Name] is [aware of / responding to] [brief factual description of the incident].

We take [the safety of our customers / the security of customer data / this matter] with the 
utmost seriousness.

We are actively investigating and have [immediate action taken]. We will provide a further 
update within [timeframe — typically 48 hours].

Affected individuals can contact [dedicated hotline / email / website] for support.

Media inquiries: [PR contact name] at [contact details].

---

Issued by: [Name], [Title]
Date/Time: [Date, Time]
Next update: [Date, Time]
```

## Full Statement Templates

### Template A: Product Safety Incident

```
[COMPANY NAME] STATEMENT ON [PRODUCT/INCIDENT]

[DATE] — [Company Name] is aware of [X] reported incidents involving [Product Name] 
resulting in [injuries / property damage]. We are actively investigating the circumstances 
in cooperation with [relevant regulatory authority].

The safety of our customers is [Company Name]'s absolute priority. We take every report 
of a potential product safety issue with the utmost seriousness.

[If recall:] As of [date], [Company Name] has [initiated a voluntary recall / stopped 
sales] of [Product Name] with model numbers [X] through [Y], sold between [dates]. 
Approximately [number] units are affected in [market(s)].

Customers who own [Product Name] should:
1. [Immediate action — e.g., "Stop using the product immediately"]
2. [Contact action — e.g., "Contact our hotline at [number] for a free replacement"]
3. [Online action — e.g., "Visit [URL] to check if your product is affected"]

We are committed to investigating this matter thoroughly and keeping affected parties 
informed. We will provide a further update by [date].

Media inquiries: [Name], [Title], [Email], [Phone]
Consumer inquiries: [Dedicated hotline], [Hours], [URL]
```

### Template B: Data Breach

```
[COMPANY NAME] DATA SECURITY NOTICE

[DATE] — [Company Name] recently discovered unauthorized access to our [system / network / 
application] that occurred between [start date] and [end date]. Upon discovery, we 
immediately [contain the incident / shut down the affected system / engaged forensic experts].

Our investigation indicates that the following information may have been accessed:
- [Category 1: e.g., names and email addresses]
- [Category 2: e.g., hashed passwords]
- [Category 3: e.g., payment card information] — [Note: include only if accurate]

At this time, we [have / have not] identified evidence that the accessed data has been 
used for fraudulent purposes. [Note: only include if this is genuinely confirmed.]

We are notifying affected users directly. As a precautionary measure, we recommend:
1. Change your password on our platform and any other accounts using the same password
2. Enable two-factor authentication where available
3. Be alert for phishing emails referencing [Company Name]

We have reported this incident to [relevant regulatory authorities: list]. 
Affected users in [jurisdictions requiring notification] are being notified directly 
in compliance with applicable law.

A dedicated support page with frequently asked questions is available at: [URL]
Affected users can contact our dedicated response line at: [Phone], [Hours]

We sincerely apologize for this incident. We take data security extremely seriously and 
are implementing additional safeguards to prevent recurrence.

Media inquiries: [Name], [Title], [Email], [Phone]
```

### Template C: Executive Misconduct Allegation

```
[COMPANY NAME] STATEMENT ON PERSONNEL MATTER

[DATE] — [Company Name] is aware of allegations regarding [a senior executive / specific 
individual]. We take all allegations of this nature with the utmost seriousness.

The individual has been [placed on administrative leave / suspended from duties] effective 
immediately, pending a thorough and independent investigation.

The Board of Directors has retained [External Law Firm / Investigation Firm] to conduct 
an independent investigation into the matter. We are committed to a fair, transparent, and 
comprehensive process.

We are unable to comment further on specific personnel matters due to confidentiality 
obligations. However, [Company Name] is committed to maintaining the highest standards of 
conduct and will take appropriate action based on the investigation findings.

We will provide further updates as the investigation progresses and as appropriate.

Media inquiries: [Name], [Title], [Email], [Phone]
```

### Template D: Service Outage / Operational Incident

```
[COMPANY NAME] SERVICE UPDATE

[DATE] — [Company Name] is currently experiencing [a service disruption affecting 
Platform X / reduced capacity in Region Y]. We became aware of the issue at [time] 
on [date] and are working to restore full service.

Our engineering team is actively investigating the cause. We have implemented [containment 
measures / workaround / recovery plan].

We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. We understand the impact this has on our 
customers and are prioritizing resolution.

[If data affected:] We [have / are investigating whether] customer data was affected. 
We will notify affected customers directly.

Current status: [Expected restoration time if known / "We are working to restore service 
as quickly as possible and will provide updates every [X hours]."]

For real-time updates, visit: [Status page URL]
For support, contact: [Support contact details]

We will provide the next update by [date/time].

Media inquiries: [Name], [Title], [Email], [Phone]
```

## FAA Framework — Template Checklist

Before releasing any statement, verify:

**FACT:**
- [ ] Every factual claim is verified and specific (no "several incidents" if the number is known)
- [ ] No speculation ("may have been caused by..." is acceptable; "was caused by..." is not)
- [ ] Dates, times, and numbers are accurate
- [ ] Affected products/services/batches are precisely identified

**ATTITUDE:**
- [ ] Empathy is expressed without admitting liability
- [ ] "We take [X] seriously" is followed by a concrete action
- [ ] No words that could be construed as admission of fault (e.g., "accident," "mistake," "negligence")
- [ ] Tone matches the severity (L1 = urgent, L3 = measured)

**ACTION:**
- [ ] Every action mentioned is specific and already underway or confirmed
- [ ] Contact information (hotline, URL) is included and verified
- [ ] A timeline for the next update is given
- [ ] Nothing is promised that cannot be delivered

**LIABILITY AUDIT:**
- [ ] Legal has reviewed and approved
- [ ] Regulatory notification has been sent or is simultaneous with release
- [ ] No statements contradict previous communications
- [ ] Spokesperson has been briefed on Q&A
