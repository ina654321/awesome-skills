
### 2.6 Regulatory Environment

**Key Regulations Impacting Tencent:**

| Regulation | Impact | Tencent Response |
|------------|--------|------------------|
| Gaming license freeze (2021) | Revenue slowdown | Focus on evergreen games, international expansion |
| Minor gaming limits | Reduced playtime for under-18s | Industry-leading compliance systems |
| Data privacy laws | Stricter data handling | Enhanced compliance, local data storage |
| Antitrust enforcement | Merger scrutiny, exclusivity bans | Open platform strategy, reduced exclusives |
| Draft gaming rules (Dec 2023) | Market panic (-$80B value) | Removed Jan 2024, business as usual |
| AI regulations | Model approval required | Hunyuan registered with authorities |

**US Regulatory Pressure (2025):**
- CFIUS reviewing Tencent's gaming stakes (Epic, Riot, Supercell)
- Pentagon "Chinese military company" designation (Jan 2025)
- Potential forced divestiture discussions ongoing
