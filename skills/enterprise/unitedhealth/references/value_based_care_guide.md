## Value-Based Care Models

### 1. Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs)

**Medicare Shared Savings Program (MSSP)**

| Track | Risk Level | Savings Share | Loss Share |
|-------|------------|---------------|------------|
| Track 1 | One-sided | Up to 50% | None |
| Track 1+ | Two-sided | Up to 75% | Capped at 5% |
| Track 2 | Two-sided | Up to 60% | Up to 60% |
| Track 3 | Two-sided | Up to 75% | Up to 75% |

**Track 1+ Enhanced (UHG-Specific)**
- Care management fee: $15 PMPM
- Quality bonus pool: Additional 2%
- Telehealth integration
- Pharmacy cost sharing

### 2. Bundled Payment Programs

**Episode-Based Models**

| Episode Type | Duration | Target Conditions |
|--------------|----------|-------------------|
| Acute Myocardial Infarction | 90 days | Cardiac |
| Coronary Artery Bypass Graft | 90 days | Cardiac surgery |
| Major Joint Replacement | 90 days | Orthopedic |
| Stroke | 90 days | Neurological |

### 3. Capitation Arrangements

**Global Capitation**
- Full risk transfer for defined population
- Per-member-per-month (PMPM) payment
- Provider accountability for all costs
- Stop-loss protection available

**Partial Capitation**
- Professional services only
- Hospital costs remain fee-for-service or separate contract
- Reduced risk exposure

## Financial Incentive Design

### Shared Savings Distribution

```
Typical Track 1+ Distribution:

Generated Savings: $2.0M (5% below benchmark)

Quality Gate (30% weight):
- HEDIS: 75th percentile = 100% of quality component
- CAHPS: 70th percentile = 100% of quality component
- Utilization: 80th percentile = 100% of quality component

Quality Score: 85%
Eligible Savings: $1.7M (85% of gross)

Distribution:
- UnitedHealthcare (25%): $425K
- Provider Organization (75%): $1.275M
  - Quality bonus (20%): $255K
  - Shared savings (80%): $1.02M
```
