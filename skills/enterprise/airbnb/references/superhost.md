# Superhost Program Requirements

## Overview

Superhost is Airbnb recognition program for experienced, highly-rated hosts. Status is reviewed quarterly.

## Requirements (All Must Be Met)

| Requirement | Threshold | Measurement Period |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Completed Stays | 10+ | Past 365 days |
| Cancellation Rate | Less than 1% | Past 365 days |
| Response Rate | 90% or higher | Past 365 days |
| Overall Rating | 4.8 or higher | Past 365 days |

## Review Schedule

- Reviews happen 4 times per year
- Check dates: January 1, April 1, July 1, October 1
- Status valid for 3 months after earning

## Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Superhost Badge | Visible on listing and profile |
| Search Boost | Priority placement in search results |
| Priority Support | Dedicated phone line |
| Exclusive Rewards | Coupons and bonus opportunities |
| Product Preview | Early access to new features |
| Faster AirCover | Priority claim processing |

## Tips for Maintaining Status

1. Respond to all inquiries within 24 hours
2. Use automated messaging for common questions
3. Update calendar regularly to avoid cancellations
4. Follow up with guests to address issues before negative reviews
5. Set realistic expectations in your listing

## Loss of Status

If requirements not met at quarterly review:
- Badge removed immediately
- Can regain at next review period
- No penalty for losing status
