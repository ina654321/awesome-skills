# Airbnb Pricing Structure

## Fee Models

### Split Fee (Traditional)

| Party | Fee | Typical Rate |
|-------|-----|--------------|
| Guest | Service fee | 0-20% (avg ~14%) |
| Host | Service fee | 3% |

### Simplified Fee (Rolling Out 2024-2025)

| Structure | Rate | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Single fee | ~15.5% | Combined host + guest fee |
| Display | Total price upfront | No hidden fees shown |

## Host Pricing Tools

### Smart Pricing

- Algorithm suggests optimal nightly rates
- Considers: demand, seasonality, local events, competitor prices
- Host sets min/max bounds
- Can be enabled/disabled per listing

### Custom Pricing

- Weekday/weekend rates
- Seasonal adjustments
- Event-based pricing
- Length-of-stay discounts
  - Weekly: 5-20% off
  - Monthly: 10-30% off

## Additional Fees

| Fee Type | Typical Range | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Cleaning fee | $50-200 | One-time per stay |
| Extra guest fee | $10-50/night | Beyond base occupancy |
| Pet fee | $25-100 | Per stay or per night |
| Security deposit | $100-500 | Held, not charged unless claim |

## Taxes

- Occupancy taxes collected and remitted by Airbnb in many jurisdictions
- VAT/GST included in displayed price for most countries
- Host responsible for income tax reporting

## Pricing Strategy Tips

1. Research comparable listings in your area
2. Price slightly below market when launching (get reviews)
3. Use dynamic pricing for high-demand periods
4. Offer discounts for longer stays
5. Monitor and adjust based on occupancy rates
