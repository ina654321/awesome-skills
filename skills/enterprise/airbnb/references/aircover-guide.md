# AirCover for Hosts - Comprehensive Guide

## Overview

AirCover is Airbnb's comprehensive protection program for hosts, included free with every booking. It provides two main pillars of protection: damage protection and liability insurance.

> **Important:** AirCover is not a substitute for homeowners or renters insurance. It's designed as a primary layer of protection during active bookings.

---

## Pillar 1: Host Damage Protection ($3 Million)

### What's Covered

| Category | Coverage | Examples |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Property Damage** | Up to $3M | Broken furniture, damaged appliances, holes in walls |
| **Pet Damage** | Up to $3M | Chewed furniture, stains, scratches from guest pets |
| **Deep Cleaning** | Up to $3M | Smoke odor removal, professional carpet cleaning |
| **Income Loss** | Up to $3M | Lost revenue from canceled bookings due to damage |
| **Art & Valuables** | Up to $3M | Damaged or stolen artwork, jewelry, collectibles |
| **Vehicles** | Up to $3M | Cars, boats, watercraft parked on property |

### What's NOT Covered

- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from natural disasters (earthquakes, floods, hurricanes)
- Pre-existing damage
- Loss of money or securities
- Intentional damage by host or co-hosts

### Eligibility Requirements

1. **Booking Source:** Damage must occur during an active Airbnb booking
2. **Time Limit:** File claim within 14 days of checkout
3. **Documentation:** Provide photos/videos of damage
4. **Before Photos:** Strongly recommended to prove condition
5. **Host Compliance:** Must follow all Airbnb policies

## Pillar 2: Host Liability Insurance ($1 Million)

### What's Covered

| Category | Coverage | Examples |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Bodily Injury** | Up to $1M | Guest slips on wet floor, falls down stairs |
| **Property Damage** | Up to $1M | Guest damages neighbor's property |
| **Theft of Guest Belongings** | Up to $1M | Guest items stolen during stay |
| **Common Areas** | Up to $1M | Damage to building lobby, shared spaces |

### Who's Covered

- Primary host
- Co-hosts
- Cleaners and property managers (when working on your behalf)

### What's NOT Covered

- Intentional acts
- Criminal activity
- Professional services (medical, legal advice given to guests)
- Automobile-related incidents

## Filing a Claim: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Immediate Documentation

**DO:**
- Take photos/videos BEFORE cleaning or repairing
- Capture multiple angles
- Include timestamps
- Find "before" photos for comparison

**DON'T:**
- Clean up before documenting
- Dispose of damaged items
- Make repairs without approval

### Step 2: Calculate Costs

Gather:
- Original receipts for damaged items
- Links to replacement items
- Professional repair estimates
- Cleaning service invoices

### Step 3: Submit in Resolution Center

1. Go to Resolution Center
2. Select booking
3. Choose "Request payment for damages"
4. Upload evidence
5. Specify exact amount with breakdown

### Step 4: Guest Response Period

- Guest has **24 hours** to respond
- Options: Pay full, pay partial, decline, or no response
- If declined or no response → Escalate to Airbnb

### Step 5: Airbnb Review

- Case manager reviews documentation
- Decision typically within 1-2 weeks
- Superhosts receive priority processing

## Tips for Successful Claims

### Before Each Stay

1. **Photo/Video Documentation**
   - Timestamped walkthrough before check-in
   - Focus on high-value items and potential problem areas
   - Save to cloud with date

2. **Inventory List**
   - Document all valuable items
   - Include serial numbers for electronics
   - Keep receipts for major purchases

3. **House Rules**
   - Clearly state policies on parties, pets, smoking
   - Document in listing and message thread

### After Damage Discovery

1. **Don't Panic**
   - Most claims are resolved amicably
   - Airbnb is generally host-friendly for legitimate claims

2. **Be Thorough**
   - More documentation = faster approval
   - Include repair estimates, not just photos

3. **Communicate Professionally**
   - Even if upset, stay factual in messages
   - Let Airbnb mediate disputes

4. **Know Your Limits**
   - Small damages may not be worth the claim process
   - Consider guest relationship for future bookings

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does AirCover cover my primary residence?**
A: Yes, as long as the booking is through Airbnb.

**Q: What if my regular insurance denies the claim?**
A: AirCover is primary—file with Airbnb first.

**Q: Can I file a claim if the next guest already checked in?**
A: Yes, if within 14 days of the damaging guest's checkout.

**Q: Does AirCover cover COVID-19 cleaning?**
A: No, standard cleaning is host responsibility. Deep cleaning for extraordinary circumstances may be covered.

**Q: What if a guest's friend causes damage?**
A: Covered—damage by guests AND their invitees.
