# LEAP Manufacturing Program

## Overview
**LEAP** = Lilly Endocrine & Autoimmune Products

Lilly's $50+ billion U.S. manufacturing expansion program to meet unprecedented demand for incretin-based therapies (Mounjaro, Zepbound, and next-generation products).
