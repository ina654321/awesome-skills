| Product | Indication | Year |
|---------|-----------|------|
| Tirzepatide | Type 2 diabetes | 2018 |
| Donanemab | Alzheimer's disease | 2021 |
| Retatrutide | Obesity | Potential |

### Fast Track Designation
- Expedited development for serious conditions with unmet need
- Multiple programs across therapeutic areas

### Priority Review
- 6-month FDA review (vs. 10-month standard)
- Granted for significant improvements over available therapy

### Accelerated Approval
- Based on surrogate endpoints
- Confirmatory trials required
- Several Lilly oncology products

## Global Regulatory Strategy

### United States (FDA)
| Aspect | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| Primary pathway | Standard PDUFA |
| Expedited programs | BTD, Priority Review when eligible |
| Advisory committees | Proactive engagement for novel mechanisms |
| Real-time oncology review | Utilized for cancer programs |
| Post-marketing | Robust REMS when required |

### European Union (EMA)
| Aspect | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| Centralized procedure | Standard for most products |
| Parallel U.S. submission | Where possible |
| CHMP engagement | Scientific advice meetings |
| Conditional approval | For unmet medical need |

### Japan (PMDA)
| Aspect | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| Sakigake designation | Japan's breakthrough pathway |
| Collaboration | Joint development with Japanese sites |
| Priority review | Reduced timeline for innovative drugs |

### China (NMPA)
| Aspect | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| Local trials | China-specific clinical data often required |
| Priority review | Innovative drugs eligible |
| Partnerships | Local collaborations for market access |
| Recent approvals | Mounjaro, Zepbound, Kisunla |

## Key Regulatory Challenges

### Obesity Drug Class
| Challenge | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| CV outcomes data | SURPASS-CVOT ongoing for tirzepatide |
| REMS requirements | Monitoring for thyroid C-cell tumors |
| Supply constraints | Manufacturing expansion (LEAP) |
| Payer access | Real-world evidence generation |

### Alzheimer's Disease
| Challenge | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| ARIA monitoring | Risk evaluation and mitigation strategy |
| Biomarker requirements | Amyloid confirmation for treatment |
| Limited duration therapy | Novel approach requiring education |
| CMS coverage | Medicare national coverage determination |

### Oncology
| Challenge | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Accelerated approval confirmatory trials | Multiple ongoing studies |
| Companion diagnostics | Partnerships with diagnostic companies |
| Novel endpoints | Engagement with FDA on novel designs |
