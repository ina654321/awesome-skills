# Eli Lilly Q4 2025 Earnings Summary

## Financial Results (Quarter Ended December 31, 2025)

| Metric | Q4 2025 | Q4 2024 | Change |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| Revenue | $19.29B | $13.53B | +43% |
| Reported EPS | $7.39 | $4.89 | +51% |
| Non-GAAP EPS | $7.54 | $5.30 | +42% |
| Net Income | $6.6B | $4.1B | +61% |

## Full Year 2025 Results

| Metric | 2025 | 2024 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Total Revenue | $65.2B* | $45.0B | +45% |
| Non-GAAP EPS | $26.50 | $12.99 | +104% |

*Updated Q4 2025 earnings confirmed full-year performance

## Product Performance Q4 2025

| Product | Q4 2025 | Q4 2024 | % Change |
|---------|---------|---------|----------|
| Mounjaro | $7.41B | $3.53B | +110% |
| Zepbound | $4.26B | $1.91B | +123% |
| Verzenio | $1.60B | $1.56B | +3% |

### Mounjaro (Tirzepatide - Type 2 Diabetes)
- U.S. revenue: $4.1B (+57% YoY)
- International revenue: $3.3B (+267% YoY)
- Full year 2025: $23.0B total revenue

### Zepbound (Tirzepatide - Obesity)
- U.S. revenue: $4.2B (+122% YoY)
- Full year 2025: $13.5B total revenue
- OSA indication driving additional growth
- KwikPen approved by FDA (single device, 4 weekly injections)

### Guidance Assumptions
- Continued volume growth for Mounjaro and Zepbound

## Key Pipeline Updates

### Orforglipron (Oral GLP-1)
- Submitted for approval in U.S. (2025)
- Submitted in Japan and EU for obesity
- U.S. approval expected Q2 2026
- ATTAIN trials: 12.4% weight loss demonstrated

### Retatrutide (GGG Tri-Agonist)
- TRIUMPH program submissions planned for 2026
- TRANSCEND diabetes program ongoing
- Six additional Phase 3 trials reading out 2026

### Other Programs
- **Jaypirca CLL**: Phase 3 positive results (BRUIN CLL-321)
- **Taltz + Zepbound**: Positive Phase 3 in psoriatic arthritis
- **ImLunestrant**: Phase 3 EMBER-3 positive results
