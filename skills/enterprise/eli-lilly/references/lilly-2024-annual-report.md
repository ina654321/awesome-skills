## Key Business Developments 2024

### Regulatory Approvals
- **Zepbound OSA**: FDA approved for moderate-to-severe obstructive sleep apnea with obesity
- **Omvoh Crohn's**: FDA approved for moderately to severely active Crohn's disease
- **Kisunla China**: Approved for early symptomatic Alzheimer's disease
- **Jaypirca**: Accelerated approval for mantle cell lymphoma

### Clinical Milestones
- SURMOUNT-5: Zepbound showed 47% greater weight loss vs. Wegovy (head-to-head)
- SURMOUNT-1: 20.9% mean weight loss at 15mg dose maintained
- EMBER-3: Imlunestrant positive in ER+/HER2- metastatic breast cancer

### Business Development
- Acquisition of Scorpion Therapeutics' PI3Kα inhibitor program
- $15B share repurchase authorization
- 15% quarterly dividend increase (7th consecutive year)

## Manufacturing & Operations

### LEAP Program Progress
- Lebanon, Indiana: $9B facility construction underway
- Kenosha County, WI: $3B investment announced
- Total U.S. manufacturing commitments: $50B+ since 2020

### Production Capacity
- H1 2025 target: 1.6x incretin dose production vs. H1 2024
- Supply improvements for Mounjaro and Zepbound
