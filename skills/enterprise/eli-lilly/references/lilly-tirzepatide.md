# Tirzepatide Clinical Profile

## Overview
Tirzepatide is a dual GIP (glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide) and GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) receptor agonist - the first-in-class dual incretin therapy.

## Brand Names
- **Mounjaro** (tirzepatide) - Type 2 Diabetes
- **Zepbound** (tirzepatide) - Obesity/Weight Management

---

| Trial | Comparator | A1C Reduction | Weight Loss |

| SURPASS-1 | Placebo | -1.9% | -7.0 kg |

| Dose | Weight Loss | A1C Reduction |
|------|-------------|---------------|

| 15mg | -14.7% | -2.1% |

### SURMOUNT-3 (Intensive Lifestyle Intervention Lead-in)
- 12-week intensive lifestyle program → tirzepatide
- **Total weight loss: 26.6%** (lifestyle + tirzepatide)

### SURMOUNT-4 (Withdrawal Study)
- After 12 weeks tirzepatide, patients randomized
- Placebo group regained 14% of weight
- Continued tirzepatide maintained weight loss

### OSA Indication (SURMOUNT-OSA)
- First obesity medication approved for OSA
- Reduced AHI (apnea-hypopnea index) by 55%

## Safety Profile

### Common Adverse Events (≥5%)
| Event | Mounjaro | Zepbound |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Nausea | 18% | 25% |
| Diarrhea | 17% | 19% |
| Decreased appetite | 10% | 16% |
| Vomiting | 9% | 12% |
| Constipation | 7% | 11% |

### Serious Considerations
- **Boxed warning**: Risk of thyroid C-cell tumors (rodent studies)
- Contraindicated: Personal/family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma, MEN2
- **GI effects**: Dose-dependent, generally mild-moderate, decrease over time
- **Gallbladder disease**: Increased risk
- **Pancreatitis**: Monitor for symptoms

### Discontinuation Rates
- Generally <5% due to GI events in clinical trials
- Lower discontinuation vs. semaglutide in some comparisons

## Manufacturing & Formulation

### Formulation
- Synthetic peptide
- Once-weekly subcutaneous injection
- Multi-dose pen or single-dose vial (LillyDirect)
- Cold chain storage required

### Dosing Schedule
| Week | Dose |
|------|------|
| 1-4 | 2.5mg (initiation only) |
| 5-8 | 5mg |
| 9-12 | 7.5mg or 10mg |
| 13+ | 10mg, 12.5mg, or 15mg (maintenance) |

### Devices
- **Auto-injector pen**: Multi-dose, 4 doses per pen
- **KwikPen** (Zepbound): Single-patient use, 4 weekly injections per device
- **Vials**: LillyDirect option, requires syringe
