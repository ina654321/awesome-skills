# Huawei Corporate Structure & Governance

## Overview
- **Founded:** 1987 by Ren Zhengfei (任正非)
- **Headquarters:** Bantian, Longgang District, Shenzhen, Guangdong, China
- **Ownership:** 100% employee-owned (private company)
- **Employees:** 208,000+ worldwide (2024)

## Rotating Chairman System

Huawei operates a unique rotating chairmanship system where three senior executives rotate as Acting Chair for six-month terms.

### Current Rotating Chair (2025-2026)
| Period | Rotating Chair | Role |
|--------|----------------|------|
| Apr 1 - Sep 30, 2025 | Eric Xu (徐直军) | Deputy Chairman |
| Oct 1, 2025 - Mar 31, 2026 | Meng Wanzhou (孟晚舟) | Deputy Chairwoman, CFO |
| Apr 1 - Sep 30, 2026 | Ken Hu (胡厚崑) | Deputy Chairman |
