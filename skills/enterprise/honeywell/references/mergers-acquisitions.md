# Honeywell M&A Strategy

## Strategic Approach

### Philosophy
- Disciplined capital deployment
- Strategic fit over financial engineering
- Bolt-on focus over transformational deals
- Capability building for organic growth

### Criteria
1. **Strategic Fit**: Aligns with three megatrends
2. **Financial Returns**: ROIC > WACC, margin accretive
3. **Cultural Compatibility**: Shared values and approach
4. **Integration Feasibility**: Clear path to value capture
