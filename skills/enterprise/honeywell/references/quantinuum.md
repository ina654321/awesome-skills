# Quantinuum - Quantum Computing

## Company Overview

Quantinuum is the world's leading integrated quantum computing company, formed in 2021 through the merger of Honeywell Quantum Solutions and Cambridge Quantum Computing.

### Ownership Structure
- **Honeywell**: 54% majority stakeholder
- **Other Investors**: Including NVIDIA (joined 2025)
- **Valuation**: $10B pre-money (2025)
- **Funding Raised**: $840M (2025)

### Technology Platform

#### H-Series Quantum Computers
- **Architecture**: Trapped-ion quantum computing
- **Advantage**: High-fidelity qubits, all-to-all connectivity
- **Performance**: World's most accurate commercial quantum computers

#### Helios (2025 Launch)
- Most accurate commercial quantum computer
- Nearly doubled qubit count vs predecessor
- Integrated with NVIDIA AI supercomputing

#### Quantum Volume Record
- **Achievement**: 8,388,608 (2^23) - highest recorded to date
- **Significance**: Demonstrates overall system capability
- **Metrics**: Qubit count, coherence, error rates combined

#### Logical Qubits
- Demonstrated error-corrected logical qubits
- Path to fault-tolerant quantum computing
- Commercial viability milestone
