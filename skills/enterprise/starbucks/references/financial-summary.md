# Starbucks Financial Summary

## Fiscal Year 2025 Performance

### Revenue & Profitability

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Net Revenues** | $37.2 billion | $36.2 billion | +3% |
| **Operating Income** | $2.9 billion | $5.4 billion | -46% |
| **Operating Margin** | 7.9% | 15.0% | -710 bps |
| **Net Earnings** | $1.9 billion | $4.0 billion | -53% |
| **Diluted EPS** | $1.63 | $3.31 | -51% |
| **Core EPS** | $2.13 | $3.31 | -36% |

### Revenue Breakdown

| Segment | FY2025 Revenue | % of Total | Comp Sales |
|---------|----------------|------------|------------|

### Q4 FY2025 Highlights

- Global comparable store sales: Flat (vs. -6% prior year)
- U.S. comparable store sales: Flat
- International comparable store sales: +3%
- China comparable store sales: +1% (transactions +4%)
