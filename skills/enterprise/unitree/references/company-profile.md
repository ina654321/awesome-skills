# Unitree Robotics Company Profile

## Overview

Unitree Robotics (宇树科技) is a Chinese robotics company founded in 2016 that has become a global leader in quadruped and humanoid robotics. Known for delivering high-performance robots at unprecedented price points, Unitree has democratized access to advanced legged robots.

## Founding Story

### Wang Xingxing (王兴兴) — Founder & CEO

**Background:**
- Master's degree in mechanical engineering
- Developed XDog quadruped as a student project in 2016
- Self-taught robotics engineer with focus on low-cost, high-performance systems
- Philosophy: "Making advanced robotics accessible to everyone"

**XDog Origins:**
- Built using low-cost external rotor brushless motors
- Full-degree-of-freedom actuation system
- Custom motor drive board design
- Simulation-based gait validation
- This approach became widely adopted in quadruped robotics

**Key Philosophy:**
- Vertical integration reduces costs
- Technology → Product → Market rapid iteration
- First master quadrupeds, then expand to humanoids
- Annual major updates with continuous improvement

## Competitive Position

### Market Disruption

| Competitor | Product | Price | Unitree Equivalent | Unitree Price |
|------------|---------|-------|-------------------|---------------|
| Boston Dynamics | Spot | $75,000+ | Go2 | $1,600 |
| Boston Dynamics | Atlas | Not for sale | H1 | ~$90,000 |
| Agility Robotics | Digit | $250,000+ | G1 EDU | $40,000 |
| Tesla | Optimus | Not available | G1 | $16,000 |

**Key Competitive Advantages:**
1. **Price-Performance**: 5-10x cost advantage over Western competitors
2. **Vertical Integration**: In-house motors (M107), LiDAR, control systems
3. **Rapid Iteration**: Annual major updates vs. multi-year cycles
4. **Open Ecosystem**: ROS2 support, SDK access, community contributions
5. **Manufacturing Scale**: Mass production capability (1000+ units/month G1)

### Technology Leadership

**M107 Joint Motor:**
- 360 N·m peak torque
- 220 N·m/kg torque density
- Self-developed, cost-optimized
- Enables humanoid mobility at consumer prices

**4D LiDAR L1:**
- Self-developed solid-state LiDAR
- 360° × 90° coverage
- 0.05m minimum detection
- Cost fraction of Velodyne/Ouster

5. **Rapid iteration outperforms perfectionism** — Annual updates vs. multi-year cycles
