**Bulls Say:**
- Dominant AI infrastructure positioning
- VMware integration exceeding targets
- Industry-leading margins and cash generation

- High customer concentration risk
- NVIDIA competition in AI
- High valuation multiple
- Integration execution risk
- Cyclical exposure in non-AI segments
