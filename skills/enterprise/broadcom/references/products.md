# Broadcom Product Portfolio Deep Dive

## Semiconductor Solutions

### 1. AI & Data Center Products

#### Custom AI Accelerators (XPUs)

**Business Model:**
- Co-development with hyperscale customers
- Multi-year engagements with volume commitments
- $100M-300M development cost per generation
- Breakeven at $500M+ annual revenue

**Current Customers:**

| Customer | Product | Generation | Status |
|----------|---------|------------|--------|
| Google | TPU (Tensor Processing Unit) | v5/v6 | In production |
| Meta | MTIA (Meta Training Inference Accelerator) | v2 | Ramping |
| ByteDance | Custom AI chip | Gen 1 | Production |
| Apple | Suspected AI accelerator | - | Development |
| OpenAI | Suspected custom chip | - | Early stage |

**Technology:**
- Process nodes: 3nm, 2nm (leading edge)
- Packaging: CoWoS, advanced 2.5D/3D
- Architecture: Workload-optimized ASICs
- IP: Arm cores, proprietary accelerators

**Competitive Advantages:**
- 2-3x performance/watt vs. general-purpose GPUs for targeted workloads
- Lower TCO at hyperscale
- IP ownership and differentiation
- Supply chain diversification

#### Ethernet Switching

**Market Position:**
- #1 in high-end data center switching silicon
- Dominant share in 100G/200G/400G/800G

**Product Lines:**

| Series | Speed | Use Case |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Tomahawk 5 | 51.2 Tbps | AI/HPC data centers |
| Trident 3-X7 | 12.8 Tbps | Enterprise cloud |
| Helix 5 | 6.4 Tbps | Edge/AI inference |

**Growth Drivers:**
- AI data center buildouts (38% CAGR through 2029)
- Hyperscaler networking upgrades
- Shift from Infiniband to Ethernet for AI

#### AI Networking Components

**Products:**
- Optical transceivers (400G/800G)
- PCIe switches and retimers
- CXL (Compute Express Link) solutions
- NVLink alternative technologies

**Revenue:**
- Combined AI networking + XPUs: $12.2B (FY2024)
- 77% YoY growth (Q1 FY2025)

### 2. Wireless Connectivity

**Market Position:**
- Top-tier supplier for premium smartphones
- Key supplier to Apple and Samsung

**Products:**

| Generation | Standard | Features |
|------------|----------|----------|
| BCM4389 | WiFi 6E | 6GHz support, premium tier |
| BCM4390 | WiFi 7 | 320MHz channels, 5.8 Gbps |

**Customer Integration:**
- iPhone: Primary WiFi/Bluetooth supplier
- Samsung Galaxy: Significant share
- Chinese OEMs: Growing presence

#### RF Front-End

**Components:**
- FBAR filters (bulk acoustic wave)
- Power amplifiers
- Low-noise amplifiers
- RF switches

**Technology Leadership:**
- Proprietary FBAR technology
- Superior filter performance
- 5G mmWave capabilities
