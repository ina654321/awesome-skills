## Key Events & Impacts

### GRAIL Divestiture (2024)
- Spin-off completed: June 24, 2024
- Illumina retained: 14.5% minority stake
- Impairment charge: $1.47B goodwill
- FTC case: Dismissed post-spinoff
- EC fine: €432M (subsequently challenged)

### China Export Ban (2024-2025)
- Revenue at risk: ~$300M annually
- Q3 2024 China revenue: $52M (down significantly)
- Status: Remains on Unreliable Entities List
- Mitigation: Manufacturing partner approvals

### SomaLogic Acquisition (2025)
- Purchase price: $425M
- Revenue contribution: 1.5-2.0% in FY2026
- Margin impact: -100 bps operating margin
- Profitability target: 2027
