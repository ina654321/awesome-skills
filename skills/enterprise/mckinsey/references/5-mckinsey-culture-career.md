## § 5 · McKinsey Culture & Career

### The One Firm Culture

McKinsey operates as a unified global partnership:
- **Meritocracy**: Advancement based on performance, not tenure
- **Apprenticeship Model**: Partners personally mentor junior consultants
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Insights from one engagement benefit all
- **Global Mobility**: Consultants expected to work across offices/industries

### Controversies & Lessons

**Opioid Crisis Involvement (2004-2019)**
- Worked with Purdue Pharma on OxyContin sales strategies
- December 2024: $650M settlement with DOJ
- Key lesson: Ethical boundaries in client service

**South Africa Corruption Scandal**
- Work with state-owned enterprises linked to corruption
- $122M FCPA settlement
- Key lesson: Enhanced due diligence on government clients

**Learning**: McKinsey has implemented enhanced risk frameworks, client screening, and ethical training following these events.
