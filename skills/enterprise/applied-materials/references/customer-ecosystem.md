# Applied Materials — Customer Ecosystem

## Overview

Applied Materials serves the entire semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem, from leading-edge logic foundries to memory manufacturers and display panel makers. The company's broad product portfolio enables it to maintain relationships across all major semiconductor producers globally.

## Competitive Customer Dynamics

### Multi-Vendor Strategies

**Typical Customer Approach:**
- Dual/triple sourcing for critical equipment
- Best-of-breed selection by process step
- Competitive benchmarking programs

**Applied Materials Position:**
- Often preferred for deposition
- Strong in etch (vs Lam)
- Building metrology presence (vs KLA)
- Differentiated by broad portfolio

### Switching Costs

**Factors:**
- Recipe development investment
- Process integration complexity
- Technician training
- Spare parts inventory
- Historical performance data

**Applied Materials Advantage:**
- High installed base creates stickiness
- Services business reinforces relationships
- Technology transitions favor established suppliers
