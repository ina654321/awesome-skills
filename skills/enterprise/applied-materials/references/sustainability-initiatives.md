# Applied Materials — Sustainability Initiatives

## Net Zero 2040 Commitment

Applied Materials has established one of the most ambitious sustainability programs in the semiconductor industry, with a commitment to achieve **Net Zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040**.

### Key Commitments

| Goal | Target | Status (2024) |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 100% Renewable Electricity | 2030 | 73% globally, 100% US |
| 50% Scope 1 & 2 Reduction | 2030 (vs 2019) | 4% above baseline (construction impact) |
| 55% Scope 3 Reduction/Value | 2030 (vs 2019) | 27% improvement from baseline |
| 30% Energy/Wafer Pass | 2030 (vs 2019) | 13% improvement achieved |
| Net Zero Emissions | 2040 | Roadmap defined |

## Program Components

### 1. Operational Emissions (Scope 1 & 2)

**Focus Areas:**

**Facilities Energy:**
- Solar installations (700kW Tainan facility, Sept 2024)
- Largest rooftop solar array in Texas (Austin logistics center)
- Renewable electricity procurement
- Smart building systems

**Process Emissions:**
- PFC (perfluorinated compound) abatement
- Process gas optimization
- HVAC efficiency improvements

**Transportation:**
- Fleet electrification
- Employee commute programs
- Logistics optimization

**Progress:**
- Scope 1 & 2 emissions 4% above 2019 baseline
- Driven by facility construction for capacity expansion
- On track for 2030 targets despite growth

### 2. Value Chain Emissions (Scope 3)

**Category 11: Use of Sold Products**
- Equipment energy consumption in customer fabs
- Represents majority of Scope 3 emissions

**Initiatives:**

**ECO Services:**
- Power and utilities optimization for customers
- Carbon emissions monitoring
- Efficiency improvement programs
- Performance-based energy contracts

**Equipment Energy Efficiency:**
- 13% average improvement in energy per wafer pass (2019-2024)
- Intelligent power management systems
- Idle power reduction features
- Next-gen equipment targets 30% improvement

**Supplier Engagement:**
- SuCCESS2030 program (Supply Chain Certification for Environmental and Social Sustainability)
- 183 suppliers surveyed (top 80% spend)
- 109 suppliers providing site-level emissions data
- 97 suppliers with GHG reduction goals
- 23 suppliers with approved science-based targets

**Progress:**
- Scope 3 emissions down 24% since 2022
- 27% decrease per million USD gross profit (vs 2019)

### 3. SuCCESS2030 Program

**Launched:** 2020
**Duration:** 10-year roadmap

**Objectives:**
1. Strengthen environmental impact of supply chain
2. Improve social sustainability
3. Enable supplier transparency

**Implementation:**
- Annual supplier sustainability surveys
- Third-party emissions calculation support
- One-on-one engagement with major suppliers
- Renewable energy transition support

**2024 Progress:**
- GHG goals collected from all top-spend suppliers
- Enhanced data quality and coverage
- Supplier transition planning initiated

## Net Zero 2040 Playbook

The Net Zero 2040 Playbook outlines Applied Materials' strategic approach to decarbonization across four building blocks:

```
1. Operations and Facilities
   ├── Renewable electricity

2. Product Sustainability
```
