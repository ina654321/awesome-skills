### Company History

**1967-1980: Foundation**
- Founded by Michael McNeilly and others in 1967
- IPO in 1980 (NASDAQ: AMAT)
- Early focus on chemical vapor deposition (CVD) systems

**1980-2000: Expansion**
- Diversified into etch, ion implant, and metrology
- Strategic acquisitions building equipment portfolio
- Global expansion into Asia and Europe

**2000-2010: Technology Leadership**
- Dominant position in semiconductor equipment
- Entry into solar (PV) and display markets
- Development of integrated processing platforms

**2010-Present: Materials Engineering**
- Shift to "Materials Engineering Solutions" positioning
- AI-driven process optimization
- EPIC Center investment ($5B) for R&D leadership
- Sustainability leadership (Net Zero 2040)
