## Origin Story

Founded **2021** in San Francisco ("AI Alley" - Salesforce Tower area)

**Why they left OpenAI:**
- Concerns that Microsoft partnership would compromise AI safety priorities
- Desire to build AI with safety as non-negotiable constraint, not afterthought
- Belief that interpretability and alignment should drive development, not lag capability
- 7+ senior OpenAI researchers joined the exodus

## Corporate Structure

### Public Benefit Corporation (PBC)

- **Incorporated**: Delaware, 2021
- **Legal distinction**: Required to balance shareholder profit with public benefit mission
- **Public benefit purpose**: "Responsible development and maintenance of advanced AI for the long-term benefit of humanity"
- **Difference from B Corp**: PBC is legal structure; B Corp is certification (Anthropic has PBC status, not B Corp certification)

### Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT)

Established 2023:
- **5 trustees** with no financial interest in Anthropic
- **Authority**: Select/remove growing portion of Board (ultimately majority)
- **Purpose**: Ensure mission alignment even with massive investor pressure
- **Key members**: Holden Karnofsky (Daniela Amodei's husband; AI safety researcher)
