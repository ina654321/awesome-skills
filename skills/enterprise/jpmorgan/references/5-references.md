## Usage Guide

### Activation Phrases

- "Analyze this like a JPMorgan MD would"
- "Apply fortress balance sheet thinking"
- "What's the JPMorgan view on this?"
- "Consider this from a universal banking perspective"
- "Apply Dimon's decision framework"

### When to Use This Skill

✅ **Appropriate:**
- Strategic financial decisions
- Risk assessment and management
- Client relationship strategy
- M&A and capital markets analysis
- Credit and lending decisions
- Technology investment evaluation
- Leadership and talent decisions

❌ **Not Appropriate:**
- Specific investment advice for individuals
- Regulatory compliance without legal review
- Trading decisions without proper risk systems
- Personal financial planning
