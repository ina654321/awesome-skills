# JPMorgan Chase Business Segments

## Overview

JPMorgan Chase operates as a **universal bank** with four reportable segments following the 2024 reorganization that combined Corporate & Investment Bank with Commercial Banking.

## Consumer & Community Banking (CCB)

### Mission
Serve consumers and small businesses with best-in-class financial products, powered by technology and delivered with exceptional service.

### Sub-Businesses

| Sub-Business | Description | 2024 Metrics |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Consumer Banking** | Deposits, checking, savings | 82M customers, 2M new accounts |
| **Credit Cards** | Branded and co-brand cards | 10M new accounts, ~20% share ambition |
| **Home Lending** | Mortgages, home equity | Market leader in originations |
| **Auto Finance** | Auto loans and leases | Top 3 national lender |
| **Business Banking** | SMB deposits, lending, cards | 6M small business customers |
| **Wealth Management** | Chase Private Client, JPMWM | Doubled since 2019 |

### Strategic Priorities
1. **Deposit Share Growth**: Gained share in 95% of top 125 markets
2. **Card Leadership**: Targeting 20% outstandings market share
3. **Wealth Expansion**: Plans to double again by 2030
4. **Digital First**: 65M+ digital users, AI-powered personalization

### Key Products
- **Chase Sapphire**: Premium travel rewards (rated best by The Points Guy)
- **Chase Freedom**: Cash back category leader
- **Chase Ink**: #1 small business card (NerdWallet 2025)
- **Chase Private Client**: Affluent segment growth driver
- **United/Marriott Co-brands**: Strong travel partnerships

### Technology Investment
- **$3.2B** technology spend (2025)
- AI/ML risk models delivering **35% value increase**
- **70%** code deployment increase in 2 years
- **20%** reduction in replanned work

## Asset & Wealth Management (AWM)

### Mission
Help institutions, financial intermediaries, and individuals achieve their long-term financial goals through world-class investment management and wealth advisory.

### Sub-Businesses

| Sub-Business | AUM/AUS | Strategy |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| **Asset Management** | $2.8T | Active management, ETFs, alternatives |
| **Global Private Bank** | $1.3T | High-net-worth advisory |
| **Wealth Management** | Growing | Mass affluent (Chase Private Client) |

### 2024 Performance
- **AUM**: $4.1T (+15% YoY)
- **Net Inflows**: $486B (2024)
- **Cumulative Inflows (2023-24)**: $976B
- **Management Fees**: +21% YoY
- **ROE**: 32%

### Growth Ambitions (by 2030+)

| Asset Class | Current | Target |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| Equities | $1.0T | $2.0T |
| Fixed Income | $1.0T | $1.5T |
| Active ETFs | $162B | $1.0T |
| Alternatives | $575B | $1.0T |
| Global Private Bank | $3.0T | $5.0T |

### Investment Capabilities
- **Equities**: Fundamental, quantitative, ESG strategies
- **Fixed Income**: Rates, credit, municipals, emerging markets
- **Alternatives**: Private equity, real estate, infrastructure, hedge funds
- **ETFs**: Rapidly growing active ETF platform
- **Multi-Asset**: Asset allocation, target date, outcome-oriented
