# JPMorgan Chase Historical Timeline

## Origins & Foundation

### The Chase Manhattan Legacy
- **1799**: Bank of the Manhattan Company founded by Aaron Burr (originally water utility)
- **1804**: Pivot to banking
- **1955**: Chase National + Bank of Manhattan = Chase Manhattan Bank
- **1996**: Chemical Bank acquires Chase Manhattan (retains Chase name)

### The J.P. Morgan Heritage
- **1871**: Drexel, Morgan & Co. founded
- **1895**: J.P. Morgan & Co. becomes independent
- **1913**: J.P. Morgan Sr. dies; Jack Morgan assumes leadership
- **1933**: Glass-Steagall Act separates commercial and investment banking
- **1940**: Morgan Stanley spins off (investment banking)
- **1959**: J.P. Morgan becomes commercial bank

## The Modern Era: JPMorgan Chase

### 2000-2005: Formation and Dimon Era Begins

| Year | Event | Significance |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **2000** | Chase Manhattan acquires J.P. Morgan & Co. | Creates J.P. Morgan Chase & Co. |
| **2000** | Jamie Dimon becomes CEO of Bank One | Turnaround begins |
| **2004** | JPMorgan Chase acquires Bank One | Dimon becomes President/COO |
| **2006** | Dimon becomes CEO and Chairman | New leadership era |
| **2006** | First Investor Day | Institutionalizes transparency |

### 2008-2010: Financial Crisis

| Date | Event | Impact |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Mar 2008** | Bear Stearns acquisition | $10/share; $30B Fed backstop |
| **Sep 2008** | Washington Mutual acquisition | $1.9B; largest bank failure in US history |
| **2008-2009** | Crisis navigation | Fortress balance sheet tested |
| **2010** | Becomes #1 US bank by assets | $2.1 trillion |

**Bear Stearns Details:**
- Acquisition price: $10/share (later $2, then $10)
- Fed provided $29 billion non-recourse loan
- Absorbed significant toxic assets
- Strategic entry to prime brokerage

**Washington Mutual Details:**
- $307 billion in assets
- $188 billion in deposits
- 2,200+ branches added
- Expanded footprint to California, Florida

### 2011-2019: Growth and Diversification

| Year | Event | Significance |

| **2011** | #1 in Markets revenue | Trading dominance |
| **2012** | #1 credit card issuer | By outstandings |
| **2014** | AI/ML fraud detection launch | Technology leadership |
| **2016** | #1 global bank by market cap | Investor recognition |
| **2017** | Jamie Dimon calls Bitcoin "fraud" | Crypto skepticism |

**London Whale Incident (2012):**
- $6 billion trading loss
- Risk management lessons

- Dimon: "We shot ourselves in the foot"

### 2023: First Republic Acquisition

| Date | Event | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Mar 2023** | Silicon Valley Bank fails | Regional banking crisis |
| **Mar 2023** | Signature Bank fails | Crypto-related bank |
| **May 1, 2023** | First Republic acquisition | FDIC-assisted transaction |

**First Republic Deal Terms:**
- Purchase price: $10.6 billion
- Assets acquired: $173 billion loans, $30B securities
- Deposits assumed: $92 billion
- FDIC loss-sharing on mortgages
- Expected $500M+ annual incremental net income

**Strategic Rationale:**
- High-net-worth client base
- Coastal geography (CA, NY)
- Jumbo mortgage expertise
- Crisis-driven opportunity
