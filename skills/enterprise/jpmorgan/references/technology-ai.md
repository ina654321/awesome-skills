# JPMorgan Chase Technology & AI

## Overview

JPMorgan Chase operates as much as a **technology company** as a financial institution. With an $18 billion annual technology budget and 50,000+ technologists, we maintain one of the largest private technology infrastructures in the world.

> *"Our tech spend is 10% of revenues—which is less than most other companies, by the way. It's table stakes. It will be for the rest of eternity."* — Jamie Dimon

## Kinexys (formerly Onyx)

### Evolution
- **2017**: Onyx blockchain initiative launched
- **2019**: JPM Coin introduced
- **2020**: Kinexys Digital Payments live
- **2024 (Nov)**: Rebranded from Onyx to Kinexys

### Current Capabilities

| Feature | Description | Status |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| **JPM Coin** | USD deposit token for 24/7 settlement | Live |
| **Euro Coin** | EUR-denominated settlement | Live (2024) |
| **On-Chain FX** | Automated multi-currency settlement | Live (Q1 2025) |
| **Programmability** | Conditional payments, smart contracts | Live (May 2024) |

### Transaction Volumes
- **$1.5 trillion** cumulative transactions (since 2020)
- **$2 billion** average daily volume
- **10x growth** in payment transactions (2023-2024)
- **Multi-billion dollar days** post-programmability launch

### Key Clients
- BlackRock (asset management)
- Ant International (fintech)
- Siemens (industrial)
- Mastercard (payments network)
- B2C2 (crypto market maker)
- Coinbase (exchange)

### JPMD (JPM Deposit Token)
- **Launch**: June 2025 proof-of-concept
- **Blockchain**: Coinbase Base (Ethereum L2)
- **Purpose**: Alternative to stablecoins for institutional clients
- **Strategy**: Dual approach—proprietary Kinexys + public blockchain JPMD
