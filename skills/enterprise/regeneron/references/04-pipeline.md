# Clinical Development Pipeline

## Overview

Regeneron maintains a robust pipeline with approximately **40 investigational candidates** in clinical development, spanning multiple therapeutic areas and modalities. The pipeline reflects Regeneron's platform-driven approach with a mix of antibody-based therapies, bispecifics, and emerging modalities.

## Pipeline Statistics

| Phase | Count | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Phase 1** | 12+ | Early safety and PK/PD |
| **Phase 2** | 28 | Proof of concept, dose finding |
| **Phase 3** | 20 | Pivotal efficacy trials |
| **Pre-registration** | 8 | BLA/NDA preparation |
| **Approved (2024)** | 3 | New approvals/indications |

## Pipeline Strategic Themes

### 1. Maximizing Blockbusters

**Dupixent Lifecycle:**
- Expand to 10+ indications
- Extend into pediatric populations
- Geographic expansion
- Combination with itepekimab

**Eylea HD Transition:**
- Migrate patients from Eylea
- Extend dosing intervals
- Defend against biosimilars

**Libtayo Build:**
- Adjuvant and neoadjuvant settings
- Combination regimens
- New tumor types

### 2. Next-Generation Platforms

**Bispecific Antibodies:**
- CD3 engagers in hematology
- Costimulatory molecules in solid tumors
- Novel target combinations

**Genetic Medicines:**
- Cell therapy capability build
- Gene editing partnerships
- RNAi collaborations

### 3. New Therapeutic Areas

**Obesity/Metabolic:**
- GDF-15 pathway
- GLP-1 combinations
- RGC-validated targets

**Pain:**
- NGF pathway re-evaluation
- Non-opioid alternatives
- Chronic pain management

### 4. Geographic Expansion

- Japan: Dupixent COPD approved (March 2025)
- China: Market development
- Emerging markets: Access strategies
