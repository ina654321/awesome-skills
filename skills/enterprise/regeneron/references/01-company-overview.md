## Corporate Culture

### Core Values

1. **Science-Driven:** "Follow the science" is the guiding principle
2. **Technology-Enabled:** Platforms multiply human capability
3. **Patient-Focused:** Transform lives of people with serious diseases
4. **Collaborative:** Partnerships amplify impact
5. **Entrepreneurial:** "We won't let bureaucracy block good ideas"

### Culture Highlights

- **#1 Biopharma Employer** (Science Magazine survey, 2012, 2020s)
- **Offices intermingled with labs** - scientists and commercial work together
- **Founder-led** for 35+ years with consistent vision
- **Long-tenured leadership** - many executives 10+ years at company

### Tarrytown Campus

- **Location:** Former Union Carbide site, Westchester County, NY
- **Size:** 1+ million square feet
- **Facilities:** Research labs, manufacturing, administrative offices
- **Expansion:** Ongoing growth with state tax credits
