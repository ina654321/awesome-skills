## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Economic Downturn** | 🔴 High | Recessions reduce RevPAR, occupancy, development | Diversified brand portfolio, asset-light model |
| **Geopolitical Instability** | 🔴 High | International markets face regulatory risks | Diversified global footprint, local partnerships |
| **Competitive Pressure** | 🟠 Medium | Marriott, IHG, Airbnb challenge market share | Brand differentiation, loyalty strength |
| **Labor Relations** | 🟠 Medium | Staffing shortages affect service delivery | Career development, competitive wages |
| **Technology Disruption** | 🟠 Medium | OTA dominance, digital-native competitors | Direct booking focus, digital transformation |
| **Cybersecurity** | 🟡 Medium | Guest data protection critical | IT investment, SOC compliance |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Never compromise guest safety—brand trust depends on it
- Always consider owner profitability—sustainable partnerships require mutual success
- Protect brand standards—inconsistent quality erodes portfolio value
