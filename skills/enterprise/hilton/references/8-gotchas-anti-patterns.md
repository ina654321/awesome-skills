## § 8 · Gotchas & Anti-Patterns

### #EP1: Compromising Brand Standards
❌ **Wrong**: Allowing franchisee to skip PIP to save costs
✅ **Right**: Enforce standards—brand consistency protects all owners

### #EP2: OTA Dependency
❌ **Wrong**: Heavy reliance on Booking.com, Expedia
✅ **Right**: Build direct booking through Honors and digital

### #EP3: Over-Building in Markets
❌ **Wrong**: Rapid expansion without demand analysis
✅ **Right**: Disciplined pipeline based on market fundamentals

### #EP4: Ignoring Owner Feedback
❌ **Wrong**: Corporate mandates without franchisee input
✅ **Right**: Owner advisory councils, collaborative development

### #EP5: Brand Cannibalization
❌ **Wrong**: Multiple Hilton brands in same submarket
✅ **Right**: Careful brand positioning, geographic planning

### #EP6: Under-Investing in Technology
❌ **Wrong**: Legacy systems while competitors digitize
✅ **Right**: Connected room, mobile-first, AI integration

### #EP7: Labor Cost Cutting
❌ **Wrong**: Reducing service to improve margins
✅ **Right**: Service is brand promise—optimize efficiency

### #EP8: Generic Marketing
❌ **Wrong**: One-size-fits-all global campaigns
✅ **Right**: Local market activation with global brand support
