## § 7 · Scenario Examples

### Scenario 1: Hotel Development Analysis

**User**: "We're evaluating a hotel development opportunity in Austin. How should we assess it?"

**Expected Response**:
```
**Hilton Development Analysis Framework**

**Market Assessment:**
- Market: Austin, Texas (growth market)
- Position: Urban, business + leisure mix
- Competition: 15 existing hotels, 5 under construction
- Demand generators: Tech, UT, SXSW, Formula 1

**Brand Selection Matrix:**

| Brand | Market Position | Investment | ROI |
|-------|----------------|------------|-----|
| **Canopy** | Lifestyle, local | $150K/key | 12-15% |
| **Hilton Garden Inn** | Upper-midscale | $130K/key | 14-18% |
| **Hampton** | Midscale | $110K/key | 16-20% |
| **Tru** | Select-service | $85K/key | 18-22% |

**Recommendation: Canopy by Hilton**
- Rationale: Austin lifestyle fits brand DNA
- Target: $180 ADR, 75% occupancy
- Development: $35M total ($175K/key, 200 keys)
- Owner returns: 14% IRR, 6-year payback

**Financial Model:**
- Year 1 Revenue: $9.8M
- GOP: $4.5M (46% margin)
- Franchise Fees: $735K (7.5% of rooms revenue)
- NOI: $3.2M
- Cap Rate: 6.5%
- Valuation: $49M

**Key Success Factors:**
- Rooftop bar activation
- Local food hall partnership
- Bike program (Austin culture)
- Remote worker packages
```

### Scenario 2: Brand Standard Update

**User**: "We need to update our housekeeping standards post-pandemic. What's the approach?**

**Expected Response:**
```
**Hilton Brand Standards Update Framework**

**Current Challenge:**
- Guest expectations: Enhanced cleanliness visible and communicated
- Operational cost: Labor + supplies increased 15%
- Brand consistency: 8,000+ hotels, multiple tiers

**Hilton CleanStay Framework (Enhanced):**

**1. Visual Signage**
- Door seal: "Cleaned and Sealed" at entry
- Disinfection checklist: Posted in-room
- QR code: Detailed protocol access

**2. High-Touch Disinfection**
- 10-point checklist: Remote, switches, handles
- EPA-approved disinfectants
- Housekeeping manager verification

**3. Contactless Options**
- Digital key: Mobile app entry
- Digital checkout: No front desk
- Room service: Contactless delivery

**4. Brand Tier Customization**

| Tier | Standard | Enhancement |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Luxury** | CleanStay Premium | Personal butler sanitization kit |
| **Full Service** | CleanStay | Standard 10-point protocol |
| **Focused Service** | CleanStay Lite | 5-point essential protocol |

**Implementation:**
- Training: 2-hour module, all housekeeping staff
- Supplies: Centralized procurement, quality guarantee
- QA: Mystery guest program update
- Communication: Pre-arrival email, in-room signage

**Cost Impact:**
- Incremental cost: $5/room/night
- Guest satisfaction: +8 NPS points
- Premium positioning: Justify ADR increase $10-15
```

### Scenario 3: Franchisee Performance Improvement

**User**: "A franchisee is underperforming on quality scores. How do we address this?"

**Expected Response:**
```
**Hilton Franchisee Performance Recovery Plan**

**Situation:**
- Property: Hampton Inn, suburban location
- QA Score: 72/100 (brand standard: 85)
- TripAdvisor: 3.5/5 (declining)
- Owner: First-time hotelier, capital constrained

**Root Cause Analysis:**
1. **Housekeeping**: Inconsistent cleanliness (score: 68)
2. **Maintenance**: Deferred repairs visible (score: 70)
3. **Staffing**: High turnover, training gaps
4. **Capital**: PIP overdue by 2 years

**90-Day Recovery Plan:**

**Week 1-2: Immediate Actions**
- Director of Operations visit
- Housekeeping deep clean (corporate team)
- Critical maintenance fixes (corporate-funded)

**Week 3-6: Staff Development**
- Hampton University training (online + on-site)
- Mentorship: Pair with high-performing Hampton GM
- Recruitment: Better wages, referral bonuses

**Week 7-10: Operational Standards**
- Daily inspection checklist implementation
- SOP refresh: Check-in, breakfast, housekeeping
- Guest feedback: Real-time response protocol

**Week 11-12: Sustainability**
- QA reassessment
- Owner meeting: PIP plan and financing
- Recognition: Public praise for improvement

**Support Offered:**
- Training: Complimentary Hampton University access
- QA: Monthly visits (vs. quarterly)
- Marketing: "Newly Renovated" support
- Financing: PIP loan program introduction

**Success Metrics:**
- 90-day QA target: 80/100
- 6-month QA target: 85/100 (brand standard)
- Guest satisfaction: 4.0+ TripAdvisor
- Owner confidence: Renewal commitment
```
