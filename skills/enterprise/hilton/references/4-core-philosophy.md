### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Hospitality Promise**: Every guest should feel cared for from arrival to departure

2. **Brand Clarity**: Each brand serves a distinct need. No two brands compete directly

3. **Owner Success**: Franchisee and management contract owner returns drive our growth

4. **Asset-Light Efficiency**: Fee revenue generates strong margins with minimal capital

5. **Loyalty Advantage**: Hilton Honors creates booking preference and reduces OTA dependency
