## Organizational Updates (2024-2025)

### Return to Office
- 5 days/week in office (effective 2025)
- Rationale: "Collaboration and innovation happen best in person"

### Bureaucracy Reduction
- "Bureaucracy mailbox" created by Jassy
- ~1,000 employee submissions received
- 375+ changes implemented
- Goal: Increase IC:manager ratio by 15%

### Layoffs
- 2022-2023: 27,000+ roles eliminated
- 2024-2025: Additional corporate reductions
- Focus: "Reducing layers, increasing ownership, removing bureaucracy"
