## Business Segments

### FedEx Express
- **Revenue:** ~$42B (48% of total)
- **Focus:** Time-definite express delivery
- **Aircraft:** 700+ (world's largest cargo airline)
- **Daily Volume:** ~6.5M packages
- **Key Services:** Priority Overnight, Standard Overnight, 2Day, International Priority

### FedEx Ground
- **Revenue:** ~$28B (32% of total)
- **Focus:** Cost-effective ground parcel delivery
- **Fleet:** 100,000+ vehicles
- **Key Services:** Ground, Home Delivery, Ground Economy

### FedEx Freight
- **Revenue:** ~$9B (10% of total)
- **Focus:** LTL (Less-Than-Truckload) freight
- **Network:** 370+ service centers
- **Key Services:** Priority, Economy, International

### FedEx Services
- **Revenue:** ~$9B (10% of total)
- **Focus:** Corporate support, technology, sales
- **Includes:** FedEx Office, FedEx Logistics, FedEx Dataworks

## Memphis SuperHub Details

### Specifications
- **Location:** Memphis International Airport (MEM)
- **Size:** 940 acres
- **Facility Space:** 3.6 million square feet
- **Aircraft Gates:** 171
- **Conveyor System:** 84 miles
- **Sort Capacity:** 484,000 packages/hour
- **Nightly Flights:** 150+ aircraft (10 PM - 5 AM)
- **Daily Volume:** 2.4M+ packages
- **Night Sort Volume:** 1.5M+ packages
- **Landing Rate:** 1 aircraft every 40 seconds at peak

### Recent Investments
- **Secondary 25 Facility:** Opened October 2024
- **Size:** 1.3 million square feet
- **Conveyor:** 11 miles
- **Capacity:** 56,000 packages/hour
- **Features:** Fully automated, six-sided scanners, AI-powered robotic arms

### Geographic Advantage
- Within 4-hour flight of 95% of U.S. population
- Strategic central U.S. location minimizes flight times
- All-weather operational capability
