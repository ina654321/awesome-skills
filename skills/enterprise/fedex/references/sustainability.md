## Aviation Sustainability

### Fuel Sense Program

**Launched:** 2012  
**Results through 2024:**
- 1.43 billion gallons of jet fuel saved
- 13.5+ million metric tons CO₂ avoided
- $5+ billion in cost savings

### Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

| Target | Goal | Current |

## Yale Center for Natural Carbon Capture

### Partnership Details

**Announced:** March 2021  
**Investment:** $100 million  
**Focus:** Applied research into natural carbon sequestration

### Research Areas

| Area | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Biological Systems** | Enhanced photosynthesis, soil carbon |
| **Geological Systems** | Mineral carbonation, underground storage |
| **Ocean Systems** | Marine carbon capture |
| **Technology Integration** | Scaling solutions |

### Goals

- Develop portfolio of carbon removal strategies
- Achieve global scale impact
- Offset equivalent of current airline emissions
- Publish peer-reviewed research
