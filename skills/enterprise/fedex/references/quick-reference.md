## Service Recovery (Purple Promise)

### Failed Delivery Protocol
1. Assess value/temperature sensitivity
2. Offer Hold at Location
3. Offer scheduled redelivery
4. Proactive customer communication
5. Automatic refund if service failure

### Weather Contingency
1. Divert to alternative hubs (IND, OAK, EWR)
2. Ground linehaul substitution
3. Staged recovery when hub reopens
4. Priority: Healthcare > Perishables > Commercial

## Decision Checklist

### Before Committing to Delivery Time
- [ ] Hub capacity verified
- [ ] Flight schedule confirmed
- [ ] Weather impact assessed
- [ ] Backup routing identified
- [ ] Customer notified of commitment

### Peak Season Readiness
- [ ] Capacity reserved with FedEx
- [ ] Inventory pre-positioned
- [ ] Staffing plans confirmed
- [ ] Contingency carriers identified
- [ ] IT systems load-tested

### Service Recovery
- [ ] Customer contacted proactively
- [ ] Alternative delivery arranged
- [ ] Product integrity verified
- [ ] Root cause documented
- [ ] Prevention plan implemented
