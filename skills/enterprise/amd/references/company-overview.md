## The AMD Turnaround Story

### 2014: Rock Bottom
- Stock: ~$2
- Market cap: ~$2 billion
- Data center share: <1%
- Burning cash, $1B+ debt
- No competitive products

### 2017: Ryzen Revolution
- Zen architecture launch
- 52% IPC uplift vs previous gen
- Return to competitiveness

### 2019: Chiplet Innovation
- Zen 2 with chiplet design
- Surpassed Intel IPC
- Cost leadership established

### 2022: Market Cap Milestone
- Surpassed Intel market cap
- Xilinx acquisition ($49B)
- Data center share: 20%+

### 2024: AI Era Entry
- MI300X launch
- $5B+ AI revenue
- Data center: 50% of revenue
- Data center share: 25%+
