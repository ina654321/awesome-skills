## Leadership Priorities

### 2020-Present: Connectivity Transformation

1. **WarnerMedia Divestiture** (2022)
   - Recognized media/content not core to connectivity mission
   - $43B transaction with Discovery, Inc.
   - Used proceeds for debt reduction and network investment

2. **DirecTV Spinoff** (2021)
   - Exited satellite TV majority ownership
   - Retained 70% through TPG partnership
   - Focused capital on 5G and fiber

3. **Fiber Acceleration**
   - 7 consecutive years of 1M+ fiber net adds
   - 28.9M passings (2024) → 50M target (2029)
   - "America's fastest internet" (Ookla)

4. **5G Deployment**
   - Nationwide coverage with C-band capacity layer
   - 270M+ POPs covered
   - FirstNet public safety network expansion

5. **Debt Reduction**
   - Reduced debt by $40B+ since 2022
   - Dividend reset to fund network investment
   - Improved financial flexibility
