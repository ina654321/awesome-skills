## Business Segments

### Communications (97% of Revenue)

**Mobility (Wireless)**
- Revenue: $85.3 billion (2024)
- Operating Income: $26.3 billion
- Postpaid phone net adds: 1.9M (2024)
- Postpaid phone churn: 0.85%
- 5G Coverage: 270M+ POPs (C-band)

**Consumer Wireline**
- Revenue: $14.4 billion
- Operating Income: $869M
- Fiber broadband: 9.3M subscribers
- Fiber net adds: 1.0M (2024)
- Internet Air (FWA): 1.3M+ subscribers

**Business Wireline**
- Revenue: $17.9 billion
- Operating Income: -$88M (transitioning to fiber)

## Strategic Priorities (2025-2029)

1. **Fiber Expansion**: 28.9M locations → 50M by 2029
2. **5G Enhancement**: 270M POPs → 300M+ by 2026
3. **Copper Retirement**: Majority retired by 2029
4. **Convergence**: 50% fiber-wireless bundle rate
5. **$250B Infrastructure Commitment**: 5-year investment plan
