# Lam Research - Financial Data Reference

## Executive Summary

Lam Research Corporation (NASDAQ: LRCX) delivered record financial performance in fiscal year 2025, with revenues reaching $20.6 billion and non-GAAP diluted EPS of $4.89. The company's dominant position in semiconductor etch and deposition equipment, combined with strong AI-driven demand for HBM and advanced logic, drove exceptional results.

## Investment Thesis Summary

### Bull Case
- Dominant market share in critical etch processes (>75% HAR etch)
- AI-driven demand for HBM and advanced logic
- Technology inflections (GAA, 3D NAND, advanced packaging) expand SAM
- High-margin CSBG business provides stability
- Strong free cash flow generation supports shareholder returns
- Management excellence under Tim Archer's leadership

### Bear Case
- China export restrictions limit addressable market
- Memory capex cyclicality creates revenue volatility
- Customer concentration risk with top memory/foundry customers
- Valuation premium vulnerable to multiple compression
- Potential technology disruption from new architectures

### Consensus View
Lam Research is a high-quality compounder with:
- Technology leadership in growing markets
- Strong competitive moats (process recipes, installed base)
- Excellent management and capital allocation
- Reasonable valuation for growth prospects
- Short-term headwinds from China, but long-term trajectory intact
