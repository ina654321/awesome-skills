# MyLowe's Pro Rewards Program

## Program Overview

Launched February 2025 as the evolution of the MVPs Pro Rewards & Partnership Program.

**Mission:** Save Pros valuable time and money with rewards from the first dollar spent.

## Program Structure

### Tier System

| Tier | Qualification | Key Benefits |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Bronze | Sign-up | Earn points, member deals |
| Silver | $2,500 annual spend | Enhanced rewards, business tools |
| Gold | $7,500 annual spend | Premium rewards, priority service |
| Platinum Pro | Credit Card holders | 5% everyday savings, highest tier |

### Earning Structure

- **Base Rate:** 1 point per $1 spent
- **Bonus Events:** Double points on featured categories
- **No Thresholds:** Earn from first dollar

### Redemption Options

- MyLowe's Money (500 points = $5)
- Gift cards
- Sports memorabilia
- Business merchandise

## Exclusive Pro Benefits

### Financial Benefits
- **MyLowe's Pro Rewards Credit Card:** 5% everyday savings on eligible purchases
- **Volume Discounts:** Automatic on qualifying purchases
- **Tax-Exempt Purchasing:** Seamless processing

### Operational Benefits
- **Dedicated Pro Desk:** Knowledgeable associates
- **Pro Parking:** Reserved spaces at stores
- **Will-Call:** Pre-staged orders
- **Loaders:** Assistance with bulky products

### Digital Benefits
- **Quick Reorder:** Replicate past purchases
- **Job Lists:** Organize multi-phase projects
- **Purchase History:** Detailed reporting
- **API Access:** Integration with estimating software

## Pro Extended Aisle (PEX)

Direct supplier integration for special orders:
- Real-time inventory visibility
- Instant quoting
- Jobsite and rooftop delivery
- Expanded catalog access

**Status:** Rolling out to all 1,759 stores by Q3 2025
