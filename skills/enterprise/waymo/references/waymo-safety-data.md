# Waymo Safety Data Reference

## Latest Safety Statistics (December 2025)

### Rider-Only Autonomous Miles
- **170.7 million miles** driven without human driver (as of Dec 2025)
- **467,000 miles per day** average
- **20+ million trips** completed
- **3.8 million passenger hours**

### Crash Rate Comparison vs Human Drivers

| Metric | Waymo Performance | Human Benchmark | Improvement |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Crashes with serious injury | Baseline | Baseline | **92% fewer** |
| Crashes with airbag deployment | Baseline | Baseline | **83% fewer** |
| Any injury crashes | Baseline | Baseline | **82% fewer** |
| Pedestrian injury crashes | Baseline | Baseline | **92% fewer** |
| Cyclist injury crashes | Baseline | Baseline | **85% fewer** |
| Motorcycle injury crashes | Baseline | Baseline | **81% fewer** |
| Intersection injury crashes | Baseline | Baseline | **96% fewer** |

Source: Waymo Safety Impact Hub, peer-reviewed study in Traffic Injury Prevention journal

### Insurance Claims Analysis (Swiss Re Partnership)

Over 25 million miles analyzed (through July 2024):
- **86% reduction** in property damage claims vs human drivers
- **90% reduction** in bodily injury claims vs human drivers
- Only 2 pending bodily injury claims (both likely external fault)
