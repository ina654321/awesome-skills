## Geographic Strategy

### Phase 1: Sun Belt (Completed)
Warm-weather cities for initial deployment
- Phoenix, SF, LA, Austin, Atlanta, Miami

### Phase 2: Expansion Cities (2026)
Diverse environments for technology validation
- Snow: Washington D.C., Detroit, Denver
- Humid subtropical: Dallas, Houston, Orlando
- Mountain: Denver
- International: London, Tokyo

### Phase 3: Scale (2027+)
Target: 20+ cities, 1M rides/week
