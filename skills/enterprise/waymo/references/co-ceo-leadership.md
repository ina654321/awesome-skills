# Waymo Co-CEO Leadership

## Leadership Structure

Waymo operates under a unique **dual-CEO structure** established in April 2021.

### Tekedra Mawakana — Co-CEO (Business Operations)

**Background:**
- Law degree, intellectual property specialization
- 12 years at AOL
- Deputy General Counsel at Yahoo!
- Head of Global Government Relations at eBay
- Joined Waymo 2017 as VP Public Policy
- Promoted to COO, then Co-CEO April 2021

**Responsibilities:**
- Business operations and strategy
- Regulatory and government affairs
- Partnerships (OEMs, fleet operators, Uber/Lyft)
- Public policy and community engagement
- Fundraising and investor relations

**Leadership Style:**
- Legal/regulatory expertise drives cautious, compliance-first approach
- Focus on public trust and transparency
- Scale with safety mantra
- "Aggressive scaling with safety at the forefront"

**Key Quote:**
> "We're not validating a concept anymore—we're scaling a commercial reality."

### Dmitri Dolgov — Co-CEO (Technology)

**Background:**
- Computer science PhD
- Original member of Google Self-Driving Car Project (2009)
- Technical lead through entire Waymo history
- Former CTO before Co-CEO promotion

**Responsibilities:**
- Technology vision and execution
- Waymo Driver development
- AI/ML research and engineering
- Sensor and hardware development
- Safety validation and testing

**Leadership Style:**
- Deep technical expertise in autonomous systems
- Data-driven decision making
- Simulation-first validation philosophy
- Long-term technology roadmap

**Key Quote:**
> "I believe a year from now, we'll look back and realize Waymo was just getting started."

## Leadership Philosophy

### Dual-CEO Model Benefits

| Aspect | Mawakana | Dolgov | Combined |
|--------|----------|--------|----------|
| **Focus** | Business, regulatory, partnerships | Technology, engineering, AI | Complete coverage |
| **Background** | Legal, policy, operations | Computer science, robotics | Diverse expertise |
| **Approach** | Cautious, stakeholder-focused | Innovative, data-driven | Balanced risk |
| **External** | Investors, regulators, media | Technical community, recruiting | Full representation |

### Shared Principles

1. **Safety Above All:** Both CEOs emphasize safety as the non-negotiable priority
2. **Transparency:** Public safety data sharing via Safety Impact Hub
3. **Regulatory Partnership:** Work with, not around, authorities
4. **Scale Responsibly:** Growth must not compromise safety
5. **Data-Driven:** Ground decisions in 170M+ miles of evidence
