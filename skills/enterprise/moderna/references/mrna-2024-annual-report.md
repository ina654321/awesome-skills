- Respiratory vaccines: ~35%
- Oncology: ~20%

- Other: ~10%

## Key Business Priorities (2025)

1. **Revenue Growth**: Drive Spikevax and mRESVIA sales
2. **Product Approvals**: Up to 10 approvals through 2027
3. **Cost Reduction**: $1.5B by 2027
4. **Cash Break-even**: Target 2028

## Risk Factors

- COVID-19 vaccine market contraction
- Competition in RSV market (GSK, Pfizer)
- Regulatory approval delays
- Manufacturing scale-up challenges
- Patent litigation (LNP technology)
