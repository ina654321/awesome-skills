# Moderna Q4 2024 Earnings Summary

## Report Date: February 14, 2025

## Financial Results

| Metric | Q4 2024 | Q4 2023 | Change |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| Total Revenue | $1.0B | $2.8B | -64% |
| Net Product Sales | $938M | $2.8B | -66% |
| Net Loss | ($1.1B) | $217M | N/M |
| Loss Per Share | ($2.91) | $0.55 | N/M |

### Cost of Sales Breakdown

- Inventory write-downs: $193M

## Cash Position

| Metric | December 31, 2024 | September 30, 2024 |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Cash & Investments | $9.5B | $9.2B |

## Key Pipeline Updates

### Regulatory Submissions Filed
1. **mRNA-1283** (Next-gen COVID): BLA submitted, PDUFA May 31, 2025
2. **mRNA-1345** (RSV 18-59): BLA submitted for high-risk adults
3. **mRNA-1083** (Flu+COVID combo): BLA submitted for 50+ adults

### Phase 3 Data Readouts
- Next-gen COVID: Positive efficacy and immunogenicity data
- RSV 18-59: Positive Phase 3 data in high-risk adults
- Flu+COVID combo: Positive Phase 3 data
- Seasonal flu (mRNA-1010): Phase 3 ongoing

## CEO Commentary (Stéphane Bancel)

> "We have made progress in 2024 across our late-stage pipeline and cost reduction 
> efforts. Our team successfully filed three Biologics License Applications in the 
> final months of the year and reduced our costs by 27 percent compared to 2023.
>
> In 2025, we remain focused on driving sales, delivering up to 10 product approvals 
> through 2027, and expanding cost efficiencies across our business. By the end of 
> 2025, we aim to remove nearly $1 billion in costs."

## 2025 Strategic Priorities

1. **Commercial Execution**
   - Drive Spikevax seasonal uptake
   - Expand mRESVIA adoption

2. **Regulatory Approvals**
   - mRNA-1283 approval (PDUFA May 31)
   - mRNA-1083 approval
   - mRNA-1345 label expansion

3. **Cost Reduction**
   - $1.5B by 2027
   - Workforce restructuring (10% reduction)
   - Manufacturing efficiency

4. **Pipeline Advancement**
   - Phase 3 readouts for CMV, norovirus
   - mRNA-4157 Phase 3 progress
   - Rare disease pivotal trials

## Workforce Update (July 2025)

- Announced 10% workforce reduction (~800 employees)
- Target headcount: <5,000 by year-end 2025
- Cost reduction: Part of $1.5B plan
- Focus: Align cost structure with business realities
