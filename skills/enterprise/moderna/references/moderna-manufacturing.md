## Norwood Technology Center (MTC)

### Overview
- **Location**: Norwood, Massachusetts
- **Original Opening**: 2018
- **Original Size**: 200,000 sq ft
- **Current Size**: ~650,000 sq ft (expanded 2021)
- **Employees**: 2,200+ (as of 2024)

### Capabilities
- mRNA drug substance production
- Lipid nanoparticle (LNP) formulation
- Fill/finish operations
- Quality control testing
- Clinical and commercial supply

### Recent Developments

**December 2024: Campus Purchase**
- Moderna purchased the MTC campus (land and buildings)
- Provides operational flexibility and long-term stability
- Previously leased facilities

**November 2025: $140M Expansion Announced**
- Onshore drug product manufacturing
- Complete full US manufacturing loop under one roof
- Construction: Started 2025
- Completion: H1 2027
- Jobs: Hundreds of skilled biomanufacturing positions

**CEO Quote (Stéphane Bancel):**
> "By onshoring drug product manufacturing to our campus in Norwood, 
> Massachusetts, we have completed the full manufacturing loop under 
> one roof in the US. As an American company committed to building and 
> producing in America, we are proud to strengthen our domestic footprint."

## Manufacturing Process

### mRNA Drug Substance Production

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ mRNA MANUFACTURING WORKFLOW                                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

PHASE 1: TEMPLATE PREPARATION
├── Plasmid DNA production
├── Linearization
└── Purification

PHASE 2: IN VITRO TRANSCRIPTION (IVT)
├── DNA template + NTPs + RNA polymerase
├── mRNA synthesis
└── DNase treatment

PHASE 3: mRNA PURIFICATION
├── Chromatography
├── Filtration
└── Concentration/diafiltration

PHASE 4: LNP FORMULATION
├── mRNA + lipids mixed via microfluidics
├── Encapsulation
├── Particle sizing (80-100 nm target)
└── Sterile filtration

PHASE 5: FILL/FINISH
├── Vial filling
├── Lyophilization (for stable products)
├── Inspection
└── Packaging
```

### Quality Control Testing

| Test | Method | Specification |
|------|--------|---------------|
| mRNA integrity | Capillary electrophoresis | >90% full-length |
| mRNA purity | HPLC | >95% |
| LNP size | Dynamic Light Scattering | 80-100 nm |
| PDI | DLS | <0.2 |
| Encapsulation | RiboGreen assay | >90% |
| Endotoxin | LAL test | <0.5 EU/mL |
| Sterility | USP <71> | Sterile |
