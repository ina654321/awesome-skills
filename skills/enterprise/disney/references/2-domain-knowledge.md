**Key Technology Initiatives:**

**MagicBand+**
- RFID-enabled wearable
- Functions: park entry, Lightning Lane, room key, payments, PhotoPass
- Interactive features: haptic feedback, LED lights for shows
- Bounty Hunter game in Galaxy's Edge

**Disney Genie+**
- Paid skip-the-line service (replaced FastPass)
- Dynamic pricing based on demand
- AI-optimized itinerary planning
- Lightning Lane individual attraction selections

**My Disney Experience App**
- Central guest hub
- Mobile ordering, virtual queues, wait times
- PhotoPass integration
- Room controls (at select resorts)

**Imagineering Innovations:**
- 600+ U.S. patents
- Audio-Animatronics (since 1963)
- Trackless ride vehicles (Rise of the Resistance)
- WEB SLINGERS spider-bots
- Stuntronics (Spider-Man animatronic)

| Hulu | 64.1M | General entertainment, FX, adult |

**Key Metrics:**
- Disney+ ARPU: $8.09 (domestic), $7.67 (international)
- Hulu ARPU: $12.20 (SVOD), $100.02 (Live TV)
- DTC Operating Income: Profitable since FY2024; Q4 FY2025: $352M (+39% YoY)
- Content Integration: Hulu + ESPN tiles within Disney+ app

**Technology Stack:**
- **Personalization**: AI-driven recommendations
- **Ad Tech**: Targeted advertising platform (Disney Real-Time Ad Exchange - DRAX)
- **CDN**: Global content delivery for 4K/HDR
- **DRM**: Multi-DRM for content protection
- **Analytics**: Real-time viewer behavior analysis

**Strategic Priorities:**
1. Unified app experience (Hulu + ESPN on Disney+)
2. Password sharing monetization (2024-2025 rollout)
3. Ad-supported tier growth
4. International local content expansion

### § 2.6 Recent Strategic Moves

**Under Bob Iger (Return: Nov 2022):**

| Year | Initiative | Impact |
|------|------------|--------|
| 2023 | $7.5B cost reduction | Restored profitability |
| 2023 | Hulu full acquisition | Unified content control |
| 2024 | Streaming profitability | DTC now profitable |
| 2024 | $60B 10-year investment | Parks expansion, streaming |
| 2024 | India merger completion | JioStar joint venture (36.84% stake) |
| 2025 | ESPN tile on Disney+ | Sports integration |
| 2025 | ESPN Unlimited launch | Full sports streaming (Aug 2025) |
| 2026 | CEO succession | Planned transition |

**India Strategic Shift:**
- **JioStar Formation**: Nov 2024 merger of Star India with Reliance's Viacom18
- **Deal Value**: $8.5 billion joint venture
- **Disney Stake**: 36.84%
- **Combined Assets**: 120+ TV channels, JioHotstar streaming platform
- **Market Position**: ~750M viewers, dominant in cricket (IPL, ICC)

**Content Strategy:**
- Quality over quantity
- Franchise focus (Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar, Disney)
- Fewer, bigger bets
- Theatrical + streaming hybrid releases
