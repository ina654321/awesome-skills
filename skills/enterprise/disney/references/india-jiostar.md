# Disney India Strategy: JioStar Joint Venture

## Overview

**JioStar** is the joint venture formed in November 2024 combining Disney's Star India with Reliance Industries' Viacom18 media assets. This represents Disney's strategic shift from direct ownership to partnership in the Indian market.

## Transaction Details

### Deal Structure

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Transaction Value** | $8.5 billion (₹70,352 crore) |
| **Closing Date** | November 14, 2024 |
| **Reliance Investment** | $1.4 billion fresh capital |
| **Annual Revenue** | $3.1 billion (FY2024) |

### Ownership Structure

| Entity | Stake |
|--------|-------|
| Viacom18 (Reliance subsidiary) | 46.82% |
| The Walt Disney Company | 36.84% |
| Reliance Industries (direct) | 16.34% |
| **Total Reliance Control** | **63.16%** |

## Strategic Rationale

### Disney's Perspective

**Challenges Addressed:**
- Loss of IPL digital rights to JioCinema (2022)
- Subscriber decline in Disney+ Hotstar
- Intense competition from JioCinema (free content strategy)
- $4B+ invested in India with declining returns

**Benefits:**
- Retains 36.84% stake in growing market
- Access to Reliance's distribution (Jio 450M+ subscribers)
- Reduced operational burden
- Preserved value through partnership

### Reliance's Perspective

**Strategic Value:**
- Dominant position in Indian media (85% streaming market share)
- Control of cricket rights (India's most popular sport)
- Integration with Jio telecom ecosystem
- 750M+ viewer reach

## Regulatory Approval

### Competition Commission of India (CCI) Conditions

**Approved with stipulations (November 2024):**

1. **Divestiture of 7 TV Channels**
   - Reduced regional/genre dominance concerns

2. **No Bundling of Ad Slots**
   - Prohibited bundling for IPL, ICC, BCCI events
   - Ensures fair market access for advertisers

3. **Independent Monitoring**
   - Trustees appointed for compliance oversight

## Post-Merger Developments

### 2025 Updates

**Star Brand Integration (November 2025):**
- Star Television Productions merged into JioStar
- 'Star' brand now directly owned by JV
- Simplified ownership structure

**Financial Performance (Q3 2025):**
- Revenue: ₹7,232 crore (~$860M)
- Profit after tax: ₹1,322 crore (~$157M)
- Strong profitability from combined operations

**Digital Platform:**
- JioHotstar now unified streaming brand
- Disney+ Hotstar content migrated
- JioCinema features integrated
