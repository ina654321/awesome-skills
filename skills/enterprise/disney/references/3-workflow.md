## § 3 · Workflow

### Disney Product Development Process

**Phase 1: Blue Sky Concept**
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Output: Creative brief + initial concept
- Key Activity: Unconstrained ideation
- Stakeholders: Creative leads, franchise owners
- **Checkpoints:**
  - [ ] Story concept approved
  - [ ] Franchise alignment confirmed
  - [ ] Target audience defined

**Phase 2: Feasibility & Strategy**
- Duration: 4-6 weeks
- Output: Business case + technical feasibility
- Key Activity: Market sizing, tech assessment
- Stakeholders: Strategy, Finance, Technology
- **Checkpoints:**
  - [ ] Market opportunity validated
  - [ ] Technical approach defined
  - [ ] Resource requirements estimated

**Phase 3: Imagineering (Design & Prototype)**
- Duration: 8-12 weeks
- Output: Detailed design + working prototype
- Key Activity: UX design, technical prototyping
- Stakeholders: Design, Engineering, Creative
- **Checkpoints:**
  - [ ] User experience designed
  - [ ] Technical prototype validated
  - [ ] Creative integration confirmed

**Phase 4: Production & Development**
- Duration: 6-12 months
- Output: Production-ready system
- Key Activity: Full development, content creation
- Stakeholders: Full cross-functional team
- **Checkpoints:**
  - [ ] Alpha release (internal)
  - [ ] Beta release (limited audience)
  - [ ] Performance benchmarks met

**Phase 5: Launch & Show**
- Duration: 2-4 weeks
- Output: Live product
- Key Activity: Go-live, marketing, operations
- Stakeholders: Marketing, Operations, Support
- **Checkpoints:**
  - [ ] Launch criteria met
  - [ ] Operations ready
  - [ ] Support systems active

**Phase 6: Plussing (Optimization)**
- Duration: Ongoing
- Output: Continuous improvements
- Key Activity: Analytics, A/B testing, enhancements
- Stakeholders: Product, Engineering, Data
- **Checkpoints:**
  - [ ] KPIs monitored
  - [ ] Guest feedback collected
  - [ ] Enhancement roadmap maintained
