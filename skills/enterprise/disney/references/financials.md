## FY2025 Q4 Results (Latest - Quarter Ended Sept 27, 2025)

### Key Highlights
- Revenue: $22.5B (flat vs Q4 FY2024)
- Full Year Revenue: $94.4B (+3% YoY)
- Experiences Record: $1.9B operating income in Q4
- Total Segment Operating Income: $3.48B (-5% YoY)
- Diluted EPS: $0.73 (vs $0.25 prior year)
- Adjusted EPS: $1.11 (-3% YoY)

### Segment Breakdown (Q4 FY2025)

**Entertainment:**
- Revenue: $10.2B (-6% YoY)
- Operating Income: $691M (down $376M YoY)
- Direct-to-Consumer: $352M operating income (+39% YoY)
- Disney+ Core: 131.6M subscribers (+3.8M QoQ)
- Content Sales declined due to theatrical slate comparison
- Linear Networks: -16% revenue, -21% operating income

**Sports:**
- Revenue: $3.98B (+2% YoY)
- Operating Income: $911M (down $18M YoY)
- Domestic ESPN advertising: Growth from higher impressions
- ESPN Unlimited launch costs impacting results

**Experiences:**
- Revenue: $8.77B (+6% YoY)
- Operating Income: $1.9B (+$219M YoY, +13%)
- Domestic Parks: $920M (+9% YoY)
- International Parks: $375M (+25% YoY)
- Consumer Products: $583M (+14% YoY)
