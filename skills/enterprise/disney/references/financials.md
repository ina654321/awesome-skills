## FY2025 Q4 Results (Latest)

### Key Highlights
- Revenue: $22.5B (comparable to Q4 FY2024)
- Full Year Revenue: $94.4B (+3%)
- Experiences Record: $10.0B operating income
- DTC Streaming: 196M subscriptions (Disney+ + Hulu)

**Entertainment:**
- Operating Income: $691M (down $376M YoY)
- Direct-to-Consumer: $352M operating income (+39%)
- Disney+ Core: 131.6M subscribers (+3.8M QoQ)
- Content Sales declined due to theatrical slate comparison

**Sports:**
- Operating Income: $911M (down $18M YoY)
- Domestic ESPN advertising: +8%
- ESPN+ subscribers: 25.6M

**Experiences:**
- Operating Income: $1.9B (+$219M YoY)
- Domestic Parks: +9%
- International Parks: +25%
- Consumer Products: +14%

### Cost Reduction Program

**Target: $7.5B (achieved by 2024)**

| Area | Savings |
|------|---------|
| Content spend optimization | $3.0B |
| Non-content operating costs | $2.5B |
| Marketing efficiency | $1.0B |
| Technology rationalization | $1.0B |

**Actions Taken:**
- 7,000 job eliminations (3% of workforce)
- Reorganization into 3 segments
- Content budget discipline
- Shared services consolidation
