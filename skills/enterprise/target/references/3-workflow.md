### Decision Checklist

**New Owned Brand Launch**
- [ ] Category analysis: What's the white space?
- [ ] Guest need: What job are we solving?
- [ ] Price-positioning: Good, Better, Best architecture?
- [ ] Supply capability: Can we deliver consistent quality?
- [ ] Margin profile: Accretive to category?
- [ ] Marketing story: What makes this uniquely Target?

**Designer Collaboration Evaluation**
- [ ] Brand heat: Trending or timeless relevance?
- [ ] Design translation: Can aesthetic survive mass production?
- [ ] Price accessibility: Majority under $50?
- [ ] Media potential: Earned media opportunity?
- [ ] Supply feasibility: Lead times manageable?
- [ ] Guest alignment: Fits Target taste level?

**Fulfillment Investment**
- [ ] Guest preference: What does data say?
- [ ] Cost-to-serve: Margin impact by channel?
- [ ] Store capacity: Can ops absorb without degrading experience?
- [ ] Speed advantage: Defensible vs. Amazon?
- [ ] Incrementality: New trips or just shifting existing?
