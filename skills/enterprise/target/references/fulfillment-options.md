# Target Omnichannel Fulfillment

## Overview

Target's fulfillment network is built on the **"Store-as-Hub"** model—leveraging 1,950+ stores to fulfill digital orders. This approach enables same-day delivery at lower cost than traditional DC-based fulfillment.

---

## Fulfillment Hierarchy

### By Guest Preference & Cost Efficiency

```
FULFILLMENT PRIORITY
│
├── 1. DRIVE UP (Highest Satisfaction, Lowest Cost)
│   ├── Average fulfillment time: 4 minutes
│   ├── Guest satisfaction: Highest of all channels
│   ├── Cost per order: ~$1-2 (vs $5-7 for home delivery)
│   └── Q4 2024 sales: $2B+ (single quarter)
│
├── 2. ORDER PICKUP
│   ├── Fulfillment time: 2-4 hours typical
│   ├── Traffic driver: 40% buy additional items
│   ├── Cost per order: ~$2-3
│   └── Strategic value: Store visit opportunity
│
├── 3. SHIPT SAME-DAY
│   ├── Delivery window: 1-2 hours
│   ├── Shoppers: Independent contractors
│   ├── Target Circle 360: Unlimited free delivery ($35+)
│   └── Cost: Subsidized for members, fee-based for others
│
├── 4. SHIP FROM STORE
│   ├── Use case: Regional inventory optimization
│   ├── Speed: 1-2 day delivery
│   └── Cost: Higher than hub but faster than DC
│
└── 5. DC FULFILLMENT (Baseline)
    ├── Standard shipping: 3-5 days
    ├── Target Circle Card: Free 2-day shipping
    └── Cost: Most expensive for single items
```

## Drive Up Deep Dive

### The Experience

```
GUEST JOURNEY
├── Order placement (Target app)
│   ├── Browse and add items
│   ├── Select "Drive Up" at checkout
│   └── Choose pickup store
│
├── Fulfillment process
│   ├── Order routed to store
│   ├── Team member picks items from floor
│   ├── Items staged in Drive Up area
│   └── Guest notified when ready (typically <2 hours)
│
├── Arrival & delivery
│   ├── Guest parks in designated spot
│   ├── "I'm here" button in app
│   ├── Team member delivers to car within 4 minutes
│   └── Optional: Starbucks beverage add-on
│
└── Post-experience
    ├── Automatic receipt
    ├── Satisfaction survey
    └── Return option via Drive Up
```

### Starbucks Integration (2023 Launch)

**Innovation**: Guests can add Starbucks beverages to Drive Up orders

**Operational Model**:
- Beverage prepared when guest is 5 minutes away
- Hot drinks delivered within 4 minutes of arrival
- No additional delivery fee

**Results**:
- Drive Up NPS increased 12 points
- +23% repeat Drive Up usage among Starbucks users
- Average order value +$18 with Starbucks add-on
- First-of-its-kind in retail

### Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Average fulfillment time | 4 minutes |
| Guest satisfaction | Highest of all channels |
| Year-over-year growth | 10%+ consistently |
| Quarterly revenue | $2B+ (Q4 2024) |

## Order Pickup

### Value Proposition

**For Guests**:
- No shipping fees
- Same-day availability (2-4 hours)
- Secure, weather-protected pickup

**For Target**:
- Traffic generation: 40% of guests buy additional items
- Lower cost than home delivery
- Inventory utilization

### Pickup Process

```
ORDER PICKUP FLOW
├── Guest orders online/app
├── Items picked from store shelves
├── Notification sent when ready
├── Guest arrives at store
│   ├── Designated pickup area (often near entrance)
│   ├── Self-service lockers or service desk
│   └── ID verification for high-value orders
└── Optional: Guest shops store while there
```

## Ship from Store

### Use Cases

1. **Inventory optimization**: Fulfill from store with excess inventory
2. **Speed**: Faster than DC fulfillment for regional delivery
3. **SKU availability**: Access store-only items

### Process

```
SHIP FROM STORE
├── Digital order received
├── Inventory check across store network
├── Order routed to optimal store
│   ├── Proximity to guest
│   ├── Inventory availability
│   └── Store capacity
├── Store team picks and packs
├── Carrier pickup from store
└── 1-2 day delivery to guest
```

## Fulfillment Economics

### Cost Comparison

| Channel | Cost per Order | Guest Fee | Margin Impact |
|---------|----------------|-----------|---------------|
| Drive Up | $1-2 | Free | Most favorable |
| Order Pickup | $2-3 | Free | Very favorable |
| Ship from Store | $3-5 | Free (Card) | Favorable |
| Shipt (paid) | $8-12 | $9.99/delivery | Neutral |
| Shipt (Circle 360) | $8-12 | Subscription | Investment |
| DC Standard | $5-8 | Free (Card) | Baseline |
| DC Expedited | $10-15 | Varies | Unfavorable |

### Store-as-Hub Advantage

**Why It Works**:
- **Proximity**: Stores closer to guests than DCs
- **Inventory**: Immediate access to 100K+ SKUs
- **Labor**: Existing store staff, flexible capacity
- **Speed**: Same-day possible without new infrastructure

**Investment Areas**:
- Backroom automation and organization
- Picking technology (handhelds, carts)
- Parking lot redesign for Drive Up
- Sortation centers for efficiency
