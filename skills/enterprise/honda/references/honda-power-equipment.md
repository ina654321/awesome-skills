# Honda Power Equipment

## Overview

Honda is the **world's largest manufacturer of engines**, producing more than 20 million engines annually for diverse applications. The Power Equipment division serves residential, commercial, and industrial markets globally.

## Business Scale

| Metric | Value |

| **Revenue Share** | ~7% of Honda total |
| **Global Position** | #1 engine manufacturer by volume |
| **Markets** | 150+ countries |
| **Applications** | 7,000+ different product types |
