# DoorDash Company Overview — Reference

## Executive Summary

DoorDash Inc. (NYSE: DASH) is the leading on-demand local commerce platform in the United States and one of the largest globally. Founded in 2013, the company operates a three-sided marketplace connecting consumers, merchants, and delivery drivers (Dashers).

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Marketplace Revenue** (71%)
   - Commission fees (15-30% of order value)
   - Delivery fees from consumers
   - Service fees

2. **Subscription Revenue**
   - DashPass: $9.99/month
   - Wolt+: International equivalent
   - Benefits: Free delivery, reduced fees, exclusive offers

3. **Advertising Revenue** ($1.2B+ run rate)
   - Promoted merchant listings
   - Sponsored products
   - High-margin retail media network

4. **Commerce Platform**
   - DoorDash Drive: White-label delivery
   - Storefront: Merchant online ordering
   - Bbot: In-store technology
