# DHL Group Company Profile

## Overview

**DHL Group** (formerly Deutsche Post DHL Group) is the world's leading logistics company, operating in more than 220 countries and territories worldwide. The company connects people and markets, serving as a critical enabler of global trade.

## Operating Divisions

### 1. Express
- **Revenue 2024:** €25.8 billion
- **Core Business:** International time-definite shipments (TDI)
- **Network:** 300+ dedicated aircraft, 2,200+ facilities
- **Key Products:** Time Definite International (TDI), Same Day, Medical Express
- **Differentiator:** Customs clearance expertise, door-to-door reliability

### 2. Global Forwarding, Freight
- **Revenue 2024:** €17.6 billion
- **Core Business:** Air/ocean freight forwarding, industrial projects
- **Services:** FCL, LCL, charter services, European road freight
- **Key Products:** GoGreen Plus Ocean, Flexitanks, Project Logistics
- **Differentiator:** Neutral freight forwarder with global carrier relationships

### 3. Supply Chain
- **Revenue 2024:** €16.8 billion
- **Core Business:** Contract logistics, warehousing, value-added services
- **Facilities:** 1,800+ warehouses globally
- **Key Sectors:** Automotive, Consumer, Healthcare, Technology, Retail
- **Differentiator:** Industry-specific expertise, lean process optimization

### 4. eCommerce
- **Revenue 2024:** €5.9 billion
- **Core Business:** Cross-border parcel, last-mile delivery
- **Markets:** Europe, US, India, Southeast Asia
- **Key Products:** Packet International, Parcel Connect, Returns Management
- **Differentiator:** E-commerce specialized network and returns solutions

### 5. Post & Parcel Germany
- **Revenue 2024:** €17.3 billion
- **Core Business:** German mail and parcel services
- **Daily Volume:** 46M letters, 6.3M parcels
- **Network:** 82M delivery addresses, 13,000+ retail outlets
- **Transformation:** "Fit for Growth" program addressing declining mail volumes

## "Fit for Growth" Program (2025-2027)

Announced March 2025 in response to economic challenges:

- **Job Reductions:** 8,000 positions in Post & Parcel Germany (through attrition)
- **Cost Savings:** >€1 billion by 2027
- **Drivers:** Declining mail volumes, wage inflation, regulatory constraints
- **Approach:** Socially responsible restructuring, automation investment
