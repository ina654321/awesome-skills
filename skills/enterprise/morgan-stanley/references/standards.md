# Morgan Stanley Standards & Frameworks

## Valuation Standards

### Trading Comparables Analysis

**Selection Criteria:**
- Minimum 4-6 comparable companies
- Similar business model and revenue drivers
- Comparable growth profile (±5% revenue growth)
- Similar margin structure (±300 bps EBITDA margin)
- Adequate trading liquidity (>$10M daily volume)

**Multiple Selection by Sector:**

| Sector | Primary Multiple | Secondary Multiple | Tertiary Multiple |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Technology | EV/Revenue (growth) | EV/EBITDA | P/E (mature) |
| Healthcare | EV/EBITDA | P/E | EV/Revenue (biotech) |
| Financials | P/BV | P/E | ROTCE |
| Industrials | EV/EBITDA | EV/EBIT | P/E |
| Energy | EV/EBITDA | EV/DACF | P/CF |
| Consumer | EV/EBITDA | P/E | EV/Revenue (growth) |

**Adjustments:**
- Control premium: 20-30% for strategic buyers
- Illiquidity discount: 10-20% for private companies
- Size discount: 10-15% for sub-scale companies

### DCF Methodology

**Projection Period:**
- Minimum 5 years explicit projections
- Maximum 7 years (beyond which uncertainty too high)
- Quarterly granularity for Years 1-2 in volatile sectors

**Key Assumptions:**
```
WACC Components:
├── Risk-free rate: 10-year Treasury yield
├── Equity risk premium: 5.0-6.0% (cycle-adjusted)
├── Beta: 2-year weekly regression vs. S&P 500
├── Cost of debt: Current yield on comparable bonds
└── Capital structure: Target long-term weights

Terminal Value:
├── Method: Perpetuity growth OR exit multiple (not both)
├── Growth rate: 2.0-2.5% (long-term GDP)
├── Exit multiple: Must reconcile with terminal growth
└── Mid-year convention: Apply for accurate timing
```

### Fairness Opinion Standards

**Required Elements:**
1. Valuation analysis using 4+ methodologies
2. Trading range analysis (1-year pre-announcement)
3. Premium paid analysis for comparable transactions
4. Board process description
5. Conflicts disclosure
6. Material assumptions and limitations

**Unqualified Opinion Criteria:**
- All valuation methods within reasonable range
- No material conflicts of interest
- Adequate due diligence performed
- Board process robust and arm's-length

| Social media posts | Compliance | 1 business day |
| Political contributions | Compliance | 5 business days |

### Information Barrier Procedures

**Wall Establishment:**
1. Identify material conflict
2. Legal assessment of wall feasibility
3. Physical/logical separation implementation
4. Need-to-know access list compilation
5. Training and acknowledgment
6. Ongoing monitoring protocols

**Prohibited Scenarios:**
- Buyer and target in same M&A process
- Competing bidders with MNPI access
- Issuer and investor in same private placement
- Adverse positions in litigation
