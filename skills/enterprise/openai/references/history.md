# OpenAI History Reference

## Timeline

### 2015: Foundation

**December 11, 2015**
- OpenAI founded as nonprofit in San Francisco
- Founders: Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Elon Musk, Reid Hoffman, Peter Thiel, Jessica Livingston, Amazon Web Services, Infosys, YC Research
- Mission: "Ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity"
- Initial funding: $1 billion pledged

**Key Early Team Members:**
- Wojciech Zaremba (Google Brain)
- Andrej Karpathy (Stanford)
- Durk Kingma (University of Amsterdam)
- John Schulman (Berkeley)

### 2016-2017: Early Research

**2016:**
- OpenAI Gym released (reinforcement learning toolkit)
- Universe platform for training agents across games/websites
- First safety research publications

**2017:**
- Pro team established
- Partnerships with Microsoft begin

### 2018: GPT Era Begins

**June 2018:**
- GPT-1 released (117M parameters)
- Demonstrated generative pre-training works

**February 2018:**
- Elon Musk departs board (cited conflict of interest with Tesla AI)
- Remained donor

### 2019: Transition to Capped-Profit

**March 2019:**
- Transition to "capped-profit" hybrid model
- OpenAI LP created as for-profit subsidiary
- Limit: 100× return on investment

**July 2019:**
- $1 billion investment from Microsoft
- Microsoft becomes exclusive cloud provider
- GPT-2 staged release controversy (concerns about misuse)

### 2020: GPT-3 Breakthrough

**June 2020:**
- GPT-3 released (175B parameters)
- API launched in beta
- Demonstrated emergent few-shot capabilities

**September 2020:**
- Exclusive GPT-3 licensing to Microsoft (others can use API)

### 2021: Multimodal Expansion

**January 2021:**
- DALL-E announced (text-to-image)
- CLIP released (vision-language understanding)

**June 2021:**
- Codex announced (code generation)
- GitHub Copilot powered by Codex

**July 2021:**
- InstructGPT research published (RLHF breakthrough)

### 2022: ChatGPT Revolution

**March 2022:**
- GPT-3.5 series launched
- InstructGPT models in API

**November 30, 2022:**
- ChatGPT launched (based on GPT-3.5)
- 1 million users in 5 days
- 100 million users in 2 months (fastest-growing app ever)

**December 2022:**
- ChatGPT Plus announced ($20/month)
- Whisper v1 released (open weights)

### 2023: GPT-4 and Board Drama

**March 14, 2023:**
- GPT-4 launched (multimodal, major capability jump)
- 32K context option
- Significant safety improvements

**May 2023:**
- ChatGPT plugins launched
- Web browsing capability
- Code Interpreter (later Advanced Data Analysis)

**September 2023:**
- ChatGPT voice and vision capabilities
- DALL-E 3 integration announced

**November 2023: The Board Crisis**

**November 17:**
- Sam Altman fired by OpenAI board
- Greg Brockman removed as board chair (remains president)
- Reasons cited: "not consistently candid in communications"

**November 18-19:**
- Chief Scientist Ilya Sutskever revealed as key figure in ouster
- Brockman resigns in solidarity with Altman
- Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella offers to hire Altman and team

**November 20:**
- 650+ of 770 employees sign letter threatening to quit unless Altman reinstated
- Letter includes Sutskever: "I deeply regret my participation"
- Altman reinstated as CEO
- Board resigns except Adam D'Angelo

**November 21:**
- New board announced: Bret Taylor (Chair), Larry Summers, Adam D'Angelo
- Microsoft receives non-voting board observer seat

### 2024: Expansion and Departures

**January 2024:**
- GPT Store launched
- Revenue sharing for GPT builders announced

**February 2024:**
- Sora announced (text-to-video)
- Elon Musk lawsuit filed (claiming breach of founding agreement)

**March 2024:**
- Figure 01 humanoid robot demo (powered by OpenAI)

**May 2024:**
- GPT-4o launched ("omni" multimodal)
- Native audio capabilities
- Real-time voice mode

**May 14, 2024:**
- Ilya Sutskever announces departure
- "I'm leaving OpenAI after almost a decade"
- Founding of Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI) announced

**June 2024:**
- Apple partnership announced (Siri, system-wide AI)

**July 2024:**
- GPT-4o mini launched (cost-effective model)

**September 2024:**
- o1-preview released (reasoning model)
- Mira Murati (CTO) announces departure
- Bob McGrew (Chief Research Officer) departs
- Barrett Zoph (VP Research) departs

**October 2024:**
- $6.6 billion funding round at $157 billion valuation
- Led by Thrive Capital, Microsoft, NVIDIA
- Convertible to equity if restructuring complete

**December 2024:**
- o1 full release with tool use, image understanding
- 12 Days of OpenAI announcements

### 2025: Scale and Restructuring

**January 2025:**
- o3-mini released
- Stargate Project announced with Trump ($100B+ AI infrastructure)

**February 2025:**
- 400M weekly active users announced
- $40 billion funding led by SoftBank at $300B valuation
- Largest private fundraising in history

**March 2025:**
- o3 and o4-mini released
- $12.7B revenue projection for 2025

**April 2025:**
- GPT-4.1 series launched
- 1M context window

**May 2025:**
- Superalignment team officially dissolved
- Safety research reorganized

**June 2025:**
- OpenAI partners with California for AI initiatives
- o3-pro released

**August 2025:**
- GPT-5 launched
- Unified reasoning + speed modes
- 800M+ weekly active users

**October 2025:**
- Restructuring to Public Benefit Corporation complete
- $500 billion valuation in secondary share sale
- World's most valuable private company
- Nonprofit retains governance control

**December 2025:**
- 910M weekly active users
- $25B annualized revenue

## Legal Issues

**Elon Musk Lawsuit (February 2024)**
- Claims OpenAI abandoned nonprofit mission
- Alleges breach of founding agreement
- OpenAI counters: no such agreement existed
- Ongoing litigation

**NYT Lawsuit (December 2023)**
- The New York Times sues for copyright infringement
- Claims unauthorized use of articles in training
- OpenAI defends as fair use
- Ongoing

**Regulatory Scrutiny**
- FTC investigation into consumer harm
- SEC inquiry around CEO statements
- EU AI Act compliance requirements

## Organizational Structure Evolution

**2015-2019: Pure Nonprofit**
- OpenAI Inc. (501(c)(3))

**2019-2025: Capped-Profit Hybrid**
- OpenAI Inc. (nonprofit parent)
- OpenAI LP (capped-profit subsidiary)
- 100× return cap for investors

**2025+: Public Benefit Corporation**
- OpenAI PBC (Public Benefit Corporation)
- Nonprofit retains governance control
- Legally committed to mission alongside profit
