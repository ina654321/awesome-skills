## Brand Heritage

- **1837**: John Deere invents self-scouring steel plow
- **1868**: Deere & Company incorporates
- **1918**: Enters tractor business with acquisition of Waterloo Gasoline Engine Company
- **1956**: First 6-cylinder agricultural tractor (Model 720)
- **1997**: Precision agriculture begins with GPS integration
- **1999**: AutoTrac automated guidance introduced
- **2012**: Operations Center cloud platform launched
- **2017**: Blue River Technology acquisition
- **2021**: See & Spray Ultimate released
- **2022**: Autonomous 8R tractor unveiled at CES
- **2025**: FTC right-to-repair lawsuit filed
