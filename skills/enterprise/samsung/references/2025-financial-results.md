## Division Performance

### Device Solutions (DS) - Semiconductors

| Metric | Q4 2025 | QoQ Change |
|--------|---------|------------|
| **Revenue** | ₩44.0T | +33% |
| **Operating Profit** | ₩16.4T | Record high |
| **% of Total Revenue** | 47% |

**Memory Business:**
- All-time record quarterly revenue and operating profit
- HBM sales expansion driving growth
- DDR5, server SSDs, enterprise SSDs strong demand

**System LSI:**
- Earnings declined in Q4 due to seasonal softness
- Image sensor revenue grew (200MP, 50MP products)

**Foundry:**
- Revenue increased QoQ on strong customer demand
- 2nm mass production commenced
- 4nm HBM base-die shipments started
