## Key Business Developments in 2024

### Memory Business
- Record-high Q4 revenue driven by high-value memory products
- HBM sales increased 70%+ year-over-year
- Server SSD sales grew mid-30% range
- Accelerated transition to 1b nanometer DRAM process
- Mass produced 9th-generation V-NAND (industry first)

### Mobile Experience (MX)
- Galaxy S24 series launched with Galaxy AI capabilities
- Maintained #1 or #2 position in global smartphone market
- GenAI-capable smartphone market share: 58% (Q1 2024)

### Display
- OLED panel business focused on premium IT and automotive
- Large panel business focused on QD-OLED and premium TVs
- Faced rising competition from Chinese display makers

### Harman
- Record operating profit since Samsung acquisition (2017)
- Digital cockpit and connected car solutions growth
- 50M+ vehicles with Harman audio/connected systems

## Strategic Priorities (2025 Outlook)

1. **AI Leadership:** Expand Galaxy AI across product lines
2. **Semiconductor Recovery:** Focus on HBM, DDR5, advanced nodes
3. **Foundry Growth:** 2nm GAA technology, Texas fab investment
4. **Display Innovation:** QD-OLED expansion, micro LED development
5. **Ecosystem Integration:** SmartThings, One UI across all devices

## Risk Factors

- Geopolitical tensions affecting semiconductor supply chains
- Currency fluctuations (KRW/USD)
- Competition in premium smartphone segment
- Memory chip price volatility
- Regulatory challenges in key markets
