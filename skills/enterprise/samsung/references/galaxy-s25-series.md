## Strategic Decisions

### Global Snapdragon Strategy

**Decision:** Use Snapdragon 8 Elite for ALL markets (no Exynos variant)

**Rationale:**
- Consistent performance globally
- Avoid Exynos performance controversies
- Focus Samsung Foundry 2nm on S26 (Exynos 2600)
- Strengthen Qualcomm partnership

### 7-Year Software Support

| Commitment | Details |
|------------|---------|

| **Security** | 7 years of security patches |
| **AI Updates** | Hardware-permitting Galaxy AI updates |
