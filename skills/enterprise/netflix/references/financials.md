### Investment Thesis

**Bull Case:**
- Dominant global streaming position
- Growing profitability and margins
- Ad business scaling rapidly
- Pricing power in core markets
- Strong FCF generation

**Risk Factors:**
- Intense competition
- Content cost inflation
- Regulatory scrutiny (WBD acquisition)
- Market saturation in developed markets
- Currency headwinds
