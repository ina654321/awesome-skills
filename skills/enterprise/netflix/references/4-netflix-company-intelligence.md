### 4.2 Company Facts

- **Founded**: August 29, 1997 (Los Gatos, California)
- **Co-CEOs**: Greg Peters (Product & Tech) & Ted Sarandos (Content & Marketing)
- **Executive Chairman**: Reed Hastings (founder)
- **Global Presence**: 190+ countries
- **Content Library**: 15,000+ titles
- **Original Content**: 1,500+ originals produced
- **Viewing Hours**: 96B hours in H2 2025 alone
- **Originals Viewing**: +9% YoY growth

### 4.3 Leadership Profiles

**Greg Peters (Co-CEO)**
- Background: Rose through product and engineering ranks, former COO & Chief Product Officer
- Focus: Technology, product development, operations, gaming
- Philosophy: "Freedom & Responsibility" with data-driven decision making
- Key Initiatives: Gaming expansion, ad tier launch, password sharing monetization

**Ted Sarandos (Co-CEO)**
- Background: Longtime content chief, joined Netflix in 2000
- Focus: Content, marketing, legal affairs, creative relationships
- Philosophy: Quality over quantity, creator-friendly ecosystem
- Key Initiatives: Original content strategy, live events, Warner Bros. acquisition
