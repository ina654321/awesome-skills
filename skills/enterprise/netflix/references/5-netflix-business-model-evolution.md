## § 5 · Netflix Business Model Evolution

### 5.1 Three Acts of Reinvention

```
ACT 1: DVD BY MAIL (1997-2007)
├── Disrupted Blockbuster
├── Built distribution network
├── Established subscription habit
└── Data on viewing preferences

ACT 2: STREAMING PIONEER (2007-2013)
├── Launched streaming service
├── Built Open Connect CDN
├── Expanded internationally (2010+)
└── Transitioned from licensed to originals

ACT 3: GLOBAL ENTERTAINMENT (2013-Present)
├── Original content production (House of Cards, 2013)
├── 190+ country expansion (2016)
├── Multiple revenue streams (ads, games, live)
└── Warner Bros. acquisition (2025, pending)
```

### 5.2 Revenue Model Architecture

| Tier | Price (US) | Key Features | Target Segment |
|------|------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Standard with Ads** | $6.99/mo | 1080p, 2 streams, ads | Price-sensitive |
| **Standard** | $15.49/mo | 1080p, 2 streams, no ads | Mainstream |
| **Premium** | $22.99/mo | 4K HDR, 4 streams, spatial audio | Premium |

**Revenue Drivers:**
1. **Membership Growth**: 325M+ paid memberships
2. **Price Increases**: Gradual ARPU optimization
3. **Ad Revenue**: $1.5B+ and growing 2.5x
4. **Password Sharing Monetization**: 2023 crackdown
5. **Games & Interactive**: Emerging revenue stream

### 5.3 Content Investment Strategy

**The 93% Success Rate Secret:**
- Traditional TV: ~35% success rate for new shows
- Netflix: 93% success rate through data-informed decisions

**Content Portfolio Mix:**
- **Global Hits**: Stranger Things, Wednesday, Squid Game ($900M+ value)
- **Local Language**: Lupin (France), Sacred Games (India), Alice in Borderland (Japan)
- **Films**: Mix of prestige (Roma, Power of the Dog) and commercial (Red Notice, Extraction)
- **Live Events**: NFL games, WWE Raw (2025), Jake Paul vs Mike Tyson
- **Games**: 140+ titles including GTA trilogy

**Investment Allocation:**
- $20B projected for 2026 (10% increase)
- ~50% on originals, ~50% on licensed content
- Heavy emphasis on local production for global markets
- Strategic partnerships (Universal, Sony, Paramount)
