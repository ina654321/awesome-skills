# Netflix Content Strategy Framework

## The 93% Success Rate

Netflix achieves a 93% success rate for original content (vs. ~35% industry average) through:

1. **Data-Informed Greenlighting**
   - Viewing pattern analysis
   - Audience overlap modeling
   - Talent track record assessment
   - Global appeal evaluation

2. **Creative Trust**
   - Give creators freedom and resources
   - Multi-year commitments
   - Minimal interference
   - Global distribution guarantee

## Key Strategic Principles

### 1. Local to Global

**The Flywheel:**
```
Local Production → Local Hit → Global Discovery → Global Hit
```

**Success Stories:**
- Squid Game (Korea): $900M+ value, 1.65B hours
- Money Heist (Spain): #1 in 90+ countries
- Lupin (France): 70M+ households
- Sacred Games (India): Global Indian content breakthrough

### 2. Content Universes

**IP Extension Strategy:**
- Series → Films → Games → Live events
- Character spin-offs
- Prequels/sequels
- Interactive content

**Example - Stranger Things:**
- 4 seasons (series)
- Soundtrack albums (music)
- Mobile games (gaming)
- Merchandise (retail)
- Experiential (Netflix House)

### 3. The "Fewer, Bigger, Better" Approach

**Rationale:**
- Better to have one hit than five mediocre titles
- Marketing concentration drives discovery
- Word-of-mouth amplifies quality
- Resource efficiency

**Implementation:**
- Cancel underperformers quickly
- Renew hits with confidence
- Invest in proven creators
- Take big swings on high-potential concepts

## Measuring Content Success

### Primary Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Viewing Hours** | Total time watched | Maximum |
| **Completion Rate** | % who finish | >70% for series |
| **Efficiency** | Viewing hours / cost | Top quartile |
| **Subscriber Impact** | New subs attributed | Positive ROI |
| **Engagement Velocity** | Speed of viewing | Front-loaded |

### Secondary Metrics

- Social media buzz
- Critical acclaim (awards)
- Cultural impact (memes, discourse)
- Talent relationship strength
- Franchise potential
