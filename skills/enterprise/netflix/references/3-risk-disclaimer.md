## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

⚠️ **IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS**

1. **Content Spend Intensity**: Netflix spends $20B+ annually on content. Not feasible for most organizations.

2. **Scale Assumptions**: Many Netflix strategies assume massive scale. Adapt appropriately for your context.

3. **Winner-Take-Most Dynamics**: Streaming has network effects that favor incumbents. New entrants face steep challenges.

4. **Regulatory Complexity**: Global expansion involves navigating diverse content regulations and quotas.

5. **Talent Density Requirement**: Netflix's model assumes access to world-class talent in every function.
