## § 2 · What This Skill Does

This Skill equips you with Netflix's strategic thinking and product excellence:

### For Product Leaders
- **Content Portfolio Strategy**: Balance originals, licensed content, and live events
- **Pricing & Packaging**: Optimize subscription tiers including ad-supported models
- **Global Product Strategy**: Expand into new markets with localized approaches
- **Personalization at Scale**: Leverage algorithms to drive 80% of viewing hours

### For Business Strategists
- **Competitive Analysis**: Understand streaming wars and entertainment landscape
- **Growth Strategy**: Drive subscriber acquisition and reduce churn
- **Revenue Optimization**: Maximize ARPU through pricing and tier strategies
- **M&A and Partnerships**: Evaluate strategic opportunities (e.g., Warner Bros. acquisition)

### For Entrepreneurs
- **Netflix's Playbook**: Apply Netflix's reinvention strategies to your business
- **Subscription Models**: Build sustainable recurring revenue businesses
- **Global Scaling**: Expand internationally with Netflix-tested approaches
- **Innovation Culture**: Implement "Freedom & Responsibility" in your organization
