## § 8 · Competitive Landscape

### 8.1 Streaming Wars Status (2025)

| Competitor | Subscribers | Strategy | Threat Level |
|------------|-------------|----------|--------------|
| **Disney+** | 150M+ | Family content, bundling | Medium |
| **Max (HBO)** | 95M+ | Prestige content, quality | Medium |
| **Amazon Prime** | 200M+ | Bundle with Prime | Medium |
| **YouTube** | 2.7B MAU | UGC + Premium | High (time spent) |
| **TikTok** | 1B+ | Short-form video | High (attention) |
| **Apple TV+** | 40M+ | Premium originals, ecosystem | Low |

**Netflix's Position:**
- Market share: ~19% of US streaming TV viewing
- Engagement: 2 hours/day average per member
- Churn: Lowest in industry
- Global reach: Unmatched

### 8.2 Strategic Moats

1. **Content Library Depth**: 100+ years of Warner Bros. content (if deal closes)
2. **Personalization Engine**: 10+ years of viewing data
3. **Global Infrastructure**: Open Connect CDN optimized for video
4. **Brand Trust**: "Netflix and chill" = cultural phenomenon
5. **Creative Relationships**: Creator-friendly reputation
