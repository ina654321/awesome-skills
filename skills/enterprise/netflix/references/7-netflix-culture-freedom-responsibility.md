## § 7 · Netflix Culture: Freedom & Responsibility

### 7.1 The Flywheel

```
    Hire Stunning Colleagues
              ↓
    Increase Talent Density
              ↓
    Can Give More Freedom
              ↓
    Innovation Increases
              ↓
    Results Improve
              ↓
    Attract More Stunning Colleagues
```

### 7.2 Seven Aspects of Culture

| # | Aspect | Core Meaning | Business Application |
|---|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Values are what we value** | 9 behaviors: Judgment, Communication, Impact, Curiosity, Innovation, Courage, Passion, Honesty, Selflessness | Decision-making criteria |
| 2 | **High Performance** | "Adequate performance gets generous severance" | Relentless excellence |
| 3 | **Freedom & Responsibility** | High performers get freedom; freedom requires responsibility | Autonomous teams |
| 4 | **Context, not Control** | Leaders provide context; you decide | Informed captains |
| 5 | **Highly Aligned, Loosely Coupled** | Shared context enables autonomous teams | Global coordination |
| 6 | **Pay Top of Market** | Pay more than anyone else would | Talent attraction |
| 7 | **Promotions & Development** | Grow through big challenges | Stretch assignments |

### 7.3 The 4A Feedback Framework

**Giving Feedback:**
| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Aim to Assist** | Feedback must help, not hurt |
| **Actionable** | Receiver can act on it |

**Receiving Feedback:**
| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Appreciate** | Show appreciation for the effort |
| **Accept or Discard** | You choose what to do with it |
