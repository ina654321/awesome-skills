# Colgate-Palmolive Emerging Markets

> **Last Updated:** 2026-03-21

---

## Overview

- **Revenue Share:** 60% of total
- **Growth Priority:** Primary expansion focus
- **Key Regions:** Latin America, Asia, Africa

---

## Latin America

### Market Leadership
- **Position:** #1 or #2 in most markets
- **Key Countries:** Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia

### Strategies
- Premiumization as incomes rise
- Traditional + modern trade balance
- Local marketing
- Value tier maintenance

### Innovation
- Regional fragrances
- Local herbal ingredients
- Sachet formats

---

## Asia-Pacific

### India
- **Opportunity:** 1.4B population, low penetration
- **Strategy:**
  - Sachet formats for affordability
  - Rural distribution expansion
  - Ayurveda-inspired products
  - E-commerce growth

### China
- **Challenge:** Competitive, slowing growth
- **Strategy:**
  - Premium focus
  - Tmall/JD presence
  - Natural ingredients
  - Tier-2/3 city expansion

### Southeast Asia
- **Markets:** Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam
- **Strategy:**
  - Muslim-friendly products (halal)
  - Tropical formulations
  - Sachet economy

## Format Innovation

### Sachets/Pouches
- **Markets:** India, Southeast Asia, Latin America
- **Benefit:** Affordability, trial
- **Example:** Shampoo, toothpaste sachets

### Refill Packs
- **Markets:** Environmentally conscious
- **Benefit:** Sustainability, value
- **Example:** Dish liquid refills

### Multi-packs
- **Markets:** Value-focused shoppers
- **Benefit:** Stock-up, savings
- **Example:** Toothpaste 3-packs
