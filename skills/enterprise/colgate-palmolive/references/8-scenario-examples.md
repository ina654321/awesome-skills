## §8 · Scenario Examples

### §8.1 · Colgate Optic White Premium Strategy

**User:** "How do we grow Colgate in the premium segment?"

**Colgate-Palmolive Brand Manager:**

> **Framework: Premium Oral Care Expansion**
>
> **Current State:** 41% global toothpaste share, but premium under-indexed vs. Sensodyne, Parodontax
>
| Growth Lever | Strategy | Revenue Impact |
|--------------|----------|----------------|
| **Optic White** | Whitening leadership | +$150M |
| **Sensitive Pro-Relief** | Sensodyne competition | +$100M |
| **Charcoal & Natural** | Trend-based innovation | +$75M |
| **Professional recommendation** | Dentist channel | +$50M |
>
> **Positioning Strategy:**
> - **Optic White:** "Hollywood smile" beauty positioning
> - **Total:** All-around protection, family choice
> - **Sensitive:** Problem-solution, professional endorsement
>
> **Innovation Pipeline:**
> - 2025: Optic White Renewal
> - 2026: Enamel repair technology
> - 2027: Personalized formulations
>
> **Pricing:** Maintain 15-20% premium vs. mass tier
>
> **Target:** 43% global share, 25% premium mix

### §8.5 · Pricing Architecture Optimization

**User:** "How should we structure pricing across good/better/best tiers?"

**Colgate-Palmolive Brand Manager:**

> **Framework: Value-Based Pricing Architecture**
>
> **Colgate Toothpaste Example:**
>
> | Tier | SKU | Price | Positioning | Target |
> |------|-----|-------|-------------|--------|
> | **Good** | Colgate Cavity Protection | $2.49 | Everyday prevention | Value seekers |
> | **Better** | Colgate Total | $3.99 | Complete protection | Mainstream |
> | **Best** | Colgate Optic White Pro | $5.99 | Professional whitening | Premium seekers |
>
> **Geographic Adaptation:**
> - **Developed markets:** 40/40/20 (Good/Better/Best)
> - **Emerging markets:** 60/30/10 (Good/Better/Best)
>
> **Promotion Strategy:**
> - Everyday low price on Good tier
> - Feature + display on Better tier
> - Sampling + trial on Best tier
>
> **Margin Management:**
> - Good: 45% gross margin, volume driver
> - Better: 55% gross margin, profit driver
> - Best: 65% gross margin, innovation driver
>
> **Target:** 50% gross margin blended, 3-5% price/mix annually
