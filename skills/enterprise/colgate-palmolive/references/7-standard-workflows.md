## §7 · Standard Workflows

### §7.1 · Brand Renovation Process

```
Phase 1: Consumer & Market Assessment (Months -12 to -9)
├── Usage and attitude research
├── Competitive landscape analysis
├── White space identification
├── Equity trend assessment
└── ✓ Output: Renovation brief

Phase 2: Concept Development (Months -9 to -6)
├── Benefit territory exploration
├── Packaging design concepts
├── Price-positioning analysis
├── Concept screening (qual + quant)
└── ✓ Output: Winning concept

Phase 3: Product Development (Months -6 to -3)
├── Formula optimization
├── Packaging finalization
├── Regulatory compliance
├── Manufacturing trial
└── ✓ Output: Production-ready

Phase 4: Launch Preparation (Months -3 to 0)
├── Trade selling
├── Marketing campaign
├── In-store execution plan
├── Digital content
└── ✓ Output: Go-to-market plan

Phase 5: Post-Launch (Months 0-12)
├── Sell-through tracking
├── Consumer feedback
├── Competitive response
├── Optimization
└── ✓ Output: Performance review
```

### §7.2 · Emerging Market Expansion

```
Phase 1: Market Assessment (Months 1-3)
├── Category size and growth analysis
├── Competitive landscape mapping
├── Distribution channel assessment
├── Consumer insight gathering
└── ✓ Output: Market entry strategy

Phase 2: Adaptation (Months 3-6)
├── Product format adaptation
├── Price architecture design
├── Packaging localization
├── Regulatory compliance
└── ✓ Output: Market-ready portfolio

Phase 3: Distribution Build (Months 6-12)
├── Key account partnerships
├── Traditional trade penetration
├── Modern trade negotiations
├── E-commerce setup
└── ✓ Output: Distribution foundation

Phase 4: Marketing Activation (Months 12-18)
├── Mass media launch
├── Sampling program
├── In-store activation
├── Digital engagement
└── ✓ Output: Brand awareness

Phase 5: Scale & Optimize (Months 18-36)
├── Portfolio expansion
├── Line extensions
├── Channel deepening
├── Efficiency improvement
└── ✓ Output: Profitable growth
```
