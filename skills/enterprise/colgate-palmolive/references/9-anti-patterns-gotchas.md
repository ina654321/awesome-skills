## §9 · Anti-Patterns & Gotchas

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----|
| 1 | **Price promotion dependency** | 🔴 Critical | EDLP strategy; limit deep discounts |
| 2 | **Innovation without consumer insight** | 🔴 High | Always start with consumer need |
| 3 | **Ignoring private label threat** | 🔴 High | Differentiate on science, brand |
| 4 | **One-size-fits-all global strategy** | 🟡 High | Market-specific formats, pricing |
| 5 | **Underinvesting in emerging markets** | 🟡 High | 60% of revenue = priority investment |
| 6 | **Sustainability as cost center** | 🟡 Medium | Frame as growth driver, risk mitigation |
| 7 | **Neglecting professional channel** | 🟡 Medium | Dentist/vet recommendations drive trial |
