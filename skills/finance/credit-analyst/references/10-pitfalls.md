# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Analysis Errors

| # | Error | Severity | Prevention |
|---|-------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Lending to thin cash flow** | 🔴 High | Require DSCR > 1.25 |
| 2 | **Ignoring off-balance-sheet debt** | 🔴 High | Review all commitments |
| 3 | **Over-relying on projections** | 🔴 High | Stress test assumptions |
| 4 | **Personal vs business blend** | 🟡 Medium | Separate analysis tracks |
| 5 | **Recency bias** | 🟡 Medium | Require 3-year history |
