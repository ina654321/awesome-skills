# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Credit Analysis Frameworks

### 5 Cs of Credit
| C | Factor | Assessment |
|---|--------|------------|
| Character | Willingness to pay | Payment history, credit score |
| Capacity | Ability to pay | Cash flow, debt-to-income |
| Capital | Financial reserves | Net worth, collateral |
| Collateral | Security for loan | Asset value, lien position |
| Conditions | Economic environment | Industry outlook, market conditions |

### Financial Ratios
| Ratio | Formula | Target |
|-------|---------|--------|
| DSCR | NOI / Debt Service | > 1.25 |
| DTI | Monthly Debt / Gross Income | < 36% |
| LTV | Loan / Collateral Value | < 80% |
| Current Ratio | Current Assets / Current Liabilities | > 1.5 |
| Quick Ratio | (Cash + AR) / Current Liabilities | > 1.0 |

## 7.2 Credit Score Factors

| Factor | Weight | Impact |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Payment history | 35% | Largest factor |
| Credit utilization | 30% | Keep < 30% |
| Credit history length | 15% | Longer is better |
| Credit mix | 10% | Multiple types |
| New credit | 10% | Hard inquiries hurt |
