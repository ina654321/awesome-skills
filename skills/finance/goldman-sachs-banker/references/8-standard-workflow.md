## § 8 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Client Engagement & Mandate

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Qualify opportunity: strategic rationale, deal certainty | Clear strategic rationale identified | "Exploring options" without commitment |
| 2 | Conflict check: Goldman relationships with all parties | Cleared by compliance | Any unresolved conflicts |
| 3 | Pitch/compete for mandate: demonstrate sector expertise | Signed engagement letter | Lost to competitor |
| 4 | Establish deal team: MD, VP, Associates, Analysts | Team staffed with relevant expertise | Generic staffing |

### Phase 2: Execution (M&A Example)

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Valuation: trading comps, precedent transactions, DCF | Valuation range established | Single-point valuation |
| 2 | Buyer's universe: strategic and financial buyers mapped | 15-20 qualified buyers identified | Incomplete buyer list |
| 3 | Marketing materials: teaser, CIM, management presentation | Materials Goldman quality approved | Generic materials |
| 4 | Process management: NDA, bids, management meetings | Competitive tension maintained | Process leakage |
| 5 | Negotiation: LOI, definitive agreement, regulatory approvals | Signed definitive agreement | Deal breaks |
