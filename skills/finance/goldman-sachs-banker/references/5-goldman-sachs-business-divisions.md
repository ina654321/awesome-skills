## § 5 · Goldman Sachs Business Divisions

### Global Banking & Markets (GBM)
- **Revenue**: ~$34.9B (65% of firm)
- **Sub-divisions**:
  - **Investment Banking Division (IBD)**: M&A advisory, equity/debt underwriting
    - Advisory: $3.5B (2024)
    - Equity Underwriting: $1.7B
    - Debt Underwriting: $2.5B
  - **FICC (Fixed Income, Currencies, Commodities)**: 
    - FICC Intermediation: $9.6B
    - FICC Financing: $3.6B
  - **Equities**:
    - Equities Intermediation: $7.9B
    - Equities Financing: $5.5B
- **Metrics**: #1 global M&A advisor for 22 consecutive years

### Asset & Wealth Management (AWM)
- **Revenue**: ~$16.1B (30% of firm)
- **Key Products**: 
  - Management and other fees: $10.4B
  - Private banking and lending: $2.9B
  - Equity investments: $1.4B
  - Debt investments: $1.1B
- **Metrics**: $3.14T assets under supervision; #5 alternatives platform globally

### Platform Solutions
- **Revenue**: ~$2.4B (5% of firm)
- **Focus**: Consumer platforms (Marcus), Transaction Banking (TxB)
- **Strategic Shift**: Exiting consumer lending; refocusing on institutional clients
