## § 4 · Core Philosophy: The 14 Business Principles

Established by John Whitehead in 1979, these principles remain foundational to Goldman Sachs culture:

### The Whitehead Principles

1. **Our clients' interests always come first.**
   > "Our experience shows that if we serve our clients well, our own success will follow."

2. **Our assets are our people, capital and reputation.**
   > "If any of these is ever diminished, the last is the most difficult to restore."

3. **Our goal is to provide superior returns to our shareholders.**
   > (Added 1998 before IPO)

4. **We take great pride in the professional quality of our work.**
   > "We have an indescribable determination to achieve excellence."

5. **We stress creativity and imagination in everything we do.**
   > "We are determined to identify and recruit the very best people."

6. **We make an unusual effort to identify and recruit the very best people.**
   > "We offer our people the opportunity to move ahead more rapidly."

7. **We offer our people the opportunity to move ahead more rapidly.**
   > "Being diverse is not optional; it is what we must be." *(Added 2001)*

8. **The dedication of our people to the firm and the intense effort they give.**
   > "These are the core of our competitive advantage."

9. **We consider our size an asset that we try hard to preserve.**
   > "We want to be big enough to undertake large transactions."

10. **We constantly strive to anticipate the rapidly changing needs.**
    > "We offer new products and services to meet those needs."

11. **We regularly receive confidential information.**
    > "If we were to misuse this information, we would forfeit our clients' trust."

12. **Our business is highly competitive, and we aggressively seek to expand.**
    > "But we always do so in a professional, ethical manner."

13. **Integrity and honesty are at the heart of our business.**
    > "We expect our people to maintain high ethical standards."

14. **Our business is people and their ideas.**
    > "We have succeeded because we have been consistently profitable."
