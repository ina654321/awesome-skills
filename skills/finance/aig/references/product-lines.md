# AIG Product Lines Deep Dive

## North America Commercial Insurance

### Property

**Target Market:**
- Fortune 1000 corporations
- Middle market companies ($50M-1B revenue)
- Real estate investment trusts (REITs)
- Educational institutions
- Healthcare facilities

**Coverage Offerings:**

| Coverage Type | Description | Typical Limits |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| Building & Personal Property | Physical damage to structures and contents | $1M-1B+ |
| Business Income | Lost profits during restoration | Actual loss sustained |
| Extra Expense | Costs to minimize business interruption | $1M-50M |
| Contingent BI | Dependent properties (suppliers/customers) | $5M-100M |
| Ingress/Egress | Physical prevention of access | $1M-10M |
| Service Interruption | Utility disruption | $1M-25M |

**Differentiators:**
- Global Property Program coordination
- Engineering-based risk improvement
- Natural catastrophe modeling
- Flexible deductible structures

### Casualty

**Primary General Liability:**
- Premises/operations
- Products/completed operations
- Personal & advertising injury
- Medical payments

**Excess/Umbrella:**
- Follow-form coverage

- Limits: $5M-200M+

**Auto Liability:**
- Commercial auto
- Hired/non-owned auto
- Garage liability

**Key Industries:**
- Construction
- Manufacturing
- Retail
- Hospitality
- Real estate

### Financial Lines

**Management Liability:**

| Product | Coverage | Target |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Public D&O | Securities claims | Public companies |
| Private D&O | Management liability | PE portfolio, private firms |
| Non-Profit D&O | Board protection | Charitable orgs |
| Employment Practices | Wrongful termination, harassment | All employers |
| Fiduciary | ERISA claims | Plan sponsors |

**Professional Liability:**

1. **Technology E&O**
   - Software/systems failure
   - Data breach coverage integration
   - Media liability
   - Target: SaaS companies, IT consultants

2. **Cyber Insurance**
   - Standalone cyber coverage
   - First and third party
   - Business interruption
   - Cybercrime (social engineering)
   - Regulatory coverage

3. **Miscellaneous E&O**
   - Architects/Engineers
   - Accountants
   - Lawyers (via partners)
   - Consultants
