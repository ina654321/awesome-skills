# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Official Documentation

- [SEC Investment Company Act of 1940](https://www.sec.gov/fast-answers/answersinvco1940html.html) - Regulation of investment companies
- [SEC Form N-2](https://www.sec.gov/forms) - Closed-end fund registration
- [FINRA Rules 4511-4550](https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance) - Member firm operations
- [SEC EDGAR Fund Documents](https://www.sec.gov/edgar/searchedgar/companysearch) - Public fund filings
- [Investment Company Institute](https://www.ici.org) - Industry association resources
- [FASB ASC 320](https://asc.fasb.org) - Investment securities accounting
- [AICPA Audit Guide: Investment Companies](https://www.aicpa.org) - Audit guidance

## 7.2 Regulatory Framework

| Regulation | Authority | Application |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Investment Company Act 1940** | SEC | Fund formation, structure |
| **Investment Advisers Act 1940** | SEC | Advisory relationships |
| **Dodd-Frank Act** | SEC/CFTC | Systemic risk, derivatives |
| **EMIR** | EU | European derivatives reporting |
| **AIFMD** | EU | Alternative fund managers |

## 7.3 Professional Standards

| Standard | Body | Purpose |
|----------|------|---------|
| **GIPS** | CFA Institute | Performance presentation |
| **Global Investment Performance Standards** | CFA Institute | Global performance standards |
| **IIRC** | International Integrated Reporting | Financial disclosure |
| **SAS 122** | AICPA | Auditing investment companies |

## 7.4 Compliance Requirements

- Form N-2: Annual and semi-annual shareholder reports
- Form N-CSR: Certified shareholder report
- Form N-PORT: Monthly portfolio holdings
- Form N-CEN: Annual fund census
- 13F: Quarterly institutional holdings
- Schedule 13D: Beneficial ownership reporting
