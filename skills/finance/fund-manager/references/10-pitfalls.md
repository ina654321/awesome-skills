# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Regulatory Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Inadequate liquidity stress testing** | 🔴 High | Implement comprehensive stress scenarios |
| 2 | **Failure to file Form N-CSR on time** | 🔴 High | Establish filing calendar with alerts |
| 3 | **Cross-fund trading violations** | 🔴 High | Centralize trade allocation process |
| 4 | **Benchmark definition inaccuracy** | 🟡 Medium | Annual benchmark review and documentation |
| 5 | **Insufficient disclosure in prospectus** | 🟡 Medium | Legal review of all marketing materials |

## 10.2 Operational Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Custody reconciliation failures** | 🔴 High | Daily reconciliation with independent verification |
| 2 | **Trade settlement misses** | 🔴 High | Automated pre-settlement matching |
| 3 | **Pricing errors in NAV calculation** | 🔴 High | Independent price verification |
| 4 | **Inadequate disaster recovery** | 🔴 High | Tested BCP with annual review |
| 5 | **Manual processes causing errors** | 🟡 Medium | Automate where possible |

## 10.3 Investment Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Concentration risk ignore** | 🔴 High | Monitor position limits daily |
| 2 | **Leverage exceeding limitations** | 🔴 High | Real-time leverage monitoring |
| 3 | **Style drift** | 🟡 Medium | Quarterly style analysis |
| 4 | **Inadequate diversification** | 🟡 Medium | Minimum position limits |
| 5 | **Performance fee miscalculation** | 🔴 High | Independent verification |

## 10.4 Compliance Checklist

- [ ] IPS current and board-approved
- [ ] Form N-2 accurate and current
- [ ] Liquidity stress testing documented
- [ ] 13F and Schedule 13D filed accurately
- [ ] Trade allocation fair and documented
- [ ] Valuation policy documented and followed
- [ ] Disaster recovery tested annually
- [ ] Personal trading records current
- [ ] Affiliate transactions disclosed
- [ ] Soft commission arrangements documented
