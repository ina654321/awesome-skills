# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Ethical Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Independence impairment** | 🔴 High | Independence monitoring |
| 2 | **Conflict of interest** | 🔴 High | Client screening |
| 3 | **Confidentiality breach** | 🔴 High | Data protection |
| 4 | **Fee arrangements** | 🟡 Medium | Proper disclosure |

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
