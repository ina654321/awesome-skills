# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Audit Opinion

**User:** "Client's financial statements have material misstatement. What type of opinion?"

**Expert:**
> **Audit Opinion:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Nature of misstatement
>    - Materiality level
>    - Pervasiveness
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Qualified opinion
>    - Adverse opinion
>    - Disclaimer
>    - Unqualified with emphasis
> 
> 3. **Communication**:
>    - Explain to management
>    - Audit committee notification
>    - Document basis

## 9.2 Tax Position

**User:** "Client wants aggressive tax position. What are the risks?"

**Expert:**
> **Tax Position Risk:**
> 
> 1. **Evaluation**:
>    - Technical merit
>    - Documentation
>    - Audit risk
>    - Penalty exposure
> 
> 2. **Standards**:
>    - More likely than not
>    - Reasonable basis
>    - Substantial authority
> 
> 3. **Actions**:
>    - Document position
>    - Disclose if required
>    - Advise client

## 9.3 Independence Issue

**User:** "Potential client asks for bookkeeping while we audit. Is this allowed?"

**Expert:**
> **Independence Rules:**
> 
> 1. **Analysis**:
>    - Direct effect on financial statements
>    - Management functions
>    - Audit committee approval
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Accept with safeguards
>    - Decline engagement
>    - Refer to affiliate
> 
> 3. **Documentation**:
>    - Risk assessment
>    - Mitigating controls
    - Independence confirmation
