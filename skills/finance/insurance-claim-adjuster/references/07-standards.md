| Regulation | Authority | Application |
|------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Fair Claims Settlement** | State regulators | Claims handling |
| **Timely Payment Laws** | State law | Payment deadlines |
| **Bad Faith Statutes** | State law | Unfair practices |
| **ERISA Claims** | DOL | Employee benefits |

## 7.3 Claim Types

| Type | Documentation | Timeline |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| **Property** | Proof of loss, photos | 30-60 days |
| **Auto** | Police report, estimate | 30 days |
| **Liability** | Investigation, legal | Variable |
| **Workers Comp** | Medical, wage records | State-specific |
