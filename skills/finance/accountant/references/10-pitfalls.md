# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Cookie-jar reserves** | 🔴 High | Build reserves consistently; do not use for earnings management |
| 2 | **Late journal entries after close** | 🔴 High | Implement hard close deadlines; review controls |
| 3 | **Manual spreadsheet-based reconciliations** | 🟡 Medium | Automate with ERP reconciliation modules |
| 4 | **Inadequate documentation** | 🟡 Medium | Maintain contemporaneous workpapers for all significant entries |
| 5 | **Ignoring intercompany eliminations** | 🔴 High | Reconcile intercompany balances monthly; automate elimination entries |
| 6 | **Applying wrong discount rate for leases** | 🟡 Medium | Use incremental borrowing rate, not risk-free rate |
| 7 | **Recognizing revenue too early (ASC 606)** | 🔴 High | Follow 5-step model; document POB determination |

## 10.2 Best Practices

1. **Close faster** — Target 5 business days; automate repetitive entries, pre-format flux analyses.
2. **Reconcile daily** — Bank and key BS accounts reconciled weekly, not just month-end.
3. **Document estimates** — Maintain contemporaneous support for all significant estimates (reserve, useful life, fair value).
4. **Review prior period adjustments** — All material adjustments require restatement and root cause analysis.
5. **Segregate duties** — No single person controls a transaction end-to-end.

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All balance sheet accounts reconciled within 5 days of period-end
- [ ] Flux analysis completed for all accounts > $10K variance
- [ ] Revenue recognized per ASC 606 5-step model
- [ ] Lease liability and ROU asset recorded for all leases > 12 months
- [ ] Intercompany eliminations completed and verified
- [ ] Goodwill impairment tested annually (ASC 350)
- [ ] Deferred tax positions reviewed per ASC 740
- [ ] Subsequent events reviewed through date of report issuance
