# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Financial Close Process

```
Phase 1: Pre-Closing (Days 1-3)
├── Receive and reconcile sub-ledger balances
├── Review and post adjusting journal entries
├── Clear suspense accounts and reconciling items
└── Prepare preliminary trial balance

Phase 2: Closing (Days 4-7)
├── Finalize all journal entries and adjustments
├── Complete intercompany eliminations
├── Review account reconciliations
├── Prepare workpaper documentation
└── Submit to supervisor for review

Phase 3: Reporting (Days 8-10)
├── Generate financial statements (P&L, BS, CF)
├── Prepare financial commentary and KPIs
├── Conduct flux analysis vs. prior period
├── Submit to controller/CFO for approval
└── File regulatory reports (10-Q, 10-K, etc.)
```

## 8.2 Audit Preparation Workflow

```
Step 1: Planning (30-60 days before year-end)
├── Receive audit request list and timeline
├── Prepare rollforward schedules for key accounts
├── Organize supporting documentation
└── Schedule inventory observation dates

Step 2: Execution (During audit)
├── Respond to auditor inquiries within 24 hours
├── Provide requested documents and explanations
├── Review and approve audit adjustments
└── Address control deficiency findings

Step 3: Completion (Post-audit)
├── Review audited financial statements
├── Approve management representation letter
├── Implement control recommendations
└── Document lessons learned for next cycle
```

## 8.3 Month-End Workflow

```
Day 1-2: Accruals and Adjustments
├── Accrue prepaid expenses
├── Record depreciation and amortization
├── Accrue wages and related taxes
├── Reconcile bank statements
└── Post intercompany entries

Day 3-4: Account Reconciliation
├── Reconcile all balance sheet accounts
├── Clear old outstanding items
├── Investigate variances > threshold
└── Prepare flux analysis

Day 5: Final Review and Sign-off
├── Controller review of trial balance
├── Approve all journal entries
├── Generate financial statements
└── Distribute to stakeholders
```
