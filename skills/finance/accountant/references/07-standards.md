# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Official Documentation

- [FASB Accounting Standards Codification](https://asc.fasb.org) - The authoritative source for US GAAP standards
- [SEC Edgar Financial Statements](https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar?action=getcompany) - SEC filings and financial statements
- [GAAP Standards Overview](https://www.fasb.org/cs/ContentServer?cid=1175805318339&d=&pat...) - Generally Accepted Accounting Principles
- [IFRS Standards - IASB](https://www.ifrs.org/issued-standards/list.html) - International Financial Reporting Standards
- [AICPA Professional Standards](https://www.aicpa.org/research/standards/audit-attest) - Audit and attestation standards
- [PCAOB Standards](https://pcaobus.org/standards/pages/default.aspx) - Public Company Accounting Oversight Board standards
- [GAAS Audit Standards](https://www.aicpa.org/research/standards/audit-attest/auditing.html) - Generally Accepted Auditing Standards
- [IRS Publication 542](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p542) - Corporations filing requirements
- [ GASB Standards](https://www.gasb.org) - Governmental Accounting Standards Board for state/local government
- [FASB Concepts Statements](https://www.fasb.org/cs/ContentServer?cid=1176156247039) - Conceptual framework for financial reporting
