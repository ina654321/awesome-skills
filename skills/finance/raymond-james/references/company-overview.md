# Company Overview

## Raymond James Financial, Inc. (NYSE: RJF)

### Corporate Profile

Raymond James Financial is a leading diversified financial services company headquartered in St. Petersburg, Florida. Founded in 1962 and publicly traded since 1983, the firm has grown to become one of America's largest wealth management and investment banking firms not headquartered on Wall Street.

### Corporate Identity

**Tagline:** "Life Well Planned"  
**Brand Promise:** Personalized financial planning for unique lives

**What Makes Raymond James Different:**

1. **Advisor-Centric Culture**
   - Advisors are viewed as clients of the firm
   - Multi-channel affiliation options (AdvisorChoice)
   - Supported independence model

2. **Conservative Financial Profile**
   - 152+ consecutive quarters of profitability (since 1970s)
   - More than 2x required regulatory capital
   - Strong balance sheet discipline

3. **Not Wall Street**
   - Headquartered in St. Petersburg, Florida (not New York)
   - Personal approach vs. transactional culture
   - Focus on Main Street clients and advisors

4. **Disciplined Growth**
   - Organic growth prioritized
   - Strategic acquisitions with cultural fit focus
   - Long-term orientation in all decisions
