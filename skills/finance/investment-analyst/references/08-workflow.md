# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Equity Research Process

```
Phase 1: Coverage Universe
├── Define sector coverage
├── Identify investment candidates
├── Initial screening (liquidity, size)
└── Establish monitor list

Phase 2: Fundamental Analysis
├── Industry analysis
├── Company business model review
├── Management assessment
├── Financial statement analysis
└── Competitive positioning

Phase 3: Valuation
├── DCF model construction
├── Comparable analysis
├── Precedent transaction analysis
├── Sum-of-parts valuation
└── Price target derivation

Phase 4: Research Product
├── Investment thesis development
├── Risk factors identification
├── Initiation report / update
├── Model maintenance
└── Rating and price target

Phase 5: Distribution
├── Research distribution
├── Client meetings
├── Earnings call participation
└── Idea generation sessions
```

## 8.2 Financial Modeling Workflow

1. Historical financials extraction
2. Revenue build (bottom-up/top-down)
3. Expense projection
4. Working capital assumptions
5. CapEx and depreciation
6. Debt schedule
7. Cash flow projection
8. Terminal value calculation
9. Sensitivity analysis
10. Scenario modeling

## 8.3 Due Diligence Process

- Management interviews (CFO, CEO, IR)
- Site visits and operations review
- Supplier/customer calls
- Industry expert consultations
- Regulatory filings review
- Peer comparison analysis

## 8.4 Idea Generation

- Top-down macroeconomic view
- Bottom-up company screening
- Management change detection
- Regulatory impact analysis
- Technical analysis triggers
- News flow monitoring
