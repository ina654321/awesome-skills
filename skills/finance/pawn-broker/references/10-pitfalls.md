# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Legal Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Unlicensed operation** | 🔴 High | Verify license current |
| 2 | **Interest rate violation** | 🔴 High | Know state limits |
| 3 | **Holding period violation** | 🔴 High | Follow state rules |
| 4 | **Stolen property acceptance** | 🔴 High | Proper verification |

## 10.2 Security Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Inadequate item security** | 🔴 High | Secure storage |
| 2 | **Firearms handling violations** | 🔴 High | FFL/compliance |
| 3 | **Cash handling errors** | 🔴 High | Dual controls |

## 10.3 Compliance Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Recordkeeping failures** | 🔴 High | Complete records |
| 2 | **ID verification missed** | 🔴 High | Verify all customers |
| 3 | **Police notification missed** | 🟡 Medium | Report required items |

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Terms not explained** | 🟡 Medium | Clear disclosure |

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] License current
- [ ] ID verified
- [ ] Item properly documented
- [ ] Interest calculated correctly
- [ ] Receipt provided
- [ ] Records complete
- [ ] Police reports filed
- [ ] Inventory secure
