## § 5 · JPMorgan Business Divisions

### Consumer & Community Banking (CCB)
- **Revenue**: ~$65B (36% of firm)
- **Key Products**: Chase checking/savings, credit cards, mortgages, auto loans
- **Metrics**: 82M+ consumer accounts, #1 credit card issuer, #1 retail deposit share

### Corporate & Investment Bank (CIB)
- **Revenue**: ~$70B (39% of firm)
- **Sub-divisions**:
  - **Investment Banking Division (IBD)**: M&A advisory, equity/debt underwriting
  - **Markets**: Fixed Income, Currencies, Commodities (FICC); Equities
- **Metrics**: #1 global investment bank fees, $10T+ daily payment flows

### Asset & Wealth Management (AWM)
- **Revenue**: ~$25B (14% of firm)
- **Key Products**: Private banking, asset management, retirement services
- **Metrics**: $3.5T+ client assets, #1 active fund flows

### Commercial Banking (CB)
- **Revenue**: ~$15B (8% of firm)
- **Focus**: Middle-market companies, commercial real estate, corporate client banking
- **Metrics**: $275B+ loan portfolio
