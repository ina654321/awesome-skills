## §2 · Basel III Endgame Framework

### §2.1 Capital Requirements Overview

| Component | Requirement | Purpose |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Minimum CET1 | 4.5% | Core loss-absorbing capacity |
| Capital Conservation Buffer | 2.5% | Absorb losses during stress |
| G-SIB Surcharge | 1.0% - 3.5% | Systemic risk buffer |
| CCyB (when active) | 0% - 2.5% | Countercyclical adjustment |
| **Total CET1 Minimum** | **7.0% - 13.5%** | Combined requirement |

### §2.2 Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA) Calculation

**Credit Risk Approaches:**
1. **Standardized Approach**: Fixed risk weights by asset class
2. **Expanded Risk-Based Approach**: Model-based with standardized floor
3. **Output Floor**: 72.5% of standardized RWA

**Key RWA Components:**
| Risk Type | Method | Impact |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Credit Risk | Internal models / Standardized | Largest component |
| Market Risk | FRTB (Fundamental Review of Trading Book) | Trading activities |
| Operational Risk | Standardized | Business indicator-based |
| CVA Risk | Model-based | Derivatives counterparty risk |

### §2.3 Leverage Requirements

| Ratio | Formula | JPMorgan Standard |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| Tier 1 Leverage | Tier 1 Capital / Average Assets | 7.0% (vs 4% min) |
| Supplementary Leverage (SLR) | Tier 1 Capital / Total Exposure | 5.5% (vs 3% min) |

## §3 · Dodd-Frank Act Requirements

### §3.1 Annual Stress Testing (CCAR/DFAST)

**Process Timeline:**
- January: Scenarios released by Fed
- April: Bank submissions due
- June: Results published

**Scenarios Required:**
1. Baseline — Moderate economic conditions
2. Adverse — Recession scenario
3. Severely Adverse — Financial crisis-level stress

**Key Metrics:**
| Metric | Minimum Through Stress |
|--------|----------------------|
| CET1 Ratio | 4.5% |
| Tier 1 Ratio | 6.0% |
| Total Capital | 8.0% |
| Tier 1 Leverage | 3.0% or 4% |

### §3.2 Living Will Requirements

- Resolution plans submitted annually
- Demonstrate orderly wind-down capability
- No taxpayer bailout required

### §3.3 Volcker Rule

- Proprietary trading restrictions
- Covered fund limitations
- Compliance program requirements

## §5 · Risk Management Standards

| Parameter | JPMorgan Standard |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Confidence Level | 99% |
| Time Horizon | 1 day (trading) |
| Method | Historical simulation |
| Lookback Period | 1 year |
| Minimum Observations | 250 |
