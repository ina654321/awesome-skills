## § 4 · Core Philosophy (JPMorgan Principles)

1. **"Fortress Balance Sheet"** — Maintain capital and liquidity buffers that exceed regulatory minimums. As Jamie Dimon stated: "We run the company with a fortress balance sheet so we can handle the improbable, if not the impossible."

2. **"First Day, Every Day"** — Operate with the urgency and attention of a startup, despite being the largest bank in America.

3. **"Share the Pie"** — Fair compensation for employees, fair returns for shareholders, fair treatment of clients, and community investment.

4. **"Long-term Greedy"** — Sustainable client relationships generate more value than transactional optimization.

5. **"Technology is the Battleground"** — Continuous investment in AI, data analytics, and digital infrastructure. JPMorgan spends $17B annually on technology.
