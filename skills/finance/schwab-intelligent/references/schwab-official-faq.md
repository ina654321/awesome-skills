### How does Schwab make money if there's no advisory fee?
Schwab Intelligent Portfolios includes a cash allocation (6-30% depending on risk profile) that is swept to an FDIC-insured deposit account at Charles Schwab Bank. Schwab Bank earns revenue from these deposits. This is disclosed in the SEC settlement of 2022.

The underlying ETFs charge management fees ranging from 0.03% to 0.18%, with a weighted average of approximately 0.12% annually. These are deducted from fund assets, not billed separately.

## Portfolio Management

### How are portfolios constructed?
Schwab uses Modern Portfolio Theory to build diversified portfolios from 51 ETFs across 20+ asset classes. Portfolios are matched to one of 12 risk profiles based on your questionnaire responses.

### What ETFs are used?
Schwab primarily uses its own proprietary ETFs (SCHB, SCHX, SCHA, SCHF, SCHE, SCHZ, etc.) along with select third-party ETFs for specific asset classes.

### How often does rebalancing occur?
Portfolios are monitored daily. Rebalancing occurs when asset allocation drifts beyond target thresholds, which varies by market volatility. In calm markets, rebalancing may be infrequent; in volatile periods, multiple rebalances may occur.

### Can I customize my portfolio?
Limited customization is available:
- You can adjust risk tolerance (moves you between 12 profiles)
- You can exclude up to 3 ETFs and specify replacements
- You cannot exclude asset classes entirely

## Tax Features

### What is tax-loss harvesting?
Tax-loss harvesting sells investments at a loss to offset capital gains taxes. Schwab's algorithm monitors daily and automatically executes TLH trades when opportunities meet thresholds.

### Who qualifies for tax-loss harvesting?
- Taxable accounts only (not IRAs)
- Account balance of $50,000 or more
- Must opt-in to the feature

### What is a wash sale?
A wash sale occurs when you sell an investment at a loss and purchase a "substantially identical" security within 30 days before or after the sale. This disallows the tax loss. Schwab's TLH algorithm uses alternate ETFs in the same asset class to avoid wash sales.

### What is Intelligent Income?
Schwab Intelligent Income is a feature for retirees that:
- Calculates safe withdrawal amounts
- Optimizes withdrawal sequencing (taxable → tax-deferred → Roth)
- Handles RMD calculations
- Deposits monthly income to your linked bank account

## Cash & Banking

### Why is there cash in my portfolio?
All Schwab Intelligent Portfolios maintain a cash allocation ranging from 6% (aggressive) to 30% (conservative). This serves as:
- Volatility dampener
- Source for rebalancing without selling securities
- Revenue source for Schwab (via Schwab Bank)

### Where is the cash held?
Cash is swept to an FDIC-insured deposit account at Charles Schwab Bank, SSB. Current interest rates are published monthly and based on the seven-day yield of Schwab Government Money Fund.

### Can I withdraw cash?
Yes. You can withdraw from your account at any time by logging in and requesting a transfer. Cash allocations can also be adjusted by changing your risk profile.
