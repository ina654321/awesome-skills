# Wells Fargo Detailed Workflows

## Commercial Lending Workflow

### Phase 1: Origination (Weeks 1-2)

| Step | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Client meeting & needs assessment | Relationship Manager | Opportunity summary |
| 2 | Preliminary credit review | Credit Analyst | Initial risk assessment |
| 3 | KYC/AML screening | Compliance | Clearance documentation |
| 4 | Term sheet preparation | Underwriter | Draft term sheet |
| 5 | Client presentation | Relationship Manager | Signed indication |

### Phase 2: Underwriting (Weeks 3-6)

| Step | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Financial analysis (3-year history) | Credit Analyst | Spread financials |
| 2 | Industry research | Research Team | Industry report |
| 3 | Collateral evaluation | Appraiser/Evaluator | Appraisal report |
| 4 | Risk rating assignment | Credit Officer | Risk rating memo |
| 5 | Covenant structure | Underwriter | Covenant package |
| 6 | Credit memo preparation | Underwriter | Complete credit memo |

### Phase 3: Approval (Weeks 7-8)

| Approval Level | Exposure | Approver |
|----------------|----------|----------|
| Delegated | <$5M | Regional Credit Officer |
| Senior Credit | $5M-$25M | Division Credit Officer |
| Credit Committee | $25M-$100M | Committee vote |
| Board Level | >$100M | Board Risk Committee |

### Phase 4: Documentation & Closing (Weeks 9-10)

| Step | Activity | Owner | Deliverable |
|------|----------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Loan documentation | Legal | Signed loan agreement |
| 2 | Collateral perfection | Loan Ops | UCC filings, mortgages |
| 3 | Insurance verification | Loan Ops | Insurance certificates |
| 4 | Funding | Treasury | Wire transfer |
| 5 | Booking | Loan Ops | System entry |

## Consumer Lending Workflow

### Credit Card Application Flow

```
Application Submitted
        ↓
System Decision (Instant)
    ├─ Approved → Account Opening
    ├─ Declined → Adverse Action Notice
    └─ Manual Review → Underwriter Review
                        ↓
            ├─ Approved → Account Opening
            └─ Declined → Adverse Action Notice
```

### Decision Criteria

| Factor | Weight | Source |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Credit Score | 35% | Credit Bureau |
| Income/Debt Ratio | 25% | Application + Verification |
| Payment History | 20% | Credit Bureau |
| Relationship Depth | 15% | Internal Data |
| Employment Stability | 5% | Application |

### Documentation Requirements

| Product | Primary ID | Income Verification | Additional |
|---------|------------|---------------------|------------|
| Credit Card | Driver's License | W-2 or Pay Stub | SSN |
| Auto Loan | Driver's License | W-2 or Tax Return | Insurance Proof |
| Personal Loan | Driver's License | W-2 or Tax Return | Purpose Statement |
| Home Equity | Driver's License | Full Documentation | Property Appraisal |

## Wealth Management Client Onboarding

### Phase 1: Discovery (Week 1)

| Activity | Detail | Deliverable |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Client questionnaire | Goals, risk tolerance, time horizon | Completed profile |
| Financial statement review | Assets, liabilities, income, expenses | Net worth statement |
| Tax return review (2 years) | Identify tax optimization opportunities | Tax summary memo |
| Estate document review | Wills, trusts, powers of attorney | Estate planning notes |

### Phase 2: Strategy Development (Weeks 2-3)

| Activity | Detail | Deliverable |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Investment policy statement | Asset allocation, restrictions | Signed IPS |
| Portfolio construction | Security selection, diversification | Model portfolio |
| Financial plan development | Retirement, education, legacy | Comprehensive plan |
| Implementation roadmap | Account setup, transfers | Project plan |

### Phase 3: Implementation (Weeks 4-6)

| Activity | Detail | Deliverable |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Account opening | New accounts, transfers | Funded accounts |
| Asset transfer | ACATS, wire transfers | Transfer confirmations |
| Insurance review | Life, disability, long-term care | Coverage analysis |
| Estate planning coordination | Attorney engagement | Draft documents |
