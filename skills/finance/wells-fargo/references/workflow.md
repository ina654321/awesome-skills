# Wells Fargo Standard Workflows

## Consumer Banking & Lending Workflow

### Credit Card Origination

```
Phase 1: Product Design (Weeks 1-4)
├── Market research and customer segmentation
├── Risk assessment and underwriting criteria
├── Regulatory compliance review
├── Pricing and rewards structure design
└── Approval: Product Committee

Phase 2: Development (Weeks 5-12)
├── Technology system development
├── Control framework implementation
├── Marketing material creation
├── Staff training program
└── Compliance sign-off

Phase 3: Launch (Week 13+)
├── Soft launch (limited market)
├── Monitor key metrics
├── Adjust as needed
└── Full market rollout
```

**Key Controls:**
- Credit underwriting model validation
- Fair lending analysis
- Disclosure compliance review
- Marketing claim substantiation

### Home Lending Origination

**Application Processing:**
1. Application intake and documentation
2. Credit and capacity assessment
3. Collateral evaluation (appraisal)
4. Underwriting decision
5. Closing and funding
6. Servicing transfer (if applicable)

**Regulatory Requirements:**
- Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
- Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA)
- Fair Lending monitoring
- Ability-to-Repay (ATR) verification

## Commercial Banking Workflow

### Relationship Onboarding

```
Step 1: Prospecting and Qualification
├── Target identification (industry, size, geography)
├── Initial creditworthiness assessment
├── Conflict and risk screening
└── Go/No-Go decision

Step 2: Credit Underwriting
├── Financial analysis (3+ years)
├── Industry and competitive assessment
├── Management evaluation
├── Site visit (material relationships)
├── Risk rating assignment
└── Credit approval (appropriate authority)

Step 3: Documentation and Closing
├── Legal documentation preparation
├── Collateral perfection
├── Compliance verification
├── Closing and funding
└── Booking in systems

Step 4: Ongoing Management
├── Annual review and renewal
├── Quarterly monitoring
├── Covenant compliance tracking
├── Early warning detection
└── Portfolio management
```

### Treasury Management Implementation

## Corporate & Investment Banking Workflow

### Investment Banking Transaction

**M&A Advisory Process:**

```
Phase 1: Engagement (Weeks 1-2)
├── Pitch and mandate competition
├── Conflict check and clearance
├── Engagement letter negotiation
├── Retainer and fee structure
└── Team assembly

Phase 2: Preparation (Weeks 3-6)
├── Valuation analysis
├── Buyer/seller universe identification
├── Marketing materials preparation
├── Management presentation coaching
└── Data room setup

Phase 3: Marketing (Weeks 7-14)
├── Teaser distribution
├── NDA execution
├── CIM distribution
├── Management meetings
├── Indication of interest (IOI) receipt
└── Bid process management

Phase 4: Execution (Weeks 15-20)
├── Final bids and negotiations
├── Letter of intent (LOI)
├── Due diligence coordination
├── Definitive agreement negotiation
├── Regulatory approvals
└── Closing
```

**Capital Markets Offering:**

1. **Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks)**
   - Underwriter selection
   - Due diligence
   - Documentation preparation
   - Roadshow preparation

2. **Marketing (2 weeks)**
   - Investor meetings
   - Book building
   - Price discovery

3. **Pricing and Allocation**
   - Final pricing
   - Allocation decisions
   - Closing

4. **Post-Closing**
   - Stabilization (if applicable)
   - Market making
   - Research coverage

## Risk Management Workflow

### Operational Risk Event Management

```
1. Event Identification
   └── Employee reports, monitoring detects, audit finds

2. Initial Assessment (24 hours)
   ├── Severity assessment
   ├── Immediate containment
   └── Escalation (if material)

3. Investigation (1-2 weeks)
   ├── Root cause analysis
   ├── Impact quantification
   ├── Control gap identification
   └── Remediation planning

4. Remediation (30-90 days)
   ├── Control enhancement
   ├── Process improvement
   ├── Training (if applicable)
   └── Monitoring enhancement

5. Closure
   ├── Validation of remediation
   ├── Sign-off by risk and business
   └── Lessons learned documentation
```

### Credit Review Process

**Annual Review:**
1. Financial statement analysis

5. Strategy and exposure review
6. Renewal decision

**Quarterly Monitoring:**
1. Financial covenant testing
2. Payment performance review
