# Chubb Corporate History

## Origins: The Chubb Corporation (1882-2016)

### Founding Era (1882-1900)

**1882 - The Beginning**
- Thomas Caldecot Chubb and his son Percy opened "Chubb & Son" in New York City
- Original business: Marine underwriting (insuring ships and cargo)
- Focused on "A1" (highest quality) risks only
- Established reputation for careful risk selection

**1901 - Federal Insurance Company**
- New York Marine Underwriters reorganized as Federal Insurance Company
- Expanded beyond marine into property and casualty
- Maintained selective underwriting philosophy

### Early Growth (1900-1960)

**1923 - Geographic Expansion**
- First branch office opened in Chicago
- Signaled intent to become national insurer
- Focus on commercial accounts in major cities

**1929 - Aviation Insurance**
- Chubb and Marine Office of America organized Associated Aviation Underwriters
- Entered emerging aviation insurance market
- Demonstrated innovation in specialty lines

**1939 - Vigilant Insurance Company**
- Founded Vigilant Insurance Company
- Expanded corporate structure for growth
- Added underwriting capacity

**1956 - Personal Lines Entry**
- Formed personal lines department
- Installed first computer (IBM 705)
- Beginning of technology investment

### Mid-Century Expansion (1960-1990)

**1967 - The Chubb Corporation**
- Formed as holding company
- Acquired Pacific Indemnity Corporation
- Strategic acquisitions became growth engine

**1971 - Diversification**
- Acquired United Life & Accident Insurance Company
- Founded Chubb Custom Market (entertainment insurance)
- Entered specialty entertainment industry coverage

**1976 - HNW Focus**
- Refocused on select markets including high-net-worth personal clients
- Recognized underserved affluent market segment
- Laid groundwork for Masterpiece concept

**1984 - Public Company**
- Listed on New York Stock Exchange (ticker: CB)
- Access to capital markets for growth
- Enhanced visibility and credibility

**1986 - Masterpiece Launch**
- Introduced Masterpiece® personal lines policy
- Revolutionary "agreed value" concept for HNW
- Set new standard for affluent homeowners insurance

### Modern Era (1990-2016)

**1990s - Market Leadership**
- Became leading HNW personal lines insurer
- Expanded commercial specialty lines
- International growth initiatives
- Consistently strong underwriting results

**2007 - Industry Recognition**
- Named "Best Admitted Property/Casualty Insurance Company" by Business Insurance
- Recognition for claims service excellence
- Market leadership confirmed

**2008-2015 - Pre-Acquisition**
- Continued organic growth in core markets
- Maintained underwriting discipline through soft market
- Strong capital position
- Premium growth constrained by pricing inadequacy

## ACE Limited (1985-2016)

### Founding and Early Years

**1985 - ACE Founded**
- Incorporated in 1985
- Opened first office in Bermuda
- Founded by a group of blue-chip companies seeking insurance capacity
- Focus on excess liability and specialty lines

**Leadership: Evan G. Greenberg**
- Became CEO of ACE in 2004
- Previously served as President and COO of AIG
- Son of Maurice "Hank" Greenberg (former AIG CEO)
- Strategic visionary focused on underwriting discipline

- Expanded global presence to 54 countries

## The Merger: ACE Acquires Chubb (2016)

### Transaction Details

**January 14, 2016 - Historic Merger**
- ACE Limited acquired The Chubb Corporation
- Purchase price: Approximately $29.5 billion
- Stock and cash transaction
- Created world's largest publicly traded property and casualty insurer

**Strategic Rationale**:
- Complementary strengths: ACE's global commercial + Chubb's HNW expertise
- Geographic diversification
- Product line expansion
- Scale advantages
- Cost synergies

### Post-Merger Integration

**Name Change Decision**
- ACE adopted the Chubb name globally
- Chubb had stronger brand recognition in target segments
- Retained ticker symbol CB
- Preserved Chubb's 130+ year heritage

**Global Integration Timeline**:

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| Jan 2016 | Transaction closed; ACE and Chubb operating as one organization in UK |
| Apr 2016 | Australia/NZ integration begins |
| May 2016 | European integration planning |
| Nov 2016 | Chubb Insurance Australia Limited formed |
| May 2017 | European legal integration completed (ACE European Group → Chubb European Group) |
| Sep 2016 | Lloyd's market integration (ACE Underwriting Agencies → Chubb Underwriting Agencies) |

**Employee Impact**:
- Combined workforce: ~31,000 (ACE) + ~10,000 (Chubb) = ~40,000+
- Integration achieved with minimal disruption
- Retained key talent from both organizations
