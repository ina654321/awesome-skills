## Financial Strategy

### Tricia Griffith's Priorities (2024-2025)

1. **"Get the Right Rate"**
   - Aggressive rate increases in 2023-2024
   - Profitability over growth when necessary
   - Result: Industry-leading combined ratio

2. **Sustainable Growth**
   - 5M+ policies added in 2024
   - Maintaining profitability during growth
   - Market share gains from competitors

3. **Capital Efficiency**
   - Strong returns on equity
   - Disciplined investment strategy
   - Balanced capital return to shareholders

4. **Technology Investment**
   - AI/ML for claims and underwriting
   - Telematics infrastructure
   - Digital customer experience
