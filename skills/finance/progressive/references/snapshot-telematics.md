# Snapshot: Progressive's Usage-Based Insurance Program

## Overview

**Snapshot** is Progressive's usage-based insurance (UBI) program, launched in 2008 as one of the first mass-market telematics offerings in the US auto insurance industry.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Launch Date** | 2008 |
| **Technology** | OBD-II plug-in device + Mobile app |
| **Market Position** | Industry-leading UBI program |
| **Participation** | Millions of active users |
| **Average Savings** | $169 at signup, $322 at renewal |

## How Snapshot Works

### Data Collection Methods

**Option 1: Plug-in Device (OBD-II)**
- Plugs into vehicle's OBD-II port (standard in cars 1996+)
- Typically located under dashboard
- Collects vehicle data directly from car's computer
- Cellular transmission to Progressive

**Option 2: Mobile App**
- Uses smartphone GPS and sensors
- Accelerometer for braking/acceleration detection
- Lower barrier to entry (no hardware)
- Most new enrollments use app

### Measured Driving Behaviors

| Behavior | How It's Measured | Risk Indication |
|----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Hard Braking** | Deceleration rate | Frequent hard braking = higher risk |
| **Rapid Acceleration** | Acceleration rate | Aggressive acceleration = higher risk |
| **Mileage** | Total miles driven | More miles = more exposure |
| **Time of Day** | When driving occurs | Late night (midnight-4am) = higher risk |
| **Phone Handling** | App detects phone use | Distracted driving = higher risk |

### The Scoring Process

```
Enrollment Period (Typically 6 months):
├── Initial participation discount: ~$26 average
├── Driving behavior monitoring active
├── Score calculated weekly
├── Final score determines renewal discount
└── Results: Discount, No Change, or Increase
```

| Outcome | Typical Impact | % of Customers |

| **Significant Discount** | Up to 30% off | ~40% |
| **Moderate Discount** | 10-20% off | ~25% |
| **No Change** | Base rate continues | ~20% |

- **At Signup**: $169 average discount for enrolling

1. **Data Depth**: 15+ years of telematics data vs competitors' 3-5 years
2. **Pricing Accuracy**: Can price both up AND down based on behavior
3. **Adverse Selection**: Risky drivers self-select out (fear of rate increases)

## Privacy and Data Security

### Data Usage

**Progressive Uses Snapshot Data For:**
- Insurance pricing
- Risk assessment
- Driving feedback
- Claims investigation (with consent)

**Progressive Does NOT:**
- Share individual data with third parties for marketing
- Use location data for non-insurance purposes
- Sell data to data brokers

### Privacy Controls

- Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- Customer can opt-out (though discount may be lost)
- Driving data deleted after policy ends (with some retention for regulatory purposes)
- California residents: Additional CCPA protections

## Common Questions

### Q: Will Snapshot always save me money?
A: No. While many drivers save, risky driving behaviors can result in higher rates. Progressive is one of the few insurers that can raise rates based on Snapshot data.

### Q: What if I drive for rideshare (Uber/Lyft)?
A: Personal Snapshot programs typically exclude commercial/rideshare driving. Progressive offers commercial rideshare coverage separately.

### Q: Does Snapshot track my location?
A: Yes, GPS data is collected to determine mileage, routes, and time of day. This data is used for insurance purposes only.

### Q: Can I opt out after enrolling?
A: Yes, but you may lose any participation discount and won't receive driving-based discounts at renewal.

### Q: What states is Snapshot available?
A: Snapshot is available in most states but not all. Not available in California for initial discount (but can be used for driving feedback).
