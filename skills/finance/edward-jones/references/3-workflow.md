### 3.2 Meeting Framework

**First Meeting Structure (90 minutes):**

| Time | Activity | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| 0-10 min | Warm welcome, rapport building | Establish comfort |
| 10-30 min | Life story, goals, values | Understand the person |
| 30-50 min | Current financial picture review | Assess starting point |
| 50-70 min | Concerns and questions | Address anxieties |
| 70-85 min | Next steps and process | Set expectations |
| 85-90 min | Schedule follow-up | Commit to relationship |

**Ongoing Review Meeting (45-60 minutes):**

| Time | Activity | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| 0-10 min | Personal check-in | Maintain relationship |
| 10-20 min | Life changes since last meeting | Update goals/circumstances |
| 20-40 min | Portfolio review and market update | Stay informed |
| 40-50 min | Adjustments and recommendations | Optimize strategy |
| 50-60 min | Questions and next steps | Clear action items |

### 3.3 Market Volatility Protocol

**When Markets Decline:**

1. **Proactive Outreach** – Contact concerned clients before they call you
2. **Educate on Normalcy** – "One out of four years, expect a downdraft"
3. **Reframe as Opportunity** – Rebalancing discounts quality investments
4. **Revisit Long-Term Goals** – Short-term volatility doesn't change life plans
5. **Avoid Predictions** – Don't time markets; stay disciplined
6. **Document Conversations** – Record advice for compliance and continuity

**Communication Templates:**
- "The market is doing what markets do—there will be volatility"
- "Your plan was built for times like these"
- "Let's look at what this means for your specific situation"
- "Rebalancing now may improve long-term returns"

### 3.4 New Client Onboarding Checklist

- [ ] Complete risk tolerance questionnaire + qualitative discussion
- [ ] Gather financial documents (statements, tax returns, insurance policies)
- [ ] Identify all goals with timelines and dollar amounts
- [ ] Create initial financial plan draft
- [ ] Present recommendations and investment strategy
- [ ] Execute account paperwork
- [ ] Set up systematic investment plan (if appropriate)
- [ ] Schedule 90-day check-in
- [ ] Add to review calendar (annual minimum)
- [ ] Introduce to branch office administrator
- [ ] Provide educational resources tailored to client
- [ ] Document client preferences and communication style
