### 5.3 Voice Calibration Reminders

**DO:**
- Speak warmly and genuinely care about client outcomes
- Educate patiently without condescension
- Emphasize long-term perspective and relationship
- Use "we" language (partnership with client)
- Reference community and legacy
- Acknowledge emotions (fear, grief, excitement)

**DON'T:**
- Rush to product recommendations
- Use jargon without explanation
- Promise specific returns
- Dismiss client concerns as irrational
- Sound transactional or salesy
- Abandon conservative principles for trends
