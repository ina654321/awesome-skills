# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Covenant Breach Analysis

**User:** "A rated company's debt service coverage ratio breached covenant. How should we respond?"

**Expert:**
> **Covenant Breach Response:**
> 
> 1. **Immediate Assessment**:
>    - Verify breach calculation
>    - Review waiver terms
>    - Assess cure period
> 
> 2. **Impact Analysis**:
>    - Estimate default probability impact
>    - Review other covenant thresholds
>    - Assess refinancing risk
> 
> 3. **Rating Action**:
>    - Place on CreditWatch negative
>    - Evaluate potential downgrade
>    - Determine timeline for action

## 9.2 Sovereign Rating Upgrade

**User:** "Country showing strong fiscal consolidation and growth. What metrics matter most?"

**Expert:**
> **Sovereign Upgrade Decision:**
> 
> 1. **Fiscal Metrics**:
>    - Primary balance improvement
>    - Debt-to-GDP trajectory
>    - Interest payment-to-revenue ratio
> 
> 2. **Structural Metrics**:
>    - GDP growth acceleration
>    - Export diversification
>    - Institutional quality scores
> 
> 3. **External Metrics**:
>    - Current account improvement
>    - Reserves adequacy
>    - Currency stability

## 9.3 Distressed Company

**User:** "Company filed for bankruptcy. What's the recovery analysis?"

**Expert:**
> **Bankruptcy Analysis:**
> 
> 1. **Case Assessment**:
>    - Chapter 11 vs. 7 analysis
>    - Asset valuation
>    - Claim priority structure
> 
> 2. **Recovery Estimation**:
>    - Enterprise value assessment
>    - Secured vs. unsecured claims
>    - Potential recovery range
> 
> 3. **Rating Action**:
>    - Downgrade to D
>    - Estimate recovery expectation
>    - Monitor proceedings
