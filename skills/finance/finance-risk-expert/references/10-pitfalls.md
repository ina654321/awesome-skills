# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Model Risk Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Inadequate model documentation** | 🔴 High | Comprehensive documentation standards |
| 2 | **Lack of independent validation** | 🔴 High | Segregation of model development/validation |
| 3 | **Backtest failures ignored** | 🔴 High | Root cause analysis required |
| 4 | **Model risk not aggregated** | 🔴 High | Enterprise model risk framework |
| 5 | **Governance gaps** | 🟡 Medium | Clear ownership and escalation |

## 10.2 Risk Measurement Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **VaR model limitations** | 🔴 High | Stress testing complement |
| 2 | **Correlation assumptions** | 🔴 High | Stress correlations |
| 3 | **Tail risk underestimation** | 🔴 High | Historical scenario analysis |
| 4 | **Data quality issues** | 🔴 High | Data governance framework |
| 5 | **Granularity inadequate** | 🔴 High | Proper segmentation |

## 10.3 Regulatory Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Capital inadequate for risk** | 🔴 High | Proper risk weighting |
| 2 | **Stress test assumptions weak** | 🔴 High | Realistic scenarios |
| 3 | **LCR calculation errors** | 🔴 High | Proper classification |
| 4 | **Risk limit breaches unreported** | 🔴 High | Escalation procedures |
| 5 | **Incomplete risk register** | 🟡 Medium | Regular updates |

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Risk taxonomy current
- [ ] Risk appetite defined and approved
- [ ] Models validated independently
- [ ] Backtesting performed
- [ ] Stress testing comprehensive
- [ ] Limits adequate for risk
- [ ] Reporting accurate and timely
- [ ] Governance documented
- [ ] Regulatory requirements met
