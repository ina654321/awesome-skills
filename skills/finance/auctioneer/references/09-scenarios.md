# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Reserve Price Dispute

**User:** "Seller won't accept winning bid. What are the seller's options?"

**Expert:**
> **Reserve Price Issues:**
> 
> 1. **Legal Options**:
>    - Reject all bids (reserve not met)
>    - Negotiate with high bidder
>    - Relist items
>    - Private sale negotiation
> 
> 2. **Contract Terms**:
>    - Review consignment agreement
>    - Check reserve terms
>    - Verify disclosure
> 
> 3. **Communication**:
>    - Explain to winning bidder
>    - Document seller decision
>    - Offer alternatives

## 9.2 Bidder Dispute

**User:** "Two bidders claim they won the same lot. How do we resolve?"

**Expert:**
> **Dispute Resolution:**
> 
> 1. **Evidence Review**:
>    - Check auction records
>    - Review audio/video if available
>    - Verify bidder numbers
> 
> 2. **Resolution**:
>    - Confirm actual winner
>    - Offer second choice lot if available
>    - Cancel lot if necessary
> 
> 3. **Prevention**:
>    - Clear announcements
>    - Proper bidder identification
>    - Written confirmation

## 9.3 Non-Paying Buyer

**User:** "Winning bidder won't pay. What's our recourse?"

**Expert:**
> **Non-Payment Handling:**
> 
> 1. **Attempts**:
>    - Contact multiple times
>    - Offer payment options
>    - Set deadline
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Resell items
>    - Report to authorities if fraud
>    - Keep deposit
>    - Legal action
> 
> 3. **Prevention**:
>    - Payment requirements upfront
>    - Credit verification
>    - Clear terms and conditions
