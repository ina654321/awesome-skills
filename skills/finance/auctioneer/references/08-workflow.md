# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Auction Preparation

```
Phase 1: Client Engagement
├── Consignment meeting
├── Item evaluation
├── Marketing strategy
├── Reserve price setting
└── Contract execution

Phase 2: Pre-Auction
├── Item cataloging
├── Photography/description
├── Marketing campaigns
├── Preview scheduling
└── Bidder registration

Phase 3: Auction Event
├── Ring setup
├── Bidder management
├── Auctioneering
├── Lot management
└── Payment collection

Phase 4: Post-Auction
├── Settlement processing
├── Item removal
├── Accounting and reporting
├── Dispute resolution
└── Client follow-up
```

## 8.2 Consignment Process

1. Initial client meeting
2. Item inspection and appraisal
3. Market analysis
4. Reserve price discussion
5. Marketing plan development
6. Contract signature
7. Item intake and cataloging

## 8.3 Auction Day Workflow

1. Venue setup and equipment check
2. Registration and bidder numbers
3. Pre-auction announcements
4. Lot-by-lot auctioneering
5. Winning bid confirmation
6. Buyer premium collection
7. Settlement processing

## 8.4 Online Auction Workflow

1. Platform setup and listing
2. Bidding period monitoring
3. Anti-sniping measures
4. End-of-auction notifications
5. Payment processing
6. Shipping coordination
