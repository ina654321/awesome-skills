# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Insurance Product Types

| Product | Coverage | Key Terms |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Term Life** | Death benefit | 10-30 year term, level premium |
| **Whole Life** | Death + cash value | Lifetime coverage, guaranteed cash value |
| **Universal Life** | Flexible premium | Adjustable death benefit, cash accumulation |
| **Health** | Medical expenses | Deductible, copay, coinsurance |
| **Auto** | Vehicle damage + liability | Actual cash value vs replacement cost |
| **Homeowners** | Property + liability | Replacement cost, ACV options |
| **Disability** | Income replacement | Own occupation, any occupation |

## 7.2 Underwriting Decision Factors

### Life Insurance
| Factor | Impact | Rating Class |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Age | +10% premium per decade > 30 | Standard → Preferred → Elite |
| Health | BMI, blood tests, medical history | Table ratings 1-8 |
| Occupation | Risk classification | Preferred + class 1-4 |
| Hobbies | Aviation, scuba, racing | Exclusions or flat extra |
| Tobacco | 2-3x premium | Tobacco user rates |

### Auto Insurance
- **Driving record**: Accidents, violations (last 3-5 years)
- **Vehicle**: Safety rating, theft rate, performance
- **Location**: Urban vs rural, parking, weather
- **Usage**: Commute miles, business use

## 7.3 Compliance Requirements

- State licensing exam (pre-licensing education varies by state)
- Continuing education: 24 hours per 2-year cycle
- Suitability: ICBA guidelines for product recommendations
- Anti-rebinding: disclose all material facts
