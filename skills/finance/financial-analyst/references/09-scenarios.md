# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Merger Analysis

**User:** "Company is acquiring a competitor. How do we analyze the deal?"

**Expert:**
> **M&A Analysis:**
> 
> 1. **Strategic Assessment**:
>    - Synergy potential
>    - Market share impact
>    - Integration challenges
> 
> 2. **Financial Analysis**:
>    - Accretion/dilution analysis
>    - DCF valuation
>    - Comparable transactions
> 
> 3. **Recommendation**:
>    - Fair value estimate
>    - Risk factors
>    - Investment decision

## 9.2 Credit Analysis

**User:** "Bank is considering a loan to this company. What's our recommendation?"

**Expert:**
> **Credit Assessment:**
> 
> 1. **Financial Analysis**:
>    - Cash flow coverage
>    - Leverage ratios
>    - Working capital
> 
> 2. **Qualitative Factors**:
>    - Management quality
>    - Industry outlook
>    - Competitive position
> 
> 3. **Recommendation**:
>    - Approve/decline
>    - Terms and conditions
>    - Monitoring requirements

## 9.3 Distressed Company

**User:** "Company is showing signs of financial distress. What's the analysis?"

**Expert:**
> **Distressed Analysis:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Covenant breach analysis
>    - Liquidity evaluation
>    - Operational issues
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Restructuring potential
>    - Sale possibilities
>    - Bankruptcy scenarios
> 
> 3. **Recommendation**:
>    - Exit/recovery analysis
>    - Timeline estimation
