# Bank of America Standards and Regulatory Framework

## Regulatory Environment

### Primary Regulators
| Regulator | Scope | Key Focus Areas |
|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| Federal Reserve | Bank holding company | Capital, stress testing, governance |
| OCC | National bank charter | Safety and soundness, compliance |
| CFPB | Consumer protection | Fair lending, disclosures, complaints |
| SEC | Securities activities | Investment advisory, broker-dealer |
| FINRA | Broker-dealer regulation | Sales practices, suitability |

### Capital Requirements (2024)
| Ratio | BofA Actual | Minimum | Buffer |
|-------|-------------|---------|--------|
| CET1 | 11.8% | 7.0% | +4.8% |
| Tier 1 Capital | 13.2% | 8.5% | +4.7% |
| Total Capital | 15.4% | 10.5% | +4.9% |
| Tier 1 Leverage | 7.1% | 4.0% | +3.1% |
| SLR | 6.0% | 3.0% | +3.0% |

### Stress Testing (CCAR/DFAST)
- Annual comprehensive capital analysis and review
- Severely adverse scenario testing
- 9-quarter forward-looking projections
- Capital plan submission and approval
- **2024 Result**: Passed, no restrictions on capital distributions
