## § 5 · Workflow: Banking Project Lifecycle

### Phase 1: Client Discovery & Needs Assessment

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Understand client profile, goals, and pain points | Complete financial/operational profile | Product-pushing without understanding |
| 2 | Assess current banking relationship and gaps | Cross-sell opportunities identified | Missing key relationship context |
| 3 | Apply "Responsible Growth" lens — can we help responsibly? | Tenets check complete | Growth at any cost mentality |
| 4 | Design BofA-specific solution leveraging full capabilities | One BofA solution designed | Single-product focus |
| 5 | Conduct risk assessment and regulatory review | Risk rating complete | Ignoring compliance requirements |

### Phase 2: Solution Design & Structuring

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Structure integrated solution (banking + lending + investing) | Multi-product proposal | Siloed product offering |
| 2 | Leverage digital capabilities (Erica, CashPro, APIs) | Digital-first delivery plan | Manual, paper-based process |
| 3 | Price responsibly — fair value for client and bank | Competitive, sustainable pricing | Race to bottom on price |
| 4 | Establish risk management framework | Covenants, monitoring in place | Inadequate risk controls |
| 5 | Ensure regulatory compliance | Compliance sign-off | Regulatory gaps |

### Phase 3: Implementation & Execution

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Client onboarding and documentation | All docs executed properly | Incomplete documentation |
| 2 | Technology integration (if applicable) | Systems connected | Poor integration |
| 3 | Training and change management | Client ready to use | Insufficient training |
| 4 | Go-live and initial support | Smooth launch | Launch issues |

### Phase 4: Relationship Management & Growth

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Regular relationship reviews | Quarterly business reviews | Set-and-forget approach |
| 2 | Cross-sell execution | Deepening roadmap progress | Single-product stickiness |
| 3 | Risk monitoring and management | Early warning systems active | Reactive risk management |
| 4 | Continuous improvement | Client satisfaction high | Stagnant relationship |
