## Case Study 1: Navigating Interest Rate Changes

### Situation

**Client**: Mid-size regional retailer ($200M revenue)
**Challenge**: Rising interest rate environment (2022-2023) impacting floating-rate debt servicing
**Existing Relationship**: $50M revolving credit facility at SOFR + 275bps

### Analysis

**Interest Rate Environment:**
| Period | Fed Funds Rate | SOFR | Client Rate |
|--------|---------------|------|-------------|
| Q1 2022 | 0.00-0.25% | 0.05% | 2.80% |
| Q4 2022 | 4.25-4.50% | 4.30% | 7.05% |
| Q4 2023 | 5.25-5.50% | 5.35% | 8.10% |

**Client Impact:**
- Annual interest expense increased from $1.4M to $4.05M (+189%)
- Debt service coverage ratio compressed from 2.5x to 1.8x
- Cash flow pressure affecting growth capital

### BofA Solution

**Phase 1: Risk Assessment**
- Conducted stress testing at various rate scenarios
- Evaluated client hedging readiness
- Assessed covenant headroom under stress

**Phase 2: Hedging Strategy**
| Instrument | Notional | Rate | Protection |
|------------|----------|------|------------|
| Interest Rate Swap | $30M | 4.50% | Fixed 7.25% effective |
| Interest Rate Cap | $20M | 6.00% | Max 8.75% effective |

**Phase 3: Credit Restructuring**
- Extended facility maturity from 3 to 5 years
- Added accordion feature for growth flexibility
- Modified covenants to accommodate hedging costs

### Outcomes

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Weighted Avg Rate | 8.10% | 6.85% |
| Annual Interest | $4.05M | $3.43M |
| DSCR | 1.8x | 2.1x |
| Rate Certainty | None | 70% hedged |

### Responsible Growth Application
- ✓ **Customer-focused**: Solution tailored to client's risk tolerance
- ✓ **Risk management**: Prudent hedging, not speculation
- ✓ **Sustainable economics**: Maintained client viability and bank returns
- ✓ **Long-term relationship**: Deepened trust through volatile period

## Case Study 2: Digital Transformation for Legacy Business

### Situation

**Client**: Family-owned distributor ($150M revenue, 75 years old)
**Challenge**: Manual processes, paper checks, limited visibility into cash position
**Pain Points**:
- 3-day check float creating working capital inefficiency
- Manual reconciliation consuming 40 hours/week
- No real-time cash visibility across 12 locations

### Analysis

**Current State Assessment:**
| Process | Time | Cost/Year |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Check deposits | 2 days | $45K (float cost) |
| Reconciliation | 40 hrs/week | $85K (labor) |
| Payment processing | Manual | $30K (errors) |
| Reporting | End-of-day | Opportunity cost |

### BofA Solution

**Phase 1: Digital Foundation**
- CashPro Online implementation
- Multi-user access with role-based permissions
- Mobile app deployment for executives

**Phase 2: Payment Modernization**
| Old Method | New Method | Improvement |
|------------|------------|-------------|
| Paper checks | ACH + RTP | Same-day availability |
| Manual wires | Online wires | Self-service, 24/7 |
| Lockbox (external) | Integrated lockbox | Same-day posting |
| Check deposits | Remote deposit | Same-day credit |

**Phase 3: Advanced Capabilities**
- CashPro Forecasting AI implementation
- ERP integration (NetSuite)
- Real-time alerts and reporting

### Outcomes

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Cash visibility | End-of-day | Real-time | Immediate |
| Reconciliation time | 40 hrs/week | 5 hrs/week | -87% |
| Float days | 3 days | 0.5 days | -83% |
| Working capital | $12M tied up | $8M tied up | +$4M freed |
| Payment errors | 2% | 0.1% | -95% |

### Responsible Growth Application
- ✓ **Customer-focused**: Solved real business problems
- ✓ **Operational Excellence**: Efficiency gains for client and bank
- ✓ **Long-term relationship**: Digital engagement deepened relationship
- ✓ **Risk management**: Better visibility = better credit monitoring

| Component | Amount | Structure |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Term Loan | $150M | 7-year |
| KPI | Renewable % | 50% by 2030 |
| Margin Adjustment | ±25bps | Based on performance |

**Phase 3: Tax Equity Financing**
| Component | Amount | Structure |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Investment Tax Credits | $50M | Partnership flip |
| Depreciation Benefits | Accelerated | 5-year MACRS |

### Outcomes

| Metric | Target | Status |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Renewable capacity | +1,600 MW | On track |
| CO2 reduction | -40% by 2030 | Exceeding |
| Green bond proceeds | 100% allocated | Verified |
| SLB margin benefit | -25bps | Achieved |

### BofA Contribution to $1.5T Goal

### Responsible Growth Application

## Case Study 4: Crisis Response and Relationship Preservation

### Situation

**Client**: Travel and hospitality company ($300M revenue pre-pandemic)
**Crisis**: COVID-19 pandemic, revenue declined 80% in Q2 2020
**Existing Relationship**: $100M revolver, $50M term loan

### Analysis

**Financial Impact:**
| Metric | Pre-COVID | Q2 2020 | Q4 2020 |
|--------|-----------|---------|---------|
| Revenue | $75M/quarter | $15M | $30M |
| EBITDA | $12M | -$8M | -$2M |
| Liquidity | $50M | $20M | $35M |
| Debt/EBITDA | 2.5x | N/A (neg) | 8x |

### BofA Response

**Phase 1: Immediate Support (March-May 2020)**
- Waived covenant testing for 2 quarters
- Increased revolver from $100M to $150M
- Deferral of principal payments on term loan

**Phase 2: Restructuring (June-August 2020)**
| Component | Before | After |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Revolver | $100M | $150M (amortizing) |
| Term Loan | $50M | $40M (extended 2 years) |
| Covenants | 3.5x leverage | Suspended → 4.5x |
| Pricing | L+275bps | L+350bps (crisis pricing) |

**Phase 3: Recovery Support (September 2020-2021)**
- Treasury management optimization
- FX hedging for international operations
- M&A advisory for strategic acquisitions at distressed prices

### Outcomes

| Metric | Crisis Low | Recovery | Current |
|--------|------------|----------|---------|
| Revenue | $15M/qtr | $45M/qtr | $90M/qtr |
| EBITDA | -$8M | $5M | $18M |
| Debt/EBITDA | N/A | 6x | 2.8x |
| Relationship | At risk | Restored | Expanded |

### Responsible Growth Application
- ✓ **Customer-focused**: Support through unprecedented crisis
- ✓ **Risk management**: Prudent forbearance, not blind support
- ✓ **Long-term view**: Relationship preservation over short-term P&L
- ✓ **Sustainable economics**: Restored to performing relationship

## Case Study 6: Cross-Border Expansion Support

### Situation

**Client**: U.S.-based technology company ($500M revenue)
**Opportunity**: Expansion into Europe and Asia-Pacific
**Challenge**: Multi-currency operations, regulatory complexity, treasury optimization

### Analysis

**Expansion Plan:**
| Region | Market Entry | Revenue Target (Y3) |
|--------|--------------|---------------------|
| UK | Acquisition | $50M |
| Germany | Greenfield | $30M |
| Singapore | Joint venture | $25M |
| Australia | Direct | $20M |

**Treasury Challenges:**
- 12 currencies, 8 banking relationships
- FX exposure: $200M annual flows
- Regulatory: GDPR, local licensing
- Working capital: 90-day cash conversion cycle

### BofA Solution

**Phase 1: Global Account Structure**
- Master accounts in each jurisdiction
- Pooling structure for USD, EUR, GBP
- Local currency accounts for operations

**Phase 2: CashPro Global Implementation**
| Component | Capability |
|-----------|------------|
| Multi-currency | 140+ currencies |
| Real-time | Intraday visibility |
| Integration | SAP, Oracle, NetSuite |
| Mobile | Global payment approvals |

**Phase 3: FX Risk Management**
| Exposure Type | Hedging Solution |
|---------------|------------------|
| Transaction | Forward contracts, options |
| Translation | Net investment hedging |
| Economic | Natural hedging optimization |

**Phase 4: Regulatory Navigation**
- Local bank partnerships
- Compliance advisory
- Reporting automation

### Outcomes

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Banking partners | 8 | 2 (BofA + 1 local each) |
| Cash visibility | Limited | Real-time global |
| FX costs | 1.5% spread | 0.8% spread |
| Cash conversion cycle | 90 days | 55 days |
| Expansion timeline | 24 months | 18 months |

### Responsible Growth Application
- ✓ **Customer-focused**: Comprehensive solution for complex need
- ✓ **One BofA**: Integrated Global Banking + Markets capabilities
- ✓ **Risk management**: FX and operational risk mitigation
- ✓ **Long-term relationship**: Expansion partner for growth

## Key Learnings

### Success Factors
1. **Deep client understanding** before solution design
2. **Integrated capabilities** across BofA segments
3. **Technology leverage** for efficiency and scale
4. **Risk-aware approach** to all engagements
5. **Long-term orientation** over short-term gains

### Common Pitfalls Avoided
- Product-pushing without needs assessment
- Siloed solutions missing cross-sell opportunities
- Inadequate risk evaluation
- Short-term pricing over relationship value
- Technology for technology's sake

### Metrics That Matter
- Client satisfaction and retention
- Relationship depth (products, revenue)
- Risk-adjusted returns
- Operational efficiency gains
- ESG impact (where applicable)
