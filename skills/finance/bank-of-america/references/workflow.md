# Bank of America Execution Workflows

## Overview

This document provides detailed execution workflows for common banking engagements at Bank of America. These workflows represent best practices for delivering "Responsible Growth" through disciplined execution.

---

## Workflow 1: Consumer Banking Account Acquisition

### Phase 1: Prospect Identification (Week 1)

**Activities:**
1. **Market Analysis**
   - Identify target demographics
   - Analyze competitive landscape
   - Assess market opportunity

2. **Prospect Sourcing**
   | Source | Description | Conversion Rate |
   |--------|-------------|-----------------|
   | Digital Marketing | SEO, SEM, social media | 2-3% |
   | Employer Partnerships | EBI program | 15-20% |
   | Branch Referrals | Walk-ins, events | 25-30% |
   | Referrals | Existing clients | 40-50% |

3. **Pre-Qualification**
   - Soft credit pull (with consent)
   - Basic KYC information
   - Product fit assessment

**Deliverables:**
- Target prospect list
- Marketing campaign plan
- Pre-qualified prospect pool

---

### Phase 2: Engagement & Application (Weeks 2-3)

**Activities:**
1. **Initial Contact**
   - Personalized outreach
   - Value proposition presentation
   - Product education

2. **Application Process**
   ```
   Digital Channel (Preferred):
   1. Mobile/online application (5 minutes)
   2. Identity verification (automated)
   3. Funding (external account, mobile deposit)
   4. Card delivery (5-7 business days)
   
   Branch Channel:
   1. In-person consultation
   2. Assisted application
   3. Instant account opening
   4. Temporary card issuance
   ```

3. **Documentation**
   - Terms and conditions disclosure
   - Fee schedule review
   - Privacy notice

**Success Metrics:**
- Application completion rate: >70%
- Time to complete: <10 minutes (digital)
- Abandonment rate: <30%

---

### Phase 3: Onboarding & Activation (Week 4)

**Activities:**
1. **Welcome Process**
   - Welcome email/letter
   - Digital enrollment setup
   - Debit card activation

2. **First Use Campaign**
   - Direct deposit setup incentive
   - First transaction bonus
   - Digital banking enrollment

3. **Erica Engagement**
   - AI assistant introduction
   - Feature education
   - Proactive insight setup

**Success Metrics:**
- Activation rate: >85%
- Digital enrollment: >80%
- Direct deposit setup: >50%

---

### Phase 4: Relationship Deepening (Months 2-12)

**Activities:**
1. **Cross-Sell Progression**
   | Month | Target Product | Rationale |
   |-------|----------------|-----------|
   | 2 | Savings account | Emergency fund building |
   | 4 | Credit card | Credit building, rewards |
   | 6 | Auto loan | Vehicle financing need |
   | 9 | Mortgage | Homeownership path |
   | 12 | Investments | Wealth building |

2. **Engagement Monitoring**
   - Transaction activity analysis
   - Digital engagement tracking
   - Satisfaction surveys

3. **Retention Activities**
   - Preferred Rewards enrollment
   - Life stage targeting
   - Proactive service outreach

**Success Metrics:**
- Products per household: 3+ by month 12
- Digital active rate: >75%
- 12-month retention: >90%

---

## Workflow 2: Commercial Banking Credit Origination

### Phase 1: Opportunity Assessment (Weeks 1-2)

**Activities:**
1. **Client Discovery**
   - Business model understanding
   - Financial performance review
   - Management assessment
   - Industry analysis

2. **Credit Need Assessment**
   ```
   Credit Types:
   ├── Working Capital
   │   ├── Revolving line of credit
   │   ├── Trade finance
   │   └── Supply chain finance
   ├── Term Financing
   │   ├── Equipment financing
   │   ├── Real estate term loans
   │   └── Acquisition financing
   └── Specialized
       ├── Asset-based lending
       ├── Cash flow lending
       └── Franchise financing
   ```

3. **Initial Structuring**
   - Facility types and sizes
   - Pricing framework
   - Collateral requirements
   - Covenant structure

**Deliverables:**
- Opportunity summary
- Preliminary credit structure
- Relationship plan

---

### Phase 2: Credit Underwriting (Weeks 3-6)

**Activities:**
1. **Financial Analysis**
   | Analysis | Components |
   |----------|------------|
   | Historical | 3-5 years financial statements |
   | Projected | 3-year projections, sensitivities |
   | Ratio Analysis | Leverage, coverage, liquidity |
   | Cash Flow | Operating, free, debt service |

2. **Risk Assessment**
   - Industry risk
   - Business risk
   - Management risk
   - Financial risk
   - Structural risk

3. **Collateral Evaluation**
   - Appraisal/valuation
   - Lien perfection
   - Monitoring requirements

4. **Credit Memo Preparation**
   - Executive summary
   - Business description
   - Financial analysis
   - Risk assessment
   - Recommendation

**Approval Authorities:**
| Facility Size | Approval Level |
|---------------|----------------|
| <$1M | Relationship Manager + Credit Officer |
| $1M-$10M | Market President |
| $10M-$50M | Division Credit Committee |
| $50M-$250M | Senior Credit Committee |
| >$250M | Board Risk Committee |

---

### Phase 3: Documentation & Closing (Weeks 7-8)

**Activities:**
1. **Legal Documentation**
   - Credit agreement
   - Security agreements
   - Guarantees
   - Collateral documents

2. **Conditions Precedent**
   - Corporate resolutions
   - Insurance certificates
   - Lien searches
   - Legal opinions

3. **Closing Process**
   - Document execution
   - Funding
   - Perfection of security interests

**Deliverables:**
- Executed credit agreements
- Funding confirmation
- Tickler file setup

---

### Phase 4: Portfolio Management (Ongoing)

**Activities:**
1. **Monitoring**
   | Frequency | Activity |
   |-----------|----------|
   | Monthly | Covenant compliance |
   | Quarterly | Financial statement review |
   | Annually | Credit review/renewal |
   | Trigger-based | Site visits, collateral inspections |

2. **Early Warning System**
   - Covenant breaches
   - Deteriorating financial trends
   - Adverse market conditions
   - Management changes

3. **Annual Review**
   - Updated financial analysis
   - Risk rating confirmation
   - Strategy assessment
   - Pricing review

## Workflow 3: Wealth Management Client Onboarding

### Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1-2)

**Activities:**
1. **Qualification**
   - Net worth verification
   - Liquidity assessment
   - Complexity evaluation

2. **Goals Assessment**
   ```
   Common Goals:
   ├── Short-term (1-3 years)
   │   ├── Liquidity management
   │   ├── Major purchase planning
   │   └── Tax optimization
   ├── Medium-term (3-10 years)
   │   ├── Education funding
   │   ├── Business transition
   │   └── Retirement preparation
   └── Long-term (10+ years)
       ├── Retirement income
       ├── Legacy planning
       └── Philanthropy
   ```

3. **Risk Profiling**
   - Risk tolerance assessment
   - Risk capacity analysis
   - Risk required evaluation

**Deliverables:**
- Client profile document
- Goals inventory
- Risk profile summary

---

### Phase 2: Strategy Design (Weeks 3-4)

**Activities:**
1. **Asset Allocation**
   | Asset Class | Conservative | Moderate | Aggressive |
   |-------------|--------------|----------|------------|
   | Cash/Fixed Income | 60% | 40% | 20% |
   | Equities | 30% | 50% | 70% |
   | Alternatives | 10% | 10% | 10% |

2. **Investment Vehicle Selection**
   - Separately Managed Accounts (SMAs)
   - Mutual funds
   - ETFs
   - Individual securities
   - Alternative investments

3. **Tax Optimization**
   - Asset location (taxable vs. tax-advantaged)
   - Tax-loss harvesting
   - Roth conversion analysis

4. **Integrated Planning**
   - Trust and estate coordination
   - Insurance review
   - Banking integration

**Deliverables:**
- Investment Policy Statement
- Financial plan
- Account opening documentation

---

### Phase 3: Implementation (Weeks 5-6)

**Activities:**
1. **Account Setup**
   - Account opening
   - Beneficiary designations
   - Trading authority
   - Fee agreements

2. **Asset Transfer**
   - ACATS transfers
   - In-kind transfers
   - Cash transfers

3. **Initial Investment**
   - Dollar-cost averaging plan
   - Lump sum deployment
   - Rebalancing to target allocation

**Success Metrics:**
- Account funding: 100% of committed assets
- Transfer completion: <2 weeks
- Initial allocation: Within 5% of target

---

### Phase 4: Ongoing Management (Ongoing)

**Activities:**
1. **Portfolio Management**
   | Frequency | Activity |
   |-----------|----------|
   | Daily | Cash management, rebalancing triggers |
   | Monthly | Performance reporting |
   | Quarterly | Review meetings, rebalancing |
   | Annually | Comprehensive plan review |

2. **Client Communication**
   - Market commentary
   - Portfolio updates
   - Life change discussions
   - Goal progress review

3. **Relationship Expansion**
   - Family member onboarding
   - Business owner services
   - Philanthropic planning

## Workflow 4: CashPro Implementation for Corporate Clients

### Phase 1: Needs Assessment (Weeks 1-2)

**Activities:**
1. **Treasury Diagnostic**
   - Current state assessment
   - Pain point identification
   - Opportunity quantification

2. **Requirements Gathering**
   ```
   Key Areas:
   ├── Payments
   │   ├── Domestic (ACH, wires, checks)
   │   ├── International (SWIFT, FX)
   │   └── Real-time (RTP, FedNow)
   ├── Receivables
   │   ├── Lockbox
   │   ├── Remote deposit
   │   └── Merchant services
   ├── Liquidity
   │   ├── Concentration
   │   ├── Pooling
   │   └── Investment
   └── Risk
       ├── FX hedging
       ├── Interest rate hedging
       └── Fraud prevention
   ```

3. **Solution Design**
   - Platform configuration
   - Integration requirements
   - Security setup

---

### Phase 2: Implementation (Weeks 3-8)

**Activities:**
1. **Technical Setup**
   | Component | Timeline | Dependencies |
   |-----------|----------|--------------|
   | Account setup | Week 1 | Legal docs |
   | User provisioning | Week 2 | Security review |
   | ERP integration | Weeks 3-6 | IT resources |
   | Testing | Weeks 6-7 | All components |
   | Training | Week 8 | System ready |

2. **Integration Options**
   - Host-to-host (H2H)
   - SWIFT connectivity
   - API integration
   - File-based (Connect)

3. **Testing Protocol**
   - Unit testing
   - Integration testing
   - User acceptance testing
   - Parallel testing

---

### Phase 3: Go-Live (Week 9)

**Activities:**
1. **Production Cutover**
   - Final data validation
   - Go-live authorization
   - Transaction processing

2. **Hypercare Support**
   - Dedicated support team
   - Issue resolution
   - User assistance

**Success Metrics:**
- Go-live on schedule: >90%
- Critical issues: <5
- User satisfaction: >85%

---

### Phase 4: Optimization (Ongoing)

**Activities:**
1. **Usage Analysis**
   - Transaction volume trends
   - Feature adoption
   - Efficiency gains

2. **Continuous Improvement**
   - New feature rollout
   - Process optimization
   - Training refreshers
