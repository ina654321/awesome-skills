## Risk Management

### Diversification
- Multiple business segments (PGIM, U.S., International)
- Geographic diversification (50+ countries)
- Product diversification (insurance, annuities, investments)
- Revenue sources (premiums, investment income, fees)

### Strategic Risk Reduction
- Reduced exposure to market-sensitive products
- Prismic reinsurance partnership ($17 billion AUM)
- Guaranteed universal life exposure reduced ~60%
- Legacy variable annuity block in run-off

### Capital Management
- Disciplined capital allocation
- Strong parent company liquidity
- Share repurchases and dividends
- Investment-grade credit ratings
