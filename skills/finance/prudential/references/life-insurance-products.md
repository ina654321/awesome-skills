# Prudential Life Insurance Products

## Term Life Insurance

### EssentialTerm Suite
**Target:** Temporary protection needs

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Coverage Amounts | $100,000 - $10+ million |
| Term Lengths | 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 years |
| Premium Guarantee | Level for term period |
| Convertibility | To permanent insurance without evidence of insurability |
| Riders Available | Waiver of premium, accidental death |

## Permanent Life Insurance

### Universal Life (UL)
**Target:** Flexible permanent protection

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Death Benefit | Flexible, can be increased or decreased |
| Premiums | Adjustable within limits |
| Cash Value | Earns declared interest rate |
| Minimum Interest Rate | Guaranteed floor (varies by product) |
| Policy Loans | Available from cash value |

**Best For:**
- Long-term protection needs
- Flexible premium payment schedules
- Estate planning with liquidity needs
- Business continuation planning

### Indexed Universal Life (IUL)
**Target:** Market upside potential with downside protection

**Momentum IUL** (Launched 2024)

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Interest Crediting | Based on index performance (S&P 500, etc.) |
| Floor Protection | 0% or minimum guarantee (no negative returns) |
| Cap Rate | Maximum crediting rate (varies, typically 8-12%) |
| Participation Rate | Percentage of index gain credited |
| Multiple Strategies | Point-to-point, monthly averaging, etc. |

**Best For:**
- Cash value accumulation potential
- Protection against market downside
- Supplemental retirement income
- Legacy planning with growth component

### Variable Universal Life (VUL)
**Target:** Maximum growth potential with investment choices

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Investment Options | Multiple subaccounts (stocks, bonds, balanced) |
| Cash Value | Fluctuates with underlying investments |
| Death Benefit | Can increase with cash value growth |
| Risk Level | Market risk borne by policyowner |
| Tax Treatment | Tax-deferred growth; tax-free loans |

**Best For:**
- Experienced investors comfortable with market risk
- Higher potential returns for cash value
- Long time horizons (15+ years)
- Sophisticated financial planning

### Whole Life
**Target:** Guaranteed lifetime protection

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Premiums | Fixed, guaranteed for life |
| Cash Value | Guaranteed growth schedule |
| Dividends | Participating policies may earn dividends |
| Dividend Options | Paid-up additions, cash, premium reduction |
| Policy Loans | Available at guaranteed rates |

**Best For:**
- Guaranteed lifetime coverage
- Predictable, conservative growth
- Dividend-paying traditional product
- Final expense and guaranteed legacy

## Survivorship Life Insurance

### Survivorship Universal Life
**Target:** Estate planning for couples

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Insureds | Two lives (typically spouses) |
| Death Benefit | Pays on second death |
| Premium | Lower than two individual policies |
| Estate Tax Planning | Pays when tax is due (second death) |
| ILIT Compatible | Often owned by irrevocable trust |

**Best For:**
- Estate liquidity for married couples
- Second-to-die estate tax planning
- Business succession for co-owners
- Wealth preservation for heirs

## Private Placement Life Insurance (PPLI)
**Target:** High-net-worth individuals ($5M+ investable assets)

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Investment Options | Hedge funds, alternatives, private equity |
| Minimum Premium | Typically $1 million+ |
| Tax Treatment | Tax-deferred growth; income tax-free death benefit |
| Costs | Lower insurance charges, higher investment minimums |
| Customization | Tailored investment options |

**Best For:**
- Tax-efficient alternative investment wrapper
- High-net-worth estate planning
- Investment flexibility within insurance
- Sophisticated wealth transfer strategies

## Riders and Options

### Common Riders
- **Waiver of Premium:** Premiums waived if disabled
- **Accidental Death Benefit:** Additional benefit for accidental death
- **Guaranteed Insurability:** Option to increase coverage without underwriting
- **Long-Term Care:** Accelerate death benefit for LTC expenses
- **Term Insurance:** Add temporary coverage to permanent policy

### Policy Provisions
- **Convertibility:** Term to permanent without evidence of insurability
- **Policy Loans:** Borrow from cash value
- **Partial Surrenders:** Withdraw cash value
- **Reduced Paid-Up:** Stop premiums, reduce face amount
- **Extended Term:** Use cash value to buy term coverage
