# MetLife Sustainability Commitments

## Environmental Stewardship Overview

MetLife has a long legacy of environmental responsibility dating back over 155 years. The company was the first U.S.-based insurer to achieve carbon neutrality in 2016 and continues to lead the industry in sustainable business practices.

## Carbon Neutrality & Net Zero

### Carbon Neutrality Achievement (2016)

**Historic Milestone:**
- First U.S. insurance company to achieve carbon neutrality
- Maintained annually since 2016
- Covers global corporate offices, vehicle fleets, and employee business travel

**Scope of Carbon Neutrality:**
| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Scope 1** | Direct emissions from owned/controlled sources (vehicle fleets) |
| **Scope 2** | Indirect emissions from purchased electricity (offices) |
| **Scope 3** | Employee business travel (Category 6) |

### Net Zero Commitment (2022)

**Aspiration:**
Net Zero greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions for:
1. MetLife global operations
2. MetLife General Account investment portfolio

**Timeline:** By 2050 or sooner

**Interim Targets:**
- 2030: 11 climate goals aligned with 1.5°C pathway
- Ongoing: Supplier engagement for emissions reduction
- Progress tracking via annual Sustainability Report

### Emissions Reduction Progress

**Since 2019:**
- 44% decrease in location-based emissions
- 49% reduction in carbon dioxide emissions
- Continued energy efficiency improvements
