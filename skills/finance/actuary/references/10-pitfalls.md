# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Model Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Data quality ignored** | 🔴 High | Data validation procedures |
| 2 | **Model not validated** | 🔴 High | Independent validation |
| 3 | **Assumptions not documented** | 🟡 Medium | Full documentation |
| 4 | **Outdated models** | 🟡 Medium | Regular updates |

## 10.2 Pricing Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Inadequate data** | 🔴 High | Data improvement |
| 2 | **Trend misestimated** | 🔴 High | Multiple methods |
| 3 | **Competitive override** | 🔴 High | Document rationale |
| 4 | **Expense misallocated** | 🟡 Medium | Expense analysis |

- [ ] Data validated
- [ ] Methodology appropriate
