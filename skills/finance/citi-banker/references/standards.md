# Citi Banking Standards & Frameworks

## Regulatory Capital Requirements (Basel III)

| Component | Citi Requirement | Notes |
|-----------|------------------|-------|
| Minimum CET1 | 7.0% | Core equity tier 1 minimum |
| Capital Conservation Buffer | 2.5% | Absorb losses during stress |
| G-SIB Surcharge | 3.0% | Global systemically important bank |
| Stress Capital Buffer | ~3.0% | CCAR-determined |
| Effective CET1 Minimum | ~13.5% | Total requirement |

## Country Risk Rating Tiers

### Tier 1: Developed Markets
- **Countries**: US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, Singapore, Australia, Canada
- **Sovereign Rating**: AA- to AAA
- **Citi Approach**: Full service, benchmark pricing
- **Risk Weight**: 20-75%

### Tier 2: Emerging Markets
- **Countries**: China, India, Brazil, Mexico, UAE, South Korea, Taiwan, Poland
- **Sovereign Rating**: BBB- to A+
- **Citi Approach**: Local expertise, structured solutions
- **Risk Weight**: 75-100%

### Tier 3: Frontier Markets
- **Countries**: Nigeria, Kenya, Vietnam, Bangladesh, Egypt, Pakistan
- **Sovereign Rating**: B- to BB+
- **Citi Approach**: Selective presence, higher pricing, limits
- **Risk Weight**: 100-150%

## Trade Finance Standards

### Letter of Credit Types
| Type | Risk Level | Use Case |
|------|------------|----------|
| Irrevocable | Low | Standard commercial transactions |
| Confirmed | Very Low | High-risk jurisdictions |
| Transferable | Medium | Middleman/intermediary deals |
| Standby | Variable | Financial guarantee alternative |

### Documentary Requirements
- Commercial invoice
- Bill of lading/transport documents
- Insurance certificate
- Packing list
- Certificate of origin
- Inspection certificate (if required)

## FX Risk Management Standards

### Hedging Instruments
| Instrument | Cost | Flexibility | Best For |
|------------|------|-------------|----------|
| Forward | Low | None | Certain future cash flows |
| Option | Medium | High | Uncertain exposures |
| Collar | Low-Medium | Limited | Cost-effective protection |
| Natural Hedge | None | N/A | Matching inflows/outflows |

### EM Currency Classifications
1. **Freely Convertible**: BRL, MXN, ZAR, THB
2. **Managed Float**: CNY, INR, MYR, RUB
3. **Pegged/Managed**: AED, SAR, HKD, SGD
4. **Restricted**: NGN (multiple windows), EGP, VEF

## Sanctions & Compliance

### Key Lists
- OFAC (US Treasury)
- EU Consolidated List
- UN Security Council
- HMT (UK)
- Local sanctions (varies by country)

### Red Flags
- Shell companies in high-risk jurisdictions
- Unusual routing of transactions
- Mismatch between goods and destination
- Cash-intensive businesses without clear rationale
