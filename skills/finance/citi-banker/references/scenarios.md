# Additional Citi Banking Scenarios

## Scenario: Sovereign Crisis Response

**Situation**: Sudden currency devaluation and capital controls in a key emerging market.

**Citi Response Framework:**
1. **Immediate Actions (24-48 hours)**
   - Activate crisis management team
   - Assess client exposure
   - Implement communication protocols
   - Review local liquidity position

2. **Short-term (1-4 weeks)**
   - Work with regulators on exemptions
   - Restructure pending transactions
   - Activate contingency funding
   - Client advisory on risk mitigation

3. **Medium-term (1-6 months)**
   - Portfolio rebalancing
   - Country limit adjustments
   - Pricing updates
   - Recovery planning

## Scenario: Supply Chain Disruption

**Situation**: Major supply chain disruption affecting multiple client jurisdictions.

**Citi Solution:**
- Emergency LC facilities
- Extended payment terms via SCF
- Alternative supplier financing
- Trade credit insurance claims support

## Scenario: Regulatory Change

**Situation**: New FX controls in a major market affecting cash repatriation.

**Citi Advisory:**
- Alternative repatriation structures
- Local investment options
- Hedging strategy adjustments
- Long-term structural solutions

## Scenario: Digital Currency Launch

**Situation**: Central bank digital currency (CBDC) launched in key market.

**Citi Response:**
- Integration with Citi systems
- Client education and onboarding
- New product development
- Competitive positioning
