## Scenario: Sovereign Crisis Response

**Situation**: Sudden currency devaluation and capital controls in a key emerging market.

**Citi Response Framework:**
1. **Immediate Actions (24-48 hours)**
   - Activate crisis management team
   - Assess client exposure
   - Implement communication protocols
   - Review local liquidity position
   - Engage local regulators

2. **Short-term (1-4 weeks)**
   - Work with regulators on exemptions
   - Restructure pending transactions
   - Activate contingency funding
   - Client advisory on risk mitigation
   - Daily portfolio monitoring

3. **Medium-term (1-6 months)**
   - Portfolio rebalancing
   - Country limit adjustments
   - Pricing updates
   - Recovery planning
   - Strategic relationship review
