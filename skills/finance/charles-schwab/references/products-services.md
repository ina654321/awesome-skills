### Trading Capabilities

#### Commission Structure
- **Stocks & ETFs**: $0 online trades
- **Options**: $0 base + $0.65 per contract
- **Mutual Funds**: $0 for no-transaction-fee (NTF) funds; up to $74.95 for others
- **Bonds**: $1 per bond (online); $10 minimum, $250 maximum
- **Treasuries**: $0 (new issues); secondary market $1/bond
- **Futures & Futures Options**: $2.25 per contract

**Schwab Mobile App**
- Biometric login

- Schwab Assistant (AI-powered)

**thinkorswim** (from TD Ameritrade)

**StreetSmart Edge**
- Advanced charting

### Lending Products

#### Pledged Asset Line (PAL)
- **Collateral**: Investment portfolio
- **Rates**: Typically lower than margin loans
- **Usage**: Any purpose (not for purchasing securities)
- **Minimum**: $25,000 line
- **Payments**: Interest-only with balloon

#### Mortgages
- **Investor Advantage Pricing**: Rate discounts based on Schwab assets
- **Types**: Fixed, ARM, jumbo
- **Minimum**: $100,000 loan amount
- **Relationship Discount**: Up to 0.50% rate reduction

#### Margin Loans
- **Purpose**: Buying securities
- **Rates**: Variable, based on Fed Funds
- **Buying Power**: Up to 50% of eligible securities
- **Maintenance Requirements**: Vary by security

## Wealth Management

### Schwab Intelligent Portfolios

#### Standard Tier

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Minimum** | $5,000 |
| **Advisory Fee** | $0 |
| **Expense Ratios** | 0.03% - 0.18% (average 0.13%) |
| **Portfolio Mix** | 51 ETFs across 20+ asset classes |
| **Rebalancing** | Automatic, daily monitoring |
| **Account Types** | Taxable, IRA, trust, custodial |

**Portfolio Strategies**:
1. **Global** - Broad diversification across markets
2. **U.S. Focus** - Domestic concentration
3. **Income** - Dividend and bond focus
4. **Tax-Optimized** - Municipal bonds for high-tax states

**Risk Profiles** (6 levels from Conservative to Aggressive Growth):
- Conservative: 20% stocks, 80% bonds/cash
- Moderate: 40% stocks, 60% bonds/cash
- Aggressive Growth: 95% stocks, 5% cash

**Important Consideration - Cash Allocation**:
- Intelligent Portfolios holds 6-30% in cash
- Conservative portfolios have higher cash (up to 30%)
- Cash earns interest but may underperform during rising markets
- This was a key factor in 2023 SEC settlement

### Schwab Wealth Advisory

- **Minimum**: Typically $500K+ investable assets
- **Fee**: ~0.80% of AUM (negotiable based on size)
- **Includes**: Dedicated financial consultant
- **Services**: Planning, investment management, ongoing reviews

### Schwab Private Client

- **Minimum**: $1 million+ investable assets
- **Team Approach**: Financial consultant + private wealth advisor
- **Enhanced Services**: Estate planning, tax strategies, lending
- **Priority Service**: Dedicated phone line, expedited processing

### Schwab Advisor Network

- **Referral Program**: Schwab refers to vetted independent advisors
- **Fee**: Advisors charge their own fees (typically 1% of AUM)
- **Oversight**: Schwab vets and monitors referred advisors
- **Best For**: Clients wanting ongoing dedicated advisor
