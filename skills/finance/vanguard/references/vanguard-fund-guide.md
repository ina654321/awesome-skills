## Fund Selection Decision Tree

```
START: What is your primary goal?
│
├── Simplicity (set and forget)
│   ├── Retirement goal known
│   │   └── Target-Date Fund (appropriate year)
│   ├── General investing
│   │   └── LifeStrategy (pick allocation)
│   └── Maximum diversification
│       └── VT (Total World)
│
├── Lowest possible cost
│   ├── DIY Three-Fund Portfolio:
│   │   ├── VTI (U.S. Total)
│   │   ├── VXUS (International)
│   │   └── BND (Bonds)
│   └── Weighted ER: ~0.05%
│
├── Tax-loss harvesting (taxable account)
│   ├── Primary: VTI
│   └── TLH Partners:
│       ├── VV (Large-cap)
│       ├── VOO (S&P 500)
│       └── VTI (Total Market)
│
└── Factor tilts / Advanced
    ├── Value tilt: VTV + VOE + VBR
    ├── Small-cap tilt: VB + VSS
    └── Dividend focus: VIG + VYM
```
