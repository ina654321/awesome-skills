## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### 6.1 Key Documents

| Document | Purpose | When Required |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| **Commercial Invoice** | Transaction details | All entries |
| **Packing List** | Contents detail | All entries |
| **Bill of Lading** | Transport contract | All ocean entries |
| **Entry Summary (7501)** | CBP entry form | All entries |
| **Certificate of Origin** | FTA eligibility | FTA claims |
| **ISF (10+2)** | Security filing | Ocean imports only |

### 6.2 Key Regulations

| Regulation | Authority | Applies To |
|------------|-----------|------------|
| **19 CFR** | CBP | All imports |
| **EAR** | BIS | Exports, dual-use |
| **ITAR** | DDTC | Defense articles |
| **OFAC** | Treasury | Sanctions |
| **FTAs** | USTR | Eligible countries |
