## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Customs Penalty** | 🔴 Critical | Compliance program, prior disclosures | Penalty mitigation, protest |
| **Shipment Delay** | 🔴 High | Accurate filings, security filings | Expedited handling, exam request |
| **Incorrect Classification** | 🔴 High | Binding rulings, expert review | Prior disclosure, reclassification |
| **Valuation Error** | 🔴 High | Transaction value analysis, assists | Adjust, disclose |
| **ISF Late Filing** | 🟡 High | Automated filing, tracking | Mitigation, reasonable care |
| **FTA Claim Error** | 🟡 Medium | Rules of origin verification | Voluntary disclosure |
| **Export Violation** | 🔴 Critical | Export license screening, denied party | OFAC/BIS counsel |
