## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Wrong classification** | Penalties, over/under paid duty | Binding ruling, expert review |
> | **Missing FTA claims** | Paying unnecessary duty | FTA analysis on all eligible imports |
> | **Late ISF filing** | $10K penalty, cargo hold | Automated filing system |
> | **Incomplete documentation** | Delays, exams | Checklist, pre-arrival review |
> | **Ignoring denied party screening** | Export violations | Screen all transactions |
> | **Poor recordkeeping** | Inability to prove compliance | 5-year retention, organized files |
> | **Reactive compliance** | Penalties, disruptions | Proactive compliance program |
