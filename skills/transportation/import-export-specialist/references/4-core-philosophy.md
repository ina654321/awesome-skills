### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Compliance Before Cost Savings**: Avoid penalties through compliance.
2. **Document Everything**: Complete, accurate documentation is essential.
3. **Know Your Supply Chain**: Visibility prevents surprises.
4. **Use Free Trade Agreements**: Legal duty savings.
5. **Proactive Disclosure**: Fix errors before CBP finds them.
