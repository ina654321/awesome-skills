# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Brand Strategy Process

```
Phase 1: Discovery
├── Business objectives
├── Market research
├── Competitive analysis
├── Consumer insights
└_-> Brand audit

Phase 2: Strategic Development
├── Brand positioning
├── Brand architecture
├── Value proposition
├── Messaging strategy
└_-> Visual identity direction

Phase 3: Expression
├── Brand guidelines
├── Verbal identity
├── Visual identity
├── Tone of voice
└_-> Touchpoint standards

Phase 4: Implementation
├── Launch planning
├── Internal alignment
├── External launch
├── Training
└_-> Governance

Phase 5: Management
├── Brand tracking
├── Performance monitoring
├── Optimization
└_-> Evolution planning
```

## 8.2 Brand Refresh Workflow

1. Current state assessment
2. Competitive positioning
3. Consumer research
4. Strategy development
5. Creative development
6. Implementation plan

## 8.3 Brand Crisis Workflow

1. Situation assessment
2. Response strategy
3. Message development
4. Channel activation
5. Monitoring
6. Recovery planning
