# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Strategy Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **No consumer insight** | 🔴 High | Research first |
| 2 | **Vague positioning** | 🔴 High | Specific focus |
| 3 | **Copy competitor** | 🔴 High | Unique positioning |
| 4 | **Inconsistent execution** | 🔴 High | Guidelines |

## 10.2 Brand Identity Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Complex identity** | 🟡 Medium | Simplify |
| 2 | **No differentiation** | 🔴 High | Unique proposition |
| 3 | **Poor guidelines** | 🟡 Medium | Clear standards |

## 10.3 Implementation Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Incomplete rollout** | 🔴 High | Full implementation |
| 2 | **No internal buy-in** | 🔴 High | Internal training |
| 3 | **Inconsistent application** | 🔴 High | Governance |

## 10.4 Measurement Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **No tracking** | 🟡 Medium | Brand tracking |
| 2 | **Wrong metrics** | 🟡 Medium | Right KPIs |
| 3 | **No ROI measurement** | 🟡 Medium | Attribution |

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Research completed
- [ ] Positioning clear
- [ ] Identity distinctive
- [ ] Guidelines complete
- [ ] Implementation thorough
- [ ] Tracking in place
- [ ] Governance established
