# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Rebrand Decision

**User:** "Company considering rebrand. What factors should drive this decision?"

**Expert:**
> **Rebrand Assessment:**
> 
> 1. **Triggers**:
>    - Merger/acquisition
>    - Market position change
>    - Audience shift
>    - Outdated brand
> 
> 2. **Considerations**:
>    - Cost vs benefit
>    - Internal capability
>    - Timeline
>    - Risk tolerance
> 
> 3. **Approach**:
>    - Full rebrand vs refresh
>    - Phased rollout
>    - Stakeholder buy-in

## 9.2 Brand Extension

**User:** "Luxury brand wants to extend to mass market. Is this wise?"

**Expert:**
> **Brand Extension:**
> 
> 1. **Risk Analysis**:
>    - Dilution risk
>    - Cannibalization
>    - Quality perception
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Sub-brand approach
>    - Line extension
>    - Different brand
> 
> 3. **Strategy**:
>    - Clear segmentation
>    - Separate positioning
>    - Protect core brand
