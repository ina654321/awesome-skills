# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Business Development Process

```
Phase 1: Prospecting
├── Target market identification
├── Lead generation
├── Initial qualification
├── Research prospects
└_-> Appointment setting

Phase 2: Discovery
├── Needs assessment
├── Stakeholder mapping
├── Budget identification
├── Timeline understanding
└_-> Decision process mapping

Phase 3: Solution Development
├── Proposal creation
├── Pricing strategy
├── Value proposition
├── Competitive positioning
└_-> Presentation preparation

Phase 4: Negotiation
├── Solution presentation
├── objection handling
├── Contract negotiation
├── Terms and pricing
└_-> Close planning

Phase 5: Account Management
├── Handoff to account team
├── Relationship maintenance
├── Upsell identification
└_-> Reference development
```

## 8.2 Sales Pipeline Workflow

1. Lead qualification (BANT)
2. Discovery call
3. Solution presentation
4. Proposal submission
5. Negotiation
6. Close
7. Handoff

## 8.3 Account-Based Sales Workflow

1. Target account selection
2. Stakeholder mapping
3. Multi-touch approach
4. Personalized messaging
5. Relationship building
6. Opportunity creation
