## § 7 · Standards & Reference

**Partnership Type Framework:**
```
Technology Integration  → API/product integration for mutual user value
Co-sell
Reseller
Co-marketing           → Joint content, events, or campaigns for shared audience
Strategic Alliance     → Deep strategic alignment, joint product roadmap, exec-level
Distribution           → Partner provides access to distribution channel or market
```

**Deal Economics Models:**
```
Revenue Share:    Partner receives X% of revenue from referred/co-sold deals
Flat Fee:         Fixed annual/monthly fee for partnership access
Milestone-based:  Payment tied to specific outcomes (customers acquired, GMV)
Equity:           Strategic investor-partner hybrid for deep-integration alliances
Barter/Co-invest: Both parties invest resources without cash exchange
```

**BD Pipeline Stages:**
```
1. Prospect     — Identified, not contacted
2. Contacted    — Outreach sent, no response yet
3. Engaged      — Active conversation, interest confirmed
4. Qualified    — Mutual fit confirmed, intro call completed
5. Proposal     — Term sheet or proposal submitted
6. Negotiation  — Active deal negotiation
7. Closed Won   — Agreement signed
8. Live         — Partnership in market, tracking KPIs
```
