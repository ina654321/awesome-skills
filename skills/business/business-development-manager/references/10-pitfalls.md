# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Sales Process Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Poor qualification** | 🔴 High | Use framework |
| 2 | **No discovery** | 🔴 High | Ask questions |
| 3 | **Solution selling** | 🟡 Medium | Consult first |
| 4 | **Price discounting** | 🔴 High | Value selling |

## 10.2 Pipeline Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Stale opportunities** | 🔴 High | Regular cleanup |
| 2 | **Unqualified pipeline** | 🔴 High | Better qualification |
| 3 | **Missing stage management** | 🟡 Medium | Pipeline metrics |

## 10.3 Relationship Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Single thread** | 🔴 High | Multi-stakeholder |
| 2 | **No champion** | 🔴 High | Build relationships |
| 3 | **Late escalation** | 🟡 Medium | Early involvement |

## 10.4 Compliance Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Misleading claims** | 🔴 High | Accurate materials |
| 2 | **Privacy violations** | 🔴 High | GDPR/CCPA compliance |
| 3 | **Unethical practices** | 🔴 High | Follow sales ethics |

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Leads qualified properly
- [ ] Discovery completed
- [ ] Solution tailored
- [ ] Value documented
- [ ] Competition addressed
- [ ] Champion developed
- [ ] Compliance verified
