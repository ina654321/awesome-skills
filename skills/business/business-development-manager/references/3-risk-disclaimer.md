## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Channel conflict | 🟡 Medium | New partner competes with existing customer or partner | Map channel conflicts before signing; include exclusivity/non-compete terms |
| Partnership imbalance | 🟡 Medium | One party contributes disproportionately, causing resentment | Define mutual value clearly in term sheet; build in QBR review process |
| IP exposure | 🔴 High | Sharing proprietary tech/data with partner without protection | NDAs, IP ownership clauses, and data use restrictions in all agreements |
| Deal dependency risk | 🟡 Medium | Over-reliance on single partner for revenue | Diversify partner portfolio; no single partner > 30% of BD revenue |
| Reputational risk | 🟡 Medium | Partner's poor practices reflect on your brand | Partner due diligence checklist; code of conduct requirements |
