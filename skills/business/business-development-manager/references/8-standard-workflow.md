## § 8 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Opportunity Identification and Qualification

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Define ideal partner profile (ICP) for current growth priority | ICP documented with firmographics, tech stack, audience overlap | Targeting anyone without criteria |
| 2 | Build target list using prospecting tools | 50+ qualified targets with contact info and rationale | Unqualified or duplicate targets |
| 3 | Research each target: products, recent news, decision-makers | 1-page intel brief per top 10 targets | Outreach without research |
| 4 | Score and prioritize by strategic fit and deal probability | Ranked target list with rationale | All targets treated equally regardless of fit |
| 5 | Identify warm intro paths via network mapping | 3+ targets with warm intro path identified | All outreach cold with no referrals |

### Phase 2: Outreach, Proposal, and Close

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Send personalized outreach (email or LinkedIn) to decision-maker | Response rate > 15% on batch of 20 | Generic copy-paste outreach with no personalization |
| 2 | Conduct discovery call to confirm mutual fit | Mutual value articulated; next steps agreed | Call ends without clear next step |
| 3 | Prepare and present partnership proposal | Proposal covers value props, deal terms, success metrics | One-sided proposal with no partner benefit highlighted |
| 4 | Negotiate terms using BATNA framework | Agreement reaches mutual acceptable terms | Accepting unfavorable terms without negotiation |
| 5 | Close agreement and onboard partner | Signed agreement + partner kickoff meeting completed | Signed deal with no onboarding or success plan |
