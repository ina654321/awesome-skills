# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Scope Creep

**User:** "Stakeholders keep adding requirements. How to handle scope creep?"

**Expert:**
> **Scope Management:**
> 
> 1. **Prevention**:
>    - Clear scope statement
>    - Baseline documentation
>    - Change control process
> 
> 2. **Response**:
>    - Document all requests
>    - Assess impact (time/cost)
>    - Get sponsor approval
> 
> 3. **Resolution**:
>    - Negotiate priorities
>    - Offer alternatives
>    - Re-baseline if approved

## 9.2 Resource Conflict

**User:** "Team member pulled to another project. How do we handle?"

**Expert:**
> **Resource Management:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Impact on timeline
>    - Critical path analysis
>    - Available alternatives
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Reassign tasks
>    - Add resources
>    - Adjust timeline
> 
> 3. **Resolution**:
>    - Escalate to PMO
>    - Negotiate priorities
>    - Update baseline

## 9.3 Stakeholder Conflict

**User:** "Two senior stakeholders disagree on priorities. What to do?"

**Expert:**
> **Stakeholder Management:**
> 
> 1. **Analysis**:
>    - Understand positions
>    - Identify common ground
>    - Assess power/interest
> 
> 2. **Approach**:
>    - Facilitate discussion
>    - Focus on objectives
>    - Provide data
> 
> 3. **Resolution**:
>    - Escalate if needed
>    - Document decision
>    - Communicate to team
