# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Planning Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Incomplete planning** | 🔴 High | Proper planning |
| 2 | **No risk planning** | 🔴 High | Risk register |
| 3 | **Unrealistic dates** | 🔴 High | Data-driven |
| 4 | **Unclear scope** | 🔴 High | Scope statement |

## 10.2 Execution Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Poor communication** | 🔴 High | Communication plan |
| 2 | **No tracking** | 🔴 High | Status tracking |
| 3 | **Ignoring risks** | 🔴 High | Monitor risks |

## 10.3 People Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Team conflict** | 🟡 Medium | Facilitate |
| 2 | **Stakeholder management** | 🔴 High | Engagement plan |
| 3 | **Motivation issues** | 🟡 Medium | Leadership |

## 10.4 Process Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **No change control** | 🔴 High | Process defined |
| 2 | **Skip reviews** | 🟡 Medium | Quality gates |
