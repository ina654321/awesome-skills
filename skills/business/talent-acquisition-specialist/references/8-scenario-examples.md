## § 8 · Scenario Examples

### Scenario 1: Executive Search for CTO

**Context:** Series B startup needs CTO to scale engineering from 20 to 100.

**Search Strategy:**
- Target: VP Engineering at successful startups (Series C+)
- Sourcing: LinkedIn, CEO network, executive search firm
- Assessment: Technical depth, scaling experience, culture fit

**Process:**
- 150 candidates sourced
- 20 initial conversations
- 8 full interview loops
- 3 finalists

**Key Differentiators:**
- Board seat and significant equity
- Autonomy to build team
- Mission alignment with candidate values

**Result:**
- Hired former VP Eng from $1B+ startup
- Start date: 6 weeks from first contact
- Engineering scaled to 80 in 12 months

### Scenario 2: High-Volume Campus Hiring

**Context:** Consulting firm hiring 200 analysts from target universities.

**Program:**
- 15 target schools (Ivy + top public)
- Recruiting season: September-November
- Offer deadline: Rolling

**Activities:**
- Info sessions: 30 events
- Coffee chats: 500+ students
- Case interview workshops: 15
- On-campus interviews: 800 candidates

**Selection:**
- Resume screen: GPA, leadership, case competition
- First round: 2 case interviews
- Final round: 3 interviews + group exercise

**Results:**
- 200 offers, 85% acceptance
- Diversity: 45% women, 30% underrepresented minorities
- Time to fill: 60 days average
- Cost per hire: $4,000

### Scenario 3: Niche Technical Role

**Context:** Need PhD-level ML researcher, very small talent pool.

**Challenge:**
- <100 qualified candidates globally
- Top tech companies competing
- Relocation required

**Sourcing:**
- Published papers analysis
- Conference speaker lists (NeurIPS, ICML)
- University lab connections
- Open source contribution tracking

**Approach:**
- Personalized outreach (not templated)
- Research their work before contact
- Academic + industry hybrid role
- Significant research budget

**Result:**
- 40 candidates identified
- 10 conversations
- 3 onsites
- 1 hire (top researcher from FAANG)

### Scenario 4: Diversity Hiring Initiative

**Context:** Engineering team 85% male, need to improve diversity.

**Strategy:**
- Diverse interview panels (mandatory)
- Diverse slates (minimum 2 underrepresented candidates)
- Sourcing from diversity-focused platforms
- Partnerships with organizations (NSBE, Grace Hopper)

**Pipeline Building:**
- Year-round relationship building
- Sponsorship of diversity conferences
- Internship program expansion
- Return-to-work program for career breaks

**Process Changes:**
- Bias training for all interviewers
- Structured interviews with rubrics
- Blind resume review (remove names/schools initially)

**Results (12 months):**
- New hires: 40% women, 25% underrepresented minorities
- Interview diversity index improved
- Culture survey: Inclusion +15 points

### Scenario 5: Rapid Scaling (50 to 200 in 6 months)

**Context:** Hypergrowth startup needs to 4x team quickly without sacrificing quality.

**Infrastructure:**
- ATS implementation (Greenhouse)
- Recruiting team expansion (3 → 12)
- Agency partnerships for volume
- Employee referral program boost

**Process Optimization:**
- Interview scheduling automation
- Standardized rubrics for all roles
- Async video interviews for initial screen
- Batch interview days

**Quality Safeguards:**
- Bar raiser program (experienced interviewers)
- Calibration sessions weekly
- 90-day quality review for all hires

**Results:**
- 150 hires in 6 months
- Time to fill: 35 days (industry avg: 45)
- Quality score: 4.0/5 (manager rating)
- Retention at 12 months: 92%
