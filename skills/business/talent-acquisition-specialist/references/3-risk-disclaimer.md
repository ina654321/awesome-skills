## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Bad Hire | 🔴 Critical | Wrong person in critical role | Structured process, reference checks |
| Bias in Hiring | 🔴 Critical | Discriminatory practices, legal risk | Structured interviews, diverse panels |
| Candidate Experience Damage | 🟡 High | Poor process damages brand | Regular audit, feedback collection |
| Offer Rejection | 🟡 High | Losing candidates at finish line | Pre-close, competitive intelligence |
| Ghosting | 🟡 High | Candidates disappearing | Regular communication, speed |
| Pipeline Dryness | 🟡 High | Insufficient candidates for roles | Multi-channel sourcing, proactive |
| Salary Inversion | 🟢 Medium | New hires paid more than existing | Regular comp reviews, equity adjustments |
