## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### Sourcing Strategy Template

```
SOURCING STRATEGY — [Role Title]

Role Requirements:
- Must-haves: [Skills, experience]
- Nice-to-haves: [Preferences]
- Target companies: [Competitors, adjacent companies]
- Years of experience: [Range]

Target Company List:
| Tier | Companies | Rationale |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 1    | [Top 5]   | Direct competitors, known talent |
| 2    | [Next 10] | Adjacent industry, transferable skills |
| 3    | [Broad]   | Universities, bootcamps |

Boolean Search Strings:
- LinkedIn: ("software engineer" OR "developer") AND (Python OR Java) AND ("machine learning" OR AI)
- GitHub: language:python followers:>100 location:"San Francisco"
- Twitter: "open to work" "software engineer" (#python OR #machinelearning)

Diversity Sourcing:
- NSBE, SHPE, Grace Hopper (tech diversity conferences)
- Vettery, Jopwell (diversity platforms)
- Women's colleges, HBCUs

Expected Pipeline:
- Sourced: 50 candidates/week
- Response rate: 30%
- Interested: 15 candidates
- Interview: 5 candidates
```

### Interview Plan Template

```
INTERVIEW PLAN — [Role Title]

Stage Overview:
| Stage | Duration | Interviewers | Focus |
|-------|----------|--------------|-------|
| Recruiter Screen | 30 min | TA | Fit, motivation, comp |
| Hiring Manager | 45 min | HM | Experience, skills |
| Technical | 60 min | Senior IC | Technical depth |
| Behavioral | 45 min | Cross-functional | Values, collaboration |
| Final | 30 min | Director/VP | Cultural fit, vision |

Rubric by Stage:
Recruiter Screen:
- [ ] Motivation aligned
- [ ] Compensation in range
- [ ] Timeline compatible
- [ ] Basic qualifications met

Technical Assessment:
- [ ] Technical skills (1-4 scale)
- [ ] Problem-solving approach
- [ ] Code quality (if applicable)
- [ ] Communication clarity

Behavioral (STAR Required):
- Tell me about a time you [competency 1]
- Tell me about a time you [competency 2]
- Tell me about a time you [competency 3]

Values Alignment:
- [Value 1]: Question + rubric
- [Value 2]: Question + rubric
- [Value 3]: Question + rubric

Decision Process:
- Debrief within 24 hours of final interview
- Hiring manager decides with TA input
- Unanimous "no" = reject
- Single strong "yes" with no red flags = proceed
```
