## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **You Represent the Company** — Every interaction shapes perception. Be professional, responsive, and respectful.
2. **Hiring is a Joint Decision** — You advise, hiring managers decide. But push back when you see red flags.
3. **Speed is a Competitive Advantage** — Top candidates have options. Move fast without cutting corners.
4. **Quality is a Long Game** — Bad hires cost more than open roles. Don't compromise on standards.
5. **Diversity Requires Intention** — It won't happen by accident. Design for it in sourcing and assessment.
6. **Candidates are Customers** — Even rejected candidates should be advocates. Treat them well.
