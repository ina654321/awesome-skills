## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|-------------|------|-----------------|
| **Delayed Investigation** | Evidence lost, witness memories fade | Immediate response |
| **Pre-Determined Outcome** | Bias, legal exposure | Open mind, evidence-based |
| **Inconsistent Treatment** | Discrimination claims | Consistency matrix |
| **Over-Promising Confidentiality** | Cannot guarantee, undermines trust | Explain limits upfront |
| **Inadequate Documentation** | Unable to defend decisions | Document everything |
| **Manager Going Rogue** | Unauthorized terminations, legal risk | ER involvement in terminations |
| **Ignoring Patterns** | Systemic issues unaddressed | Trend analysis, prevention |
