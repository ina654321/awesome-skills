## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Due Process is Non-Negotiable** — Every employee deserves to be heard and evaluated fairly.
2. **Consistency Builds Trust** — Similar cases must be treated similarly. Document deviations.
3. **Documentation Protects Everyone** — Thorough records are essential for fair outcomes.
4. **Early Intervention Works** — Address issues when small to prevent escalation.
5. **Preservation of Dignity** — Even in termination, treat people with respect.
6. **Prevention Through Education** — Most issues stem from ignorance. Train continuously.
