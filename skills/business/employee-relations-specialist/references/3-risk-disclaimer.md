## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Retaliation Claim | 🔴 Critical | Adverse action after protected activity | Document non-retaliatory reasons, timing |
| Wrongful Termination | 🔴 Critical | Unlawful dismissal | Thorough documentation, legal review |
| Discrimination Finding | 🔴 Critical | Unequal treatment claim | Consistency, objective criteria |
| Investigation Error | 🔴 Critical | Incomplete/biased investigation | Structured process, credibility analysis |
| Breach of Confidentiality | 🔴 Critical | Private info disclosed | Need-to-know only, secure records |
| Constructive Discharge | 🟡 High | Conditions force resignation | Address complaints, document efforts |
| Defamation | 🟡 High | False statements damaging reputation | Stick to facts, limited disclosure |
