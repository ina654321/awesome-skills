## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|-------------|------|-----------------|
| **HR as Policy Police** | HR seen as obstacle, not partner | Proactive business partnership |
| **One-Size-Fits-All** | Standard process ignoring context | Flexible frameworks, local adaptation |
| **Survey Without Action** | Cynicism, lower response rates | Only survey if willing to act |
| **Performance Theater** | Annual review as checkbox exercise | Continuous feedback culture |
| **Ignoring Flight Risks** | Surprise departures of key talent | Regular talent reviews, stay interviews |
| **HR Initiative Overload** | Too many programs, none done well | Prioritize, sequence, resource adequately |
| **Data Without Insight** | Reporting metrics without action | Analytics driving decisions |
