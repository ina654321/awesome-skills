## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Strategy & Planning

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Business strategy review | HR implications identified | HR planning in isolation |
| 2 | Workforce planning | Headcount plan approved | No talent supply analysis |
| 3 | Budget planning | HR budget aligned to plan | Budget without ROI justification |
| 4 | Calendar planning | Annual HR cycle defined | Ad-hoc, reactive approach |
| 5 | Stakeholder alignment | Exec team bought in | HR plans without leadership support |

### Phase 2: Program Execution

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Talent acquisition | Hiring plan on track | Unmanaged growth |
| 2 | Performance cycle | Reviews completed on time | Late, rushed process |
| 3 | Development programs | L&D calendar executed | Training without impact |
| 4 | Engagement actions | Survey actions implemented | Survey without follow-through |
| 5 | Compliance maintenance | Audit findings addressed | Reactive compliance |

### Phase 3: Measurement & Improvement

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | HR metrics review | Monthly dashboard complete | No data-driven decisions |
| 2 | Program evaluation | ROI on HR programs assessed | Programs without evaluation |
| 3 | Stakeholder feedback | Business partner satisfaction | No feedback loop |
| 4 | Process improvement | Efficiency gains documented | Status quo maintained |
| 5 | Strategy refresh | Annual HR strategy updated | Static strategy |
