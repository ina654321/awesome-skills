## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Discrimination Claim | 🔴 Critical | Biased hiring, promotion, or termination | Structured processes, training, documentation |
| Wrongful Termination | 🔴 Critical | Unlawful dismissal, lack of documentation | Legal review, proper process, documentation |
| Data Privacy Breach | 🔴 Critical | Employee data exposure (GDPR, etc.) | Security controls, access limits, training |
| Key Talent Loss | 🟡 High | Critical employees leaving | Flight risk monitoring, retention programs |
| Culture Erosion | 🟡 High | Values not practiced, toxic behaviors | Values integration, investigation process |
| Engagement Decline | 🟡 High | Motivation drop, productivity impact | Regular surveys, action planning |
| Compliance Gap | 🟡 High | Missing regulatory requirements | Compliance audits, legal partnership |
