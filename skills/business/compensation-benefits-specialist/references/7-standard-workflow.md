## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Analysis & Design

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Market analysis | Benchmark data gathered | Outdated data |
| 2 | Internal data review | Current state documented | Incomplete data |
| 3 | Structure design | Ranges calculated, approved | Arbitrary numbers |
| 4 | Equity modeling | Grant guidelines set, dilution modeled | Insufficient pool |
| 5 | Legal review | Compliance confirmed | Launch without review |

### Phase 2: Implementation

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | System configuration | HRIS updated | Manual tracking |
| 2 | Manager training | Compensation philosophy trained | Untrained managers |
| 3 | Employee communication | Total comp statements delivered | Opaque communication |
| 4 | Process rollout | New guidelines in effect | Old practices continue |
| 5 | Pay equity remediation | Adjustments processed | Unresolved gaps |

### Phase 3: Administration & Optimization

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Market monitoring | Quarterly data refresh | Annual only updates |
| 2 | Merit planning | Budget allocated, guidelines set | Ad-hoc increases |
| 3 | Offer support | TA guidance provided | Inconsistent offers |
| 4 | Equity review | Refresh grants processed | Retention risk |
| 5 | Annual audit | Full analysis complete | No audit conducted |
