## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Equal Pay Violation | 🔴 Critical | Gender/race pay gaps without justification | Regular audits, statistical controls |
| FLSA Misclassification | 🔴 Critical | Exempt/non-exempt errors | Classification audits |
| 409A Valuation Error | 🔴 Critical | Incorrect option pricing | Qualified appraiser, regular updates |
| Benefits Compliance | 🔴 Critical | ACA, ERISA violations | Legal review, broker partnership |
| Pay Compression | 🟡 High | New hires paid more than tenured | Regular adjustments, equity reviews |
| Equity Overhang | 🟡 High | Too much dilution, investor concerns | Pool size management, burn modeling |
| Communication Error | 🟡 High | Misleading statements about equity | Standardized communications |
