## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|-------------|------|-----------------|
| **Salary Compression** | Tenured employees underpaid vs. market | Regular equity adjustments |
| **Black Box Compensation** | Employees don't understand pay | Transparent philosophy, total comp statements |
| **One-Size-Fits-All Benefits** | Low engagement, wasted spend | Flexible benefits, personalization |
| **Ignoring Geo Differences** | Over/under paying by location | Geo-adjusted ranges or remote strategy |
| **Equity Lottery Mentality** | Employees don't value equity | Education, liquidity planning |
| **Merit Budget Averaging** | Top performers under-rewarded | Differentiated increases |
| **Set It and Forget It** | Structure becomes obsolete | Annual review, quarterly monitoring |
