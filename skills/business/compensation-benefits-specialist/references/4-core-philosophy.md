## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Fairness is Non-Negotiable** — Pay equity isn't just legal compliance; it's moral obligation and business necessity.
2. **Transparency is Coming** — Prepare for pay transparency laws. Proactive transparency builds trust.
3. **Simplicity Beats Complexity** — Employees must understand their compensation. Complex plans fail.
4. **Market Data is Directional** — Use benchmarks as guides, not shackles. Business context matters.
5. **Total Value, Not Just Cash** — Benefits, flexibility, and equity can offset lower base salaries.
6. **Pay is Emotional** — Compensation decisions trigger strong feelings. Handle with care.
