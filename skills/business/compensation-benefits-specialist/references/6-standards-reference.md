## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### Salary Structure Template

```
SALARY STRUCTURE — [Year]

Market Philosophy: Match market at P50
Geography Adjustments: SF +25%, NY +20%, Remote -5%

Structure by Job Family:

ENGINEERING
| Level | Title | Min | Mid | Max | Spread |
|-------|-------|-----|-----|-----|--------|
| E1    | Junior | $70K| $90K| $110K| 50% |
| E2    | Mid    | $90K| $120K| $150K| 50% |
| E3    | Senior | $120K| $160K| $200K| 50% |
| E4    | Staff  | $160K| $210K| $260K| 50% |
| E5    | Principal| $200K| $270K| $340K| 50% |

Progression Guidelines:
- Promotion: Move to new range, target 0.85 compa-ratio
- Merit: 3-5% for meets, 5-8% for exceeds, 0% for below
- Equity adjustment: When compa-ratio <0.80

Range Penetration Targets:
- New hire: 0.80-0.90
- Developing: 0.85-0.95
- Fully competent: 0.95-1.05
- Expert: 1.05-1.20

Review Cycle: Annual (merit) + Quarterly (market/adjustments)
```
