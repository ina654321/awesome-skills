## § 5 · Professional Toolkit

### Document Types

| Document | Purpose | Typical Length | Timeline |
|----------|---------|----------------|----------|
| **Protocol** | Study conduct | 50-100 pages | 2-3 months |
| **CSR** | Study results | 200-400 pages | 3-4 months |
| **Manuscript** | Publication | 3,000-5,000 words | 2-3 months |
| **Abstract** | Congress | 250-500 words | 2-4 weeks |
| **IB** | Investigator info | 50-100 pages | 1-2 months |
| **CTD Module 2.5** | Clinical overview | 50-100 pages | 2-3 months |
| **CTD Module 2.7** | Clinical summary | 100-200 pages | 3-4 months |

### Writing Standards

| Standard | Application | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------------|------------------|
| **ICH E3** | CSRs | Structure, content guidelines |
| **CONSORT** | RCT manuscripts | Checklist, flow diagram |
| **STROBE** | Observational studies | Reporting guidelines |
| **PRISMA** | Systematic reviews | Flow diagram, checklist |
| **ICMJE** | Authorship | 4 criteria for authorship |
| **GPP3** | Industry publications | Transparency, disclosures |

### Software Tools

| Category | Tools | Purpose |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **Writing** | Microsoft Word, Google Docs | Document creation |
| **References** | EndNote, Zotero, Mendeley | Citation management |
| **Graphics** | GraphPad Prism, Adobe Illustrator | Figures, diagrams |
| **Statistics** | SAS, R, Stata | Data presentation |
| **Collaboration** | SharePoint, Veeva Vault | Document sharing |
| **Plagiarism** | iThenticate, Turnitin | Originality checking |
