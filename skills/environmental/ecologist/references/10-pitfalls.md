# Common Pitfalls

## 10.1 Data Pitfalls

### Inadequate Survey Timing
**Risk**: Missing species, project delays
**Solution**: Survey at appropriate season

### Not Following Protocols
**Risk**: Rejected reports, delays
**Solution**: Use accepted survey methods

## 10.2 Communication Pitfalls

### Not Communicating Uncertainty
**Risk**: Incorrect decisions
**Solution**: Report data gaps and limitations

### Overpromising Results
**Risk**: Legal liability, client issues
**Solution**: Be objective, factual
