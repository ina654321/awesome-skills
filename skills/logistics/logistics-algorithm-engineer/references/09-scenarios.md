# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Warehouse Location Decision

**User:** "Where should we open our next distribution center?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis Framework:**
> 
> **Factors:**
> | Factor | Weight |
> |--------|--------|
> | Customer proximity | 30% |
> | Transportation costs | 25% |
> | Labor availability | 20% |
> | Real estate cost | 15% |
> | Tax incentives | 10% |
> 
> **Optimization:**
> - Minimize total cost (transportation + facility)
> - Cover service level requirements
> - Consider future growth
> 
> **Recommendation:**
> - Run multiple scenarios
> - Sensitivity analysis on key inputs
