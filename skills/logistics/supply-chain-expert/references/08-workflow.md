## 8.2 Inventory Optimization Workflow

1. **Inventory Classification (ABC Analysis)**
   - A items: 80% value, 20% items (high attention)
   - B items: 15% value, 30% items (moderate)
   - C items: 5% value, 50% items (minimal)
   
2. **Safety Stock Calculation**
   - Service level target (typically 95%)
   - Demand variability (standard deviation)
   - Lead time variability
   - Replenishment frequency

3. **Reorder Point Determination**
   - Formula: ROP = (Average Daily Usage Г— Lead Time) + Safety Stock
   - Adjust for demand trends
   - Consider seasonality

4. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Review inventory accuracy (cycle counts)
   - Adjust classifications quarterly
   - Update demand patterns
   - Optimize storage locations

4. **Supplier Development**

   - Technology sharing
   - Capacity planning collaboration
   - Risk mitigation planning

## 8.5 Supply Chain Risk Management

1. **Risk Identification**
   - Supplier disruption risks
   - Transportation risks
   - Demand variability
   - Regulatory changes
   - Natural disasters

2. **Risk Assessment**
   - Probability Г— Impact matrix
   - Historical incident analysis
   - Industry threat intelligence
   - Supply chain mapping

3. **Mitigation Strategies**
   - Supplier diversification
   - Safety stock increases
   - Alternate transport routes
   - Contract clauses
   - Insurance coverage

4. **Contingency Planning**
   - Business continuity plans
   - Emergency contacts
   - Pre-qualified alternates
   - Communication protocols

## 8.7 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Forecast accuracy within target (MAPE < 20%)
- [ ] Inventory levels at optimal range
- [ ] Supplier performance meeting SLA
- [ ] Order fulfillment rate > 98%
- [ ] No critical supply chain disruptions
- [ ] Cost targets achieved
- [ ] Customer complaints resolved
