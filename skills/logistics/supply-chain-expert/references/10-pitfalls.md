# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Planning and Forecasting Errors

| # | Pitfall | Impact | Solution |
|---|---------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Single-point forecast | High risk | Use range-based forecasting |
| 2 | Ignoring demand signals | Accuracy loss | Implement demand sensing |
| 3 | Not updating forecasts | Stale data | Rolling forecast updates |
| 4 | Siloed planning | Gaps, conflicts | Implement S&OP process |
| 5 | Over-reliance on historical data | Missed trends | Include market intelligence |

## 10.2 Inventory Management Mistakes

| Mistake | Result | Correct Approach |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Excessive safety stock | Carrying costs | Statistical calculation |
| Insufficient buffer | Stockouts | Risk-based sizing |
| Ignoring obsolescence | Write-offs | Lifecycle management |
| Poor inventory accuracy | Fulfillment errors | Cycle count program |
| Not ABC classifying | Misallocated attention | Regular segmentation |

## 10.3 Supplier Management Errors

| Error | Impact | Mitigation |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Single-source critical items | Supply risk | Dual-source strategy |
| No supplier visibility | Disruption risk | Regular communication |
| Selecting on price only | Quality issues | Total cost approach |
| No performance scorecard | No accountability | KPI tracking |
| Ignoring supplier financial health | Continuity risk | Credit monitoring |

## 10.4 Technology Implementation Failures

| Pitfall | Result | Best Practice |
|---------|--------|---------------|
| Big bang implementation | High risk | Phased rollout |
| Not involving end users | Adoption failure | User involvement |
| Ignoring integration | Data silos | API-first design |
| No change management | Resistance | Training and communication |
| Over-customization | Maintenance burden | Standard platform first |

## 10.5 Cost Optimization Mistakes

| Anti-Pattern | Consequence | Better Approach |
|--------------|-------------|-----------------|
| Price-focused negotiations | Quality trade-offs | Total cost analysis |
| Cutting safety stock | Service risk | Cost-benefit analysis |
| Eliminating redundancies | Flexibility loss | Resiliency planning |
| Delayed technology investment | Competitiveness | ROI-based timing |
| Ignoring indirect costs | Budget surprises | Full cost visibility |

## 10.6 Process Execution Failures

- [ ] Skipping documentation of processes
- [ ] Not measuring key performance indicators
- [ ] Ignoring exception handling procedures
- [ ] Failing to communicate changes
- [ ] Not conducting regular reviews
- [ ] Underestimating lead times

## 10.7 Risk Management Oversights

| Risk | Common Mistake | Mitigation |
|------|---------------|------------|
| Supplier failure | No backup plan | Alternate suppliers |
| Transportation disruption | Single carrier | Multi-carrier strategy |
| Demand spike | No surge plan | Flexible capacity |
| Regulatory change | Unprepared | Monitoring and adaptation |
| Cybersecurity | No incident plan | Response protocols |

## 10.8 Collaboration Failures

| Error | Impact | Fix |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Internal silos | Inefficiency | Cross-functional teams |
| Poor supplier relationships | Collaboration failure | Partnership approach |
| Ignoring customer input | Misalignment | Voice of customer program |
| No executive support | Resource constraints | Business case advocacy |
| Lack of transparency | Trust issues | Open communication |

## 10.9 Metrics and Measurement Errors

- **Tracking wrong metrics** - Focus on actionable KPIs
- **No baseline comparison** - Establish benchmarks
- **Ignoring leading indicators** - Predictive metrics
- **Inconsistent measurement** - Standardized definitions
- **No regular review cadence** - Monthly performance reviews

## 10.10 Strategic Planning Mistakes

| Mistake | Long-term Impact | Prevention |
|---------|------------------|------------|
| Short-term focus | Sustainability risk | Long-range planning |
| Ignoring trends | Competitiveness loss | Horizon scanning |
| Overexpansion | Cash flow strain | Phased growth |
| Not scenario planning | Preparedness gap | What-if analysis |
| Lack of flexibility | Agility loss | Adaptable strategies |
