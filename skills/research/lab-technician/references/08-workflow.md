# Lab Technician Workflow Reference

Common workflow issues and challenges in laboratory technical practice.

## Safety & Compliance Issues

- **Problem**: Chemical containers not properly labeled with contents, hazard, and date.
  - **Issue**: Workers may not identify hazards; OSHA HazCom violations; emergency response compromised.
  - **Reference**: OSHA 29 CFR 1910.1200; institutional Chemical Hygiene Plan.

- **Problem**: Safety showers and eyewash stations not tested weekly.
  - **Issue**: Equipment may malfunction during emergency; OSHA compliance failure.
  - **Reference**: ANSI/ISEA Z358.1; OSHA requirements for emergency equipment inspection.

- **Problem**: Fume hoods not functioning properly; face velocity below acceptable range.
  - **Issue**: Vapor exposure; inadequate containment; regulatory non-compliance.
  - **Reference**: ASHRAE 110; OSHA Laboratory Standard; annual fume hood certification.

- **Problem**: Personal protective equipment (PPE) worn incorrectly or not at all.
  - **Issue**: Chemical exposure; biological exposure; radiation exposure.
  - **Reference**: OSHA 29 CFR 1910.132-138; institutional PPE policy.

- **Problem**: Hazardous waste not segregated properly; incompatible chemicals mixed.
  - **Issue**: Fire and explosion risk; regulatory violations; environmental contamination.
  - **Reference**: EPA RCRA; institutional chemical waste disposal guidelines; NFPA 45.

## Chemical Handling Issues

- **Problem**: Chemicals stored beyond expiration date or in improper conditions.
  - **Issue**: Degraded reagents producing erroneous results; safety hazards from unstable materials.
  - **Reference**: Manufacturer recommendations; institutional chemical inventory policy.

- **Problem**: Stock solutions not labeled with identity, concentration, preparer, and date.
  - **Issue**: Misidentification; incorrect concentrations; wasted reagents.
  - **Reference**: GLP requirements; institutional SOPs; laboratory best practices.

- **Problem**: Hazardous chemicals transported between buildings without proper containment.
  - **Issue**: Spill risk; regulatory violations (DOT); personal exposure.
  - **Reference**: DOT 49 CFR Parts 171-180; institutional transport guidelines.

- **Problem**: Mixing of incompatible chemical waste streams (e.g., acid with organic solvents).
  - **Issue**: Heat generation; toxic gas evolution; explosion risk.
  - **Reference**: EPA RCRA; institutional chemical waste management SOP; SDS hazard information.

## Biological Safety Issues

- **Problem**: Biohazardous waste not autoclaved or properly decontaminated before disposal.
  - **Issue**: Worker exposure; environmental release; regulatory violations (CDC, state health department).
  - **Reference**: BMBL; NIH Guidelines; institutional biosafety SOPs.

- **Problem**: BSL-2 practices not followed consistently; biosafety cabinet not used.
  - **Issue**: Worker exposure to bloodborne pathogens or biohazardous materials.
  - **Reference**: BMBL; institutional biosafety manual; OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens Standard.

- **Problem**: Recombinant DNA work performed without IBC approval or notification.
  - **Issue**: Regulatory violation; institutional IBC non-compliance; potential biohazard.
  - **Reference**: NIH Guidelines Section III; institutional IBC protocols.

## Equipment & Instrument Issues

- **Problem**: Preventive maintenance schedules not followed; instrument performance degrades.
  - **Issue**: Invalid data; instrument damage; increased downtime; increased repair costs.
  - **Reference**: Manufacturer PM schedules; CAP/CLSI QC requirements; institutional instrument management SOP.

- **Problem**: QC failures not investigated and resolved before sample analysis continues.
  - **Issue**: Invalid research data; regulatory findings; clinical diagnostic errors.
  - **Reference**: CLSI C24; Westgard rules; laboratory QC SOPs.

- **Problem**: Instrument calibration certificates expired or not traceable to NIST.
  - **Issue**: Data integrity questions; regulatory non-compliance in CLIA/GLP environments.
  - **Reference**: ISO 17025; CLSI guidelines; CAP checklist requirements.

- **Problem**: Pipettes not calibrated regularly; volumetric errors propagate through all experiments.
  - **Issue**: Systematic measurement error; irreproducible results; wasted reagents and samples.
  - **Reference**: Manufacturer calibration requirements; GLP requirements; laboratory QA policy.

## Documentation & Record Keeping Issues

- **Problem**: Laboratory notebook entries incomplete, unsigned, or undated.
  - **Issue**: IP disputes; data integrity questions; inability to reconstruct experiments.
  - **Reference**: GLP 21 CFR Part 58; institutional notebook policy; research integrity standards.

- **Problem**: Electronic data not backed up; original files overwritten.
  - **Issue**: Data loss; ALCOA+ violations; inability to reproduce results.
  - **Reference**: FDA 21 CFR Part 11; institutional data management policy; GLP requirements.

- **Problem**: Instrument logbooks not maintained; maintenance and use not recorded.
  - **Issue**: Audit findings; inability to troubleshoot problems; warranty claims missed.
  - **Reference**: CAP, CLSI, GLP requirements; institutional SOPs.

## Sample Handling Issues

- **Problem**: Samples stored at incorrect temperature; degradation not recognized.
  - **Issue**: Invalid analytical results; wasted sample; erroneous conclusions.
  - **Reference**: Sample-specific storage requirements; institutional sample management SOP.

- **Problem**: Chain of custody not maintained for forensic or regulated samples.
  - **Issue**: Legal inadmissibility; regulatory non-compliance; data integrity questions.
  - **Reference**: Chain of custody SOP; FDA GLP; CAP forensic standards.

- **Problem**: Sample labeling ambiguous or inconsistent; sample mix-ups.
  - **Issue**: Cross-contamination; incorrect results; wasted samples.
  - **Reference**: Laboratory SOPs; CAP/CLSI specimen identification standards.

## Animal Care Issues

- **Problem**: Animal health checks not performed per protocol schedule.
  - **Issue**: Undetected illness; animal welfare violation; data quality impact.
  - **Reference**: PHS Policy; ILAR Guide; IACUC protocol requirements.

- **Problem**: Anesthetic and analgesic records incomplete or not maintained.
  - **Issue**: Animal welfare violations; regulatory findings; IACUC audit findings.
  - **Reference**: ILAR Guide; AVMA Guidelines on Euthanasia; IACUC policy.

- **Problem**: Surgical monitoring not documented per GLP requirements.
  - **Issue**: Regulatory non-compliance; animal welfare concerns.
  - **Reference**: FDA GLP 21 CFR Part 58; ILAR Guide; AVMA surgical monitoring guidelines.
