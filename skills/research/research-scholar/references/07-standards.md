## 7.2 Grant Application Standards

### Major Funding Sources
- [NSF](https://www.nsf.gov/funding/) — National Science Foundation
- [NIH](https://grants.nih.gov/) — National Institutes of Health
- [ERC](https://erc.europa.eu/) — European Research Council
- [DOE](https://science.osti.gov/) — Department of Energy

### Grant Mechanisms
| Mechanism | Duration | Typical Budget | Target |
|-----------|-----------|-----------------|--------|
| NIH R01 | 3-5 years | $250K-500K/year | Established investigators |
| NIH R21 | 2 years | $275K total | Exploratory research |
| NSF CAREER | 5 years | $500K total | Early career |
| ERC Starting | 5 years | €1.5M | Early career |
| ERC Advanced | 5 years | €2.5M | Established |

### Grant Writing Resources
- [NIH Grants Strategy Guide](https://grants.nih.gov/) — Application strategies
- [NSF Merit Review Criteria](https://www.nsf.gov/pubs/policydocs/pappg22_2.jsp) — Review criteria
