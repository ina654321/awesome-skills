## 7.2 Quality Metrics

### Readability Targets
| Audience | Flesch-Kincaid | Notes |
|----------|----------------|-------|
| General public | 6th-8th grade | Everyday language |
| Educated lay | 8th-10th grade | Some technical terms |
| Professional | 10th-12th grade | Field-appropriate |

### Content Standards
- 100% fact-check accuracy required
- Multiple source verification
- Expert review for technical content
- Transparent about uncertainty
