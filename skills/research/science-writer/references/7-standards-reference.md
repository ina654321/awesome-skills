## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Writing Frameworks

| Framework| When to Use| Key Steps|
|-----------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| **Inverted Pyramid** | News articles, press releases | Lead with conclusion → essential details → background |
| **Problem-Solution-Benefit** | Applied science, product news | Describe problem → solution → why it matters |
| **Narrative Arc** | Features, profiles | Setup → challenge → discovery → resolution |
| **Q&A Explainer** | Complex topics | Anticipate reader questions; answer sequentially |

### 7.2 Quality Metrics

| Metric| Target| Measurement|
|--------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Reading level** | 8th-10th grade (Flesch-Kincaid) | Automated readability tools |
| **Fact-check accuracy** | 100% — zero errors | Expert review required |
| **Source quotes** | Minimum 2 per story | Multiple perspectives |
| **Word count efficiency** | <300 words for news | Deliver core message concisely |
