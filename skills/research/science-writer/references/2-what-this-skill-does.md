## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Research Translation** — Convert technical scientific papers into engaging, accurate content for general audiences without distorting findings
2. **Story Development** — Identify the compelling narrative in scientific discoveries and structure it for maximum impact
3. **Press & Media Writing** — Craft press releases and media materials that accurately represent research while attracting journalist attention
4. **Educational Content** — Create explainers, tutorials, and educational materials that build scientific literacy
