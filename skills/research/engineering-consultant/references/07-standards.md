# Engineering Consultant Reference Standards

Professional standards, regulations, and institutional policies governing engineering consulting practice.

## Professional Licensure & Certification

- **National Council of Examiners for Engineering and Surveying (NCEES)**: Administers the Principles and Practice of Engineering (PE) exam and model laws for licensure. [ncees.org](https://ncees.org)
- **State Licensing Boards**: Each U.S. state boards sets specific requirements for engineering licensure, seal requirements, and continuing education (e.g., Texas Board of Professional Engineers, California Board for Professional Engineers and Land Surveyors).
- **Structural Engineering Certification**: The National Council of Structural Engineers Associations (NCSEA) and the Structural Engineers Association (SEA) provide certification frameworks for structural engineers.
- **International Engineering Alliance (IEA)**: Governs the Washington Accord (engineer), Sydney Accord (engineering technologist), and Dublin Accord (engineering technician) for international mobility.

## Safety & Regulatory Standards

- **Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA)**: Federal regulations (29 CFR 1910 for general industry, 1926 for construction) governing workplace safety. [osha.gov](https://osha.gov)
- **American Society of Safety Professionals (ASSP)**: Professional standards including Z10 Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems. [assp.org](https://assp.org)
- **National Fire Protection Association (NFPA)**: Codes including NFPA 70 (National Electrical Code), NFPA 101 (Life Safety Code). [nfpa.org](https://nfpa.org)
- **International Building Code (IBC)**: Model building code adopted by most U.S. jurisdictions, published by the International Code Council (ICC). [iccsafe.org](https://iccsafe.org)
- **Federal Highway Administration (FHWA)**: Regulations and design guides (AASHTO) for transportation infrastructure. [fhwa.dot.gov](https://fhwa.dot.gov)

## Engineering Standards Organizations

- **American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE)**: Standards (ASCE 7 for loads, ASCE 24 for flood-resistant design), codes of ethics, and policy statements. [asce.org](https://asce.org)
- **American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME)**: Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (BPVC), standards for mechanical engineering design and manufacturing. [asme.org](https://asme.org)
- **Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)**: Standards for electrical, electronic, and computing systems (e.g., IEEE 519 for harmonic control). [ieee.org](https://ieee.org)
- **ASTM International**: Material standards, test methods, and specifications across industries. [astm.org](https://astm.org)
- **American Concrete Institute (ACI)**: Building code requirements for reinforced concrete (ACI 318) and related standards. [aciorg.org](https://aciorg.org)
- **American Institute of Steel Construction (AISC)**: Steel construction standards, specifications, and manuals. [aisc.org](https://aisc.org)
- **National Society of Professional Engineers (NSPE)**: Code of Ethics, licensure advocacy, and professional practice guidelines. [nspe.org](https://nspe.org)
- **American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE)**: Energy efficiency standards, building HVAC standards. [ashrae.org](https://ashrae.org)

## Professional Liability & Insurance

- **National Society of Professional Engineers (NSPE)**: Model language for engineering contracts, professional liability guidance, and risk management. [nspe.org](https://nspe.org)
- **American Council of Engineering Companies (ACEC)**: Business practice guidelines, contract standards, and insurance program recommendations. [acec.org](https://acec.org)
- **Engineers' Joint Contract Documents Committee (EJCDC)**: Standard contract documents for engineering services (C-141, C-191, C-520). [ejcdc.org](https://ejcdc.org)

## Building & Infrastructure Codes

- **International Code Council (ICC)**: I-Codes family including IBC, IRC, IECC, and referenced standards. [iccsafe.org](https://iccsafe.org)
- **International Energy Conservation Code (IECC)**: Energy efficiency standards for buildings. [iccsafe.org](https://iccsafe.org)
- **American Association of State Highway and Transportation Officials (AASHTO)**: LRFD Bridge Design Specifications, Roadside Design Guide, and transportation standards. [aashto.org](https://aashto.org)
- **U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC)**: LEED certification standards for sustainable building. [usgbc.org](https://usgbc.org)

## Environmental Regulations

- **U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)**: Clean Air Act, Clean Water Act, RCRA, CERCLA compliance for engineering projects. [epa.gov](https://epa.gov)
- **Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ)**: National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) implementing regulations and guidance. [ceq.doe.gov](https://ceq.doe.gov)

## Quality Management

- **International Organization for Standardization (ISO)**: ISO 9001 (quality management), ISO 14001 (environmental management), ISO 45001 (occupational health and safety). [iso.org](https://iso.org)
- **American Society for Quality (ASQ)**: Certifications including Certified Quality Engineer (CQE), Quality Auditor (CQA). [asq.org](https://asq.org)

## Continuing Education

- **National Society of Professional Engineers (NSPE)**: Continuing professional competency requirements by state. [nspe.org](https://nspe.org)
- **American Society of Civil Engineers (ASCE)**: Continuing education guidelines and seminars. [asce.org](https://asce.org)
- **Engineering Accreditation Commission (EAC/ABET)**: Continuing competency frameworks for accredited engineering programs. [abet.org](https://abet.org)
