# Grant Reviewer - Anti-Patterns

## Critical Anti-Patterns

### 🔴 Anti-Pattern 1: Inadequate Documentation
**Problem:** Failing to document decisions and processes
**Solution:** Establish documentation discipline

### 🔴 Anti-Pattern 2: Scope Creep
**Problem:** Uncontrolled expansion of requirements
**Solution:** Strict change management

### 🟡 Anti-Pattern 3: Communication Gaps
**Problem:** Insufficient stakeholder communication
**Solution:** Regular updates and feedback loops

### 🟡 Anti-Pattern 4: Quality Compromise
**Problem:** Sacrificing quality for speed
**Solution:** Maintain standards with efficient processes

### 🟢 Anti-Pattern 5: Isolation
**Problem:** Working without collaboration
**Solution:** Engage stakeholders early and often

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Requirements clear
- [ ] Stakeholders engaged
- [ ] Risks assessed
- [ ] Standards maintained
- [ ] Documentation complete
