# Instrument Manager Workflow Reference

Common workflow issues and challenges in scientific instrumentation management.

## Procurement & Acquisition Issues

- **Problem**: Instruments ordered without adequate needs assessment or consultation with end users.
  - **Issue**: Equipment purchased that doesn't meet actual research needs; poor utilization rates.
  - **Reference**: Core facility best practices recommend user surveys and pilot testing before purchase.

- **Problem**: Vendor demonstrations not structured to evaluate key performance parameters.
  - **Issue**: Demo units optimized for performance; purchased units don't match demo results.
  - **Reference**: Request standardized test protocols and reference materials for demonstrations.

- **Problem**: Warranty and service contracts not negotiated before purchase.
  - **Issue**: Post-purchase service costs exceed budget; vendor monopolizes service market.
  - **Reference**: Negotiate service contracts and pricing before PO issuance; request OEM service options.

- **Problem**: Inadequate infrastructure assessment (power, space, ventilation, network) before instrument arrival.
  - **Issue**: Instrument arrives and cannot be installed; costly modifications required.
  - **Reference**: Site preparation checklists from vendors; coordinate with facilities and IT.

## Installation & Validation Issues

- **Problem**: Installation qualification (IQ) and operational qualification (OQ) not performed systematically.
  - **Issue**: Performance problems not identified; GLP/cGMP compliance failures.
  - **Reference**: CLSI guidelines; 21 CFR Part 820 for medical device manufacturing environments.

- **Problem**: Instrument software not validated for intended use before production use.
  - **Issue**: Data integrity issues; regulatory compliance failures in GLP/GMP environments.
  - **Reference**: FDA 21 CFR Part 11; GAMP 5 guidelines for computer system validation.

- **Problem**: Network integration issues (LIMS, data servers, remote access) not resolved before go-live.
  - **Issue**: Data cannot be transferred automatically; manual workarounds create errors.
  - **Reference**: IT integration testing protocols; coordinate with research IT and LIMS administrators.

- **Problem**: Users not trained before independent instrument access is granted.
  - **Issue**: Instrument damage, data quality issues, and safety incidents.
  - **Reference**: Competency assessment requirements; documented training records.

## Maintenance & Calibration Issues

- **Problem**: Preventive maintenance schedules not established or followed.
  - **Issue**: Instrument performance degrades; unplanned downtime increases; warranty claims missed.
  - **Reference**: Manufacturer PM schedules; institutional QC policies; CAP/CLSI guidelines.

- **Problem**: Calibration certificates not traceable to NIST standards.
  - **Issue**: Data quality questions; regulatory compliance failures in regulated environments.
  - **Reference**: ISO 17025 requirements; NIST traceability guidelines.

- **Problem**: Calibration results not reviewed against acceptance criteria before instrument use.
  - **Issue**: Out-of-specification instruments used for production runs.
  - **Reference**: CLSI QC guidelines; laboratory accreditation requirements.

- **Problem**: Service logs and maintenance records not kept or inaccessible.
  - **Issue**: Audit findings; inability to diagnose intermittent performance problems.
  - **Reference**: 21 CFR Part 11; FDA inspection references service logs; CAP checklist requirements.

## Performance & Quality Control Issues

- **Problem**: QC rules not appropriate for the instrument or clinical context.
  - **Issue**: False rejections or false acceptances; quality management system failures.
  - **Reference**: CLSI C24 (Statistical Quality Control for Quantitative Measurements); Westgard rules.

- **Problem**: QC materials not stored correctly or past expiration used.
  - **Issue**: Invalid QC data; erroneous patient/research results.
  - **Reference**: CLSI guidelines; manufacturer storage recommendations; laboratory SOPs.

- **Problem**: Performance qualification (PQ) / proficiency testing not performed at required intervals.
  - **Issue**: Instrument drift not detected; accreditation deficiencies.
  - **Reference**: CAP proficiency testing requirements; CLSI guidelines.

## Safety Issues

- **Problem**: Cryogenic liquid transfer procedures not followed; burns and asphyxiation risks.
  - **Issue**: Liquid nitrogen burns; oxygen depletion in enclosed spaces; regulatory violations.
  - **Reference**: CGA guidelines; institutional cryogenic safety programs; NFPA 45.

- **Problem**: High-pressure systems (HPLC, gas cylinders) not inspected and maintained.
  - **Issue**: Catastrophic failure; explosion risk; regulatory violations.
  - **Reference**: ASME BPVC; CGA standards; OSHA 29 CFR 1910.134.

- **Problem**: Laser equipment operated without appropriate laser safety controls.
  - **Issue**: Eye and skin injuries; failure to register Class 3B/4 lasers with institutional laser safety officer.
  - **Reference**: ANSI Z136 series; institutional Laser Safety Program; OSHA regulations.

- **Problem**: Radiation-producing equipment (X-ray, electron microscopes) used without required surveys.
  - **Issue**: Regulatory violations; potential overexposure; fines from NRC or agreement state.
  - **Reference**: NRC regulations 10 CFR Parts 20, 30-36; state radiation control regulations.

## Data Integrity & Compliance Issues

- **Problem**: Electronic data not backed up or archived per retention policies.
  - **Issue**: Data loss; inability to recreate study results; regulatory findings.
  - **Reference**: 21 CFR Part 11; institutional data management policies; ALCOA+ principles.

- **Problem**: Manual integration of chromatographic data without documentation.
  - **Issue**: Data integrity concerns; peak areas reported incorrectly; regulatory findings.
  - **Reference**: FDA guidance on integration; ALCOA+ principles; FDA data integrity guidance.

- **Problem**: Instrument computer clocks not synchronized or not audit-logged.
  - **Issue**: Timestamp discrepancies; data integrity findings in regulated environments.
  - **Reference**: FDA 21 CFR Part 11; computer system validation requirements.

## User Access & Training Issues

- **Problem**: Users not formally enrolled in instrument access system; untrained users operating equipment.
  - **Issue**: Instrument damage; data quality problems; safety incidents; audit failures.
  - **Reference**: CAP/CLSI competency requirements; institutional training documentation policies.

- **Problem**: Instrument access not revoked when users leave the institution or change roles.
  - **Issue**: Unauthorized use; security concerns; intellectual property risk.
  - **Reference**: Institutional offboarding procedures; core facility access management policies.

## Disposition & Decommissioning Issues

- **Problem**: Instruments disposed without proper decontamination certification.
  - **Issue**: Liability exposure; environmental violations; biohazard/radiation contamination spread.
  - **Reference**: EPA RCRA regulations; state environmental agency requirements; institutional EHS policies.

- **Problem**: Asset records not updated when instruments are relocated, surplus, or decommissioned.
  - **Issue**: Inventory inaccuracies; insurance gaps; depreciation tracking errors.
  - **Reference**: Institutional property management policies; sponsor requirements.
