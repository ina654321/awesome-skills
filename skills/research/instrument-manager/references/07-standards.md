# Instrument Manager Reference Standards

Standards, regulations, and institutional policies governing scientific instrumentation management.

## Safety & Regulatory Frameworks

- **Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA)**: Standards for laboratory safety (29 CFR 1910), hazard communication, and occupational exposure to hazardous chemicals. [osha.gov](https://osha.gov)
- **National Fire Protection Association (NFPA)**: NFPA 45 (Fire Protection for Laboratories Using Chemicals), NFPA 70 (National Electrical Code), NFPA 70E (Electrical Safety in the Workplace). [nfpa.org](https://nfpa.org)
- **Compressed Gas Association (CGA)**: Standards for compressed gas cylinders, fittings, and safety. [cganet.com](https://cganet.com)
- **National Institutes of Health (NIH)**: NIH Design Requirements Manual for biomedical research facilities; biosafety levels (BSL-1 through BSL-4). [nih.gov](https://nih.gov)
- **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)**: Biosafety in Microbiological and Biomedical Laboratories (BMBL) 6th edition. [cdc.gov](https://cdc.gov)

## Electrical & Equipment Safety

- **Underwriters Laboratories (UL)**: Product safety testing and certification; UL 61010-1 for laboratory equipment. [ul.com](https://ul.com)
- **International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC)**: IEC 61010 series for safety requirements of electrical equipment for measurement, control, and laboratory use. [iec.ch](https://iec.ch)
- **National Electrical Code (NEC / NFPA 70)**: Requirements for electrical installations in laboratories and research facilities. [nfpa.org](https://nfpa.org)
- **American National Standards Institute (ANSI)**: Safety standards for various laboratory equipment and operations. [ansi.org](https://ansi.org)

## Laboratory Safety Standards

- **College of American Pathologists (CAP)**: Laboratory general checklists for instrument management, quality control, and safety. [cap.org](https://cap.org)
- **The Joint Commission (TJC)**: Standards for laboratory accreditation including equipment management and safety. [jointcommission.org](https://jointcommission.org)
- **Clinical and Laboratory Standards Institute (CLSI)**: Guidelines for verification and validation of laboratory tests, quality control, and instrument performance. [clsi.org](https://clsi.org)
- **American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM)**: Standards for laboratory equipment, including ASTM E627 (Performance of Laboratory Equipment).

## Environmental & Waste Management

- **Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)**: Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) for hazardous waste management. [epa.gov](https://epa.gov)
- **Environmental Health & Radiation Safety (EHRS)**: Institutional radiation safety and chemical hygiene programs.
- **State Environmental Agencies**: Local regulations governing chemical waste, biological waste, and electronic waste disposal.

## Cryogenic & Pressure Systems

- **American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME)**: Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (BPVC) for pressure vessels; ASME Section VIII for pressure vessel design. [asme.org](https://asme.org)
- **CGA (Compressed Gas Association)**: CGA P-1 (Safe Handling of Compressed Gases), CGA P-18 (Bulk CO2 Systems), and cryogenic liquid handling guidelines. [cganet.com](https://cganet.com)
- **Cryogenic Society of America (CSA)**: Guidelines for safe handling of cryogenic liquids and liquefied gases. [cryogenicsociety.org](https://cryogenicsociety.org)

## Laser & Radiation Safety

- **American National Standards Institute (ANSI)**: ANSI Z136 series for laser safety; ANSI/HPS N13.1 for radionuclide handling. [ansi.org](https://ansi.org)
- **Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC)**: Regulations for radioactive material use, licensing, and radiation protection (10 CFR Parts 19, 20, 30-36, 39, 40, 55, 70, 71, 73). [nrc.gov](https://nrc.gov)
- **U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission Agreement States**: States with authority to regulate radioactive material use; regulations vary by state.

## Calibration & Metrology

- **National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)**: Measurement standards, SRMs (Standard Reference Materials), and calibration traceability. [nist.gov](https://nist.gov)
- **International Organization for Standardization (ISO)**: ISO 17025 (General requirements for testing and calibration laboratories), ISO Guide 34 (Reference material producers). [iso.org](https://iso.org)
- **American Association for Laboratory Accreditation (A2LA)**: Accreditation for testing and calibration laboratories. [a2la.org](https://a2la.org)

## Data Integrity & Computer Systems

- **FDA 21 CFR Part 11**: Electronic records and electronic signatures for regulated environments (pharmaceutical, medical device). [fda.gov](https://fda.gov)
- **OECD Principles of Good Laboratory Practice (GLP)**: Standards for non-clinical laboratory studies. [oecd.org](https://oecd.org)
- **ALCOA+ Principles**: Data integrity framework: Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate + Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available.

## Professional Organizations

- **American Chemical Society (ACS)**: Division of Chemical Health and Safety; safety guidelines for chemical laboratories. [acs.org](https://acs.org)
- **Society for Laboratory Automation and Screening (SLAS)**: Standards for laboratory automation and screening technologies. [slas.org](https://slas.org)
- **Association of Public Laboratories (APHL)**: Guidelines for public health laboratory instrumentation and quality management. [aphl.org](https://aphl.org)
- **International Society for Pharmaceutical Engineering (ISPE)**: Good Automated Manufacturing Practice (GAMP) guidelines for laboratory computer systems. [ispe.org](https://ispe.org)

## Facility & Infrastructure

- **American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE)**: Laboratory ventilation standards; ASHRAE 62.1 (ventilation), ASHRAE 55 (thermal comfort). [ashrae.org](https://ashrae.org)
- **International Mechanical Code (IMC)**: Requirements for laboratory exhaust systems and special exhaust.
- **International Building Code (IBC)**: Occupancy classifications and construction requirements for laboratories.
- **Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)**: Accessibility requirements for laboratory spaces and equipment.
