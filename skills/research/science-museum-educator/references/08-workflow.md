# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Exhibit Interpretation Session

### Phase 1: Orientation (2-3 minutes)

**Step 1.1 - Welcome**
- Greet visitors warmly
- Introduce yourself
- Set expectations: "Feel free to touch..."

**Step 1.2 - Space Orientation**
- Point out key areas
- Explain any rules
- Set the tone

**Step 1.3 - Opening Invitation**
- Ask an open question
- "What catches your eye?"
- "What do you notice?"

### Phase 2: Exploration (5-15 minutes)

**Step 2.1 - Independent Exploration**
- Let visitors explore first
- Don't rush to explain
- Observe what interests them

**Step 2.2 - Circulate and Observe**
- Watch for engagement
- Note what generates questions
- Join when appropriate

**Step 2.3 - Encourage Hands-On**
- Prompt interaction
- "Would you like to try?"
- Support exploration

### Phase 3: Facilitation (5-10 minutes)

**Step 3.1 - Draw Attention**
- Point out key features
- Connect to visitors' observations
- Build on what they noticed

**Step 3.2 - Ask Probing Questions**
- "Why do you think...?"
- "What might happen if...?"
- "How does this connect to...?"

**Step 3.3 - Introduce Concepts**
- Use vocabulary naturally
- Connect to everyday experience
- Explain without lecturing

### Phase 4: Synthesis (2-5 minutes)

**Step 4.1 - Make Connections**
- Help visitors see links
- "So what does this mean?"
- Connect to daily life

**Step 4.2 - Encourage Continued Exploration**
- Point to related exhibits
- Suggest questions to explore
- Welcome their return

## 8.2 Program Development Workflow

### Step 1: Learning Objectives
- What will visitors understand?
- What will they be able to do?
- What attitudes will change?

### Step 2: Audience Analysis
- Who is this for?
- What do they already know?
- What interests them?

### Step 3: Activity Design
- What hands-on experience?
- How does it create learning?
- What materials needed?

### Step 4: Facilitation Guide
- What will educator say?
- What questions to ask?
- How to manage time?

### Step 5: Materials List
- Everything needed
- Quantities
- Setup requirements

### Step 6: Safety Review
- Identify hazards
- Mitigation measures
- Emergency procedures

### Step 7: Pilot Testing
- Try with real visitors
- Observe and note issues
- Refine based on feedback

### Step 8: Documentation
- Final guide
- Facilitator training
- Continuous improvement
