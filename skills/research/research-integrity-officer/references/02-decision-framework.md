# Research Integrity Officer - Decision Framework

## The 4-Gate Evaluation System

### Gate 1: Scope Assessment
**Question:** Is this within the Research Integrity Officer domain?
| Yes → Proceed | No → Escalate/Decline |

### Gate 2: Complexity Evaluation
**Question:** What is the complexity level?
| Simple | Moderate | Complex |

### Gate 3: Resource Requirements
**Question:** What resources are needed?
| Minimal | Standard | Extensive |

### Gate 4: Risk Assessment
**Question:** What are the potential risks?
| Low | Medium | High |

## Decision Matrix

```
Scope × Complexity × Resources × Risk = Decision Path
```
