# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns in Research Project Management

| # | Anti-Pattern| Severity| Quick Fix|
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Missing internal institutional deadlines** | 🔴 High | SRO needs 2 weeks for compliance review; submit early |
| 2 | **Calculating F&A on total direct costs** | 🔴 High | Calculate on MTDC (exclude equipment >$5K, subcontract >$25K) |
| 3 | **Key personnel changes without prior approval** | 🔴 High | NIH requires prior approval for PI change, absence >3 months, effort reduction ≥25% |
| 4 | **Enrolling participants before IRB approval** | 🔴 High | Research cannot begin until IRB approval in effect |
| 5 | **Missing NIH public access compliance** | 🔴 High | Deposit publications in PMC within 12 months |

## 10.2 Budget Mistakes

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|----------|----------------|
| "Include all direct costs in F&A calculation" | Calculate MTDC: exclude equipment >$5K, first $25K subcontract |
| "Modular budget for $280K direct costs" | Detailed budget required when direct costs >$250K |
| "No budget justification needed" | Every category requires narrative justification |

## 10.3 Timeline Errors

### Common Problems
- Submitting at the last minute without SRO review
- Missing LOI deadlines for required Letters of Intent
- Not allowing time for IRB revisions
- Waiting until the last minute for key personnel letters

### Best Practice Timeline
```
T-12 weeks: Start Specific Aims
T-8 weeks: Complete Research Strategy
T-6 weeks: Gather all forms and letters
T-4 weeks: Submit to SRO
T-2 weeks: Address SRO comments
T-0: Agency deadline
```

## 10.4 Compliance Pitfalls

### IRB Issues
- Continuing review expired mid-study → must STOP all research
- Protocol amendments not submitted before implementing changes
- Consent form not updated when required

### Reporting Issues
- RPPR not submitted on time → can affect future funding
- No-cost extension requested too late
- Final invention statement not filed

### Public Access Issues
- Not depositing in PMC → hold on future awards
- Not tracking PMCIDs in eRA Commons

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

Before submission, verify:
- [ ] Specific Aims reviewed by co-investigators
- [ ] Biosketches in current format (5 pages max)
- [ ] Budget matches narrative justification
- [ ] F&A calculated on MTDC
- [ ] Equipment listed if >$5K
- [ ] Human subjects section complete
- [ ] Key personnel effort documented
- [ ] Letters of support collected
- [ ] SRO has reviewed for compliance
