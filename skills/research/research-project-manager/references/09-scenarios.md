# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 NIH R01 Budget Development

**User:** "I need to develop a Year 1 budget for a 5-year R01. PI at 25% effort, 1 postdoc at 100%, coordinator at 50%, $50K equipment, $30K supplies, $15K travel. F&A rate is 55%."

**Research Project Manager:**
> **Year 1 Budget Calculation:**
>
> | Category | Direct Costs |
> |----------|---------------|
> | PI (25% + fringe) | $45,000 |
> | Postdoc (100% + fringe) | $85,000 |
> | Coordinator (50% + fringe) | $35,000 |
> | Equipment | $50,000 |
> | Supplies | $30,000 |
> | Travel | $15,000 |
> | **Total Direct** | **$260,000** |
>
> **MTDC Calculation:**
> - Equipment >$5K excluded: $50,000 - $5,000 = $45,000 excluded
> - MTDC = $260,000 - $45,000 = $215,000
>
> **F&A (Indirect) = $215,000 × 0.55 = $118,250**
>
> **Total Year 1 = $260,000 + $118,250 = $378,250**
>
> **Note:** Direct costs >$250K requires detailed budget (not modular).

## 9.2 IRB Protocol Submission

**User:** "We're submitting a multi-site clinical trial with 200 participants across 3 sites. What IRB category applies?"

**Research Project Manager:**
> **IRB Classification:**
>
> **Step 1: Human subjects?** Yes — requires IRB review
>
> **Step 2: Exempt?** Check 45 CFR 46.104
> - Not exempt — behavioral intervention with identifiable data
>
> **Step 3: Minimal risk?** Yes — standard clinical trial risk
>
> **Step 4: Expedited eligible?** Possibly — blood pressure measurement only
>
> **Step 5: Multi-site?** Yes — requires cIRB (single IRB) per NIH policy
>
> **Recommendation:** Expedited review + cIRB arrangement
>
> **Required Documents:**
> - Protocol (version-dated)
> - Consent form (≤12 pages, 6th grade reading level)
> - HIPAA authorization or waiver request
> - Investigator CVs
> - Recruitment materials
> - Data management plan

## 9.3 Project Behind Schedule

**User:** "Year 2 of R01. Enrollment at 40% of target. Key postdoc left 3 months ago. How do we recover?"

**Research Project Manager:**
> **EVM Analysis:**
>
> | Metric | Value | Status |
> |--------|-------|--------|
> | Planned Value (PV) | $100,000 | - |
> | Earned Value (EV) | $68,000 | - |
> | Actual Cost (AC) | $95,000 | - |
> | SPI = EV/PV | 0.68 | BEHIND |
> | CPI = EV/AC | 0.72 | OVER BUDGET |
>
> **Recovery Plan:**
> 1. Hire replacement immediately — request prior approval if salary changes
> 2. Proactively request NCE at Year 4 if not caught up by Year 3
> 3. Submit protocol amendment to reduce exclusion criteria
> 4. Document in RPPR: personnel gap, steps taken, timeline
> 5. Re-analyze critical path — can Aim 2 activities begin in parallel?
> 6. Contact NIH Program Officer — don't hide problems
>
> **Estimated at Completion:** $95K/0.72 = $132,700 (vs. $100K planned)
