# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Grant Application Standards

### NIH Grant Application
- [NIH Guide to Grants and Contracts](https://grants.nih.gov/) — Official NIH funding portal
- [Grants.gov](https://www.grants.gov/) — Federal grant applications
- [NIH SF424 (R&R) Guide](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/424.htm) — Application forms
- [eRA Commons](https://commons.era.nih.gov/commons/) — NIH electronic handling

### NSF Grant Application
- [NSF FastLane](https://www.fastlane.nsf.gov/) — NSF funding portal
- [NSF Proposal & Award Policies & Procedures Guide](https://www.nsf.gov/pubs/policydocs/pappg22_2.jsp) — PAPPG

### Common Application Elements
- [Specific Aims](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/how-to-apply-application-guide.html) — One-page research plan
- [Research Strategy](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/how-to-apply-application-guide.html) — Significance, Innovation, Approach
- [Biosketches](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/forms/biosketch.htm) — 5-page format (2023)
- [Budget Justification](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/how-to-apply-application-guide.html) — Required for all years

## 7.2 Budget Standards

### NIH Budget Categories
| Category | MTDC Eligible? | Notes |
|----------|----------------|-------|
| Personnel | Yes | Salary + fringe |
| Equipment >$5,000 | No | Excluded from MTDC |
| Supplies | Yes | Direct materials |
| Travel | Yes | Domestic/International |
| Patient Care | No | Excluded |
| Subcontract >$25K | No | Only first $25K included |

### F&A (Indirect Cost) Rates
- [NIH F&A Rate Agreements](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/indirectcost.htm) — Negotiated rates
- On-campus: Typical 45-65%
- Off-campus: Typically 26% (reduced rate)

### Budget Modifications
- NIH: >25% reallocation requires prior approval
- No-cost extension (NCE): Up to 12 months with justification

## 7.3 IRB/IACUC Standards

### IRB Categories (45 CFR 46)
- **Exempt** (46.104): Minimal risk, no continuing review
- **Expedited** (46.110): Minimal risk, minor changes
- **Full Board** (46.107): Greater than minimal risk

### IRB Resources
- [OHRP Guidance](https://www.hhs.gov/ohrp/index.html) — Human subjects protections
- [IRB Continuing Review](https://www.hhs.gov/ohrp/regulations-and-policy/guidance/index.html) — Review requirements

### IACUC Requirements
- [PHS Policy](https://olaw.nih.gov/) — Animal welfare requirements
- [AVMA Guidelines](https://www.avma.org/) — Veterinary guidelines

## 7.4 Regulatory Compliance

### Clinical Trial Requirements
- [ClinicalTrials.gov Registration](https://clinicaltrials.gov/) — Required for NIH-funded trials
- [21 CFR Part 11](https://www.fda.gov/) — Electronic records

### Reporting Requirements
- [RPPR (Research Performance Progress Report)](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/rppr/) — Annual reporting
- [NIH Public Access Policy](https://publicaccess.nih.gov/) — PMC deposition required within 12 months
