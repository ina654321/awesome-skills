# Common Workflow Issues & Troubleshooting

## 8.1 Pre-Surgical Preparation Problems

| Problem | Symptom | Root Cause | Solution |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Animal weight miscalculation | Over/under drug dosing | Scale not calibrated | Calibrate scale daily; weigh each animal |
| Incorrect anesthetic depth | Movement during surgery | Insufficient induction time | Extend induction 3-5 min; verify pedal reflex |
| Surgical site contamination | Post-op infection | Aseptic technique breach | Review sterile technique; replace instruments |
| Instrument rust | Corrosion on tools | Improper drying after sterilization | Dry thoroughly; store in dry cabinet |
| Missing recovery supplies | Delayed post-op care | Inadequate preparation | Prepare full recovery kit before starting |

## 8.2 Injection Technique Problems

| Problem | Symptom | Root Cause | Solution |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Tail vein blow | Swelling, no injection | Wrong angle, damaged vein | Use warmer animal to dilate veins; adjust angle |
| IP injection into bladder | Bloody urine after IP | Wrong location | Always inject in lower left quadrant; use small gauge |
| SC injection leakage | Wet fur, reduced volume | Needle angle too shallow | Insert at 45°; withdraw slightly before injecting |
| IM injection into sciatic nerve | Limb paralysis | Wrong site | Inject into upper quadriceps only; avoid posterior |
| Oral gavage aspiration | Respiratory distress | Tube in trachea | Measure tube length to xiphoid; verify placement |

## 8.3 Euthanasia Workflow Problems

| Problem | Symptom | Root Cause | Solution |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------|
| Incomplete euthanasia | Animal revives after CO2 | Insufficient exposure time | Use approved method per species; verify cessation |
| Tissue autolysis | Degraded samples post-euthanasia | Delay between death and collection | Collect tissues immediately; use appropriate method |
| Vertebral fracture | Spinal cord visible after cervical dislocation | Incorrect technique | Receive certified training; use 2-hand method for larger animals |
| Blood contamination | Bloody tissues on necropsy | Euthanasia method artifact | Use appropriate method for intended tissue use |

## 8.4 Welfare Monitoring Escalation

```
Normal Indicators:
✓ Alert, active, normal posture
✓ Normal food/water consumption
✓ Grooming behavior present
✓ Normal respiratory rate (40-60/min mouse; 70-100/min rat)
✓ Pink mucosa, warm extremities

CONCERN Indicators (Increase monitoring to 2x/day):
⚠ Weight loss 10-15%
⚠ Reduced activity, hunched posture
⚠ Rough hair coat, porphyrin staining
⚠ Reduced food/water intake
⚠ Isolation from cage mates

CRITICAL Indicators (Immediate veterinary consult):
🔴 Weight loss >15%
🔴 Moribund state, unresponsive
🔴 Severe respiratory distress
🔴 Seizures or circling
🔴 Self-mutilation
🔴 Unexpected death
```

## 8.5 Post-Operative Complication Troubleshooting

| Complication | Signs | Immediate Action | Follow-up |
|-------------|-------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Hemorrhage** | Bleeding from incision | Apply pressure; contact vet | Monitor for 24 hr; check hematocrit |
| **Dehiscence** | Open incision | Cover with sterile gauze; contact vet | Rescue surgery may be needed |
| **Infection** | Redness, swelling, discharge | Photograph; contact vet | Culture and antibiotics |
| **Pain indicators** | Vocalization, porphyrin, hunched | Additional analgesia per protocol | Increase monitoring frequency |
| **Hypothermia** | Cold body, reduced movement | Place on warming pad | Monitor temperature until normal |
| **Dehydration** | Skin tenting, sunken eyes | Subcutaneous fluids | Weigh and assess hydration |

## 8.6 Common IACUC Compliance Issues

| Issue | Problem | Prevention | Corrective Action |
|-------|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Personnel not trained | Untrained person performs procedure | Maintain training records; certify before procedure | Retrain; reassign to trained personnel |
| Protocol deviation | Procedure differs from approved | Follow protocol exactly; document if needed | Report deviation to IACUC immediately |
| Expired protocol | Research continues after expiration | Set calendar alerts 60 days before expiration | Stop research until reapproved |
| Missing documentation | No record of procedure | Document in real-time; never after the fact | Create contemporaneous records |
| Unauthorized species | Working with unapproved species | Verify species approval in protocol | Halt work; submit amendment |
