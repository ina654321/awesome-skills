# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Invention Evaluation Process

### Phase 1: Invention Disclosure Review (Week 1-2)

**Step 1.1 - Receipt and Logging**
- Complete invention disclosure form
- Assign disclosure number
- Log in tracking system

**Step 1.2 - Completeness Check**
- Is there sufficient detail?
- Are all inventors identified?
- Is there enabling disclosure?

**Step 1.3 - Inventor Interview**
- Understand vision for commercialization
- Discuss prior public disclosures
- Identify competing interests

### Phase 2: IP Protection Decision (Week 3-6)

**Step 2.1 - Prior Art Search**
- Patent database search
- Publication search
- Freedom to operate analysis

**Step 2.2 - Patentability Assessment**
- Novelty determination
- Non-obviousness assessment
- Write patent claims

**Step 2.3 - Decision: File or Abandon**
- Provisional vs. non-provisional
- International filing (PCT)
- Trade secret alternative

### Phase 3: Market Assessment (Week 7-12)

**Step 3.1 - Application Identification**
- What problems does this solve?
- Who would pay?
- What's the market size?

**Step 3.2 - Competitive Analysis**
- Who else is working in this space?
- What's their approach?
- What's our differentiation?

**Step 3.3 - Development Assessment**
- What's needed to commercialize?
- What's the timeline?
- What's the cost?

### Phase 4: Commercialization Path (Week 13+)

**Step 4.1 - Strategy Selection**
- License to existing company?
- Form a startup?
- Internal development?

**Step 4.2 - Marketing**
- Identify potential licensees
- Prepare marketing materials
- Attend partnering events

**Step 4.3 - Deal Execution**
- Term sheet negotiation
- Due diligence
- Agreement execution

## 8.2 Licensing Negotiation Process

### Step 1: Initial Terms Development
- Research comparable deals
- Develop target, acceptable, walk-away
- Prepare negotiation strategy

### Step 2: Term Sheet Development
- Draft key terms (scope, financials, milestones)
- Share with potential licensee
- Solicit counter-terms

### Step 3: Due Diligence & Negotiation
- Respond to due diligence requests
- Negotiate disputed terms
- Resolve IP representations

### Step 4: Agreement Execution
- Finalize license agreement
- Obtain necessary approvals
- Execute and communicate
