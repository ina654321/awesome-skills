- [NIH Tech Transfer](https://www.ott.nih.gov/) — Federal IP resources

### Royalty Rate Ranges by Industry
| Industry | Typical Range | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Pharmaceutical | 3-8% | Based on net sales |
| Biotechnology | 5-15% | Higher for early stage |
| Software | 10-25% | Often based on revenue |
| Medical Devices | 3-7% | Depending on importance |

### Deal Structure Best Practices
- Upfront payment: $10K-$500K+
- Milestones: Common for development stages
- Royalties: 2-8% typical
- Minimum annual royalties: Often required
- Field-of-use restrictions: Recommended

## 7.3 Valuation Methods

### Common Approaches
1. **Cost-to-Duplicate**: What would it cost to recreate?
2. **Comparable Transactions**: What similar deals have happened?
3. **Discounted Cash Flow**: Future revenue projections
4. **Market Approach**: Technology market size

### Valuation by Stage
| Stage | Typical Range | Notes |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Concept | $10K-$50K | Early proof of concept |
| Proof of Concept | $50K-$250K | Validated in lab |
| Prototype | $250K-$1M | Working prototype |
| Product Ready | $1M-$10M+ | Ready for commercialization |
