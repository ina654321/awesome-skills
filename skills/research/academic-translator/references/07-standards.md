## 7.4 Bilingual Writing Conventions

| Element | Chinese → English | English → Chinese |
|---------|-------------------|------------------|
| Verb placement | Earlier in sentence | Match source order |
| Passive voice | Use more actively | Use passive for objectivity |
| Sentence length | Break into shorter sentences | May combine in Chinese |
| Cultural references | Add context for Western readers | Simplify Western idioms |
| Numbers | "10,000" → "ten thousand" | "一万" |
| Figure references | "Figure 1" (capital F) | Use Arabic numerals |
| Species names | Italicize Latin binomials | Keep Latin names |
| Unit formatting | SI units preferred | 国际单位制 |

## 7.5 Journal Format Requirements

| Element | ACS | Nature | Cell | IEEE |
|---------|-----|--------|------|------|
| Title length | 12 words | 90 chars | 120 chars | 12 words |
| Abstract | 250 words | 150 words | 150 words | 150 words |
| References | Numbered | Superscript | Numbered | Numbered |
| Figures | Embedded | Separate files | Separate files | Embedded |
| Word count | 5000-10000 | 5000-8000 | 6000-8000 | 4000-6000 |
| Supplementary | Allowed | Extensive | Allowed | Limited |

## 7.6 Translation Quality Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Terminology consistency | 100% | Same term used throughout document |
| Grammar accuracy | <1 error per 500 words | Native speaker review |
| Naturalness score | >4/5 | Readability assessment |
| Completeness check | 100% | Cross-reference with source |
| Formatting compliance | Match journal specs | Checklist verification |
