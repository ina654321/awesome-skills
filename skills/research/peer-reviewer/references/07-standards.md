# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Peer Review Guidelines & Ethics

### COPE Guidelines (Committee on Publication Ethics)
- [COPE Core Practices](https://publicationethics.org/core-practices) — Ethical guidelines for peer review
- [COPE Guidelines for Peer Reviewers](https://publicationethics.org/guidelines) — Best practices for manuscript evaluation
- [ flowcharts for handling publication ethics issues](https://publicationethics.org/flowcharts) — Decision trees for reviewers

### ICMJE Recommendations
- [International Committee of Medical Journal Editors](http://www.icmje.org/) — Author responsibilities and peer review standards
- [ICMJE Disclosure Form](http://www.icmje.org/disclosure-of-interest/) — Conflict of interest declaration requirements

### Journal-Specific Standards
- [Elsevier Reviewer Guidelines](https://www.elsevier.com/reviewers) — Publisher-specific peer review expectations
- [Springer Peer Reviewer Resources](https://www.springer.com/gp/authors-editors/peer-review) — Best practices and templates
- [Wiley Peer Review Guide](https://hub.wiley.com/community/wileyresearch/peers) — Reviewer training materials

## 7.2 Manuscript Evaluation Checklists

### STROBE Checklist (Observational Studies)
- [STROBE Statement](https://www.strobe-statement.org/) — Strengthening observational studies in epidemiology
- Use for: Cohort studies, case-control studies, cross-sectional studies

### CONSORT Checklist (Clinical Trials)
- [CONSORT 2010](http://www.consort-statement.org/) — Consolidated standards for reporting trials
- Use for: Randomized controlled trials

### PRISMA Checklist (Systematic Reviews)
- [PRISMA Statement](http://www.prisma-statement.org/) — Preferred reporting items for systematic reviews
- Use for: Meta-analyses and systematic reviews

### ARRIVE Guidelines (Animal Research)
- [ARRIVE 2.0](https://arriveguidelines.org/) — Animal research reporting in experiments
