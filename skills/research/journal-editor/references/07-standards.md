# Journal Editor Reference Standards

Standards and ethical guidelines for scientific journal associate editors and editorial roles.

## Publication Ethics Frameworks

- **Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE)**: Flowcharts and guidelines for handling publication ethics cases — authorship disputes, peer review manipulation, data fabrication, redundant publication. [cope.org](https://cope.org)
- **International Committee of Medical Journal Editors (ICMJE)**: Uniform Requirements for Manuscripts Submitted to Biomedical Journals; authorship criteria, peer review standards, conflict of interest management. [icmje.org](https://icmje.org)
- **World Association of Medical Editors (WAME)**: Best practices for peer review, editorial independence, and journal governance. [wame.org](https://wame.org)
- **Council of Science Editors (CSE)**: White Paper on Promoting Integrity in Scientific Journal Publications. [cses members.org](https://cses members.org)
- **ICMJE Conflict of Interest Form**: Standardized disclosure form for authors; adopted by many journals. [icmje.org](https://icmje.org)

## Peer Review Standards

- **Peer Review Standards**: Minimum standards for peer review conduct, confidentiality, and reviewer responsibilities.
- **Reviewer Recognition**: Programs including ORCID for reviewer credit, Publons (Clarivate) for review verification. [orcid.org](https://orcid.org)
- **Double-Blind vs. Single-Blind Review**: ICMJE and COPE provide guidance on appropriate blinding models.
- **Pre-Publication Registries**: Clinical trial registration (ClinicalTrials.gov, WHO ICTRP) required for clinical research.

## Journal Metrics & Performance

- **Journal Impact Factor (Clarivate)**: Use alongside other metrics; COPE warns against over-reliance. [clarivate.com](https://clarivate.com)
- **Scopus CiteScore**: Elsevier metric for journal performance. [scopus.com](https://scopus.com)
- **Altmetric**: Tracking online attention (news, social media, policy) for published articles. [altmetric.com](https://altmetric.com)

## Industry & Technical Standards

- **Crossref**: DOI assignment, Crossmark for article updates, funder metadata, and ORCID integration. [crossref.org](https://crossref.org)
- **NISO RP-8**: Recommended practices for journal article versioning. [niso.org](https://niso.org)
- **JATS (Journal Article Tag Suite)**: XML standard for scientific article markup; NLM/NCBI. [jats](https://jats)
- **FAIR Data Principles**: Data should be Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable; increasingly required by funders and journals. [go-fair.org](https://go-fair.org)
- **ORCID**: Unique researcher identifier; reduces author disambiguation; supported by major publishers. [orcid.org](https://orcid.org)

## Open Access & Copyright

- **Creative Commons (CC) Licenses**: CC-BY, CC-BY-NC, CC-BY-ND, CC-BY-SA, CC0; governs article reuse. [creativecommons.org](https://creativecommons.org)
- **Plan S / cOAlition S**: Mandates immediate open access for publicly funded research; requires CC-BY licensing. [cograr](https://cograr)
- **Sherpa/RoMEO**: Publisher copyright policies and self-archiving rights. [sherpa.ac.uk](https://sherpa.ac.uk)
- **DOAJ**: Criteria for open access journal indexing; requires APC transparency. [doaj.org](https://doaj.org)
- **NIH Public Access Policy**: Public Law 111-148; requires NIH-funded articles to be deposited in PMC within 12 months. [nih.gov](https://nih.gov)

## Research Integrity

- **Office of Research Integrity (ORI)**: HHS oversight of research integrity in funded research; handles misconduct allegations. [ori.hhs.gov](https://ori.hhs.gov)
- **Retraction Guidelines (COPE)**: Standards for issuing corrections, expressions of concern, and retractions. [cope.org](https://cope.org)
- **Image Integrity**: Nature Publishing Group and Journal of Cell Biology standards for image manipulation detection.
- **Reproducibility**: NIH rigor and reproducibility requirements (NOT-OD-15-103); RRID (Research Resource Identifiers) for antibodies, cell lines, and model organisms. [scicrunch.org](https://scicrunch.org)

## Specialized Standards

- **CONSORT Statement**: Reporting guidelines for randomized controlled trials. [consort-statement.org](https://consort-statement.org)
- **STROBE Statement**: Reporting guidelines for observational studies (cohort, case-control, cross-sectional). [strobe-statement.org](https://strobe-statement.org)
- **PRISMA Statement**: Reporting guidelines for systematic reviews and meta-analyses. [prisma-statement.org](https://prisma-statement.org)
- **ARRIVE Guidelines**: Reporting guidelines for animal research. [nc3rs.org.uk](https://nc3rs.org.uk)
- **EQUATOR Network**: Comprehensive database of reporting guidelines for health research. [equator-network.org](https://equator-network.org)
