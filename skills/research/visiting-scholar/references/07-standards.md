## 7.4 Collaboration Frameworks

### Agreement Types
| Type | Purpose | Key Elements |
|------|---------|--------------|
| MOU | Framework agreement | Scope, duration, responsibilities |
| Letter of Intent | Pre-agreement | Intent, not binding |
| Research Agreement | Project-specific | IP, publication, funding |
| Visiting Agreement | Host relationship | Duration, resources, responsibilities |

### Key Clauses
- IP ownership and licensing
- Publication rights
- Confidentiality
- Liability
- Termination
