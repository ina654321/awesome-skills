# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Fellowship Application Process

### Phase 1: Research & Match Discovery (8-12 months before deadline)

**Step 1.1 - Identify Target Institutions**
- List 10-15 potential hosts in your field
- Consider: research quality, location, funding, culture

**Step 1.2 - Map Researcher Alignments**
- Review potential hosts' publications
- Identify researchers with similar interests
- Check citation networks for connections

**Step 1.3 - Review Program Requirements**
- Read FOA carefully
- Check eligibility requirements
- Note required documents

**Step 1.4 - Create Shortlist**
- Evaluate fit for each
- Prioritize 5-7 viable options

### Phase 2: Initial Contact (6-8 months before deadline)

**Step 2.1 - Draft Inquiry Email**
- Brief research proposal
- Why this host?
- What you can contribute

**Step 2.2 - Request Conversation**
- Ask for 15-30 minute call
- Discuss mutual fit
- Gauge interest

**Step 2.3 - Align Expectations**
- Discuss resources available
- Confirm mutual interest
- Timeline

**Step 2.4 - Secure Host Commitment**
- Get verbal commitment
- Request formal letter

### Phase 3: Formal Application (4-6 months before deadline)

**Step 3.1 - Complete Application**
- Fill out forms
- Write research proposal
- Get required documents

**Step 3.2 - Host Letter**
- Request formal invitation
- Provide supporting materials

**Step 3.3 - Internal Review**
- Have colleagues review
- Check for compliance

**Step 3.4 - Submit**
- Submit before deadline
- Confirm receipt

### Phase 4: Visa & Logistics (2-4 months before departure)

**Step 4.1 - Receive Acceptance**
- Confirm with host
- Begin visa process

**Step 4.2 - Visa Processing**
- J-1: DS-2019 from host
- Medical exam if required
- Schedule interview

**Step 4.3 - Logistics**
- Housing arrangements
- Insurance
- Workspace setup
- Bank, phone, etc.

## 8.2 Host Institution Integration

### First Week: Observation & Relationship Building
- Attend departmental seminars
- Identify key players
- Schedule 1:1 meetings
- Understand informal hierarchies

### Months 1-2: Research Foundation
- Set up lab/office access
- Present home institution research
- Identify potential collaborations
- Get necessary credentials

### Months 3-4: Active Collaboration
- Launch joint research project
- Begin co-supervision if applicable
- Present preliminary results
- Build publication plans

### Final Month: Transition & Legacy
- Document all ongoing work
- Establish follow-up mechanisms
- Write thank-you notes
- Acknowledge all contributors
