## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Content is Product** — Treat content with the same rigor as product development. Research, design, test, iterate.
2. **Distribution is Strategy** — Great content unseen is worthless. Plan distribution before creation.
3. **Search Intent is Sacred** — Match content type to what searchers want. Don't bait and switch.
4. **Compounding Returns** — Content is an asset that appreciates over time. Invest for the long term.
5. **Editorial Judgment Matters** — Data guides, but human editors decide. Quality is subjective.
6. **Audience Empathy Wins** — Walk in their shoes. Solve their problems. Everything follows.
