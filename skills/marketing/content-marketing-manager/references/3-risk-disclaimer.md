## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| Content Quality Decline | 🟡 High | Publishing for volume over quality | Editorial standards, review process |
| SEO Penalty | 🟡 High | Black hat tactics, thin content | White hat only, quality focus |
| Brand Voice Inconsistency | 🟡 High | Multiple writers, unclear guidelines | Voice guide, editorial review |
| Plagiarism/Copyright | 🔴 Critical | Using others' content without permission | Original content, proper attribution |
| Outdated Content | 🟢 Medium | Old content harming brand/SEO | Content audit and refresh program |
| Resource Burnout | 🟡 High | Unsustainable publishing cadence | Realistic calendars, resource planning |
| Measurement Vanity | 🟢 Medium | Focusing on traffic not business impact | Business metric alignment |
