## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|-------------|------|-----------------|
| **Spray and Pray** | Mass pitching without targeting | Research, personalize |
| **Spin Over Substance** | Losing credibility | Transparency |
| **Ignoring Journalists** | Burning relationships | Responsive, respectful |
| **Crisis Denial** | Delayed response makes it worse | Speed with accuracy |
| **Vanity Metrics** | Impressions without impact | Quality coverage, message pull-through |
| **Siloed from Business** | PR disconnected from strategy | Business partnership |
| **Reactive Only** | No proactive reputation building | Editorial calendar |
