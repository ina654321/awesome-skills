# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Professional Standards

### Certifications & Licensing
- **Pet Funeral Director Certification**: State-specific licensing requirements
- **Crematory Operator Certification**: Training in cremation equipment and procedures
- **Grief Counseling Certificate**: Pet loss support training (ASPCA, AAHA resources)

### Industry Organizations
- **International Association of Pet Cemeteries and Crematories (IAEPCC)**: iaepcc.com
- **Association of Pet Loss Professionals**: Support and certification for pet loss workers
- **American Pet Products Association (APPA)**: Industry trends and standards

## 7.2 Service Options

### Cremation Types
| Service | Description | Typical Return |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Private Cremation** | Pet cremated alone; ashes returned inurned | 100% of ashes returned |
| **Partitioned Cremation** | Cremated with other pets; ashes separated | Partial ashes returned |
| **Communal Cremation** | Cremated with other pets; ashes not returned | Memorial only |
| **Aquamation (Alkaline Hydrolysis)** | Water-based process; eco-friendly alternative | 100% ashes returned |

### Memorial Products
- Urns (biodegradable, metal, wooden, ceramic)
- Keepsake jewelry (holds small portion of ashes)
- Paw print impressions (ink or clay)
- Memorial diamonds (cremated remains turned to diamond)
- Fur clippings or lockets
- Custom memorial portraits
- Plush toys with embedded fur

## 7.3 Aftercare Procedures

### Standard Handling Protocol
1. **Transfer**: Transport remains with dignity (vehicle prepared, sanitized)
2. **Identification**: Track throughout process with secure tagging system
3. **Preparation**: Gentle cleaning, positioning for viewing if requested
4. **Storage**: Temperature-controlled environment until disposition
5. **Documentation**: Record chain of custody; photos of pet with family

### Cremation Process
- **Loading**: Individual trays for private; documented sequence
- **Processing**: Proper handling of remains after cremation
- **Ash Preparation**: Removing metal debris; grinding to uniform consistency
- **Packaging**: Sealed container with identification; verification by family
