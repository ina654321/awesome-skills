# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Professional Certifications

### Core Credentials
- **CPCC (Certified Professional Career Coach)**: ICF's flagship certification — 60+ hours training required
- **PCC (Professional Certified Coach)**: ICF credential requiring 500+ coaching hours
- **MCC (Master Certified Coach)**: ICF's highest level — 2,500+ hours experience
- **Certified Resume Writer (CRW)**: Resume Writing Institute certification

### Specializations
- **Executive Coaching**: CEC (Certified Executive Coach) through International Coach Federation
- **Career Transition**: Certified Career Management Coach (CCMC)
- **Leadership Development**: Marshall Goldsmith Stakeholder Centered Coaching

## 7.3 Coaching Methodologies

### Career Construction Theory (Savickas)
- Vocational personality: interests, values, capacities
- Life story: narrative identity and career meaning
- Career adaptability: readiness to navigate transitions

### GROW Model (Whitmore)
- **G**oal: What do you want to achieve?
- **R**eality: Where are you now?
- **O**ptions: What could you do?
- **W**ill: What will you do?

### Values-Based Career Framework
1. Identify core values through card sort exercises
2. Map current role against values alignment
3. Explore alternatives that better align with values
4. Create action plan for transition

## 7.4 Resume Standards

### ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Optimization
- Use standard headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
- Avoid tables, graphics, headers/footers in body text
- Include keywords from job description naturally
- Match job title terminology where accurate

### Resume Sections
- Professional summary (3-5 sentences, value proposition)
- Core competencies/skills (keyword-rich)
- Professional experience (reverse chronological)
- Education and certifications
- Optional: Awards, publications, volunteer leadership

### Recommended Length
- Entry-level: 1 page
- Mid-career: 1-2 pages
- Executive: 2 pages maximum
- Federal/Military: Can exceed with specific requirements
