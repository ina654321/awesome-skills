# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Initial Discovery Session

### Pre-Session Preparation
- Client completes intake form: current role, industry, target roles, challenges
- Review client's current resume (if provided)
- Research client's target industry/companies if known

### Session Structure (60-90 minutes)
1. **Rapport Building** (5-10 min): Establish trust, confirm confidentiality
2. **Deep Discovery** (25-30 min): Explore values, strengths, motivations using Socratic questioning
3. **Gap Analysis** (15-20 min): Identify skills, experience gaps between current and target
4. **Options Exploration** (15 min): Brainstorm potential paths, validate feasibility
5. **Next Steps** (5-10 min): Commit to specific action items with deadlines

## 8.2 Career Transition Framework

### Phase 1: Self-Assessment (2-4 weeks)
- Values identification exercise (card sort or wheel of life)
- Skills inventory (transferable skills analysis)
- Strengths assessment (strengths finder, VIA character strengths)
- Career narrative: What story does client tell about their career?

### Phase 2: Market Exploration (4-8 weeks)
- Target role research: responsibilities, requirements, salary bands
- Informational interviews (10-15 minimum in target industry)
- Skills gap identification and learning plan
- Network mapping: Who do you know? Who do they know?

### Phase 3: Positioning (4-8 weeks)
- Resume transformation: Repackage experience for new direction
- LinkedIn profile optimization
- Personal brand development: "elevator pitch" for new direction
- Portfolio or work samples if relevant to new field

### Phase 4: Active Job Search (Ongoing)
- Application strategy: Quality over quantity (target 10-15 high-quality apps/week)
- Interview preparation: STAR method, stories, questions to ask
- Negotiation preparation: Know your worth, have alternatives

## 8.3 Interview Coaching Workflow

### Pre-Interview Prep
- Company research: culture, recent news, products, leadership
- Role research: job description, team structure, challenges
- Story development: 5-7 success stories covering different competencies
- Mock interview: Practice out loud

### Interview Types & Strategies
- **Phone Screen**: 20-30 min; focus on fit and basic qualifications
- **Hiring Manager**: 45-60 min; behavioral questions, deeper dive
- **Panel/Team**: Multiple interviewers; prepare for different perspectives
- **Executive**: Strategic conversations; show vision and leadership

### Post-Interview
- Send thank you within 24 hours
- Document what went well and what to improve
- Follow up appropriately (typically 1 week if no response)
- Address any feedback quickly

## 8.4 Coaching Engagement Structure

### Single Session (One-Time)
- Focus on one specific question or challenge
- Provide immediate value and actionable next steps
- Set clear expectations: This is advice, not ongoing coaching

### Package (4-12 sessions)
- Monthly check-ins with progress accountability
- Evolving agenda based on client needs
- Between-session support via email/messaging
- Typically 3-6 month engagement

### Retainer (Ongoing)
- Weekly or bi-weekly calls
- Priority access for urgent career issues
- Strategic planning for career development
- Usually for executives or rapidly growing professionals
