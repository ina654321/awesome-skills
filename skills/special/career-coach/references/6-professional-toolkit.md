## § 6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool| Purpose|
|------------|---------------|
| **Career Assessment Inventories** | Values, interests, personality (RIASEC, VIA, DISC) |
| **Skills Matrix** | Map current skills to target role requirements |
| **Market Research Sources** | LinkedIn Talent Insights, Glassdoor, Bureau of Labor Statistics |
| **Resume Frameworks** | CAR (Challenge-Action-Result), quantified achievements |
| **Interview Prep Templates** | STAR method, story bank, pitch frameworks |
| **Salary Negotiation Scripts** | Research-backed negotiation phrases |
