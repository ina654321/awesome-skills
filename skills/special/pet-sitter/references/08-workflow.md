# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Client Intake Process

### Step 1: Initial Consultation
- Meet pet and owner in person
- Gather pet information: name, species, breed, age, weight
- Document medical conditions and medications
- Note behavioral quirks, fears, triggers
- Discuss feeding schedule and dietary needs

### Step 2: Home Assessment
- Tour home to identify pet's spaces
- Locate supplies: food, medications, leashes, litter
- Confirm emergency vet contact and code if needed
- Set up communication preferences with owner

### Step 3: Documentation
- Complete pet sitting agreement
- Document all pet information in writing
- Record owner's contact information and backup
- Get permission for emergencies (vet authorization)
- Take photos of pet for identification

## 8.2 Daily Care Routine

### Morning Check-In
1. Arrive at scheduled time
2. Greet pet calmly (dogs: leash check; cats: let them come to you)
3. Feed according to schedule (measure food)
4. Fresh water refill
5. Administer medications if needed
6. Quick health check: eyes, nose, energy level

### Mid-Day Care (if applicable)
1. Dog walking: 20-30 minutes
2. Litter box cleaning (cats)
3. Play and enrichment time
4. Medication check if twice-daily

### Evening Check-Out
1. Final feeding
2. Fresh water
3. Play/exercise
4. Evening medication if needed
5. Secure home (lights, doors, windows)
6. Leave notes for owner if any concerns

## 8.3 Dog Walking Protocol

### Before Walk
- Check leash and collar/harness condition
- Verify dog is comfortable with gear
- Bring water and treats
- Note weather conditions

### During Walk
- Maintain control at all times
- Clean up after pet immediately
- Watch for signs of distress (panting, limping)
- Avoid aggressive dogs or hazards
- Keep to usual route if dog is anxious with changes

### After Walk
- Check paws for debris, cuts, or irritation
- Offer water
- Reward calm behavior
- Secure pet back in home

## 8.4 End-of-Assignment Handoff

### Final Day
- Complete final feeding and medication
- Clean and restock as needed
- Leave home in clean condition
- Take final photos if documenting
- Write detailed notes for owner
- Review departure checklist with owner

### Owner Return Meeting
- Walk through what happened
- Highlight any concerns or discoveries
- Answer questions
- Provide written daily logs
- Discuss next booking if applicable
