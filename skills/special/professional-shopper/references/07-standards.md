# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Sourcing Channels

### Primary Retail Channels
| Channel | Best For | Considerations |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| **Brand Direct** | Authentic products, warranty | Usually highest price |
| **Authorized Dealers** | Authenticated resale, warranty | Verify authorization |
| **Department Stores** | Wide selection, return policies | May lack expertise |
| **Outlet Stores** | Discounted authentic goods | Inventory varies |
| **Warehouse Clubs** | Bulk savings, some exclusives | Membership required |

### Secondary Markets
- **eBay**: Wide selection; authentication services recommended
- **Poshmark/Mercari**: Consumer resale; authenticity verification important
- **StockX/GOAT**: Sneaker/authentication focused; verified by platform
- **The RealReal/Fashionphile**: Luxury authentication; verification included
- **Facebook Marketplace**: Local deals; high risk; meet safely

### International Sourcing
- **Japan**: Electronics, anime merch, fashion
- **UK**: British brands, vintage, certain collectibles
- **Europe**: Luxury goods, design items
- **China**: Electronics, bulk manufacturing, replicas (verify authenticity)
- **South Korea**: K-beauty, K-pop merchandise

## 7.2 Authentication Standards

### Luxury Handbags
- Check serial number/location (brand-specific)
- Verify stitching quality and pattern
- Examine hardware (zippers, clasps, engravings)
- Compare logos and typography to authentic
- Professional authentication recommended for high-value items

### Watches
- Verify serial number with manufacturer
- Check movement (automatic vs quartz markers)
- Examine caseback engravings
- Verify box and papers consistency
- Use professional authentication for $1000+

### Sneakers
- Check tag format and placement (year/model specific)
- Verify sole construction and materials
- Compare to known authentic pairs
- Use StockX/GOAT verification when buying resale

## 7.3 Price Research Standards

### Price Comparison Framework
1. Identify MSRP (manufacturer suggested retail price)
2. Research current market price (3+ sources)
3. Consider seasonal patterns (Black Friday, end of season)
4. Factor in authenticity risk premium
5. Include shipping/import costs for cross-border

### Price Tracking Tools
- **CamelCamelCamel**: Amazon price history
- **Keepa**: Amazon price tracking
- **Honey**: Browser extension for discounts
- **Slickdeals**: Community deal sharing

## 7.4 Negotiation Strategies

### When to Negotiate
- **Always acceptable**: Flea markets, Craigslist, some small businesses
- **Sometimes possible**: Boutique stores, independent retailers
- **Never negotiate**: Brand stores, clearance items already marked down

### Negotiation Approaches
- Research comparable pricing beforehand
- Ask about "best price" or "final offer"
- Bundle multiple items for discount
- Build rapport before discussing price
- Know your walk-away price before starting
