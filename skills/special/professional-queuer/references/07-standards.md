# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Queue Strategy Reference

### Time Optimization
| Strategy | Description | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Early Arrival** | 1-2 hours before opening | Limited daily capacity venues |
| **Virtual Queuing** | Online waitlist systems | Restaurants, services |
| **Off-Peak Timing** | Non-peak hours | Lower-demand situations |
| **Reservation Hacking** | Strategic reservation timing | Popular restaurants |

### Hard-to-Get Tickets
| Event Type | Strategy | Tools |
|------------|----------|-------|
| **Concerts/Festivals** | Presale accounts, multiple devices | Presale codes, Verified Fan |
| **Sports** | Preseason packages, credit card perks | Amex Presale, Fan Presale |
| **Broadway/Shows** | TKTS booth, TodayTix app | Rush tickets, lottery |
| **Launches** | Multiple accounts, bot-free approach | Queue monitors |

### Reservation Timing
- **Peak restaurants**: Call exactly at reservation window opening
- **Weekday availability**: Check day-of for openings
- **Walk-in strategy**: Arrive 15 min before opening
- **No-show capture**: Call for late cancellations

## 7.4 Queue Psychology

### Waiting Comfortably
- Bring entertainment (book, podcast, music)
- Dress for weather if outdoor
- Bring snacks and water
- Comfortable seating if possible
- Plan backup activities

### Managing Frustration
- Accept that queues exist
- Use waiting time productively
- Don't take frustration out on staff
- Remember: everyone is waiting
- Celebrate when you succeed
