# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 High-Severity Anti-Patterns

### 🔴 Anti-Pattern 1: Paying Scalper Prices
```
❌ BAD: Buying from street scalpers or paying 5x face value
✅ GOOD: Use official resale; wait for lottery/rush; go to different show
```
**Why it matters**: Supports illegal scalping; prices often drop after initial rush; you may get scammed.

### 🔴 Anti-Pattern 2: Not Reading Terms
```
❌ BAD: Buying tickets that are non-transferable or non-refundable
✅ GOOD: Read all details before purchase; understand your rights
```
**Why it matters**: Many "deals" have hidden restrictions; you may not be able to attend or get refund.

### 🔴 Anti-Pattern 3: Missing Presale Window
```
❌ BAD: "I'll buy whenever" — waiting until general sale
✅ GOOD: Register for all presales; presale is your best chance
```
**Why it matters**: For high-demand events, general sale often sells out in minutes; presale is critical.

### 🔴 Anti-Pattern 4: Single Point of Failure
```
❌ BAD: Only trying one method or platform
✅ GOOD: Multiple accounts, platforms, and strategies increase success
```
**Why it matters**: Technology fails; sites crash; having backup options is essential.

## 10.2 Medium-Severity Anti-Patterns

### 🟡 Anti-Pattern 5: Wrong Date/Time
```
❌ BAD: Booked the wrong date, time, or location
✅ GOOD: Triple-check all details before confirming; read confirmation email
```

### 🟡 Anti-Pattern 6: Not Having Payment Ready
```
❌ BAD: Fumbling for credit card while in checkout queue
✅ GOOD: Save payment method in advance; have backup ready
```

### 🟡 Anti-Pattern 7: Ignoring Refund/Flexibility Options
```
❌ BAD: Buying no-refund tickets for uncertain plans
✅ GOOD: Pay slightly more for flexibility if plans aren't certain
```

### 🟡 Anti-Pattern 8: Arriving Late to Physical Queue
```
❌ BAD: Showing up 10 minutes before opening for popular attraction
✅ GOOD: Arrive 1-2 hours early for high-demand in-person experiences
```

## 10.3 Best Practices Checklist

### Before Purchase
- [ ] Research all options and alternatives
- [ ] Know sale times exactly
- [ ] Register for presales if available
- [ ] Have all accounts set up and verified
- [ ] Payment method ready and authorized

### During Purchase
- [ ] Sign in early
- [ ] Stay on page once in queue
- [ ] Have backup options ready
- [ ] Complete transaction quickly
- [ ] Confirm immediately after

### After Purchase
- [ ] Verify details immediately
- [ ] Check email confirmations
- [ ] Note refund/transfer policies
- [ ] Add to calendar with reminders
- [ ] Note any pre-event requirements

## 10.4 Red Flags to Watch For

| Red Flag | Risk Level | Action |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Price >3x face value | High | Wait for official resale or alternatives |
| "Guaranteed" ticket offers | High | Legitimate sellers don't guarantee sold-out tickets |
| Non-transferable restrictions | Medium | Only buy if you're certain you can attend |
| Third-party site with poor reviews | High | Use official platforms only |
| "One more chance" pressure tactics | Medium | Don't rush; there are always alternatives |
