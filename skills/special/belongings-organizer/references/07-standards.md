# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Professional Standards

### Certification & Credentials
- **Certified Estate Organizer (C EO)**: Primary certification through the Estate Organizer Professional Association (EOPA)
- **Certified Senior Move Manager (SSM)**: National Association of Senior Move Managers (NASMM)
- **Certificate in Grief Counseling**: Recommended for handling bereavement situations

### Professional Ethics
- Maintain strict confidentiality regarding estate contents and family matters
- Never make decisions for families — provide frameworks and guidance
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest (e.g., being a family friend)
- Follow state laws regarding executor responsibilities and beneficiary rights

## 7.2 Industry Resources

- **Digital Asset Laws**: Revised Uniform Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets Act (UFADAA)

## 7.3 Valuation Standards

### Item Categorization Framework

| Category | Description | Action |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| **High Value** | Items over $500 individually, jewelry, art, collectibles | Professional appraisal required |
| **Moderate Value** | $100-500 range, electronics, quality furniture | Family assessment with research |
| **Sentimental** | Low financial but high emotional value | Family discussion, photo documentation |
| **Practical** | Usable household items | Donation, sale, or family distribution |
| **Discard** | Worn, broken, or outdated items | Responsible disposal/recycling |

### Appraisal Resources
- **Antique Appraisal Association**: antiqueappraisers.org — certified appraisers directory
- **ASCLA (American Society of Appraisers)**: appraisers.org — specialty appraisers
- **Auction Houses**: Christie's, Sotheby's for high-value estates; local auctioneers for moderate value

## 7.4 Documentation Standards

### Estate Inventory Checklist
- Complete photographic documentation of all items
- Written inventory with estimated values (family consensus or professional)
- Categorization by disposition path (keep, sell, donate, discard)
- Signed acknowledgment from all decision-making family members

### Digital Asset Inventory Template
- Financial accounts (banking, investment, retirement)
- Email and social media accounts
- Subscription services and recurring payments
- Digital photos and cloud storage
- Cryptocurrency and online payment accounts (PayPal, Venmo)
- Loyalty programs and reward points
