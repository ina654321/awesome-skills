# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Initial Assessment

### Step 1: Client Consultation
- Identify primary concerns (wealth, relationships, career, health)
- Understand timeline and budget for remedies
- Discuss client birth data (BaZi if relevant for calculations)
- Note any major life changes or upcoming events

### Step 2: Space Analysis
- Request floor plans with compass directions
- Identify building period (for Flying Star calculations)
- Note external environment (buildings, roads, water near home)
- Assess overall layout and flow patterns

### Step 3: Site Visit (if possible)
- Take compass readings for each room
- Observe natural light and ventilation
- Note current furniture placement
- Identify clutter, dead plants, or stagnant areas

## 8.2 Analysis Phase

### Step 4: Calculate Flying Star Chart
- Determine building period (year built)
- Calculate Flying Star chart for current period
- Identify afflicted stars in each sector
- Map to life areas and affected inhabitants

### Step 5: Apply Ba Zhai
- Determine facing direction and center Gua
- Analyze which sectors favor occupants
- Identify clashes between person and space
- Map life aspirations to house sectors

### Step 6: Synthesize Findings
- Cross-reference Flying Star and Ba Zhai
- Identify highest-priority problem areas
- Consider occupant-specific remedies
- Balance urgency with practical implementation

## 8.3 Recommendations Phase

### Step 7: Prioritized Remedies
- Start with highest-impact, lowest-cost adjustments
- Focus on command position and center first
- Recommend element-specific remedies as needed
- Consider timing for major changes (Date Selection)

### Step 8: Implementation Plan
- Categorize: immediate (do today), short-term (this month), long-term (renovation)
- Budget considerations for each remedy
- Suggest phased approach for larger spaces
- Provide clear rationale for each recommendation

## 8.4 Follow-Up

### Step 9: Review
- Follow up after 30-60 days to assess impact
- Make adjustments based on results
- Address any new concerns that emerge
- Refine recommendations as space evolves

### Common Immediate Actions
1. Clear clutter immediately — blocks qi
2. Fix broken items — represents obstacles
3. Add live plants to stagnant areas
4. Improve lighting in dark corners
5. Reposition bed to command position
