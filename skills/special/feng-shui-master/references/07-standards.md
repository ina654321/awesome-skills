# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Feng Shui Schools & Traditions

### Major Schools
| School | Focus | Best Application |
|--------|-------|------------------|
| **San He (Three Combinations)** | Landform, water, and directions | Site selection, external environment |
| **San Yuan (Three Cycles)** | Time and Flying Star calculations | Period-based analysis |
| **Ba Zhai (Eight Houses)** | Eight mansions, life sectors | Residential, home layout |
| **Xuan Kong Fei Xing** | Flying Star, period calculations | Building age, timing |
| **Eight Aspirations** | Life goals mapped to sectors | Goal-oriented enhancements |

### Certification Standards
- **Certified Feng Shui Practitioner**: International Feng Shui Association (IFSA)
- **BaZi/Feng Shui Master**: Various lineage-based certifications
- **Classical Feng Shui certifications**: Via specific lineages (BTB, Master Yap)

### Five Elements Cycle
- **Wood** feeds **Fire**
- **Fire** creates **Earth** (ash)
- **Earth** bears **Metal**
- **Metal** collects **Water**
- **Water** nourishes **Wood**

## 7.3 Core Principles

### Guiding Rules
1. **Qi Must Flow**: Smooth, gentle flow of energy throughout space
2. **Balance**: Yin/Yang, Five Elements in harmony
3. **Center is Sacred**: Keep the heart of the home/office clear
4. **Command Position**: Important items face the main entrance
5. **Bed Position**: Should see door but not be in direct line of sight

### Common Remedies
- **Mirrors**: Redirect and expand qi, but avoid reflecting bed or door
- **Plants**: Alive and thriving; dead plants bring negative energy
- **Water Features**: Flowing water for wealth, but placement critical
- **Crystals**: Specific types for specific purposes
- **Colors**: Each element has associated colors
