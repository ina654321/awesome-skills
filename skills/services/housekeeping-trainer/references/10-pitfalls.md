# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Critical Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Consequence | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Mixing bleach and ammonia | 🔴 Critical | Toxic gas, death | Chemical safety training |
| 2 | No PPE when required | 🔴 Critical | Chemical burns, illness | Mandatory PPE policy |
| 3 | Leaving wet floors unmarked | 🔴 High | Slip and fall injuries | Wet floor signage always |
| 4 | Improper chemical storage | 🔴 High | Fire, chemical exposure | Follow MSDS guidelines |
| 5 | Blocking emergency exits | 🔴 Critical | Code violation, injury | Maintain clear egress |

## 10.2 Cross-Contamination Risks

| Mistake | Result | Proper Technique |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| Same cloth for toilet and sink | Pathogen spread | Color-coded microfiber |
| Not changing gloves | Cross-contamination | Change between areas |
| Reusing dirty water | Surface recontamination | Fresh solution for each area |
| Skipping hand contact surfaces | Infection spread | Follow sequence strictly |

## 10.3 Ineffective Cleaning Practices

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Correct Approach |
|--------------|---------|------------------|
| Spray and wipe immediately | No dwell time | Allow contact time per product |
| Using dirty equipment | Spreads germs | Regular equipment sanitization |
| Over-diluting chemicals | Ineffective cleaning | Follow dilution ratios exactly |
| Ignoring high-touch areas | Infection risk | Prioritize high-touch surfaces |

## 10.4 Training Errors

| Mistake | Impact | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| No documented procedures | Inconsistent quality | Create SOPs for all tasks |
| No refresher training | Skills decay | Quarterly competency reviews |
| Skipping safety training | Injury risk | Mandatory safety orientation |
| No cross-training | Staffing gaps | Multi-task training program |

## 10.5 Communication Failures

| Error | Result | Fix |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Not reporting damages | Liability issues | Document and report immediately |
| Skipping customer notification | Trust damage | Communicate all issues |
| Improper shift handoff | Missed tasks | Standardized handoff process |
| No feedback loop | No improvement | Regular team meetings |

## 10.6 Equipment Mistakes

| Issue | Risk | Protocol |
|-------|------|----------|
| Using wrong cleaner on surface | Damage | Check manufacturer guidelines |
| Improper vacuum maintenance | Performance loss | Empty bag/canister regularly |
| Damaged mops and cloths | Poor results | Replace when worn |
| Skipping equipment inspection | Breakdowns | Pre-use equipment checks |

## 10.7 Quality Control Failures

- [ ] Skipping final inspection
- [ ] No cleaning verification
- [ ] Incomplete checklists
- [ ] Not documenting special requests
- [ ] Ignoring customer feedback
- [ ] No follow-up on issues

## 10.8 Time Management Errors

| Mistake | Result | Better Approach |
|---------|--------|-----------------|
| Rushing through tasks | Poor quality | Follow time standards |
| Poor route planning | Inefficiency | Organize by zone |
| Improper supply management | Wasted time | Stock cart properly |
| Multi-tasking incompatible tasks | Errors | Sequential task completion |

## 10.9 Environmental Compliance Violations

- **Improper chemical disposal** - EPA violations, fines
- **Excessive water use** - Waste, higher costs
- **Unnecessary single-use plastics** - Environmental impact
- **Improper recycling** - Contamination issues
- **Chemical overuse** - Health and environmental risks

## 10.10 Staff Management Pitfalls

- Assigning tasks without clear instructions
- No accountability for quality
- Failure to recognize good performance
- Inadequate break coverage
- Poor scheduling leading to fatigue
- Ignoring staff concerns and safety
