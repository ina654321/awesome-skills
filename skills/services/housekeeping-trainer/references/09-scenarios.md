# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Hotel Turndown Service

**Customer:** "We need to implement a turndown service for our boutique hotel. Can you develop a training program?"

**Expert:**
> **Assessment:**
> 
> Turndown service is a premium hospitality offering:
> 
> 1. **Service Scope:**
>    - Evening refresh of guest rooms
>    - Bed preparation for sleep
>    - Minor tidying and refreshments
> 2. **Timing:** Typically 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM
> 3. **Guest Experience:** Should be seamless, non-intrusive
> 
> **Training Program Components:**
> - Knock protocol (2 knocks, 5-second wait)
> - Visual cues for "do not disturb"
> - Refresh amenities and turn down beds
> - Evening turndown treats placement
> - Bathroom refresh and robes laid out
> 
> **Quality Metrics:**
> - Average service time: 10-15 minutes
> - Guest satisfaction scores
> - Repeat service requests tracking

## 9.2 Post-Construction Cleaning

**Customer:** "We just finished renovating a 5000 sq ft office space. We need a complete post-construction clean."

**Expert:**
> **Assessment:**
> 
> Post-construction cleaning requires specialized procedures:
> 
> 1. **Phase 1 - Initial Clean:**
>    - Remove large debris
>    - Vacuum and sweep all areas
>    - Remove stickers and labels
> 2. **Phase 2 - Deep Clean:**
>    - Clean all surfaces (walls, ceilings)
>    - Remove paint overspray
>    - Clean windows and frames
> 3. **Phase 3 - Final Polish:**
>    - Buff and seal concrete
>    - Clean all fixtures
>    - Final inspection
> 
> **Equipment Needed:**
> - HEPA vacuums (mandatory)
> - Pressure washers for exterior
> - Specialty solvents for construction debris
> - Extension poles for high areas
> 
> **Safety:** Construction dust may contain silica - proper respiratory protection required

## 9.3 Healthcare Facility Biohazard Response

**Customer:** "A patient room needs terminal cleaning after discharge. What are the critical steps?"

**Expert:**
> **Critical Protocol:**
> 
> Terminal cleaning in healthcare is non-negotiable for infection control:
> 
> 1. **PPE Required:**
>    - Gown and gloves (minimum)
>    - N95 mask if airborne pathogens suspected
>    - Face shield for splash risk
> 2. **Sequencing (Top to Bottom):**
>    - High surfaces first
>    - Clean to dirty flow
>    - Bathroom last
> 3. **Special Requirements:**
>    - UV-C disinfection after manual cleaning
>    - Terminal cleaning checklist completion
>    - Sign-off required before next patient
> 
> **Key Areas:**
> - All horizontal surfaces
> - Medical equipment
> - Bed frame and mattress
> - Bathroom including toilet
> - High-touch areas (call lights, remote)

## 9.4 School Cleaning During Pandemic

**Customer:** "How should we modify our school cleaning protocols during flu season?"

**Expert:**
> **Assessment:**
> 
> Schools require modified protocols during illness outbreaks:
> 
> 1. **Increased Frequency:**
>    - Bathrooms: 3-4 times daily
>    - High-touch surfaces: Twice daily
>    - Classrooms: Daily with focus on desks
> 2. **Product Selection:**
>    - EPA-approved disinfectants
>    - Focus on non-toxic for children
>    - Hand sanitizer stations
> 3. **Staff Training:**
>    - Proper PPE use
>    - Contact time awareness
>    - Safe handling procedures
> 
> **Coordination:**
> - Align with custodial schedules
> - Communicate with teachers
> - Flexible response to outbreaks
> 
> **Documentation:**
> - Cleaning logs for each room
> - Supply inventory tracking
> - Staff wellness checks

## 9.5 Move-Out Deep Clean

**Customer:** "We need a move-out cleaning service for a 3-bedroom apartment. What does this include?"

**Expert:**
> **Comprehensive Scope:**
> 
> Move-out cleaning ensures full deposit return:
> 
> 1. **Kitchen Deep Clean:**
>    - Inside/outside appliances
>    - Cabinets (inside and doors)
>    - Countertops and backsplash
>    - Oven and refrigerator (inside)
> 2. **Bathrooms:**
>    - Tile and grout restoration
>    - Caulking repair/replacement
>    - All fixtures polished
>    - Mold and mildew treatment
> 3. **All Rooms:**
>    - Inside closets
>    - Walls (spot cleaning)
>    - Light fixtures
>    - Baseboards and doors
>    - Windows (interior)
> 4. **Floors:**
>    - Carpet deep cleaning or shampooing
>    - Hard floor refinishing if needed
>    - Grout cleaning and sealing
> 
> **Documentation:**
> - Before and after photos
> - Checklist for landlord verification
> - Invoice for service
