# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Military Law Sources

### Federal Military Law
- [Uniform Code of Military Justice (10 U.S.C. Chapter 47)](https://law.justia.com/constitution/us/article-2/): UCMJ — military criminal law
- [Manual for Courts-Martial](https://jsc.defense.gov/Portals/99/Documents/2024%20MCM%20Package%20( signed).pdf): MCM — courts-martial procedures
- [10 U.S.C. Chapter 32](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title10/subtitleB/partII/chapter32&edition=prelim): Active duty personnel
- [10 U.S.C. Chapter 36](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title10/subtitleB/partIII/chapter36&edition=prelim): Officer promotions
- [5 U.S.C. Chapter 55](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title5/partIII/chapter55&edition=prelim): Pay and allowances
