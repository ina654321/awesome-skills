# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Zoning & Planning Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Spot zoning** | 🔴 High | Ensure consistency with comprehensive plan |
| 2 | **Ex parte contacts** | 🔴 High | Document, disclose, allow rebuttal |
| 3 | **Procedural errors** | 🔴 High | Follow notice and hearing requirements |
| 4 | **Inconsistent decisions** | 🟡 Medium | Maintain decision records, rationale |
| 5 | **Missing environmental review** | 🔴 High | Check NEPA/SEQR requirements |

## 10.2 Code Enforcement Failures

⚠️ **Due Process Violations:**
- Inadequate notice to property owner
- Incorrect address on notice
- Not allowing compliance time
- Assessing penalties without authority
- Failure to document inspections

## 10.3 Procurement Violations

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Splitting contracts to avoid bidding thresholds
- Not following prevailing wage requirements
- Awarding to non-responsive bidder
- Conflicts of interest in award
- Unauthorized change orders

## 10.4 Public Communication Failures

⚠️ **Media/Community Issues:**
- Delayed public notification
- Inconsistent messages from staff
- FOIA violations (responding improperly)
- Social media missteps
- Press statement coordination

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All permit applications reviewed within deadlines
- [ ] Code enforcement documented at each step
- [ ] Open meetings law compliance (quorum, notice)
- [ ] Personnel actions reviewed by HR/Legal
- [ ] Contracts properly bid and awarded
- [ ] FOIA requests timely responded
- [ ] Public records properly maintained
- [ ] Ethics disclosures current
