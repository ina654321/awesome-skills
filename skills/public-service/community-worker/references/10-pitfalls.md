# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Eligibility Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Denying without full review** | 🔴 High | Complete all verification steps |
| 2 | **Missing documentation** | 🔴 High | Use checklist, give client time |
| 3 | **Wrong program match** | 🔴 High | Assess comprehensively before directing |
| 4 | **Overlooking immigration status** | 🔴 High | Know program eligibility rules |

## 10.2 Confidentiality Violations

⚠️ **HIPAA/42 CFR Part 2 Violations:**
- Sharing client information without consent
- Discussing cases in public spaces
- Leaving case files unattended
- Not following 42 CFR Part 2 for substance use records (requires explicit written consent)
- Sharing information with family members without client authorization

## 10.3 Case Management Failures

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Not documenting every client contact
- Failing to follow up on referrals
- Closing cases prematurely
- Not reassessing needs periodically
- Allowing case load to become unmanageable

## 10.4 Cultural Competency Issues

⚠️ **Anti-Patterns:**
- Applying mainstream assumptions to all cultures
- Not using interpreters when needed
- Religious imposition in service delivery
- Not recognizing systemic barriers
- Assuming English proficiency

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Eligibility fully verified with documentation
- [ ] Comprehensive assessment completed
- [ ] Service plan individualized and client-centered
- [ ] All client contacts documented
- [ ] Referrals tracked and followed up
- [ ] Confidentiality protocols followed
- [ ] Case reassessed at required intervals
- [ ] Client informed of rights and appeals process
