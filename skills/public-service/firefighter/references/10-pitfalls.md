## 10.1 Public Service Mistakes

| # | Mistake | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Bureaucratic delays** | 🟡 Medium | Set response time standards |

| 3 | **Conflict of interest** | 🔴 High | Recusal policies, disclosure |
| 4 | **Unequal treatment** | 🔴 High | Standardized procedures |
| 5 | **Failure to document** | 🟡 Medium | Audit trail requirements |
