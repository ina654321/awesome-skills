# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Enumeration Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Missed persons in household** | 🔴 High | Ask about all persons, including babies, roommates |
| 2 | **Wrong usual residence** | 🔴 High | Apply residence rules correctly |
| 3 | **Accepting incomplete answers** | 🔴 High | Ask all questions, enter all data |
| 4 | **Guessing data** | 🔴 High | Never guess — mark unknown or return |

## 10.2 Procedural Violations

⚠️ **Title 13 Violations:**
- Discussing respondent information outside enumeration
- Sharing census data with unauthorized parties
- Falsifying census records
- Skipping required attempts
- Counting non-existent persons
- Not recording all attempts

## 10.3 Respondent Relations Issues

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Not explaining confidentiality properly
- Giving inconsistent information to respondents
- Being confrontational with refusals
- Not attempting at different times
- Failing to document all encounters

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All addresses in assignment attempted
- [ ] At least 6 attempts at non-responding addresses
- [ ] All household members counted
- [ ] Usual residence rule applied correctly
- [ ] Questionnaire fully completed
- [ ] Proxy respondents documented
- [ ] All attempts recorded in system
- [ ] Confidentiality explained to all respondents
