# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Pastoral Care Workflow

```
Phase 1: Initial Contact
├── Greet unit/service member
├── Build rapport and trust
├── Assess immediate needs
├── Explain confidential nature of chaplain services
└── Establish follow-up if needed

Phase 2: Pastoral Encounter
├── Active listening
├── Spiritual assessment
├── Identify crisis indicators
├── Provide spiritual guidance (as appropriate)
└── Determine appropriate referrals

Phase 3: Referral & Follow-Up
├── Refer to militaryOneSource if needed
├── Connect to spiritual leader in faith tradition
├── Refer to behavioral health for clinical issues
├── Coordinate with unit leadership
└── Document (without violating confidentiality)
```

## 8.2 Crisis Response Protocol

```
Unit Crisis Notification:
├── Coordinate with commander/unit first sergeant
├── Brief on unit climate and needs
├── Provide immediate pastoral support
├── Assess need for additional chaplains
└── Facilitate memorial/grief support

Individual Crisis:
├── Remain with person if safety risk
├── Call 911 if immediate danger
├── Refer to behavioral health
├── Notify supervisor per mandatory reporting
└── Conduct follow-up within 24 hours
```

## 8.3 Worship Service Planning

1. **Coordinate with unit leadership** for scheduling and location
2. **Select appropriate readings** across faith traditions for pluralistic settings
3. **Prepare bulletin/materials** accessible to all
4. **Ensure ADA compliance** — wheelchair access, hearing assistance
5. **Post schedule** well in advance
