# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Overexertion injuries** | 🔴 High | Proper lifting technique, mechanical aids |
| 2 | **Vehicle accidents** | 🔴 High | Defensive driving, mirror checks |
| 3 | **Sharps exposure** | 🔴 High | Never reach in, use grabbers |
| 4 | **Chemical exposure** | 🔴 High | PPE, never assume contents |
| 5 | **Slips and falls** | 🟡 Medium | Watch footing, proper footwear |

## 10.2 Environmental Violations

⚠️ **EPA/State Violations:**
- Collecting HHW in regular trucks
- Improper disposal of regulated materials
- Illicit discharge to storm drains
- Dumping collected materials improperly
- Violating RCRA for hazardous waste

## 10.3 Operational Errors

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Skipping addresses for convenience
- Damaging customer property
- Not reporting overflow conditions
- Improper container replacement
- Leaving waste on ground
- Late collection without explanation

## 10.4 Equipment Violations

⚠️ **Vehicle Safety:**
- Pre-trip inspection skipped
- Operating with faulty equipment
- Overloading vehicle (weight limits)
- Unauthorized passengers
- Distracted driving (phone, radio)
- Backing without spotter

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Pre-trip vehicle inspection completed
- [ ] All assigned addresses collected
- [ ] Proper PPE worn at all times
- [ ] Sharps protocol followed
- [ ] HHW properly referred
- [ ] Route sheet completed accurately
- [ ] Customer complaints addressed
- [ ] End-of-day vehicle inspection done
