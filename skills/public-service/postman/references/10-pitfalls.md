# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Delivery Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Misdelivered mail** | 🔴 High | Triple-check address before delivery |
| 2 | **Undeliverable not processed** | 🔴 High | RTS all undeliverable mail |
| 3 | **Skipping addresses** | 🔴 High | Follow route sequence exactly |
| 4 | **Wrong Informed Delivery** | 🔴 High | Verify address match before scanning |

## 10.2 Security Violations

⚠️ **Criminal Exposure:**
- Theft of mail contents (18 U.S.C. §1702) — up to 5 years
- Possessing stolen mail (18 U.S.C. §1708) — up to 5 years
- Obstruction of mail (18 U.S.C. §1701) — up to 6 months
- Opening mail not addressed to you (18 U.S.C. §1702)
- Tampering with mail (39 U.S.C. §301)

## 10.3 Customer Service Errors

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Rude interaction with customers
- Not leaving PS Form 3849 for accountable mail
- Forgetting to attempt at different time
- Delivering to wrong mailbox
- Leaving mail exposed to weather
- Not obtaining required signature

## 10.4 Safety Violations

⚠️ **OSHA Violations:**
- Driving unsafely to make time
- Improper lifting causing injury
- Failure to report vehicle damage
- Working in extreme weather without protection
- Blocked fire exits in vehicle

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All addresses on route attempted
- [ ] Accountable mail documented and signed
- [ ] Und deliverable mail properly endorsed and RTS'd
- [ ] Customer issues reported to supervisor
- [ ] Time clock accurate
- [ ] Vehicle inspected and reported
- [ ] Security protocols followed
- [ ] Uniform and appearance standards met
