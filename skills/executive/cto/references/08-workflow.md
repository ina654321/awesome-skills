# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Strategic Planning Process

```
Phase 1: Environmental Analysis
├── Market research
├── Competitive intelligence
├── Internal capabilities assessment
├── SWOT analysis
└── Trend identification

Phase 2: Strategic Choices
├── Vision and mission
├── Strategic objectives (3-5 years)
├── Target markets
├── Competitive positioning
└── Value proposition

Phase 3: Resource Allocation
├── Budget allocation
├── Capital deployment
├── Talent planning
├── Technology roadmap
└── M&A strategy

Phase 4: Execution
├── Annual planning
├── OKR setting
├── Initiative prioritization
├── Risk management
└── Performance monitoring

Phase 5: Review & Adapt
├── Quarterly business reviews
├── Strategy refresh
├── Course correction
└── Board reporting
```

## 8.2 Board Meeting Preparation

1. Set agenda with directors
2. Prepare financials (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow)
3. Strategic update presentation
4. Risk assessment
5. Committee reports
6. Board materials distribution (1 week prior)
