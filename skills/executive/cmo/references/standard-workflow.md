## 8. Standard Workflow

### 8.1 Go-to-Market Launch

```
Phase 1: Market Intelligence & Positioning (Month 1)
├── Customer research: 20+ ICP interviews + behavioral data analysis
├── Competitive landscape: positioning map, messaging audit, win/loss analysis
├── ICP definition: firmographics + psychographics + buying triggers + negative ICP
├── Positioning statement: draft → test with 10 customers → refine
├── Message house: master narrative + 3 pillars + proof points
└── [✓ Done]: Positioning validated by 8/10 target customers recognizing differentiation
    [✗ FAIL]: Customers describe positioning like a competitor → return to differentiation work

Phase 2: GTM Design & Content (Month 2)
├── GTM motion selection: Sales-Led / Product-Led
├── Channel strategy: 70% proven + 30% experimental; channel-ICP behavioral match
├── Content matrix: format × funnel stage × persona (at least 12 pieces pre-launch)
├── Sales enablement: battlecards, objection handling, ROI calculator, case studies
└── [✓ Done]: Sales team can articulate positioning without the slide deck; content ready
    [✗ FAIL]: Sales team defaulting to old messaging → conduct 2-hour enablement session

Phase 3: Launch & Optimize (Month 3+)
├── Beta: 50-100 target customers → collect NPS, feature gap data, testimonials
├── Limited: 500-1000 customers → paid channel tests, PR push, analyst briefings
├── GA: Full launch — coordinated press, paid, SEO, sales motion activation
├── Weekly: channel KPI review + A/B test results + pipeline quality check
└── [✓ Done]: Day 30 metrics meet targets (MQL volume, CAC within plan, NPS > 40)
    [✗ FAIL]: Trial-to-paid < 10% at Day 30 → pause scale; investigate activation gap
```

### 8.2 Annual Marketing Planning

```
Phase 1: Performance Audit (October, 2 weeks)
├── Full-funnel analysis: Impressions → MQL → SQL → Closed-Won, by channel
├── CAC by channel (fully-loaded): identify efficient vs. saturating channels
├── Customer cohort analysis: retention, expansion, NPS by acquisition cohort
├── Competitive position: share of voice, organic ranking, analyst positioning
└── [✓ Done]: 1-page marketing scorecard: what worked, what didn't, and why

Phase 2: Strategy & Budget (November)
├── Revenue goal → marketing pipeline goal → channel allocation
├── Apply Kill/Reduce/Maintain/Scale to each channel based on audit
├── Define 3 big bets for next year (experimental initiatives with test budgets)
├── Build headcount plan: hire in order of leverage (PMM → Content → Growth)
└── [✓ Done]: Board-approved marketing budget with channel-level MQL targets
    [✗ FAIL]: Marketing targets not connected to revenue plan → rebuild top-down

Phase 3: Execution Rhythm (Year-round)
├── Weekly: channel KPI review; A/B test results; pipeline coverage check
├── Monthly: funnel conversion analysis; CAC trend; content performance
├── Quarterly: positioning effectiveness check; competitive update; budget reallocation
└── [✓ Done]: Marketing Efficiency Ratio improving quarter-over-quarter
    [✗ FAIL]: CAC rising >20% → immediate channel mix audit before next spend cycle
```
