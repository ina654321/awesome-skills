## 7.2 Delivery Operations Standards

| Standard | Scope | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------|------------------|
| **FMCSA Hours of Service** | ELD rule, driving limits | 11-hr driving, 14-hr duty, 70-hr/8-day |
| **DOT Physical** | Medical certification | Valid every 2 years; report all medical conditions |
| **FMCSA Drug/Alcohol Testing** | Pre-employment, random, post-accident | 49 CFR Part 40 |
| **State CDL Requirements** | Class A/B with endorsements | HAZMAT, tanker, passenger, school bus |
| **FMCSA 49 CFR 396** | Vehicle inspection | Daily pre-trip, annual inspection |
| **FHWA Hazmat 49 CFR 171-180** | Hazmat transport | Placarding, shipping papers, training |
| **OSHA General Duty Clause** | Workplace safety | Securing loads, PPE, ergonomic |

## 7.3 Performance Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **On-time delivery rate** | > 95% | Delivery records |
| **Vehicle inspections** | 100% pre-trip completed | Inspection logs |
| **Preventable accidents** | Zero | Accident reports |
| **Fuel efficiency** | Varies by vehicle and route | Fleet telematics |
| **Customer satisfaction** | > 90% positive | Delivery surveys |
| **Safety incidents** | Zero | Incident reports |
| **Route adherence** | > 90% within time window | Route compliance reports |
| **Package handling damage** | < 0.5% | Damage claims |

## 7.4 Route & Time Management Standards

| Scenario | Time Standard | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| **Residential delivery** | 2–4 packages/route stop | Average |
| **Business delivery** | 3–8 packages/hour | Varies by access |
| **Last-mile delivery** | 150–200 stops/day | Peak season |
| **Signature required** | +1 min per stop | If customer available |
| **Re-attempt delivery** | 1–3 attempts | Per company policy |
| **Customer wait time** | < 5 min | Loading dock access |
