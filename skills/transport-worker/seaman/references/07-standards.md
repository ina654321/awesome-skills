## 7.2 STCW Certification Standards

| Certificate | Required For | Key Requirements |
|------------|-------------|-----------------|
| **AB (Able Seafarer Deck)** | Deck watch duties | 36 months sea service + written exam |
| **OS (Ordinary Seaman)** | Entry-level deck | Basic safety training (BST) |
| **QMED (Qualified Member)** | Engine room ratings | Firefighting, engine familiarization |
| **BST (Basic Safety Training)** | All crew | Firefighting, survival craft, first aid, personal survival |
| **STCW Manning** | International vessels | Minimum safe manning document |
| **Tankerman (Barge)** | Fuel/bulk liquid | Endorsement for tank barge operations |
| **RFPNW** | Rating forming part of navigation watch | Basic training certificate |

## 7.3 Safety & Regulatory Standards

| Regulation | Scope | Key Requirements |
|------------|-------|------------------|
| **SOLAS Chapter V** | Safety of navigation | Bridge resource management, ECDIS, voyage planning |
| **SOLAS Chapter III** | Life-saving appliances | Lifeboat drill monthly, abandon ship drill quarterly |
| **SOLAS Chapter XI-1** | Surveys and inspections | Port state control, safety certificate renewal |
| **33 CFR Part 95** | Operating a vessel under influence | BUI — Blood alcohol > 0.08% prohibited |
| **46 CFR Part 199** | Lifesaving equipment | PFD type, survival suit, EPIRBs |
| **MTSA 2002** | Maritime Transportation Security | TWIC, vessel security plan, SSAS |
| **EPA VGP** | Vessel general permit | Ballast water management, sewage, oil |

## 7.4 Deck Operations Metrics

| Operation | Standard | Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Mooring watch** | One person per 2 lines | More in heavy weather |
| **Anchor watch** | Continuous | 4-hour rotations minimum |
| **Cargo operations** | Qualified信号员 | Communication with dock/shore |
| **Ballast water exchange** | Per EPA VGP/BWM Convention | >95% exchange efficiency |
| **Watch turnover** | 15-min overlap briefing | ECDIS, logbook, passage plan |
| **Maintenance rounds** | Daily | Engine room, steering, deck |
