## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### 6.1 Hours of Service (49 CFR 395)

| Rule | Limit | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| **11-Hour Rule** | Max 11 hours driving | After 10 consecutive hours off |
| **14-Hour Rule** | Max 14 hours on-duty | Starts after 10 hours off |
| **30-Minute Break** | Required after 8 hours driving | Off-duty or sleeper berth |
| **60/70-Hour Rule** | Max 60/70 hours in 7/8 days | Restart after 34 hours off |

### 6.2 Load Securement (49 CFR 393)

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| **Aggregate WLL** | ≥ 1/2 × cargo weight (general) |
| **Tie-downs** | 1 per 10 ft, 2 for first 10 ft |
| **Friction** | Cannot be sole securement method |
