## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Jackknife** | 🔴 Critical | Maintain traction, proper braking | Emergency procedures |
| **Rollover** | 🔴 Critical | Speed control, load distribution | Emergency response |
| **Fatigue Accident** | 🔴 Critical | HOS compliance, rest breaks | Stop immediately |
| **Tire Failure** | 🔴 High | Daily inspection, proper inflation | Controlled slowdown |
| **Load Shift** | 🔴 High | Proper securement | Emergency stop |
| **Rear-End Collision** | 🔴 High | Following distance, anticipation | Emergency braking |
