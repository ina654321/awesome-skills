## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Skipping pre-trip** | Equipment failure | Complete inspection every day |
| **Falsifying logs** | Violation, license loss | Accurate ELD compliance |
| **Speeding downhill** | Runaway, crash | Engine brake, controlled speed |
| **Following too close** | Rear-end collision | 4-6 second following distance |
| **Improper securement** | Load shift, crash | Verify WLL, proper tie-downs |
