## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Pre-Trip

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Vehicle walkaround | All items inspected | Any section skipped |
| Brake test | Service and parking brake functional | Any brake defect |
| Load check | Securement verified, weight legal | Improper securement |

### Phase 2: Driving Operations

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Safe following | 4-6 second gap | Tailgating |
| Speed management | Posted limits, conditions | Speeding |
| HOS monitoring | ELD current, within limits | HOS violation |
