# Northrop Grumman Company Overview

## Corporate Profile

**Northrop Grumman Corporation** (NYSE: NOC) is a leading global aerospace and defense technology company, ranked as the #2 U.S. defense contractor.

### Founding & History

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1929 | Grumman Aircraft Engineering Corporation founded (Bethpage, NY) |
| 1939 | Northrop Aircraft, Inc. founded by Jack Northrop (Hawthorne, CA) |
| 1941 | Grumman begins mass production of F4F Wildcat for WWII |
| 1969 | Grumman-built Apollo Lunar Module lands first humans on the Moon |
| 1989 | B-2 Spirit stealth bomber first flight |
| 1994 | Northrop acquires Grumman for $2.17B, forming Northrop Grumman Corporation |
| 1996 | Acquires Westinghouse Electronic Systems ($2.9B) - radar systems |
| 2001 | Acquires Litton Industries ($3.8B) and Newport News Shipbuilding ($2.6B) |
| 2002 | Acquires TRW Inc. - space systems, lasers, automotive |
| 2011 | Spins off shipbuilding operations as Huntington Ingalls Industries |
| 2018 | Acquires Orbital ATK for $9.2B - space, missiles, launch systems |
| 2019 | Kathy Warden becomes CEO (succeeding Wes Bush) |
| 2021 | James Webb Space Telescope launches (Northrop prime contractor) |
| 2023 | B-21 Raider completes first flight |
| 2024 | Awarded second LRIP contract for B-21; record backlog growth |

### Business Segments

#### Aeronautics Systems
Design, development, production of manned and unmanned aircraft, ISR systems, strike weapons.

**Key Programs:**
- B-21 Raider stealth bomber
- E-2 Hawkeye AEW&C
- F-35 Lightning II (major subcontractor)
- MQ-4C Triton

#### Space Systems
Satellites, space systems, launch vehicles, strategic missiles.

**Key Programs:**
- James Webb Space Telescope
- Military communications satellites
- Missile warning systems
- E-130J TACAMO
- Solid rocket motors

#### Mission Systems
C4ISR, radar, electronic warfare, cyber, intelligence systems.

**Key Programs:**
- AN/TPS-80 G/ATOR radar
- F-35 integrated avionics
- AN/APR-39 EW systems
- Cyber defense solutions

#### Defense Systems
Strategic missiles, precision weapons, integrated air & missile defense.

**Key Programs:**
- LGM-35A Sentinel ICBM (GBSD)
- IBCS air defense command system
- Precision munitions
- Tactical rocket motors

### Competitive Position

**Primary Competitors:**
- Lockheed Martin (#1 defense contractor)
- RTX Corporation (Raytheon Technologies)
- General Dynamics
- Boeing Defense
- L3Harris Technologies

**Competitive Advantages:**
- Leading position in stealth technology
- Dominant HALE UAV market share
- Critical F-35 subcontractor role
- Prime contractor for B-21 and Sentinel
- Strong space systems portfolio
- Proven systems integration capabilities

### Strategic Priorities (2025-2026)

1. **B-21 Production Ramp:** Accelerating to meet Air Force demand
2. **Sentinel Program:** Restructuring to address cost/schedule challenges
3. **International Growth:** Expanding foreign military sales
4. **Digital Transformation:** Model-based engineering, digital threads
5. **Supply Chain Resilience:** Industrial base expansion investments
6. **Innovation:** Autonomy, AI/ML, directed energy, hypersonics
