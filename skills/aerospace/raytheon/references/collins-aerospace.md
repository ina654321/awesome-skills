# Collins Aerospace Systems

## Business Overview

Collins Aerospace is RTX's largest segment by revenue, providing advanced aerospace and defense technologies for commercial, military, business aviation, and airport solutions markets.

**2024 Performance:**
- Sales: $28.3 billion (+8% YoY)
- Adjusted Operating Profit: $4.5 billion (15.9% margin)
- Commercial OE Growth: +16%
- Commercial Aftermarket Growth: +13%

## Avionics & Mission Systems

### Commercial Avionics

**Pro Line Fusion:**
- Integrated flight deck for business jets and regional aircraft
- Touchscreen controls and cursor control device
- Synthetic vision, weather radar integration
- Head-up display (HUD) compatibility

**Connected Aviation Solutions:**
- Global air-to-ground connectivity
- Cabin connectivity and entertainment
- Aircraft health monitoring and data analytics
- Flight operations optimization

### Military Mission Systems

**F-35 Distributed Aperture System (DAS):**
- Six infrared sensors providing 360-degree coverage
- Missile warning, navigation, situational awareness
- Northrop Grumman prime; Collins subsystems

**ARC-210 Radios:**
- Secure, multi-band airborne communication
- Software-defined architecture
- Anti-jam capabilities
- Fielded on 180+ platform types

**Helmet-Mounted Displays:**
- Joint Helmet Mounted Cueing System (JHMCS)
- Display systems for F-35, F-22, F-16, F/A-18
- Night vision integration
