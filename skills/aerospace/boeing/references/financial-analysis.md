## Investment Considerations

### Bull Case Drivers

1. **Backlog Quality**: $521B provides revenue visibility
2. **Duopoly Structure**: Only major US commercial aircraft manufacturer
3. **Services Moat**: 14,000+ aircraft in service; recurring revenue
4. **Defense Stability**: $24B revenue base; government priority
5. **Ortberg Effect**: Engineering-first CEO with turnaround credibility

### Bear Case Risks

1. **Cash Burn**: $12B+ annual operating cash outflow
2. **Certification Delays**: 737-7, 737-10, 777X all delayed
3. **Defense Losses**: Fixed-price contracts may generate more charges
4. **Labor Costs**: New IAM contract increases structural costs
5. **Market Share Loss**: Airbus gaining in narrow-body segment
