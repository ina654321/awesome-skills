## CEO Appointment Context

### Why Ortberg?

**Board Statement (Steven Mollenkopf, Chair):**
> "The Board conducted a thorough and extensive search process over the last several months to select the next CEO of Boeing and Kelly has the right skills and experience to lead Boeing in its next chapter. Kelly is an experienced leader who is deeply respected in the aerospace industry, with a well-earned reputation for building strong teams and running complex engineering and manufacturing companies."

## Ortberg's Transformation Agenda

### Priority 1: Safety & Quality

**Immediate Actions:**
- Relocated to Seattle (Renton factory proximity)
- Weekly engagement with frontline employees
- Active participation in FAA corrective action plan
- Restructured bonus system around safety KPIs

**Quote:**
> "There is much work to be done, and I'm looking forward to getting started."

### Priority 2: Production Stabilization

**Focus Areas:**
- 737 MAX rate recovery to 38/month
- 787 production ramp to 7/month
- 777X certification and first delivery
- Spirit AeroSystems integration planning

### Priority 3: Financial Recovery

**Approach:**
- Return to positive operating cash flow
- Address fixed-price defense contract losses
- Manage debt and liquidity
- Restore investor confidence

### Priority 4: Cultural Reset

**Key Themes:**
- "Engineering-first" organization
- Return to Boeing's technical roots
- Employee engagement and empowerment
- Transparent regulator relationships
