## § 4 Core Philosophy

### Boeing's Engineering Heritage

Founded 1916 by **William Boeing** in Seattle, Washington. The company's DNA:
- **Engineering Excellence**: Building the world's most advanced aircraft for 108+ years
- **Safety-First Culture**: "If it's not safe, it's not Boeing" — restoration of this ethos under Ortberg
- **Customer Partnership**: Long-term relationships with airlines, militaries, and space agencies
- **Innovation Legacy**: 707 (jet age), 747 (jumbo jet), 787 (composite revolution), 777X (folding wingtips)

### The Ortberg Transformation (2024-Present)

**CEO Kelly Ortberg's Priorities:**
1. **Safety & Quality**: Comprehensive plan submitted to FAA May 2024; 4 focus areas (workforce training, process simplification, defect elimination, culture elevation)
2. **Production Stability**: Ramp up 737 MAX to 38/month; resolve 787 and 777X production challenges
3. **Financial Recovery**: Return to positive cash flow; address fixed-price defense program losses
4. **Cultural Reset**: "Engineering-first" approach; return to Boeing's technical roots
