## Marine Systems Programs

### Virginia-class Attack Submarines

**Program Overview**
- **Builder**: Electric Boat (prime) + Newport News (teaming)
- **Contract Type**: Multi-year procurement
- **Recent Award**: $12.4B for 2 FY2024 submarines (May 2025)
- **Current Block**: Block V (with Virginia Payload Module)

**Block V Enhancements**
- Virginia Payload Module (VPM): +4 vertical launch tubes
- 28 additional Tomahawk cruise missiles
- Payload capacity increased 225%

**Production Status**
- Rate: ~2 submarines per year (target: 2.3+)
- Tonnage: +13% increase at Electric Boat in 2025
- Challenge: Industrial base capacity constraints

### Columbia-class SSBN (Ballistic Missile Submarine)

**Program Overview**
- **Mission**: Replace Ohio-class SSBNs
- **Ships**: 12 planned (SSBN-826 through SSBN-837)
- **Builder**: Electric Boat (prime) + Newport News (teaming)
- **Service Life**: Through 2084

**Lead Boat: USS District of Columbia (SSBN-826)**
- **Status**: 60% complete (as of October 2025)
- **Delivery**: 2028 (delayed from 2027)
- **Milestones**:
  - Stern section delivered from Newport News (January 2024)
  - Major modules at Groton for assembly
  - Next year "pivotal" per CEO

**Second Boat: USS Wisconsin (SSBN-827)**
- **Delivery**: End of decade (2030)

**Program Investment**
- **Recent Contract**: $15.4B modification (March 2026)
- **Scope**: Lead yard support, sustainment, industrial base development
- **Completion**: June 2035
- **Funded By**: National Sea-Based Deterrence Fund

**Technical Specifications**
- Length: 560 ft (171 m)
- Beam: 43 ft (13 m)
- Displacement: 20,815 tons submerged
- Missile tubes: 16 x Trident D5
- Propulsion: Nuclear reactor with electric drive

## Combat Systems Programs

### M1 Abrams Main Battle Tank

**Current Production: M1A2 SEPv4**
- **Status**: Production ongoing
- **Weight**: 73+ tons
- **Crew**: 4 (commander, gunner, loader, driver)
- **Engine**: Honeywell AGT1500 gas turbine
- **APS**: Trophy add-on active protection

**Next Generation: M1E3 Abrams**
- **Status**: Prototype development
- **Prototype Delivery**: Summer 2026 (4 units)
- **Key Innovations**:

| Feature | M1E3 | M1A2 SEPv4 |
|---------|------|------------|
| Weight | ~60 tons | 73+ tons |
| Propulsion | Hybrid-electric | Gas turbine |
| Fuel Efficiency | +40% | Baseline |
| Crew | 3 (autoloader) | 4 (manual loader) |
| Turret | Unmanned | Manned |
| APS | Integrated | Add-on |
| Silent Watch | Yes | No |

**Development Approach**
- **Budget**: ~$75M for prototypes
- **Components**: Commercial off-the-shelf
  - Caterpillar engine
  - SAPA transmission
  - Roush cockpit with Recaro seats
- **Developer**: GD Land Systems, Lima, Ohio
