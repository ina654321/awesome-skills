## Financial Strengths

1. **Record Backlog**: $118B provides 2+ years of revenue visibility
2. **Strong Cash Flow**: 94% FCF conversion is industry-leading
3. **Conservative Balance Sheet**: Investment-grade credit rating
4. **Consistent Returns**: 30+ years of dividend growth
5. **Margin Expansion**: Operating margins improving across segments
6. **Diversified Portfolio**: No single program >15% of revenue

## Financial Risks

1. **Columbia-class Execution**: Potential cost overruns on critical program
2. **Defense Budget Pressure**: Federal deficit concerns
3. **Aerospace Tariffs**: International cost pressures
4. **Submarine Supplier Base**: Industrial base erosion
5. **GDIT Growth**: Low single-digit growth challenges
