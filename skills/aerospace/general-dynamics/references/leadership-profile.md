# General Dynamics Leadership Profile

## Phebe Novakovic

### Current Position
**Chairman and Chief Executive Officer**, General Dynamics Corporation

### Tenure
- **Appointed CEO**: January 2013
- **Appointed Chairman**: March 2013
- **Years in Role**: 13+ years (as of 2026)

### Background

**Early Career**
- Joined General Dynamics in 2001
- Previously served in U.S. government intelligence and defense roles
- Deep understanding of defense acquisition and national security

**GD Career Progression**
- Senior Vice President, Planning and Development
- President, Marine Systems
- Vice President, Corporate Development
- Chief Operating Officer (2012)
- Chief Executive Officer (2013-present)

### Leadership Philosophy

**Strategic Priorities**
1. **Disciplined Capital Allocation**
   - Focus on ROIC (Return on Invested Capital)
   - Conservative balance sheet management
   - No debt for growth philosophy

2. **Operational Excellence**
   - Productivity improvements across all segments
   - Margin expansion through execution
   - Supply chain optimization

3. **Shareholder Returns**
   - Consistent dividend growth (30+ consecutive years)
   - Opportunistic share buybacks
   - Strong free cash flow conversion

4. **Customer Partnership**
   - Long-term relationships with DoD
   - International expansion where GD has advantages
   - Through-life support focus

### Management Style

**Earnings Call Communication**
- Direct, data-driven communication
- Detailed segment analysis
- Candid discussion of challenges
- Conservative guidance philosophy

**Key Quotes**

### Key Achievements as CEO

**Financial Performance (2013-2025)**
- Revenue growth: ~$32B → ~$54B (+70%)
- Backlog growth: Significant expansion to $118B
- Shareholder returns: Consistent dividend increases
- Credit rating: Maintained investment grade

**Strategic Transformations**
1. **Gulfstream Product Cycle**
   - Launched G650 (now retired)
   - Led G700/G800 development
   - Maintained market leadership

2. **Submarine Production Acceleration**
   - Virginia-class rate increases
   - Columbia-class program launch
   - Electric Boat capacity expansion

3. **Combat Systems Modernization**
   - M1E3 next-generation tank program
   - International expansion (Europe, Middle East)
   - Strong order intake in 2024-2025

4. **GDIT Positioning**
   - Scaled federal IT services
   - C4ISR leadership
   - Cybersecurity capabilities
