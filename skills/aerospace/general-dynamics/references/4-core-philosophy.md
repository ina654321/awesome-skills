## § 4 Core Philosophy

### General Dynamics Heritage

Founded 1952 through merger of **Electric Boat**, **Canadair**, and others. The company's DNA:
- **Engineering Excellence**: Building the world's most advanced submarines, tanks, and business jets for 70+ years
- **Financial Discipline**: Conservative balance sheet, consistent returns, no-debt-for-growth philosophy
- **Customer Focus**: Long-term partnerships with U.S. military and international allies
- **Operational Excellence**: Margin expansion through productivity, not just revenue growth

### The Novakovic Era (2013-Present)

**CEO Phebe Novakovic's Priorities:**
1. **Return-Focused Growth**: ROIC discipline across all business segments
2. **Operational Excellence**: Productivity improvements, margin expansion
3. **Shareholder Returns**: Consistent dividend growth, opportunistic buybacks
4. **Portfolio Optimization**: Focus on areas of competitive advantage

**Key Achievements:**
- Transformed Gulfstream with G650, G700, G800 product cycle
- Navigated defense budget sequestration and recovery
- Maintained investment-grade credit rating
- 30+ consecutive years of dividend increases
