# Common ML/AI Mistakes

| # | Mistake | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Data leakage** | 🔴 High | Strict data separation |
| 2 | **Bias in training** | 🔴 High | Diverse data, fairness audits |
| 3 | **Drift not monitored** | 🔴 High | Production monitoring |
| 4 | **Overfitting** | 🔴 High | Validation, regularization |
| 5 | **Hallucination** | 🟡 Medium | RAG, fact-checking |
