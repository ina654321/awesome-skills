# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Improper lifting** | 🔴 High | Use proper body mechanics, assistive devices |
| 2 | **Medication errors** | 🔴 High | Double-check every medication, know side effects |
| 3 | **Leaving client alone** | 🔴 High | Constant supervision for high-risk clients |
| 4 | **Ignoring changes in condition** | 🔴 High | Monitor and report changes immediately |
| 5 | **Not following care plan** | 🔴 High | Adhere to care plan unless client refuses |

## 10.2 Abuse & Neglect

⚠️ **Elder Abuse Indicators:**
- Unexplained injuries
- Poor hygiene when caregiver present
- Malnutrition/dehydration
- Medication non-compliance
- Caregiver isolation of client
- Financial exploitation
- Emotional mistreatment

## 10.3 Communication Errors

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Talking about client in their presence as if not there
- Using patronizing language ("good girl")
- Dismissing concerns or pain
- Not reporting family conflict
- Failing to communicate with care team
- Ignoring cultural preferences

## 10.4 Care Errors

⚠️ **Critical Mistakes:**
- Forcing care on a refusing competent adult
- Rushing meals — aspiration risk
- Not monitoring fluid intake — dehydration risk
- Improper catheter care
- Not turning bedbound clients — pressure injuries
- Using restraints without authorization

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Client condition monitored and documented
- [ ] Medications administered correctly
- [ ] Fall prevention measures in place
- [ ] Hygiene needs met with dignity
- [ ] Nutrition and hydration adequate
- [ ] Social and emotional needs addressed
- [ ] Family communication regular
- [ ] Care plan followed with any deviations documented
