# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Daily Care Routine

```
Morning Care (6:00-9:00 AM):
├── Check on client — observe condition
├── Assist with toileting, hygiene
├── Dress client (respect preferences)
├── Prepare and serve breakfast
├── Administer medications (per schedule)
├── Encourage light activity if able
└── Monitor for changes from baseline

Afternoon Care (12:00-3:00 PM):
├── Prepare and serve lunch
├── Encourage social interaction
├── Assist with ambulation/exercise
├── Monitor fluid intake
├── Rest period
└── Light housekeeping as needed

Evening Care (5:00-8:00 PM):
├── Prepare and serve dinner
├── Assist with personal hygiene
├── Prepare for sleep (toileting, incontinence care)
├── Evening medications
├── Prepare next day's clothes
└── Document daily care log
```

## 8.2 Medication Management

```
Medication Safety Protocol:
├── Know every medication — purpose, dose, timing, side effects
├── Use pill organizer or blister packs
├── Never skip, double, or share medications
├── Monitor for adverse reactions
├── Report medication errors immediately
├── Keep medication list current
└── Dispose of expired medications properly

Signs of Medication Problems:
├── Confusion or drowsiness
├── Dizziness/falls
├── Nausea/vomiting
├── Rash
├── Changes in appetite or sleep
└── Mood changes
```

## 8.3 Fall Prevention

```
Fall Risk Assessment:
├── Gait and balance
├── Vision/hearing changes
├── Medication effects
├── Home hazards
├── Orthostatic hypotension
└── Footwear

Prevention Measures:
├── Clear pathways, remove rugs
├── Proper lighting, especially at night
├── Grab bars in bathroom
├── Non-slip footwear
├── Adequate hydration (prevents dizziness)
└── Regular exercise as tolerated
```
