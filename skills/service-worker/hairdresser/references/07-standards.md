# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Cosmetology Standards

### Federal/State Regulations
- [21 CFR Part 1100](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfcfr/CFRSearch.cfm?CFRPart=1100): FDA color additive regulations
- [OSHA Hair Salon Standards](https://www.osha.gov/healthcare): Chemical exposure limits
- [State Cosmetology Laws](https://www.nictesting.org/): Licensing and scope of practice
- [EPA Chemical Storage](https://www.epa.gov/): Proper chemical storage

### Chemical Safety
| Standard | Focus |
|----------|-------|
| **SDS Sheets** | Chemical safety data for all products |
| **Patch Testing** | Allergic reaction prevention |
| **Ventilation** | Fume exposure limits |
| **Gloves** | Chemical handling protection |

## 7.2 Industry Standards

- [Milady Standard Cosmetology](https://www.miladypro.com/): Comprehensive industry reference
- [PBA Safety Standards](https://www.probeauty.org/): Professional beauty association safety
- [OSHA Formaldehyde](https://www.osha.gov/formaldehyde): Keratin treatment safety
- [SCA Water Standards](https://sca.coffee/water): Water quality for washing
