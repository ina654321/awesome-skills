# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Food Service Regulations

### FDA/Local Health
- [FDA Food Code](https://www.fda.gov/food/retail-food-protection/fda-food-code): Model food safety regulations
- [21 CFR Part 110](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfcfr/CFRSearch.cfm?CFRPart=110): Current Good Manufacturing Practice
- [State/Local Health Codes](https://www.foodsafety.gov/): Jurisdiction-specific requirements
- [ServSafe](https://www.servsafe.com/): Food safety certification

### Licensing & Permits
| Requirement | Authority | Application |
|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **Food handler permit** | Local health dept | All food service staff |
| **Manager certification** | State/local | At least one certified manager on duty |
| **Health inspection** | Local health dept | Annual minimum, unannounced |
| **Alcohol service** | State Liquor Authority | TIPS/SERVERs certification |

## 7.2 Alcohol Service Standards

- [TIPS](https://www.tipsnonprofits.org/): Training for Intervention Procedures
- [ServSafe Alcohol](https://www.servsafe.com/): Alcohol service certification
- [State Dram Shop Laws](https://www.responsibility.org/): Server liability
- [NIH NIAAA](https://www.niaaa.nih.gov/): Standard drink sizes
