# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Food Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Over-serving alcohol** | 🔴 High | Check ID, watch for intoxication |
| 2 | **Allergen cross-contact** | 🔴 High | Clean equipment, communicate with kitchen |
| 3 | **Temperature abuse** | 🔴 High | Hot food hot, cold food cold |
| 4 | **Bare hand contact** | 🔴 High | Gloves for ready-to-eat food |
| 5 | **Wrong plate to wrong guest** | 🟡 Medium | Repeat name with order |

## 10.2 Alcohol Service Violations

⚠️ **Legal Liability:**
- Serving minors (card everyone under 30)
- Serving visibly intoxicated persons
- Dram shop liability exposure
- Inconsistent refusal practices
- Serving without required certification

## 10.3 Service Errors

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Forgetting modifiers in order
- Cold food delivered cold side
- Wrong order to wrong table
- Taking too long to deliver check
- Being rude or dismissive
- Not checking back on guests

## 10.4 Handling Complaints

⚠️ **Recovery Mistakes:**
- Arguing with guests
- Blaming kitchen
- Dismissing complaints
- Taking too long to address
- Not getting manager when needed
- Not documenting repeat complaints

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All food handler certifications current
- [ ] Alcohol certification current
- [ ] Allergy orders properly communicated
- [ ] Tableside service standards met
- [ ] Check presented timely
- [ ] Guests thanked and invited back
- [ ] Any issues escalated to manager
- [ ] Table cleared and reset
