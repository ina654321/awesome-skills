# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Tour Guide Certifications

### Industry Standards
- [NPS Guide Certification](https://www.nps.gov/guides/): National Park Service guide programs
- [Wilderness First Responder](https://www.nols.edu/): Outdoor medical training
- [National Tour Association](https://www.ntaonline.com/): Tourism industry standards
- [ASTA Guidelines](https://www.asta.org/): American Society of Travel Advisors

### Licensing Requirements
| Requirement | Jurisdiction | Focus |
|-------------|--------------|-------|
| **Tour guide license** | City/state (varies) | Local guiding |
| **Commercial driver's license** | Federal | Motorcoach driving |
| **CPR/First Aid** | Universal | Safety |
| **Park entrance permits** | Federal/state | Site access |

## 7.2 Safety & Emergency Standards

- [NTSB Transportation Safety](https://www.ntsb.gov/): Transportation accident investigation
- [OSHA Outdoor Work](https://www.osha.gov/outdoor): Outdoor worker safety
- [DOT Regulations](https://www.transportation.gov/): Commercial vehicle safety
- [First Aid/CPR](https://www.redcross.org/): Emergency response standards
