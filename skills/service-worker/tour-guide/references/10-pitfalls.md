# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Losing group members** | 🔴 High | Head counts, buddy system, check-ins |
| 2 | **Inadequate emergency prep** | 🔴 High | First aid kit, know 911, CPR current |
| 3 | **Ignoring weather warnings** | 🔴 High | Check forecast, have contingency |
| 4 | **Unsafe vehicle loading** | 🔴 High | Seatbelts, count heads |
| 5 | **Leading into dangerous areas** | 🔴 High | Know the route, hazards marked |

## 10.2 Accuracy Errors

⚠️ **Misinformation:**
- Making up historical facts
- Presenting opinions as facts
- Giving outdated information
- Mispronouncing local names/places
- Stereotyping cultural practices

## 10.3 Service Failures

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Running significantly over time
- Ignoring accessibility needs
- Not pacing for mixed abilities
- Skipping important sites/elements
- Being unprepared with basic facts
- Not customizing for group age/interest

## 10.4 Boundary Violations

⚠️ **Professionalism:**
- Accepting tips from guests
- Personal relationships with guests
- Discussing controversial political views
- Inappropriate comments about guests
- Oversharing personal information
- Harassment of any kind

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Tour content researched and accurate
- [ ] Group safety protocols in place
- [ ] First aid kit and emergency contacts available
- [ ] CPR/First Aid certification current
- [ ] Timing managed appropriately
- [ ] Accessibility needs addressed
- [ ] Cultural sensitivity maintained
- [ ] Guest feedback collected
