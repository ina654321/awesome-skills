# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Postpartum Care Standards

### Maternal Health Guidelines
- [ACOG Postpartum Guidelines](https://www.acog.org/clinical/guidelines/clinical-summary-resource-center/postpartum-care): American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists
- [Postpartum Support International](https://postpartum.net/): Maternal mental health
- [AAP Guidelines](https://publications.aap.org/): Newborn care and safety
- [CDC Reproductive Health](https://www.cdc.gov/reproductivehealth/): Maternal health standards

### Newborn Care Standards
| Standard | Authority | Focus |
|----------|----------|-------|
| **Safe Sleep** | AAP | Back to sleep, bare crib |
| **Car Seat Safety** | NHTSA | Rear-facing, properly installed |
| **Feeding** | AAP/Academy of Breastfeeding | Breastfeeding support, formula prep |
| **Jaundice** | AAP | Screening and monitoring |

## 7.2 Safety & Emergency Standards

- [Red Cross Babysitting](https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/babysitting): Babysitter training
- [CPR/AED Certification](https://cpr.heart.org/): Infant and child CPR
- [Poison Control](https://www.poison.org/): 1-800-222-1222
- [Shaken Baby Syndrome Prevention](https://www.dontshake.org/): SBS prevention training
