## Four-Stage Meal Framework

### Stage 1: Week 1 (排毒期 - Detox/Expel)
**Purpose:** Expel lochia, heal wounds, establish lactation gently

**Principles:**
- Light, easily digestible foods
- Warm temperature only
- Hydration focus
- Minimal strong herbs

**Sample Daily Menu:**

| Meal | Menu |
|------|------|
| Breakfast | Millet congee with egg, ginger tea |
| Mid-morning | Red date tea (5-8 dates) |
| Lunch | Steamed fish, spinach with ginger, white rice |
| Afternoon | Warm papaya milk |
| Dinner | Chicken congee, bok choy, sesame oil drizzle |
| Evening | Brown sugar ginger tea |

### Stage 2: Week 2 (修复期 - Repair)
**Purpose:** Blood building, tissue repair, milk establishment

**Principles:**
- Introduce blood-building foods
- Begin herbal soups
- Increase protein
- Continue warming focus

### Stage 3: Week 3 (滋补期 - Nourish)
**Purpose:** Qi restoration, energy building, sustained lactation

### Stage 4: Week 4 (调理期 - Regulate)
**Purpose:** Full recovery preparation, transition to normal diet

## Core Herbal Soups

### 1. Pig Trotter Vinegar Soup (猪脚姜醋)
**Best for:** Week 2+, collagen, calcium, warming

**Ingredients:**
- 2 pig trotters (cut into pieces)
- 500ml sweet black vinegar
- 200g ginger (sliced)
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 4 hard-boiled eggs (optional)

**Method:**
1. Blanch pig trotters in boiling water, rinse
2. Heat sesame oil, fry ginger until fragrant
3. Add pig trotters, stir-fry 5 minutes
4. Add vinegar and water to cover
5. Simmer 2-3 hours until tender
6. Add eggs in last 30 minutes

### 2. Black Chicken Herbal Soup (乌鸡汤)
**Best for:** Week 3+, deep nourishment, blood building

**Ingredients:**
- 1 black chicken (whole or pieces)
- 10 red dates
- 15g goji berries
- 10g dang gui (optional, week 3+)
- 5g astragalus (optional, week 3+)
- Ginger slices

**Method:**
1. Blanch chicken in boiling water
2. Add all ingredients to pot
3. Cover with water
4. Simmer 2-3 hours
5. Season lightly with salt

### 3. Dang Gui Astragalus Chicken Soup (当归黄芪鸡汤)
**Best for:** Week 3-4, qi and blood tonic

**Caution:** Not for early postpartum (too strong)

### 4. Fish and Papaya Soup (木瓜鱼汤)
**Best for:** All weeks, lactation support, gentle
