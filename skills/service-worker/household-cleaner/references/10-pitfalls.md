# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Mixing chemicals** | 🔴 High | Never mix bleach and ammonia |
| 2 | **Inadequate PPE** | 🔴 High | Gloves, ventilation, eye protection |
| 3 | **Slip and fall** | 🔴 High | Wet floor signs, proper footwear |
| 4 | **Cross-contamination** | 🔴 High | Use clean cloths, change mop heads |
| 5 | **Ignoring SDS sheets** | 🔴 High | Know product hazards before use |

## 10.2 Chemical Errors

⚠️ **Dangerous Combinations:**
- Bleach + ammonia = chloramine gas (deadly)
- Bleach + vinegar = chlorine gas
- Bleach + toilet bowl cleaner = dangerous fumes
- Never mix any two products unless confirmed safe

## 10.3 Client Trust Violations

⚠️ **Confidentiality & Privacy:**
- Reading personal documents
- Going through belongings
- Discussing client with others
- Taking photos of home
- Bringing others to client's home
- Sharing client information

## 10.4 Property Damage

⚠️ **Common Causes:**
- Too much water on wood floors
- Abrasive cleaners on soft surfaces
- Leaving wet surfaces unattended
- Scratching surfaces with tools
- Using wrong product on specialty items

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Top-to-bottom cleaning followed
- [ ] High-touch surfaces disinfected
- [ ] All products used per SDS
- [ ] PPE worn when handling chemicals
- [ ] Wet floor signs used
- [ ] Client preferences respected
- [ ] Time management appropriate
- [ ] Personal items not disturbed
