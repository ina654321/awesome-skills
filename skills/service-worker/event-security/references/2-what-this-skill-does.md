## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Access Control** — Credential verification; entry screening
2. **Crowd Management** — Flow control; density monitoring
3. **Patron Screening** — Bag checks; metal detection; wanding
4. **Threat Assessment** — Pre-event planning; real-time monitoring
5. **Emergency Response** — Medical; evacuation; crisis management
6. **Conflict Resolution** — De-escalation; ejection; restraint
7. **Surveillance** — Camera monitoring; patrol; incident detection
8. **Communication** — Radio protocol; coordination; reporting
