# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Moving Industry Regulations

### Federal Regulations
- [49 CFR Part 375](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-49/subtitle-B/chapter-III/part-375): FMCSA household goods moving
- [USDOT Number](https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/registration): Interstate mover registration
- [FMCSA Consumer Protection](https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/protect/moving): Consumer rights
- [HUD Interstate Moving](https://www.hud.gov/topics/rental_assistance/tenantrights): Fair housing in moving

### State Regulations
| Requirement | Authority | Application |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **License** | State PUC or commerce dept | Operating authority |
| **Insurance** | State minimum | Liability coverage |
| **Rate filing** | State tariff | Published rates |
| **Claims** | State law | Damage claims process |

## 7.2 Safety Standards

- [OSHA Ergonomics](https://www.osha.gov/ergonomics): Safe lifting practices
- [NIOSH Lifting Guide](https://www.cdc.gov/niosh/topics/ergonomics/): Weight limits
- [FMCSA Safety](https://www.fmcsa.dot.gov/safety/): Commercial driver safety
