# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Improper lifting** | 🔴 High | Training, team lift, mechanical aids |
| 2 | **Blocking traffic unsafely** | 🔴 High | Proper permits, cones, flaggers |
| 3 | **Overloading truck** | 🔴 High | Know weight limits, distribute evenly |
| 4 | **Inadequate securing** | 🔴 High | Strap and pad everything |
| 5 | **Distracted driving** | 🔴 High | No phone while operating |

## 10.2 Damage & Claims Issues

⚠️ **Common Damage Causes:**
- Dragging furniture across floors
- Not padding sufficiently
- Overloading boxes
- Improper stacking
- Not securing load with straps
- Trying to force oversized items

## 10.3 Pricing Violations

⚠️ **Customer Protection Issues:**
- Charging more than estimate without disclosure
- Adding charges not discussed
- Not providing required paperwork
- Holding goods for ransom
- Misrepresenting binding vs. non-binding quotes

## 10.4 Property Damage

⚠️ **Common Mistakes:**
- Scratching hardwood floors
- Dinging walls on stairs
- Breaking glass items
- Scuffing doors and frames
- Damaging elevators
- Leaving debris at delivery

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Pre-move inspection documented and signed
- [ ] All items inventoried
- [ ] Heavy items properly padded
- [ ] Load secured with straps
- [ ] Boxes stacked with heavy on bottom
- [ ] Final walk-through at both locations
- [ ] Customer signs delivery receipt
- [ ] All damage noted before signing
