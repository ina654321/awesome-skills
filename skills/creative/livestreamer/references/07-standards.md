## 7.2 Platform Monetization Standards

| Platform | Min Requirements | Monetization Methods | Payout Threshold |
|----------|-----------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| YouTube | 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours | Ad revenue, memberships, Super Chats, YouTube Shopping | $100 |
| Twitch | 50 followers + 500 minutes + 7 streams | Subs, Bits, ads, donations | $100 |
| TikTok | 1,000 followers (varies) | Live gifts, brand deals, TikTok Shop | $50 |
| Facebook | 10,000 followers | Stars, ad breaks, brand collaborations | $100 |
| Kick | Open (invite only) | Subscriptions, tips, rev share | $50 |

## 7.3 Technical Stream Quality Standards

| Specification | Minimum | Recommended | Professional |
|--------------|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Resolution | 720p | 1080p | 4K |
| Frame rate | 30 fps | 60 fps | 60 fps |
| Bitrate (Twitch) | 4,500 kbps | 6,000 kbps | 10,000+ kbps |
| Bitrate (YouTube) | 4,500 kbps | 6,000+ kbps | 10,000+ kbps |
| Audio | 128 kbps | 320 kbps | 320 kbps AAC |
| Upload speed | 15 Mbps | 25+ Mbps | 50+ Mbps |

## 7.4 Equipment Standards

| Component | Minimum | Recommended |
|-----------|---------|-------------|

| Microphone | USB condenser ($80+) | XLR + audio interface |
| Lighting | Basic ring light | Key + fill + backlight setup |
| Internet | 15 Mbps upload | 25+ Mbps upload, wired ethernet |
| Capture card | N/A | Elgato HD60 for console |
| Green screen | Basic collapsible | Wide, well-lit, proper depth |

## 7.5 Copyright & Content ID Standards

| Type | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Original music | Low | You own it |
| Licensed music (per-stream) | Medium | Live莲花, Epidemic Sound |
| DMCA-safe music | Low | YouTube Audio Library, Free Music Archive |
| Movie/TV clips | High | Almost never fair use |
| Game footage | Low-Medium | Most games allow streaming |
| Third-party content | High | Never use without permission |

## 7.6 FTC Disclosure Requirements

- Sponsored streams require disclosure: "This stream is sponsored by X" or #sponsored
- Affiliate links must be disclosed
- Free products for review require disclosure
- Use clear, upfront language within the stream
