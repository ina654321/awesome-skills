# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Inconsistent schedule** | High | Set 2-3 day schedule and stick to it |
| 2 | **Ignoring chat** | High | Respond to every viewer, especially lurkers |
| 3 | **No audio backup** | High | Always have audio backup ready |
| 4 | **Copyright music** | High | Use DMCA-safe music services |
| 5 | **Burning out with 6-7 day weeks** | High | 3-4 days sustainable long-term |
| 6 | **Bad audio quality** | High | Invest in proper microphone |
| 7 | **Reading donor names inappropriately** | Medium | Know your community's comfort level |
| 8 | **No engagement between streams** | Medium | Post daily, engage with comments |
| 9 | **Ignoring analytics** | Medium | Review stats weekly, adapt strategy |
| 10 | **Not setting aside taxes** | High | 25-30% of revenue goes to taxes |

## 10.2 Technical Mistakes

- **Insufficient upload speed**: Causes buffering and dropped frames
- **No backup internet**: Mobile hotspot as backup essential
- **Cluttered, distracting overlay**: Clean > complex
- **Bad lighting**: Ring light or softbox essential
- **Echoey room**: Acoustic treatment or carpet/curtains
- **No stream key protection**: Never share your stream key

## 10.3 Community Management Mistakes

- **Enabling toxic behavior**: Set standards early
- **Playing favorites**: All regulars should feel valued
- **Not moderating chat**: Enable slow mode and sub-only when needed
- **Ignoring mental health**: Streaming is performance, take breaks
- **Fueling drama**: Stay above the fray

## 10.4 Business Mistakes

- **No written agreements with sponsors**: Get everything in writing
- **Accepting all sponsors**: Vet for reputation
- **Not tracking expenses**: Deductible equipment and software
- **No separate business account**: Personal/business must separate
- **Not insuring equipment**: Content creation equipment is valuable
- **Going full-time too early**: Build audience before quitting day job

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Stream schedule set and communicated
- [ ] All equipment tested before going live
- [ ] Internet connection stable (wired preferred)
- [ ] Audio levels properly set
- [ ] Copyright-safe music ready
- [ ] Alerts and overlays working
- [ ] Chat rules clear (no harassment, etc.)
- [ ] Moderators identified and prepared
- [ ] VOD upload settings configured
- [ ] Sponsor disclosures ready
- [ ] Emergency backup plan prepared
- [ ] Analytics tracked post-stream
- [ ] Social media engagement done
- [ ] Taxes set aside from revenue
