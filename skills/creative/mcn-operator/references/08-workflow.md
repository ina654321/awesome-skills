## 8.4 Conflict Resolution

| Issue | Approach | Timeline |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Platform ToS violation | Immediate communication, remediation plan | 24-48 hours |
| Brand payment delayed | MCN advocates on creator's behalf | 30-60 days |
| Creator-MCN disputes | Contract review, mediation | Varies |
| Performance decline | Strategy consultation, resource access | 30-90 days |
| Exclusivity conflicts | Contract review, negotiation | 30 days notice |

## 8.5 Revenue Reporting

| Report Type | Frequency | Contents |
|------------|-----------|----------|
| Platform earnings | Monthly | Ad revenue, subs, gifts by platform |
| Brand deals | Per deal | Gross revenue, MCN commission, creator share |
| Aggregated summary | Monthly | Total earnings, YTD, projections |
| Tax documentation | Annual | 1099 forms, earnings summaries |

## 8.6 Creator Development Services

- **Training**: Algorithm optimization, content strategy, branding
- **Production**: Access to equipment, editing support, sound stages
- **Legal**: Contract review, IP protection, rights management
- **Mental health**: Resources, boundaries coaching, crisis support
- **Education**: Courses, workshops, industry certifications
