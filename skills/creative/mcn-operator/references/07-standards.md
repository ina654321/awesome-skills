## 7.2 MCN Revenue Split Standards

| Service Level | Typical Split (Creator/MCN) | Included Services |
|--------------|----------------------------|-----------------|
| Basic | 90/10 - 80/20 | Platform support, some monetization |
| Standard | 70/30 - 60/40 | Full monetization, brand deals, tools |
| Premium | 50/50 - 40/60 | Full service, production support, exclusivity |

## 7.3 Creator Contract Requirements

| Clause | Purpose | Standard Terms |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| Term | Duration of agreement | 1-2 years standard |
| Exclusivity | Platform/content restrictions | Varies by MCN |
| Revenue share | Monetization splits | Documented per stream |
| Brand deals | Commission on facilitated deals | 15-30% MCN commission |
| Termination | Exit conditions | 30-90 day notice typical |
| IP ownership | Content rights | Usually creator-owned |
| Performance metrics | Minimum requirements | Viewers, subs, posts |

## 7.5 Brand Deal Valuation

| Creator Size | Avg Rate (per video) | Notes |
|-------------|----------------------|-------|
| Nano (1K-10K) | $50-500 | Building portfolio |
| Micro (10K-100K) | $500-5,000 | Most accessible |
| Mid (100K-500K) | $5,000-25,000 | Professional tier |
| Macro (500K-1M) | $25,000-75,000 | Full-time territory |
| Mega (1M+) | $75,000+ | Top-tier negotiations |

## 7.6 FTC/ASA Disclosure Standards

- "This video is sponsored by X" or #sponsored at start
- In-feed posts: "Ad" or "#ad" label
- Affiliate links: " affiliate link" in description
- Native advertising disclosures must be unambiguous
