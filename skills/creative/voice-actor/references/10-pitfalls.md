# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Poor audio quality** | High | Invest in proper equipment and acoustic treatment |
| 2 | **Over-selling range** | High | Only market voices you can consistently deliver |
| 3 | **Ignoring direction** | High | Adapt to director's vision, not yours |
| 4 | **Cold recording without warm-up** | Medium | 10-15 minute vocal warm-up before every session |
| 5 | **Not following audition instructions** | High | Read every breakdown carefully |
| 6 | **Unprofessional communication** | Medium | Respond within 24 hours, be courteous |
| 7 | **No demos or show reel** | High | Professional demo is essential for booking |
| 8 | **Taking every job offered** | Medium | Build rates over time, decline low-ballers |
| 9 | **Not tracking income/expenses** | High | Use separate business accounts, save for taxes |
| 10 | **Ignoring vocal health** | High | Hydrate, rest, warm up, seek help when strained |

## 10.2 Recording Quality Mistakes

- **Plosives (P sounds)**: Keep mic at 45-degree angle, use pop filter
- **Sibilance (S sounds)**: Adjust distance, de-ess in post
- **Room noise/reflections**: Acoustic treatment is non-negotiable
- **Peaks/clipping**: Watch levels, don't get closer than 6 inches
- **Inconsistent levels**: Monitor throughout recording
- **Mouth sounds**: Hydrate, use mouthwash before session

## 10.3 Audition Mistakes

- **Late submissions**: Auditions are often time-sensitive
- **Wrong file format**: Always follow submission requirements
- **No slate when requested**: Missing requirement = automatic rejection
- **Reading the direction notes aloud**: Don't narrate the instructions
- **Reading other roles**: Only record the role you're auditioning for
- **Audio quality lower than demo**: Your audition quality should match your demo

## 10.4 Business Mistakes

- **No contract**: Always have written agreement before recording
- **No deposit**: 50% minimum before session
- **Not documenting approvals**: Email confirmation of "approved" protects you
- **Late delivery**: Build in buffer time for editing and upload
- **No backup equipment**: USB cable, backup mic, backup laptop
- **Mixing personal/professional finances**: Separate bank account required

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Audition specs match requirements exactly
- [ ] Audio recorded at 44.1kHz/48kHz, 16/24-bit WAV
- [ ] Noise floor below -60 dBFS
- [ ] Peak levels at -3 dBFS or below
- [ ] No plosives or sibilance issues
- [ ] Consistent performance across reads
- [ ] Slate included (if required)
- [ ] File named correctly
- [ ] Cover note professional and specific
- [ ] Submitted within deadline
- [ ] Follow-up if no confirmation received
