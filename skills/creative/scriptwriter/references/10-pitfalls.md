# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Overwriting description** | High | Cut 30%; let action speak |
| 2 | **On-the-nose dialogue** | High | Characters mean more than they say |
| 3 | **Camera directions** | High | Directors decide camera; you're writing story |
| 4 | **Unfilmable action** | Medium | If you can't shoot it, don't write it |
| 5 | **Passive scenes** | High | Every scene needs conflict |
| 6 | **Backstory dumps** | High | Reveal through present action |
| 7 | **Info dumps in dialogue** | High | Cut 50% of exposition |
| 8 | **Melodrama without stakes** | Medium | If it doesn't matter, why should we watch? |
| 9 | **Convenient plot resolution** | High | Problems should be solved creatively |
| 10 | **Writing without outline** | Medium | Outline prevents structural collapse |

## 10.2 Structural Mistakes

- **No inciting incident** (page 10-15): Story doesn't start
- **No midpoint reversal**: Middle drags
- **No act break complications**: Acts fall flat
- **Climax is action, not decision**: Character must choose
- **Resolution doesn't satisfy**: Set up payoff throughout

## 10.3 Character Mistakes

- **Passive protagonist**: Character wants something and acts
- **Flat villain/antagonist**: They believe they're right
- **Same-voice dialogue**: Each character should sound distinct
- **No character arc**: Change from beginning to end
- **Supporting characters as function**: Give them desires too

## 10.4 Format & Presentation Mistakes

- **Wrong font**: Courier 12-point only
- **Cuts and transitions in wrong place**: RIGHT-ALIGNED
- **Sluglines not in caps**: INT./EXT. - LOCATION - TIME
- **No title page standard**: Title, Written by, contact info
- **Overly long scripts**: Industry has page counts for a reason

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Logline is compelling and specific
- [ ] Protagonist has clear want/need by page 10
- [ ] Inciting incident by page 15
- [ ] Midpoint has major reversal
- [ ] Every scene has conflict
- [ ] Dialogue sounds distinct per character
- [ ] No exposition without conflict
- [ ] Climax is character decision, not event
- [ ] Resolution addresses all story threads
- [ ] Written in proper format
- [ ] Proofread for spelling/grammar
- [ ] Page count within industry standard
- [ ] Readable as a complete story
