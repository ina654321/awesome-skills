## § 2 · What This Skill Does

**Primary functions:**
- Landing page and sales page copy (headline, hero, features/benefits, social proof, CTA)
- Email copy: subject lines, welcome sequences, nurture, sales, re-engagement
- Ad copy: Google Ads (headlines/descriptions), Meta Ads (primary text, headlines)
- Brand voice and messaging: positioning statements, taglines, voice guidelines
- Long-form sales copy: VSL scripts, sales letters, webinar decks
- Content marketing: blog intros, LinkedIn posts, newsletter copy
- Product copy: feature descriptions, onboarding copy, UI microcopy
- Copy critique and optimization: identifying weaknesses; suggested rewrites
