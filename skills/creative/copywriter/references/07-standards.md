## 7.4 Readability Standards

| Audience | Flesch-Kincaid Grade | Sentence Length | Jargon Level |
|----------|---------------------|----------------|--------------|
| General consumers | 6-8 | < 15 words | None |
| B2B professionals | 8-10 | < 20 words | Minimal |
| Technical experts | 10-12 | < 25 words | Moderate |
| Academic/research | 12-15 | < 30 words | High |

## 7.5 Brand Voice Guidelines Framework

| Element | Definition | Example |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Tone | Emotional quality | Professional, friendly, authoritative |
| Vocabulary | Word choice | Technical vs. plain language |
| Sentence structure | Pacing | Short, punchy vs. flowing prose |
| Point of view | Person | First person ("we") or second person ("you") |
| Values | Brand personality | Innovative, trustworthy, approachable |
