# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Job Intake Process

### Step 1: Requirements Gathering
- Meet with hiring manager
- Document: role, responsibilities, success metrics
- Confirm: must-have vs. nice-to-have skills
- Establish: compensation band, level, team structure
- Set: interview panel, timeline, hiring manager availability

### Step 2: Job Description Creation
- Write compelling role summary
- List 3-5 key responsibilities
- Include impact: what will this person achieve?
- Remove biased language
- Set clear qualifications

### Step 3: Sourcing Strategy
- Identify target companies
- Build Boolean strings for LinkedIn
- Create outreach sequence
- Activate employee referrals
- Post to appropriate channels

## 8.2 Candidate Journey

### Phone Screen (20-30 min)
1. Introduction and rapport building
2. Interest confirmation
3. Basic qualifications check
4. Compensation expectations
5. Availability and timeline
6. Red flag assessment

### Technical Interview (45-60 min)
1. Structured format with rubrics
2. Job-relevant exercises
3. Problem-solving demonstration
4. Collaboration assessment
5. Ask candidate questions

### Final Round (1-2 hours)
1. Leadership/senior stakeholder meetings
2. Deeper role-specific evaluation
3. Culture fit assessment
4. Team interaction
5. Offer discussion

## 8.3 Offer and Negotiation

### Step 1: Pre-Offer Alignment
- Confirm candidate interest and expectations
- Verify compensation requirements
- Check references
- Align with hiring manager on offer strategy

### Step 2: Offer Delivery
- Deliver verbally first (phone call)
- Walk through total compensation
- Allow time for consideration
- Be available for questions

### Step 3: Negotiation Handling
- Listen to concerns without defending
- Gather specific counter details
- Work with hiring manager on flexibility
- Close quickly once agreed

## 8.4 Post-Hire Follow-Up

### Week 1-2
- Check in with new hire
- Address any onboarding issues
- Communicate with rejected candidates

### Week 30/60/90
- Success check-ins with hiring manager
- Address early warning signs
- Gather feedback on process
- Complete hiring manager review
