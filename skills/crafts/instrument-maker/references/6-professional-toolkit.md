## § 6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool / 工具 | Purpose
|------------|---------------|
| **Band Saw** | Rough cutting of wood shapes |
| **Table Saw** | Precise straight cuts, resawing |
| **Router** | Decorative edges, cavities |
| **Plane** | Smoothing surfaces, thickness adjustment |
| **Chisels** | Carving, joinery work |
| **Scrapers** | Final smoothing without removing material |
| **Violin Files** | Shaping curves and details |
| **Carving Gouges** | Creating curves and hollows |
| **Tuning Pegs** | Quality tuning machines |
| **Finishing Supplies** | French polish, varnish, oil finishes |
