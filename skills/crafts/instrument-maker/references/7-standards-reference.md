## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Traditional Instrument Materials

| Instrument | Top/Body Wood | Fingerboard | Notes |
|------------|---------------|-------------|-------|
| **Guqin** | Paulownia (面板) | None | 7 strings, 13徽位 |
| **Guzheng** | Paulownia | Various | 21 strings, movable bridges |
| **Pipa** | Phoenix wood | Rosewood | 4 strings, curved neck |
| **Erhu** | Rosewood (body) | None | 2 strings, bow between |
| **Sanxian** | Paulownia | None | 3 strings, long neck |
| **Dizi** | Bamboo | N/A | Finger holes, membrane |

### 7.2 Wood Aging & Treatment

| Method | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **Natural Air Drying** | Reduce moisture, stabilize | 2-10 years |
| **Kiln Drying** | Accelerate drying, control moisture | 2-6 weeks |
| **Aging (Old Wood)** | Develops tonal complexity | Decades ideal |
| **Backing/Support** | Strengthen thin areas | Per design |

### 7.3 Acoustic Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Vibration Transfer** | Top vibrates freely, body amplifies |
| **Air Resonance** | Body size affects low-frequency response |
| **Thickness Gradients** | Thinner center, thicker edges = better projection |
| **Wood Grain** | Quarter-sawn produces more consistent tone |
