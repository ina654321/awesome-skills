# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Flower Care & Handling

### Storage Standards
| Flower Type | Temperature | Water | Special Notes |
|------------|-------------|-------|--------------|
| **Roses** | 34-36°F | Deep | Remove guard petals |
| **Tulips** | 34°F | Shallow | Can continue growing |
| **Lilies** | 36°F | Medium | Remove anthers (pollen) |
| **Orchids** | 55-65°F | Low | Misting ok, not too wet |
| **Sunflowers** | 36-40°F | Deep | Condition overnight |

### Conditioning Steps
1. Remove foliage below water line
2. Cut stems at 45° angle
3. Use clean bucket and fresh water
4. Add flower food solution
5. Let hydrate 2-4 hours before arranging

## 7.2 Design Principles

### Color Theory
| Combination | Effect | Usage |
|-------------|--------|-------|
| **Analogous** | Harmonious, calming | Garden arrangements |
| **Complementary** | Vibrant, high contrast | Special occasions |
| **Monochromatic** | Elegant, sophisticated | Weddings, corporate |
| **Neutral + accent** | Modern, fresh | Contemporary design |

### Form and Balance
- **Symmetrical**: Formal, traditional arrangements
- **Asymmetrical**: Modern, natural arrangements
- **Ikebana**: Minimalist, line-focused Japanese style

## 7.3 Wedding Florals

### Standard Components
| Item | Typical Size | Stems Needed |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Bridal bouquet** | 8-10" diameter | 25-40 |
| **Bridesmaid bouquet** | 6-8" diameter | 15-25 |
| **Boutonniere** | 1-2 stems | 3-5 |
| **Ceremony arch** | 6-8 ft | 200-400 |
| **Centerpieces** | 6-10" | 15-30 each |

### Pricing Factors
- Seasonality (peak vs. off-peak flowers)
- Complexity of design
- Labor for installation
- Rental vs. purchase items
- Delivery and setup
