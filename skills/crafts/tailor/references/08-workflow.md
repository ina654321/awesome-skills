# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Client Consultation

### Step 1: Initial Assessment
- Greet client and build rapport
- Understand the garment's purpose
- Discuss client's expectations
- Assess client's body shape and posture

### Step 2: Examination
- Inspect garment construction quality
- Check fabric condition
- Identify previous alterations
- Note potential challenges

### Step 3: Taking Measurements
- Measure client if making new garment
- Measure garment if altering existing piece
- Document measurements clearly
- Note asymmetry or special considerations

### Step 4: Recommendations
- Explain what can be altered
- Provide honest assessment of outcome
- Discuss options and trade-offs
- Give time and cost estimate

## 8.2 Alteration Process

### Simple Alterations (15-30 min)
1. Mark new length
2. Remove original hem
3. Cut to new length
4. Fold and press
5. Stitch new hem (machine or hand)

### Medium Alterations (30-60 min)
1. Mark alteration line
2. Remove stitching at alteration point
3. Adjust pattern pieces
4. Re-cut if needed
5. Reassemble and press
6. Finish seams

### Complex Alterations (1-2+ hours)
1. Full pattern analysis
2. Disassemble garment partially
3. Alter multiple pattern pieces
4. Reconstruct and fit
5. Multiple fittings may be needed
6. Finish all edges properly

## 8.3 Fitting Process

### Initial Fitting
- Try garment on client
- Mark necessary changes with chalk
- Check balance and proportions
- Note movement and comfort
- Allow client to walk and sit

### Intermediate Fitting (if needed)
- Review previous changes
- Check fit progress
- Make additional adjustments
- Refine details

### Final Fitting
- Verify all alterations complete
- Check symmetry
- Test movement and comfort
- Press entire garment
- Discuss care instructions

## 8.4 Completion

### Quality Check
- All stitches secure
- Seams lie flat
- Pattern matches at seams
- No puckering or pulling
- Proper pressing throughout

### Client Handoff
- Show completed work
- Demonstrate any special care
- Explain what to expect over time
- Offer follow-up appointment if needed
- Process payment and schedule pickup
