# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Fabric Types & Characteristics

### Natural Fibers
| Fiber | Characteristics | Care | Best For |
|-------|----------------|------|----------|
| **Cotton** | Breathable, absorbent, wrinkles | Machine washable | Casual, summer |
| **Linen** | Light, absorbent, wrinkles | Dry clean or hand wash | Summer suits, blazers |
| **Wool** | Warm, resilient, naturally wrinkle-resistant | Dry clean | Suits, coats |
| **Silk** | Luxurious, delicate, temperature regulating | Dry clean | Formal, linings |

### Synthetic Fibers
| Fiber | Characteristics | Care | Best For |
|-------|----------------|------|----------|
| **Polyester** | Wrinkle-resistant, durable | Machine washable | Workwear, blends |
| **Nylon** | Strong, lightweight | Machine washable | Activewear, outerwear |
| **Spandex** | Stretchy, form-fitting | Hand wash | Stretch garments |

### Blends
- **Poly-cotton**: Easy care, casual
- **Wool-polyester**: Reduced cost, increased durability
- **Silk-cotton**: Luxury with practicality

## 7.2 Measurement Standards

### Key Body Measurements
| Measurement | Location | Allowance |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| **Chest** | Around fullest part | 4-6" for jacket |
| **Waist** | Natural waistline | 2-4" for pants |
| **Hips** | Fullest part | 4-6" for pants |
| **Inseam** | Crotch to floor | Based on heel height |
| **Outseam** | Waist to floor | Based on heel height |
| **Sleeve** | Shoulder to wrist | With arm slightly bent |
| **Back length** | Neck to waist | For jacket length |

### Fit Standards
| Garment | Key Fit Points |
|---------|---------------|
| **Suit jacket** | Shoulder seam at shoulder tip, button lies flat, no pulling |
| **Dress shirt** | Collar allows two fingers, no pulling across chest |
| **Trousers** | Sit at natural waist, no bunching, appropriate break |

## 7.3 Alteration Types

### Common Alterations
| Alteration | Difficulty | Time |
|------------|------------|------|
| **Hem** | Easy | 15-30 min |
| **Waist take-in** | Medium | 30-60 min |
| **Sleeve shorten** | Medium | 30-45 min |
| **Lateral seat adjustment** | Hard | 1-2 hours |
| **Major restructuring** | Expert | 2+ hours |

### What's Alterable
| Usually Alterable | Usually Not |
|------------------|-------------|
| Lengths | Silhouette change |
| Waist in | Major style change |
| Sleeve length | Poor original fit |
| Some structural | Seams already altered |

## 7.4 Professional Resources

### Industry Standards
- **RTW (Ready-to-Wear)**: Standard sizing with limited customization
- **MTM (Made-to-Measure)**: Adjusted patterns for better fit
- **Bespoke**: Full custom pattern for unique fit

### Equipment
- Industrial sewing machines
- Serge for finishing
- Button machines
- Steamers and presses
- Fitting forms
