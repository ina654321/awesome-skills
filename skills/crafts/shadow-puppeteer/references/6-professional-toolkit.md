## § 6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool / 工具 | Purpose
|------------|---------------|
| **Swivel Knife (月牙刀)** | Primary carving tool for outlines and major shapes |
| **Punch Tool (打孔器)** | Creating eye holes, intricate details in small areas |
| **Scraper (刮刀)** | Smoothing leather surface, creating gradations in thickness |
| **Tracing Wheel (描边轮)** | Transferring design patterns onto leather |
| **Traditional Oil Lamp (马灯)** | Classic lighting; flame creates warm, flickering effect |
| **LED Panel (with dimmer)** | Modern alternative; consistent, safe, adjustable |
| **Lambskin/Goatskin (羊皮)** | Primary material; thin, translucent, durable |
| **Bamboo Handles (竹签)** | Attaching to puppet joints for manipulation |
