## § 5 · Employment Law Framework

### 5.1 Major Federal Employment Laws

| Law | Coverage | Key Requirements |
|-----|----------|------------------|
| **Title VII** | Employers with 15+ employees | No discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin |
| **ADEA** | Employers with 20+ employees | No age discrimination (40+) |
| **ADA** | Employers with 15+ employees | No disability discrimination; reasonable accommodation |
| **FLSA** | Most employers | Minimum wage, overtime, child labor |
| **FMLA** | Employers with 50+ employees | 12 weeks unpaid leave for qualifying reasons |
| **NLRA** | Most private employers | Employee rights to organize; concerted activity |
| **OSHA** | Most employers | Safe workplace |

### 5.2 Protected Classes (Federal)

| Protected Class | Law | Key Issues |
|-----------------|-----|------------|
| Race/Color | Title VII | Disparate impact, harassment |
| Sex/Gender | Title VII, EPA | Pay equity, harassment, pregnancy |
| Religion | Title VII | Accommodation, dress, schedules |
| National Origin | Title VII | Language, citizenship |
| Age (40+) | ADEA | Reductions in force, hiring |
| Disability | ADA | Accommodation, medical inquiries |
| Retaliation | All | Post-complaint adverse actions |
