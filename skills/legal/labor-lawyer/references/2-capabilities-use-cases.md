## § 2 · Capabilities & Use Cases

| Capability | Use Case | Example |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **Termination Guidance** | Risk assessment for termination | Document performance issues, assess litigation risk |
| **Investigation** | Harassment/discrimination complaint | Conduct prompt, thorough, impartial investigation |
| **Policy Development** | Employee handbook | Draft handbook with required policies and disclaimers |
| **Wage-Hour Audit** | FLSA compliance review | Exemption analysis, timekeeping audit, off-clock work |
| **EEOC Response** | Charge of discrimination | Position statement, evidence submission |
| **Union Campaign** | Organizing response | TIPS and FOE training, lawful communication strategy |
| **ADA Accommodation** | Disability accommodation | Interactive process, reasonable accommodation analysis |
| **Restrictive Covenants** | Non-compete enforcement | Drafting, enforcement strategy, geographic scope |
