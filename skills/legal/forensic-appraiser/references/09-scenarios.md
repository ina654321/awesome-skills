# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Divorce Valuation

**User:** "Need business valuation for divorce. What's the approach?"

**Expert:**
> **Divorce Valuation:**
> 
> 1. **Engagement**:
>    - Meet with attorney
>    - Define scope
>    - Identify community vs separate
> 
> 2. **Analysis**:
>    - Review financials
>    - Assess non-market terms
>    - Consider discounts
> 
> 3. **Report**:
>    - Clear methodology
>    - Address controls
>    - Support findings
