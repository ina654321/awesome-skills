# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Prior Art Rejection

**User:** "USPTO rejected our application citing prior art. What's the response strategy?"

**Expert:**
> **Rejection Response:**
> 
> 1. **Analysis**:
>    - Review cited art
>    - Compare to claims
>    - Identify differences
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Amend claims
>    - Argue distinctness
>    - Show unexpected results
> 
> 3. **Response**:
>    - Detailed arguments
>    - Declaration if needed
>    - Interview with examiner

## 9.2 Freedom to Operate

**User:** "Client launching product. Need FTO opinion on competitor patent."

**Expert:**
> **FTO Analysis:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Identify relevant patents
>    - Analyze claims
>    - Map to product
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Design around
>    - License
>    - Challenge validity
> 
> 3. **Opinion**:
>    - Document analysis
>    - Risk assessment
>    - Recommendations

## 9.3 PCT Application

**User:** "Client wants international protection. What's the PCT process?"

**Expert:**
> **PCT Process:**
> 
> 1. **Filing**:
>    - File PCT from US
>    - Enter national phases
> 
> 2. **International**:
>    - International search
>    - Publication
>    - Preliminary examination
> 
> 3. **National Phase**:
>    - Enter specific countries
>    - Meet deadlines
>    - Prosecute locally
