# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Regulatory Compliance Standards

### Federal Frameworks
- [Sarbanes-Oxley Act](https://www.soxlaw.com/): Public company financial oversight
- [Dodd-Frank Act](https://www.dodd-frank.com/): Financial reform and consumer protection
- [HIPAA](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/): Healthcare information privacy
- [GDPR](https://gdpr.eu/): EU data protection (extraterritorial reach)
- [PCI-DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/): Payment card data security

### Industry-Specific Regulations
| Regulation | Industry | Focus |
|------------|----------|-------|
| **SOX** | Public companies | Financial reporting |
| **HIPAA** | Healthcare | PHI protection |
| **GLBA** | Financial services | Consumer data |
| **FTC Act §5** | All industries | Unfair/deceptive practices |
| **FCPA** | Cross-border | Anti-bribery |
| **OFAC sanctions** | International | Sanctions compliance |

## 7.2 Compliance Program Frameworks

- [Federal Sentencing Guidelines §8B2.1](https://www.ussc.gov/guidelines/2018-guidelines-manual/2018-chapter-8): Effective compliance programs
- [ISO 37301](https://www.iso.org/standard=75033): Compliance management systems
- [OCEG GRC Capability Model](https://www.oceg.org/): Governance, risk, compliance integration
- [COSO ERM Framework](https://www.coso.org/): Enterprise risk management

## 7.3 Anti-Corruption Standards

- [FCPA](https://www.justice.gov/criminal/fraud/fcpa): Foreign Corrupt Practices Act
- [UK Bribery Act 2010](https://www.gov.uk/bribery-corporate-offence): Corporate liability for failure to prevent
- [OECD Anti-Bribery Convention](https://www.oecd.org/daf/anti-bribery/): International anti-bribery standards
- [ISO 37001](https://www.iso.org/standard/71682.html): Anti-bribery management systems
