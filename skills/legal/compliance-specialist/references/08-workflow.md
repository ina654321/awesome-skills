# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Compliance Risk Assessment

```
Phase 1: Identify
├── Map business activities
├── Identify applicable regulations
├── Catalog data flows
└── Inventory third-party relationships

Phase 2: Assess
├── Likelihood of violation (1-5 scale)
├── Severity of potential penalty
├── Reputational impact assessment
└── Calculate risk score (likelihood × severity)

Phase 3: Prioritize
├── High-risk areas → immediate remediation
├── Medium-risk → monitor and control
└── Low-risk → document and accept
```

## 8.2 Policy Development Workflow

1. **Identify Need**: Gap analysis, regulatory change, incident
2. **Draft Policy**: Legal counsel review, stakeholder input
3. **Internal Review**: Affected departments, HR, IT
4. **Legal Approval**: Counsel approval of legal sufficiency
5. **Executive Sign-off**: CCO and CEO or board approval
6. **Distribution**: Training, acknowledgment tracking
7. **Implementation**: System updates, process changes
8. **Monitoring**: attestations, audit testing

## 8.3 Incident Response

```
Suspected Violation Detected:
├── Immediate: Contain and document
├── 24 hrs: Notify CCO/legal counsel
├── 48 hrs: Investigate scope and severity
├── 72 hrs: Decision on regulatory disclosure
│   ├── Self-disclose to regulator?
│   ├── Is disclosure mandatory? (SOX, HIPAA, etc.)
│   └── Voluntary vs. mandatory distinction
├── Remediation: Fix root cause
└── Lessons learned: Update controls
```

## 8.4 Third-Party Due Diligence

| Tier | Risk Level | Due Diligence |
|------|------------|---------------|
| **Critical** | High | Full background, financial review, site visit |
| **Standard** | Medium | Questionnaire, sanctions screening |
| **Low** | Low | Basic questionnaire, annual re-certification |

- [OFAC SDN List](https://www.treasury.gov/ofac/sdn): Sanctions screening
- [SAM.gov](https://sam.gov/): Government exclusion database
- [WorldBank debarment](https://www.worldbank.org/en/projects-operations/procurement/debarment): Debarred parties
