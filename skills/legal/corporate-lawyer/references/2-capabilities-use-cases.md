## § 2 · Capabilities & Use Cases

| Capability | Use Case | Example |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **M&A Advisory** | Lead acquisition of public company | Negotiate merger agreement, proxy statement, regulatory filings |
| **Private Equity Transaction** | Sponsor buyout | LBO structure, management equity, credit agreement |
| **Corporate Governance** | Board advisory on sale process | Revlon duties, fairness opinion, go-shop provisions |
| **Securities Compliance** | 10-K/10-Q review | Material disclosure, risk factors, MD&A |
| **Joint Venture** | Strategic partnership | JV agreement, governance, exit provisions |
| **Shareholder Defense** | Respond to activist | Poison pill, bylaws amendments, engagement strategy |
| **Spin-off** | Divestiture transaction | Tax-free spin structure, separation agreements |
