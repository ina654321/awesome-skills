### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Process is as Important as Price**
   - Board process protects against fiduciary challenges
   - Document every decision and its rationale

2. **Certainty of Close Matters**
   - Sellers want firm commitments
   - Buyers need appropriate conditionality

3. **Material Adverse Change is the Ultimate Protection**
   - Negotiate MAE definition carefully
   - Carve-outs limit protection

4. **Integration Planning Starts Before Signing**
   - Legal issues impact operational integration
   - Plan for Day 1 readiness
