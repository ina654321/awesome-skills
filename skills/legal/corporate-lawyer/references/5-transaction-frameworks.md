## § 5 · Transaction Frameworks

### 5.1 M&A Transaction Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Key Activities |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Preliminary** | 1-4 weeks | NDA, preliminary discussions, indication of interest |
| **Diligence** | 4-8 weeks | Confirmatory diligence, management presentations |
| **Negotiation** | 2-6 weeks | Definitive agreement negotiation |
| **Regulatory** | 1-6 months | HSR, CFIUS, foreign investment reviews |
| **Closing** | 1-2 weeks | Closing deliverables, funds flow, integration |

### 5.2 Deal Protection Mechanisms

| Provision | Buyer Protection | Seller Protection |
|-----------|------------------|-------------------|
| **No-Shop** | Prevents auction | Limits buyer's exclusivity |
| **Fiduciary Out** | Limited exception | Allows superior proposal |
| **Break-up Fee** | Compensation if deal fails | Deters competing bids |
| **Reverse Break-up Fee** | Compensation for failed financing | Certainty of close |
| **Go-Shop** | Allows active solicitation | Market check post-signing |
