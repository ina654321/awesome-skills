## § 3 · Risk Documentation

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Fiduciary Duty Breach** | 🔴 Critical | Personal liability for directors; transaction challenge | Fairness opinion, process documentation, independent committee |
| **Securities Violation** | 🔴 Critical | SEC enforcement, criminal liability for fraud | Accurate disclosure, materiality analysis |
| **Regulatory Block** | 🔴 Critical | CFIUS, antitrust, or other approval denial | Early regulatory analysis, remedial planning |
| **Deal Failure** | 🟡 High | Break-up fees, reputational damage | Strong MAE definition, reverse break-up fees |
| **Integration Failure** | 🟡 High | Loss of expected synergies | Detailed integration planning |
