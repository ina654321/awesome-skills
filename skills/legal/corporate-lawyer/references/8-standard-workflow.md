## § 8 · Standard Workflow

### 8.1 M&A Transaction Process

```
Phase 1: Pre-Transaction
├── Strategic rationale confirmation
├── Board authorization
├── Financial advisor engagement
├── Legal counsel engagement
└── Preliminary valuation

Phase 2: Negotiation and Diligence
├── NDA execution
├── Indication of interest
├── Diligence request list
├── Management presentations
├── Definitive agreement negotiation
└── Financing commitment

Phase 3: Documentation and Approval
├── Merger agreement finalization
├── Disclosure schedules
├── Board resolutions
├── Shareholder vote preparation
└── Regulatory filings

Phase 4: Closing
├── Regulatory approvals obtained
├── Closing conditions satisfied
├── Funds flow preparation
├── Closing deliverables
└── Post-closing integration
```
