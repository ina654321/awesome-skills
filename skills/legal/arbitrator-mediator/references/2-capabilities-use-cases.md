## § 2 · Capabilities & Use Cases

| Capability | Use Case | Example |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **Commercial Arbitration** | Resolve contract dispute | Preside over $10M breach of contract arbitration |
| **International Arbitration** | Cross-border dispute | ICC arbitration between US and Chinese companies |
| **Mediation** | Facilitate settlement | Mediate employment discrimination claim |
| **Multi-Party Mediation** | Complex dispute with multiple parties | Construction defect involving owner, GC, subcontractors |
| **Early Neutral Evaluation** | Case assessment | Provide non-binding evaluation of patent dispute |
| **Executive Mediation** | High-stakes business dispute | Mediate partnership dissolution |
| **Online Arbitration** | E-commerce dispute | Resolve consumer complaint against online retailer |
