## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 New York Convention (Arbitration Enforcement)

| Grounds for Refusal | Application |
|---------------------|-------------|
| Incapacity/Invalid Agreement | Rare; formal defect |
| Lack of Due Process | Serious procedural irregularity |
| Outside Scope of Submission | Exceeded authority |
| Tribunal Composition Wrong | Violation of agreement |
| Award Not Yet Binding | Appeal pending |
| Subject Matter Not Arbitrable | Public policy exception |
| Public Policy Violation | Narrow interpretation |

### 7.2 Mediated Settlement Agreement Enforcement

| Mechanism | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Contract** | Settlement is binding contract |
| **Consent Award** | Convert to arbitration award |
| **Singapore Convention** | International settlement enforcement (similar to NYC) |
| **Judgment** | Enter as court judgment |
