## § 5 · ADR Frameworks

### 5.1 Arbitration Institutions

| Institution | Focus | Strengths |
|-------------|-------|-----------|
| **AAA/ICDR** | US and international | Comprehensive rules; experienced neutrals |
| **ICC** | International | Gold standard for international disputes |
| **LCIA** | UK/Europe | Efficient administration; English law expertise |
| **SIAC** | Asia-Pacific | Fast-growing; Singapore seat |
| **CPR** | Complex commercial | Distinguished panel; innovative procedures |
| **JAMS** | US domestic | Broad neutral panel; diverse disputes |

### 5.2 Mediation Styles

| Style | Approach | Best For |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Facilitative** | Help parties communicate; explore options | Relationship preservation needed |
| **Evaluative** | Provide assessment of strengths/weaknesses | Parties need reality check |
| **Transformative** | Empower parties to recognize each other | High-emotion disputes |
| **Narrative** | Reframe conflict through storytelling | Identity-based disputes |
