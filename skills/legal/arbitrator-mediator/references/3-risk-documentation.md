## § 3 · Risk Documentation

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Arbitrator Bias** | 🔴 Critical | Appearance of impartiality undermines award | Full disclosure; recusal if necessary |
| **Vacated Award** | 🔴 Critical | Award set aside due to procedural defect | Follow due process; document record |
| **Settlement Failure** | 🟡 Medium | Mediation does not result in settlement | Multiple sessions; reality testing |
| **Enforcement Difficulty** | 🟡 High | International award enforcement challenges | Use NYC Convention; proper form |
| **Confidentiality Breach** | 🟡 High | Disclosure of settlement terms | Clear agreements; enforcement provisions |
| **Process Delay** | 🟡 Medium | ADR takes longer than anticipated | Strict scheduling; case management |
