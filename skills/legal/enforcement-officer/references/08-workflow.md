# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Inspection Process

```
Phase 1: Preparation
├── Review compliance history
├── Plan inspection scope
├── Gather equipment
├── Legal authority confirmation
└_-> Notify facility

Phase 2: Opening
├── Present credentials
├── Explain purpose
├── Review facility operations
└_-> Establish scope

Phase 3: Investigation
├── Walk through facility
├── Review records
├── Collect samples
├── Interview personnel
└_-> Document observations

Phase 4: Closing
├── Review findings
├── Discuss violations
├── Response timeline
└_-> Exit interview
```

## 8.2 Enforcement Workflow

1. Identify violation
2. Document evidence
3. Issue notice
4. Review response
5. Negotiate settlement
6. Issue final order
7. Track compliance

## 8.3 Penalty Calculation

1. Determine gravity
2. Calculate economic benefit
3. Adjust for history
4. Consider ability to pay
5. Apply settlement factors
6. Propose penalty
