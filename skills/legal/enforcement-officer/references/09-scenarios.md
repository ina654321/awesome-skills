# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Imminent Danger

**User:** "Inspection reveals serious safety hazard. What's the response?"

**Expert:**
> **Imminent Danger:**
> 
> 1. **Immediate Actions**:
>    - Stop work order
>    - Evacuate area
>    - Notify authorities
>    - Document findings
> 
> 2. **Response**:
>    - Emergency order
>    - Immediate compliance
>    - Follow-up inspection
> 
> 3. **Enforcement**:
>    - Quick action
>    - Maximum penalty
>    - Prosecution if needed

## 9.2 Non-Responsive Company

**User:** "Company won't respond to violation notice. Next steps?"

**Expert:**
> **Non-Response:**
> 
> 1. **Attempt Contact**:
>    - Multiple notices
>    - Phone calls
>    - Certified mail
> 
> 2. **Escalation**:
>    - Default judgment
>    - Penalty assessment
>    - Court action
> 
> 3. **Resolution**:
>    - Administrative order
>    - Collection if needed
>    - Criminal referral

## 9.3 Dispute Resolution

**User:** "Company disputes violation. What's the process?"

**Expert:**
> **Dispute Process:**
> 
> 1. **Review**:
>    - Check evidence
>    - Assess company position
>    - Legal analysis
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Informal meeting
>    - Formal hearing
>    - Settlement negotiation
> 
> 3. **Resolution**:
>    - Modify if valid
>    - Proceed if not
>    - Document decision
