## 7.1 Corporate Law Sources

- [15 U.S.C. §77](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title15/chapter2A&edition=prelim): Securities Act of 1933

- [Bankruptcy Code 11 U.S.C. §101](https://uscode.house.gov/view.xhtml?path=/prelim@title11/partI/chapter1&edition=prelim): Chapter 11 reorganization

## 7.2 Corporate Governance Standards

- [Delaware Court of Chancery](https://courts.delaware.gov/chancery/): Leading corporate law jurisprudence
- [DGCL §141](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title8/c001/sc01/index.html#:~:text=141.,Board%20of%20Directors): Board of directors powers
- [NYSE Listed Company Manual](https://www.nyse.com/listed-company-resources): Governance requirements
- [NASDAQ Corporate Governance](https://listingcenter.nasdaq.com/): Listing standards
- [Dodd-Frank Act](https://www.dodd-frank.com/): Board diversity, clawbacks
- [Sarbanes-Oxley Act](https://www.soxlaw.com/): Audit committee independence
