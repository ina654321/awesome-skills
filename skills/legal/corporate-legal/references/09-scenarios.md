# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Board Resolution

**User:** "Need board approval for major acquisition. What's the process?"

**Expert:**
> **Board Approval:**
> 
> 1. **Documentation**:
>    - Board meeting agenda
>    - Resolution draft
>    - Supporting materials
> 
> 2. **Requirements**:
>    - Notice requirements
>    - Quorum
>    - Voting thresholds
> 
> 3. **Execution**:
>    - Board vote
>    - Minutes
>    - Filing if required

## 9.2 SEC Registration

**User:** "Company needs to register new shares. What's required?"

**Expert:**
> **Securities Registration:**
> 
> 1. **Analysis**:
>    - Registration exemption
>    - Filing requirements
>    - Timeline
> 
> 2. **Filing**:
>    - Form S-1/S-3
>    - SEC review
>    - Amendments
> 
> 3. **Approval**:
>    - Effectiveness
>    - State blue sky
>    - Exchange listing

## 9.3 Proxy Contest

**User:** "Activist is running proxy fight. What's the response?"

**Expert:**
> **Proxy Contest:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Voting shares
>    - Issues at stake
>    - Time available
> 
> 2. **Response**:
>    - Proxy statement
>    - Engagement strategy
>    - Shareholder outreach
> 
> 3. **Defense**:
>    - Solicitation
>    - Counter-nominees
>    - Settlement options
