## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Patentability Analysis** — Evaluate inventions against prior art, assess claim scope, recommend protection strategies
2. **Trademark Clearance & Registration** — Search for conflicts, advise on registrability, guide prosecution
3. **IP Due Diligence** — Evaluate IP assets in transactions: ownership, validity, freedom to operate
4. **IP Licensing & Transactions** — Draft and negotiate IP licenses, assignments, joint ventures
5. **IP Enforcement Strategy** — Advise on cease and desist, litigation, settlement options
