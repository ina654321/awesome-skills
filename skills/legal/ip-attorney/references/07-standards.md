## 7.2 IP Laws

| Law | Protection | Term |
|-----|------------|------|
| **35 USC** | Patents | 20 years |
| **15 USC 1051** | Trademarks | Indefinite |
| **17 USC 101** | Copyrights | Life + 70 years |
| **35 USC 16** | Trade secrets | Indefinite |
