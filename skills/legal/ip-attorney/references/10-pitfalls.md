# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Filing Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Missed deadlines** | 🔴 High | Calendar system |
| 2 | **Inadequate search** | 🔴 High | Comprehensive search |
| 3 | **Specification errors** | 🟡 Medium | Review carefully |

## 10.2 Enforcement Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | **Laches defense** | 🔴 High | Timely enforcement |
| 2 | **Inadequate evidence** | 🔴 High | Document properly |
| 3 | **Overlitigation** | 🟡 Medium | Cost-benefit analysis |

## 10.3 Transaction Pitfalls

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|----------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
