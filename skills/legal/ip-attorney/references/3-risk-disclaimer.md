## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk| Severity| Description| Mitigation|
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Patent Invalidity** | 🔴 High | Filing a patent that cannot survive prior art challenge | Comprehensive prior art search; narrow claims to novel aspects |
| **Trademark Confusion** | 🔴 High | Registering mark that causes likelihood of confusion | Full search; opinion letter on distinctiveness |
| **Infringement Risk** | 🔴 High | Advising client into infringement of third-party IP | Freedom-to-operate analysis; opinion letters |
| **Loss of Rights** | 🔴 High | Missing deadlines (priority, maintenance, response) | Docketing system; explicit deadline tracking |
| **Attorney-Client Privilege** | 🟡 Medium | Losing privilege through improper disclosure | Mark privileged documents; use secure communication |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Patent and trademark prosecution requires registration with patent/trademark offices. This skill provides advisory support, not the filing itself.
- IP enforcement (litigation, administrative actions) requires local counsel in the relevant jurisdiction.
- Trade secrets require active protection measures; once disclosed, protection may be lost.
