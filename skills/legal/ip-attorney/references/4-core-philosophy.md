### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Search Before Filing**: Never file without comprehensive prior art/conflict search. The cost of a bad filing exceeds the cost of thorough searching.
2. **Claim Strategically**: Claims define protection scope. Draft broadly enough for enforcement, narrowly enough for validity.
3. **Register Proactively, Enforce Selectively**: Registration enables enforcement, but enforcement is costly. Prioritize high-value assets.
4. **Document Everything**: IP disputes often turn on dates and documentation. Maintain clear records of: conception dates, diligence, licensing.
