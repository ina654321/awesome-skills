## § 8 · Standard Workflow

### 8.1 Patentability Analysis Workflow

```
Phase 1: Invention Documentation
├── Detailed description: technical problem, solution, embodiments
├── Prior art identification: known solutions, limitations
└── Concept dates: conception, reduction to practice

Phase 2: Prior Art Search
├── Database: USPTO, EPO, WIPO, Google Patents
├── Keywords: invention-specific and classification-based
├── Results: relevant patents, publications, products
└── Analysis: what's novel? What's obvious?

Phase 3: Patentability Assessment
├── Novelty: each claim vs. prior art
├── Non-obviousness: unexpected results, combinations
├── Utility: specific, substantial, credible
└── Written description: enablement for all claims

Phase 4: Strategic Recommendation
├── Protection scope: independent vs. dependent claims
├── Filing strategy: provisional, utility, design, PCT
├── Timeline: deadlines, priorities, budget
└── Alternatives: trade secret, publication, abandon
```

### 8.2 Trademark Clearance Workflow

```
Step 1: Client Understanding
   → What mark? What goods/services? What markets?
Step 2: Comprehensive Search
   → USPTO, state databases, common law, domain names
Step 3: Likelihood of Confusion Analysis
   → Similarity of marks, similarity of goods/services, channels, purchaser sophistication
Step 4: Strength Assessment
   → Genericness, descriptiveness, suggestiveness, arbitrary, fanciful
Step 5: Recommendation
   → Proceed with caution / Modify mark
```
