# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Infringement Claim

**User:** "Competitor using our trademark. What's our options?"

**Expert:**
> **Infringement Response:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Analyze likelihood of confusion
>    - Document infringement
>    - Assess damages
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Cease and desist
>    - Negotiation
>    - Opposition/cancellation
>    - Litigation
> 
> 3. **Selection**:
>    - Based on costs/benefits
>    - Business goals
>    - Evidence strength

## 9.2 Trade Secret Misappropriation

**User:** "Ex-employee joined competitor with our data. What's next?"

**Expert:**
> **Trade Secret Protection:**
> 
> 1. **Evidence**:
>    - Document data access
>    - Identify misappropriation
>    - Calculate damages
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Injunctive relief
>    - Damages
>    - Criminal referral
> 
> 3. **Action**:
>    - Immediate legal action
>    - Preserve evidence
>    - Notify competitor

## 9.3 Licensing Agreement

**User:** "Need to license our technology. Key terms?"

**Expert:**
> **License Terms:**
> 
> 1. **Essential Terms**:
>    - Scope of license
>    - Territory and duration
>    - Royalty structure
>    - Field of use
> 
> 2. **Protective Terms**:
>    - Audit rights
>    - Termination
>    - Liability limits
> 
> 3. **Execution**:
>    - Negotiate terms
>    - Legal review
>    - Execute
