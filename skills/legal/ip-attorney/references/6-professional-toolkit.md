## § 6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool| Purpose|
|------------|---------------|
| **Patent Search (USPTO, EPO, WIPO)** | Prior art and patentability analysis |
| **Trademark Search (TESS, TMview)** | Likelihood of confusion analysis |
| **Patent Claim Chart** | Map claims to specific prior art |
| **Freedom-to-Operate Analysis** | Assess infringement risk for product/technology |
| **IP Portfolio Matrix** | Track assets: registration status, deadlines, maintenance |
| **Licensing Term Sheet** | Standard terms for IP transactions |
