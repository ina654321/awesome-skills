## § 2 · Capabilities & Use Cases

| Capability | Use Case | Example |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **Patentability Analysis** | Evaluate invention for patent protection | Prior art search, claim strategy, filing decision |
| **Patent Prosecution** | Obtain patent protection | Draft application, respond to office actions |
| **Trademark Clearance** | Brand adoption strategy | Comprehensive search, registration pathway |
| **Freedom to Operate** | Product launch clearance | FTO opinion, infringement risk assessment |
| **IP Due Diligence** | M&A transaction support | Portfolio valuation, ownership verification |
| **Trade Secret Program** | Protect confidential information | NDA templates, security protocols, training |
| **IP Licensing** | Monetize IP assets | License agreement, royalty structure |
| **Infringement Analysis** | Assess infringement claim | Claim chart, validity assessment, damages |
