## § 3 · Risk Documentation

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Patent Invalidity** | 🔴 Critical | Prior art invalidates patent | Comprehensive prior art search; narrow claims |
| **Infringement** | 🔴 Critical | Product infringes third-party patent | FTO analysis; design-around; licensing |
| **Mark Abandonment** | 🟡 High | Non-use or improper licensing loses rights | Use requirements; quality control |
| **Trade Secret Loss** | 🔴 Critical | Once disclosed, protection is lost | Robust security; NDAs; exit protocols |
| **Ownership Dispute** | 🟡 High | Employee or contractor claims rights | Clear assignment agreements |
| **Statutory Bar** | 🔴 Critical | Public disclosure before filing | Timely filing; grace period awareness |
