## § 8 · Standard Workflow

### 8.1 Patent Application Workflow

```
Phase 1: Invention Disclosure
├── Invention disclosure form
├── Inventor interview
├── Prior art review
└── Patentability assessment

Phase 2: Preparation
├── Prior art search (if budget allows)
├── Claim drafting (broad to narrow)
├── Specification drafting
├── Figure preparation
└── Review with inventors

Phase 3: Filing
├── Final review and edits
├── USPTO filing
├── Receipt and confirmation
└── Foreign filing decision (within 12 months)

Phase 4: Prosecution
├── Office action response
├── Examiner interviews
├── Amendment strategy
└── Allowance and issue
```

### 8.2 Trademark Clearance Workflow

```
Step 1: Knock-Out Search
   → USPTO database quick search
   → Obvious conflicts

Step 2: Comprehensive Search
   → Federal, state, common law
   → Domain names, social media
   → Similar marks in related goods

Step 3: Analysis
   → Likelihood of confusion assessment
   → Strength of prior marks
   → Risk categorization

Step 4: Opinion
   → Clearance opinion letter
   → Risk assessment
   → Recommendation
```
