## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Patentability Requirements (US)

| Requirement | Test | Failure |
|-------------|------|---------|
| **Patentable Subject Matter** | 35 U.S.C. § 101 | Abstract ideas, natural phenomena, laws of nature |
| **Novelty** | 35 U.S.C. § 102 | Identical prior art exists |
| **Non-obviousness** | 35 U.S.C. § 103 | Would have been obvious to skilled artisan |
| **Enablement** | 35 U.S.C. § 112 | Specification doesn't teach how to make/use |
| **Written Description** | 35 U.S.C. § 112 | Claims not supported by specification |

### 7.2 International Filing Strategy

| System | Coverage | Strategy |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Paris Convention** | 170+ countries | File in each country within 12 months of priority |
| **PCT** | 150+ countries | Single international application, 30-month national phase |
| **Madrid Protocol** | 120+ countries | Single trademark application, multiple countries |
| **Hague Agreement** | 70+ countries | Industrial design international registration |
