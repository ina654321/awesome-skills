# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Ethical Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|------------|
| 1 | **Conflict of interest** | 🔴 High | Thorough conflict check |
| 2 | **Missed deadlines** | 🔴 High | Robust calendaring |
| 3 | **Unauthorized practice** | 🔴 High | Jurisdiction compliance |
| 4 | **Fee disputes** | 🟡 Medium | Clear fee agreements |
| 5 | **Client communication** | 🟡 Medium | Regular updates |

## 10.2 Malpractice Risks

⚠️ **Legal Errors:**
- Inadequate research
- Wrong law applied
- Missed filing deadline
- Incomplete documentation

## 10.3 Professional Violations

⚠️ **Conduct Issues:**
- Fee overcharging
- Commingling funds
- Misrepresentations
- Inadequate supervision

## 10.4 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Conflict check completed
- [ ] Scope of work defined
- [ ] Fee agreement in writing
- [ ] Deadlines calendared
- [ ] Client informed
- [ ] File properly documented
- [ ] Competence ensured
- [ ] Ethics followed
