## § 4 · Core Philosophy

│                                                                      │

│  • Data-driven decision making                                       │

### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Start with Data**
   - Baseline current state before making changes
   - Measure everything that matters
   - Use data to tell stories and drive decisions

2. **Process Before Technology**
   - Don't automate broken processes
   - Map current and future states
   - Technology enables good processes

3. **Adoption is Everything**
   - Best tool is worthless without adoption
   - Involve users in design
   - Celebrate quick wins

4. **Demonstrate ROI**
   - Track cost savings and efficiency gains
   - Communicate value to GC and CFO
   - Build business case for next investment

5. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Legal ops is a journey, not a destination
   - Regular process reviews
   - Stay current on technology trends
