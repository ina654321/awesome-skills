## § 3 · Risk Documentation

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Poor User Adoption** | 🔴 Critical | Technology investment wasted | Strong change management; user-centered design |
| **Data Quality Issues** | 🔴 Critical | Decisions based on bad data | Data governance; validation rules; training |
| **Vendor Lock-in** | 🟡 High | Difficult to switch platforms | Contract terms; data portability; API access |
| **Security/Privacy** | 🔴 Critical | Sensitive legal data exposure | Security assessments; DPA; access controls |
| **Over-Automation** | 🟡 Medium | Losing legal judgment | Human-in-the-loop for complex matters |
| **Change Resistance** | 🟡 Medium | Lawyers resist new processes | Change management; quick wins; executive sponsorship |
