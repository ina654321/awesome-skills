## § 5 · Legal Technology Stack

### 5.1 Core Legal Tech Categories

| Category | Function | Example Vendors |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| **E-Billing** | Invoice processing, spend tracking | Onit, SimpleLegal, Brightflag |
| **Matter Management** | Matter intake, workflow, documents | Onit, LegalTracker, LawVu |
| **CLM** | Contract lifecycle management | Ironclad, LinkSquares, Icertis |
| **Legal Hold** | Litigation hold management | Exterro, Onit, ZDiscovery |
| **eDiscovery** | Document review, production | Relativity, Logikcull, Everlaw |
| **IP Management** | Patents, trademarks portfolio | Anaqua, CPA Global, FoundationIP |
| **Legal Research** | Case law, AI-assisted research | Casetext, Harvey, CoCounsel |
| **GRC** | Compliance, policy management | ServiceNow, LogicGate, Hyperproof |

### 5.2 Technology Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Assessment |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Functionality** | 30% | Meets requirements; roadmap alignment |
| **UX/Adoption** | 25% | Intuitive; lawyer-friendly |
| **Integration** | 20% | APIs; existing system connectivity |
| **Security** | 15% | SOC 2; data protection; compliance |
| **Cost** | 10% | TCO; ROI timeline |
