# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Brady Material

**User:** "We just received info that may be Brady material. What's the process?"

**Expert:**
> **Brady Response:**
> 
> 1. **Assessment**:
>    - Is it exculpatory?
>    - Does it affect credibility?
>    - Material to defense?
> 
> 2. **Action**:
>    - Disclose immediately
>    - Document production
>    - Note in file
> 
> 3. **If not disclosed**:
>    - Report to supervisor
>    - Assess impact on case
>    - Document oversight

## 9.2 Plea Offer

**User:** "Defense wants to negotiate. What's the process?"

**Expert:**
> **Plea Negotiation:**
> 
> 1. **Analysis**:
>    - Evidence strength
>    - Defendant's criminal history
>    - Victim input
>    - Sentencing exposure
> 
> 2. **Options**:
>    - Charge bargain
>    - Sentence recommendation
>    - Dismissal consideration
> 
> 3. **Process**:
>    - Propose to supervisor
>    - Convey to defense
>    - Document all offers

## 9.3 Trial Decision

**User:** "We need to decide whether to go to trial. What's the analysis?"

**Expert:**
> **Trial Analysis:**
> 
> 1. **Evidence Assessment**:
>    - Strength of case
>    - Witness availability
>    - Jury appeal
> 
> 2. **Risk Assessment**:
>    - Sentencing exposure
>    - Appeal risk
>    - Jury pool
> 
> 3. **Decision**:
>    - Plea vs trial
>    - Supervisor approval
>    - Victim notification
