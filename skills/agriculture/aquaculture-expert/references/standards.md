## 7. Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Water Quality Standards

| Parameter | Target (Tilapia) | Target (Catfish) | Target (Shrimp) | Crisis Level |
|-----------|-----------------|------------------|-----------------|--------------|
| **DO (mg/L)** | >5.0 | >4.0 | >4.0 | <2.0 |
| **pH** | 6.5-8.5 | 6.5-8.0 | 7.5-8.5 | <6.0 or >9.0 |
| **Ammonia-N (mg/L)** | <0.5 | <0.5 | <0.1 | >5.0 |
| **Nitrite-N (mg/L)** | <1.0 | <1.0 | <0.5 | >5.0 |
| **Temperature (°C)** | 25-30 | 25-30 | 26-32 | <15 or >35 |

### 7.2 Production Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| **FCR** | Feed given / Weight gained | <1.6 (optimal), <2.0 (acceptable) |
| **SGR** | (ln W₂ - ln W₁) / days × 100 | >3%/day (optimal) |
| **Survival Rate** | Harvest count / Stock count × 100 | >80% |
| **Production Density** | Harvest weight / Volume | 30-50 kg/m³ (extensive), 50-100 (intensive) |

### 7.3 Species Selection Guide

| Species | Temp Range | Salinity | FCR | Market | System |
|---------|------------|----------|-----|--------|--------|
| **Tilapia** | 25-30°C | 0-15 ppt | 1.3-1.6 | Domestic/export | Pond, RAS, cage |
| **Catfish** | 25-30°C | 0-5 ppt | 1.5-1.8 | Domestic | Pond, RAS |
| **Shrimp** | 26-32°C | 10-25 ppt | 1.2-1.5 | Export | Pond, biofloc |
| **Sea bass** | 20-28°C | 25-35 ppt | 1.3-1.6 | Premium | Cage, RAS |
