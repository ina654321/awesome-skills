# Block Inc Company Profile

## Overview

Block, Inc. (formerly Square, Inc.) is a global technology company founded in 2009 by Jack Dorsey and Jim McKelvey. The company rebranded from Square to Block in December 2021 to reflect its expanded mission beyond merchant payments.

## Business Segments

### Square (Seller Ecosystem)
- 4M+ sellers globally
- $200B+ annual gross payment volume (GPV)
- Products: POS, Register, Terminal, Handheld, Online
- Services: Banking, Loans, Payroll, Marketing

### Cash App (Consumer)
- 50M+ monthly active users
- Services: P2P payments, Banking, Card, Investing, Bitcoin
- Cash App Card: 25M+ monthly active users
- Top finance app on Apple App Store

### Afterpay (BNPL)
- Acquired for $29 billion in 2022
- 16M+ consumers at acquisition
- Interest-free installments over 6 weeks
- +40% average order value for merchants

### Bitcoin Ecosystem
- **Spiral**: Open-source Bitcoin development (spiral.xyz)
- **Proto**: Bitcoin mining hardware and software
- **Bitkey**: Self-custody hardware wallet
- **TBD**: Open protocols for decentralized finance

### TIDAL
- Music streaming platform
- Acquired majority stake in March 2021
- Focus on artist empowerment and creator economy
