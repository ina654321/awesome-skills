## Founders

### Vlad Tenev - CEO & Co-Founder
- **Education:** Stanford University (B.S. Mathematics), UCLA (M.S. Mathematics)
- **Background:** Prior to Robinhood, built trading software for financial institutions
- **Role:** Drives product vision, international expansion, regulatory strategy
- **Public Profile:** Testified before Congress (Feb 2021), active on social media

### Baiju Bhatt - Co-Founder
- **Education:** Stanford University (B.S. Physics, M.S. Mathematics)
- **Background:** Met Vlad Tenev at Stanford, worked together at previous startups
- **Role:** Product design, creative direction (stepped back from day-to-day operations in 2022)

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction-Based Revenues (~55%)**
   - Payment for Order Flow (PFOF)
   - Options: 49% of transaction revenue
   - Cryptocurrency: 30% of transaction revenue
   - Equities: 21% of transaction revenue

2. **Net Interest Income (~25%)**
   - Margin lending interest
   - Securities lending
   - Cash sweep program interest

3. **Robinhood Gold (~15%)**
   - Subscription fees: $5/month or $50/year

4. **Other Revenues (~5%)**
   - Gold Card interchange fees
   - ADR fees
   - Miscellaneous
