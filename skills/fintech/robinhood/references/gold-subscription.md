# Robinhood Gold Subscription

## Overview

Robinhood Gold is the premium subscription tier that provides enhanced features for active investors. Launched in 2016, it has evolved significantly to become a comprehensive financial services package.

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Savings |
|------|-------|---------|
| Monthly | $5/month | - |
| Annual | $50/year | $10 (17% savings) |

## Feature Breakdown

### Cash Management

| Feature | Free | Gold |
|---------|------|------|
| Brokerage cash APY | 0.01% | 3.35% |
| Break-even cash balance | - | ~$1,495 |

**Value Proposition:** With $1,495 in cash, Gold pays for itself through interest alone.

### Retirement Accounts

| Feature | Free | Gold |
|---------|------|------|
| IRA match | 1% | 3% |
| Break-even contribution | - | ~$2,500 |

**Example:** Contributing the 2025 maximum ($7,000) yields:
- Free: $70 match

### Margin Trading

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| First $1,000 | Interest-free |
| Beyond $1,000 | 5% (as of Dec 2025) |
| Break-even usage | ~$50K margin balance |

**Calculation:**
- $50,000 margin @ 5% = $2,500/year interest
- First $1,000 free saves $50/year
- Gold costs $50/year → Break-even achieved
