## Culture & Values

### Mission-Focused Company

In 2020, Brian Armstrong declared Coinbase a **"mission-focused company"** where employees commit to focusing on the mission rather than engaging in political activism at work. This approach has been influential in the tech industry.

### Core Values

1. **Top Talent:** Hire the best, regardless of location
2. **Clear Communication:** Written-first, async communication
3. **Efficient Execution:** Ship high-quality code fast
4. **Act Like an Owner:** Take responsibility for outcomes
5. **Top-Tier Talent Density:** Small teams, high impact

### Remote-First Philosophy

- No physical headquarters since 2021
- Distributed across 20+ countries
- Async-first communication culture
- Quarterly in-person gatherings

## Competitive Position

### Market Position

- **Largest US crypto exchange** by volume and users
- **#1 in US spot ETF custody** (80%+ market share)
- **Leading Ethereum L2** by revenue (Base)
- **Top 2 global stablecoin** (USDC, ~30% market share)

### Key Competitors

| Competitor | Differentiation | Relative Position |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Binance | Global volume leader | Larger globally, less US presence |

| Gemini | Regulated NY exchange | Smaller, similar compliance focus |
| Robinhood | Retail-focused | Traditional finance integration |
