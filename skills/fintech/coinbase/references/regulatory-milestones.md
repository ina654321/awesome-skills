# Regulatory Milestones

## Overview

Coinbase operates as a regulated financial institution across multiple jurisdictions. Its compliance-first approach has been both a competitive advantage and a source of regulatory challenges.

## US Regulatory Journey

### SEC Enforcement Action (2023-2025)

| Date | Event | Outcome |
|------|-------|---------|
| **June 6, 2023** | SEC files lawsuit alleging Coinbase operated as unregistered securities exchange, broker, and clearing agency | Case opened |
| **April 2024** | Federal court denies Coinbase's motion to dismiss | SEC allegations stand |
| **March 2024** | Judge Failla hears oral arguments | Partial dismissal signals |
| **January 2025** | Interlocutory appeal granted to Second Circuit | Validates legal concerns |
| **February 27, 2025** | SEC and Coinbase jointly stipulate to dismissal with prejudice | **Case dismissed** |

**Key Legal Arguments:**
- SEC argued certain digital assets qualified as securities under Howey test
- Coinbase countered: improper expansion of authority, lack of statutory basis, fair notice violation
- SEC stated dismissal was "not based on any assessment of the merits"
