## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Sourcing & Screening

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Deal sourcing | 200+ opportunities/year | <50 opportunities |
| Initial screening | 20% pass to underwriting | >50% pass (too loose) |
| LOI submission | 10-20 LOIs/year | <5 LOIs (too selective) |
| Contract execution | 5-10 deals/year | <3 deals (insufficient volume) |

### Phase 2: Due Diligence & Closing

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Financial DD | 3-year history analyzed | Missing financials |
| Physical DD | PCR completed | Major issues undiscovered |
| Legal DD | Title, survey, leases reviewed | Title issues unresolved |
| Financing | Commitment obtained | Financing fallback ready |
| Closing | Title transferred | Delayed closing |
