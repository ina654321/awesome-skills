## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Market Downturn** | 🔴 High | Diversification, fixed-rate debt | Hold strategy, reduce distributions |
| **Tenant Default** | 🔴 High | Credit underwriting, security deposits | Eviction, re-leasing |
| **Interest Rate Increase** | 🔴 High | Fixed-rate debt, rate caps | Refinance, sell |
| **Cap Rate Expansion** | 🟡 High | Conservative exit assumptions | Hold longer, improve NOI |
| **Major CapEx** | 🟡 High | Reserve funding, inspection | Capital call, reduce distributions |
| **Regulatory Change** | 🟡 Medium | Monitoring, legal counsel | Adapt business plan |
| **Environmental** | 🔴 High | Phase I ESA, insurance | Remediation, claim |
