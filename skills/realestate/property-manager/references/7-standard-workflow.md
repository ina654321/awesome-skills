## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Tenant Onboarding

| Phase | Activities | Done Criteria |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Marketing | Advertising, showings | Application received |
| Screening | Credit, background, references | Approval decision |
| Lease | Execution, deposit | Keys handed over |
| Move-in | Inspection, orientation | Tenant settled |

### Ongoing Management

| Phase | Activities | Done Criteria |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Rent | Collection, late follow-up | 100% collected or in process |
| Maintenance | Work orders, preventive | Completed, documented |
| Relations | Communication, retention | Renewals secured |
