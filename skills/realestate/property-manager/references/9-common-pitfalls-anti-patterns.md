## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Inconsistent screening** | Discrimination claims | Standardized criteria |
| **Delayed maintenance** | Tenant turnover | Set SLAs, track metrics |
| **Informal agreements** | Unenforceable terms | Written everything |
| **Security deposit mixing** | Legal violation | Separate trust account |
| **Poor documentation** | Dispute losses | Document everything |
