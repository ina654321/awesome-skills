# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|-------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Not conducting move-in inspection with photos** | 🔴 High | Always document with time-stamped photos; protects both PM and owner |
| 2 | **Failing to serve legally required notices** | 🔴 High | Know state-specific notice requirements: form, delivery method, timing |
| 3 | **Not verifying renter's insurance annually** | 🟡 Medium | Require annual certificate; owner liability for tenant-caused damage |
| 4 | **Commingling owner and PM funds** | 🔴 High | Maintain separate trust accounts per state law |
| 5 | **Failing to document reasonable accommodation requests** | 🔴 High | Create formal request log; respond within 10 days per HUD guidance |
| 6 | **Not conducting routine inspections** | 🟡 Medium | Annual or bi-annual inspections catch problems early |

## 10.2 Best Practices

1. **Always use state-standard lease forms** — updated annually
2. **Document everything in writing** — tenant communications, maintenance requests, inspections
3. **Maintain separate trust accounts** — never commingle with operating or PM funds
4. **Screen tenants thoroughly** — credit, criminal, eviction history, income verification
5. **Respond to maintenance requests in writing** — create paper trail
6. **Know Fair Housing rules cold** — training required annually
7. **Budget for turnover** — each vacancy costs 1–2 months rent + turnover costs

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] All leases signed, current, and stored securely
- [ ] Move-in inspections completed with photos and video
- [ ] Annual renter's insurance certificates on file
- [ ] Security deposit in trust account with proper accounting
- [ ] Fair Housing training completed (annual for all staff)
- [ ] All maintenance requests documented and closed
- [ ] Quarterly property inspections completed
- [ ] Annual budget approved by owner
- [ ] HOA compliance verified for all lease units
- [ ] Eviction filings processed per state law
- [ ] Property management software updated with all tenant data
