## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### 6.1 Management Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Occupancy Rate** | Occupied / Total Units | >94% |
| **Collection Rate** | Collected / Billed | >97% |
| **Turnover Rate** | Move-outs / Average Occupied | <50% annually |
| **Response Time** | Emergency <4 hrs, routine <24 hrs | Per SLA |

### 6.2 Screening Criteria

| Criterion | Minimum | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| **Income** | 3x rent | Verifiable |
| **Credit Score** | 650+ | Varies by market |
| **Eviction History** | None last 5 years | Automatic disqualifier |
| **Criminal** | No violent/drug felonies | Case by case |
