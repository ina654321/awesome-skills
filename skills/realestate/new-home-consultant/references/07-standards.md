## 7.2 New Home Construction Standards

| Standard | Scope | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------|------------------|
| **ICC IRC** | One- and two-family dwellings | Building, plumbing, mechanical, electrical codes |
| **ICC IBC** | Commercial/new home subdivisions | Fire safety, structural, egress |
| **EPA Lead Paint RRP** | Pre-1978 homes renovation | Certified firm, test kit, cleanup verification |
| **Energy Star v3** | New construction thermal envelope | Envelope, HVAC, lighting, water heating requirements |
| **WaterSense** | EPA water-efficient new homes | Indoor/outdoor water use criteria |
| **UL/GreenGuard** | Low VOC materials | Formaldehyde limits, chemical emissions |
| **State HOA statutes** | HOA formation and governance | Disclosure, assessment, CC&Rs |

## 7.3 Warranty Standards

| Warranty Type | Duration | Coverage |
|--------------|----------|---------|
| **Builder Structural** | 1–10 years | Foundation, framing, major structural defects |
| **Major Systems** | 1–2 years | HVAC, plumbing, electrical systems |
| **Workmanship** | 1 year | Finishes, trim, paint, flooring |
| **Appliances** | Per manufacturer | Ranges, dishwashers, HVAC equipment |
| **RWC/Builder Performance** | 10-year transferable | Workmanship + structural via third-party |

## 7.4 Performance Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Air leakage (blower door) | < 3 ACH50 (Energy Star) | ACH50 test |
| Duct leakage | < 4 CFM25/100 ft² ( conditioned) | Duct blaster test |
| Insulation R-value | Per IRC Table N1102.1.1 | Visual + certificate |
| Window U-factor | ≤ 0.30 (Climate Zone dependent) | NFRC label |
| HVAC sizing | Per ACCA Manual J/S | Load calculation |
