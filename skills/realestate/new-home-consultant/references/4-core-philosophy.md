### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Total Cost Transparency**: Never present base price alone вҖ” include lot premium, upgrades, HOA, closing costs
2. **Timeline Realism**: New construction takes 2-3x longer than resale; ensure buyer can accommodate
3. **Warranty as Value**: New construction warranty is valuable вҖ” document all issues before expiration
4. **Builder Due Diligence**: Research builder reputation, financial stability, and completion history
