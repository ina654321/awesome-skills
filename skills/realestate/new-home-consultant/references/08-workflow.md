# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 New Home Buyer Consultation Workflow

```
Phase 1: Pre-Purchase Assessment
├── Assess buyer readiness: financing pre-approval, budget range
├── Identify buyer priorities: location, size, features, energy efficiency
├── Explain buyer representation in new construction
├── Determine builder options: tract vs. custom vs. semi-custom
└── Sign buyer representation agreement (if required by state)

Phase 2: Community & Builder Evaluation
├── Research builder track record: BBB ratings, HOA complaints, past projects
├── Review HOA documents: CC&Rs, financials, reserves, pending assessments
├── Compare communities: HOA fees, amenities, special assessments
├── Verify builder licensing and insurance
└── Evaluate lot premiums: view, sun exposure, drainage, easements

Phase 3: Contract Negotiation & Upgrade Decisions
├── Review base price vs. options: cost-per-square-foot comparison
├── Negotiate upgrades: flooring, countertops, lighting (markup vs. builder cost)
├── Request closing cost credits if market allows
├── Add builder performance warranty (RWC or similar)
└── Execute purchase agreement with specs and completion date

Phase 4: Construction Oversight
├── Schedule periodic inspections: foundation, framing, mechanical rough-in
├── Review punch list at pre-drywall and final walk-through
├── Verify Energy Star certification requirements met
├── Coordinate with lender: construction-to-permanent loan draws
└── Final walk-through: 24–48h before closing

Phase 5: Closing & Post-Settlement
├── Review closing disclosure: ensure upgrades reflected in sales price
├── Confirm HOA transfer: owner induction, fee setup, disclosure packet
├── Document all warranty items in written log
└── Establish relationship with HOA management company
```

## 8.2 Pre-Drywall Inspection Checklist

1. Foundation: reinforcement, waterproofing, drainage
2. Framing: structural connections, notching, blocking
3. Plumbing: pressure test, drain-waste-vent, gas line
4. Electrical: panel, wiring, circuit labeling
5. HVAC: ductwork, equipment placement, insulation
6. Exterior envelope: sheathing, housewrap, flashing
7. Fire blocking and draft stopping per IRC R501.3/R502.7

## 8.3 Final Walk-Through Protocol

1. Verify all contracted items: appliances, finishes, fixtures
2. Test all systems: electrical, plumbing, HVAC, garage door
3. Check cosmetic conditions: paint, drywall, flooring, trim
4. Confirm punch list items from pre-closing walk-through are complete
5. Verify HOA move-in requirements: insurance certificate, deposit
6. Obtain all warranty documentation, as-built plans, equipment manuals
