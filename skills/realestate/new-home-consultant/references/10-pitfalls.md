# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|-------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Not reviewing HOA documents before purchase** | 🔴 High | Review CC&Rs, financials, meeting minutes within contingency period |
| 2 | **Accepting builder's square footage at face value** | 🟡 Medium | Measure independently; verify lot dimensions and actual living space |
| 3 | **Skipping pre-drywall inspection** | 🔴 High | Always hire independent inspector at pre-drywall stage |
| 4 | **Not verifying builder licensing** | 🔴 High | Check state contractor licensing board for complaints and license status |
| 5 | **Failing to get builder warranty in writing** | 🟡 Medium | Verbal promises are unenforceable — require written warranty document |
| 6 | **Underestimating HOA fee increases** | 🟡 Medium | HOA fees historically increase 3–5%/year; verify reserve study |

## 10.2 Best Practices

1. **Independent inspections at every phase** — foundation, framing, pre-drywall, final
2. **Document everything** — photograph all stages of construction
3. **Review HOA financials thoroughly** — reserves below 70% funded is a warning sign
4. **Negotiate from builder cost on upgrades** — request itemized pricing
5. **Add a builder performance warranty** — third-party structural warranty (RWC, 2-10 Home Buyers Warranty)
6. **Verify Energy Star certification** — reduces buyer utility costs and protects resale

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Buyer representation agreement signed before tour
- [ ] Builder licensing and complaint history verified
- [ ] HOA documents reviewed: CC&Rs, financials, meeting minutes, pending assessments
- [ ] Independent inspector hired for pre-drywall and final walk-through
- [ ] All upgrades documented with builder cost vs. retail value
- [ ] Energy Star certification verified
- [ ] Closing cost credits negotiated if applicable
- [ ] Warranty terms reviewed: structural, workmanship, appliance
- [ ] Final punch list completed and signed by builder
- [ ] HOA transfer documents: disclosure packet, owner induction, insurance
