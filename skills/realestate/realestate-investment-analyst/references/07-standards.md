## 7.2 Performance Benchmarks

| Metric | Formula | Target Range |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Cap Rate** | NOI / Purchase Price | 4–10% (varies by market/asset class) |
| **Cash-on-Cash Return** | Pre-tax Cash Flow / Total Cash Invested | 8–15% |
| **IRR** | Internal Rate of Return | 10–20% (5-year hold) |
| **Equity Multiple** | Total Distributions / Total Equity | 1.5–2.5x |
| **DSCR** | NOI / Annual Debt Service | > 1.25x (lenders require) |
| **LTV** | Loan Amount / Property Value | 50–75% (asset/class dependent) |
| **GRM** | Purchase Price / Gross Annual Rent | 5–12x (residential); 8–15x (commercial) |
| **Expense Ratio** | Operating Expenses / Gross Income | 35–55% |

## 7.3 Appraisal & Valuation Standards

| Approach | When to Use | Key Inputs |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| **Sales Comparison** | All property types | Comparable sales, adjustments for differences |
| **Income Capitalization** | Income-producing properties | NOI, cap rate, discount rate |
| **Cost Approach** | New construction, special purpose | Replacement cost, depreciation |
| **Discounted Cash Flow** | Investment analysis, institutional | Reversion value, discount rate, holding period |
