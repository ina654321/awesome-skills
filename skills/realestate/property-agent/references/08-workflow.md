# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Buyer Agent Transaction Workflow

```
Phase 1: Pre-Tour Consultation
├── Assess buyer needs: budget, location, timeline, must-haves
├── Review buyer representation agreement: exclusive or non-exclusive
├── Explain compensation: negotiate with listing broker or buyer broker fee
├── Set up MLS portal and search criteria
└── Obtain pre-approval letter from lender

Phase 2: Property Tours
├── Schedule and attend showings
├── Provide CMA notes per property
├── Identify red flags: condition, location, HOA issues
├── Record tour feedback for comparison
└── Track days on market vs. price trends

Phase 3: Offer Preparation & Submission
├── Analyze comparable sales: recent sales, pending, active
├── Draft offer: price, earnest money, contingencies, closing date
├── Coordinate with lender for pre-approval and proof of funds
├── Review HOA docs and property disclosure
└── Submit offer with professional cover letter

Phase 4: Negotiation & Acceptance
├── Present counter-offers to buyer with analysis
├── Negotiate terms: price, closing costs, repairs, inclusions
├── Execute final contract with all signatures
└── Deliver earnest money to escrow within 24–48h

Phase 5: Contract to Close
├── Coordinate inspections: general, radon, termite, septic, pool
├── Review HOA documents within due diligence
├── Verify financing: appraisal, loan approval, conditions
├── Negotiate repairs or credits based on inspection findings
├── Final walk-through with buyer
└── Attend closing; ensure all documents reviewed before signing

Phase 6: Post-Closing
├── Confirm recording and funds distribution
├── Ensure keys and access codes transferred
├── Follow up on any post-closing items
└── Request referral if satisfied with service
```

## 8.2 Seller Agent Listing Workflow

1. Pre-listing consultation: CMA, pricing strategy, marketing plan
2. Sign listing agreement: exclusive right to sell or exclusive agency
3. Order photography, video, 3D tour
4. Input into MLS; prepare marketing materials
5. Schedule and conduct open houses (2–4 minimum)
6. Review and present all offers (NAR Code of Ethics Article 1)
7. Negotiate best terms with seller
8. Coordinate contract execution and earnest money
9. Manage inspections and appraisal process
10. Final walk-through and closing attendance
