## 7.2 Agency Relationships

| Relationship | Fiduciary Duties | Compensation | Notes |
|-------------|-----------------|--------------|-------|
| **Buyer's Agent** | Loyalty, confidentiality, disclosure, obedience | Negotiated (post-Aug 2024) | Exclusive buyer's agent agreement required |
| **Seller's Agent** | Loyalty, disclosure, confidentiality | Listing side typically 2.5–3% | NAR settlement affects co-op offers |
| **Transaction Broker** | Limited confidentiality | Flat fee or hourly | Does not represent either party exclusively |
| **Dual Agent** | Limited loyalty to both | Split commission | Requires consent; some states prohibit |
| **Non-agent** | No fiduciary duty | Transaction fee only | Only facilitates transaction |

## 7.3 Disclosure Requirements

| Disclosure | When Required | Recipient | Legal Basis |
|------------|--------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Seller's Property Disclosure** | Before offer accepted | Buyer | Most states required |
| **Lead Paint Disclosure** | Pre-1978 homes | Buyer | Federal law |
| **Agency Disclosure** | First substantive contact | Buyer/Seller | State licensing law |
| **Material Facts** | Any known defect | All parties | State law |
| **Mold Disclosure** | Known mold issues | Buyer | State law (varies) |
| **Flood Zone Disclosure** | In SFHA | Buyer | Federal law |
| **HOA Documents** | HOA-governed properties | Buyer | State HOA disclosure law |
| **Death/Grisly Event** | Varies by state | Buyer | Most states: no disclosure required |

## 7.4 Compensation Standards (Post-NAR Settlement 2024)

| Practice | Status | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| MLS offers of buyer compensation | Prohibited on MLS | Must be negotiated separately |
| Buyer representation agreement | Required before touring | Exclusive or non-exclusive |
| MLS co-op compensation fields | Removed | Brokers negotiate directly |
| Buyer agent compensation | Buyer-negotiated | Can be seller-conceded in offer |
| Offer of compensation in offers | Must be explicit | Buyer offers must state compensation |
