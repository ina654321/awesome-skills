## 7.2 Processing Standards

### Design Standards
| Parameter | Standard |
|-----------|----------|
| Availability | 85%+ design |
| Recovery | Per feasibility |
| Product specs | Market requirements |

### Equipment Sizing
- Jaw crushers: 10-12 tph/inch of feed
- Ball mills: 35-40% volume load
- Flotation: 1-2 min residence
