# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Gear & Power Transmission Standards

- [ISO 6336 (all parts)](https://www.iso.org/standard/63624.html) - Calculation of load capacity of spur and helical gears: bending strength (Part 1), surface durability/pitting resistance (Part 2), material quality (Part 3), fatigue life calculation (Part 5)
- [ISO 1328 (all parts)](https://www.iso.org/standard/64187.html) - Cylindrical gears: ISO system of accuracy (Part 1: numbering system, Part 2: definitions and allowable values of variations)
- [ISO 281:2007](https://www.iso.org/standard/38194.html) - Rolling bearings: dynamic load ratings and rating life (L10 life calculation)
- [ISO 15243:2017](https://www.iso.org/standard/64585.html) - Rolling bearings: damage and failures — vocabulary, characteristic and causes
- [AGMA 2001](https://www.agma.org/) - Fundamental rating factors and calculation methods for involute spur and helical gear teeth (American Gear Manufacturers Association)
- [AGMA 2100](https://www.agma.org/) - Marine gear rating and selection (for naval and commercial marine reducers)
- [DIN 3961](https://www.din.enstandard.eu/DIN-3961/) - Tolerances for cylindrical gear teeth

## 7.2 Harmonic Drive & RV Reducer Standards

- [Harmonic Drive SE / AG](https://www.harmonic-drive.net/technical-information/standards) - Harmonic gear engineering standards and sizing methodology
- [Nabtesco Precision Reducer Manual](https://www.nabtesco.com/) - RV reducer selection, mounting, and maintenance specifications
- [SHG / CSG Series Technical Documentation](https://www.harmonic-drive.net/) - Flexspline, wave generator, circular spline design standards
- [ISO 15144](https://www.iso.org/standard/64194.html) - Calculation of microgeometry of spur and helical gears — influences on microgeometry causing transmission error

## 7.3 Robotics & Precision Motion Standards

- [ISO 9283](https://www.iso.org/standard/18174.html) - Manipulating industrial robots — performance criteria and related test methods
- [ISO 10218 (Parts 1 & 2)](https://www.iso.org/standard/51376.html) - Robots and robotic devices — safety requirements for industrial robots
- [ISO 15018](https://www.iso.org/standard/40978.html) - Robots and robotic devices — safety requirements for household appliances
- [VDI 2861](https://www.vdi.de/guidelines/vdi_2861) - Mounting dimensions and input/output characteristics for gear units and geared motors
- [VDI 2141](https://www.vdi.de/guidelines/vdi_2141) - Blanks for gear cutting — design recommendations
- [VDI 2739](https://www.vdi.de/guidelines/vdi_2739) - Calculation of gear dimensions and tooth thickness allowances

## 7.4 Material & Heat Treatment Standards

- [ISO 683 (all parts)](https://www.iso.org/standard/56579.html) - Heat-treated steels, alloy steels, and free-cutting steels
- [AMS (Aerospace Material Specifications)](https://www.sae.org/standards) - Aerospace-grade material specifications for high-performance reducers
- [ASTM A29/A29M](https://www.astm.org/) - Standard specification for general requirements for steel bars, carbon and alloy
- [Nadcap](https://www.nadcap.com/) - Special process accreditation for heat treatment, nondestructive testing

## 7.5 Testing & Validation Standards

- [ISO 6336-2](https://www.iso.org/standard/63625.html) - Gear surface durability testing methodology
- [AGMA 2100](https://www.agma.org/) - Testing and performance verification
- [VDI/VDE 2601](https://www.vdi.de/guidelines/vdi_vde_2601) - Testing of gear measuring instruments
- [API 613](https://www.api.org/) - Special-purpose gear units for petroleum, chemical, and gas industry services
- [JIS B 1702](https://www.jsa.or.jp/) - Cylindrical gears — standards for tooth contact and backlash
