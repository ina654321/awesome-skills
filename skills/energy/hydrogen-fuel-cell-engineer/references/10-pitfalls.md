# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Critical Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Consequence | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Hot work near hydrogen | 🔴 Critical | Explosion | Hot work permit required |
| 2 | Improper LOTO | 🔴 Critical | Death | Follow procedure exactly |
| 3 | Ignoring gas alarms | 🔴 Critical | Explosion, injury | Evacuate and investigate |
| 4 | Working alone | 🔴 High | Delayed rescue | Maintain communication |
| 5 | Improper ventilation | 🔴 High | Explosion risk | Maintain airflow |

## 10.2 Hydrogen System Errors

| Error | Result | Correct Approach |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Over-pressurization | Rupture | Pressure relief |
| Incomplete purge | Explosion | Follow purge procedure |
| Wrong fittings | Leak | Certified components |
| Poor ventilation | Accumulation | Flow testing |
| Delayed inspection | Failures | Regular schedule |

## 10.3 Operating Mistakes

| Mistake | Impact | Mitigation |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Rapid load changes | Cell damage | Gradual changes |
| Operating outside spec | Degradation | Follow limits |
| Ignoring warnings | System failure | Investigate |
| Improper startup | Failures | Follow procedure |
| Shortcuts during shutdown | Damage | Full shutdown |

## 10.4 Maintenance Errors

| Issue | Risk | Prevention |
|-------|------|------------|
| Wrong parts | Failure | Verify part numbers |
| Improper torque | Leaks | Follow specs |
| Skipped PM | Failure | Maintain schedule |
| Wrong lubricant | Damage | Manufacturer approved |
| Improper cleaning | Contamination | Approved methods |

## 10.5 Fire and Explosion Hazards

| Violation | Risk | Protocol |
|-----------|------|----------|
| Ignition sources | Explosion | Eliminate all sources |
| Blocked vents | Accumulation | Keep clear |
| Improper grounding | Static spark | Ground all equipment |
| Smoking | Fire | No smoking areas |
| Hot work | Explosion | Permit required |

| Issue | Consequence | Prevention |

| Coolant leaks | Environmental | Secondary containment |
| Improper disposal | EPA violation | Proper disposal |

## 10.8 Human Factors

| Factor | Impact | Mitigation |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Complacency | Missed hazards | Training |
| Time pressure | Shortcuts | Proper scheduling |

## 10.9 Training Deficiencies

- Inadequate hydrogen safety training
- Missing equipment-specific training
- Lack of emergency response training
- No refresher training
- Unqualified personnel performing work

## 10.10 Project and Installation Issues

- Design not meeting codes
- Improper commissioning
- Missing safety systems
- Inadequate ventilation design
- Poor material selection for hydrogen
