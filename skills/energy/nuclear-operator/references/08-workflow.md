# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Control Room Operations Workflow

```
Control Room Operations
├── Pre-Shift
│   ├── Read shift log and briefings
│   ├── Review plant status
│   ├── Verify watch relief schedule
│   └── Conduct shift turnover
├── Active Operations
│   ├── Monitor all plant parameters
│   ├── Respond to alarms
│   ├── Execute approved procedures
│   └── Document all actions
└── Post-Shift
    ├── Complete shift logs
    ├── Brief incoming shift
    └── Outstanding items handed over
```

## 8.2 Startup Procedure Workflow

1. **Preparation Phase**
   - Verify prerequisites met
   - Complete system lineup
   - Verify support systems available
   - Document initial conditions

2. **Reactor Startup**
   - Remove reactor criticality hold points
   - Withdraw control rods per startup procedure
   - Monitor criticality parameters
   - Achieve desired power level

3. **Generator Synchronization**
   - Warm up turbine
   - Match generator parameters to grid
   - Close generator breaker
   - Load pick-up

4. **Power Ascension**
   - Raise power per rate limits
   - Monitor all safety parameters
   - Verify equipment status
   - Complete startup tests

## 8.3 Shutdown Procedure Workflow

1. **Normal Shutdown**
   - Reduce generator load
   - Turbine trip and steam admission closure
   - Control rod insertion
   - Cool down per temperature gradients

2. **Reactor Trip Response**
   - Immediate actions per E-0
   - Verify reactor trip complete
   - Stabilize plant parameters
   - Assess cause

3. **Cooldown Operations**
   - Control pressure and temperature
   - Monitor containment integrity
   - Verify decay heat removal
   - Complete post-trip checks

## 8.4 Emergency Operating Procedures

```
Emergency Response Framework
├── E-0: Reactor Trip or Safety Injection
│   ├── Immediate response
│   ├── Stabilize plant
│   └── Notify shift supervisor
├── E-1: Loss of Reactor or Secondary Cooling
│   ├── Assess cooling capability
│   ├── Implement contingency measures
│   └── Prevent core damage
├── E-2: Low Core Cooling or Containment
│   ├── Maximize cooling
│   ├── Containment control
│   └── Prepare for accident mitigation
└── E-3: Core Damage or High Containment
    ├── Accident mitigation
    ├── Emergency response activation
    └── Public protection measures
```

## 8.5 Surveillance Testing Workflow

| Frequency | Testing | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Startup | Diesel generators | Emergency power verification |
| Monthly | Safety system tests | Operational readiness |
| Quarterly | Instrument calibration | Accuracy verification |
| Fuel cycle | Integrated tests | Performance confirmation |

## 8.6 Abnormal Operating Procedures

| Procedure | Trigger | Response |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| AOP-0 | Loss of component | Immediate stabilization |
| AOP-1 | Loss of flow | Alternate cooling path |
| AOP-2 | Loss of inventory | Makeup initiation |
| AOP-3 | High radiation | Protective actions |
