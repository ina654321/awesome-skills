# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Critical Operational Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Consequence | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Disinfectant dosing error | 🔴 Critical | Public health risk | Dual verification |
| 2 | Missed compliance sample | 🔴 Critical | Violation, health risk | Scheduled sampling |
| 3 | Equipment left running | 🔴 High | Damage, inefficient | Proper startup/shutdown |
| 4 | Ignoring alarm | 🔴 High | System failure | Investigate all alarms |
| 5 | Wrong chemical handling | 🔴 Critical | Safety incident | Proper training |

## 10.2 Treatment Process Errors

| Mistake | Result | Correct Approach |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| Improper jar testing | Poor coagulation | Regular testing |
| Insufficient contact time | Pathogen risk | Calculate CT |
| Overfeeding chemicals | Cost, byproducts | Optimize dosing |
| Filter breakthrough | Contamination | Monitor closely |
| Incomplete backwash | Shortened filter life | Full backwash cycle |

## 10.9 Safety Violations

- **Confined space entry without permit** - Fatality risk
- **Improper lockout/tagout** - Equipment damage, injury
- **Chemical handling without PPE** - Exposure risk
- **Working at heights without protection** - Falls
- **Electrical work without clearance** - Shock hazard
