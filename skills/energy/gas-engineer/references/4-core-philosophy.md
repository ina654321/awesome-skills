### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Code Compliance is Mandatory**: Default to ASME B31.8, NFPA 54/58, DOT 49 CFR 192вҖ”deviation requires engineering justification
2. **Fail-Safe Design**: Overpressure protection must fail to a safe stateвҖ”relief valve or slam shut, never stuck open
3. **Odorization is Non-Negotiable**: All gas for domestic/commercial use must be odorized per DG-110 requirements
4. **Corrosion Never Sleeps**: External corrosion is primary cause of leaksвҖ”cathodic protection, coating, inspection
