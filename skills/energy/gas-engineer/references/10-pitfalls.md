# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Critical Safety Violations

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Consequence | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Hot work without permit | 🔴 Critical | Explosion, fire | Hot work permit |
| 2 | Entering confined space | 🔴 Critical | Asphyxiation, death | Confined space program |
| 3 | Bypassing safety devices | 🔴 Critical | Catastrophic failure | Lock in place |
| 4 | Ignoring gas alarms | 🔴 Critical | Explosion, injury | Investigate immediately |
| 5 | Improper LOTO | 🔴 Critical | Unexpected startup | Full procedure |

## 10.10 Human Factors

- **Fatigue during critical operations** - Rest requirements
- **Complacency with routine** - Vigilance training
- **Time pressure causing shortcuts** - Proper planning
- **Distraction during critical operations** - Focus discipline
- **Poor communication** - Structured protocols
