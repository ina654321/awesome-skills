# Standard Workflow

Gas Processing Workflow
├── Feed Gas Inlet
│   ├── Feed gas metering
│   ├── Inlet separation
│   ├── Feed gas analysis
│   └── Pretreatment

│   ├── Acid gas removal
│   ├── Dehydration
│   ├── NGL recovery
│   └── Fractionation

│   ├── Natural gas pipeline spec
│   ├── NGL storage
│   ├── Product specifications

    ├── Fuel gas system
    ├── Water treatment

## 8.2 Compressor Station Operations

1. **Startup Sequence**
   - Pre-startup inspection
   - Suction pressure verification
   - Coolant system check
   - Lubrication system check
   - Start and load gradually

2. **Normal Operations**
   - Monitor discharge pressure
   - Check cylinder temperatures
   - Verify lube oil system
   - Monitor fuel gas
   - Check for unusual vibration

3. **Shutdown**
   - Unload compressor
   - Allow cool down
   - Vent fuel gas
   - Document operating hours

4. **Emergency Response**
   - Gas leak response
   - Fire response
   - Mechanical failure
   - Loss of containment

## 8.4 Gas Quality Monitoring

1. **Sampling Schedule**
   - Continuous: Flow, pressure, temperature
   - Daily: BTU content, Wobbe index
   - Weekly: Full laboratory analysis
   - Monthly: Moisture, sulfur content

2. **Off-Spec Response**
   - Identify source of contamination
   - Isolate offending source
   - Adjust processing
   - Notify downstream users
   - Document event

## 8.5 Leak Survey Workflow

1. **Ground Survey**
   - Bar hole sampling
   - Flame ionization detection
   - Infrared imaging
   - Soil gas analysis

2. **Above Ground Survey**
   - Visual inspection
   - Acoustic detection
   - Thermal imaging
   - Cathodic protection check

3. **Reporting**
   - GIS mapping of leaks
   - Leak severity classification
   - Repair prioritization
   - Repair verification
