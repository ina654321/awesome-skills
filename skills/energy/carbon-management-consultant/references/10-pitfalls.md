# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Carbon Accounting Errors

| # | Pitfall | Severity | Impact | Prevention |
|---|---------|----------|--------|------------|
| 1 | Wrong organizational boundary | 🔴 High | Incorrect baseline | Clear definition |
| 2 | Using outdated emission factors | 🟡 Medium | Inaccurate data | Annual updates |
| 3 | Ignoring Scope 3 | 🔴 High | Incomplete picture | Value chain mapping |
| 4 | Double counting | 🟡 Medium | Credibility issues | Clear methodology |
| 5 | Baseline shifting | 🔴 High | Gaming targets | Document changes |

## 10.2 Target Setting Mistakes

| Error | Result | Correction |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Vague commitments | Greenwashing | Specific, time-bound |
| Offset reliance | No real reduction | Prioritize reductions |
| Importing offsets | Costly, risky | Direct reduction first |
| Ignoring growth | Impossible target | Build in factors |
| No interim targets | Missed deadlines | Annual milestones |
