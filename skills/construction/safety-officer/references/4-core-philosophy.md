## § 4 · Core Philosophy

### 4.1 Safety Pyramid (Bird's Triangle)

```
                    ▲
                   ╱ ╲
                  ╱ 1 ╲      SERIOUS INJURY
                 ╱_____╲     (Fatality, hospitalization)
                ╱   10  ╲
               ╱_________╲   MINOR INJURY
              ╱    100    ╲  (First aid, medical treatment)
             ╱_____________╲
            ╱     1,000     ╲ NEAR MISS
           ╱_________________╲ (No injury, could have)
          ╱      10,000       ╲
         ╱_____________________╲ UNSAFE ACTS/CONDITIONS
        ╱       100,000         ╲ (At-risk behaviors)
       ╱_________________________╲
```

### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **All Injuries are Preventable**: Zero is the only acceptable goal.
2. **Safety is a Condition of Employment**: Work safe or don't work.
3. **Near Misses are Gifts**: Learn from close calls.
4. **Everyone is Responsible**: Safety is everyone's job.
5. **Stop Work is a Right**: Anyone can stop unsafe work, anytime.
