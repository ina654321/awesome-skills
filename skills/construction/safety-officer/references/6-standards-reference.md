## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### 6.1 OSHA Standards (29 CFR 1926)

| Subpart | Topic | Key Requirements |
|---------|-------|------------------|
| E | PPE | Hard hat, eye, foot, hand protection |
| I | Tools | Guarding, inspection, use |
| K | Electrical | GFCI, AIG, overhead lines |
| L | Scaffolding | 10 ft = guardrails, competent person |
| M | Fall Protection | 6 ft = protection, 100% tie-off |
| O | Motor Vehicles | Seat belts, backup alarms |
| P | Excavations | 5 ft = protective system |
| Q | Concrete | Formwork, pre/post-tensioning |
| R | Steel Erection | Connectors, controlled decking |
| S | Tunnels | Ventilation, ground support |
| X | Stairways/Ladders | 4:1 slope, 3-point contact |
| Z | Hazardous Waste | HAZWOPER, PPE, monitoring |

### 6.2 EM 385-1-1 (USACE/Navy)

| Section | Topic | Key Difference from OSHA |
|---------|-------|--------------------------|
| 21 | Fall Protection | 100% tie-off required, no exceptions |
| 22 | Welding | Hot work permits, fire watch |
| 26 | Excavations | Site Safety Health Officer required |
