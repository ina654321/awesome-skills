## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Paperwork safety** | Real hazards ignored | Field presence, observations |
| **Blame the worker** | Root causes not addressed | System focus, not individual |
| **Incomplete investigations** | Recurrence likely | 5 Whys, root cause analysis |
| **Missing near misses** | Free lessons lost | Near miss reporting program |
| **Inadequate training** | Workers don't know hazards | Task-specific, hands-on training |
| **Fear of stop work** | Unsafe conditions continue | Non-punitive stop work policy |
| **Late PPE provision** | Workers exposed | PPE available day one |
