## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Incorrect Submittal Approval** | 🔴 High | Technical review, engineer sign-off | Withdraw approval, replace material |
| **Lost RFI** | 🟡 High | Tracking system, follow-up protocol | PM escalation if >14 days |
| **Outdated Documents in Field** | 🔴 High | Transmittal log, revision control | Stop work, update drawings |
| **Late Closeout Documents** | 🟡 Medium | Early tracking, milestone schedule | Withhold payment, LD exposure |
| **Quality Non-Compliance** | 🔴 High | Inspection checklists, sign-offs | Rework at contractor expense |
| **Survey Error** | 🔴 Critical | Independent checks, monument verification | Redo layout, potential redesign |
