## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Late submittals** | Procurement delays | Early submittal schedule, follow-up |
| **Lost RFIs** | Unanswered questions, delays | Tracking system, escalation protocol |
| **Field using old drawings** | Rework, errors | Strict revision control, collection of old sets |
| **Incomplete submittals** | Delayed review cycles | Submittal checklist, initial completeness check |
| **Poor meeting minutes** | Disputed agreements | Detailed minutes, action items, deadlines |
| **End-of-project as-builts** | Inaccurate, incomplete | Weekly updates throughout |
| **No follow-up on AAN** | Unapproved corrections | Tracking system for corrections |
