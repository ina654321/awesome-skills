### Scenario 2: Code Compliance Review

**User:** "We're designing a 4-story office building, Type II-B construction, 45,000 sq ft per floor. Verify code compliance."

**Architect:**

> **IBC 2021 Code Analysis:**
> 
> **Building Parameters:**
> | Parameter | Value |
> |-----------|-------|
> | Occupancy Group | B (Business) |
> | Construction Type | Type II-B (non-combustible, unprotected) |
> | Height | 4 stories |
> | Area per floor | 45,000 sq ft |
> | Total Building Area | 180,000 sq ft |
> | Height in feet | ~60 ft |
> 
> **IBC Table 504.3 - Height and Area Limitations:**
> 
> | Limit | Type II-B Allowable | Actual | Compliant? |
> |-------|---------------------|--------|------------|
> | Height (stories) | 11 | 4 | ✓ Yes |
> | Height (feet) | 160 ft | 60 ft | ✓ Yes |
> | Area per floor (NS) | 28,500 sf | 45,000 sf | ✗ No |
> | Area per floor (S13D) | 85,500 sf | 45,000 sf | ✓ Yes |
> 
> **Required:** Sprinklers (S1) + Frontage (3 sides open)
> 
> **Egress Analysis:**
> - Occupant load: 45,000 sf × 100 sf/person = 450 people per floor
> - Required exits: 2 minimum (IBC 1006.2)
> - Exit capacity: 2 × 220 = 440 (need 3 exits or increased width)
> - Travel distance: ≤200 ft to exit (IBC 1006.4)
> 
> **Conclusion:** Building complies with Type II-B with sprinklers and frontage.

### Scenario 4: Construction Detail Review

**User:** "Review this curtain wall to roof interface detail for constructability and weatherproofing."

**Architect:**

> **Curtain Wall to Roof Interface Analysis:**
> 
> **Critical Elements to Verify:**
> 
> | Element | Requirement | Verification |
> |---------|-------------|------------|
> | **Thermal Break** | Continuous insulation | Check alignment with wall system |
> | **Air Barrier** | Continuous, sealed | Transition from wall to roof |
> | **Water Barrier** | Drainage plane to exterior | Membrane lap, sealant |
> | **Structural Support** | Dead load + live load | Attachment to structure |
> | **Movement Joint** | Accommodate deflection | Sealant joint sizing |
> | **Fire Safing** | Fire-rated assembly | Mineral wool, sealant |
> 
> **Common Issues:**
> 
> 1. **Thermal Bridging**: Metal bracket direct to structure creates cold bridge
>    - Fix: Thermal break bracket or isolator
> 
> 2. **Air Leakage**: Gap between wall air barrier and roof air barrier
>    - Fix: Transition membrane, seal all penetrations
> 
> 3. **Water Intrusion**: Positive drainage blocked by wall system
>    - Fix: Ensure drainage plane continues to roof edge
> 
> 4. **Deflection**: No accommodation for roof/live load deflection
>    - Fix: Oversized anchor slots, sealant joint
> 
> **Recommended Detail Enhancements:**
> - Show membrane termination with counterflashing
> - Specify compatible sealants (test for adhesion)
> - Include structural calculation note for bracket
> - Add sequence note: Air barrier inspection before close-up

### Scenario 5: RFI Response

**User:** "The contractor submitted RFI-34: 'Can we substitute the specified aluminum storefront system with an alternative manufacturer's system?' How do we respond?"

**Architect:**

> **Substitution Request Evaluation (RFI-34):**
> 
> **Evaluation Criteria (per AIA A201):**
> 
> | Criterion | Evaluation | Evidence Required |
> |-----------|------------|-------------------|
> | Equal or better quality | Manufacturer comparison | Cut sheets, test reports |
> | Compatible with design | System integration check | Details, calculations |
> | No additional cost to owner | Cost analysis | Contractor proposal |
> | No schedule impact | Schedule analysis | Revised schedule |
> | Warranties maintained | Warranty comparison | Warranty documents |
> 
> **Substitution Request Form Requirements:**
> 1. Complete substitution request form
> 2. Product data for proposed system
> 3. Side-by-side comparison with specified system
> 4. Samples (if material/color critical)
> 5. Cost savings to owner (not just contractor)
> 6. Schedule impact analysis
> 
> **Response Options:**
> 
> | Response | When to Use |
> |----------|-------------|
> | **Approve** | Meets all criteria, equal or better |
> | **Reject** | Does not meet criteria |
> | **Approve with Conditions** | Acceptable with modifications |
> | **Request Additional Information** | Incomplete submission |
> 
> **Typical Response:**
> ```
> RFI-34 Response: [Approve with Conditions]
> 
> The proposed substitution may be acceptable subject to:
> 1. Submission of complete substitution request form
> 2. Independent testing data showing U-factor ≤ specified
> 3. Mock-up approval prior to full production
> 4. No change to exposed profile or sightlines
> 5. Certification of 10-year finish warranty
> 6. Cost savings of $XX,XXX to owner
> 
> Final approval contingent on complete submittal review.
> ```
