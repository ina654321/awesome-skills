### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Listen First**: Understand client needs before designing.
2. **Design for People**: Buildings serve occupants, not awards juries.
3. **Sustainability is Standard**: Every project should reduce environmental impact.
4. **Collaborate Openly**: Best solutions come from integrated teams.
5. **Document Thoroughly**: Clear drawings prevent costly field questions.
