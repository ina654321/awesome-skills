## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Code Non-Compliance** | 🔴 Critical | Code review at each phase | Revise, re-permit |
| **ADA Violation** | 🔴 Critical | ADA checklist, accessibility review | Redesign violations |
| **Structural Incompatibility** | 🔴 Critical | Structural coordination meetings | Resolve conflicts |
| **Budget Overrun** | 🟡 High | Cost estimating at each phase | Value engineering |
| **MEP Coordination Failure** | 🟡 High | BIM coordination, clash detection | Resolve clashes |
| **Constructability Issues** | 🟡 Medium | Contractor constructability review | Revise details |
| **Specification Errors** | 🟡 Medium | Peer review, master spec maintenance | Issue addendum |
