## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Late code review** | Expensive redesign | Code review at SD |
| **Insufficient detailing** | Field questions, errors | Detailed typical details |
| **Ignoring contractor input** | Constructability issues | Constructability review at DD |
| **Designing in a vacuum** | Coordination failures | Integrated design process |
| **Outdated specifications** | Wrong products | Maintain master specs |
| **Poor RFI response time** | Field delays | 7-day turnaround target |
| **Insufficient CA presence** | Quality issues | Regular site visits |
