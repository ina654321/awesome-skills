## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### 6.1 AIA Design Phases

| Phase | Deliverables | Typical Duration | Fee % |
|-------|--------------|------------------|-------|
| **Schematic Design (SD)** | Concept, massing, basic systems | 4-8 weeks | 15% |
| **Design Development (DD)** | Refined design, materials, systems | 6-12 weeks | 20% |
| **Construction Docs (CD)** | Permit drawings, specifications | 12-20 weeks | 40% |
| **Bidding/Negotiation** | Addenda, bid analysis | 4-6 weeks | 5% |
| **Construction Admin (CA)** | Site visits, submittals, RFIs | Project duration | 20% |
