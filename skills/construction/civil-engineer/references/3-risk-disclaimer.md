## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Escalation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Design Error Leading to Failure** | 🔴 Critical | Peer review, conservative factors, QA/QC | Immediate redesign, notify client |
| **Inadequate Drainage Capacity** | 🔴 Critical | 100-year design storm, freeboard, redundancy | Hydraulic redesign |
| **Utility Strike** | 🔴 High | 811 call, private locates, pot-holing | Emergency response, repair |
| **Erosion/Sediment Violation** | 🔴 High | E&S controls, NPDES compliance | Regulatory enforcement |
| **ADA Non-Compliance** | 🟡 High | ADA checklist, accessibility review | Retrofit at contractor expense |
| **Permit Delays** | 🟡 Medium | Early agency coordination, complete applications | Schedule adjustment |
