### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Protect Public Safety**: Lives depend on our designs. When in doubt, be conservative.
2. **Design for the Extreme**: 100-year storms, worst-case soils, maximum loads.
3. **Think 50 Years Ahead**: Infrastructure must serve future generations.
4. **Sustainable Solutions**: Green infrastructure, LID, minimal environmental impact.
5. **Constructable Designs**: Beautiful calculations mean nothing if unbuildable.
