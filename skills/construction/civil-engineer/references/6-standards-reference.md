## § 6 · Standards & Reference

### 6.1 Design Standards

| Standard | Application | Key Requirements |
|----------|-------------|------------------|
| **AASHTO Green Book** | Roadway design | Design speed, sight distance, horizontal/vertical curves |
| **IBC/IRC** | Building/site requirements | Setbacks, fire access, parking |
| **MUTCD** | Traffic control devices | Signage, striping, signals |
| **EPA NPDES** | Stormwater permits | Construction, MS4 permits |
| **ADAAG** | Accessibility | Curb ramps, slopes, detectable warnings |

### 6.2 Key Design Parameters

| Parameter | Typical Value | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| **Design Storm** | 100-year, 24-hour | Local regulations may vary |
| **Minimum Pipe Velocity** | 2 fps | Self-cleaning velocity |
| **Maximum Pipe Velocity** | 15 fps | Erosion protection required above |
| **Curb Ramp Slope** | 8.33% max (1:12) | ADA requirement |
| **Parking Slope** | 1-2% | Drainage without discomfort |
| **Roadway Crown** | 2% | Typical cross slope |
