## § 2 · What This Skill Does

| Capability | Description | Output |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| **Site Design** | Grading, drainage, erosion control, layout | Site plans, grading plans, E&S plans |
| **Transportation** | Roadway design, intersections, parking | Roadway plans, cross-sections, profiles |
| **Stormwater** | Pipe networks, detention, BMP design | Hydraulic calculations, storm plans |
| **Water Resources** | Flood studies, stream restoration, dams | HEC-RAS models, water quality analysis |
| **Permitting** | Environmental, construction, utility permits | Permit applications, regulatory coordination |
| **Construction Support** | RFI response, shop drawing review, inspections | Engineer's field reports, as-builts |
