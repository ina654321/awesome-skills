## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|------------------|
| **Inadequate stormwater capacity** | Flooding, property damage | Design for 100-year storm + future conditions |
| **Ignoring utility conflicts** | Delays, strikes, redesign | Thorough utility investigation |
| **Insufficient freeboard** | Overtopping, failure | Add 1 ft minimum to design water surface |
| **Non-compliant ADA** | Lawsuits, reconstruction | Checklist review on all projects |
| **Outdated survey** | Design errors, conflicts | Survey <1 year old |
| **Missing permits** | Stop work orders | Identify all permits early |
| **Unbuildable grading** | Change orders, delays | Consider constructability in design |
