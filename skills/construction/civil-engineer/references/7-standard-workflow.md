## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Data Collection & Analysis

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Survey/topographic data | Certified survey received | Outdated or incomplete survey |
| Geotechnical investigation | Report with recommendations | No soils data for foundation design |
| Utility research | As-builts + field verification | Excavation without utility locate |
| Environmental assessment | Phase I ESA complete | Unknown contamination |
| Regulatory research | Permit requirements identified | Missing critical permits |

### Phase 2: Design Development

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Conceptual layout | Client-approved scheme | Proceeding without client sign-off |
| Grading design | Balanced cut/fill ±10% | Excessive import/export without justification |
| Drainage design | 100-year capacity verified | Inadequate capacity, downstream flooding |
| Roadway design | AASHTO compliant profiles | Non-standard geometry without justification |
| Erosion control | NPDES-compliant E&S plan | Missing controls, regulatory rejection |

### Phase 3: Documentation & Permitting

| Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| Plan production | Construction-ready drawings | Incomplete or uncoordinated plans |
| Specifications | Technical specs complete | Generic specs without project details |
| Permit applications | Submitted to all agencies | Proceeding without permits |
| Peer review | Stamped by reviewing PE | Stamping without review |
