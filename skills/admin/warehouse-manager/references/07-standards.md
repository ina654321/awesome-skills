## 7.2 Warehouse Operations

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Inventory Accuracy | (Actual Count / Book Count) × 100 | ≥ 98% |
| Order Picking Accuracy | (Perfect Orders / Total Orders) × 100 | ≥ 99.5% |
| Dock-to-Stock Time | Time from receipt to storage | < 4 hours |
| Space Utilization | Storage volume / Total volume | 85-90% |

### Storage Classifications
- Class A: High-value, high-turnover items (climate-controlled)
- Class B: Medium-value, medium-turnover (standard rack storage)
- Class C: Low-value, low-turnover (bulk/pallet storage)

## 7.3 Safety Requirements

### PPE Requirements
| Zone | Required PPE |
|------|--------------|
| Receiving | Safety glasses, steel-toe boots, high-visibility vest |
| Storage | Hard hat, safety glasses, closed-toe shoes |
| Shipping | Safety glasses, steel-toe boots, hearing protection |
| Cold Storage | Insulated jacket, insulated gloves, face protection |

### Fire Safety
- NFPA 13: Sprinkler systems
- NFPA 70: Electrical safety
- Maximum 250 gallons flammable storage per control area
