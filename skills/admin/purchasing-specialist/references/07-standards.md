# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Procurement Regulations

### Federal Requirements (US)
| Regulation | Description | Threshold |
|------------|-------------|-----------|
| FAR | Federal Acquisition Regulation | All federal contracts |
| DFARS | Defense FAR Supplement | DoD contracts |
| TINA | Truth in Negotiations Act | >$750K |
| Berry Amendment | Domestic source restrictions | Defense items |

### Corporate Policies
| Policy | Description | Authority |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Delegation of Authority | Approval limits by role | CFO/CEO |
| Conflict of Interest | Disclosure requirements | Ethics |
| Supplier Diversity | Minority/women-owned goals | Procurement policy |
| Sustainable Sourcing | Environmental criteria | CSR policy |

## 7.2 Purchase Order Standards

### PO Required Fields
- Vendor name and address
- PO number (sequential)
- Line items (description, qty, unit price, total)
- Shipping terms (Incoterms)
- Payment terms
- Delivery date/location
- Authorized signature

### Terms and Conditions
| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Net 30 | Payment due 30 days from invoice |
| 2/10 Net 30 | 2% discount if paid within 10 days |
| FOB Origin | Buyer pays shipping, owns goods in transit |
| FOB Destination | Seller pays shipping, owns until delivery |

## 7.3 Supplier Qualification

### Required Documentation
| Document | Purpose | Renewal |
|----------|---------|---------|
| W-9 | Tax identification | When information changes |
| Certificate of Insurance | Liability coverage | Annual |
| Business License | Operating legality | Annual |
| ISO Certification (if applicable) | Quality systems | Per certification |

### Risk Assessment
| Factor | Weight | Assessment |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Financial stability | 30% | D&B score, financial statements |
| Delivery performance | 25% | On-time delivery history |
| Quality record | 25% | Defect rate, rejects |
| Geographic risk | 10% | Political, natural disaster |
| Strategic value | 10% | Sole source, unique capability |
