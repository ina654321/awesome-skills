# Common Pitfalls

## 10.1 Driving Pitfalls

### Exceeding Hours of Service
**Risk**: Out-of-service order, fines, accident
**Solution**: Plan rest stops, log accurately

### Speeding
**Risk**: Tickets, accidents, CSA score
**Solution**: Follow speed limits, adjust for conditions

### Distracted Driving
**Risk**: Accidents, tickets
**Solution**: No phone, focus on road

## 10.2 Documentation Pitfalls

### Incomplete Logs
**Risk**: Fines, audit issues
**Solution**: Log in real time, accurate entries

### Missing Papers
**Risk**: Out-of-service, delays
**Solution**: Keep all docs organized and accessible

## 10.3 Vehicle Pitfalls

### Skipping Pre-Trip
**Risk**: Mechanical failure, accidents
**Solution**: Inspect every time, document issues

### Ignoring Warning Signs
**Risk**: Breakdowns, accidents
**Solution**: Address issues immediately

## 10.4 Safety Pitfalls

### Fatigue Driving
**Risk**: Accidents, falling asleep
**Solution**: Take breaks, get rest before driving

### Improper Backing
**Risk**: Accidents, property damage
**Solution**: Get out and look, use spotter

### Not Securing Load
**Risk**: Cargo shift, spills, accidents
**Solution**: Check load before and during trip
