# Common Pitfalls

## 10.1 Food Safety Pitfalls

### Cross-Contamination
**Risk**: Foodborne illness, health code violations
**Solution**: Color-coded cutting boards, separate raw/cooked, hand washing

### Time Temperature Abuse
**Risk**: Bacteria growth, food poisoning
**Solution**: Monitor temps, rapid cooling, proper holding

### Improper Hand Washing
**Risk**: Contamination, illness spread
**Solution**: Wash hands every 30 min, after bathroom, after raw meat

## 10.2 Kitchen Operations Pitfalls

### Poor Prep Planning
**Risk**: Slow service, running out, stress
**Solution**: Daily prep list, par levels, forecast menu

### Inconsistent Portions
**Risk**: Food cost issues, customer complaints
**Solution**: Portion scales, training, quality checks

### Not Following Recipes
**Risk**: Inconsistent quality, cost variances
**Solution**: Standardized recipes, proper training

## 10.3 Communication Pitfalls

### Not Communicating Specials
**Risk**: Wrong dishes served, customer disappointment
**Solution**: Clear communication to all staff

### Hiding Problems
**Risk**: Escalation, bigger issues
**Solution**: Report issues immediately, work as team

### Not Listening to Team
**Risk**: Missed feedback, low morale
**Solution**: Daily briefings, open-door policy

## 10.4 Personal Pitfalls

### Not Training Staff
**Risk**: Inconsistent quality, turnover
**Solution**: Cross-training program, ongoing development

### Avoiding Leadership
**Risk**: No direction, chaos
**Solution**: Take charge, make decisions

### Not Managing Cost
**Risk**: Profitability issues
**Solution**: Monitor food cost weekly, waste tracking
