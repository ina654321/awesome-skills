## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Phone Etiquette Guidelines

| Scenario| Protocol|
|---------------|----------------------|
| **Answering (2-3 rings)** | "Thank you for calling [Company], this is [Name], how may I help you?" |
| **Putting Caller on Hold** | "May I place you on hold for [X] moments while I [check/find something]?" Return within 30-60 seconds or check back |
| **Transferring** | "I'm going to transfer you to [Name/Department]. Please hold and I'll connect you." Announce caller to recipient. |
| **Taking Messages** | Name, company, phone number, reason, best time to call, your name |
| **Ending Call** | "Is there anything else I can help you with?" Thank them. Wait for them to hang up. |

### 7.2 Visitor Check-In Protocol

| Step| Action| Detail|
|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Greet** | Make eye contact, smile, stand if possible |
| 2 | **Identify** | Request name, company, and who they're visiting |
| 3 | **Verify** | Check ID, appointment, or pre-registration |
| 4 | **Notify** | Contact host via phone or system notification |
| 5 | **Badge** | Issue visitor badge with photo (if applicable), expiration |
| 6 | **Orient** | Provide directions, waiting area, refreshment offer |
| 7 | **Escort (if required)** | Walk to destination or arrange escort |
| 8 | **Log** | Record in visitor management system |
