# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Emergency Department Overcrowding

**User:** "Our ED wait times have increased to 6+ hours and patient satisfaction is declining. Boarded patients are causing OR delays. What strategic approach would you recommend?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Root Cause Assessment:**
> ```
> Input Factors:
> ├── Increased ED volumes
> ├── Reduced inpatient bed availability
> ├── Staffing constraints
> └── Boarding from admissions
>
> Throughput Factors:
> ├── Lab/T-ray turnaround
> ├── Consultant availability
> ├── Discharge delays
> └── Documentation backlog
> ```
>
> **Strategic Interventions:**
> ```
> Immediate (0-3 months):
> ├── Activate surge protocols
> ├── Implement rapid assessment zone
> ├── Increase observation unit capacity
> └── Deploy hospitalists for admissions
>
> Short-term (3-6 months):
> ├── Flow coordinator position
> ├── Parallel processing (labs while waiting for room)
> ├── Early discharge rounding
> └── Telemedicine for consults
>
> Long-term (6-12 months):
> ├── Build/renovate ED expansion
> ├── Community partnership for alternative care
> ├── Predictive analytics for staffing
> └── Destination protocols for specialties
> ```
>
> **Metrics to Track:**
> - Door-to-provider time
> - Length of stay
> - Boarding hours
> - Patient satisfaction (Press Ganey)
> - Staff satisfaction

## 9.2 Service Line Development

**User:** "We want to develop a cardiovascular service line to compete with the regional competitor. What market analysis and strategic steps should we take?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Market Assessment:**
> ```
> Market Analysis Framework:
> ├── Service area definition
> ├── Population demographics
> ├── Disease prevalence
> ├── Competitor capabilities
> └── Referral patterns
> ```
>
> **Competitive Analysis:**
> ```
> Strengths to Build:
> ├── Advanced cardiac surgery (TAVR, MitraClip)
> ├── Structural heart program
> ├── 24/7 cath lab coverage
> ├── Comprehensive heart failure program
> └── Cardiac rehabilitation network
> ```
>
> **Strategic Roadmap:**
> ```
> Phase 1: Foundation
> ├── Recruit/interview key physicians
> ├── Gap analysis against competitors
> ├── Capital equipment planning
> ├── Build space design
> └── Technology assessment
>
> Phase 2: Development
> ├── Program certification (Chest Pain, Heart Failure)
> ├── Marketing strategy
> ├── Community physician engagement
> ├── EMS partnership
> └── Quality metrics establishment
>
> Phase 3: Growth
> ├── Expand services (EP, VAD)
> ├── Research program
> ├── Outreach clinics
> ├── Tele-cardiology network
> └── Outcome public reporting
> ```
>
> **Investment Required:**
> - Cath lab equipment: $2-3M
> - Facility renovation: $5-10M
> - Physician recruitment: $2-5M/year
> - Marketing: $500K-1M/year
