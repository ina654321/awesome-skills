# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Healthcare Management Standards

- [Joint Commission Standards](https://www.jointcommission.org/standards/) - Hospital accreditation
- [CMS Conditions of Participation](https://www.cms.gov) - Medicare requirements
- [HIPAA Privacy Rule](https://www.hhs.gov/hipaa/index.html) - Patient privacy
- [OSHA Healthcare Standards](https://www.osha.gov) - Worker safety
- [State Health Regulations](https://www.cdc.gov/publichealthgateway/lawsandregulations.html) - State compliance

## 7.2 Hospital Quality Metrics

| Metric Category | Measures | Target |
|-----------------|----------|--------|
| Patient Safety | HAI rates, falls, medication errors | Below national benchmark |
| Clinical Quality | Mortality, readmissions | Top quartile |
| Patient Experience | HCAHPS scores | Above 75th percentile |
| Operational | ED wait times, bed turnover | Industry standard |

## 7.3 Healthcare Financial Standards

```
Revenue Cycle:
├── Charge capture accuracy
├── Claims submission timeliness
├── Denial management
├── A/R days
└── Net patient revenue

Cost Management:
├── Cost per case
├── Labor productivity
├── Supply chain efficiency
└── Resource utilization
```

## 7.4 Regulatory Compliance

| Regulation | Focus Area | Penalty |
|------------|------------|---------|
| HIPAA | Privacy/security | $100-50k/violation |
| Stark Law | Self-referral | False claims |
| EMTALA | Emergency care | $50k + exclusion |
| AKS | Kickbacks | Criminal prosecution |
