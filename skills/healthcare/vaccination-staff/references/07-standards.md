## 7.2 Vaccine Storage Standards

```
Temperature Monitoring:
├── Refrigerator: 2-8°C (36-46°F)
├── Freezer: -15 to -50°C
├── Continuous monitoring required
├── Daily temperature logs
├── Excursion response protocol
└── Backup power recommended

CDC Storage Requirements:
├── Purpose-built units preferred
├── Household units acceptable with monitoring
├── Do NOT use dorm-style refrigerators
├── Temperature alarm systems
└── Regular equipment maintenance
```

## 7.3 Contraindications

```
True Contraindications:
├── Severe allergic reaction (prior dose or component)
├── Anaphylaxis to vaccine component
├── Encephalopathy within 7 days (DTaP)
└── Immunosuppression (live vaccines)

Precautions:
├── Moderate or severe illness
├── History of Guillain-Barré (DTaP)
├── Prior severe reaction
├── Pregnancy (live vaccines)
└── Altered immunocompetence
```

## 7.4 Immunization Schedule

```
Pediatric (Birth-18 years):
├── Birth: HepB
├── 2, 4, 6 months: DTaP, Hib, IPV, PCV13, Rotavirus
├── 12-15 months: MMR, Varicella, HepA, Hib, PCV13
├── 4-6 years: DTaP, IPV, MMR, Varicella
└── 11-12 years: Tdap, HPV, MenACWY

Adult (19+ years):
├── Annual: Influenza
├── Tdap/Td: Every 10 years
├── Zoster: Age 50+ (RZV), 60+ (ZVL)
├── Pneumococcal: Age 65+ (PCV15/20, PPSV23)
└── COVID-19: Per current recommendations
```
