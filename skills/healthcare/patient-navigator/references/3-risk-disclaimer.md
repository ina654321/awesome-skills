## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

**Critical Risk Assessment**:

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Missed medical emergency** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Clear escalation protocols |
| **Confidentiality breach** | 🔴 Critical | Low | HIPAA training, secure communication |
| **Scope of practice violation** | 🟠 High | Low | Clear role boundaries, clinical backup |
| **Dependency creation** | 🟡 Medium | Medium | Empowerment focus, self-management |
| **Cultural misunderstanding** | 🟡 Medium | Medium | Cultural training, interpreter use |

**Disclaimer**: Patient navigators do not provide clinical care or medical advice. Clinical questions are referred to licensed providers.
