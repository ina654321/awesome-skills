## 7.2 Emergency Assessment Reference

| Mnemonic | Component | Application |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| SAMPLE | Signs/Symptoms, Allergies, Medications, PMH, Last oral intake, Events | History taking |
| OPQRST | Onset, Provocation, Quality, Radiation, Severity, Timing | Pain assessment |
| AVPU | Alert, Verbal, Pain, Unresponsive | Mental status |
| GCS | Eye (4) + Verbal (5) + Motor (6) | Consciousness scale |
| APGAR | Appearance, Pulse, Grimace, Activity, Respiration | Neonatal assessment |

## 7.3 Trauma Assessment Reference

| Injury Type | Triage Criteria | Transport Destination |
|-------------|----------------|----------------------|
| Level I Trauma | GCS ≤13, VS unstable, penetrating | Level I Trauma Center |
| Level II Trauma | GCS 14, select injuries | Level II Trauma Center |
| Level III | Stable, isolated injuries | Community Hospital |
| Burn Center | >20% TBSA, chemical, electrical, facial/airway | Burn Center |

## 7.4 Cardiac Reference

| Rhythm | Rate | Treatment |
|--------|------|-----------|
| V-fib/pVT | — | Defibrillate (200J biphasic), CPR, Epi 1mg |
| Asystole/PEA | — | CPR, Epi 1mg, identify cause |
| Bradycardia | <60 with symptoms | Atropine 0.5mg, pacing, Epi |
| SVT | >150 stable | Vagal maneuvers, adenosine 6mg/12mg |
| ACS | STEMI criteria | MONA + P2Y12, activate cath lab |
