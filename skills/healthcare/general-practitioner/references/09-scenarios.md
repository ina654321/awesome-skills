# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Hypertension Management

**User:** "55-year-old male with newly diagnosed hypertension, BP 158/96 mmHg. No other comorbidities. Non-smoker. What is the appropriate initial management?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Assessment:**
> - Stage 2 hypertension (≥140/90)
> - Low cardiovascular risk (no diabetes, smoking, CKD)
> - Treatment indicated per USPSTF
>
> **Step 1: Lifestyle Modification (concurrent)**
> ```
> - DASH diet counseling
> - Sodium restriction (<2300mg/day)
> - Weight management
> - Exercise (150 min/week moderate)
> - Limit alcohol
> ```
>
> **Step 2: Pharmacotherapy**
> - First-line agents: Thiazide, ACE-I, ARB, or CCB
> - Consider patient's preference and comorbidities
> - Start with moderate dose, titrate in 4 weeks
>
> **Recommended Approach:**
> ```
> Option 1: Hydrochlorothiazide 25mg daily
> Option 2: Lisinopril 10mg daily (if ACE-I preferred)
> Option 3: Amlodipine 5mg daily (if CCB preferred)
> ```
>
> **Follow-up:**
> - Recheck BP in 4 weeks
> - Home BP monitoring log
> - Target: <130/80 (2017 ACC/AHA guidelines)
> - Labs: BMP, lipid panel at 3 months

## 9.2 Uncontrolled Diabetes

**User:** "60-year-old female with T2DM on metformin 1000mg BID. HbA1c is 8.9%. She has nausea and polyuria. What's the appropriate next step?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Assessment:**
> - Above target HbA1c (goal <7%)
> - Symptoms suggesting hyperglycemia
> - Current therapy inadequate
>
> **Step 1: Evaluate Current Therapy**
> ```
> - Is she taking medications as prescribed?
> - Dietary factors contributing?
> - Other medications causing hyperglycemia?
> - Recent weight changes?
> ```
>
> **Step 2: Consider Adding Second Agent**
> ```
> Drug Selection Factors:
> ├── ASCVD: Consider SGLT2i or GLP-1 RA
> ├── Heart failure: SGLT2i preferred
> ├── CKD: SGLT2i or GLP-1 RA
> ├── Weight concern: GLP-1 RA or SGLT2i
> └── Cost: Sulfonylurea or DPP-4i
> ```
>
> **Recommended Plan:**
> ```
> 1. Add GLP-1 RA (semaglutide) - addresses weight, CV benefit
> 2. Continue metformin
> 3. Reinforce diet/exercise
> 4. Check postprandial glucose
> 5. Recheck HbA1c in 3 months
> ```
>
> **Monitoring:**
> - Watch for hypoglycemia with new agent
> - Assess GI tolerance of GLP-1
> - Consider self-monitoring of blood glucose
