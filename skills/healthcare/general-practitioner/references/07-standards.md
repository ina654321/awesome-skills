# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Primary Care Standards

- [AAFP Guidelines](https://www.aafp.org/journals/afp/explore.html) - Family medicine clinical recommendations
- [USPSTF Recommendations](https://www.uspreventiveservicestaskforce.org/uspstf/) - Preventive care screening
- [ACIP Immunization Schedule](https://www.cdc.gov/vaccines/schedules/) - CDC vaccine guidelines
- [AHRQ Primary Care Research](https://www.ahrq.gov) - Healthcare delivery research
- [Chronic Care Model](https://www.improvingchroniccare.org) - Chronic disease management

## 7.2 Preventive Care Guidelines

| Screening | Age Range | Frequency | Test |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|------|
| Colorectal cancer | 45-75 | Varies | Colonoscopy/FOBT/FIT-DNA |
| Breast cancer | 40+ | Annual | Mammography |
| Cervical cancer | 21-65 | 3-5 years | Pap/HPV co-test |
| Lung cancer | 50-80 | Annual | Low-dose CT (if high-risk) |
| Diabetes | 35-70 | Every 3 years | HbA1c/FPG |
| Hypertension | All adults | Each visit | BP measurement |

## 7.3 Clinical Decision Support

```
ABCDES Framework (Cardiovascular Risk):
├── Aspirin therapy (if indicated)
├── Blood pressure optimization
├── Cholesterol management (statins)
├── Cigarette cessation
├── Diabetes management
└── Stroke prevention (anticoagulation if AF)

Motivational Interviewing Principles:
├── Express empathy
├── Develop discrepancy
├── Roll with resistance
└── Support self-efficacy
```

## 7.4 Quality Metrics (HEDIS)

- Comprehensive diabetes care (HbA1c <8%)
- Controlling high blood pressure (<140/90)
- Adult BMI assessment
- Tobacco use screening and cessation
- Depression screening and follow-up
- Colorectal cancer screening completion
