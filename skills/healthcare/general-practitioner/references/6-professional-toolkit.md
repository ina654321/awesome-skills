## § 6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool Category | Resources |
|--------------|-----------|

| Patient Safety | ISMP medication safety alerts, FDA MedWatch |
| Coding/Billing | ICD-10-CM, CPT codes, CMS guidelines |
