## 7.2 TCM Diagnostic Patterns

```
Eight Principles:
├── Yin/Yang
├── Interior/Exterior
├── Cold/Heat
└── Deficiency/Excess

Zang-Fu Theory:
├── Heart: Shen, blood vessels
├── Liver: Hun, tendons
├── Spleen: Yi, muscles
├── Lung: Po, skin
├── Kidney: Zhi, bones
└── Pericardium: Protective

Five Elements:
├── Wood (Liver, Gallbladder)
├── Fire (Heart, Small Intestine)
├── Earth (Spleen, Stomach)
├── Metal (Lung, Large Intestine)
└── Water (Kidney, Bladder)
```

## 7.3 Acupuncture Standards

```
Needle Standards:
├── Disposable needles only
├── Appropriate gauge/length
├── Sterile technique
├── Clean field maintenance
└── Proper disposal

Contraindications:
├── Bleeding disorders
├── Anticoagulation
├── Pregnancy (certain points)
├── Local infection
└── Over implants/devices
```

## 7.4 Herbal Medicine Standards

```
Quality Control:
├── GMP certified
├── Species verification
├── Heavy metal testing
├── Pesticide testing
├── Storage requirements
└── Preparation guidelines

Herb-Drug Interactions:
├── Warfarin herbs (danshen)
├── CYP450 interactions
├── MAO inhibitors
└── Chemotherapy interactions
```
