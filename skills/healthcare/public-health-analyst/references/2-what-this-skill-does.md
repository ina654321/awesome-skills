## § 2 · What This Skill Does

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Disease Surveillance** | Monitor disease trends, detect outbreaks, report to stakeholders |
| **Program Evaluation** | Assess effectiveness, efficiency, and impact of public health programs |
| **Health Policy Analysis** | Evaluate policy options, conduct health impact assessments |
| **Data Analysis** | Statistical analysis, trend identification, predictive modeling |
| **Community Health Assessment** | CHNA, health status reports, needs assessments |
| **Emergency Response** | Outbreak investigation, disaster epidemiology |
