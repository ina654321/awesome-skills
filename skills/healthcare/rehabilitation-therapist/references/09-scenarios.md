# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Post-Stroke Rehabilitation

**User:** "A 68-year-old male 3 weeks post-stroke with right hemiparesis. Currently requires max assistance for transfers. Goal is household ambulation with cane. What is the treatment plan?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Current Status:**
> ```
> Motor: Right UE LE weakness (LE > UE)
> Sensation: Decreased right side
> Balance: Moderate deficits, sitting unsupported
> Transfers: Max assist
> Communication: Mild aphasia
> ADL: Total assist for upper body dressing
> ```
>
> **Short-Term Goals (4 weeks):
> ```
> 1. Transfer with mod assist
> 2. Sit to stand with min assist
> 3. Standing balance 30 seconds
> 4. Ambulate 50 ft with walker
> ```
>
> **Treatment Plan:**
> ```
> Week 1-2: Bed mobility, sitting balance, transfers
> ├── Bridge, rolling, supine-sit
> ├── Weight shifting in sitting
> ├── Standing pivot transfer practice
> └── Electrical stimulation to LE
>
> Week 3-4: Standing, balance, gait initiation
> ├── Sit to stand from varying heights
> ├── Weight-bearing through affected LE
> ├── Balance activities (reaching, stepping)
> └── Parallel bar ambulation with walker
> ```
>
> **Home Program:**
> - ROM exercises
> - Strengthening (SLR, heel raises)
> - Balance activities (tandem stance, stepping)
> - Functional practice

## 9.2 Total Knee Arthroplasty

**User:** "Day 1 post-op TKA. Patient reports pain 6/10, ROM 45-70 degrees. Wants to go home today. What do you prioritize?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Day 1 Priorities:**
> ```
> 1. Pain management (coordinate with MD)
> 2. Safe mobility assessment
> 3. Transfer training
> 4. Gait training with walker
> 5. Basic ADL training
> ```
>
> **Treatment Approach:**
> ```
> Mobility:
> ├── Bed mobility (supine-sit, log roll)
> ├── Transfer to chair with assist
> ├── Ambulate 50-100 ft with walker
> └── Incentive spirometry education
>
> ROM:
> ├── Passive ROM as tolerated
> ├── Heel slides with CPM if ordered
> ├── Straight leg raises if able
> └── Quadriceps sets
>
> Patient Education:
> ├── Pain management strategies
> ├── Activity precautions
> ├── Ice and elevation
> └── Home exercise program
> ```
>
> **Discharge Planning:**
> - Assess home environment
> - Equipment needs (walker, commode)
> - Family training
> - PT/OT home health referral
