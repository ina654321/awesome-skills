# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Documentation Errors

| Pitfall | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Missing daily notes | Audit finding | Daily documentation |
| Vague treatment notes | Non-compliance | Specific treatment |
| No progress documented | Denials | Weekly progress notes |
| Goals not measurable | Denials | SMART goals |

## 10.2 Treatment Errors

| Error | Impact | Prevention |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Overly aggressive | Re-injury | Stay within precautions |
| Under-treatment | Poor outcomes | Challenge appropriately |
| Wrong modality | Ineffective | Clinical reasoning |
| Ignoring patient goals | Poor engagement | Patient-centered |

## 10.3 Medical Necessity Issues

```
Common Issues:
├── Not documenting skilled need
├── Plateaus without discharge
├── Minimal treatment provided
├── Not reassessing appropriately
└── Goals met but continue treatment

Prevention:
├── Document clinical reasoning
├── Progress notes with objective data
├── Reassess per timeline
├── Discharge when appropriate
```

## 10.4 Safety Errors

| Error | Risk | Prevention |
|-------|------|------------|
| Ignoring fall risk | Injury | Fall precautions |
| Equipment not fitted | Ineffective | Proper fit |
| Not checking vitals | Medical emergency | Vitals as needed |
| Skin breakdown | Infection | Skin checks |

## 10.5 Communication Failures

| Failure | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Not communicating with MD | Medical issues | Weekly updates |
| Not coordinating with team | Fragmented care | Team conferences |
| Poor family education | Non-compliance | Training sessions |
| Incomplete handoff | Care gaps | Standardized handoff |

## 10.6 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Evaluation complete and dated
- [ ] Goals measurable and patient-centered
- [ ] Treatment plan appropriate
- [ ] Daily treatments documented
- [ ] Weekly progress documented
- [ ] Reassessments timely
- [ ] Home program provided
- [ ] Equipment appropriate
- [ ] Discharge planning begun early
- [ ] Documentation supports medical necessity
