# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 CLABSI Cluster Investigation

**User:** "We've identified 3 CLABSIs in the ICU this month (normally 0-1/month). What investigation steps should we take?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Step 1: Verify the Increase**
> ```
> - Review each case in detail
> - Confirm CLABSI definition met
> - Assess if common time period
> - Check baseline rate
> ```
>
> **Step 2: Case Review**
> ```
> For each case, assess:
> ├── Insertion site (subclavian vs IJ vs femoral)
> ├── Line days before infection
> ├── Insertion location (OR vs bedside)
> ├── Dressing change frequency
> ├── Line access frequency
> ├── Dedicated nurse vs travel nurse
> ├── Any breaks in sterile technique
> └── Patient risk factors
> ```
>
> **Step 3: Environmental Assessment**
> - Hand hygiene compliance data
> - PPE availability
> - Supply shortages
- Equipment issues
> - Staffing levels
>
> **Step 4: Interventions**
> ```
> Immediate Actions:
> ├── Line necessity review daily
> ├── Mandatory line necessity checklist
> ├── Chlorhexidine bathing for ICU patients
> ├── Enhanced surveillance
> └── Re-educate on insertion checklist
>
> If no improvement:
> ├── Antimicrobial-coated catheters
> ├── Chest X-ray for line placement verification
> └── Consider temporary removal if non-essential
> ```
>
> **Documentation:**
> - Incident report completed
> - Root cause analysis scheduled
> - Board report if needed

## 9.2 C. difficile Outbreak

**User:** "We've seen a 50% increase in C. difficile infections on the medical floor over the past 2 weeks. How do we respond?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Epidemiologic Assessment:**
> ```
> - Confirmed C. diff cases: review dates
> - Prior antibiotic exposure
> - Shared rooms or staff
> - Shared equipment
> - Cleaning practices
> - Soap vs alcohol-based hand rub usage
> ```
>
> **Immediate Actions:**
> ```
> Infection Control Measures:
> ├── Contact isolation for all cases
> ├── Dedicated equipment
> ├── Private room or cohorting
> ├── Hand washing with soap (not ABHR)
> ├── Enhanced environmental cleaning (bleach)
> ├── Antibiotic stewardship review
> └── Daily round participation
> ```
>
> **Antibiotic Stewardship:**
> ```
> Review antibiotic orders for:
> ├── Fluoroquinolones (high risk)
> ├── Clindamycin (high risk)
> ├── Extended-spectrum cephalosporins
> ├── Duration of therapy
> └── De-escalation when possible
> ```
>
> **Environmental Intervention:**
> ```
> Enhanced Cleaning Protocol:
> ├── Terminal cleaning with bleach
> ├── UV disinfection if available
> ├── High-touch surfaces focus
> ├── Terminal clean before new admission
> └── Daily cleaning verification
> ```
>
> **Communication:**
> - Notify nursing leadership
> - Alert infectious disease
> - Report to public health if outbreak threshold
> - Update medical staff
