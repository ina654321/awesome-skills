# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Surveillance Errors

| Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Passive surveillance only | 🟡 Medium | Active rounding |
| Definition misapplication | 🔴 High | Regular training |
| Delayed reporting | 🟡 Medium | Automated alerts |
| Data quality issues | 🟡 Medium | Validation protocols |

## 10.2 Isolation Compliance Failures

| Failure | Impact | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Inconsistent PPE use | Transmission | Education, feedback |
| Door signage missing | Staff confusion | Checklist |
| Patient leaving room | Exposure | Patient education |
| Equipment sharing | Cross-contamination | Dedicated equipment |

## 10.3 Hand Hygiene Oversights

```
Common Issues:
├── ABHR used for C. diff
├── Missed opportunities
├── Poor technique
├── Jewelry/nails
├── Dry hands

Corrective Actions:
├── C. diff: Soap and water
├── Direct observation
├── Video monitoring
├── Education on technique
└── Bare below elbows policy
```

## 10.4 Environmental Cleaning Gaps

| Gap | Risk | Fix |
|-----|------|-----|
| Missed high-touch areas | Infection | Checklist, fluorescent marking |
| Wrong disinfectant | Ineffective | Verify contacts |
| Terminal cleaning incomplete | Transmission | Audit, feedback |
| Shared equipment | Cross-contamination | Dedicated or clean between |

## 10.5 Antimicrobial Stewardship Failures

| Failure | Consequence | Intervention |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Prolonged prophylaxis | Resistance, C. diff | Audit and feedback |
| Broad-spectrum overuse | Resistance | De-escalation protocol |
| Missing documentation | Inappropriate use | CPOE decision support |

## 10.6 Quality Checklist

```
Surveillance:
├── Definitions applied correctly
├── Data validated
├── Reports submitted timely
├── Trends identified

Prevention:
├── Hand hygiene >95%
├── Isolation precautions followed
├── Environmental cleaning audited
├── Device utilization appropriate

Outcomes:
├── HAI rates declining
├── Outbreaks contained
├── Staff educated
├── Policies current
```
