# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Off-Label Inquiry

**User:** "A physician asks if our drug can be used for a patient population not in the approved label. They have case reports suggesting efficacy. How should the MSL respond?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Response Framework:**
> ```
> What the MSL CAN do:
> ├── Acknowledge the inquiry
> ├── Share published scientific literature
> ├── Discuss ongoing research in that area
> ├── Encourage physician-initiated research
> └── Refer to ongoing clinical trials
>
> What the MSL CANNOT do:
> ├── Recommend off-label use
> ├── Provide dosing guidance for unapproved use
> ├── Share anecdotal evidence as endorsement
> └── Discuss specific patient cases
> ```
>
> **Appropriate Response:**
> ```
> "Thank you for sharing that case. I appreciate your interest in our compound.
>
> Regarding your question:
> - Our product is only approved for [indication]
> - I can share published literature on [topic] if that would be helpful
> - There are ongoing studies in [area] that may be relevant
> - I'm not able to provide guidance on unapproved uses
>
> I'd be happy to connect you with our Medical Information team
> for any formal requests."
> ```
>
> **Documentation:**
> - Log inquiry in CRM
> - Document response provided
> - Note any follow-up commitments

## 9.2 Adverse Event Report

**User:** "During a discussion with a physician, they mention a patient on our drug developed liver failure. What should the MSL do?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Immediate Actions:**
> ```
> 1. Listen carefully without prompting for details
> 2. Express concern for the patient
> 3. Collect minimal necessary information
> 4. Explain the safety reporting process
> 5. Facilitate proper reporting
> ```
>
> **Response:**
> ```
> "I'm sorry to hear about this patient's experience. The safety of patients
> is our company's top priority.
>
> To ensure this is properly reported to our pharmacovigilance team,
> I would recommend:
> - Reporting directly to our PV department
> - Using our adverse event reporting portal
> - Or I can facilitate the connection
>
> I cannot collect detailed patient information in this setting,
> but can provide you with the contact information."
> ```
>
> **Follow-up:**
> - Document conversation in CRM
> - Note that safety reporting was discussed
> - Do not collect identifiable patient information
