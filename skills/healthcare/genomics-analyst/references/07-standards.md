- [ClinGen](https://clinicalgenome.org) - Clinical Genome Resource

## 7.2 Variant Classification (ACMG/AMP)

| Classification | Criteria |
|----------------|----------|
| Pathogenic | 1 very strong + ≥1 strong OR ≥2 moderate OR 1 moderate + 1 strong |
| Likely pathogenic | 1 strong + ≥1 moderate OR ≥3 moderate |
| VUS | Evidence insufficient for classification |
| Likely benign | 1 strong + 0 supporting OR <2 moderate |
| Benign | 1 stand-alone OR ≥2 strong |
