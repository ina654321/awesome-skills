# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Routine Inspection Workflow

```
Phase 1: Pre-Inspection
├── Review past inspection reports
├── Check for complaints
├── Gather facility information
├── Prepare inspection checklist
└── Arrange equipment (thermometer, UV light, etc.)

Phase 2: On-Site Inspection
├── Arrival and credential presentation
├── Opening conference
├── Facility walk-through
├── Document findings
└── Closing conference

Phase 3: Post-Inspection
├── Complete inspection report
├── Issue notice of violations
├── Schedule follow-up if needed
├── Enter data into tracking system
└── Archive documentation
```

## 8.2 Food Safety Inspection

```
Step 1: Kitchen Assessment
├── Temperature checks (refrigeration, hot holding)
├── Food source verification
├── Storage practices
├── Cross-contamination prevention
└── Personal hygiene of staff

Step 2: Equipment Evaluation
├── Equipment condition
├── Calibration status
├── Cleaning and sanitation
├── Pest control measures
└── Maintenance records

Step 3: Documentation Review
├── Permits displayed
├── Employee health records
├── Training certifications
├── Temperature logs
└── Pest control contracts
```

## 8.3 Complaint Investigation

```
Step 1: Intake
├── Document complaint details
├── Identify complainant (if provided)
├── Determine jurisdiction
└── Assign priority level

Step 2: Investigation
├── Unannounced inspection
├── Evidence collection
├── Witness interviews
└── Sample collection if needed

Step 3: Resolution
├── Determine findings
├── Issue corrective action
├── Notify complainant (as appropriate)
└── Document outcome
```

## 8.4 Sample Collection Protocol

| Sample Type | Collection Method | Storage | Analysis |
|-------------|-------------------|---------|----------|
| Food | Aseptic technique | Refrigerated | Lab testing |
| Water | Sterile bottle | Refrigerated | Potability |
| Surface swab | Sterile swab | Ambient | Culture |
| Air | BioSampler | N/A | Culture |
