# Johnson & Johnson Scenario Reference

## Scenario Library

### S1: New Product Introduction
**Context:** Launch a new surgical stapler in the MedTech portfolio.

**Key Activities:**
- Design controls per 21 CFR 820.30
- Biocompatibility testing (ISO 10993)
- Clinical evaluation
- FDA 510(k) submission
- Manufacturing scale-up
- Commercial launch

**Decision Points:**
- Predicate device selection
- Clinical data requirements
- Sterilization validation
- Packaging validation

---

### S2: Process Change
**Context:** Change API supplier for established pharmaceutical product.

**Key Activities:**
- Supplier qualification audit
- Equivalency testing
- Regulatory assessment
- Stability studies
- Supplement submission
- Supply chain transition

**Decision Points:**
- Prior approval supplement vs. changes-being-effected
- Validation strategy
- Inventory management
- Customer notification

---

### S3: Supply Disruption
**Context:** Critical raw material shortage threatens drug supply.

**Key Activities:**
- Alternative source identification
- Expedited qualification
- Regulatory filing
- Inventory prioritization
- Customer communication
- Long-term supply strategy

**Decision Points:**
- Risk-based sourcing decision
- Regulatory notification timing
- Allocation strategy
- Investment in alternative sources

---

### S4: Quality Investigation
**Context:** Out-of-specification result in finished product testing.

**Key Activities:**
- Laboratory investigation
- Manufacturing review
- Root cause analysis
- Batch disposition
- CAPA implementation
- Regulatory reporting assessment

**Decision Points:**
- Laboratory error vs. product failure
- Batch release decision
- FDA notification requirement
- Market action determination

---

### S5: Cybersecurity Update
**Context:** Vulnerability discovered in connected medical device.

**Key Activities:**
- Threat assessment
- Patch development
- Validation testing
- Customer notification
- Regulatory reporting
- Deployment strategy

**Decision Points:**
- Exploitability assessment
- Patient safety impact
- Update deployment method
- Timing of disclosure
