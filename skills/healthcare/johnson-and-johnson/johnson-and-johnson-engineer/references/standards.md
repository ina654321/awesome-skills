# Johnson & Johnson Standards Reference

## Regulatory Frameworks

### FDA Regulations (US)
| Regulation | Scope | J&J Application |
|------------|-------|-----------------|
| 21 CFR Part 11 | Electronic Records & Signatures | Manufacturing batch records, clinical data |
| 21 CFR Part 210/211 | Pharmaceutical CGMP | Drug manufacturing, quality control |
| 21 CFR Part 820 | Medical Device QSR | MedTech design and manufacturing |
| 21 CFR Part 600-680 | Biologics | Vaccine and cell therapy production |
