# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Dental Laboratory Standards

- [NADL Standards](https://www.nadl.org) - National Association of Dental Laboratories
- [ADA Specifications](https://www.ada.org/resources/research/science-and-research-institute) - Material standards
- [ISO 6877](https://www.iso.org/standard) - Dental ceramic materials
- [ISO 22698](https://www.iso.org/standard) - Zirconia materials
- [CDRH Dental Devices](https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/dental-devices) - FDA guidance

## 7.2 Dental Material Standards

| Material | Standard | Key Properties |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Dental ceramics | ISO 6872/6877 | Flexural strength, translucency |
| Zirconia | ISO 22698 | Y-TZP grade, aging resistance |
| Cobalt-chrome | ASTM F75 | Biocompatibility, corrosion resistance |
| PMMA | ISO 20795-1 | Denture base polymers |
| Dental plaster | ISO 6873 | Type III, IV stone classification |

## 7.4 Quality Control Standards

| Checkpoint | Specification | Tolerance |
|------------|---------------|-----------|
| Margin fit | Vertical discrepancy | <120 μm |
| Internal fit | Crown cement space | 25-50 μm |
| Surface roughness | Ra value | <1.25 μm |
| Color accuracy | ΔE from prescription | <2.0 |
| Occlusal adjustment | Centric contacts | Even distribution |
