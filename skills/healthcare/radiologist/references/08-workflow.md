# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Imaging Interpretation Workflow

```
Phase 1: Study Verification
├── Confirm patient identity (2 identifiers)
├── Verify examination type and protocol
├── Review clinical indication and history
├── Check prior imaging for comparison
└── Assess technical quality (motion, artifact, coverage)

Phase 2: Systematic Review
├── Review all available series/datasets
├── Follow structured search pattern
├── Characterize all findings
├── Measure quantifiable parameters
└── Compare with priors when available

Phase 3: Differential Diagnosis
├── Consider most likely diagnosis
├── Generate differential for equivocal findings
├── Assess for concerning features
└── Evaluate incidental findings

Phase 4: Reporting
├── Impression first format
├── BI-RADS/TI-RADS/LI-RADS categorization when applicable
├── Specific recommendations with timeframes
└── Communicate urgent findings per protocol
```

## 8.2 Critical Findings Communication

| Finding | Communication Protocol | Documentation |
|---------|----------------------|---------------|
| Pulmonary embolism | Direct verbal + write | Time, recipient, acknowledgment |
| Tension pneumothorax | Immediate verbal to ED MD | Within 30 minutes |
| Aortic aneurysm >5.5 cm | Direct verbal to referring MD | Within 24 hours |
| Acute stroke on CT | STAT verbal to stroke team | Document time |
| Unexpected malignancy | Send written report; direct call if advanced | Document |

## 8.3 Common Diagnostic Pathways

### Pulmonary Nodule Workup (Fleischner Guidelines)

1. **<6mm, low risk**: No routine follow-up
2. **6-8mm, any risk**: CT at 6-12 months, then 18-24 months
3. **>8mm**: CT at 3 months, PET/CT, or biopsy based on risk

### Breast Imaging Assessment (BI-RADS)

| Category | Meaning | Management |
|----------|---------|------------|
| BI-RADS 1 | Negative | Routine screening |
| BI-RADS 2 | Benign | Routine screening |
| BI-RADS 3 | Probably benign | Short-interval follow-up (6mo) |
| BI-RADS 4A/4B/4C | Suspicious | Biopsy |
| BI-RADS 5 | Highly suggestive of malignancy | Biopsy |
| BI-RADS 6 | Known biopsy-proven malignancy | Treatment planning |

## 8.4 Quality Assurance Checklist

- [ ] Correct patient and study verified
- [ ] Clinical indication reviewed
- [ ] Prior imaging compared
- [ ] All series reviewed
- [ ] Measurements documented
- [ ] Appropriate lexicon used (BI-RADS, etc.)
- [ ] Impression is clear and actionable
- [ ] Recommendations include timeframe
- [ ] Critical findings communicated per protocol
