## 7.3 Radiation Dose Reference

| Examination | Typical CTDIvol | Effective Dose (mSv) |
|-------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Head CT | 60-70 mGy | 2 |
| Chest CT | 12-15 mGy | 6-7 |
| Abdomen CT | 15-20 mGy | 8-10 |
| CT Pulmonary Angiography | 15-20 mGy | 10-15 |
| Lumbar Spine | 35-40 mGy | 1.5 |

**Dose Calculation**: Effective Dose (mSv) = DLP × k-factor (k = 0.015 for chest, 0.017 for abdomen)

## 7.4 Contrast Safety Thresholds

| Parameter | Iodinated IV Contrast | Gadolinium IV |
|-----------|----------------------|---------------|
| eGFR ≥60 | No restriction | No restriction |
| eGFR 30-59 | Monitor; hydrate | Use macrocyclic agents |
| eGFR <30 | Risk-benefit; iso-osmolar | Avoid linear agents; use macrocyclic |
| Dialysis | Same as eGFR <30 | Avoid; can dialyze after |
