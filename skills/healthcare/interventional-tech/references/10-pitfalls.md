# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Patient Safety Errors

| Pitfall | Severity | Prevention |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Wrong patient/procedure | 🔴 Critical | Two-person verification |
| Missed allergies | 🔴 Critical | Pre-procedure checklist |
| Wrong side marking | 🔴 Critical | Universal protocol |
| Incomplete consent | 🟡 Medium | Checklist review |
| Missing labs | 🟡 Medium | Pre-procedure verification |

## 10.2 Radiation Safety Violations

| Violation | Impact | Prevention |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Extended fluoro time | Skin injury | Optimization training |
| Improper shielding | Staff exposure | Lead compliance |
| Ignoring dose alerts | Patient harm | Protocol acknowledgment |
| Missing dosimeter | Exposure unknown | Badge reminders |

## 10.3 Sterile Field Breaches

```
Common Issues:
├── Contaminated instruments
├── Broken sterile technique
├── Equipment touching floor
├── Mask/face shield issues
└── Hair uncovered

Prevention:
├── Annual sterile technique review
├── Direct observation
├── Immediate correction
└── Incident reporting
```

## 10.4 Equipment Errors

| Error | Consequence | Prevention |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Wrong catheter selected | Delay, complication | Verify with physician |
| Equipment malfunction | Patient safety | Pre-procedure check |
| Contrast extravasation | Tissue injury | Proper injection technique |

## 10.5 Communication Failures

| Failure | Impact | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Handoff errors | Missed info | Standardized handoff |
| Unclear orders | Wrong procedure | Read-back verification |
| Equipment needs | Delay | Pre-announced |

## 10.6 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Patient identification verified
- [ ] Procedure consent complete
- [ ] Labs reviewed
- [ ] Allergies documented
- [ ] Correct site marked
- [ ] NPO verified
- [ ] Radiation safety measures in place
- [ ] Emergency equipment available
- [ ] Sterile field maintained
- [ ] Post-procedure documentation complete
