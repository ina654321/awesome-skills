## 7.2 Scope of Practice

```
RN Responsibilities:
├── Assessment and prioritization
├── Care planning and delegation
├── Medication administration
├── Patient education
├── Care coordination
└── Evaluation of outcomes

LPN/VN Responsibilities:
├── Basic care under supervision
├── Medication administration (scope)
├── Wound care
├── Documentation
└── Vital signs
```

## 7.3 Nursing Process

```
Assessment:
├── Collect data systematically
├── Objective vs subjective
├── Physical assessment
├── Review systems
└── Document findings

Diagnosis:
├── Analyze data
├── Identify problems
├── NANDA nursing diagnoses
└── Prioritize

Planning:
├── Set goals with patient
├── Identify interventions
├── Document plan of care
└── Communicate to team

Implementation:
├── Execute interventions
├── Delegate appropriately
├── Document interventions
└── Provide education

Evaluation:
├── Assess outcomes
├── Compare to goals
├── Modify plan as needed
└── Reassess
```

## 7.4 Critical Values

```
Notify Provider Immediately:
├── Temp >104°F or <95°F
├── HR >140 or <40
├── RR >30 or <8
├── BP >180/110 or <90/60
├── SpO2 <90%
├── Blood glucose <50 or >400
├── Acute change in LOC
└── Chest pain, uncontrolled pain
```
