# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Deteriorating Patient

**User:** "Your patient is normally alert and oriented but suddenly becomes confused and agitated. Vital signs show BP 88/50, HR 115, SpO2 89% on room air. What do you do?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Clinical Picture:**
> - Signs of acute deterioration
> - Hemodynamic instability
> - Possible sepsis, hemorrhage, or cardiac event
>
> **Immediate Actions:**
> ```
> 1. Stay with patient
> 2. Call for help - activate rapid response
> 3. Position patient (supine, legs elevated if hypotensive)
> 4. Apply O2 - non-rebreather mask
> 5. Establish IV access if not present
> 6. Draw STAT labs if able
> ```
>
> **Communication:**
> ```
> Call to physician/Rapid Response:
> "I have a rapid response for room [number].
> Patient [name] is a [age]-year-old with [diagnosis].
> They are acutely confused and:
> - BP 88/50, HR 115, SpO2 89% on RA
> - Labored breathing, pale and diaphoretic
> - I have applied O2 and called for help.
> Please come immediately."
> ```
>
> **Documentation:**
> - Time of change
> - Vital signs
> - Interventions performed
> - Physician notification time
> - Response to interventions

## 9.2 Family Conflict

**User:** "A patient's family is demanding to see the medical record and questioning why we're not doing more tests. The patient is alert and has not authorized family access. How do you handle this?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **HIPAA Considerations:**
> - Patient has right to privacy
> - Without patient authorization, cannot share
> - Family may be acting as patient advocates
>
> **Response to Family:**
> ```
> "I understand your concern for [patient name]. I cannot share
> medical information without their permission.
>
> I encourage you to speak directly with Dr. [physician name]
> who can discuss the treatment plan with you if [patient]
> provides authorization.
>
> In the meantime, I'm happy to help facilitate that
> conversation."
> ```
>
> **Actions:**
> ```
> 1. Acknowledge family's concerns
> 2. Explain privacy rules
> 3. Offer to help facilitate communication
> 4. Notify care team
> 5. Document family request
> ```
>
> **Documentation:**
> - Family's request for information
> - Explanation given
> - Care team notified
