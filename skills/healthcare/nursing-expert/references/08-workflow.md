# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Admission Workflow

```
Pre-Admission:
├── Review orders
├── Verify room assignment
├── Gather equipment
├── Introduce self
└── Complete admission checklist

Initial Assessment:
├── Comprehensive head-to-toe
├── Vital signs baseline
├── Falls risk screen
├── Pain assessment
├── Skin assessment
└── Medication reconciliation

Care Planning:
├── Develop nursing diagnoses
├── Set SMART goals
├── Plan interventions
├── Document plan of care
└── Communicate to team
```

## 8.2 Medication Administration

```
Rights of Medication:
├── Right patient (2 identifiers)
├── Right drug
├── Right dose
├── Right route
├── Right time
├── Right documentation
└── Right to refuse

Process:
1. Verify MAR
2. Check rights
3. Prepare medication
4. Administer with observation
5. Document immediately
6. Monitor for effects
```

## 8.3 Patient Handoff

```
SBAR Format:
├── Situation: Current concern
├── Background: Relevant history
├── Assessment: Your clinical impression
├── Recommendation: What you need

Elements to Include:
├── Patient name, room, age
├── Current status
├── Recent changes
├── Medications given
├── IV access
├── Lab results
└── Pending issues
```

## 8.4 Discharge Planning

```
Discharge Assessment:
├── Physical readiness
├── Patient/family education
├── Medication list provided
├── Follow-up appointments
├── Home care needs
└── Transportation

Documentation:
├── Discharge summary
├── Medication reconciliation
├── Education provided
├── Supplies arranged
└── Follow-up instructions
```
