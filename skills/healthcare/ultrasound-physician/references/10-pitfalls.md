# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Technical Errors

| Pitfall | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Incomplete survey | Missed anomalies | Protocol adherence |
| Wrong dates | Wrong management | Confirm dates |
| Missing multiple gestations | Complications | Careful screening |
| Poor image quality | Inadequate assessment | Optimize settings |

## 10.2 Measurement Errors

| Error | Impact | Prevention |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Wrong plane | Inaccurate measurement | Training |
| Oblique BPD | Overestimation | Standard plane |
| Poor caliper placement | Error | Practice |
| Chart errors | Wrong classification | Double-check |

## 10.3 Diagnostic Errors

```
Common Misses:
├── CNS anomalies (early)
├── Cardiac defects
├── Limb abnormalities
├── Facial clefts
├── Neural tube defects (if no spina bifida protocol)
└── Chromosomal markers

Prevention:
├── Systematic approach
├── Proper training
├── Quality assurance
├── Peer review
└── CME requirements
```

## 10.4 Communication Failures

| Failure | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Not explaining limitations | Unrealistic expectations | Informed consent |
| Delayed reporting | Delayed treatment | Timely reporting |
| Wrong urgency communication | Delayed care | Clear communication |
| Incomplete documentation | Continuity | Complete report |

## 10.5 Safety Issues

| Issue | Risk | Prevention |
|-------|------|------------|
| Thermal index too high | Tissue heating | Keep TI <1.0 diagnostic |
| Mechanical index too high | Cavitation | Keep MI <0.5 |
| Prolonged exposure | Unknown effects | ALARA principle |
| Untrained operator | Misdiagnosis | Credentialing |

## 10.6 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Indication documented
- [ ] Protocol complete
- [ ] All measurements correct
- [ ] Images labeled properly
- [ ] Findings clearly described
- [ ] Recommendations appropriate
- [ ] Report timed and signed
- [ ] Critical results called
- [ ] Comparison with priors
- [ ] Limitations noted
