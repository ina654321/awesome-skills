# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Growth Restriction

**User:** "30-year-old G2P1 at 32 weeks with EFW in 8th percentile, decreased amniotic fluid. Umbilical artery shows absent end-diastolic flow. How do you manage this?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Assessment:**
> ```
> Fetal Status:
> ├── EFW <10th percentile = SGA
> ├── AEDV = significant
> ├── Oligohydramnios = compromised
> └── Classification: Early-onset FGR
> ```
>
> **Management Protocol:**
> ```
> Immediate:
> ├── Hospital admission
> ├── Antenatal corticosteroids (28-34 weeks)
> ├── Daily fetal surveillance
> ├── Umbilical artery Doppler q48h
> ├── MCA Doppler
> └── Biophysical profile daily
>
> Monitoring:
> ├── Umbilical artery (UA)
> │   └── Progressive worsening → delivery
> ├── Middle cerebral artery (MCA)
> │   └── Brain-sparing → chronic compensation
> └── Ductus venosus
>     └── Reversed A-wave → imminent decompensation
>
> Delivery Timing:
> ├── <32 weeks: Balance RDS vs FGR
> ├── If UA becomes reversed: Deliver regardless of gestation
> ├── If MCA abnormal: Consider delivery
> ├── If BPP <4/8 persistently: Deliver
> └── Deliver at 34 weeks if stable
> ```
>
> **Delivery Planning:**
> - Cesarean likely
> - Neonatology present
> - Level III NICU prepared

## 9.2 Placenta Previa

**User:** "28-year-old G3P2 at 20 weeks with painless vaginal bleeding. Ultrasound shows placenta covering internal os. What is the counseling and management?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Diagnosis:**
> ```
> Placenta Previa Classification:
> ├── Complete: Internal os completely covered
> ├── Partial: Partially covered
> ├── Marginal: Edge at internal os
> └── Low-lying: <2cm from os
>
> Clinical Significance:
> ├── Complete: Requires cesarean
> ├── Risk of hemorrhage
> └── Transvaginal delivery contraindicated
> ```
>
> **Management Plan:**
> ```
> First Trimester (20 weeks):
> ├── Transabdominal + transvaginal ultrasound
> ├── Confirm diagnosis
> ├── Reassure about resolution
> └── Schedule follow-up
>
> Third Trimester (32 weeks):
> ├── Repeat ultrasound
> ├── Assess for vasa previa
> ├── Rule out morbidly adherent placenta
> └── Plan delivery
>
> Delivery Planning:
> ├── Cesarean section scheduled at 36-37 weeks
> ├── Anterior previa: Consider celestone at 34 weeks
> ├── Cross-match blood
> ├── Anesthesia evaluation
> └── Neonatology aware
>
> Patient Counseling:
> ├── Activity restrictions
> ├── Warning signs (bleeding, contractions)
> ├── When to go to hospital
> └── High probability of cesarean
> ```
