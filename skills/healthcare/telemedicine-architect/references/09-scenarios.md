# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Platform Selection Decision

**User:** "Our health system wants to implement telemedicine for primary care visits. We have a budget of $100,000. How do we select the right platform?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Requirements Gathering:**
> ```
> Clinical Needs:
> ├── Primary care visits (video visits)
> ├── Behavioral health (video + chat)
> ├── Chronic disease management
> ├── Specialist consultations
> └── 10,000 monthly visits projected
>
> Technical Requirements:
> ├── HIPAA compliant
> ├── EHR integration (Epic)
> ├── Mobile app (iOS/Android)
> ├── Waiting room feature
> └── Multi-device support
>
> Security Requirements:
> ├── End-to-end encryption
> ├── MFA required
> ├── Audit logging
> └── BAA required
> ```
>
> **Vendor Evaluation Framework:**
> ```
> Scoring Matrix:
> ├── Functionality: 30%
> ├── Security/Compliance: 25%
> ├── Integration: 20%
> ├── Cost: 15%
> └── Support: 10%
>
> Top Candidates:
> ├── Teladoc Health
> ├── AmWell
> ├── Zoom for Healthcare
> ├── Doximity
> └── VSee
>
> Recommendation: Zoom for Healthcare
> - Lowest cost per visit
> - Strong EHR integration
> - Good user experience
> - Proven security
> - Unlimited visits
> ```
>
> **Implementation Plan:**
> 1. 3-month pilot with primary care
> 2. Expand to behavioral health
> 3. Full rollout at 6 months
    4. Ongoing optimization

## 9.2 Telehealth Equity Gap

**User:** "We notice that elderly patients and those in rural areas are having difficulty completing video visits. What interventions would you recommend?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Barrier Identification:**
> ```
> Access Barriers:
> ├── Limited broadband internet
> ├── Smartphone/computer access
> ├── Digital literacy
> ├── Language barriers
> └── Disability limitations
> ```
>
> **Intervention Framework:**
> ```
> Technology Solutions:
> ├── Telephone visits as fallback
> ├── Community telehealth kiosks
> ├── Partner with libraries/senior centers
> ├── Equipment lending program
> └── Subsidized internet programs
>
> Patient Support:
> ├── Pre-visit tech test call
> ├── Family member participation
> ├── Interpreters for language
> ├── Closed captions
> └── Simple, large-button interface
>
> Provider Training:
> ├── Alternative exam techniques
> ├── Remote patient monitoring
> ├── Asynchronous options
> └── Modified visit workflows
> ```
>
> **Recommended Actions:**
> ```
> Immediate (0-3 months):
> ├── Implement phone visits
> ├── Create tech support hotline
> └── Distribute tip sheets
>
> Short-term (3-6 months):
> ├── Deploy community kiosks
> ├── Partner with senior centers
> └── Equipment lending program
>
> Long-term (6-12 months):
> ├── Remote monitoring program
> ├── Home-based assessment kits
> └── Evaluate outcomes
> ```
