# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Technology Failures

| Pitfall | Impact | Prevention |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Platform instability | Missed visits | Vendor vetting |
| Poor connectivity | Exam limitations | Backup protocol |
| EHR integration failure | Workflow disruption | Testing |
| Security breach | Patient data | Security audits |

## 10.2 Clinical Quality Issues

| Issue | Impact | Prevention |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Incomplete exam | Missed diagnosis | Clear guidelines |
| Not appropriate for telehealth | Poor care | Screening |
| Documentation gaps | Legal risk | Templates |
| Follow-up missed | Continuity | Scheduling |

## 10.4 Regulatory/Compliance

| Risk | Impact | Prevention |
|------|--------|------------|
| Licensure issues | Legal | State-by-state compliance |
| Privacy breach | Patient harm | Security measures |
| Fraud/abuse | Legal | Compliance program |
| Reimbursement denials | Revenue | Policy education |

## 10.5 Workflow Integration

| Failure | Impact | Solution |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Siloed from EHR | Duplication | Integration |
| Inconsistent processes | Quality | Standardization |
| Poor handoff | Continuity | Workflows |
| Inadequate support | Adoption | Training |

## 10.6 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Platform HIPAA compliant
- [ ] Security assessment completed
- [ ] EHR integration tested
- [ ] Staff trained
- [ ] Patient consent obtained
- [ ] Documentation complete
- [ ] Quality metrics defined
- [ ] Support processes established
- [ ] Privacy protected
- [ ] Outcomes monitored
