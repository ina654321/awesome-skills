# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Clinical Research Standards

- [ICH E6(R2) GCP](https://database.ich.org/sites/default/files/E6_R2_Addendum.pdf) - Good Clinical Practice guidelines
- [FDA 21 CFR Part 312](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfcfr/CFRSearch.cfm?CFRPart=312) - IND regulations
- [FDA 21 CFR Part 11](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/cdrh/cfdocs/cfcfr/CFRSearch.cfm?CFRPart=11) - Electronic records
- [HHS 45 CFR Part 46](https://www.hhs.gov/ohrp/regulations-and-policy/regulations/45-cfr-46/) - Human subjects protections
- [Declaration of Helsinki](https://www.wma.net/policies-post/wma-declaration-of-helsinki/) - Ethical principles

## 7.2 ICH-GCP Essential Documents

| Document | Purpose | Timing |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Protocol | Study design and procedures | Before trial |
| Informed consent | Subject protection | Before enrollment |
| IRB/IEC approval | Ethics review | Before enrollment |
| Investigator CV | Qualifications | Before trial |
| Financial disclosure | Conflict management | Before enrollment |
| Insurance certificate | Liability coverage | Before trial |
| Trial site qualification | Adequate resources | Before trial |

## 7.3 FDA Clinical Hold Types

```
Full Clinical Hold:
- Study cannot start
- No new subjects may be enrolled
- Current subjects must stop treatment
- Reasons: Unreasonable risk, Invalid protocol

Partial Clinical Hold:
- Certain studies/arms搁置
- New enrollment prohibited
- Current subjects may continue if benefit outweighs risk
```

## 7.4 Safety Reporting Timelines

| Report Type | Deadline | Recipient |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| IND safety report (unexpected fatal/life-threatening) | 7 calendar days | FDA |
| IND safety report (unexpected serious) | 15 calendar days | FDA |
| SUSAR (EU) | 7/15 days depending on severity | Ethic committees |
| Annual safety report (DSUR) | Annually | Regulatory authority |
