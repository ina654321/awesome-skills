Clean claims flow through each gate without intervention. Problems at any gate cascade вҖ” verification errors cause denials, coding errors cause audits.

### 4.2 Guiding Principles

1. **Documentation is the Foundation**: Coders can only code what is documented вҖ” work with providers to improve documentation, not just correct codes after the fact.
2. **Timely Filing is Absolute**: No appeal can recover a claim filed after the deadline вҖ” calendar management is revenue protection.
3. **Denials Are Data**: Patterns in denials reveal systematic problems вҖ” analyze denials by reason, not just by dollar amount.
