# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Wrong Site Discovery

**User:** "During the time-out before surgery, the team realizes the surgical site was not marked. What should happen?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **This is a Universal Protocol violation:**
>
> **Immediate Actions:**
> ```
> 1. STOP - Do not proceed with surgery
> 2. Time-out must be completed before any incision
> 3. Surgeon must mark the site
> 4. Re-verify all information
> 5. Document the correction
> ```
>
> **Correct Process:**
> ```
> 1. Verify patient identity
> 2. Verify procedure
> 3. Verify consent matches
> 4. Surgeon marks site with initials
> 5. All team members acknowledge
> 6. Proceed with surgery
> ```
>
> **Documentation:**
> - Document site was unmarked
> - Document marking was completed
> - Document who was involved
> - Incident report may be required
> - Quality review post-procedure

## 9.2 Instrument Count Discrepancy

**User:** "At the final count before skin closure, you count 5 retractors but started with 6. What do you do?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Count Discrepancy Protocol:**
>
> **Step 1: Do NOT Close**
> ```
> - Immediately notify surgeon
> - Surgery must stop
> - Cannot close until resolved
> ```
>
> **Step 2: Investigate**
> ```
> - Re-count with circulator
> - Check drapes and floor
> - Check Mayo stand and back table
> - Inspect wound if visible
> - Check suction canister
> ```
>
> **Step 3: Resolution**
> ```
> Options:
> ├── Retractor found → continue closure
> ├── X-ray ordered → surgeon decides
> ├── Wound exploration → surgeon decision
> └── Document everything
> ```
>
> **Documentation:**
> - When discrepancy discovered
> - All attempts to locate
> - How resolved
> - Surgeon notified
> - Incident report completed
