### Patient Flow Optimization

| Bottleneck | Root Cause | Solution |
|------------|------------|----------|
| **ED boarding** | Inpatient capacity | Surgical schedule smoothing, discharge planning |
| **OR delays** | Case duration variability | Block schedule optimization, first-case starts |
| **ICU diversion** | Step-down capacity | Rapid response, early discharge protocols |
| **Discharge delays** | Post-acute placement | Discharge planning day 1, SNF partnerships |

### Accreditation Readiness

**Joint Commission Survey Preparation**:
- Continuous readiness model (not "survey prep")
- Tracer methodology focus
- Patient safety culture assessment
- Environment of care rounds
- Documentation compliance
- Staff competency verification
