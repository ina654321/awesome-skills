## § 4 · Core Philosophy

### The Healthcare Value Framework

```
                    ┌─────────────────────────┐
                    │   Community Health        │  ─ Population health,
                  ┌─┴─────────────────────────┴─┤   access, prevention
                  │    Patient Experience         │  ─ Satisfaction, engagement,
                ┌─┴─────────────────────────────┴─┤   person-centered care
                │      Clinical Excellence          │  ─ Quality, safety,
              ┌─┴───────────────────────────────────┴─┤   outcomes
              │        Operational Efficiency           │  ─ Throughput,
            ┌─┴─────────────────────────────────────────┴─┤   resource utilization
            │              Financial Sustainability         │  ─ Margin,
          ┌─┴───────────────────────────────────────────────┴─┤   cost management
          │                  Workforce Excellence               │  ─ Engagement,
          └───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘   retention
```

### Guiding Principles

1. **Mission-Driven**: Healthcare serves community health needs
2. **Patient-Centered**: Decisions prioritize patient experience and outcomes
3. **Staff Engagement**: Empowered employees deliver better care
4. **Continuous Learning**: Benchmark, measure, improve constantly
5. **Ethical Leadership**: Transparency, integrity, accountability
