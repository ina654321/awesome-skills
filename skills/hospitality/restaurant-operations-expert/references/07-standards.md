# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Official Documentation

- [FDA Food Code](https://www.fda.gov/food/retail-food-protection/fda-food-code) — Model food safety code adopted by most state/local jurisdictions
- [HACCP Principles & Application Guidelines](https://www.fda.gov/food/hazard-analysis-critical-control-point-haccp/haccp-principles-application-guidelines) — The 7 principles of hazard analysis and critical control points
- [ServSafe Manager Certification](https://www.servsafe.com/servsafe-manager) — ANSI-accredited food protection manager certification
- [National Restaurant Association ANSI Certification](https://www.restaurant.org/certification) — Manager food safety certification
- [FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)](https://www.fda.gov/food/food-safety-modernization-act-fsma) — Preventive controls for human food

## 7.2 Key Metrics & Benchmarks

| Metric | Formula | Target Range | Red Flag |
|--------|---------|---------------|----------|
| **Food Cost %** | Food cost ÷ Food sales × 100 | 28–33% | > 35% |
| **Labor Cost %** | Labor cost ÷ Total sales × 100 | 28–35% | > 38% |
| **Prime Cost %** | (Food + Labor) ÷ Total sales | 55–65% | > 68% |
| **Guest Satisfaction** | Aggregate review score | 4.3–5.0 / 5.0 | < 4.0 |
| **Table Turn Time** | Seat to checkout | QSR: 5–10 min; FSR: 45–60 min | > 90 min |
| **Cover Turnover** | Revenue ÷ # covers | FSR: $25–$60/cover | Below forecast |
| **Waste %** | Waste cost ÷ Food cost | 3–6% | > 8% |
| **Inventory Turn** | COGS ÷ Average inventory | 6–10× per month | < 5× |

## 7.3 Food Safety Temperature Standards

| Category | Safe Temperature |
|----------|-----------------|
| Cold Holding | ≤ 41°F (5°C) |
| Hot Holding | ≥ 135°F (57°C) |
| Cooking — Poultry | ≥ 165°F (74°C) |
| Cooking — Ground Meat | ≥ 160°F (71°C) |
| Cooking — Fish/Shellfish | ≥ 145°F (63°C) |
| Reheating (for hot holding) | ≥ 165°F (74°C) within 2 hours |
| Cooling (cooked food) | 135°F → 70°F in 2h; 70°F → 41°F in 4h |

## 7.5 Recipe & Menu Engineering Standards

| Framework | Purpose | Key Metric |
|-----------|---------|------------|
| **Menu Engineering Matrix** | Profitability optimization | Contribution margin per item |
| **Plate Cost Calculation** | Per-dish ingredient costing | Food cost % per menu item |
| **Menu Mix Analysis** | Popularity vs. profitability | Items sold per week × margin |
| **Recipe Standardization** | Consistency across shifts | Each recipe on costing card |
| **Spec Card System** | Portion and quality standards | Gram weight ± tolerance |

## 7.6 Health Inspection Checkpoints

- [x] Temperature logs for all cold/hot storage (recorded twice daily)
- [x] Date marking for ready-to-eat TCS foods (held > 24 hours)
- [x] Handwashing stations stocked and accessible
- [x] sanitizer concentration correct (50–200 ppm quat or per local code)
- [x] All staff with valid food handler certifications on file
- [x] Pest control log current (last service within 30 days)
- [x] Equipment calibration records (thermometers, sanitizer test kits)
- [x] Allergen management protocols posted in kitchen
- [x] Receiving logs (temperature of incoming product logged)
