# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Restaurant Opening Process

```
Phase 1: Pre-Opening (8–12 weeks before launch)
├── Site selection: demographics, traffic count, competition analysis
├── Lease negotiation: CAM charges, exclusivity clause, build-out allowance
├── Build-out design: kitchen layout (HVAC, grease trap, exhaust), dining room flow
├── Permits: health department, building, liquor license, signage
├── Equipment procurement: major equipment list, delivery schedule, installation
├── Key hires: GM, Executive Chef, Sous Chef, Floor Manager
├── Vendor contracts: food suppliers, linen, pest control, POS vendor
└── Timeline: milestone schedule with accountability chart

Phase 2: Staffing & Training (6–8 weeks before launch)
├── Mass hiring event: open interviews, application processing, offers within 48h
├── Orientation: culture, policies, benefits, safety
├── Menu training: every recipe, every station, every shift
├── Cross-training: servers cover host, hosts cover bussers
├── Practice service: friends & family nights (minimum 2)
├── Systems testing: POS, inventory, scheduling, reporting
└── Feedback loop: collect issues, adjust, repeat practice service

Phase 3: Soft Opening (2 weeks before launch)
├── Limited menu (70% of full menu)
├── Limited hours (e.g., 5pm–9pm only)
├── Invite-only guests: friends, family, local influencers, industry peers
├── Test every system: POS, kitchen timing, server flow, expo, host stand
├── Document every complaint and failure
├── GM on floor every single soft opening shift
└── Adjust: fix 100% of issues before grand opening

Phase 4: Grand Opening & Stabilization (Weeks 1–4)
├── Full menu, full hours
├── Marketing push: social, local press, influencer outreach
├── Extra staff scheduled (110–120% of projected need)
├── Leadership on floor all shifts (GM, Chef, Sous)
├── Daily debrief: 15 min, what worked / what broke / what to fix
├── 7-day forecast vs. actual revenue reconciliation
└── Transition out of "opening mode" by week 3
```

## 8.2 Daily Shift Workflow

### AM Shift Opening (6am–2pm for breakfast/lunch concepts)

```
6:00 AM — Manager arrives
├── Review yesterday's close: waste log, revenue, guest feedback
├── Check walk-in/refrigerator temps: log on temperature sheet
├── Confirm today's delivery schedule
└── Set up manager station: reports, guest list, VIP notes

6:30 AM — BOH prep crew arrives
├── Prep list review with sous chef
├── Portioning: proteins, sauces, bases per spec cards
├── Date mark all prep items (use-by date = prep date + 3 days max)
└── Mise en place: every station, every item, labeled

7:00 AM — FOH arrives
├── Section assignments
├── Daily specials walk-through (server tasting)
├── Table conditions check: linens, glassware, silverware
├── Reservation book review: covers, special requests, allergies
└── Team huddle: 5-min briefing (specials, VIPs, goals)

11:00 AM — Lunch service begins
├── Host: greet, seat, manage waitlist
├── Server: order, check allergies, ring in accurately
├── Expo: time courses, quality check, presentation
├── Manager: monitor pacing, guest satisfaction, solve problems
└── Kitchen: fire tickets in sequence, call times respected

2:00 PM — Shift change / Lunch close
├── Handoff: what's prepped for PM, what's depleted
├── Shift report: covers, revenue, comps, complaints
├── Temp checks: all refrigeration units
└── Afternoon prep: items that can be done ahead for dinner
```

### PM Shift (Dinner service: 5pm–close)

```
5:00 PM — PM crew arrives
├── Full team huddle: dinner specials, private dining notes
├── Reset dining room
└── Kitchen: fresh prep, station setup, grill/oven preheat

5:30 PM — Dinner service begins
├── Same flow as lunch: host → server → expo → kitchen
├── Higher touch: more courses, wine service, dessert
└── Manager on floor: active guest engagement

10:00 PM — Last seating / Wind down
├── No new tables after last call (communicate clearly)
├── Begin closing procedures for stations
└── Manager: start closing checklist

11:00 PM — Close
├── Full breakdown: clean, sanitize every station
├── Temperature logs: all refrigeration (log readings)
├── Deep clean: floors, exhaust hoods, grease traps
├── Inventory: par check for tomorrow (what's low?)
├── Cash reconciliation: POS reports vs. cash drawer
├── Security: lock up, alarm set, safe drop
└── Shift report: final numbers, issues for tomorrow's briefing
```

## 8.3 Food Safety Daily Checklist

- [ ] Morning temp log: all refrigeration ≤ 41°F, freezer ≤ 0°F
- [ ] Sanitizer concentration verified (quat test strips)
- [ ] Handwashing stations stocked (soap, paper towels)
- [ ] Date marking check: all ready-to-eat TCS foods dated
- [ ] Receiving: incoming product temps logged (refrigerated ≤ 41°F)
- [ ] Pest control: no activity signs (drop traps checked)
- [ ] Allergy protocols: server team briefed on top allergens
- [ ] Evening temp log: all refrigeration ≤ 41°F
- [ ] Hot holding: all hot-held items ≥ 135°F
- [ ] Closing: all equipment off / proper night mode / alarm set

## 8.4 Menu Engineering Workflow

```
Step 1: Data Collection (2–4 weeks)
├── Pull POS menu mix report: items sold per week, avg check, item price
├── Pull food cost report: actual COGS per item
├── Calculate contribution margin per item: (price − food cost) × sold/week
└── Source guest feedback: reviews mentioning specific dishes

Step 2: Classification (use Menu Engineering Matrix)
├── X-axis: popularity (% of total covers ordering this item)
├── Y-axis: contribution margin
├── Stars (high popularity, high margin): feature prominently
├── Workhorses (high popularity, low margin): raise price or reduce portion
├── Puzzles (low popularity, high margin): promote or reposition
└── Dogs (low popularity, low margin): remove immediately

Step 3: Implementation
├── Rewrite menu descriptions for Stars
├── Move puzzles to "Chef's Selections" or feature board
├── Raise prices on Workhorses 3–5%
├── Cut Dogs from menu (reduce to 25–30 items from 45)
└── Test new prices for 4 weeks; monitor guest reaction

Step 4: Monitor & Iterate
├── Weekly menu mix analysis: are Stars still selling?
├── Food cost % tracking: did it improve?
├── Guest feedback: is quality consistent on new menu?
└── Adjust seasonally: rotate puzzles in, introduce new Stars
```

## 8.5 Incident Response Workflow

```
Guest Complaint — Food Safety / Foreign Object
├── Remove dish: take it off the table immediately
├── Apologize: "I am so sorry. This is completely unacceptable."
├── Compensate: offer remake, comp the meal, and a gift card for return
├── Collect info: guest name, contact, what they found
├── Document: incident report form within 30 minutes
├── Escalate: GM notified within 15 minutes
└── Follow up: GM calls guest within 2 hours personally

Health Inspector Arrival
├── Greet warmly: "Welcome. I'm [name], the manager on duty."
├── Don't volunteer information; answer questions directly
├── Provide requested documents (temp logs, certifications, pest control records)
├── Walk inspector through kitchen; don't touch equipment during inspection
├── Take notes on every observation (not just violations)
├── Sign the inspection report; thank them for their time
└── If violations: correct on the spot if possible; schedule re-inspection

Labor Violation Concern (employee raises issue)
├── Listen without admitting fault
├── Document: what the employee claims, when, which shifts
├── Escalate to HR / ownership immediately
├── Do not retaliate; do not promise resolution until facts are known
└── Consult employment attorney if claim involves > $5K or class action risk
```
