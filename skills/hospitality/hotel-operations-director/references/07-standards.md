# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Hotel Industry Standards & Benchmarks

| Metric | Formula | Target | Luxury | Upscale | Select-Service |
|--------|---------|--------|--------|---------|----------------|
| **RevPAR Index** | Hotel RevPAR ÷ Comp Set RevPAR × 100 | > 100 | > 110 | > 105 | > 95 |
| **ADR** | Room Revenue ÷ Rooms Sold | Market competitive | $250–$500 | $150–$250 | $90–$150 |
| **Occupancy %** | Rooms Sold ÷ Rooms Available | 65–85% | 70–85% | 65–80% | 70–85% |
| **GOPPAR** | GOP ÷ Available Rooms | > $50/room | > $120/room | > $60/room | > $35/room |
| **Labor %** | Labor Cost ÷ Total Revenue | 28–35% | 35–40% | 30–35% | 28–32% |
| **Guest Satisfaction** | Survey / Review Score | 4.2 / 5.0 | 4.5+ | 4.2+ | 4.0+ |
| **NRevPAR** | RevPAR − CAC (booking fees) | Maximize | Minimize OTA | Minimize OTA | Balance |

## 7.4 Housekeeping & Maintenance Standards

| Standard | Metric | Frequency |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| **Room Turnover** | 25–30 min standard; 15–20 min express | Per checkout |
| **Deep Clean** | Full detail (windows, baseboards, vents) | Every 7th departure or monthly |
| **Preventive Maintenance** | PM schedule completion rate | > 95% on-time |
| **Work Order Response** | Guest-facing issue resolution | < 2 hours |
| **Capital Reserve** | Annual maintenance budget | 1–3% of gross revenue |

## 7.5 Front Office Service Standards

| Checkpoint | Standard | Non-Negotiable |
|------------|----------|----------------|
| **Check-in Time** | < 3 minutes | Warm greeting, name usage |
| **Check-out Time** | < 2 minutes (express) | Folio accuracy, no surprises |
| **Reservation Confirmation** | Within 24 hours | Rate, dates, special requests |
| **Room Ready** | By 3pm guaranteed | 100% of confirmed arrivals |
| **Call Answer** | < 3 rings / < 30 sec | Warm transfer, not dump |
| **VIP Recognition** | Per VIP tier protocol | Complimentary amenity, GM note |
| **Complaint Response** | Within 10 min on property | Generous compensation |

## 7.7 Staffing Benchmarks

| Role | Labor % Allocation | Scheduling Ratio |
|------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Front Desk** | 8–12% of rooms labor | 1:50 (guests:FTE) |
| **Housekeeping** | 40–50% of rooms labor | 1:14–1:18 (rooms:FTE) |
| **Maintenance** | 5–10% of rooms labor | 1:80–1:100 rooms |
| **F&B (if applicable)** | 30–40% of F&B revenue | Per industry F&B standards |
| **Management** | 10–15% of total labor | 1 GM + 2–3 department heads |
