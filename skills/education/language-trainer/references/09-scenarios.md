## Scenario 9.1: Beginner English: First Conversation

**User:** "完全不会英语，想从头开始学，应该怎么开始？" (I don't know any English, want to start from scratch. How should I begin?)

**Language Trainer Approach:**

> **零基础入门方案 (Zero to Beginner Plan)**
>
> **Step 1: 基础词汇（第一个月）**
> ```
> 每天学习10个新单词：
>
> 第一周：数字、颜色、家庭成员
> - one, two, three, four, five...
> - red, blue, green, yellow, black, white...
> - mother, father, sister, brother...
>
> 第二周：日常物品
> - table, chair, door, window, phone, book...
>
> 第三周：动词（最常用的20个）
> - be, have, do, go, get, make, say, think, know, want...
>
> 第四周：实用短语
> - How are you? → I'm fine, thank you.
> - What's your name? → My name is...
> ```
>
> **Step 2: 听力输入（每天30分钟）**
> - ESL Podcasts（慢速英语）
> - BBC Learning English
> - 不需要全部听懂，熟悉英语的声音和节奏
>
> **Step 3: 口语练习（从第一天开始）**
> - 跟读（Shadowing）：听一句，跟读一句
> - 自言自语：用英语描述正在做的事情
>
> **关键提醒：**
> - 坚持比强度重要：每天15分钟好过一周一次3小时
> - 听是基础：听力先行，口语自然会变好

## Scenario 9.2: Intermediate: Improving Fluency

**User:** "英语学了很多年，语法都知道，但就是说不流利，怎么突破？" (Studied English for years, know all grammar, but can't speak fluently. How to break through?)

> **流利度突破方案 (Fluency Breakthrough)**
>
> **常见"流利度杀手"：**
> - 翻译思维（中译英）
> - 完美主义（一定要想清楚再说）
> - 词汇不够（不知道说什么）
>
> **方法1: 词汇块（Chunks）**
> ```
> ❌ 单词：however, although, nevertheless
> ✅ 短语：
> - however things may go = 无论事情如何发展
> - although I understand = 虽然我理解
>
> 学习搭配（Collocations）：
> - make a decision (不是 do a decision)
> - take a break (不是 give a break)
> ```
>
> **方法2: 影子跟读（Shadowing）**
> - 选择1-2分钟音频，先听2-3遍
> - 一边听一边同步跟读（延迟1-2秒）
> - 模仿语调、语速、重音
>
> **方法3: 限时反应（Timed Response）**
> - 给问题，必须在X秒内回答
> - 不许犹豫，不许等"完美答案"
>
> **方法4: 思维转换**
> ```
> ❌ 翻译思维：先想中文，再翻译成英文
> ✅ 直接反应：用英语思考，直接说
> ```

## Scenario 9.3: Pronunciation: Reducing Accent

**User:** "我的英语发音不准，尤其是th/s, r/l这种音，应该怎么练？" (My pronunciation is inaccurate, especially th/s, r/l sounds. How to practice?)

> **英语发音训练方案**
>
> **常见问题（中国学习者）：**
> | Problem | Example | Solution |
> |---------|---------|----------|
> | TH → S/Z | think → sink | 舌尖放在齿间，轻轻咬住 |
> | R → L | rice → lice | R是舌根抬起，L是舌尖抬起 |
> | V → W | very → wery | 上齿轻触下唇 |
>
> **最小对练习（Minimal Pairs）：**
> ```
> th/s: think, path, theme
> r/l: rice, correct, row
> v/w: very, vest, vet
> ```
>
> **连读和弱读：**
> - "kind of" → "kinda"
> - "going to" → "gonna"
> - "want to" → "wanna"
>
> **语调（Intonation）：**
> - 陈述句：降调 ↓
> - 一般疑问句：升调 ↗
> - 列举：前升后降

## Anti-Patterns Quick Reference (§10)

| Severity | Anti-Pattern | Fix |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| 🔴 High | Learning rules without practice | 80% of study time in using language |
| 🔴 High | Waiting until "ready" | Speak from day one |
| 🔴 High | Perfect pronunciation obsession | Intelligibility over accent |
| 🟡 Medium | Learning words in isolation | Learn in sentences and collocations |
| 🟡 Medium | Avoiding native content | Gradual introduction (i+1) |
| 🟡 Medium | Comparing to others | Compare yourself to yourself last month |
