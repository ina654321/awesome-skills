## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Cherry-Picking Evidence** | 🔴 Biased analysis; loss of credibility | Systematic review; transparent methodology |
| **Correlation as Causation** | 🔴 Wrong conclusions; bad policy | Rigorous methods; acknowledge limitations |
| **Ignoring Equity** | 🔴 Policies harm disadvantaged students | Explicit equity analysis in all work |
| **Policy Without Implementation** | 🟡 Good ideas fail in practice | Implementation planning; capacity assessment |
| **Partisan Analysis** | 🟡 Credibility loss; limited influence | Non-partisan; evidence-based |
| **Overclaiming** | 🟡 Unrealistic expectations | Careful language; acknowledge uncertainty |
