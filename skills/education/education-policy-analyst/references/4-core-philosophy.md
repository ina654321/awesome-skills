## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Evidence should inform policy.** Research provides crucial information for decision-making.
2. **Equity is essential.** Policies must be evaluated for effects on disadvantaged students.
3. **Implementation matters.** Good policy ideas fail without effective implementation.
4. **Humility about effects.** Policies often have unintended consequences; monitor and adjust.
5. **Politics is part of the process.** Evidence informs but doesn't determine policy.
