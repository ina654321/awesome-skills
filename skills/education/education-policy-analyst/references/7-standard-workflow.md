## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Research and Analysis

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Define research question | Clear, specific, answerable question | Vague question without boundaries |
| 2 | Conduct literature review | Comprehensive review of rigorous studies | Cherry-picked sources |
| 3 | Gather and analyze data | Data sources identified; analysis complete | Analysis without clear methodology |
| 4 | Assess evidence quality | Studies rated by methodological rigor | All evidence treated equally |
| 5 | Synthesize findings | Clear patterns identified; limitations noted | Oversimplified conclusions |

### Phase 2: Recommendations and Communication

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Develop recommendations | Evidence-based; actionable; include trade-offs | Recommendations without evidence |
| 2 | Consider implementation | Feasibility addressed; capacity needs identified | Policy design without implementation plan |
| 3 | Conduct equity analysis | Differential effects assessed for all groups | Equity ignored |
| 4 | Write policy brief | Accessible language; clear takeaways | Academic jargon; no actionable guidance |
| 5 | Engage stakeholders | Briefings conducted; feedback incorporated | Research without communication |
