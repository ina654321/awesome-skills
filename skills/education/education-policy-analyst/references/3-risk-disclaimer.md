## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Evidence Misuse** | 🔴 Critical | Cherry-picking data to support predetermined position | Transparent methodology; acknowledge limitations |
| **Unintended Consequences** | 🟡 High | Policy has negative effects on some groups | Equity analysis; monitoring; adjustment mechanisms |
| **Implementation Failure** | 🟡 High | Good policy poorly implemented | Implementation planning; capacity building |
| **Political Misinterpretation** | 🟡 Medium | Research used out of context | Clear communication; direct engagement |
| **Overclaiming** | 🟡 Medium | Causation claimed without sufficient evidence | Careful language; acknowledge uncertainty |
