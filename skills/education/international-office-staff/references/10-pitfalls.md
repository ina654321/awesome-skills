# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|---------------|:---:|-----------|
| 1 | **Late issuance of acceptance documents** | High | Set calendar alerts: issue I-20 within 5 business days of enrollment confirmation |
| 2 | **Assuming all students understand the system** | High | Use plain language; translate key documents; verify comprehension verbally |
| 3 | **Treating visa issues as student problems** | High | Visa compliance is shared responsibility; proactively provide documentation |
| 4 | **One-size-fits-all orientation** | Medium | Segment by region, language, and prior educational background |
| 5 | **Understaffing during peak season** | Medium | Hire part-time coordinators 6 weeks before each intake |

## 10.2 Compliance Risks

1. **SEVIS errors**: Always double-check name spelling, program dates, and SEVIS ID against passport
2. **Late enrollment reporting**: Report enrollment within 30 days of start date or risk program eligibility
3. **Unauthorized employment**: Students must understand CPT/OPT limits; unauthorized work = visa violation
4. **Unauthorized absence**: Students leaving the country without DSO permission lose status
5. **Over-enrollment pressure**: Never pressure advisors to enroll students just to maintain numbers

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Acceptance documents issued within 10 business days of enrollment confirmation
- [ ] Pre-departure orientation delivered to all admitted international students
- [ ] Visa application support materials customized by country of origin
- [ ] Orientation attendance tracked; absent students followed up within 48 hours
- [ ] SEVIS check-in completed within 30 days of program start
- [ ] Annual review of all active international students' enrollment status
- [ ] Emergency contact information for all students current and accessible
- [ ] Partner institution agreements reviewed and renewed on schedule
- [ ] Student exchange balance tracked quarterly
- [ ] All staff trained on GDPR/PDPA data handling for student records
