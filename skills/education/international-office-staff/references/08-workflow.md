# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Inbound International Student Lifecycle

```
Pre-Arrival (3-6 months before start)
├── Application review and admission decision
├── Issue Acceptance Letter and I-20/COE/CoE
├── Visa application guidance (country-specific)
├── Pre-departure orientation (virtual or in-person)
├── Arrange airport pickup and temporary housing
└── Send arrival guide with campus map and local SIM card info

Arrival Week
├── Airport pickup service
├── Check-in and on-campus housing setup
├── Bank account assistance (with international banking partners)
├── Local SIM card and mobile money setup
├── Campus tour with student buddy
└── Medical check-up coordination (if required by visa)

Semester 1 Support
├── International student orientation (mandatory)
├── Course registration with academic advising
├── Cross-cultural adjustment counseling
├── Employment workshop (on-campus work, CPT/OPT eligibility)
├── Social integration events (culture nights, language exchanges)
└── 30-day check-in meeting with advisor

Ongoing Support
├── Academic progress monitoring per semester
├── Visa status maintenance review (SEVIS check-in)
├── Health insurance enrollment and claims support
├── Cultural and religious accommodation coordination
├── Emergency preparedness briefing (natural disaster, civil unrest)
└── Pre-graduation transition planning (post-study work visa, return home)
```

## 8.2 Exchange Partnership Workflow

1. **Partner outreach**: Identify potential partner institutions; check accreditation equivalency
2. **MOU drafting**: Legal review; academic senate approval; finance office sign-off
3. **Annual review**: Student exchange balance; academic quality of outbound vs inbound; partner satisfaction survey
4. **Credit transfer**: Outbound student submits syllabus → home institution evaluates → pre-approval issued before departure
5. **Return processing**: Official transcript requested within 4 weeks of exchange end; credit posted within 30 days

## 8.3 Visa Compliance Workflow

1. New international student: Complete SEVIS check-in within 30 days of program start
2. Address change: Report to DSO within 10 days via SEVIS
3. Course load reduction: Must receive DSO approval; maintain full-time status
4. Employment authorization: CPT/OPT application filed with USCIS before start date
5. Program extension: DSO authorization required before I-20 expiration
6. Transfer between schools: SEVIS release within 60 days of program completion
