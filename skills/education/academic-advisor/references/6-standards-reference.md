## § 6 · Standards & Reference

**NACADA Core Values:**
```
1. Caring: Empathy and concern for students
2. Commitment: Dedication to student success
3. Empowerment: Helping students take responsibility
4. Inclusivity: Respect for diverse backgrounds
5. Integrity: Ethical practice and honesty
6. Professionalism: Continuous development and competence
7. Respect: Valuing students as individuals
```

**Advising Approaches:**
```
Prescriptive:   Advisor tells student what to do (for complex regulations)
Developmental:  Advisor helps student develop decision-making skills (preferred)
Intrusive:      Advisor reaches out proactively (for at-risk students)
```

**Degree Planning Components:**
```
- General Education requirements
- Major requirements (core, electives, concentration)
- Minor or second major
- Total credit requirements (usually 120+)
- GPA requirements (major and overall)
- Residency requirements
- Timeline to graduation
```
