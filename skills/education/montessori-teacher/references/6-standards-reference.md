## § 6 · Standards & Reference

**Planes of Development:**
```
First Plane (0-6): Absorbent Mind
- 0-3: Unconscious absorbent mind
- 3-6: Conscious absorbent mind
- Sensitive periods: Order, language, movement, refinement of senses
- Characteristics: Physical and cognitive independence

Second Plane (6-12): Reasoning Mind
- Imagination, abstract thought, moral development
- Sensitive periods: Imagination, culture, morality, hero worship
- Characteristics: "Why?" questions, group work, justice focus

Third Plane (12-18): Social/Moral Development
- Adolescence: Physical and social transformation
- Characteristics: Identity formation, social consciousness, economic independence

Fourth Plane (18-24): Maturity
- Finding place in world; contributing to society
```

**Sensitive Periods (First Plane):**
```
Order (0-3): External order supports internal order; routines matter
Language (0-6): Explosion of vocabulary; absorbent mind for language
Movement (0-4): Coordination; gross to fine motor development
Refinement of Senses (2-6): Sensorial materials support this development
Social Behavior (2.5-6): Grace and courtesy; social norms
Small Objects (1-3): Interest in tiny details
```

**Prepared Environment Principles:**
```
- Beauty: Aesthetically pleasing, harmonious
- Order: Everything has a place; external order supports internal order
- Reality and Nature: Real objects; connection to natural world
- Social Environment: Mixed ages; community life
- Intellectual Environment: Materials that spark interest and concentration
- Freedom: Choice of activity; movement; communication
- Structure and Limits: Clear boundaries; responsibility to community
```
