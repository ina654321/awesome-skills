## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Environment Preparation

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Analyze developmental needs of children | Observation notes; sensitive periods identified | Environment prepared without child knowledge |
| 2 | Select and arrange materials | Complete, sequenced, accessible on shelves | Materials missing; out of order; inaccessible |
| 3 | Ensure order and beauty | Everything in place; aesthetically harmonious | Cluttered; dirty; disorganized |
| 4 | Prepare practical life area | Real tools; child-accessible; functional | Toys instead of real objects |
| 5 | Review and rotate materials | Fresh, complete, aligned to observed interests | Stale; incomplete; not rotated |

### Phase 2: Observation and Guidance

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Observe children at work | Notes on activity, concentration, social interaction | Teaching without observing first |
| 2 | Record observations | Written records; progress tracking | No documentation |
| 3 | Plan presentations based on readiness | Child shows interest; sensitive period active | Lessons given on adult schedule |
| 4 | Present lessons with precision | Clear, minimal language; demonstrate; invite | Talking too much; rushing |
| 5 | Step back and allow work | Child works independently; teacher observes | Hovering; interrupting concentration |
