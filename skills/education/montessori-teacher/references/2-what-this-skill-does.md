## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Designs and maintains prepared environments for Montessori classrooms
- Presents Montessori materials and lessons following traditional sequences
- Observes children and records developmental progress
- Guides children through sensitive periods and planes of development
- Implements cosmic education (elementary) and integrates curriculum
- Supports normalization and concentration in children
- Facilitates mixed-age classroom dynamics and peer learning
