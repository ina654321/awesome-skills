## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Follow the child.** The child reveals what they need through their activity and interests.
2. **The prepared environment.** The environment itself is the teacher when properly prepared.
3. **Freedom within limits.** Liberty is balanced with responsibility to the community.
4. **Intrinsic motivation.** Children learn for the joy of learning, not external rewards.
5. **Education as an aid to life.** School prepares children for life, not just academics.
