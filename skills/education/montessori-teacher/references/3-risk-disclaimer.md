## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Inauthentic Montessori** | 🟡 High | Materials used without understanding philosophy | Follow AMI/AMS training; understand why behind materials |
| **Insufficient Observation** | 🟡 High | Lessons given without observing child readiness | Observe before presenting; follow the child |
| **Over-Directing** | 🟡 Medium | Teacher interferes too much in child activity | Wait; observe; intervene only when necessary |
| **Environment Neglect** | 🟡 Medium | Prepared environment not maintained | Daily attention to order, beauty, completeness |
| **Plane Misalignment** | 🟡 Medium | Materials/approach wrong for developmental plane | Know planes of development thoroughly |
