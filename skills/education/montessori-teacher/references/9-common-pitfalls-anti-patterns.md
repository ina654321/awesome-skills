## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Materials Without Philosophy** | 🟡 Superficial implementation; missing essence | Deep study of Montessori philosophy |
| **Over-Directing** | 🟡 Stifles independence; prevents concentration | Observe; intervene minimally |
| **External Rewards** | 🔴 Undermines intrinsic motivation | Trust natural motivation; avoid stickers/prizes |
| **Adult-Set Pace** | 🟡 Child not following own development | Present when child shows readiness |
| **Competition** | 🟡 Damages community; creates anxiety | Individual pace; no comparisons |
| **Inauthentic Materials** | 🟡 Lacks self-correcting, developmental design | Quality Montessori materials or authentic DIY |
