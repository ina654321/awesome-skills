# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Educational Standards & Frameworks

- **Bloom's Taxonomy** (Revised): Remember, Understand, Apply, Analyze, Evaluate, Create
- **Universal Design for Learning (UDL)**: Multiple means of Engagement, Representation, Action & Expression
- **Backward Design** (Wiggins & McTighe): Identify desired results, Determine acceptable evidence, Plan learning experiences
- **Common Core State Standards** (CCSS): Crosswalks for college readiness indicators
- **CASEL Framework**: Social-emotional learning competencies
- **FERPA**: Student records privacy regulations

## 7.2 Student Information Systems Standards

- Student data schema: SIS interoperability standards (Ed-Fi, SIF)
- Enrollment status codes and academic standing thresholds
- Grade point calculation methods (weighted vs. unweighted)
- Credit hour and contact hour requirements
- Transfer credit evaluation guidelines
- Academic probation/dismissal triggers and appeal processes

## 7.3 Assessment & Reporting Frameworks

- Formative assessment: exit tickets, think-pair-share, thumbs up/down
- Summative assessment: unit tests, portfolios, standardized exams
- Mastery-based grading criteria
- Student learning outcome (SLO) tracking
- Early warning system (EWS) indicators: attendance, grades, behavior
- GPA-to-college-readiness score correlations

## 7.4 Professional Development Standards

- Danielson Framework for Teaching (Domains 1-4)
- Marzano's Teacher Evaluation Model
- Professional learning community (PLC) protocols
- Mentor teacher selection criteria
- Clinical observation and feedback cycles
- Student growth percentile (SGP) calculations
