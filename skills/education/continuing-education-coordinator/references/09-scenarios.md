# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Regulatory Change Adaptation

**User:** "A new state regulation requires all nurses to complete 4 hours of implicit bias training by next year. We need to develop this training quickly. How do you approach this?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Requirements Analysis:**
> ```
> Regulatory Requirements:
> ├── 4 contact hours
> ├── Implicit bias content
> ├── Evidence-based curriculum
> ├── Accredited provider
> └── Completion documentation
>
> Timeline: 12 months
> Audience: 500 nurses
> ```
>
> **Development Plan:**
> ```
> Phase 1: Content Development (3 months)
> ├── Hire subject matter expert
> ├── Develop learning objectives
> ├── Create content outline
> ├── Develop materials
> └── Submit for ANCC accreditation
>
> Phase 2: Accreditation (2 months)
> ├── Prepare application
> ├── Submit with curriculum
> ├── Respond to feedback
> └── Receive accreditation
>
> Phase 3: Launch (ongoing)
> ├── LMS integration
> ├── Registration system
> ├── Marketing campaign
> └── Support resources
> ```
>
> **Delivery Options:**
> ```
> Option A: Self-paced online
> ├── Flexible
> ├── Trackable
> ├── Most efficient for 500 learners
> └── Cost-effective
>
> Option B: Live virtual
> ├── Interactive
> ├── Consistent messaging
> ├── Schedule challenges
> └── Multiple sessions needed
>
> Recommendation: Hybrid approach
> - Core content: Self-paced (3 hours)
> - Live session: Case studies (1 hour)
> ```
>
> **Success Metrics:**
> - Completion rate >90%
> - Post-test pass rate >80%
> - Evaluation satisfaction >4/5

## 9.2 Engagement Challenge

**User:** "Our mandatory compliance training has only a 45% completion rate. Learners complain it's boring and they can't remember anything. How do you improve this?"

**Expert:**
> **Analysis:**
>
> **Root Cause:**
> ```
> Common Issues:
> ├── Passive watching only
> ├── No real-world application
> ├── Text-heavy slides
> ├── Lengthy modules
> ├── Irrelevant to daily work
> └── Compliance fear vs. learning
> ```
>
> **Redesign Approach:**
> ```
> Microlearning:
> ├── 5-10 minute segments
> ├── Single learning objective each
> ├── Just-in-time delivery
> ├── Mobile-friendly
> └── spaced repetition
>
> Interactive Elements:
> ├── Branching scenarios
> ├── Decision-point challenges
> ├── Social discussion prompts
> ├── Real case studies
> └── Application exercises
>
> Gamification:
> ├── Points and badges
> ├── Leaderboards
> ├── Progress visualization
> ├── Unlockable content
> └── Certificates
>
> Motivation:
> ├── Connect to professional values
> ├── Show real consequences
> ├── Recognize completers
> ├── Manager accountability
> └── Celebration
> ```
>
> **Expected Outcome:**
> - Target: 85% completion
> - 20% improvement in retention
> - Satisfaction >4/5
