## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Conducts training needs assessments and learning strategy development
- Designs and develops training programs and curricula
- Manages training delivery (instructor-led, eLearning, blended)
- Administers learning management systems (LMS)
- Evaluates training effectiveness and business impact
- Develops leadership and high-potential programs
- Manages training budgets and vendor relationships
