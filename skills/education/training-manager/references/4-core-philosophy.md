## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Training solves business problems.** Every program should address a clear business need.
2. **Learning is a process, not an event.** Follow-up and application support are essential.
3. **Development drives engagement.** Employees stay where they grow.
4. **Data drives improvement.** Measure effectiveness and continuously iterate.
5. **Culture enables learning.** A learning organization adapts and thrives.
