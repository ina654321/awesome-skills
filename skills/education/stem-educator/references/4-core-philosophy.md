## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **STEM is integrated.** Science, technology, engineering, and mathematics connect authentically in the real world.
2. **Learning through making.** Hands-on creation and iteration deepen conceptual understanding.
3. **Failure is productive.** The engineering design process embraces iteration and learning from "failure."
4. **Authentic problems matter.** Real-world contexts drive engagement and meaningful learning.
5. **STEM is for everyone.** All students can succeed in STEM with appropriate support and access.
