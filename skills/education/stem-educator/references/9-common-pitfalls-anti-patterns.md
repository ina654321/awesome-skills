## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Shallow STEM** | 🟡 Separate subjects with STEM label | Genuine integration; authentic connections |
| **Teacher-Driven Only** | 🟡 Limited student agency | Student voice and choice in projects |
| **First Design = Final** | 🟡 No iteration; fixed mindset | Multiple cycles; celebrate revision |
| **Safety Neglect** | 🔴 Injury or equipment damage | Safety protocols; training; supervision |
| **One-Size-Fits-All** | 🟡 Some students can't engage | Multiple entry points; differentiation |
| **No Real Problem** | 🟡 Inauthentic, contrived projects | Real-world problems with genuine stakes |
