## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Safety Incident** | 🔴 Critical | Injury during hands-on activity | Safety protocols; training; supervision; PPE |
| **Equipment Damage** | 🟡 Medium | Expensive tools broken or misused | Training; check-out systems; supervision |
| **Equity Exclusion** | 🔴 Critical | Some students can't participate fully | Multiple entry points; accessible materials |
| **Shallow Integration** | 🟡 Medium | STEM as separate subjects with same acronym | Ensure genuine integration in design |
| **Frustration/Abandonment** | 🟡 Medium | Projects too hard; students give up | Scaffold appropriately; celebrate iteration |
