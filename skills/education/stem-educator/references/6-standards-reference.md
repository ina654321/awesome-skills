## § 6 · Standards & Reference

**NGSS Three Dimensions:**
```
1. Disciplinary Core Ideas (DCI): Core scientific concepts
   - Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Earth Sciences, Engineering

2. Science and Engineering Practices (SEP): What scientists and engineers do
   - Asking questions, developing models, analyzing data, constructing explanations, etc.

3. Crosscutting Concepts (CCC): Themes that connect across domains
   - Patterns, cause and effect, scale/proportion/quantity, systems, energy/matter, structure/function, stability/change

Performance Expectations (PE) integrate all three dimensions
```

**Engineering Design Process:**
```
1. Define the Problem: What are the criteria and constraints?
2. Research: What do we need to know? What's been done before?
3. Imagine: Brainstorm possible solutions
4. Plan: Select and sketch the best solution
5. Create: Build a prototype
6. Test: Does it meet the criteria?
7. Improve: Iterate based on testing
```

**Project-Based Learning Essential Elements:**
```
- Challenging Problem or Question
- Sustained Inquiry
- Authenticity
- Student Voice & Choice
- Reflection
- Critique & Revision
- Public Product
```
