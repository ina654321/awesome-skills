# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 IT Training Session Workflow

```
Pre-Training (1-2 weeks before)
├── Conduct needs analysis with manager/stakeholder
├── Assess trainee baseline knowledge (pre-assessment)
├── Customize curriculum to organizational context
├── Book training room (min. one week before)
├── Ensure all trainees have lab environment access
├── Prepare troubleshooting guides for common errors
└── Send pre-reading or video 3 days before session

Training Day
├── 15 min: Welcome, objectives review, icebreaker
├── 60 min: Instruction block 1 (concept + demo)
├── 15 min: Hands-on practice 1 (guided lab)
├── 15 min: Break
├── 60 min: Instruction block 2 (advanced concepts)
├── 15 min: Hands-on practice 2 (independent lab)
├── 15 min: Scenario-based exercise (real-world problem)
├── 15 min: Q&A and wrap-up
└── Send post-training survey and resources

Post-Training (within 1 week)
├── Grade assessments and provide individual feedback
├── Follow up with manager on on-the-job application plan
├── Address any trainee questions that came up post-session
└── Update training materials based on feedback
```

## 8.2 Blended Learning Workflow

1. **Self-paced (30-40%)**: Video lectures, reading, interactive modules — completed before face-to-face session
2. **Virtual instructor (20-30%)**: Live online Q&A, demonstrations, group discussions — recorded for replay
3. **In-person (30-40%)**: Hands-on labs, scenario exercises, peer collaboration
4. **On-the-job (ongoing)**: Job aids, cheat sheets, Slack/Teams support channel

## 8.3 Assessment Design Workflow

1. **Define objectives**: What should trainees be able to do after training? (Use Bloom's taxonomy: Level 2+)
2. **Build rubric**: Each objective gets its own criterion on the assessment
3. **Write items**: Mix question types — multiple choice for knowledge, hands-on labs for application
4. **Pilot test**: Administer to 3-5 colleagues; check for ambiguous wording
5. **Set passing threshold**: 70-80% for certification-track; 60% for awareness-level
6. **Retake policy**: Define number of attempts and cooling-off period between attempts
