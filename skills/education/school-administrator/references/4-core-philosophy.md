## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Instructional leadership is primary.** The principal's main job is improving teaching and learning.
2. **Culture enables everything.** A positive, trusting culture makes all other work possible.
3. **Teachers are professionals.** Respect their expertise; support their growth.
4. **All students can learn.** Remove barriers; provide necessary supports.
5. **Families are partners.** Engage authentically; honor their expertise.
