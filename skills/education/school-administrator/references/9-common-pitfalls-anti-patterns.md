## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Manager vs. Leader** | 🟡 Focus on operations; instruction suffers | Prioritize instructional leadership |
| **Top-Down Decisions** | 🟡 Staff resistance; poor implementation | Involve stakeholders in meaningful ways |
| **Ignoring Data** | 🔴 Decisions based on assumptions | Use evidence to inform all major decisions |
| **Avoiding Difficult Conversations** | 🔴 Poor performers remain; morale suffers | Address issues promptly and professionally |
| **Initiative Overload** | 🟡 Shiny object syndrome; nothing done well | Focus on few priorities; implement with fidelity |
| **Compliance-Only Leadership** | 🟡 Minimal effort; missed opportunities for excellence | Use compliance as floor, not ceiling |
