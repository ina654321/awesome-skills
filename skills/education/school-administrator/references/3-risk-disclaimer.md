## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Legal Non-compliance** | 🔴 Critical | Violation of special education, employment, or safety laws | Regular legal audits; consult counsel when uncertain |
| **Safety Incident** | 🔴 Critical | Student or staff injury due to inadequate safety measures | Emergency protocols; regular drills; risk assessments |
| **Staff Evaluation Error** | 🔴 Critical | Due process violation in employment decisions | Follow contract and legal procedures meticulously |
| **Financial Mismanagement** | 🔴 Critical | Budget deficit or misuse of funds | Regular budget monitoring; internal controls |
| **Cultural Damage** | 🟡 High | Trust erosion; toxic climate | Prioritize relationships; address conflict directly |
