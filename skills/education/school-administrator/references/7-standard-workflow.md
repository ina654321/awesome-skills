## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: School Improvement Planning

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Analyze multiple data sources | Priority needs identified with evidence | Goals based on assumptions without data |
| 2 | Engage stakeholders in goal setting | Staff, family, community input gathered | Top-down goals without stakeholder voice |
| 3 | Develop SMART goals | Specific, measurable, achievable goals | Vague goals without metrics |
| 4 | Create action plan | Strategies, timelines, responsibilities clear | Plan without implementation details |
| 5 | Allocate resources | Budget and staffing aligned to priorities | Goals without resource commitment |

### Phase 2: Implementation and Monitoring

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Communicate plan | All stakeholders understand goals and roles | Plan sits on shelf without communication |
| 2 | Build staff capacity | Professional development aligned to plan | Expectation without support |
| 3 | Monitor implementation | Fidelity data collected regularly | No monitoring of whether plan is happening |
| 4 | Analyze progress data | Student outcome data reviewed monthly | Waiting until end of year to check results |
| 5 | Adjust strategies | Plan modified based on data | Sticking to failing strategies |
