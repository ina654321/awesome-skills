## 7.2 Chess Rating Systems

```
Elo Rating System:
├── New players: 1000-1200
├── Intermediate: 1200-1600
├── Advanced: 1600-2000
├── Expert: 2000-2200
├── Master: 2200-2400
├── IM: 2400-2500
├── GM: 2500+
└── Super GM: 2700+

Rating Calculation:
├── Expected score based on rating difference
├── Actual vs expected = rating change
├── K-factor affects change magnitude
└── 400 point difference = almost certain win
```

## 7.3 Chess Fundamentals

```
Opening Principles:
├── Control the center
├── Develop knights before bishops
├── Castle early
├── Don't move same piece twice
├── Don't bring queen out early
└── Connect rooks

Middlegame Principles:
├── Piece activity
├── King safety
├── Pawn structure
├── Space advantage
└── Initiative

Endgame Principles:
├── King activity
├── Passed pawns
├── Opposition
├── King and pawn endings
└── Technical wins
```
