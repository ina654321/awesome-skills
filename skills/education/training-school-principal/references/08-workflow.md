# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 School Improvement Planning Workflow

```
Phase 1: Data Review (September-October)
├── Analyze prior year state assessment results
├── Review attendance, graduation, and discipline data
├── Survey teacher, parent, and student satisfaction
├── Conduct faculty focus groups
└── Identify 2-3 priority areas for improvement

Phase 2: Goal Setting (November)
├── Draft SMART goals aligned to priority areas
├── Share draft with faculty for input
├── School Improvement Team review and approval
└── Submit draft to district office for review

Phase 3: Action Planning (December-January)
├── Identify specific strategies for each goal
├── Allocate budget resources to support strategies
├── Assign leadership teams and accountability measures
└── Calendar professional development to support goals

Phase 4: Implementation (January-May)
├── Monthly progress monitoring with data dashboard
├── Quarterly check-ins with school improvement team
├── Mid-year adjustment if data shows ineffective strategies
└── Documentation for state reporting

Phase 5: Evaluation and Reflection (June-August)
├── End-of-year data analysis
├── Comprehensive annual report to school community
├── Plan adjustments for next cycle
└── Submit final report to district/state
```

## 8.2 Teacher Evaluation Workflow

### Pre-Observation Conference (15-30 min)
1. Teacher shares lesson objective and context
2. Discuss anticipated challenges and supports
3. Agree on observation focus (not all domains at once)
4. Confirm schedule and logistics

### Observation (20-60 min)
1. Arrive before lesson starts; minimize classroom disruption
2. Focus on pre-agreed upon indicators
3. Take objective, specific notes
4. Note specific evidence of both strengths and areas for growth

### Post-Observation Conference (20-30 min)
1. Teacher self-assesses first: "What went well? What would you change?"
2. Share evidence: "I observed [specific evidence]."
3. Connect evidence to framework: "This aligns with Domain 3b..."
4. Collaboratively identify 1-2 specific next steps
5. Agree on follow-up support
6. Complete evaluation form within 5 business days

## 8.3 Crisis Management Workflow

1. **Immediate response** (0-15 min): Secure the scene; call emergency services if needed; account for all students and staff
2. **Communication** (15-30 min): Notify district office; draft parent notification (approved by district PR)
3. **Student reunification** (30-90 min): Controlled check-out process; verify identity of each adult picking up student
4. **Staff debrief** (within 24 hours): Gather facts; provide emotional support; assign counseling as needed
5. **Parent briefing** (within 48 hours): Town hall or individual meetings depending on situation
6. **Media management**: All media inquiries directed to district communications office
7. **Recovery planning**: Academic and emotional recovery support for returning students
