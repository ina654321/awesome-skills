# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Official Documentation

- [ISLLC Educational Leadership Standards](https://www.ccssso.org/) - Interstate School Leaders Licensure Consortium standards for school administrators
- [AASA National Superintendent Standards](https://www.aasa.org/) - American Association of School Administrators professional standards
- [NAESP Principal Standards](https://www.naesp.org/) - National Association of Elementary School Principals standards
- [TALIS OECD Survey Framework](https://www.oecd.org/education/talis/) - Teaching and Learning International Survey benchmarking

## 7.2 School Leadership Domains

| Domain | Key Responsibilities |
|--------|---------------------|
| Vision and Mission | Establish shared vision; align all initiatives to mission |
| Instructional Leadership | Monitor instruction; coach teachers; set high expectations |
| Human Capital | Recruit, develop, and retain effective staff |
| Organizational Management | Operations, scheduling, budgeting, facilities |
| Community Engagement | Parent communication; community partnerships |
| Ethical Leadership | Equity, integrity, fairness; modeling values |
| School Improvement | Data-driven decision making; accountability |

## 7.3 Evaluation Frameworks

### Danielson's Framework for Teaching (for teacher evaluation)
- Domain 1: Planning and Preparation
- Domain 2: Classroom Environment
- Domain 3: Instruction
- Domain 4: Professional Responsibilities

### Marzano School Leader Evaluation
- Domain 1: A Guaranteed and Viable Curriculum
- Domain 2: Challenging Goals and Effective Feedback
- Domain 3: Safe and Supportive Environment
- Domain 4: External Relations and Community Partnerships

## 7.4 Legal and Compliance Standards

- IDEA: Special education compliance and LRE requirements
- FERPA: Student records confidentiality
- Title IX: Gender equity in athletics and programs
- OSHA: Staff safety and wellness compliance
- State accreditation standards: Course offerings, graduation requirements, teacher certification ratios
