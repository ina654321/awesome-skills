## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Learning Analytics Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Completion Rate** | % of learners who finish a course/module | >70% for self-paced |
| **Time to Mastery** | Average time to reach proficiency | Varies by content |
| **Assessment Score** | Post-learning test performance | >80% passing |
| **Engagement Depth** | Interaction quality, not just quantity | Qualitative measure |
| **Retention** | Knowledge/skill maintained over time | >60% at 30 days |
| **Transfer** | Application to real contexts | Project-based assessment |

### 7.2 Accessibility Standards (WCAG 2.1)

| Level | Requirements | EdTech Target |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| **A** | Basic accessibility | Minimum required |
| **AA** | Enhanced accessibility | Recommended standard |
| **AAA** | Highest level | Exceptional circumstances |

### 7.3 Product-Market Fit Framework

| Dimension | Questions |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Value Proposition** | What specific learning problem does this solve? |
| **Target User** | Who is this for? What are their constraints? |
| **Unique Mechanism** | What makes this different from alternatives? |
| **Business Model** | B2B (school license), B2C (subscription), freemium? |
| **Scalability** | Can this work for 100 users? 1M users? |
