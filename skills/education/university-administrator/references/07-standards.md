# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Official Documentation

- [FERPA Compliance Standards](https://studentprivacy.ed.gov/) - Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act requirements for higher education
- [Title IX Higher Education](https://www2.ed.gov/policy/rights/guid/ocr/sex offenses.html) - Gender equity in higher education programs
- [Higher Education Act Title IV](https://studentaid.gov/understand-aid/types/federal-loans) - Student financial aid compliance standards
- [Middle States Commission on Higher Education](https://www.msche.org/) - Regional accreditation standards for universities

## 7.2 Enrollment Management Standards

| Process | Standard | Timeline |
|---------|:---:|---------|
| Application review | Holistic review per enrollment policy | 4-6 weeks after complete application |
| Acceptance notification | Written or electronic notification | Within 2 weeks of decision |
| Deposit deadline | Usually May 1 (national candidates) | Per offer letter |
| Financial aid award | Award letter with all aid types | Within 2 weeks of SAR |
| Orientation registration | Class registration with advisor | 4-6 weeks before term start |

## 7.3 Student Affairs Standards

| Area | Standard |
|------|----------|
| Residence life | Fire safety inspection annually; RA training 20+ hours pre-semester |
| Student conduct | Due process per institutional policy; appeal process documented |
| Career services | One career advisor per 500 students; 80%+ students served by graduation |
| Counseling center | 1 counselor per 1000-1500 students; after-hours crisis protocol |
| Title IX | Coordinator designated; annual training for all staff; climate survey annually |

## 7.4 Records and Data Standards

- Student information system (Banner, PeopleSoft, or equivalent): Real-time update of enrollment, grades, and demographics
- Data retention: Academic records permanent; financial aid records 5 years; personnel records 7 years
- Data governance: FERPA for student data; PCI-DSS for financial transactions; GDPR for international student data
- Analytics: Retention and completion rates tracked by cohort, demographics, and first-generation status
