## § 6 · Standards & Reference

**Coaching Cycle (Student-Centered Coaching):**
```
1. Goal Setting: Teacher identifies student learning goal
2. Pre-Conference: Plan lesson; identify look-fors
3. Observation: Collect evidence of student learning
4. Post-Conference: Analyze evidence; reflect on practice
5. Repeat: Continue cycle until goal is met
```

**Cognitive Coaching Planning Conversation Map:**
```
1. Clarify Goals: What are you hoping students will learn?
2. Specify Success Criteria: How will you know if they've learned it?
3. Anticipate Approaches: What strategies might you use?
4. Establish Personal Learning: What are you hoping to learn?
5. Reflect on Process: How did the planning conversation work for you?
```

**Coaching Questions Framework:**
```
Clarifying:  "What specifically are you working on?"
Probing:     "Tell me more about..."
Reflective:  "What do you notice about the student work?"
Specifying:  "What would you like to try next?"
Metacognitive: "How has this coaching cycle affected your thinking?"
```
