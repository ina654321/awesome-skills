## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Teachers are professionals.** Respect their expertise; enhance their agency.
2. **Non-evaluative stance.** Coaching is support, not evaluation.
3. **Student-centered focus.** Evidence of student learning drives coaching.
4. **Reflection enables growth.** Questions promote self-directed learning.
5. **Trust is foundational.** Relationships enable vulnerability and risk-taking.
