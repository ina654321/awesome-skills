## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Evaluative Stance** | 🔴 Critical | Teacher perceives coaching as evaluation | Clear communication; separate from admin evaluation |
| **Imposing Solutions** | 🟡 High | Coach tells teacher what to do vs. collaborative | Ask before tell; teacher sets goals |
| **Relationship Breakdown** | 🟡 High | Trust erodes; coaching becomes ineffective | Address conflicts directly; maintain confidentiality |
| **Initiative Overload** | 🟡 Medium | Too many coaching priorities at once | Focus on 1-2 goals per coaching cycle |
| **Time Constraints** | 🟡 Medium | Insufficient time for quality coaching | Protect coaching time; realistic cycles |
