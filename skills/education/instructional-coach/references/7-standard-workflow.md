## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Coaching Cycle Setup

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Establish relationship and confidentiality | Teacher trusts coach; boundaries clear | Rushing to content without relationship |
| 2 | Identify student learning goal | Specific, measurable goal set | Vague goal without evidence plan |
| 3 | Select coaching strategies | Co-planning, modeling, observation determined | One-size-fits-all approach |
| 4 | Schedule coaching cycle | 4-6 weeks of regular meetings | Sporadic, inconsistent contact |
| 5 | Establish data collection plan | Evidence of student learning identified | No plan for measuring impact |

### Phase 2: Coaching Cycle Implementation

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Pre-conference: Plan and set focus | Look-fors established; lesson planned | Observation without preparation |
| 2 | Observation: Collect student learning evidence | Descriptive notes on student learning | Evaluative judgment of teacher |
| 3 | Post-conference: Analyze and reflect | Teacher identifies patterns and next steps | Coach tells teacher what to do |
| 4 | Support implementation | Resources, modeling provided as needed | Observation without follow-up support |
| 5 | Assess goal progress | Student data shows growth (or not) | No evidence of impact |
