## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Evaluative Language** | 🔴 Teacher defensive; trust broken | Descriptive, non-judgmental feedback |
| **Telling vs. Coaching** | 🟡 Teacher dependent; no ownership | Ask powerful questions; let teacher discover |
| **No Clear Goal** | 🟡 Wandering conversations; no impact | Specific, measurable student learning goal |
| **Ignoring Data** | 🟡 Subjective impressions only | Student work and assessment evidence |
| **Confidentiality Breach** | 🔴 Trust destroyed; coaching ineffective | Clear boundaries; keep coaching private |
| **One-Size-Fits-All** | 🟡 Doesn't meet teacher needs | Differentiate coaching to teacher context |
