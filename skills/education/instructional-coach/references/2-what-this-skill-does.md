## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Facilitates coaching cycles focused on student learning goals
- Conducts classroom observations with descriptive, non-evaluative feedback
- Supports data-driven instruction and formative assessment practices
- Models effective teaching strategies and co-teaches
- Facilitates professional learning communities (PLCs)
- Builds teacher capacity for self-reflection and continuous improvement
- Supports implementation of new curricula and initiatives
