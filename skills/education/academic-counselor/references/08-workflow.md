# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Academic Counseling Session Workflow

```
Session Structure (50-min model)
├── Check-in (5 min)
│   ├── Rapport building
│   ├── Mood/safety check
│   └── Review of previous session action items
├── Main discussion (35 min)
│   ├── Agenda setting (student-led)
│   ├── Academic review (grades, transcripts, credit status)
│   ├── Goal clarification and planning
│   └── Problem-solving or skill-building
├── Action planning (7 min)
│   ├── Identify 1-2 concrete next steps
│   └── Assign student action item
└── Closing (3 min)
    └── Schedule next session; confirm contact preferences
```

## 8.2 College/Career Planning Workflow

1. **Freshman year**: Introduce ICAP; administer interest inventory; explore career clusters
2. **Sophomore year**: PSAT administration; research college fit; identify target/reach/safety schools
3. **Junior year**: Full practice SAT/ACT; campus visits; college fair attendance; summer program applications
4. **Senior year**: Application cycle management; scholarship search; FAFSA completion; financial aid comparison

## 8.3 Crisis Intervention Workflow

1. **Immediate safety assessment**: Is the student in immediate danger? (Suicidal ideation, abuse, threat)
2. **Active listening**: Use CPSS (Care, Perceive, Summarize, Strategize) or similar crisis counseling model
3. **Notify designated administrator** per school protocol
4. **Contact parent/guardian** as appropriate; mandatory reporting for minors
5. **Refer to mental health professional**; provide local crisis line information
6. **Document incident** using school incident report form
7. **Follow-up**: Check in with student within 24-48 hours

## 8.4 Small-Group Counseling Workflow

1. Needs assessment → identify theme (grief, social skills, executive function, anxiety)
2. Recruit members via teacher/admin referral or self-referral
3. Obtain parent consent; ensure FERPA compliance
4. Schedule 6-8 sessions; structure using evidence-based curriculum
5. Pre/post assessment to measure outcomes
6. Debrief with stakeholders; plan for individual follow-up
