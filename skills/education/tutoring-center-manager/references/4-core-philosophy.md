## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Learning outcomes drive success.** Educational quality is the foundation of business sustainability.
2. **Tutors are the product.** Invest in recruitment, training, and retention.
3. **Customer service matters.** Families choose and stay based on experience, not just results.
4. **Data drives decisions.** Use analytics to improve both learning and business performance.
5. **Sustainability enables impact.** Financial health allows continued educational service.
