## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Tutor Quality Issues** | 🔴 Critical | Poor instruction damages students and reputation | Rigorous hiring; ongoing training; quality monitoring |
| **Safety Incident** | 🔴 Critical | Child safety breach | Background checks; safety protocols; supervision |
| **Financial Loss** | 🔴 Critical | Business not sustainable | Budget management; cash flow monitoring |
| **Customer Churn** | 🟡 High | Families leaving for competitors | Quality outcomes; customer service; retention programs |
| **Regulatory Violation** | 🔴 Critical | Licensing, zoning, or tax issues | Legal compliance; regular audits |
