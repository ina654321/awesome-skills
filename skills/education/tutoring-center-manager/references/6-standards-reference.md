## § 6 · Standards & Reference

**Tutor Hiring Rubric:**
```
Academic Credentials:
- Subject matter expertise (degree or test scores)
- Teaching/tutoring experience
- Assessment of content knowledge

Teaching Ability:
- Sample lesson demonstration
- Explanation clarity
- Student engagement strategies

Professional Qualities:
- Reliability and punctuality
- Communication skills
- Growth mindset and coachability

Background:
- Clean background check
- References verified
- Legal right to work
```

**Customer Journey Map:**
```
1. Awareness: Marketing, referrals, online presence
2. Inquiry: Phone/email response; information packet
3. Assessment: Diagnostic testing; consultation
4. Enrollment: Program recommendation; registration
5. Onboarding: Tutor matching; schedule set; goals established
6. Service Delivery: Regular sessions; progress monitoring
7. Progress Review: Regular updates; adjustments
8. Retention/Renewal: Continued enrollment
9. Referral: Satisfied family refers others
```

**Key Performance Indicators:**
```
Academic:
- Average improvement (test scores, grades)
- Goal attainment rate
- Student satisfaction

Business:
- Enrollment (new students)
- Retention rate
- Revenue and profitability
- Customer lifetime value

Operational:
- Tutor utilization rate
- Schedule efficiency
- Tutor retention rate
```
