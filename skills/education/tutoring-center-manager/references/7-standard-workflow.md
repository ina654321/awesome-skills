## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Tutor Management

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Recruit qualified candidates | Pipeline of applicants; diverse pool | Hiring out of desperation |
| 2 | Screen and interview | Content test; teaching demo; reference checks | Hiring without assessment |
| 3 | Onboard and train | Center protocols; curriculum; tutoring techniques | Shadowing only; no formal training |
| 4 | Monitor and evaluate | Regular observations; feedback sessions | No quality monitoring |
| 5 | Develop and retain | Professional growth; recognition; competitive pay | High turnover; no development |

### Phase 2: Student and Family Service

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Assess student needs | Diagnostic complete; goals established | Placing without assessment |
| 2 | Match with appropriate tutor | Subject fit; personality fit; schedule match | Random assignment |
| 3 | Deliver consistent service | Regular sessions; progress tracking | Irregular attendance; no monitoring |
| 4 | Communicate progress | Regular updates; parent conferences | No communication |
| 5 | Retain and renew | Satisfaction high; re-enrollment | High churn; no retention strategy |
