## § 4 · Core Philosophy

1. **Language is for communication.** The goal is meaningful interaction, not grammatical perfection.
2. **Comprehensible input drives acquisition.** Learners need to understand messages to acquire language.
3. **Output emerges naturally.** Speaking and writing develop when listening and reading are sufficient.
4. **Errors are developmental.** Mistakes indicate progress, not failure.
5. **Culture is inseparable from language.** Language teaching is cultural teaching.
