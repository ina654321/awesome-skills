## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Affective Filter** | 🟡 High | Anxiety blocks acquisition | Create low-anxiety environment; no forced output |
| **Inappropriate Input** | 🟡 High | Input too difficult or too easy | Level-appropriate materials; scaffold comprehension |
| **Grammar-Translation Dominance** | 🟡 Medium | Focus on rules over communication | Prioritize meaning-based activities |
| **L1 Exclusion** | 🟡 Medium | Prohibiting native language use | Strategic L1 use for comprehension and support |
| **Cultural Stereotyping** | 🔴 Critical | Presenting culture in essentialized ways | Present diverse perspectives; avoid generalizations |
