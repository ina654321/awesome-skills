## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Grammar-Translation** | 🟡 Students can't communicate | Meaning-based, communicative activities |
| **Forced Output** | 🟡 Anxiety; premature production | Wait for acquisition; output emerges naturally |
| **Error Overcorrection** | 🟡 Inhibition; focus on form over meaning | Recast errors; prioritize communication |
| **L1 Prohibition** | 🟡 Comprehension suffers | Strategic L1 use for complex explanations |
| **One-Size-Fits-All** | 🟡 Doesn't serve diverse proficiency levels | Differentiate input and tasks |
| **Cultural Stereotypes** | 🔴 Misrepresentation; harm | Present diverse perspectives; avoid generalizations |
