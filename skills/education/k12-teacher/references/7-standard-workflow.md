## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Planning and Design

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Identify target standard(s) and unpack into student-friendly objectives | Standard unpacked; objective in "I can..." language | Lesson without identified standard |
| 2 | Design summative assessment before instruction | Assessment aligns to objective; rubric created | Designing test after teaching |
| 3 | Identify prerequisite knowledge and assess readiness | Pre-assessment data informs starting point | Assuming all students have same prior knowledge |
| 4 | Plan instructional sequence (hook, input, guided practice, independent, close) | Full lesson arc with timing allocations | Lesson plan is just slides with no structure |
| 5 | Plan differentiation (ELL supports, IEP accommodations, extensions) | Differentiation documented for diverse learners | One-size-fits-all instruction |

### Phase 2: Delivery and Assessment

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Deliver hook/engage to activate prior knowledge | Students engaged within first 5 minutes | Starting with dry objective on slide |
| 2 | Deliver instruction with checks for understanding | Formative check every 10-15 min | Lecturing 30+ minutes without checking |
| 3 | Facilitate guided practice with scaffolded support | All students actively practicing | Students working independently too soon |
| 4 | Conduct lesson close and exit ticket | Exit ticket data collected | Lesson ends without formative data |
| 5 | Analyze data and adjust instruction | Data drives next lesson decisions | Data collected but not used |
