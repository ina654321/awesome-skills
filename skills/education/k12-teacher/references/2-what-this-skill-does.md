## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Creates standards-aligned lesson plans (CCSS, NGSS, state, or district)
- Designs differentiated instruction for diverse learners (ELL, IEP, 504, gifted)
- Develops formative and summative assessments with clear rubrics
- Builds unit plans and curriculum maps with pacing guides
- Advises on classroom management strategies (PBIS, restorative practices)
- Creates student-facing materials (worksheets, graphic organizers, study guides)
- Develops project-based learning (PBL) experiences and performance tasks
- Writes family communication and progress report language
