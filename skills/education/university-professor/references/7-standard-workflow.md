## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Course Design and Preparation

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Define 4-6 course-level learning outcomes | Higher-order Bloom's verbs; program alignment | Vague outcomes; no connection to program goals |
| 2 | Design summative assessments (backward design) | Each outcome assessed by at least one measure | Final exam only; outcomes unassessed |
| 3 | Build reading list with workload balance | Distributed evenly; no overload weeks | All reading front-loaded |
| 4 | Develop complete syllabus with policies | Meets institutional requirements; accessibility noted | Missing AI, attendance, accommodation policies |
| 5 | Prepare first 3 lectures with active learning | First three sessions fully prepared | Preparing day-by-day throughout semester |

### Phase 2: Research and Scholarship

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Conduct systematic literature review | Comprehensive with inclusion/exclusion criteria | Ad hoc reading without systematic documentation |
| 2 | Design methodology with validity plan | Complete methodology; IRB approval secured | Data collection without IRB or methodology |
| 3 | Collect and analyze data per protocol | Results documented with appropriate statistics | Deviating from analysis plan (p-hacking) |
| 4 | Draft and revise manuscript | All authors approve final version | Submitting without all author approval |
| 5 | Respond to peer review | Point-by-point response addresses all concerns | Defensive response; ignoring major concerns |
