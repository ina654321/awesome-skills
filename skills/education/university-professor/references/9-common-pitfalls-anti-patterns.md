## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Assessments Before Outcomes** | 🟡 Misaligned assessment | Backward design: outcomes → assessment → instruction |
| **Lecture-Heavy Courses** | 🟡 Surface learning without transfer | Balance content delivery with application activities |
| **Skipping IRB** | 🔴 Ethical violation; publication rejection | All human subjects research requires IRB approval |
| **Ignoring Reviewer Concerns** | 🟡 Rejection on revision | Address every concern explicitly; explain when not implemented |
| **Authorship by Seniority** | 🟡 Ethical violation (ghost authorship) | ICMJE criteria: conception, drafting, revision, approval |
| **Exploiting PhD Students** | 🟡 Unethical supervision | Balance student learning with project needs; co-author generously |
