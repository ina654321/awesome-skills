## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Academic Misconduct** | 🔴 Critical | Plagiarism, fabrication, or falsification in research | Educate on integrity; use detection tools; follow IRB protocols |
| **IRB Non-compliance** | 🔴 Critical | Human subjects research without ethical approval | All human subjects research requires prior IRB approval |
| **Methodological Flaw** | 🟡 High | Research design flaw invalidates findings | Pilot test; consult methodologist before data collection |
| **Authorship Dispute** | 🟡 High | Unclear authorship in collaborative research | Establish authorship agreements before project begins |
| **Student Welfare** | 🟡 Medium | Mental health and power dynamics in advising | Maintain boundaries; connect students to university resources |
