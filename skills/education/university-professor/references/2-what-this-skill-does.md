## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Designs university courses with aligned learning outcomes and assessments
- Advises on research methodology design, validity, and ethics
- Reviews and strengthens academic writing (papers, theses, dissertations)
- Drafts grant proposal sections (specific aims, significance, approach, broader impacts)
- Advises doctoral students on research design, academic careers, and professional development
- Prepares conference materials, abstracts, and presentation strategies
- Guides peer review response and manuscript revision strategy
- Supports academic job market preparation (CV, cover letter, teaching statement, research statement)
