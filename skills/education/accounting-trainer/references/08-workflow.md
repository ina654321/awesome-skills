# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Accounting Course Planning Workflow

```
Course Development Cycle
├── Pre-Course
│   ├── Conduct needs assessment (industry demand, student background)
│   ├── Define learning objectives using Bloom's taxonomy
│   ├── Select curriculum framework (GAAP/IFRS, CPA exam blueprint)
│   └── Prepare syllabus with grading policy and learning outcomes
├── Weekly Execution
│   ├── Review prerequisite knowledge (bell work, exit tickets)
│   ├── Deliver instruction (lecture, demonstration, guided practice)
│   ├── Provide formative feedback on practice problems
│   └── Assign homework with real-world application
├── Assessment
│   ├── Formative: daily problem sets, quizzes, participation
│   ├── Summative: exams covering financial statements, ledgers, analysis
│   └── Performance: comprehensive capstone project (full accounting cycle)
└── Post-Course
    ├── Review pass rates and topic-level performance data
    └── Update curriculum based on student outcomes
```

## 8.2 Accounting Lesson Delivery Workflow

1. **Hook/Review** (5-10 min): Connect to prior learning; real-world accounting news item
2. **Direct Instruction** (15-20 min): Model the accounting concept with worked examples
3. **Guided Practice** (15-20 min): Students work through problems with instructor support
4. **Independent Practice** (15-20 min): Students complete problems independently
5. **Closure** (5-10 min): Exit ticket; key takeaways; preview of next lesson

## 8.3 Practical Accounting Training Workflow

1. **Chart of accounts setup**: Students build a complete COA for a fictional business
2. **Transaction analysis**: Identify accounts affected; determine debit/credit treatment
3. **Journal entry recording**: Post to general journal; maintain proper formatting
4. **Posting to ledger**: Transfer entries to T-accounts; calculate balances
5. **Trial balance preparation**: Prepare unadjusted trial balance; identify errors
6. **Adjusting entries**: Accruals, deferrals, depreciation adjustments
7. **Financial statement preparation**: Income statement, balance sheet, cash flow
8. **Closing entries**: Close temporary accounts; prepare post-closing trial balance

## 8.4 Exam Preparation Workflow (CPA Exam Candidates)

1. Diagnostic test to identify weak areas
2. Create study schedule mapped to exam blueprints (AUD, BEC, FAR, REG)
3. Content review with active recall and spaced repetition
4. Practice multiple-choice questions daily
5. Simulated practice exams under timed conditions
6. Skills assessment: BEC writing simulations, tax research problems
7. Review sessions focusing on high-weight exam topics
