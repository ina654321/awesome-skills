# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|---------------|:---:|-----------|
| 1 | **Teaching to the test only** | High | Develop communicative competence; test strategies are supplementary, not primary |
| 2 | **Ignoring feedback on practice tests** | High | Review every practice test in detail; treat each error as a learning opportunity |
| 3 | **Over-focusing on vocabulary lists** | Medium | Vocabulary in context is more effective than decontextualized memorization |
| 4 | **No timed practice** | High | Every practice session should include at least one timed exercise |
| 5 | **Same prompt used repeatedly** | Medium | Use a bank of prompts to avoid memorization; variety builds adaptability |

## 10.2 Common Training Errors

1. **Assuming reading/writing weakness is a vocabulary problem**: Often it's comprehension or task awareness, not word knowledge
2. **Forcing conversation practice on introverted learners**: Build confidence through writing first, then transition to speaking
3. **Ignoring pronunciation**: Even at B2+ level, poor pronunciation can cap speaking scores
4. **Teaching grammar rules without production practice**: Knowing the rule does not guarantee correct use under time pressure
5. **Skipping the rubric**: Students should know exactly what each band score looks like. Rubric familiarity improves self-assessment.

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Diagnostic assessment administered before program begins
- [ ] Individualized Learning Plan (ILP) created and shared with student
- [ ] Each session has a clear language focus aligned to ILP
- [ ] Practice tests administered at baseline, midpoint, and end of program
- [ ] Feedback on practice tests provided within 48 hours
- [ ] Test-taking strategies integrated throughout, not just in final weeks
- [ ] Speaking practice conducted in test-condition format at least once per week
- [ ] Student self-assessment compared against trainer assessment (tracked over time)
- [ ] Accessibility accommodations arranged if needed (extended time, separate room)
- [ ] Test fee, registration deadlines, and ID requirements reviewed with student
