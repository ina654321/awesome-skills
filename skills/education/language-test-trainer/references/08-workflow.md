# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Diagnostic Assessment Workflow

```
Initial Session (60-90 minutes)
├── Placement test administration (standardized instrument)
├── Structured speaking interview (5-10 minutes)
├── Writing sample under timed conditions
├── Learner self-assessment against CEFR descriptors
└── Identify strengths, gaps, and target score

Analysis (within 48 hours)
├── Score diagnostic assessment against CEFR rubrics
├── Identify macro-skills most in need of development
├── Calculate gap between current level and target score
├── Estimate training hours needed (roughly 100-120 hours per CEFR band)
└── Produce individualized learning plan (ILP)

Follow-Up Review (every 4-6 weeks)
├── Mini mock test under timed conditions
├── Adjust ILP based on progress
├── Update practice focus areas
└── Reassure or recalibrate target score if needed
```

## 8.2 Training Session Workflow

### Typical 90-Minute Session Structure
1. **Warm-up (5 min)**: Quick conversation in target language; today's topic preview
2. **Focus skill instruction (20 min)**: Grammar, vocabulary, or strategy for one specific skill
3. **Guided practice (15 min)**: Structured tasks with feedback (e.g., paraphrasing exercise)
4. **Independent practice (30 min)**: Timed test-condition practice of the skill
5. **Feedback and reflection (15 min)**: Self-assessment against rubric; peer feedback if applicable
6. **Next steps (5 min)**: Assign homework aligned with ILP; preview next session

### Speaking Practice Protocol
1. Give 2-minute prompt (e.g., "Describe a book that changed your perspective...")
2. Record trainee response
3. Score using CEFR holistic rubric
4. Provide specific feedback: what worked (1 thing), what to improve (2 things)
5. Repeat same prompt after 48 hours to measure improvement

## 8.3 Test Preparation Curriculum Design

1. **Baseline**: Conduct full mock test under official conditions
2. **Gap analysis**: Identify section-by-section strengths and weaknesses
3. **Priority ranking**: Rank gaps by (impact on score × difficulty to fix)
4. **Allocation**: Assign practice hours proportional to priority
5. **Weekly rhythm**: 2 sessions per week minimum; one full practice test every 2 weeks
6. **Final week**: Light practice only; rest; mental preparation
