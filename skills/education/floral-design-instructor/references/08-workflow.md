## 8. Standard Workflow

### 8.1 Wedding Consultation

```
Phase 1: Discovery
├── Discuss couple's vision: style, colors, flowers they love/hate
├── Establish budget realistically
├── Determine event date, venue, and timeline
├── Review previous arrangements they've seen
└── Ask about flower allergies and scent sensitivities

Phase 2: Conceptualization
├── Create mood board: color palette, style references
├── Propose flower varieties: seasonal availability, cost
├── Sketch preliminary designs: bridal bouquet, ceremony, reception
├── Discuss ceremony-specific needs (arch, aisle markers)
└── Review delivery and setup logistics

Phase 3: Proposal
├── Present itemized quote with design descriptions
├── Include deposit terms and payment schedule
├── Outline timeline: design day, delivery, setup
├── Provide contract for review
└── Schedule follow-up meeting before signing

Phase 4: Confirmation
├── Collect signed contract and deposit
├── Schedule final meeting 2-3 weeks before event
├── Confirm flower orders with growers/wholesalers
└── Finalize timeline with venue and planner

[✓ Done]: Confirmed booking with deposit paid
```

### 8.2 Arrangement Creation

```
Step 1: Preparation
  → Clean and sanitize all containers
  → Prepare mechanics: foam soaked, chicken wire shaped
  → Strip stems: remove thorns, leaves below water line
  → Condition flowers: cut stems at angle, hydrate

Step 2: Structural Foundation
  → Add foliage and line materials first
  → Establish shape and silhouette
  → Check balance from all angles
  → Add mechanics support where needed

Step 3: Focal Flowers
  → Add focal flowers in odd numbers
  → Create visual "landing spots"
  → Vary heights for dimension
  → Position best faces outward

Step 4: Filler & Texture
  → Add filler flowers in spaces
  → Include texture elements (berries, pods)
  → Fill gaps completely
  → Ensure full, lush appearance

Step 5: Finishing Touches
  → Adjust individual flower angles
  → Check for gaps or visible mechanics
  → Add final detail elements
  → Clean container exterior
  → Label with flower names and care instructions

[✓ Done]: Complete arrangement ready for delivery
```
