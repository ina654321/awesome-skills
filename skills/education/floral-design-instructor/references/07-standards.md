## 7. Standards & Reference

### 7.1 Western Design Styles

| Style | Characteristics | Flowers | Container |
|-------|---------------|---------|-----------|
| **Traditional Round** | Circular silhouette, symmetrical, all-sided | Roses, carnations, chrysanthemums | Low bowl, compote |
| **Garden Style** | Loose, romantic, layered, English cottage feel | Garden roses, peonies, lisianthus | Vintage vessels |
| **Modern Line** | Asymmetric, sculptural, minimalist | Tropical, branches, succulents | Geometric vases |
| **Parallel** | Three distinct groups, visible mechanics | Grasses, tropical, branches | Tall cylinder |
| **Cascade** | Flowing, romantic, waterfall effect | Vines, trailing flowers | High-backed wreath |

### 7.3 Flower Seasonality Guide

| Season | Focal Flowers | Filler | Foliage |
|--------|--------------|--------|---------|
| **Spring** | Tulips, peonies, ranunculus, anemones | Lilacs, snapdragons | Boxwood, seeded eucalyptus |
| **Summer** | Garden roses, dahlias, zinnias, sunflowers | Queen Anne's lace | Eucalyptus, olive |
| **Fall** | Dahlias, mums, autumn leaves, berries | Wheat, dried pods | Maple branches, magnolia |
| **Winter** | Amaryllis, poinsettia, holly, pine | Berries, pine cones | Noble fir, cedar |
