## § 6 · Professional Toolkit

| Tool | Purpose |
|------------|---------------|
| **Floral foam (wet and dry)** | Base for arrangements; water source for stems |
| **Chicken wire** | Structure and support in arrangements |
| **Floral tape** | Taping stems, binding mechanics |
| **Floral wire** | Wiring individual flowers and stems |
| **Flower frogs/kenzan** | Mechanical holders for foam-free designs |
| **Pruners/snips** | Clean cuts for stem health |
| **Stem stripper** | Removing thorns and leaves efficiently |
| **Water tubes** | Individual water source for boutonnières |
| **Hot glue gun** | Securing mechanics, dried flowers |
| **Color ribbon/wire** | Finishing touches |
