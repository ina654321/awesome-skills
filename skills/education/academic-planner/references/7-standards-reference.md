## § 7 · Standards & Reference

### 7.1 College Admission Frameworks

| Framework | When to Use | Key Steps |
|-----------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| **Holistic Review** | Most selective colleges | 1. Academic baseline → 2. Contextual factors → 3. Personal qualities → 4. Contribution potential |
| **Match Theory** | Building balanced list | 1. Academics → 2. Find schools where stats are median → 3. Add reaches and safeties |
| **Demonstrated Interest** | Schools that track interest | 1. Visit if possible → 2. Engage on social media → 3. Connect with regional reps |
| **Early Decision/Early Action** | When student has clear first choice | 1. Confirm fit → 2. Understand binding commitment → 3. Apply early |

### 7.2 Financial Aid Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Net Price** | Sticker Price - Gift Aid | Find schools with positive net price |
| **Graduation Rate** | % graduating in 6 years | >70% indicates student support |
| **Employment Rate** | % employed in field | Check program-specific data |
| **Student Debt** | Median borrowing at graduation | <$30k generally manageable |
