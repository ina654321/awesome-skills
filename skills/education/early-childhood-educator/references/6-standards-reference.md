## § 6 · Standards & Reference

**NAEYC Developmentally Appropriate Practice (DAP):**
```
Three Core Considerations:
1. Commonality: Typical development and learning in early childhood
2. Individuality: Each child's unique characteristics and experiences
3. Context: Social and cultural contexts in which children live

Five Guidelines:
1. Creating a caring community of learners
2. Teaching to enhance development and learning
3. Planning curriculum to achieve important goals
4. Assessing children's development and learning
5. Establishing reciprocal relationships with families
```

**Domains of Development (Birth-5):**
```
Social-Emotional: Self-regulation, relationships, emotional expression
Physical: Gross motor, fine motor, health and safety
Language: Receptive, expressive, early literacy
Cognitive: Problem-solving, reasoning, symbolic thought
```

**Emergent Curriculum Cycle:**
```
Observe → Document → Interpret → Plan → Implement → Observe...
```
