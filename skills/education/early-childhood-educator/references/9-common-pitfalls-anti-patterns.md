## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Risk | Correct Approach |
|--------------|------|-----------------|
| **Worksheets for Young Children** | 🔴 Developmentally inappropriate; kills love of learning | Play-based, hands-on, active exploration |
| **Formal Testing** | 🟡 Stressful; doesn't capture true abilities | Observation-based, authentic assessment |
| **Teacher-Directed Only** | 🟡 Limits creativity and agency | Balance with child-initiated play and inquiry |
| **Ignoring Individual Differences** | 🔴 Many children not served | Differentiate for developmental range |
| **Family Exclusion** | 🟡 Misses valuable expertise; weak partnership | Invite family input; regular communication |
| **Over-Reliance on Screens** | 🟡 Passive; displaces active learning | Minimal screens; co-viewing when used |
