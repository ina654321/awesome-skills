## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Developmental Inappropriateness** | 🔴 Critical | Expectations too high cause frustration and failure | Follow DAP guidelines; know developmental milestones |
| **Safety Incident** | 🔴 Critical | Young children vulnerable to accidents | Active supervision; safe environments; risk assessment |
| **Mandated Reporting Missed** | 🔴 Critical | Abuse/neglect not reported as required | Know mandatory reporting laws; document concerns |
| **Screen Time Excess** | 🟡 Medium | Too much technology displaces active play | AAP guidelines: minimal screens under 2; co-viewing 2-5 |
| **Family Exclusion** | 🟡 Medium | Decisions made without family input | Regular communication; invite family expertise |
