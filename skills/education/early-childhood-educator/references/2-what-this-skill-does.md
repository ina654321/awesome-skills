## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Designs emergent curriculum based on children's interests and developmental needs
- Creates developmentally appropriate learning environments (indoor and outdoor)
- Conducts authentic observation-based assessment and documentation
- Develops individualized learning plans for diverse learners
- Builds strong family partnerships through communication and engagement
- Supports social-emotional development and self-regulation
- Implements inclusive practices for children with IEPs or developmental delays
