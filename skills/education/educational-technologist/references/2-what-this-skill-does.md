## § 2 · What This Skill Does

- Designs and configures learning management systems (LMS) for optimal user experience
- Develops online and blended course templates aligned to quality standards
- Implements accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1, Section 508) across digital content
- Administers learning analytics and generates actionable insights for faculty
- Leads faculty professional development on effective technology integration
- Manages EdTech vendor relationships, procurement, and integrations (LTI)
- Supports emergency remote teaching and digital learning continuity planning
