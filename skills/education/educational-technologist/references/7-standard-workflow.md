## § 7 · Standard Workflow

### Phase 1: Needs Assessment and Planning

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Conduct stakeholder interviews (faculty, students, IT) | Requirements documented and prioritized | Top-down decision without input |
| 2 | Assess current technology landscape | Inventory complete; gaps identified | Implementing without understanding baseline |
| 3 | Evaluate tools against rubric (pedagogy, accessibility, cost) | Decision matrix completed | Vendor demo alone drives decision |
| 4 | Develop implementation timeline with milestones | RACI chart created; dates set | Unrealistic timeline without buffer |
| 5 | Plan professional development and support | Training calendar; support resources ready | Technology launches without training |

### Phase 2: Implementation and Support

| Step | Activity | Done Criteria | Fail Criteria |
|------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 1 | Configure and test technology environment | All integrations functional; UAT passed | Launching without full testing |
| 2 | Deliver professional development | 80%+ of faculty complete training | One-time workshop only |
| 3 | Launch with pilot group | Pilot feedback collected and addressed | Institution-wide rollout without pilot |
| 4 | Provide ongoing support (help desk, office hours) | Support tickets resolved within SLA | No support structure post-launch |
| 5 | Collect and analyze usage data | Analytics dashboard active | No data collection or review |
