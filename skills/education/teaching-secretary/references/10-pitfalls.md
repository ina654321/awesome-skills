# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|---------------|:---:|-----------|
| 1 | **Sharing student info informally** | High | Only share with staff who have legitimate educational interest; FERPA applies |
| 2 | **Processing changes without verification** | High | Always verify identity before making changes to emergency contacts, pickup lists |
| 3 | **Promising outcomes you can't control** | High | "I'll check on that" is safer than "the teacher will definitely do that" |
| 4 | **Ignoring the front desk during rush** | High | Even when busy, greet every person — even regular parents |
| 5 | **Keeping sensitive info on personal devices** | High | All student data stays on school-issued equipment only |

## 10.2 Security and Safety Errors

1. **Propping open security doors**: Even for 2 minutes — this is a major security vulnerability
2. **Not checking ID for late pickups**: Always verify identity; never assume a familiar face is authorized
3. **Leaving records unattended**: Lock your desk when stepping away; never leave student files open
4. **Giving school schedules to unauthorized callers**: Verify purpose before sharing any schedule information
5. **Ignoring suspicious behavior**: Report — don't investigate. Your job is to notice and escalate.

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Phone calls answered within 3 rings with professional greeting
- [ ] All visitors signed in and issued badge before entering school
- [ ] Student check-ins/checkouts recorded in SIS within 5 minutes
- [ ] Messages taken accurately with date, time, caller name, and detailed message
- [ ] Parent contact information verified at least quarterly
- [ ] Incident reports filed within 24 hours of any incident
- [ ] FERPA guidelines followed for all record requests
- [ ] Office supplies stocked and equipment functioning
- [ ] School calendar updated and announcements posted daily
- [ ] Emergency procedures reviewed and contact list current
- [ ] Sensitive documents locked at end of each day
- [ ] Petty cash reconciled daily if applicable
- [ ] Professional development hours completed as required
