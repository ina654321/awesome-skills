# Standards & Reference

- [School Secretaries' Code of Conduct](https://www.shadysideoffice.com/) - Professional standards for school administrative staff
- [Microsoft School Data Sync](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/education/school-data-sync) - Student information system standards
- [PowerSchool SIS Standards](https://www.powerschool.com/) - Student information system management and security

## 7.2 Student Records Management

| Record Type | Retention Period | Access Level |
|-----------|:---:|-----------|
| Permanent academic records | Indefinite | Admin + student (upon request) |
| Attendance records | 7 years after graduation | Admin + authorized staff |
| Disciplinary records | 7 years after graduation | Admin; shared with teachers on need-to-know |
| Special education records | Duration + 7 years | Special ed team; parents have rights |
| Health records | 7 years after last enrollment | Nurse + admin; shared per HIPAA |
| Incident reports | 5 years | Admin; legal if requested |

## 7.3 Communication Standards

| Communication Type | Standard |
|-------------------|----------|
| Phone calls | Answer within 3 rings; identify school and name; use professional tone |
| Email | Respond within 24 hours on business days; use professional signature |
| Parent meetings | Document outcomes; follow up in writing within 48 hours |
| Emergency notifications | Follow school emergency protocol; verify parent contact info quarterly |
| Student info sharing | FERPA-compliant; need written consent for third-party disclosure |

## 7.4 Professional Conduct Standards

- Attendance at front desk: Always project calm professionalism
- Student check-in/out: Verify identity; update records in real time
- Visitor management: All visitors sign in; verify purpose; issue visitor badge
- Confidentiality: All student information is confidential — discuss only with authorized staff
- Conflict de-escalation: Remain calm; do not match aggressive tone; involve administrator if needed
