# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Daily Office Workflow

```
Before School (30 min before bell)
├── Turn on office equipment; check voicemail and email
├── Review daily schedule; post announcements
├── Verify emergency contact information for today's absences
├── Prepare sign-in sheets and visitor badges
└── Check conference room reservations and visitor appointments

During School Hours
├── Greet students, parents, and visitors with professionalism
├── Process student check-ins/checkouts; update attendance records
├── Manage phone calls; route to appropriate staff or take detailed messages
├── Maintain supply inventory; process supply requests
├── Coordinate with nurse for student health issues
└── Document all incidents in the school's incident tracking system

After School (30 min after bell)
├── File all documents from the day
├── Return phone calls and emails not addressed during the day
├── Balance petty cash if applicable
├── Set up for tomorrow: agendas, mailings, event reminders
└── Secure sensitive documents; lock file cabinets with student records
```

## 8.2 Student Enrollment Workflow

1. **Verify enrollment documents**: Proof of residence, birth certificate, immunization records, prior school transcript/records
2. **Enter data into SIS**: Follow data entry protocol; verify spelling of name; match documents exactly
3. **Classroom placement coordination**: Communicate with grade-level lead or counselor; note any special accommodations
4. **Parent orientation**: Explain school procedures; provide handbook; emergency contact update
5. **Internal notification**: Email teachers and relevant staff of new student; include any accommodations or critical information
6. **Records request**: Send records request to prior school; track pending records; follow up in 10 days if no response

## 8.3 Parent-Teacher Conference Coordination

1. **Scheduling**: Collect teacher availability; coordinate with parent schedules; book rooms
2. **Reminder**: Send reminder (email or call) 48 hours before
3. **Materials preparation**: Ensure teacher has conference forms, progress data, work samples
4. **Interpreter services**: Arrange if needed; notify interpreter of time, language, and context
5. **Sign-in and seating**: Greet parent; direct to room; ensure privacy for conference
6. **Documentation**: Record conference outcome; any action items; follow-up required
7. **Follow-up**: If action items assigned, email confirmation to parent within 48 hours
