# Standards & Reference

## 7.1 Culinary & Baking Standards

- **ServSafe Food Handler/Manager Certification**: USDA/FDA food safety standards
- **FDA Food Code**: Food handling, temperature control, contamination prevention
- **American Culinary Federation (ACF) Certification**: Certified Pastry Culinarian, Certified Journeyman Cook
- **Retail Bakers of America (RBA) Standards**: Professional baking competencies
- **Dietary Reference Intakes (DRI)**: Nutritional standards for recipe development
- **Labeling requirements**: FDA food allergen labeling (Big 9 allergens)

## 7.2 Baking Science Frameworks

- **The Baker's Percentage**: Flour = 100%; all other ingredients expressed relative to flour weight
- **Gluten development mechanics**: Glutenin and gliadin interaction; hydration ratios; mixing methods
- **Maillard reaction**: Browning chemistry; temperature and sugar relationships
- **Leavening agent chemistry**: Biological (yeast), chemical (baking powder/soda), mechanical (steam/meringue)
- **Emulsification science**: HLB values; emulsifiers in baking (lecithin, monoglycerides)
- **Pasteurization and food safety temperatures**: 135°F (hot hold), 145°F (fish), 160°F (ground meat), 165°F (poultry)

## 7.3 Assessment Standards

- **Practical skills assessment**: Mixing methods, shaping techniques, oven loading, finishing
- **Written assessment**: Recipe costing, food safety, ingredient function identification
- **Production timing**: Speed and efficiency under timed conditions
- **Sanitation audit**: Station setup, breakdown, and sanitation compliance
- **Plate presentation**: Visual appeal, portioning, garnish technique

## 7.4 Professional Development Standards

- **Knife skills certification**: Cuts (julienne, brunoise, chiffonade, dice variations)
- **Temperature management**: Hot and cold holding, thaw methods, tempering chocolate
- **Recipe scaling**: Ratio conversion, baker's percentage calculations, yield adjustments
- **Menu costing**: Food cost percentage, recipe costing sheets, profit margin analysis
