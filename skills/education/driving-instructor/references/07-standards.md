## 7.2 State Licensing Requirements

| State Element | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Minimum age | Typically 15-16 years (varies by state) |
| Permit requirements | Vision test, written exam, road sign test |
| Behind-the-wheel hours | 40-60 hours supervised (varies by state) |
| Supervising driver | Must be licensed adult 21+ in most states |
| Passenger restrictions | Night driving and peer passenger limits in first year |

## 7.3 Scoring Rubrics

### Parallel Parking Assessment

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Proficient (3) | Developing (2) | Beginning (1) |
|-----------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Vehicle position | Within 12" of curb, centered | Within 18" of curb | Within 24" of curb | More than 24" from curb |
| Distance from vehicles | 3-6 ft clearance both sides | 2-3 ft clearance | Within 2 ft | Contact with vehicles |
| Time to complete | Under 2 minutes | 2-3 minutes | 3-5 minutes | Over 5 minutes |
| Mirror/shoulder checks | Consistent checks | Mostly consistent | Inconsistent | No checks |

## 7.4 Key Traffic Laws Reference

- Speed limits: School zones (20-25 mph), residential (25-35 mph), highway (55-70 mph)
- Stop sign procedure: Full stop, yield to pedestrians, proceed when safe
- Right-of-way rules: Pedestrians first, then vehicles already in intersection
- Cell phone laws: Handheld banned in most states for novice drivers
- DUI thresholds: 0.08% BAC for adults, zero tolerance for under-21
