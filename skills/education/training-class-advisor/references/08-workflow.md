# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Academic Advising Session Workflow

```
Pre-Session Preparation
├── Review student's degree audit (degree works or transcript)
├── Check current semester grades and progress
├── Flag any outstanding holds (admission, financial, immunization)
├── Prepare tentative course recommendations based on degree plan
└── Send pre-session questionnaire if available (assess major uncertainty, concerns)

Session Structure (20-30 minutes)
├── 3 min: Warm welcome; check-in on how semester is going
├── 5 min: Review semester performance; discuss any challenges
├── 15 min: Plan next semester courses; discuss major/minor decisions
├── 5 min: Identify campus resources if needed; set action items
└── 2 min: Confirm next appointment; provide written summary

Post-Session
├── Update advising notes in SIS or advising notes system
├── Upload signed degree plan or course schedule
├── Submit any referral forms (tutoring, counseling, financial aid)
├── Send follow-up email summarizing discussed items and action items
└── Schedule follow-up if academic concerns were identified
```

## 8.2 Early Alert Workflow

1. **Identification**: Faculty mid-term grade alert or advisor observation of at-risk behavior (attendance drop, expressed stress)
2. **Referral**: Submit early alert form in SIS within 48 hours of concern
3. **Outreach**: Academic support staff contacts student within 3 business days
4. **Intervention**: Tutoring referral, study skills workshop, counseling referral, or financial aid advising — matched to the concern
5. **Follow-up**: Academic advisor notified of outreach outcome; student encouraged to meet with advisor
6. **Documentation**: Outcome recorded in SIS; patterns tracked for institutional analysis

## 8.3 Major/Minor Declaration Workflow

1. **Initial inquiry**: Student expresses interest in change; review current degree audit
2. **Gap analysis**: Calculate courses needed for new major; identify impact on graduation timeline
3. **Advising conversation**: Discuss motivation for change; confirm commitment; review financial/timeline implications
4. **Department consultation**: Verify prerequisites and enrollment capacity in new major
5. **Formal change**: Student submits Change of Major form; advisor signature; department approval
6. **Updated degree plan**: New degree audit generated; student receives updated pathway
7. **Financial aid check**: Verify financial aid implications if change affects program length
8. **Follow-up**: Schedule 30-day check-in after major change
