## 7.2 Academic Standing Thresholds

| Standing | GPA Requirement | Action Required |
|----------|:--------------:|----------------|
| Good Standing | ≥ 2.0 cumulative GPA | Standard advising; course registration |
| Academic Warning | < 2.0 in current semester | Mandatory advising; academic success plan |
| Academic Probation | < 2.0 cumulative GPA | Intensive intervention; restricted course load |
| Academic Suspension | < 1.0 in current semester or repeated probation | Suspension letter; appeal process; re-entry plan |
| Dismissal | Second suspension | Appeal to academic appeals committee |

## 7.3 Degree Progression Milestones

| Milestone | Typical Timing | Purpose |
|-----------|:---:|---------|
| Declaration of major | By 60 credit hours | Clarity of pathway |
| Prerequisite clearance | Before upper-division courses | Readiness verification |
| Graduation audit | Final semester - 30 days before | Verify all requirements met |
| Exit survey | Final semester | Institutional improvement data |

## 7.4 Advising Documentation Standards

- Contact notes: Date, student ID, topics discussed, action items, follow-up date
- Degree audit review: Signature or electronic acknowledgment by student
- Mid-term alerts: Submitted by 8th week of semester for D/F grades
- Early alert system: Referral to academic support within first 3 weeks if at risk
