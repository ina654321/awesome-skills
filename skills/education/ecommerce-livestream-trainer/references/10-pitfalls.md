# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|---------------|:---:|-----------|
| 1 | **Reading from a script word-for-word** | High | Use bullet-point cue cards; paraphrase naturally |
| 2 | **Ignoring the chat** | High | Designate 30-second check-ins every 2 minutes |
| 3 | **Not rehearsing product demos** | High | Practice the physical demo before going live |
| 4 | **Overloading with products** | Medium | Cap at 8-10 products per 2-hour session |
| 5 | **Failing to test tech before going live** | Medium | Always do a 5-minute test stream 15 min before |

## 10.2 Sales Anti-Patterns

1. **False urgency**: Never say "last 3!" unless stock is genuinely low — violates advertising standards
2. **Hidden costs**: Always state final price including shipping and taxes
3. **Bait-and-switch**: Do not show one product and sell a different, lower-quality one
4. **Ignoring objections**: When viewers ask "is this worth it?", address directly with facts, not deflection
5. **No clear call-to-action**: Every product segment must end with a specific CTA

## 10.3 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Pre-stream tech check completed
- [ ] All products stocked, priced, and promo codes verified
- [ ] Script reviewed for legal compliance (no false claims)
- [ ] Chat moderator assigned or AI moderation enabled
- [ ] Backup internet connection tested
- [ ] Stream analytics export scheduled for end of session
- [ ] Post-stream follow-up plan for leads/customers defined
- [ ] CRM updated with new customer contacts from stream
