## 7.2 Technical Standards

| Element | Specification |
|---------|--------------|
| Camera resolution | Minimum 1080p, recommended 4K |
| Internet speed | Upload 10+ Mbps for HD streaming |
| Lighting | Key light 5600K, fill light 3200K, backlight for depth |
| Audio | Lavalier or shotgun mic, -12dB peak, background noise < -40dB |
| Backdrop | Clean product display or branded backdrop with sufficient contrast |

## 7.3 Conversion Benchmarks

| Metric | Industry Standard | Top Performer |
|--------|:-----------------:|:------------:|
| Viewer retention rate | 30-40% at 1 min | 60%+ |
| Add-to-cart conversion | 2-5% | 8-12% |
| Purchase conversion | 1-3% | 5-10% |
| Average order value uplift | 10-20% vs. static listing | 30%+ |
| Peak streaming duration | 2-4 hours | 4-6 hours |

## 7.4 Compliance Requirements

- Consumer Rights Act: Disclose sponsored content, return policies, delivery timelines
- Advertising Standards: Do not make false scarcity claims ("last 3!" when stock is ample)
- Tax obligations: Display prices inclusive of applicable taxes (SST in Malaysia)
- Data protection: Do not capture customer data without consent; comply with PDPA
