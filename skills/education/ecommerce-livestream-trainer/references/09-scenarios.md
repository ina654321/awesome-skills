# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Common Scenario: Low Engagement in First 5 Minutes

**User:** "Every time I go live, viewers drop off immediately. What am I doing wrong?"

**Expert:**
> **Engagement Bootcamp:**
>
> 1. **Segment 1 (0-60s)**: Do NOT start with "Hi everyone, welcome to my stream." Start with a question or a bold statement. "I've got a product here that is going to save you RM500 this month — let me show you why."
> 2. **Segment 2 (1-3 min)**: Immediately show your best/most exciting product. This is your "hook product." If you save the best for last, most viewers won't see it.
> 3. **Interaction**: Ask a yes/no question every 30 seconds. "Has anyone bought a [product category] before? Drop a 'YES' in the comments."
> 4. **Pacing**: Talk faster during demos, slower during explanations. Monotone delivery kills retention.
> 5. **Reward**: Give small discount codes to early viewers as a loyalty signal.

## 9.2 Complex Scenario: Tech Failure Mid-Stream

**User:** "My internet dropped for 2 minutes during my peak viewership. How do I recover?"

**Expert:**
> **Disaster Recovery Protocol:**
>
> 1. **During outage**: Have a colleague or co-host continue on a backup device immediately
> 2. **Return to live**: Open with acknowledgment — "Sorry for the brief interruption! The traffic was so good we crashed the server — good problem to have!"
> 3. **Rebuild momentum**: Quickly recap what viewers missed ("During break, 50 viewers already bought the RM99 item at RM49")
> 4. **Double down on urgency**: Run a limited-time flash deal for 10 minutes to reclaim attention
> 5. **Prevent recurrence**: Always have a 4G/5G hotspot as backup; hardwire ethernet when possible; close background apps

## 9.3 Edge Case: Hostile Commenter / Troll

**User:** "A commenter is repeatedly calling my products fake. What should I do?"

**Expert:**
> **Moderation Response:**
>
> 1. **Do NOT argue publicly** — this amplifies negativity
> 2. **Timeout or ban immediately** — most platforms have quick-moderation tools
> 3. **Redirect**: "Let's keep this space positive for everyone. Next up: our best-selling [product]!"
> 4. **Post-stream**: Review if there's any truth to the criticism; if not, document for any future dispute
> 5. **Proactive**: Set chat rules in your stream description; have a moderator assigned to monitor chat in real time
