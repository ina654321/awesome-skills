# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Misrepresenting client ability** | High | Truthful marketing only |
| 2 | **Conflicts of interest** | High | Disclose all relationships |
| 3 | **Unauthorized negotiations** | High | Always have player approval |
| 4 | **NCAA violations** | High | Know rules thoroughly |
| 5 | **Over-promising results** | High | Manage expectations |
| 6 | **Poor financial referrals** | High | Only recommend vetted advisors |
| 7 | **Neglecting player development** | Medium | Ongoing career investment |
| 8 | **Not diversifying client base** | Medium | Don't depend on one sport/player |
| 9 | **Ignoring contract details** | High | Every clause matters |
| 10 | **Burning bridges** | High | Sports is small world |

## 10.2 Legal & Compliance Mistakes

- **Operating without registration**: Always verify state requirements
- **Tampering violations**: Never contact represented players
- **NCAA improper benefits**: Zero tolerance
- **Undisclosed referral fees**: Full disclosure required
- **Misrepresenting contract terms**: No misleading statements

## 10.3 Negotiation Mistakes

- **Showing all cards**: Keep negotiating positions private
- **Bad-faith negotiating**: Always honor agreements
- **Not knowing comparable deals**: Research is non-negotiable
- **Emotional negotiating**: Stay professional
- **Signing bad deals**: Sometimes the best deal is no deal

## 10.4 Client Management Mistakes

- **Neglecting less marketable clients**: All clients matter
- **Not documenting conversations**: Always document
- **Sharing confidential information**: Client trust is paramount
- **Not communicating regularly**: Weekly check-ins minimum
- **Ignoring player mental health**: Referral to professionals when needed

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] State registration current and verified
- [ ] Player agreement signed before negotiations
- [ ] All negotiations documented
- [ ] NCAA compliance verified (if applicable)
- [ ] Commission terms clearly agreed
- [ ] Financial advisor disclosures provided
- [ ] Marketing agreements reviewed by attorney
- [ ] FTC compliance on all endorsements
- [ ] Player informed of all developments
- [ ] Renewal opportunities tracked
- [ ] Post-season reviews completed
- [ ] Commission payments tracked and invoiced
