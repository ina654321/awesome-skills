# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Client Acquisition

```
Recruitment Process
├── 1. Scouting
│   ├── College/professional game attendance
│   ├── Video analysis and scouting reports
│   ├── Consult with coaches and advisors
│   └── Identify developmental prospects
│
├── 2. Initial Contact
│   ├── NCAA compliance check (if applicable)
│   ├── Professional introduction
│   ├── Express genuine interest in career
│   └── Request meeting
│
├── 3. Pitch Meeting
│   ├── Present agency track record
│   ├── Introduce team and services
│   ├── Discuss career goals and timeline
│   └── Assess mutual fit
│
└── 4. Agreement
    ├── Review representation contract thoroughly
    ├── Explain all terms and fees
    ├── NCAA notification (if applicable)
    ├── Sign representation agreement
    └── Welcome to roster
```

## 8.2 Contract Negotiation

```
Negotiation Workflow
├── Pre-Negotiation (2-4 weeks before deadline)
│   ├── Meet with player to understand priorities
│   ├── Gather all comparable contracts
│   ├── Build negotiation strategy
│   ├── Identify team constraints
│   └── Prepare walk-away position
│
├── Negotiation Phase
│   ├── Initial contact with team management
│   ├── Present proposal with justification
│   ├── Counter-negotiate through back channels
│   ├── Keep player informed throughout
│   └── Manage expectations realistically
│
├── Agreement
│   ├── Final terms reviewed with player
│   ├── All questions answered
│   ├── Contract signed
│   ├── Press conference/announcement
│   └── Commission collected
│
└── Post-Signing
    ├── Welcome package delivered
    ├── Financial advisor introduction
    ├── Marketing outreach begun
    └── Player check-in scheduled
```

## 8.3 Marketing & Endorsement Workflow

```
Endorsement Process
├── 1. Portfolio Building
│   ├── Professional headshots and video
│   ├── Media training (if needed)
│   ├── Social media audit and strategy
│   └── Brand positioning development
│
├── 2. Outreach
│   ├── Identify target brands (fit + value)
│   ├── Prepare media kit
│   ├── Direct brand outreach
│   └── Attend industry events
│
├── 3. Deal Structuring
│   ├── Negotiate terms and rates
│   ├── Define deliverables and timeline
│   ├── FTC disclosure compliance
│   └── Contract review with attorney
│
├── 4. Execution
│   ├── Coordinate with brand marketing team
│   ├── Produce agreed content
│   ├── Track deliverables vs. contract
│   └── Invoice and collect payment
│
└── 5. Relationship Management
    ├── Post-campaign debrief
    ├── Performance reporting to brand
    ├── Renewal discussions
    └── Long-term relationship building
```

## 8.4 NCAA Compliance

| Situation | Requirement | Timing |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Initial contact | Verbal OK, written before meeting | Before in-person |
| Verbal commitment | NCAA form required | Within 7 days |
| Written contract | NCAA agent disclosure | Before signing |
| Financial advice | Disclose referral arrangements | Before advice |
| Professional draft | Athlete must pay own expenses | Per NCAA rules |

## 8.5 Emergency Protocols

| Situation | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Player injury | Contact team medical, assess career impact |
| Public scandal | Immediate crisis communications response |
| Contract dispute | Legal review, negotiate resolution |
| Brand PR crisis | Coordinate with PR team, assess contract implications |
| Family interference | Professional boundaries, player choice respected |

## 8.6 Post-Season Workflow

- Contract evaluations and options
- Off-season marketing push
- Training and development planning
- Charity and community commitments
- Equipment and appearance prep for next season
