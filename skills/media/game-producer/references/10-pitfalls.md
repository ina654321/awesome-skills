# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Unrealistic timeline promises** | High | Conservative estimation with buffer |
| 2 | **Feature creep without scope control** | High | Change control board required |
| 3 | **Insufficient playtesting** | High | Weekly minimum playtests |
| 4 | **Ignoring QA feedback** | High | Every bug report is valid |
| 5 | **Unrealistic budgets** | High | Include 20-30% contingency |
| 6 | **Key person dependency** | High | Knowledge sharing culture |
| 7 | **Platform certification neglect** | High | Start cert prep early |
| 8 | **Crunch culture normalization** | High | Sustainable shipping schedule |
| 9 | **Underestimating localization** | Medium | Start localization early |
| 10 | **Post-launch support not planned** | Medium | Budget live ops from start |

## 10.2 Production Mistakes

- **Late requirements changes**: Freezing GDD at alpha
- **Inadequate documentation**: Undocumented systems
- **No vertical slice**: Trying to prototype in production
- **Over-polishing early**: Core loop must work first
- **Platform-specific too early**: Platform cert at the end

## 10.3 Team Management Mistakes

- **Micromanagement**: Trust your leads
- **Poor communication**: Weekly updates to all
- **Scope changes mid-sprint**: Change control process
- **Underperforming team members**: Address early
- **Crunch without cause**: Sustainable pace shipping

## 10.4 Business Mistakes

- **Unrealistic budgets**: Hidden costs always emerge
- **No marketing plan**: Marketing needs 12-month runway
- **Publisher relationship poor**: Regular communication essential
- **IP ownership unclear**: Contract terms explicit
- **No post-launch plan**: DLC, updates require planning

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Game Design Document complete and approved
- [ ] Budget approved with contingency
- [ ] Timeline realistic and communicated
- [ ] Team staffed appropriately
- [ ] Change control process established
- [ ] Weekly playtests scheduled
- [ ] QA process documented
- [ ] Platform requirements understood
- [ ] Localization plan in place
- [ ] Marketing plan coordinated
- [ ] Post-launch support budgeted
- [ ] Publisher communication regular
- [ ] Risk register maintained
