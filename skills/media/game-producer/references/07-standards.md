## 7.2 Development Timeline Standards

| Production Phase | Duration | Deliverables |
|----------------|----------|-------------|
| Pre-production | 3-6 months | GDD, vertical slice, team hiring |
| Production | 12-36 months | Game builds, content creation |
| Alpha | 3-6 months | Feature complete, major bugs fixed |
| Beta | 2-4 months | Polish, balance, testing |
| Release | 1-2 months | Gold master, launch, hotfixes |
| Post-release | Ongoing | DLC, updates, live ops |

## 7.3 Game Rating Requirements

| Region | Body | Submission | Fees |
|--------|------|-----------|------|
| North America | ESRB | Online submission | $5,000-10,000 |
| Europe | PEGI | Online submission | 500-2,000 EUR |
| UK | UKIE/PEGI | PEGI submission | Included |
| Germany | USK | In-person review | 2,000-5,000 EUR |
| Australia | ACB | Online submission | AUD $500-2,000 |
| Japan | CERO | In-person review | 100,000+ JPY |

## 7.4 Milestone Payment Structure

| Milestone | % of Budget | Typical Deliverables |
|-----------|-------------|---------------------|
| Contract signing | 10-20% | Signed agreement |
| Vertical slice | 15-25% | Core mechanics playable |
| Alpha | 20-30% | Feature complete |
| Beta | 15-20% | Content complete, ship-ready |
| Gold/Launch | 15-20% | Final build delivered |

## 7.5 Platform Certification Standards

| Platform | Requirements | Timeline |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| PlayStation (Sony) | LOTG compliance, technical specs | 3-4 weeks |
| Xbox (Microsoft) | XLCR compliance, TRC | 2-4 weeks |
| Nintendo | LotCheck, quality standards | 4-6 weeks |
| Steam | No certification, self-publish | Immediate |
| Mobile (iOS) | App Store guidelines | 24-48 hours |
| Mobile (Android) | Play Store policies | 1-7 days |

## 7.6 Content Quality Standards

| Element | Standard | QA Coverage |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Frame rate | 30 fps minimum, 60 fps target | Per frame analysis |
| Crash rate | < 1% at launch | 10,000+ hours testing |
| Bug severity 1 | Zero at launch | Priority fixing |
| Bug severity 2 | < 5 at launch | Priority fixing |
| Accessibility | WCAG 2.1 AA | Platform requirements |
| Localization | Full translation + cultural | Linguistic QA |
