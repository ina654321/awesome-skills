## 8.5 Deadline Management

| Deadline Type | Standard | Emergency Override |
|--------------|---------|-------------------|
| Breaking news | 30 min to 2 hours | Fastest possible |
| Daily deadline | 6 PM local / midnight | Breaking news exception |
| Weekly deadline | Wednesday/Thursday | Publication day minus 1 |
| Long-form | 2-4 weeks | Negotiated per project |
| Investigation | 3-12 months | Extended exclusivity |
