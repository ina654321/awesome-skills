## 7.2 Broadcast Standards

| Element | Standard | FCC Requirement |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Obscenity | Zero tolerance | No broadcast |
| Profanity | Major penalties | Document-based |
| Indecency | Post-watershed rules | 10 PM - 6 AM | 
| Sponsorship ID | Required | Clear identification |
| Contests | Disclose terms | Full transparency |
| Political ads | Equal time may apply | Consult attorney |

## 7.3 Teleprompter Standards

| Element | Standard | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Font | Sans-serif, large | Minimum 22-point on screen |
| Speed | 2.5-3 words/second | Adjust for complexity |
| Contrast | High contrast | White on dark or dark on white |
| Margins | Safe area maintained | 80% of screen width |
| Grammar | AP style | End punctuation for pauses |

## 7.4 Makeup & Wardrobe

| Element | Standard | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| HD makeup | Required | Eliminates shine, defines features |
| Colors | Avoid extremes | Muted, professional tones |
| Patterns | Avoid fine patterns | Creates moire on camera |
| Movement | Minimal | Stud earrings, simple jewelry |
| Glasses | Anti-glare coating | Essential for broadcast |

## 7.5 Technical Performance

| Metric | Standard | Measurement |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| Pacing | Natural, conversational | Not rushed or dragged |
| Eye contact | Camera, not script | Practice memorization |
| Breathing | Controlled | Pre-read warmup |
| Articulation | Clear consonants | Especially sibilants |
| Projection | Slightly louder than normal | Not shouting |

## 7.6 Breaking News Protocol

| Situation | Response Time | Authority |
|----------|--------------|-----------|
| Breaking news alert | 2-5 minutes | Anchor/EP decision |
| Live coverage | As warranted | Executive producer |
| Breaking graphics | Within 60 seconds | CG operator |
| Social media verification | Before air | Digital producer |
| Correction | Immediate if live error | Anchor discretion |
