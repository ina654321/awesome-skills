# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Morning Show Preparation

```
Morning Show Day (3 AM - 10 AM)
├── 3:00 AM: Arrival
│   ├── Review overnight developments
│   ├── Check email and urgent messages
│   └── Prepare personal setup
│
├── 3:30 AM: Hair and Makeup
│   ├── Professional HD makeup
│   ├── Hair styled appropriately
│   └── Wardrobe confirmed
│
├── 4:00 AM: Editorial Meeting
│   ├── Discuss top stories with EP
│   ├── Review rundown changes
│   ├── Guest confirmations
│   └── Weather check
│
├── 4:30 AM: Script Review
│   ├── Review all stories
│   ├── Verify facts and pronunciation
│   ├── Add personal touches
│   └── Mark ad-lib moments
│
├── 5:00 AM: Run-through
│   ├── Read-through with EP
│   ├── Timing check
│   └── Coordinate with control room
│
└── 6:00 AM: LIVE Broadcast
    ├── 3-hour morning show
    ├── Breaks every 9-12 minutes
    └── Breaking news integrated
```

## 8.2 Segment Workflow

```
Per-Segment Process
├── Pre-Segment (5-10 min before)
│   ├── Review script and graphics
│   ├── Confirm guest positioning
│   ├── Practice pronunciation
│   └── Discuss with co-anchor/producer
│
├── During Segment (2-5 minutes)
│   ├── Lead with hook
│   ├── Natural eye contact
│   ├── Let graphics support, not lead
│   └── End with forward angle
│
└── Post-Segment
    ├── Note feedback from EP
    ├── Adjust for next segment
    └── Continue flow to break
```

## 8.3 Breaking News Response

```
Breaking News Protocol
├── Immediate (0-5 minutes)
│   ├── Get confirmation from EP
│   ├── Update rundown
│   ├── Alert control room
│   ├── Break into programming if warranted
│   └── Alert social media team
│
├── First Break (5-10 minutes)
│   ├── Brief scripted update
│   ├── Live reporter/mobile unit check-in
│   ├── Graphics updated
│   └── Meteorologist if weather-related
│
└── Ongoing Coverage
    ├── Regular updates every 10-15 min
    ├── New information highlighted
    ├── Expert/guest booking
    └── Extended coverage if story warrants
```

## 8.4 Interview Workflow

```
Interview Process
├── Pre-Interview
│   ├── Know the guest's background thoroughly
│   ├── Prepare 8-10 questions
│   ├── Anticipate answers and follow-ups
│   └── Brief guest on format, timing
│
├── During Interview
│   ├── Lead with strongest question
│   ├── Follow interesting threads
│   ├── Keep to time limits
│   ├── Moderate panel discussions
│   └── Maintain neutrality
│
└── Post-Interview
    ├── Thank guest
    ├── Coordinate with producer
    └── Transcribe key quotes
```

## 8.5 Live Shot Preparation

```
Live Shot Workflow
├── Pre-Shot (30 minutes before)
│   ├── Location confirmed with photog
│   ├── Scripts/bullets reviewed
│   ├── Technical check with truck
│   └── Wardrobe adjustment for location
│
├── On Location
│   ├── Eyeline marked
│   ├── Audio check
│   ├── Sightlines confirmed
│   └── Relax and prepare
│
└── Live Shot
    ├── Natural delivery
    ├── Environmental awareness
    ├── Reference camera, not photog
    └── Extended ad-lib if warranted
```

## 8.6 End of Shift

- Debrief with EP on performance
- Check messages for follow-ups
- Review next day's potential stories
- Personal grooming check
- Professional appearance for off-air photos
