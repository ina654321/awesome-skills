# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Reading from script instead of camera** | High | Practice memorization, eye contact |
| 2 | **Appearing scripted** | Medium | Ad-lib naturally, vary delivery |
| 3 | **Ignoring breaking news queue** | High | Constant rundown awareness |
| 4 | **Pronouncing names incorrectly** | Medium | Pre-broadcast pronunciation review |
| 5 | **Filler words (um, uh)** | Medium | Practice and conscious reduction |
| 6 | **Speaking too fast** | Medium | Pacing practice, breathing |
| 7 | **Looking tired/bored** | Medium | Sleep, hydration, makeup |
| 8 | **Dressing inappropriately** | Medium | Consult wardrobe guidelines |
| 9 | **Missing pronunciation cues** | Medium | Mark scripts thoroughly |
| 10 | **Losing composure on air** | High | Emotional neutrality |

## 10.2 On-Air Mistakes

- **Forgetting to breathe**: Causes rushed delivery
- **Laughing at wrong time**: Maintain professionalism
- **Looking at monitors**: Always camera
- **Bad-mouthing stories off-air**: Mics are always hot
- **Phone use on air**: Silence all devices

## 10.3 Legal & Ethical Mistakes

- **Defamation**: Never state unverified accusations
- **Broadcasting unconfirmed rumors**: Verify first
- **Invasion of privacy**: Grief, minors, uninvolved parties
- **Anonymous sourcing**: Only in rare, justified cases
- **FCC violations**: Know the rules

## 10.4 Personal Brand Mistakes

- **Over-branding yourself**: News comes first
- **Taking political positions**: Neutrality required
- **Social media controversies**: Separate personal/professional
- **Appearance inconsistency**: Reliable, professional look
- **Complaining publicly**: News is a team sport

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Script reviewed and marked
- [ ] All names pronounced correctly
- [ ] Stories verified with producers
- [ ] Wardrobe and makeup professional
- [ ] Ready 30 minutes before broadcast
- [ ] Run-through attended
- [ ] Technical cues confirmed
- [ ] Graphics and video reviewed
- [ ] Breaking news awareness current
- [ ] Co-anchor and crew coordinated
- [ ] Post-show debrief completed
