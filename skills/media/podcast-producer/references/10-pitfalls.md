# Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

## 10.1 Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Severity | Quick Fix |
|---|--------------|----------|-----------|
| 1 | **Poor audio quality** | High | Invest in $100+ microphone and acoustic treatment |
| 2 | **Inconsistent publishing schedule** | High | Set weekly/biweekly schedule and stick to it |
| 3 | **No intro/outro music** | Medium | Create consistent branding package |
| 4 | **Ignoring SEO** | Medium | Optimize titles, descriptions, show notes |
| 5 | **No backup recording** | High | Always record locally in addition to platform |
| 6 | **Selling out to bad sponsors** | Medium | Vet sponsors for audience fit |
| 7 | **Not repurposing content** | Medium | Create audiograms, clips, blog posts |
| 8 | **Ignoring analytics** | Medium | Review downloads, retention, drops |
| 9 | **Monotonous format** | Medium | Vary episode structure, add segments |
| 10 | **Copyright music** | High | Use royalty-free music exclusively |

## 10.2 Recording Mistakes

- **Plosives (P sounds)**: Use pop filter, mic off-axis
- **Room echo**: Acoustic panels, closet recording
- **Inconsistent levels**: Monitor throughout recording
- **Background noise**: Quiet room, close mic
- **No backup**: Always have local backup recording

## 10.3 Production Mistakes

- **Over-editing**: Remove mistakes, not personality
- **Under-editing**: Long dead air frustrates listeners
- **No leveling**: Normalize audio to consistent loudness
- **Crushed dynamics**: Light compression, not heavy
- **No chapter markers**: Add value for listener navigation

## 10.4 Distribution Mistakes

- **Wrong RSS feed settings**: Wrong category hurts discovery
- **Unoptimized titles**: Include searchable keywords
- **No show notes**: Help search engines understand content
- **Over-submitting directories**: Some rediscovery needed
- **Ignoring Apple Podcasts reviews**: Respond professionally

## 10.5 Quality Checklist

- [ ] Audio recorded at 48kHz/24-bit minimum
- [ ] Noise floor below -60 dBFS
- [ ] Consistent audio levels throughout
- [ ] Intro/outro music branded
- [ ] Sponsor reads inserted naturally
- [ ] Show notes SEO-optimized
- [ ] Episode published on schedule
- [ ] Submitted to all major directories
- [ ] Social media promotion done
- [ ] Analytics reviewed after 48 hours
- [ ] Guest thanked and episode sent
