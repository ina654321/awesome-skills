## 7.2 Cultural Heritage Classification

| Category | Definition | Protection Level |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Tangible heritage | Physical artifacts, sites | UNESCO World Heritage |
| Intangible heritage | Traditions, practices | UNESCO ICH Lists |
| Natural heritage | Natural sites, biodiversity | UNESCO World Heritage |
| Digital heritage | Born-digital cultural content | Emerging frameworks |
| Indigenous heritage | Native cultural expressions | ILO 169, UNDRIP |

## 7.3 Intellectual Property Standards

| IP Type | Duration | Cultural Application |
|---------|----------|---------------------|
| Copyright | Life + 70 years | Cultural works, performances |
| Traditional knowledge | Perpetual (inherent) | Indigenous practices |
| Geographical indications | Indefinite renewal | Regional crafts, foods |
| Trade secrets | Until disclosed | Cultural techniques |
| Patents | 20 years | Innovative cultural methods |

## 7.4 Cultural Sensitivity Standards

| Principle | Application | Documentation |
|----------|------------|--------------|
| Free, Prior, Informed Consent (FPIC) | Indigenous involvement | Written agreements |
| Attribution | Cultural source acknowledgment | Credit practices |
| Non-appropriation | Avoiding exploitation | Review protocols |
| Benefit sharing | Community compensation | Revenue sharing |
| Context preservation | Cultural meaning maintenance | Scholarly accuracy |

## 7.5 Cultural Impact Assessment

| Element | Assessment Criteria | Standard |
|---------|---------------------|----------|
| Historical accuracy | Primary sources consulted | Academic standard |
| Community involvement | Indigenous/affected community consulted | FPIC standard |
| Representation | Diverse perspectives included | Equity framework |
| Economic impact | Benefit to source communities | Fair trade principles |
| Environmental impact | Sustainability of cultural practices | UNESCO guidelines |
