# Quality Inspector — Standards & Reference

## Industry Standards & Certifications

### Quality Management Systems

**ISO 9001:2015** — Quality Management System standard. The most widely recognized QMS standard globally. Defines requirements for quality policy, objectives, processes, and continuous improvement. Website: iso.org

**IATF 16949:2016** — Automotive QMS standard. Required for automotive supply chain. Combines ISO 9001 with automotive-specific requirements. Website: iatfglobalwatch.org

**AS9100** — Aerospace QMS standard. Based on ISO 9001 with aerospace additions. Required for aerospace suppliers.

**ISO 13485** — Medical devices QMS. Required for medical device manufacturing.

**ISO 14001** — Environmental management systems.

### Inspection & Testing Standards

**ANSI/ASQ Z1.4 (ISO 2859-1)** — Sampling procedures for inspection by attributes. The standard AQL sampling tables. Website: asq.org

**ANSI/ASQ Z1.9 (ISO 3951)** — Sampling procedures for inspection by variables. For continuous measurements rather than pass/fail.

**ISO 2859-2** — Switching rules for AQL sampling. Defines normal, tightened, and reduced inspection switching.

**ASTM E29** — Standard practice for using significant digits in test data to determine conformance with specifications.

### Dimensional Standards

**ASME Y14.5** — Dimensioning and Tolerancing. The North American standard for GD&T.

**ISO 1101** — Geometrical product specifications. International tolerancing standard.

**ISO 8015** — Fundamental tolerancing principles.

**ISO 2768** — General tolerances for ISO standards.

### Measurement Standards

**ISO 10012** — Measurement management systems. Requirements for measurement processes and equipment.

**NIST Handbook 44** — Specifications, tolerances, and other technical requirements for commercial weighing and measuring devices.

**ISO 17025** — General requirements for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories.

## Professional Certifications

**ASQ Certified Quality Inspector (CQI)** — ASQ certification for quality inspection professionals. Website: asq.org

**ASQ Certified Quality Engineer (CQE)** — Broader quality engineering certification.

**ASQ Certified Quality Auditor (CQA)** — For quality auditing professionals.

**CMQ/OE** — Certified Manager of Quality/Organizational Excellence.

**Six Sigma Green Belt / Black Belt** — Statistical process control and improvement methodology.

## Measurement & Inspection Tools

### Dimensional

**Outside Micrometer (0.001")** — Precise measurement of external dimensions. 1" to 12" range.

**Inside Micrometer** — Measuring internal dimensions (holes, slots).

**Height Gauge** — Vertical measurement from a reference surface.

**Dial Indicator (0.0001")** — Measuring runout, concentricity, flatness.

**CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine)** — 3D measurement for complex geometries.

**Thread Gauge (Go/No-Go)** — Threaded gauge for verifying thread acceptance.

### Attribute

**Go/No-Go Gauge** — Pass/fail check for fit, thread, or dimension.

**Pin Gauge** — Set of precision pins for hole verification.

**Surface Plate** — Reference surface for height gauges and dial indicators.

**Radius Gauge** — Template gauges for verifying radius specifications.

### Surface

**Surface Roughness Tester (Ra)** — Measures surface texture micro-variations.

** profilometer** — Detailed surface topography measurement.

## Inspection Documentation

**First Article Inspection (FAI)** — Complete dimensional verification of first part from new production run.

**PPAP (Production Part Approval Process)** — Automotive supplier submission package proving production capability.

**Dimensional Inspection Report (DIR)** — Documented results of dimensional verification.

**Non-Conformance Report (NCR)** — Formal record of specification deviation.

**Certificate of Conformance (COC)** — Document confirming product meets specifications.

**Material Test Report (MTR)** — Verification of material properties (heat number traceable).
