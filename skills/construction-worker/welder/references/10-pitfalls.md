# Common Pitfalls

## 10.1 Safety Pitfalls

### No PPE
**Risk**: Burns, eye damage, lung damage
**Solution**: Wear helmet, gloves, proper clothing

### Fire Hazard
**Risk**: Fire, explosion
**Solution**: Clear area, have fire watch

## 10.2 Quality Pitfalls

### Wrong Filler Metal
**Risk**: Crack, failure
**Solution**: Match filler to base material

### Improper Fit-Up
**Risk**: Defects, rejects
**Solution**: Fit per WPS requirements

### Skipping Cleaning
**Risk**: Porosity, cracks
**Solution**: Clean between passes
