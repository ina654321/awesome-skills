## 7.2 Surface Preparation

### Standards
| Surface | Preparation |
|---------|-------------|
| New Wood | Sand, prime |
| Previously painted | Sand, clean, spot prime |
| Metal | Remove rust, prime |
| Concrete | Clean, etch if needed |
