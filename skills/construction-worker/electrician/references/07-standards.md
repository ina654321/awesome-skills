## 7.3 Safety Standards

### Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)
1. Notify affected employees
2. Turn off equipment
3. Isolate from energy source
4. Apply lock and tag
5. Release stored energy
6. Verify zero energy

### PPE Requirements
- Safety glasses
- Arc-rated clothing
- Insulated gloves (Class 00-4)
- Hearing protection
- Hard hat
