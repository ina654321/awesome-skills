# Scenario Examples

## 9.1 Leaking Membrane Scenario

### Situation
Water coming through after membrane applied.

### Steps
1. Identify leak location
2. Repair per manufacturer
3. Verify proper installation
4. Test again

## 9.2 Poor Adhesion Scenario

### Situation
Membrane not sticking to substrate.

### Steps
1. Check surface prep
2. Verify moisture content
3. Check temperature
4. May need primer
