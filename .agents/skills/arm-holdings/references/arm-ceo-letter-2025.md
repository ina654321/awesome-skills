# Message from Arm CEO Rene Haas - SoftBank Group Report 2025

## Strategic Vision

> "We are increasing investment to advance Arm's strategy. The AI revolution is creating unprecedented demand for efficient compute, and Arm is uniquely positioned to power this transformation."

— Rene Haas, CEO, Arm Holdings plc

## Key Messages

### On AI Opportunity

"The deployment of AI is accelerating across all markets—from cloud data centers to edge devices. Arm's energy-efficient compute architecture is the foundation for this AI era. By 2025, we expect over 100 billion Arm devices will be AI-capable."

### On Compute Subsystems (CSS)

"CSS represents Arm's evolution from IP provider to platform provider. By delivering pre-integrated, pre-validated compute platforms, we reduce our partners' time-to-market by 12-18 months while capturing significantly higher value per chip."

### On Data Center Growth

"The data center is undergoing a fundamental transformation. Power consumption is the limiting factor for AI infrastructure, and Arm's efficiency advantage—2-3x better performance-per-watt than x86—is driving adoption by AWS, Microsoft, Google, and Oracle."

### On Windows PC Ambition

"We are targeting 50% of the Windows PC market within five years. With our CSS for Client platform, featuring Cortex-X925 and Immortalis-G925, we now have the performance to compete at the premium tier while maintaining our efficiency advantage."

### On Automotive

"Software-defined vehicles represent a massive opportunity. Our new Zena CSS for Automotive enables automakers to deploy AI-defined vehicle platforms a full year faster than custom designs."

## Investment Priorities

Arm is increasing R&D investment in:

1. **Next-generation CPU architectures** (v9 evolution, v10 planning)
2. **AI accelerators** (Ethos NPU family expansion)
3. **Chiplets and advanced packaging** (UCIe interconnect support)
4. **Security** (Confidential Compute Architecture)
5. **Software ecosystem** (KleidiAI libraries, optimization tools)

## Long-Term Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Revenue Growth | 20%+ CAGR through FY2027 |
| Operating Margin | 40%+ (long-term) |
| Armv9 Royalty Mix | 50%+ by FY2027 |
| Data Center Royalty | 25% of total by FY2030 |

## Partnership Philosophy

"Arm's success is inseparable from our partners' success. We don't compete with our licensees—we enable them. Whether you're a hyperscaler designing custom silicon or a startup creating your first chip, Arm provides the foundation for innovation."
