## Strategic Priorities (FY2025)

1. **AI Everywhere**: From cloud to edge, all products AI-optimized
2. **Compute Subsystems Expansion**: CSS driving higher value per chip
3. **Automotive Acceleration**: ADAS and software-defined vehicles
4. **PC Market Disruption**: Windows on Arm growth initiative
5. **RISC-V Defense**: Flexible licensing to maintain ecosystem lock-in

## Risk Factors

1. **Concentration Risk**: Top 5 customers = ~55% of revenue
2. **China Exposure**: ~25% of revenue, geopolitical uncertainty
3. **RISC-V Competition**: Open ISA gaining traction
4. **Smartphone Market**: Mature, lower growth expectations
5. **Customer Insourcing**: Large licensees may develop custom architectures
