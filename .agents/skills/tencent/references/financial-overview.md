# Tencent Financial Overview (FY2024)

> Last Updated: March 2025 (FY2024 Earnings Release)
> 
> Source: Tencent Holdings Limited 2024 Annual Results

---

## Executive Summary

Tencent delivered exceptional financial performance in FY2024, with record profits driven by AI-enhanced advertising, gaming strength, and disciplined cost management.

| Metric | FY2024 | YoY Change |
|--------|--------|------------|
| **Revenue** | ¥660.3B (~$91.9B USD) | +8% |
| **Gross Profit** | ¥349.2B | +19% |
| **Operating Profit (Non-IFRS)** | ¥237.8B | +24% |
| **Net Profit** | ¥194.1B (~$27B USD) | +68% |
| **Q4 Revenue** | ¥172.5B | +11% |
| **Q4 Net Profit** | ¥55.3B | +30% |

## Revenue Breakdown by Segment

### 1. Value-Added Services (VAS) - ¥319.2B (+7%)

**Gaming Revenue: ¥197.7B**

| Category | Revenue | YoY Growth |
|----------|---------|------------|
| Domestic Gaming | ¥139.7B | +10% |
| International Gaming | ¥58.0B | +9% |

**Key Gaming Drivers:**
- Honor of Kings: 100M+ DAU milestone
- Valorant & Valorant Mobile strong performance
- Dungeon & Fighter Mobile contribution
- PUBG Mobile international strength
- Black Myth: Wukong (portfolio investment hit)

**Social Networks: ¥121.5B**
- WeChat subscription services
- QQ virtual items
- Live streaming gifts
- Tencent Music subscriptions (121M subscribers)
- Tencent Video subscriptions (113M subscribers)

### 2. Marketing Services (Advertising) - ¥121.4B (+20%)

| Ad Category | Growth Driver |
|-------------|---------------|
| Video Accounts Ads | +60% YoY |
| WeChat Search Ads | AI-enhanced relevance |
| Mini Program Ads | E-commerce integration |
| Traditional Social Ads | AI targeting improvements |

**AI-Powered Advertising Enhancements:**
- LLM-powered ad ranking systems
- AI-generated ad creatives
- Improved click-through rates
- Better advertiser ROI

### 3. FinTech & Business Services - ¥212.0B (+4%)

**FinTech:**
- WeChat Pay commercial volumes stabilized
- Merchant tech fees optimization
- Risk control improvements
- Payment funding cost reductions

**Business Services (Cloud):**
- AI cloud revenue approximately doubled
- GPU capacity constraints limited external growth
- Tencent Meeting, Docs enterprise adoption
- Expected acceleration in 2025 with new GPU deployments

## Profitability Analysis

### Margin Expansion

| Margin Type | FY2024 | FY2023 | Change |
|-------------|--------|--------|--------|
| Gross Margin | 52.9% | 48.1% | +4.8pp |
| Operating Margin (Non-IFRS) | 36.0% | 31.4% | +4.6pp |
| Net Margin | 29.4% | 18.9% | +10.5pp |

**Drivers of Margin Expansion:**
1. High-margin advertising growth (Video Accounts)
2. AI efficiency in ad targeting
3. Evergreen games with lower acquisition costs
4. Cloud profitability at scale
5. Disciplined cost management

### Operating Expenses

| Expense Category | FY2024 | YoY Change |
|------------------|--------|------------|
| R&D Expenses | Increased | Focus on AI |
| Marketing | Increased | Yuanbao promotion |
| General & Administrative | +11% | Headcount growth |

**Capital Expenditure:**
- FY2024: ¥76.7B (+221% YoY)
- Q4 2024: ¥36.6B (+386% YoY)
- Primary use: AI infrastructure (GPUs for inference)

## Segment Performance Deep Dive

### Gaming: The Evergreen Strategy

**Domestic Gaming (¥139.7B, +10%):**
- 12 games for domestic market in evergreen portfolio
- Flagship: Honor of Kings, Game for Peace
- New contributors: Dungeon & Fighter Mobile, Valorant

**International Gaming (¥58.0B, +9%):**
- 7 games for international markets
- Key titles: PUBG Mobile, Brawl Stars, Riot titles
- Level Infinite (publishing brand) growth

**Evergreen Games Framework:**
- Games designed for 5+ year lifecycles
- Continuous content updates (live-ops)
- Esports ecosystem integration
- Community-driven development

### Advertising: AI-Powered Growth

**Video Accounts Momentum:**
- Total user time: Rapid YoY growth
- Ad revenue: +60% YoY in Q4
- Live commerce integration
- Creator economy support

**AI Enhancements:**
- Ad targeting accuracy improvement
- Creative generation automation
- Closed-loop measurement via Mini Programs
- Advertiser ROI improvement

### FinTech & Cloud: Steady Contributors

**WeChat Pay:**
- Transaction volumes stabilized
- Commercial payment growth
- Wealth management steady
- Credit services cautious growth

**Tencent Cloud:**
- AI services demand surge
- GPU constraints (to be resolved 2025)
- Enterprise WeChat adoption
- Vertical solutions (gaming, media, finance)

## 2025 Outlook

### Guidance & Priorities

**AI Investment:**
- CapEx increase planned for 2025
- AI investments = "low double-digit %" of revenue
- Expected total: ¥100B+ CapEx
- Focus: Yuanbao growth, WeChat AI agents, cloud AI services

**Revenue Growth Drivers:**
1. Video Accounts ad revenue (continued momentum)
2. Gaming (domestic + international evergreen)
3. AI cloud services (post-GPU deployment)
4. WeChat e-commerce (Mini Shops)

**Margin Expectations:**
- Near-term: AI investments may pressure margins
- Medium-term: AI efficiency gains to improve margins
- Long-term: Operating leverage from AI platform

### Risk Factors

**Regulatory:**
- China gaming license cadence
- Data privacy enforcement
- Antitrust scrutiny
- US regulatory pressure (CFIUS review)

**Competitive:**
- ByteDance (Douyin/TikTok) in ads/short video
- Alibaba Cloud competition
- NetEase in premium gaming
- DeepSeek/open-source AI models

**Operational:**
- GPU supply constraints
- Talent competition in AI
- International expansion execution
