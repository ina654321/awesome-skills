# Tencent AI & Cloud Strategy

> "All-in on AI" | Hunyuan Foundation Model | Yuanbao AI Assistant | Tencent Cloud

---

## Executive Summary

AI has become Tencent's highest strategic priority for 2024-2026. The company is deploying unprecedented capital expenditure to build AI infrastructure, develop its Hunyuan foundation model, and integrate AI across its entire product ecosystem.

**Key Metrics (2024):**
- CapEx: ¥76.7B (+221% YoY)
- AI Cloud Revenue: ~doubled YoY
- Yuanbao DAU: 20x growth (Feb-Mar 2025)
- Hunyuan Model: 3rd generation released
- AI Integration: Across all major products

## Hunyuan Foundation Model (混元大模型)

### Model Evolution

| Version | Release | Parameters | Capabilities |
|---------|---------|------------|--------------|
| Hunyuan 1.0 | 2023 | 100B+ | Initial release |
| Hunyuan 2.0 | 2024 | 1T+ | Multimodal improvements |
| **Hunyuan 3.0** | **2025** | **Undisclosed** | **SOTA performance** |

### Technical Specifications

**Architecture:**
- Transformer-based
- Mixture of Experts (MoE) components
- Multimodal (text, image, video)
- Chinese language optimization

**Training:**
- Chinese and English corpora
- Tencent product data (privacy-compliant)
- Synthetic data generation
- Continuous fine-tuning

**Capabilities:**
- Text generation and understanding
- Code generation
- Mathematical reasoning
- Image generation
- Video understanding
- Long context windows

### Performance Claims

**Benchmarks:**
- Chinese language: Competitive with GPT-4
- Mathematical reasoning: Strong performance
- Code generation: Good for Chinese developers
- Cost efficiency: Optimized for inference

**Pony Ma on Hunyuan vs. DeepSeek:**
> "We admire DeepSeek's openness and efficiency. China's engineers have a cost and efficiency advantage. We see our own model and DeepSeek as complementary, much like how we balanced self-developed and licensed games in the past."

**Features:**
