## Governance Framework

### Three-Line Model

```
ALLIANZ GOVERNANCE STRUCTURE
│
├── FIRST LINE (Business Operations)
│   ├── Operating Entities (70+ countries)
│   ├── Business Segments (P&C, L/H, AM)
│   └── Revenue-generating activities
│
├── SECOND LINE (Risk & Compliance)
│   ├── Group Risk Management
│   ├── Compliance
│   ├── Actuarial
│   └── Legal
│
└── THIRD LINE (Internal Audit)
    └── Group Audit
```

### Risk Management

**Group Risk Management:**
- Chief Risk Officer (CRO) function
- Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) framework
- Solvency II compliance
- Stress testing and scenario analysis

**Key Risk Categories:**
1. Insurance risk (underwriting, catastrophe, longevity)
2. Market risk (interest rates, equity, currency)
3. Credit risk (investments, reinsurance)
4. Operational risk (IT, processes, people)
5. Strategic risk (competition, regulation)
6. Liquidity risk

### Compliance & Ethics

**Code of Conduct:**
- Integrity in all business dealings
- Compliance with laws and regulations
- Anti-corruption and anti-bribery
- Conflicts of interest management
- Data protection and privacy

**Whistleblower System:**
- Allianz Integrity Line (global)
- Anonymous reporting option
- Investigation and remediation process
