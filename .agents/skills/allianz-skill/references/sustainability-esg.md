# Allianz Sustainability & ESG

## Sustainability Strategy Overview

Allianz integrates environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors across all business activities, recognizing that sustainability is both a responsibility and a business opportunity. The company is recognized as a leader in the Dow Jones Sustainability Index.

---

## Climate Strategy

### Net-Zero Commitments

| Target | Timeline | Scope |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Net-Zero Operations** | 2030 | Allianz sites and activities across 70+ markets |
| **Net-Zero Investment Portfolio** | 2050 | Proprietary investments, science-based targets |
| **Net-Zero Insurance Underwriting** | 2050 | Insurance portfolio emissions |
| **50% Emissions Reduction** | 2030 | vs. 2019 baseline (interim target) |

### Science-Based Targets

Allianz has committed to targets aligned with the 1.5°C pathway:

**Investment Portfolio:**
- Listed equity and corporate bonds: 50% absolute owned emissions reduction by 2030 (vs. 2019)
- Progress: 36% achieved by end of 2022
- Real estate: 1.5°C-aligned total emissions pathways

**Oil & Gas Policy (Effective 2023):**
- No new investments or underwriting for:
  - Single-site/stand-alone oil and selected gas risks
  - Arctic/Antarctic oil and gas activities
  - Extra-heavy oil and ultra-deep sea risks
- By 2025: Net-zero commitment required from largest hydrocarbon producers for company-level coverage

### Renewable Energy Leadership

**Underwriting:**
- Significant capacity for renewable energy projects
- Wind, solar, hydro, battery storage expertise
- Green energy projects not restricted (even for oil & gas companies)

**Investment:**
- Growing portfolio of renewable infrastructure
- Green bonds allocation
- Climate solutions strategies

### Climate Risk Management

**Allianz RE Catastrophe Database:**
- 125+ million catastrophe data points
- Predictive capabilities for early warnings
- Example: November 2024 flood warning for Valencia, Spain (hours before event)

**Scenario Analysis:**
- NGFS (Network for Greening the Financial System) scenarios
- Portfolio stress testing
- TCFD-aligned disclosures

### PIMCO

**ESG Approach:**
- ESG integration across fixed income strategies
- Engagement with issuers

- Green bond expertise

Note: As a fiduciary, PIMCO follows client mandates rather than setting outcome-oriented ESG targets.

### Sustainable Investment Policies

**Exclusions (Proprietary Investments & P&C Underwriting):**
- Controversial weapons (cluster munitions, landmines)
- Thermal coal (phasing out)
- Oil sands
- Arctic drilling
- Serious human rights violators

**ESG Integration Process:**
1. ESG risk assessment in due diligence
2. Portfolio monitoring and reporting
3. Active engagement with issuers
4. Voting at shareholder meetings
5. Exit for non-responsive ESG laggards
