# Allianz Business Segments

Allianz operates through three core business segments that create a diversified, resilient business model:

## Property-Casualty Insurance

### Segment Overview

Allianz is the world's largest property-casualty insurer, serving both retail and commercial customers across nearly 70 countries.

**2025 Performance:**
- Total Business Volume: €86.7 billion
- Operating Profit: €9.0 billion (record)
- Combined Ratio: 92.2% (excellent profitability)
- Internal Growth: 8.2%

### Retail Business

**Target Customers:** Individuals, families, small businesses (SMEs)

**Key Products:**

| Product | Description | Market Position |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Motor Insurance** | Comprehensive, third-party, usage-based | #1 in most core markets |
| **Home Insurance** | Buildings, contents, all-risk | Market leader |
| **Accident Insurance** | Personal accident, disability | Strong portfolio |
| **Travel Insurance** | Single-trip, annual, assistance | Global leader via Allianz Partners |
| **Pet Insurance** | Veterinary coverage | Growing segment |

**Allianz Direct:**
- European digital insurer platform
- "60-second claim" capability via AI
- 15% YoY revenue growth
- 30-50% cost reduction vs. traditional model
- 90%+ customer satisfaction

**2025 Retail Performance:**
- Internal Growth: 9%
- Combined Ratio: 92.4% (improved 1.8pp)
- Customer satisfaction: Loyalty leader in 80% of entities

### Commercial Business

**Target Customers:** Mid-market, large corporates, multinational enterprises

**Key Divisions:**

#### Allianz Global Corporate & Specialty (AGCS)
- **Focus**: Large corporate and specialty risks
- **Headquarters**: Munich
- **Premiums**: Multi-billion scale
- **Specialties**: Marine, aviation, energy, construction, cyber, financial lines
- **Global Network**: Local presence in 30+ countries

#### Allianz Trade (formerly Euler Hermes)
- **Focus**: Trade credit insurance, surety, debt collection
- **Market Position**: Global leader in credit insurance
- **Services**: Risk assessment, receivables management, bond insurance
- **Digital**: Online credit risk platforms

#### Allianz Partners
- **Focus**: Assistance, travel insurance, automotive services
- **Services**: Roadside assistance, travel protection, concierge services
- **Reach**: 84 countries, 78 assistance centers
- **Customers**: B2B partnerships (automakers, airlines, credit cards)

**2025 Commercial Performance:**
- Internal Growth: 7%
- Combined Ratio: 91.7% (improved 0.5pp)
- Careful management of market cycle

### Underwriting Excellence

**Combined Ratio Components (2025):**

| Component | Ratio | Change |

| Loss Ratio | 68.3% | -1.0pp |

## Life/Health Insurance

### Segment Overview

Allianz ranks among the top 5 global life/health insurers, offering protection, savings, and retirement solutions.

**2025 Performance:**
- PVNBP (Present Value of New Business Premiums): €84.7 billion
- Operating Profit: €5.6 billion
- Value of New Business: €4.8 billion
- New Business Margin: 5.7%
- CSM: €55.7 billion

### Life Insurance

**Product Categories:**

| Category | Products | Target |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **Protection** | Term life, whole life, critical illness | Family protection |
| **Savings** | Endowment, unit-linked, fixed annuities | Wealth accumulation |
| **Retirement** | Annuities, pension products, deferred income | Retirement security |

**Distribution Channels:**
- Exclusive agents (largest channel)
- Brokers and independent advisors
- Bancassurance partnerships
- Direct/online (growing)
- Employee benefits (group)

### Health Insurance

**Product Lines:**
- Private health insurance (Germany: #1 market position)
- Supplemental health (dental, vision, hospital cash)
- International health (expatriates, high-net-worth)
- Long-term care

**Growth Drivers:**
- Aging populations in developed markets
- Rising healthcare costs
- Increased awareness post-pandemic
- Asia-Pacific expansion opportunity

### IFRS 17 Metrics

**Contractual Service Margin (CSM):**
- Opening Balance: €55.6 billion
- Normalized Growth: +5.2%
- FX Impact: Negative
- Closing Balance: €55.7 billion

**New Business Quality:**
- Preferred lines share: 91%
- Capital-efficient products prioritized
- Protection focus over pure savings

### Geographic Strategy

**Key Markets:**
1. **Germany**: Largest market, strong agency force
2. **France**: Bancassurance leader
3. **Italy**: Strong protection focus
4. **United States**: Fixed annuity specialist (Allianz Life)
5. **Asia-Pacific**: Growth engine (China, Southeast Asia)

## Asset Management

### Segment Overview

Allianz is a top-3 global asset manager with €2.5 trillion in total assets under management, including €1.99 trillion third-party assets.

**2025 Performance:**
- Operating Profit: €3.3 billion
- Cost-Income Ratio: 60.7% (ahead of 61% target)
- Third-Party Net Inflows: €139 billion
- Third-Party AuM: €1.990 trillion (record)

### PIMCO

**Profile:**
- **Founded:** 1971, acquired by Allianz 2000
- **Headquarters:** Newport Beach, California
- **AUM:** €1.7+ trillion
- **Specialty:** Fixed income and credit
- **Operating Profit:** €2.59 billion (2025)
- **Margin:** 37.4 bps

**Investment Capabilities:**
- Active bond management
- Alternative credit
- Real assets
- ESG-integrated strategies
- Institutional and retail distribution

**2025 Highlights:**
- Net inflows: €133 billion (9% organic growth)
- Strong performance in fixed income
- CIR: 59.8% (excellent)

**Investment Capabilities:**
