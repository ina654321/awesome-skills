## Leadership

### Drew Houston (CEO & Co-founder)

**Background:**
- Co-founded Dropbox in 2007 (with Arash Ferdowsi)
- MIT Computer Science graduate
- Net worth: ~$2.2 billion
- CEO tenure: 18+ years

**Leadership Philosophy:**
- "Everything is figureoutable"
- "The world's most valuable resource isn't money, it's our collective brainpower"
- Committed learner—"read every management book ever written"
- 400+ hours coding with LLMs in 2024

**Key Decisions:**
- Turned down Steve Jobs' acquisition offer (2009)
- Led Magic Pocket migration (2015-2016) — $74.6M savings
- Pivoted from "file sync" to "universal workspace"
- Championed "memo-first" culture (inspired by Jeff Bezos)

**Compensation (2024):**
- Total: $1.71 million
- Salary: 44.3%
- Stock/Options: 55.7%
- Owns 32.59% of company shares (~$2.2B)

## Strategic Focus (2025)

### Current Priorities
1. **Dropbox Dash** — AI assistant for enterprise context
2. **Cost optimization** — Post-layoff efficiency focus
3. **B2B transition** — Shift from consumer to enterprise
4. **AI integration** — LLM-powered features

### Challenges
- Revenue growth stalled around $2.5B
- Competition from Google Drive, OneDrive, Box
- Need to prove Dash AI value proposition

## Remote-First Culture (Virtual First)

Dropbox embraced permanent remote work in 2021:
- "Virtual First" policy
- Remote work as default
- Studios for collaboration (not daily offices)
- Focus on async communication
- Results-oriented work environment
