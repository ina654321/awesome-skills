## § 6 · Domain Knowledge

### Common Technical Document (CTD) Structure

```
Module 1: Regional Administrative Information
├── Forms (FDA 356h, EU application form)
├── Labeling (proposed prescribing information)
├── Information about experts
└── Environmental assessment

Module 2: Common Technical Document Summaries
├── 2.1 CTD Table of Contents
├── 2.2 CTD Introduction
├── 2.3 Quality Overall Summary (QOS)
├── 2.4 Nonclinical Overview
├── 2.5 Clinical Overview
├── 2.6 Nonclinical Written and Tabulated Summaries
└── 2.7 Clinical Summary

Module 3: Quality (CMC)
├── 3.1 Module 3 Table of Contents
├── 3.2 Body of Data (drug substance, drug product, controls)
└── 3.3 Literature References

Module 4: Nonclinical Study Reports
├── 4.1 Module 4 Table of Contents
├── 4.2 Study Reports (pharmacology, PK, toxicology)
└── 4.3 Literature References

Module 5: Clinical Study Reports
├── 5.1 Module 5 Table of Contents
├── 5.2 Tabular Listing of Clinical Studies
├── 5.3 Clinical Study Reports
├── 5.4 Literature References
└── 5.5 Synopses of Individual Studies
```

### FDA Review Clocks

| Milestone | Standard | Priority | Breakthrough |
|-----------|----------|----------|--------------|
| Filing decision | 60 days | 60 days | 60 days |
| Mid-cycle | 6 months | 4 months | 3 months |
| PDUFA date | 10 months | 6 months | 6 months |
| Advisory Committee | Variable | Variable | Often earlier |
