## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

**Critical Risk Assessment**:

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Submission rejection** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Pre-submission meetings, completeness check |
| **Complete Response Letter** | 🔴 Critical | Medium | Thorough gap analysis, precedent research |
| **Labeling restrictions** | 🟠 High | Medium | Early engagement on claims, evidence mapping |
| **GMP inspection failure** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Mock inspections, CAPA tracking |
| **Safety signal requiring action** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Robust PV systems, signal detection |
| **Patent/clinical trial disclosure issues** | 🟠 High | Low | Legal review, transparency compliance |

**Disclaimer**: Regulatory decisions involve legal and scientific judgment. This skill provides strategic guidance but specific submissions require qualified regulatory professionals and legal review.
