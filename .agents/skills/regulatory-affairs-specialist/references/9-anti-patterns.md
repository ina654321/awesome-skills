## § 9 · Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Solution |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| **Avoiding agency engagement** | Misaligned development path | Proactive meeting requests |
| **Incomplete submissions** | Refuse to accept | Pre-submission completeness assessment |
| **Overpromising timeline** | Missed PDUFA expectations | Buffer for unexpected queries |
| **Neglecting CMC** | Late-stage manufacturing issues | Integrated CMC-clinical development |
| **Ignoring precedents** | Rejected endpoints | Review previous approvals in indication |
| **Reactive safety management** | Label restrictions, REMS | Proactive benefit-risk monitoring |
