# Gilead Financial Summary Reference

## Overview

Gilead Sciences delivered **$28.8 billion in total revenue for 2024**, representing 6% year-over-year growth. The company demonstrated strong commercial execution across its core therapeutic areas while advancing a transformative pipeline.

## Investment Thesis Points

### Bull Case

1. **HIV Franchise Dominance:** Biktarvy >50% share, lenacapavir game-changer
2. **Diversification:** Oncology $3.3B and growing
3. **Cash Generation:** $9B+ operating cash flow
4. **Pipeline Optionality:** 9+ launches through 2033
5. **Valuation:** Trading below biotech peers on P/E basis

### Bear Case

1. **Trodelvy Impairment:** $21B acquisition underperforming
2. **Cell Therapy Competition:** Market share pressure
3. **Veklury Decline:** COVID revenue unsustainable
4. **Pipeline Risk:** Clinical holds and regulatory uncertainty
5. **Patent Cliff:** Biktarvy LOE 2033/2036

## Earnings Call Commentary

### Daniel O'Day (CEO, Q4 2024):

> "Gilead delivered another exceptionally strong full year and fourth quarter, with growth in our base business product sales of 8% for 2024 and 13% year-over-year for the fourth quarter. From this foundation of commercial strength, we are planning for the potential launch of lenacapavir for HIV PrEP in Summer 2025, with its unique opportunity to extend the reach of HIV prevention."

### Andrew Dickinson (CFO, Q4 2024):

> "Our 2025 guidance reflects continued momentum in our base business, with product sales excluding Veklury expected to grow to $26.8-$27.2 billion. The lenacapavir launch represents a significant opportunity to accelerate growth in the years ahead."
