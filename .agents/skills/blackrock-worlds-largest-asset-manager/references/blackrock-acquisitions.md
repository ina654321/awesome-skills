# BlackRock Acquisitions: Strategic Transformation

## Overview

BlackRock executed transformative acquisitions in 2024-2025, fundamentally reshaping its capabilities in private markets and data analytics. These deals represent the most significant strategic moves since the 2009 BGI acquisition that created iShares.

## Global Infrastructure Partners (GIP)

### Transaction Summary

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Announced** | January 2024 |
| **Closed** | October 1, 2024 |
| **Purchase Price** | $12.5 billion ($3B cash + $9B stock) |
| **Strategic Rationale** | Scale infrastructure platform for energy transition |

### About GIP

**Founded:** 2006

**Founding Partners:**
- **Bayo Ogunlesi** (Chairman & CEO) — former Credit Suisse executive
- Team from Credit Suisse's infrastructure group

**Pre-Acquisition Scale:**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| AUM | $70+ billion |
| Infrastructure Companies | 40+ portfolio companies |
| Investors | 100+ institutional clients |
| Geographic Reach | Global (Americas, Europe, Asia, Australia) |

### Portfolio Highlights

| Sector | Key Assets | Investment Thesis |
|--------|------------|-------------------|
| **Aviation** | London Gatwick, Edinburgh Airport | Essential transport infrastructure |
| **Energy** | Freeport LNG, power generation | Energy security & transition |
| **Digital** | Hyperscale data centers | AI/cloud computing demand |
| **Transport** | Port facilities, rail assets | Global trade infrastructure |
| **Utilities** | Water, waste, renewables | Essential services |

### Strategic Fit

**Why GIP?**

1. **Scale:** Instant top-5 infrastructure manager status
2. **Track Record:** 18-year history, strong returns
3. **Talent:** Bayo Ogunlesi and team's expertise retained
4. **Pipeline:** $40B+ in assets under construction
5. **Energy Transition:** Direct play on decarbonization

**Larry Fink on GIP:**
> "I had been thinking about this trend and called an old colleague, Bayo Ogunlesi. Both Bayo and I started our careers in finance at the investment bank First Boston... Now, we plan to join our forces again."

### Post-Acquisition Structure

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Combined Infrastructure AUM** | $100+ billion |
| **Management** | Bayo Ogunlesi continues as Global Head of Infrastructure |
| **Integration** | GIP operations integrated with BlackRock alternatives platform |
| **Retention** | Key investment professionals retained with long-term incentives |

## HPS Investment Partners

### Transaction Summary

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Announced** | December 2024 |
| **Expected Close** | Mid-2025 |
| **Purchase Price** | $12 billion (all stock) |
| **Strategic Rationale** | Build premier private credit franchise |

### About HPS

**Founded:** 2007 (spun out from Honeywell pension management)

**Leadership:**
- **Scott Kapnick** (CEO)
- Team with deep credit expertise

**Pre-Acquisition Scale:**
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| AUM | ~$148 billion |
| Strategy Focus | Private credit, specialty finance |
| Client Base | Institutional investors, pensions |

### Strategy Breakdown

| Strategy | Description | Target Returns |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Senior Direct Lending** | First-lien loans to middle market | 9-11% |
| **Asset-Based Finance** | Loans secured by specific assets | 10-13% |
| **Specialty Finance** | Esoteric, complex credit | 12-15% |
| **Mezzanine/Junior** | Subordinated capital | 13-16% |
| **Opportunistic** | Distressed, special situations | 15-20% |

### Strategic Fit

**Why HPS?**

1. **Market Timing:** Banks retreating from lending creates opportunity
2. **Yield Premium:** Private credit offers 200-400bps over public markets
3. **Floating Rate:** Benefits from higher interest rate environment
4. **Scale:** Creates $220B pro forma private credit platform
5. **Synergies:** Cross-sell with BlackRock's institutional relationships

**Larry Fink on HPS:**
> "We have always sought to position ourselves ahead of our clients' needs. Together with the scale, capabilities, and expertise of the HPS team, BlackRock will deliver clients solutions that seamlessly blend public and private."

### Pro Forma Combined

| Metric | Pre-HPS | Post-HPS (Pro Forma) |
|--------|---------|---------------------|
| Private Credit AUM | ~$70B | ~$220B |
| Market Position | Top 10 | Top 5 |
| Competitive Set | Ares, Blue Owl | Direct peer to Apollo, Blackstone |

## Historical Context: The BGI Acquisition

### 2009: Creating iShares

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Purchase Price** | $13.5 billion |
| **Acquired From** | Barclays |
| **Asset** | Barclays Global Investors (BGI) |
| **Included** | iShares ETF platform |

**Impact:**
- Created world's largest ETF manager overnight
- Established BlackRock as diversified asset manager
- Platform for $5+ trillion in current iShares AUM
- Proved BlackRock's ability to execute transformative M&A

**Analogy:**
The 2024-2025 acquisitions aim to replicate the BGI success in private markets — establishing BlackRock as a top-tier alternatives manager through strategic M&A.

## Financial Impact

### Deal Economics

| Deal | Price | Funding | Expected Synergies |
|------|-------|---------|-------------------|
| GIP | $12.5B | $3B cash, $9B stock | Revenue scale, cross-selling |
| HPS | $12.0B | All stock | Fee growth, margin expansion |
| Preqin | $3.2B | Cash/stock | Data monetization, Aladdin integration |
| **Total** | **~$27.7B** | Mixed | Transformational |

### Stock Consideration Rationale

- **GIP:** 72% stock — aligns GIP principals with BlackRock shareholders
- **HPS:** 100% stock — HPS partners become BlackRock shareholders
- **Benefit:** Conserves cash, aligns incentives, tax efficiency

### Executive Compensation Alignment

| Change | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| Added Apollo, KKR, Blackstone to peer group | Compete for alternatives talent |
| Carried interest participation | Retain GIP/HPS investment teams |
| Long-term equity vesting | Align with integration timeline |
