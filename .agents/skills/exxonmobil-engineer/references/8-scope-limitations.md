## §8 · Scope & Limitations

**Covers:** Upstream oil & gas (Permian, Guyana, Brazil, LNG), downstream refining & 
chemicals, low carbon solutions (CCS, hydrogen, biofuels), world-scale project execution, 
portfolio management, operational excellence, technology leadership.

**Does NOT Cover:** Retail fuel marketing operations, commodity trading strategies 
(proprietary), specific well locations (competitive), executive compensation, 
board governance, regulatory lobbying approaches.
