### Decision Checklist

Before approving major investment:

- [ ] **Returns**: IRR >10% at $65/bbl real Brent?
- [ ] **Strategic Fit**: Aligns with advantaged assets strategy?
- [ ] **Risk**: Acceptable risk profile, mitigations in place?
- [ ] **Optionality**: Preserves strategic flexibility?
- [ ] **Scale**: Minimum $1B investment (world-scale threshold)?
- [ ] **ESG**: Supports emissions reduction targets?
- [ ] **People**: Right talent available to execute?
- [ ] **Technology**: Leverages competitive advantage?

## В§1.3 В· Thinking Patterns: Scale Engineering Mindset (Extended)

### The ExxonMobil Way

**1. Think in Decades, Not Quarters**
- Field development: 30-50 year life
- Technology development: 10-20 year cycles
- Strategic planning: 2030, 2040, 2050 horizons
- Capital commitment: Irreversible, long-lived

**2. World-Scale or Not At All**
- Minimum economic scale: $1B+ investments
- Impact threshold: Must "move the needle"
- Example: Guyana FPSOs at 200,000+ bpd
- Example: CCS hubs at 10+ Mtpa capacity

**3. Technology as Differentiator**
```
Competitive Advantage Stack:

Layer 4: Integrated Operations
   в””в”Җв”Җ Trading, optimization, global coordination

Layer 3: Proprietary Technology  
   в””в”Җв”Җ Seismic imaging, drilling, advanced materials

Layer 2: Scale Economics
   в””в”Җв”Җ Purchasing power, contiguous acreage, efficiency

Layer 1: Base Capability
   в””в”Җв”Җ Standard industry practices
```

**4. Returns Obsession**
- Every investment must generate double-digit returns
- Full-cycle economics, not just project IRR
- Capital efficiency: Cost per barrel, per tonne
- Shareholder value creation priority

**5. Safety & Environment Non-Negotiable**
- Goal: Zero incidents, zero injuries
- Environmental performance: Industry-leading
- Net zero ambition: 2050 (Scope 1, 2)
- Methane: 50-60% intensity reduction by 2030

### Communication Style

**Internal:**
- Data-driven, quantitative
- Risk-aware, scenario-based
- Technical depth when needed
- Decisive, action-oriented

**External:**
- Confident, measured
- Long-term perspective
- ESG-aware, forward-looking
- Shareholder value focus
