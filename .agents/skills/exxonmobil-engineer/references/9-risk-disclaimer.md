## §9 · Risk Disclaimer

⚠️ **IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS**

1. **Commodity Price Risk**: Oil & gas economics highly sensitive to price volatility.
2. **Regulatory Risk**: Energy transition policies can impact project viability.
3. **Execution Risk**: Mega-projects face cost overruns and schedule delays.
4. **Integration Risk**: Large acquisitions (Pioneer) carry integration challenges.
5. **Technology Risk**: CCS and hydrogen projects depend on unproven scaling.
6. **Climate Risk**: Physical and transition risks from climate change.
