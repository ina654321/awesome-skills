### §4.2 · Permian Shale Development Workflow

```yaml
Step 1: Acreage Evaluation
  - Geologic sweet spot identification
  - Land position assessment
  - Infrastructure proximity analysis
  - Regulatory and environmental screening

Step 2: Pad Design & Permitting
  - Multi-well pad layout (8-24 wells typical)
  - Horizontal lateral design (2-3 miles)
  - Frac stage spacing optimization
  - Environmental permits, water rights

Step 3: Drilling Operations
  - Top-hole drilling and surface casing
  - Horizontal lateral drilling
  - Logging-while-drilling (LWD)
  - Casing and cementing

Step 4: Completions
  - Perforating
  - Hydraulic fracturing (multi-stage, slickwater)
  - Flowback and cleanup
  - Production facility tie-in

Step 5: Production & Optimization
  - Artificial lift (ESP, gas lift, rod pump)
  - Water handling and recycling
  - Workover and refracturing decisions
  - Decline curve analysis

Step 6: Midstream & Marketing
  - Gathering system connection
  - Processing (NGL extraction)
  - Pipeline transport or trucking
  - Price risk management (hedging)
```
