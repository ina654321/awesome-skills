# VelociSuite® Technology Platforms

## Overview

**VelociSuite®** is Regeneron's proprietary collection of technologies that accelerate, improve, and disrupt traditional drug discovery and development. The platforms were developed over three decades, primarily under the scientific leadership of Dr. George Yancopoulos.

> "The capabilities of the VelociSuite of Technologies allow Regeneron scientists to efficiently evaluate hundreds of new gene targets every year."

## Core Technologies

### 1. VelociGene® — Genetic Engineering Platform

**Purpose:** Rapid manipulation of mouse DNA to create disease models and validate targets

**Capabilities:**
- High-throughput gene targeting
- Mega-base-scale genetic humanization
- Creation of knock-out, knock-in, and conditional alleles
- Rapid model generation (months vs. years)

**Key Innovation:**
Can humanize over 6 megabases of the mouse immune genome—**100x larger** than any previous genetic humanization reported.

**Applications:**
- Target validation
- Disease model creation
- Mechanism of action studies
- Safety assessment

**Publication:** First VelociGene paper published 2003

---

### 2. VelocImmune® — Human Antibody Platform

**Purpose:** Generate fully human monoclonal antibodies using genetically humanized mice

**The Science:**
- Proprietary genetically-engineered mouse platform
- Genetically-humanized immune system
- Mice produce optimized, fully human antibodies
- Immune system functions as efficiently as normal mouse

**Key Advantage:**
Natural in vivo affinity maturation produces high-quality antibodies with optimal characteristics.

**Products Enabled:**
| Drug | Target | Indication |
|------|--------|------------|
| Dupixent® | IL-4Rα | Atopic dermatitis, asthma, etc. |
| Libtayo® | PD-1 | Skin cancer |
| Praluent® | PCSK9 | High cholesterol |
| Kevzara® | IL-6R | Rheumatoid arthritis |
| Evkeeza® | ANGPTL3 | HoFH |
| Veopoz® | C5a | CHAPLE disease |
| Inmazeb® | Ebola | Ebola infection |
| REGEN-COV® | SARS-CoV-2 | COVID-19 |

**Statistics:**
- Dr. Yancopoulos and team have created "a substantial proportion of all original, FDA-approved fully human monoclonal antibodies"
- 2-3 new antibodies enter clinical trials each year

---

### 3. VelociMab® — Antibody Screening Platform

**Purpose:** Rapid identification and generation of manufacturing cell lines

**Process:**
1. Extract antibodies from VelocImmune mice
2. High-throughput screening for desirable characteristics
3. Generate manufacturing lines
4. Produce clinical/commercial grade material

**Advantages:**
- Speed: Reduced timeline from discovery to IND
- Quality: Selection of optimal biophysical properties
- Yield: High-expressing cell lines

---

### 4. Veloci-Bi® — Bispecific Antibody Platform

**Purpose:** Create dual-targeting antibodies (binds two different targets simultaneously)

**Technology:**
- Novel bispecific formats
- Maintains natural antibody structure
- Optimized for manufacturing and stability

**Applications:**
- Immuno-oncology (e.g., CD3xCD20)
- Dual cytokine blockade
- Cell engagers

**Pipeline Examples:**
- Odronextamab (CD20xCD3) for lymphoma
- Linvoseltamab (BCMAxCD3) for multiple myeloma
- Fianlimab (LAG-3) + Libtayo combinations

---

### 5. VelociT® — T Cell Receptor Platform

**Purpose:** T cell receptor (TCR) discovery and engineering

**Applications:**
- Cell therapy (CAR-T, TCR-T)
- Targeting intracellular antigens
- Solid tumor applications

---

### 6. Trap Technology — Soluble Receptors

**Purpose:** Create "decoy receptors" that neutralize signaling proteins

**Mechanism:**
Fuses two distinct fully human receptor components with immunoglobulin constant region, creating high-affinity trap molecules.

**Products:**
| Drug | Target | Indication |
|------|--------|------------|
| Eylea® | VEGF-A, VEGF-B, PlGF | Wet AMD, DME, DR, RVO |
| Eylea HD® | VEGF (higher dose) | Same, extended dosing |
| Zaltrap® | VEGF | Metastatic colorectal cancer |
| Arcalyst® | IL-1 | CAPS, DIRA |

**Eylea Differentiation:**
- Binds VEGF-A, VEGF-B, and PlGF (broader than competitors)
- Higher affinity than native receptor
- Less frequent dosing enabled

---

### 7. VelociVax™ — mRNA Platform

**Purpose:** mRNA-based vaccine and immunotherapy development

**Technology:**
- Flexible mRNA backbone for protein design
- Rapid manufacturing capability
- Combination testing agility

**Applications:**
- Infectious disease vaccines
- Cancer vaccines (personalized neoantigen)
- Immune-modulating therapies

**Status:** Latest addition to VelociSuite platform

---

### 8. VelociMouse® — Direct Model Generation

**Purpose:** Generate genetically modified mice without ES cells

**Advantage:**
- Faster timeline
- Direct-to-mouse genetic modification
- Reduced technical complexity
