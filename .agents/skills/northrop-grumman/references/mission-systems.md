# Mission Systems

## Overview

Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is a leader in advanced mission solutions and multifunction systems, primarily serving the U.S. defense and intelligence communities. Headquartered in Linthicum, Maryland.

### Core Competencies
- Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Intelligence, Surveillance, Reconnaissance (C4ISR)
- Radar systems (airborne, ground, naval)
- Electronic warfare (EW)
- Cyber solutions
- Advanced communications and networks
- Intelligence processing

## Radar Systems

### AN/TPS-80 Ground/Air Task Oriented Radar (G/ATOR)

**Description:** Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA) radar for U.S. Marine Corps

**Capabilities:**
- **Multi-Role:** Air surveillance, counter-fire target acquisition, air traffic control
- **AESA Technology:** Gallium Nitride (GaN) amplifiers
- **Mobility:** Designed for rapid deployment
- **Software-Defined:** Upgradable via software

**Status:** Full-rate production, fielded with Marine Corps

### Scalable Agile Beam Radar (SABR)

**Description:** AESA radar upgrade for F-16 fighters

**Features:**
- Drop-in replacement for legacy F-16 radars
- Multi-target tracking
- High-resolution synthetic aperture radar (SAR)
- Growth path for F-16 operators

### F-35 Fire Control Radar (AN/APG-81)

**Features:**
- AESA technology
- Interleaved air-to-air and air-to-ground modes
- Electronic attack capability
- Low probability of intercept

**Northrop Role:** Design and production for F-35 program

## Electronic Warfare

### AN/APR-39 Radar Warning Receiver Family

**AN/APR-39D(V)2**
- **Description:** Digital radar warning receiver and EW management system
- **Coverage:** 360° RF threat detection
- **Features:** 
  - Digital receiver technology
  - Automatic countermeasures initiation
  - Growth path to RF countermeasures
- **Platforms:** Rotary-wing and fixed-wing aircraft
- **Contract:** $124.7M production award (2025)

**AN/APR-39E(V)2**
- **Description:** Next-generation development
- **Enhancements:** Millimeter wave detection, extended range
- **Architecture:** Digital receiver exciter (DRE)

### F-35 Distributed Aperture System (DAS)

**Description:** Six IR sensors providing 360° spherical coverage

**Functions:**
- Missile warning
- Infrared search and track
- Navigation (forward-looking infrared)
- Situational awareness
- Battle damage assessment

**Significance:** First-ever 360° IR system on a tactical aircraft

## C4ISR Systems

### Integrated Air and Missile Defense Battle Command System (IBCS)

**Description:** Revolutionary command and control system for air and missile defense

**Innovation:**
- **Network-Centric:** Integrates disparate sensors and shooters
- **Plug-and-Fight:** Any sensor, best shooter
- **Open Architecture:** Rapid integration of new capabilities

**Deployments:**
- U.S. Army
- Poland (WISŁA program) - $900M contract 2024

### Battlefield Airborne Communications Node (BACN)

**Description:** Airborne communications relay and gateway

**Function:** Translates between incompatible communications systems

**Platforms:** E-11A (Bombardier Global Express), EQ-4B (Global Hawk)

### Enterprise Airborne Gateway Network (EAGN)

**Description:** Communications gateway enabling coalition interoperability
