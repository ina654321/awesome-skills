## Investment Thesis Summary

### Bull Case
- Skyrizi + Rinvoq growth offsetting Humira decline
- Strong pipeline in neuroscience (tavapadon, Vyalev)
- Dividend aristocrat with 3%+ yield
- Aesthetics recovery expected
- M&A optionality with strong balance sheet

### Bear Case
- High debt load ($66.8B)
- Cerevel impairment raises pipeline risk concerns
- Imbruvica competition intensifying
- Aesthetics softness in consumer environment
- Emraclidine failure demonstrates execution risk
