## Corporate History

### Origins: Abbott Laboratories Spin-Off (2013)

AbbVie was created on January 1, 2013, when Abbott Laboratories separated its research-based proprietary pharmaceuticals business into a new, independent company. The name "AbbVie" derives from:
- **Abb**ott
- **Vie** (from Latin "vita," meaning life)

**Strategic Rationale for Spin-Off:**
- Enable focused investment in pharmaceutical R&D
- Create distinct investment profiles for medical devices/diagnostics (Abbott) vs. pharmaceuticals (AbbVie)
- Allow tailored capital allocation strategies
- Improve operational efficiency
