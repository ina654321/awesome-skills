## Competitive SWOT Analysis

### AbbVie Strengths
- Market-leading immunology franchise
- Strong commercial execution track record
- Diversified portfolio post-Allergan
- Dividend aristocrat status
- Robust cash flow generation

### AbbVie Weaknesses
- High debt load from M&A
- Humira biosimilar erosion
- Imbruvica competitive pressure
- Emraclidine pipeline setback

### Opportunities
- Skyrizi + Rinvoq growth to $31B+
- ADC expansion in oncology
- Parkinson's disease franchise build
- Obesity market entry

### Threats
- Intensifying immunology competition
- Pricing pressure globally
- Regulatory scrutiny of JAK class
- Economic headwinds (aesthetics)
