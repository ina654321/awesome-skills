## GoGreen Plus

### What is GoGreen Plus?

GoGreen Plus is DHL's **carbon insetting** solution that enables customers to reduce CO2 emissions directly within their supply chain through **Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)**.

**Insetting vs. Offsetting:**
| Approach | Mechanism | Impact |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| **Offsetting** | Invest in external carbon projects | Indirect compensation |
| **Insetting** | Reduce emissions within own value chain | Direct reduction |

### How GoGreen Plus Works

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  1. CUSTOMER COMMITS to GoGreen Plus                        │
│     └─ Selects CO2 reduction target (30%, 50%, 70%, etc.)   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  2. DHL INVESTS in SAF procurement                          │
│     └─ Partnerships with Neste, bp, World Energy, Cosmo     │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  3. SAF IS BLENDED into aviation fuel supply                │
│     └─ Book & Claim methodology enables global offering     │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  4. CO2 REDUCTION is attributed to customer shipments       │
│     └─ Independent SGS verification annually                │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  5. CUSTOMER RECEIVES certificate and reporting             │
│     └─ Scope 3 emissions reduction documentation            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

**What is SAF?**
- Produced from renewable feedstocks (used cooking oil, agricultural waste, etc.)
- Drop-in fuel compatible with existing aircraft
- Lifecycle CO2 reduction: up to **80%** vs. conventional jet fuel

**DHL SAF Partnerships:**
- **Neste** (Finland) - renewable diesel and SAF
- **bp** - sustainable aviation fuel supply
- **World Energy** - low-carbon fuel solutions
- **Cosmo Oil Marketing** (Japan) - 7.2M liters contracted

## Implementing Sustainability

### Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1: Baseline Assessment**
- Measure current logistics carbon footprint
- Identify high-emission segments
- Set reduction targets

**Step 2: Mode Optimization**
- Shift air to ocean where feasible
- Combine shipments for efficiency
- Optimize routing

**Step 3: Activate GoGreen Plus**
- Start with 30% default
- Scale up based on goals
- Communicate to stakeholders

**Step 4: Operational Changes**
- Sustainable packaging
- Consolidation strategies
- EV last-mile where available

**Step 5: Report & Certify**
- Annual SGS certificates
- CSRD compliance documentation
- Marketing sustainability claims
