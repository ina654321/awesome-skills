## Harmonized System (HS) Codes

### What are HS Codes?
- International standardized product classification
- 6-digit harmonized globally
- Additional digits vary by country
- Determines duty rates and regulatory requirements

### Structure
```
HS 61.09.10.00.11 (US example)
│   │   │   │   │
│   │   │   │   └── Statistical suffix
│   │   │   └────── Subheading
│   │   └────────── Heading
│   └────────────── Chapter
└────────────────── Section
```

### Classification Principles
1. **General Rules of Interpretation (GRI)** - 6 rules for classification
2. **Section and Chapter Notes** - Legal guidance
3. **Explanatory Notes** - Official interpretation
4. **Rulings** - Binding classification decisions

### Common Classification Issues
- **Composite goods** (GRI 3)
- **Unfinished vs. finished goods**
- **Parts vs. complete articles**
- **Mixtures and sets**
- **Functional units**

### DHL Classification Services
- HS code determination
- Binding ruling assistance
- Product database management
- Classification training

## Incoterms 2020

### Overview
International Commercial Terms define responsibilities between buyers and sellers in international trade.

### Incoterms by Mode

#### Any Mode of Transport
| Term | Risk Transfer | Transport Arranged By | Insurance |
|------|---------------|----------------------|-----------|
| **EXW** | Seller's premises | Buyer | Buyer |
| **FCA** | Carrier/named place | Buyer/Seller* | Buyer |
| **CPT** | Carrier/handover | Seller | Buyer |
| **CIP** | Carrier/handover | Seller | Seller |
| **DAP** | Named destination | Seller | Seller |
| **DPU** | Named place unloaded | Seller | Seller |
| **DDP** | Named destination delivered | Seller | Seller |

*FCA: Seller arranges to carrier, buyer arranges main carriage*

#### Sea and Inland Waterway Only
| Term | Risk Transfer | Transport Arranged By |
|------|---------------|----------------------|
| **FAS** | Alongside ship | Buyer |
| **FOB** | On board vessel | Buyer |
| **CFR** | On board vessel | Seller |
| **CIF** | On board vessel | Seller |

### DHL Recommended Terms
- **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid):** DHL handles everything to customer's door
- **DAP (Delivered at Place):** DHL delivers, customer pays duties
- **EXW (Ex Works):** Customer handles all from seller's location

### Common Mistakes
- Using FOB for containerized cargo (use FCA)
- Confusing risk transfer and cost allocation
- Omitting named place
- Mixing Incoterms with payment terms

## Free Trade Agreements (FTAs)

### Major FTAs

| Agreement | Countries | Key Benefits |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| **USMCA** | US-Mexico-Canada | Duty elimination, simplified rules |
| **EU FTAs** | EU-Various | Preferential access to 70+ countries |
| **RCEP** | Asia-Pacific 15 | Largest FTA by GDP |
| **CPTPP** | 11 Pacific nations | Comprehensive trade liberalization |
| **UK-EU TCA** | UK-EU post-Brexit | Duty-free trade with rules |

### Rules of Origin
Determine if goods qualify for FTA preferences:

1. **Wholly Obtained** - Natural resources, grown products
2. **Substantial Transformation** - Change in tariff classification
3. **Regional Value Content** - Percentage of originating materials
4. **Specific Process Rules** - Industry-specific requirements

### FTA Utilization Services
- Origin determination analysis
- Certificate of Origin issuance
- Supplier declaration management
- Binding origin rulings
- FTA compliance audits
