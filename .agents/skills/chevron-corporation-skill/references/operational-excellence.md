# Chevron Operational Excellence Reference

## Operational Excellence Management System (OEM)

Chevron's OEM provides a comprehensive framework for safe, reliable, and efficient operations across all business segments.

### Core Elements

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Leadership** | Visible commitment, accountability, culture setting |
| **Risk Management** | Hazard identification, risk assessment, mitigation |
| **Operational Processes** | Standardized procedures, work management, change control |
| **Capability** | Training, competency, workforce development |
| **Learning** | Incident investigation, best practice sharing, continuous improvement |

## Safety Performance

### Safety Priorities
1. **Process Safety**: Prevent major incidents (fires, explosions, releases)
2. **Personal Safety**: Protect workers from injuries
3. **Transportation Safety**: Safe movement of people and products
4. **Product Safety**: Ensure safe handling of products

### Key Metrics

| Metric | 2023 | 2024 | Industry Comparison |
|--------|------|------|---------------------|
| TRIR (Total Recordable Incident Rate) | Industry competitive | Improving | Top quartile |
| Process Safety Events | Declining trend | Ongoing focus | Leading |
| Spill Volume | Minimal | Continued reduction | Best in class |

## Environmental Management

### Environmental Stewardship

#### Water Management
- **Recycling**: Maximize produced water recycling
- **Freshwater reduction**: 60% reduction at El Segundo refinery
- **Treatment**: Advanced wastewater treatment

#### Air Quality
- **Flare minimization**: Reduce routine flaring
- **VOC controls**: Vapor recovery, leak detection
- **Emissions monitoring**: Continuous monitoring systems

#### Waste Management
- **Reduce**: Minimize waste generation
- **Reuse**: Find beneficial uses for byproducts
- **Recycle**: Maximize material recycling

### Biodiversity
- **Sensitive areas**: Protect areas of high conservation value
- **Offsets**: Mitigation for unavoidable impacts
- **Marine protection**: Especially in Australia (Gorgon)
