## Operating Segments

ConocoPhillips manages operations through six geographic segments:

### 1. Lower 48

**Overview:** The company's largest production segment, focused on unconventional shale resources.

| Basin | Net Acreage | 2024 Production | Key Characteristics |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Permian** | 1.5M+ acres | ~900 MBOED | Delaware focus, 3-mile laterals |
| **Eagle Ford** | ~600K acres | ~370 MBOED | <$25/bbl cost of supply |
| **Bakken** | ~600K acres | ~198 MBOED | Refrac opportunities |
| **Midland** | ~200K acres | ~194 MBOED | Concho legacy position |

**Development Strategy:**
- **Factory-style execution:** Consistent rig counts, pad drilling
- **Longer laterals:** 2-mile standard, 3-mile pilots
- **Simul-frac:** Multi-well simultaneous completion
- **Digital integration:** Real-time DAS/DTS monitoring

**Marathon Integration Impact:**
- Added significant Bakken and Eagle Ford inventory
- $500M capital reduction through optimized programs
- Consolidated operations requiring fewer rigs/frac crews

### 2. Alaska

**Overview:** Legacy conventional production on the North Slope with major growth project.

| Asset | Working Interest | Production | Notes |
|-------|-----------------|------------|-------|
| **Kuparuk River** | ~95% | ~150 MBOED | Largest US oil field by production |
| **Prudhoe Bay** | ~36% | ~150 MBOED | World-class supergiant field |
| **Alpine** | 100% | ~50 MBOED | Satellite development |
| **Nuna** | 100% | New (2024) | Pad-based satellite |
| **Willow** | 100% | 2029 startup | $9B major project |

**Willow Project Detail:**
- **Location:** National Petroleum Reserve-Alaska (NPR-A)
- **Investment:** ~$9 billion
- **Peak Production:** 180,000 BOE/day
- **First Oil:** 2029
- **Field Life:** 30+ years
- **Permits:** All federal approvals secured 2024
- **Strategic Value:** Low-decline, long-life barrels diversify shale portfolio

### 3. Canada

**Overview:** Oil sands and unconventional positions.

| Asset | Working Interest | Production | Type |
|-------|-----------------|------------|------|
| **Surmont** | 100% | ~150 MBOED | SAGD oil sands |
| **Montney** | Various | Growing | Unconventional gas/liquids |

**Surmont SAGD Operations:**
- Steam-Assisted Gravity Drainage (SAGD) thermal production
- Full ownership since 2023 acquisition from Cenovus
- Pad-based development with high uptime
- Low decline, long-life production

### 4. Europe, Middle East & North Africa

**Norway Operations:**
- **Ekofisk:** Legacy North Sea field, extended-reach drilling
- **Eldfisk North:** Started production 2024 (ahead of schedule)
- **Johan Sverdrup:** Non-operated interest

**Qatar LNG:**

- **North Field South (NFS):** Similar interest, later startup
- **Strategic Value:** Oil-linked LNG pricing diversification

**Libya:**

### 5. Asia Pacific

**China:**

**Malaysia:**

## Technology & Innovation

### Unconventional Technologies

**Drilling Optimization:**
- **Extended-reach laterals:** 3-mile pilots showing 40% cost reduction vs. 1-mile
- **Pad drilling:** Up to 24+ wells from single surface location
- **Walking rigs:** Minimize rig move time

**Completion Technologies:**
- **Simul-frac:** Two wells fracked simultaneously
- **Proppant optimization:** Local sand sourcing where feasible
- **Refracturing:** Restimulating parent wells for incremental production

**Digital Oilfield:**
- **DAS/DTS:** Distributed acoustic/temperature sensing
- **Digital twins:** Virtual field models for optimization
- **Predictive analytics:** Well performance forecasting

### Conventional Technologies

**Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR):**
- **Waterflood:** Secondary recovery in Alaska, Norway
- **Gas injection:** Miscible/immiscible projects
- **Thermal:** SAGD in Canada

**Extended-Reach Drilling:**
- Alaska records for longest lateral reach
- Reduced surface footprint via directional drilling
- Access to offshore resources from land bases
