# Zone Profiles

## Overview: 2025 Organizational Structure

Effective January 1, 2025, Nestlé reorganized into **three geographic zones** and **three global businesses**:

| Structure Type | Units |
|---------------|-------|
| **Geographic Zones** | Zone AMS, Zone AOA, Zone EUR |
| **Global Businesses** | Nespresso, Nestlé Health Science, Nestlé Waters & Premium Beverages |

---

## ZONE AMERICAS (AMS)

### Profile

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Formation** | January 1, 2025 (NA + LATAM merger) |
| **CEO** | Steve Presley |
| **Headquarters** | Vevey, Switzerland (CEO relocated) |
| **% of Group Sales** | ~41% |
| **Key Markets** | USA, Brazil, Mexico, Canada |

### Historical Context

| Period | Structure |
|--------|-----------|
| 2022-2024 | Zone North America (USA + Canada) |
| 2022-2024 | Zone Latin America (LatAm + Caribbean) |
| 2025+ | Combined Zone AMS |

### Market Characteristics

| Aspect | North America | Latin America |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| **Market Maturity** | Mature | Mixed (Brazil/Mexico growing) |
| **Consumer Trends** | Premiumization, health, convenience | Affordability, urbanization |
| **Key Channels** | Walmart, Amazon, club stores | Traditional trade, modern retail |
| **Competitive Landscape** | Intense private label | Local players strong |

### Category Mix (AMS)

| Category | Key Brands | Performance |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Frozen Foods** | Stouffer's, Digiorno, Lean Cuisine | Challenged |
| **Coffee** | Nescafé, Starbucks, Nespresso | Growing |
| **PetCare** | Purina (all tiers) | Strong |
| **Confectionery** | KitKat (licensed in US) | Selective |
| **Creamers** | Coffee-mate | Declining |
| **Infant** | Gerber | Stable |

### Strategic Priorities

1. **Coffee Growth**
   - Nespresso Vertuo US expansion
   - Starbucks partnership leverage
   - RTD coffee innovation

2. **PetCare Premiumization**
   - ProPlan expansion
   - Veterinary channel growth
   - Premiumization of mass brands

3. **Frozen Turnaround**
   - Innovation pipeline
   - Value equation optimization
   - Portfolio reshaping

4. **E-commerce**
   - Amazon partnership
   - Direct-to-consumer (Nespresso, PetCare)
   - Omnichannel integration

### Key Financials (Pre-merger, 2024)

| Zone | Sales (CHF) | Organic Growth |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Zone North America | ~CHF 25B | Slight decrease |
| Zone Latin America | ~CHF 12B | Positive |

   - RTD coffee expansion

   - Nido fortification

   - Shopee, Laz partnerships

### China Integration (2025)

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Previous** | Standalone Zone Greater China |
| **New** | Part of Zone AOA |
| **Leadership** | David Zhang remains Chairman/CEO Greater China |
| **Rationale** | Simplified structure, speed |

**China Performance Context:**
- Soft consumer demand in 2024
- Consumer hesitancy toward global brands
- Focus on innovation and local relevance

## ZONE EUROPE (EUR)

### Profile

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **CEO** | Guillaume Le Cunff |
| **% of Group Sales** | ~23% |
| **Coverage** | Western, Central, Eastern Europe |
| **HQ** | Vevey (retained from EMENA restructure) |

### Market Characteristics

| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Market Maturity** | Highly mature |
| **Consumer Trends** | Sustainability, organic, premium |
| **Retail Landscape** | Discounter dominance (Aldi, Lidl) |
| **Regulatory** | Strict (sugar taxes, labeling, packaging) |
| **Sustainability** | Highest consumer expectations |

### Category Mix (EUR)

| Category | Key Brands | Dynamics |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Coffee** | Nescafé, Nespresso, Starbucks | Premium focus |
| **PetCare** | Purina (all tiers) | Premiumization |
| **Water** | Perrier, S.Pellegrino | Premium out-of-home |
| **Culinary** | Maggi, Buitoni | Innovation-driven |
| **Confectionery** | KitKat, Smarties | Premium/seasonal |
| **Dairy** | Various | Declining volume |

### Strategic Priorities

1. **Sustainability Leadership**
   - Regenerative agriculture showcase
   - Packaging innovation
   - Carbon-neutral products

2. **Premiumization**
   - Nespresso expansion
   - Perrier/S.Pellegrino growth
   - PetCare premium tiers

3. **Innovation Hub**
   - R&D center (Vevey, Lausanne)
   - Plant-based testing
   - Sustainability pilots

4. **Discounter Optimization**
   - Value equation refinement
   - Private label defense
   - Pack size optimization
