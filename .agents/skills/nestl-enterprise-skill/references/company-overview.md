## Business Model

### Creating Shared Value (CSV)

Nestlé's approach to business integrates three pillars:

1. **Individuals & Families**: Nutrition, health, wellness
2. **Communities**: Rural development, responsible sourcing
3. **Planet**: Climate, water, biodiversity, packaging

## Recent Strategic Initiatives

### 2024-2025 Priorities

1. **Growth Acceleration**
   - Increase marketing investment (+40 bps in 2024)
   - Focus on winning brands and platforms
   - Innovation "big bets" scaling

2. **Cost Optimization**
   - CHF 2.5 billion three-year savings program
   - CHF 300M+ secured for 2025
   - Operational efficiency improvements

3. **Portfolio Transformation**
   - Divesting non-strategic brands
   - Focus on high-growth, high-margin categories
   - Premiumization across portfolio

4. **Organizational Simplification**
   - New zone structure (5→3 zones)
   - Leaner Executive Board
   - Faster decision-making
