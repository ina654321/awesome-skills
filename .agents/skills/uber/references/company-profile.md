# Uber Company Profile

## Overview

**Uber Technologies, Inc.** (NYSE: UBER) is an American multinational transportation technology company offering services including ride-hailing, food delivery (Uber Eats), freight transportation (Uber Freight), and logistics.

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Founded** | March 2009 (as UberCab) |
| **Founders** | Travis Kalanick, Garrett Camp |
| **Headquarters** | San Francisco, California, USA |
| **CEO** | Dara Khosrowshahi (since August 2017) |
| **IPO Date** | May 10, 2019 (NYSE: UBER) |
| **IPO Price** | $45 per share |
| **IPO Valuation** | $75.5 billion |
| **Current Market Cap** | ~$152 billion (March 2026) |

---

## Leadership Transition

### Travis Kalanick Era (2009-2017)
- Aggressive growth-at-all-costs strategy
- Disrupted traditional taxi industries globally
- Faced numerous controversies and regulatory battles
- Resigned as CEO in June 2017

### Dara Khosrowshahi Era (2017-Present)
- Former CEO of Expedia
- Transformed company culture and governance
- Led company to profitability (2023 first profitable year)
- IPO in 2019
- Strategic focus on autonomous vehicles and AI
