## Masterpiece Policy Structure

### Coverage Sections

#### 1. Deluxe House Coverage (Dwelling)

**Key Features**:
- **Extended Replacement Cost**: Up to 200% of dwelling limit if reconstruction costs exceed coverage
- **Cash Settlement Option**: Choice to receive cash instead of rebuilding
- **Code Upgrade Coverage**: Pays to bring damaged areas up to current building codes
- **Your Choice of Craftsmen**: Policyholder selects contractors (not insurer-mandated)

**Coverage Includes**:
- Primary residence (all types of homes including historic, custom, coastal)
- Second homes and vacation properties
- Other structures (detached garages, guest houses, pools, etc.)
- landscaping and outdoor features
- Building materials in transit or storage

**Special Features**:
- No coinsurance penalties
- Agreed value approach (no disputes over valuation)
- Worldwide coverage extension available

#### 2. Deluxe Contents Coverage

**Key Features**:
- **Replacement Cost**: Full cost to replace items without depreciation deduction
- **Cash Settlement**: Option for cash instead of replacement
- **Worldwide All-Risk Coverage**: Contents covered anywhere in the world
- **No Sub-limits**: Unlike standard policies, minimal restrictions on categories

**Coverage Includes**:
- Furniture and furnishings
- Electronics and appliances
- Clothing and personal items
- Sporting equipment
- Books and collectibles
- Items in transit or storage

**Special Extensions**:
- Fine art and collectibles (scheduled separately)
- Wine collections
- Musical instruments
- Home office equipment

#### 3. Valuable Articles Coverage

**Agreed Value Approach**:
- Items appraised and valued upfront
- No depreciation disputes at claim time
- Guaranteed payout for total loss

**Categories Covered**:

| Category | Coverage Features |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Jewelry** | Scheduled items, mysterious disappearance covered |
| **Fine Art** | Agreed value, pairs and sets clause |
| **Antiques** | Full replacement or agreed value |
| **Watches** | Worldwide coverage, automatic new acquisition coverage |
| **Furs** | Seasonal storage coverage |
| **Silverware** | Scheduled or blanket coverage |
| **Wine Collections** | Temperature-controlled storage coverage |
| **Rare Books/Manuscripts** | Specialized valuation and coverage |

**Newly Acquired Items**:
- Automatic 90-day coverage for new acquisitions
- Up to 25% of existing total scheduled amount
- Must report and schedule within 90 days

**Pairs and Sets Clause**:
If one item of a pair or set is lost/damaged:
- Option 1: Chubb pays to match the lost/damaged item
- Option 2: Policyholder surrenders remaining item(s), Chubb pays full replacement cost of entire pair/set

#### 4. Family Protection Coverage

**Identity Theft Protection**:
- Credit monitoring services
- Identity restoration services
- Legal fee reimbursement
- Lost wage coverage during resolution

**Cyber Protection**:
- Online fraud coverage
- Cyber extortion coverage
- Data restoration costs

**Home Computer Coverage**:
- Hardware and software
- Data recovery costs
- Business use extension available

#### 5. Personal Liability Coverage

**Coverage Scope**:
- Personal injury liability
- Property damage liability
- Libel, slander, defamation
- False arrest, detention, imprisonment
- Malicious prosecution
- Shock/mental anguish
- Rental property liability (scheduled locations)

**Excess Liability (Umbrella)**:
- Available up to $100M+
- Worldwide coverage
- Defense costs outside limits
- Uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage available

**Additional Coverages**:
- Directors and Officers liability for non-profit board service
- Employment practices liability for domestic employees
- Professional liability for certain occupations

## Coverage Comparison: Masterpiece vs Standard Homeowners

| Feature | Masterpiece | Standard Homeowners |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| Replacement Cost | Up to 200% of limit | 100% of limit only |
| Coinsurance Penalty | None | Yes (typically 80%) |
| Cash Settlement | Always available | Often limited/restricted |
| Contractor Choice | Policyholder choice | Insurer-preferred vendors |

### Rating Variables

**Property Characteristics**:
- Replacement cost (dwelling value)
- Construction type (frame, masonry, concrete)

- Square footage
- Unique architectural features
- Security systems
- Fire protection (hydrants, fire station proximity)

- Weather patterns

**Coverage Selections**:

- Liability limits
- Additional living expense limits
