## Grid Solutions

### High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC)

HVDC systems enable efficient long-distance transmission of bulk power and integration of remote renewable resources.

**Technology Types:**

1. **LCC-HVDC (Line-Commutated Converter)**
   - Traditional bulk power transmission
   - Higher capacity, lower cost per MW
   - Requires strong AC grid

2. **VSC-HVDC (Voltage-Source Converter)**
   - Modular Multilevel Converter (MMC) technology
   - Black start capability
   - Connects to weak grids
   - Enables multi-terminal systems

**Capabilities:**
- Up to 1,100 kV UHVDC
- 10,000+ MW transmission capacity
- 2,000+ km transmission distance
- Bipole and monopole configurations

**Notable Projects:**

| Project | Location | Capacity | Distance | Year |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|------|
| Rio Madeira | Brazil | 3,150 MW | 2,375 km | 2014 |
| Nelson River | Canada | 2,000 MW | 900 km | 1970s-2010s |
| GCCIA | Gulf States | 600 MW | 950 km | 2009 |
| EGL1 | UK | 2,000 MW | Cross-border | 2025+ |
| SunZia | USA | 3,000 MW | 520 miles | 2026 |

**Applications:**
- Remote renewable integration (offshore wind, desert solar)
- Cross-border interconnections
- Urban power feed (underground/submarine)
- Grid stabilization

### Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS)

FACTS enhance controllability and power transfer capability of AC networks.

**Products:**

1. **Static Compensators (STATCOM)**
   - Reactive power support
   - Voltage regulation
   - Grid stability

2. **Series Compensation**
   - Series Capacitors (SC)
   - Thyristor-Controlled Series Capacitors (TCSC)
   - Increase line power transfer

3. **Static VAR Compensators (SVC)**
   - Dynamic reactive power
   - Flicker mitigation
   - Industrial applications

**Grid-Forming STATCOM:**
- Virtual synchronous machine capability
- Essential for inverter-based resource integration
- Supports weak grids

- Synchronous and asynchronous applications
