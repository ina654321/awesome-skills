# GE Vernova Wind Energy Solutions

## Overview

GE Vernova's Wind segment provides onshore and offshore wind turbines, along with blade manufacturing through LM Wind Power. The business has faced significant challenges in offshore wind but is executing a turnaround focused on profitability and execution discipline.

**Current Status:**
- Onshore Wind: Profitable (high single-digit EBITDA margin in 2024)
- Offshore Wind: Restructuring, not accepting new orders
- LM Wind Power: World's largest independent blade manufacturer

### Recent Challenges

**Quality Issues (2023-2024):**
- Blade failures at Vineyard Wind (Massachusetts)
- Blade issues at UK offshore projects
- Root cause: Manufacturing quality control at Quebec blade facility
- Financial impact: Hundreds of millions in losses

**Response:**
- Suspended new offshore orders
- Enhanced quality assurance protocols
- Third-party blade inspections
- Manufacturing process improvements
