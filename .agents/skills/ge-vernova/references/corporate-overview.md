# GE Vernova Corporate Overview

## Company Profile

GE Vernova is a purpose-built global energy company dedicated to electrifying and decarbonizing the world. Spun off from General Electric in April 2024, the company combines over 130 years of energy expertise with a focused mission to power economies and deliver electricity that is vital to health, safety, security, and quality of life.

### Corporate History

**GE Heritage (Pre-2024):**
- 1892: General Electric founded through merger of Edison General Electric and Thomson-Houston
- 1900s: Pioneered steam turbines for power generation
- 1970s-2000s: Global expansion in gas turbines, wind energy, and nuclear power
- 2015: Acquisition of Alstom Power grid business
- 2018: Formation of GE Renewable Energy combining wind, grid, and solar

**Spin-off (2024):**
- GE executed a three-way split:
  - **GE Aerospace** (aviation)
  - **GE Vernova** (energy)
  - **GE HealthCare** (healthcare, spun off 2023)
- GE Vernova became independent publicly-traded company on April 2, 2024

### Sustainability Framework

**Three Pillars:**

1. **Electrify**
   - Enable universal access to reliable electricity
   - Support 3x global nuclear capacity by 2050
   - Grid solutions for renewable integration

2. **Decarbonize**
   - Hydrogen-ready gas turbines (50% → 100% H2)
   - SF6-free grid equipment (99% GWP reduction)
   - Carbon-free nuclear generation

3. **Sustain**
   - Circular economy for equipment lifecycle
   - Supply chain decarbonization
   - Workforce development for energy transition

**ESG Commitments:**
- Net zero operations by 2050
- 50% reduction in Scope 1 & 2 emissions by 2030
- GE Vernova Foundation: Skilled workforce development
