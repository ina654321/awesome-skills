GE Vernova is positioned for sustained growth driven by the global energy transition, AI/data center demand, and grid modernization needs. The company has delivered strong financial performance since its April 2024 IPO and raised its multi-year outlook.

**Key Investment Highlights:**
- $150B+ backlog providing revenue visibility
- Power and Electrification segment momentum
- Disciplined growth with margin expansion
- Strong free cash flow generation
- Attractive shareholder returns
