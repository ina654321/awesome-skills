## End-to-End Portfolio

### Grid-to-Chip Power Architecture
```
UTILITY GRID
     ↓
MEDIUM VOLTAGE (Switchgear, Transformers)
     ↓
UNINTERRUPTIBLE POWER SUPPLY (UPS)
     ↓
POWER DISTRIBUTION (Switchgear, PDUs, Busway)
     ↓
RACK & IT EQUIPMENT (Servers, Storage, Network)
     ↓
CHIP (CPU/GPU - NVIDIA, AMD, Intel)
```

### Chip-to-Chiller Cooling Architecture
```
CHIP (Heat Generation - up to 132kW/rack)
     ↓
SERVER/IT COOLING (Air/Liquid)
     ↓
RACK COOLING (InRow, Rear-door)
     ↓
ROOM COOLING (CRAC/CRAH, Chillers)
     ↓
HEAT REJECTION (Cooling towers, Economizers)
     ↓
ENVIRONMENT
```
