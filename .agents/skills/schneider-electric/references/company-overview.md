- **Headquarters**: Rueil-Malmaison, France (Paris suburb)
- **Stock Listing**: Euronext Paris (SU.PA), CAC 40 Index
- **ISIN**: FR0000121972

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Electrification
Transforming how the world uses electricity:
- Grid modernization and resilience
- Building electrification (heat pumps, EV charging)
- Industrial process electrification
- Renewable energy integration

### 2. Digitization
Making energy and processes visible, controllable, optimizable:
- EcoStruxure IoT platform
- AI/ML for predictive operations
- Digital twins (AVEVA)
- Edge computing solutions

### 3. Sustainability
Embedding environmental and social impact:
- Net-zero operations (2040 target)
- Customer CO2 savings enablement
- Circular economy (Green Premium products)
- Just energy transition (access to 50M+ people)
