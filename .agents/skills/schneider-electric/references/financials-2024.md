### Margin Progression
```
EBITA Margin Trend:
2022: 16.8%
2023: 17.5%
2024: 18.1%
2025 Target: 18.3-18.5%
```

### Margin Expansion Drivers
1. **Volume leverage**: Operating leverage on strong growth
2. **Mix improvement**: Higher software/services content
3. **Pricing discipline**: Value-based pricing sustained
4. **Cost productivity**: SFC (selling, field, general) efficiency
5. **Strategic investments**: R&D at 5.6% of sales

## Investor Value Proposition

### Why Invest in Schneider Electric?
1. **Growth**: Exposed to structural megatrends (electrification, digitization, sustainability)
2. **Resilience**: Diversified portfolio, recurring revenue base (software/services)
3. **Profitability**: Industry-leading margins with expansion trajectory
4. **Cash Generation**: Strong FCF conversion supports dividends and growth
5. **Sustainability**: Recognized leader attracting ESG capital

### Risk Factors
- Industrial automation cyclicality
- China economic uncertainty
- Input cost inflation (mitigated via pricing)
- FX volatility (EUR strength headwind)
- Competition from specialized players
