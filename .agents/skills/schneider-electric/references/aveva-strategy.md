# AVEVA Integration Strategy

## Acquisition Overview

### Transaction Details
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Acquisition Date** | January 18, 2023 |
| **Valuation** | £10 billion (~$12 billion) |
| **Ownership** | 100% (full acquisition) |
| **Previous Stake** | 60% (since 2018 merger) |
| **Offer Price** | 3,100 pence per share |

### Strategic Rationale
> "The acquisition creates the global leader in industrial software, combining Schneider Electric's expertise in energy management with AVEVA's leadership in industrial digital transformation."

**Synergy Thesis**:
1. **Data Convergence**: Energy + process data on single platform
2. **End-to-End Lifecycle**: Engineering through operations to maintenance
3. **Accelerated SaaS Transition**: Subscription business model focus
4. **Market Expansion**: Cross-selling opportunities

## Investment Thesis Summary

The AVEVA acquisition transforms Schneider Electric from a **hardware-centric industrial company** to a **software-enabled digital transformation leader**.

| Strategic Element | Value |
|-------------------|-------|
| **Recurring Revenue** | SaaS ARR growth +15% |
| **Higher Margins** | Software margins accretive |
| **Customer Stickiness** | Digital twin creates switching costs |
| **Sustainability Enablement** | Software tracks and optimizes carbon |
| **Innovation Platform** | Foundation for AI/ML applications |

**Long-term Vision**: The combination of Schneider's energy expertise and AVEVA's industrial software creates the **definitive platform for sustainable industrial transformation**.
