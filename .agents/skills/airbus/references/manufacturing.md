# Airbus Manufacturing & Supply Chain

## Manufacturing Philosophy

**Lean Manufacturing:**
- Airbus Production System (APS) based on lean principles
- Continuous improvement (Kaizen) culture
- Just-in-time delivery systems
- Waste reduction focus

**Digital Transformation:**
- Digital twin for aircraft design and production
- Factory of the Future initiatives
- Predictive maintenance in manufacturing
- Data-driven quality control

## Supply Chain

| Supplier | Components | Aircraft |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Spirit AeroSystems | Fuselages, pylons | A320, A350 (portions) |
| GKN Aerospace | Wings, empennage | Multiple programs |
| Leonardo | Aerostructures | Various |
| FACC | Interior components | A320, A350 |

**Tier 2 - Sub-assemblies:**
- Wing boxes
- Landing gear components
- Avionics systems
- Interior modules

**Tier 3 - Components:**
- Raw materials
- Standard parts
- Electronic components

**Hydraulics:**

### Supply Chain Challenges (2025)

**Engine Constraints:**
- CFM LEAP production delays
- Pratt & Whitney GTF inspection campaign
- ~60 A320neo "gliders" (aircraft without engines)

**Spirit AeroSystems Situation:**
- 2024: Spirit financial difficulties
- 2025: Airbus to acquire key work packages
- Integration ongoing

**Labor Availability:**
- Skilled workforce shortage
- Post-COVID attrition
- Training pipeline rebuilding

**Raw Materials:**
- Aluminum supply constraints
- Titanium (Russian source replacement)
- Composites capacity

## Quality Management

### Quality Standards

**ISO Certifications:**
- ISO 9001 (Quality Management)
- AS/EN 9100 (Aerospace Quality)
- ISO 14001 (Environmental)
- ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety)

**Regulatory Compliance:**
- EASA Part 21 (Design/Production)
- FAA regulations (US market)
- CAAC requirements (China)

### Quality Incidents (2025)

**A320 Fuselage Panels:**
- December 2025: Quality issue discovered
- Supplier: Spirit AeroSystems
- Impact: Production slowdown, inspections required
- Response: Enhanced quality controls implemented

**General Approach:**
- Root cause analysis
- Corrective action implementation
- Supplier quality audits
- Continuous monitoring
