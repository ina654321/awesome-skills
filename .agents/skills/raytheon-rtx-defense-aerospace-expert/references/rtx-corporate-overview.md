# RTX Corporate Overview

## Company Profile

**RTX Corporation** (NYSE: RTX) is the world's largest aerospace and defense company, formed through the 2020 merger of Raytheon Company and United Technologies Corporation (UTC).

### Key Statistics (2024)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Annual Sales | $80.7 billion |
| Net Income | $4.8 billion |
| Adjusted Operating Profit | $10.2 billion |
| Total Backlog | $218 billion |
| Global Employees | 185,000+ |
| Countries Served | 60+ |
| Headquarters | Arlington, Virginia |
| CEO | Greg Hayes |

### Business Segments

## Collins Aerospace

**2024 Performance:**
- Sales: $28.3 billion (+8% YoY)
- Adjusted Operating Profit: $4.5 billion (15.9% margin)
- Commercial OE: +16% growth
- Commercial Aftermarket: +13% growth

**Key Product Lines:**
- **Avionics:** Pro Line Fusion, flight management systems, weather radar
- **Mission Systems:** Military communications, electronic warfare, displays
- **Aerostructures:** Nacelles, thrust reversers, landing gear
- **Interiors:** Seats, galleys, lavatories, oxygen systems
- **Connected Aviation:** Inflight connectivity, airport systems

**Major Programs:**
- Boeing 787 nacelles and interior components
- Airbus A350 landing gear
- F-35 distributed aperture system (DAS)
- B-21 Raider subsystems

## Pratt & Whitney

**2024 Performance:**
- Sales: $28.1 billion (+14% YoY)
- Adjusted Operating Profit: $2.3 billion (8.1% margin)
- GTF Shipments: +14% YoY

**Key Product Lines:**
- **Commercial:** GTF (PW1000G series), PW800, V2500
- **Military:** F135 (F-35), F119 (F-22), F100/F117, TF33
- **Business/General Aviation:** PT6, PW800, PW300 series
- **Aftermarket:** Maintenance, repair, overhaul services

**Major Programs:**
- F135 Engine Core Upgrade (ECU)
- GTF Advantage development
- F119 sustainment (U.S. Air Force)
- Commercial engine fleet management

## Raytheon

**2024 Performance:**
- Sales: $26.8 billion (+2% YoY, +10% organic ex-divestiture)
- Adjusted Operating Profit: $2.7 billion (10.2% margin)
- Book-to-Bill: 1.48x
- Backlog: $63 billion

**Key Product Lines:**
- **Air & Missile Defense:** Patriot, NASAMS, radar systems
- **Strike Systems:** Tomahawk, AMRAAM, StormBreaker
- **Electronic Warfare:** Next Generation Jammer, decoys
- **Space & C2:** Satellite systems, command and control

**Major Contracts (2024):**
- $1.9 billion classified awards
- $848 million (Romania Patriot)
- $763 million (Stinger missiles)
- $591 million (Next Generation Jammer)
- $698.9 million (Taiwan NASAMS)

- **2020:** Raytheon-UTC merger creates Raytheon Technologies
- **2023:** Corporate headquarters relocates to Arlington, Virginia
- **2023:** Rebrands as RTX Corporation
- **2024:** Cybersecurity, Intelligence and Services business divested
