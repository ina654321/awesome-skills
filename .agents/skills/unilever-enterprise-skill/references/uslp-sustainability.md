# Unilever Sustainable Living Plan (USLP)

## Overview

The **Unilever Sustainable Living Plan (USLP)** is Unilever's blueprint for sustainable business, embedding environmental and social purpose into the core strategy. Evolved from the original 2010 plan to current commitments.

## Three Big Goals

### 1. Health & Wellbeing

**Target:** Improve health and hygiene for 1 billion people by 2030

**Key Initiatives:**

| Program | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Lifebuoy Handwashing** | Reached 1 billion+ people with hygiene education |
| **Oral Care** | Pepsodent brushing behavior change |
| **Safe Drinking Water** | Pureit water purification |
| **Mental Wellbeing** | Dove Self-Esteem Project |

**Dove Self-Esteem Project:**
- Reached 60+ million young people
- Educational programs in schools
- Body confidence curriculum
- Digital resources

### 2. Environmental Impact

**Target:** Halve environmental footprint of products by 2030

**Climate Action:**

| Commitment | Target | Status |
|------------|--------|--------|
| **Net Zero** | 2039 (across value chain) | Science-based targets |
| **Operations Net Zero** | 2030 | 100% renewable grid electricity |
| **Carbon Reduction** | Scope 1, 2, 3 | On track |

**Key Initiatives:**
- Renewable electricity: 100% for operations
- Refrigeration: Transition to climate-friendly
- Logistics: Optimize transport, EV pilots
- Manufacturing: Energy efficiency

**Circular Economy:**

| Pillar | Actions |
|--------|---------|
| **Less Plastic** | 55%+ reduction in virgin plastic (since 2008) |
| **Better Plastic** | 100% recyclable, compostable, reusable packaging |
| **No Plastic** | Refill, reuse, naked packaging |

**Packaging Progress:**
- 55%+ of plastic packaging is recyclable
- Refill systems scaling (Dove, Cif)
- Concentrated products (less packaging)
- Alternative materials pilots

**Water Stewardship:**

| Target | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Water efficiency in manufacturing | 49% reduction since 2010 |
| Water stewardship in water-stressed areas | Multiple programs |
| Product formulations | Water-smart design |

**Agriculture & Nature:**

| Commitment | Status |
|------------|--------|
| **Deforestation-Free** | 95%+ sustainable sourcing |
| **Palm Oil** | 100% certified sustainable |
| **Soy** | Certification progress |
| **Paper & Board** | FSC/PEFC certified |
| **Tea** | Sustainable sourcing |
| **Cocoa** | Certification progress |
| **Regenerative Agriculture** | Pilot programs expanding |

### 3. Enhanced Livelihoods

**Target:** Improve livelihoods for millions in value chain

**Living Wage:**
- Commitment to living wage for supply chain workers by 2030
- Priority commodities: Tea, cocoa, factory workers
- Partnerships with suppliers

**Smallholder Farmers:**
- 800,000+ smallholders in supply chain
- Training and support programs
- Sustainable agriculture practices
- Income improvement

**Inclusivity:**
- Diverse supplier program
- Women in supply chain
- Youth employment
- Inclusive business models

## Climate Transition Action Plan (CTAP)

### 2039 Net Zero Commitment

**Emissions Breakdown:**

| Scope | Source | Reduction Strategy |
|-------|--------|-------------------|
| **Scope 1** | Operations, vehicles | 100% renewable, EVs |
| **Scope 2** | Purchased electricity | 100% renewable |
| **Scope 3** | Supply chain, consumer use | Supplier engagement, product innovation |

**Science-Based Targets:**
- 1.5°C aligned
- Validated by SBTi
- Annual progress reporting

### Key Initiatives

**Regenerative Agriculture:**
- Goal: Regenerative practices on 1 million hectares by 2030
- Pilot programs: Knorr vegetables, Lipton tea
- Farmer partnerships
- Soil health, biodiversity, carbon sequestration

**Reforestation:**
- Trees for farmers program
- Carbon sequestration projects
- Landscape restoration
- Community engagement
