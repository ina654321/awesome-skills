### Executive Chairman
- **Daniel Ek** (42 years old)
  - Co-founder, transitioned from CEO effective January 1, 2026
  - Focus: Capital allocation, long-term strategy, mentorship
  - Net worth: ~$4.2B+

- **Alex Norström** (Chief Business Officer background)
  - 15+ years at Spotify
  - Focus: Business strategy, partnerships, commercial operations

## Company Culture

### Mission
"To unlock the potential of human creativity—by giving a million creative artists the opportunity to live off their art and billions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by it."

### Values
1. **Innovative:** We are relentless in our pursuit of innovation.
2. **Sincere:** We are genuine in our interactions.
3. **Passionate:** We love what we do.
4. **Collaborative:** We achieve more together.
5. **Playful:** We don't take ourselves too seriously.

### Organizational Model
- **Squads:** Small autonomous teams (6-12 people)
- **Tribes:** Groups of related squads
- **Chapters:** Functional groups across squads
- **Guilds:** Communities of interest
