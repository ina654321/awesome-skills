# Spotify Glossary

## A

**ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**
- Average monthly revenue generated per Premium subscriber
- Q4 2025: €4.70
- Important metric for investor analysis

**Ad-Supported Tier**
- Free version of Spotify with advertisements
- ~461 million users (Q4 2025)
- Lower revenue per user than Premium

**AI DJ**
- Personalized radio feature with AI-generated commentary
- Uses generative AI and text-to-speech technology
- Launched 2023

**Anchor**
- Podcast creation and hosting platform acquired by Spotify (2019)
- Free podcasting tools for creators

## B

**Backstage**
- Open-source developer portal platform created by Spotify
- Donated to CNCF (Cloud Native Computing Foundation)
- Used by 1000+ companies

**Blend**
- Collaborative playlist feature combining tastes of 2+ users
- Updates daily
- Popular social feature

**Billions Club**
- Playlist featuring songs with 1 billion+ streams
- Marketing events for artists reaching milestone

## C

**Canvas**
- 3-8 second looping video that plays with tracks
- Artist tool for visual engagement
- Available in Spotify for Artists

**Collaborative Filtering**
- Recommendation technique based on user behavior similarity
- "Users who liked X also liked Y"
- Core to Discover Weekly

**Completion Rate**
- Percentage of listeners who play a song fully
- Critical algorithm signal (70%+ considered good)
- Influences recommendation distribution

## D

**Daily Mix**
- Series of personalized playlists by genre cluster
- Mix of favorites and discoveries
- Updates daily

**Discover Weekly**
- Personalized playlist of 30 new recommendations
- Updated every Monday
- One of Spotify's most popular features
- Uses collaborative filtering primarily

**Discovery Mode**
- Artist promotion tool that reduces royalty rate for algorithmic boost
- Controversial "pay for play" feature
- 30% royalty reduction in exchange for promotion

**Duo Mix**
- Playlist for Spotify Duo subscribers
- Combines tastes of two accounts

## E

**Echonest**
- Music intelligence company acquired by Spotify (2014)
- Powers audio analysis features
- Technology for audio feature extraction

**Engaged User**
- Spotify's metric: user who viewed at least one Wrapped story
- Key metric for Wrapped campaign success

## F

**Family Mix**
- Playlist for Spotify Family plan members
- Combines tastes of up to 6 accounts

**Feature Flag**
- Software development practice for gradual feature rollout
- Enables A/B testing
- Critical for safe deployments at scale

**Freemium**
- Business model combining free and paid tiers
- Spotify's core model since launch

## G

**Gimlet Media**
- Podcast production company acquired by Spotify (2019)
- $230 million acquisition
- Known for high-quality narrative podcasts

**Gross Margin**
- Revenue minus cost of revenue, as percentage
- Spotify target: 35-40%
- Q4 2025: 33.1% (record high)

**Guild**
- Cross-functional community at Spotify
- Example: Web Performance Guild
- Voluntary interest-based groups

## H

**HiFi**
- Lossless audio quality tier (announced but not yet launched as of 2025)
- CD-quality streaming
- Competitive response to Apple Music, Tidal

## I

**ISRC (International Standard Recording Code)**
- Unique identifier for sound recordings
- Used in royalty tracking

**ISWC (International Standard Musical Work Code)**
- Unique identifier for musical compositions
- Used for publishing rights

## K

**Kafka**
- Apache Kafka - distributed event streaming platform
- Spotify's central nervous system for data
- Billions of events processed daily

## L

**Listening Personality**
- Archetype assigned in Wrapped (16 types)
- Based on listening behavior analysis
- Examples: Adventurer, Specialist, Replayer

**Loud & Clear**
- Spotify's transparency initiative for artist payments
- Annual reports on royalty distribution
- Website: loudandclear.byspotify.com

**Lottie**
- Animation library by Airbnb
- Used for Wrapped animations
- JSON-based, cross-platform

## M

**MAU (Monthly Active Users)**
- Users who stream at least once in 28-day window
- Q4 2025: 751 million
- Key growth metric

**Marquee**
- Sponsored recommendation ad product
- Full-screen ad in Home feed
- CPC pricing ($0.50-$1.00 per click)

**Matrix Factorization**
- Collaborative filtering technique
- Reduces user-item matrix to latent factors
- Used in recommendation systems

**Megaphone**
- Podcast hosting and monetization platform
- Acquired by Spotify (2020)
- For enterprise podcasters

**Microservices**
- Architecture of small, independent services
- Spotify runs 1000+ microservices
- Enables autonomous squad teams

## N

**NLP (Natural Language Processing)**
- Analyzing text data for music understanding
- Used for genre classification, trend detection
- Processes blogs, reviews, playlist descriptions

## P

**Platform APIs**
- TypeScript abstraction layer
- Enables shared code between Web and Desktop
- Key to 2021 desktop redesign

**Podcast**
- Audio (and increasingly video) episodic content
- 7 million+ titles on Spotify
- 28% of MAUs engage with podcasts

**Premium**
- Paid subscription tier
- 290 million subscribers (Q4 2025)
- $11.99/month Individual in US

**Prompted Playlist**
- AI feature for natural language playlist creation
- Beta launched 2025
- "Make me a playlist for..."

## R

**Release Radar**
- Weekly playlist of new releases from followed artists
- Updated every Friday
- Critical for artist fan engagement

**Royalties**
- Payments to rights holders for music usage
- Streamshare model at Spotify
- ~$11 billion paid in 2025

## S

**SMB (Sort Merge Bucket)**
- Data processing optimization technique
- Reduced Wrapped processing costs by 60%
- Efficient join operations on large datasets

**Save Rate**
- Percentage of listeners who add track to library
- Strongest positive signal for algorithm
- Critical metric for artists

**Skip Rate**
- Percentage who skip within 30 seconds
- Strong negative signal
- 28% average across platform

**Squad**
- Small autonomous team at Spotify (6-12 people)
- Owns features end-to-end
- Core to Spotify's organizational model

**Spotify for Artists**
- Dashboard for musicians
- Analytics, promotion tools, profile management
- Free to use

**Spotify for Podcasters**
- Platform for podcast creators
- Hosting, analytics, monetization
- Replaced Anchor as primary brand

**Streamshare**
- Royalty calculation method
- (Artist Streams / Total Streams) × Revenue Pool
- Not a fixed per-stream rate

## T

**Taste Profile**
- Dynamic representation of user's musical preferences
- Updated in real-time from listening behavior
- Powers personalization

**The Ringer**
- Sports and pop culture media company
- Acquired by Spotify (2020)
- $196 million acquisition

**Tribe**
- Collection of related squads at Spotify
- Aligned around common mission
- Larger organizational unit

**30-Second Rule**
- Only streams >30 seconds count as "plays"
- Determines royalty eligibility
- Algorithm only learns from counted plays

## U

**UMAW (Union of Musicians and Allied Workers)**
- Advocacy group for streaming fairness
- Critic of Spotify's royalty model
- Pushed for Living Wage for Musicians Act

## V

**Valence**
- Audio feature measuring musical positiveness
- 0.0 (sad/angry) to 1.0 (happy/cheerful)
- Used in mood-based recommendations

**Video Podcast**
- Podcast format with video component
- Growing rapidly on Spotify
- Higher ad CPMs than audio-only

## W

**Widevine**
- Google's DRM technology
- Used by Spotify for content protection
- Prevents unauthorized ripping

**Wrapped**
- Annual personalized listening recap campaign
- Launched 2016
- 300M+ engaged users in 2025
- Spotify's biggest marketing event
