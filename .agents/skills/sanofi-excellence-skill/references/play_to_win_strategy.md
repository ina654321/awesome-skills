# Sanofi "Play to Win" Strategy

## Strategy Overview

The "Play to Win" strategy was introduced by CEO Paul Hudson in December 2019 and updated in October 2023. It represents a fundamental transformation of Sanofi from a diversified healthcare company to a pure-play biopharmaceutical leader.

## Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1: Focus on Growth Drivers

**Core Areas:**
1. **Immunology** - Dupixent expansion and pipeline
2. **Rare Diseases** - Specialty care growth
3. **Vaccines** - Leadership position expansion
4. **Oncology** - Selective opportunities

**Divestitures Completed:**
- Zentiva (generics) - 2018
- EU generics business - 2021
- Opella (consumer healthcare) - May 2025

### Pillar 2: Innovation Excellence

**R&D Transformation:**
- Increased R&D investment +66% over 3 years
- "Lean and fast" development engine
- AI-powered drug discovery
- Target: 12 NMEs with €2-5B+ potential

**Key Metrics:**
- Phase 3 trial starts: +50% by 2025
- Probability of success: Industry-leading
- Development timelines: Shortening through efficiency

### Pillar 3: Operational Efficiency

**Post-Opella Improvements:**
- Gross margin expansion trajectory
- Simplified organizational structure
- Focused capital allocation
- Cost discipline while investing in growth

**Targets:**

## Competitive Positioning

### Before Play to Win (2019)
- Diversified healthcare conglomerate
- Consumer, generics, and pharma mixed
- Lower growth, lower margins
- Unclear strategic focus

### After Play to Win (2025)
- Pure-play biopharma
- Clear focus: Immunology, vaccines, rare diseases
- Higher growth, expanding margins
- Differentiated scientific positioning
