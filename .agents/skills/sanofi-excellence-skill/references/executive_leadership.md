## CEO Profile: Paul Hudson

### Background
- **Born:** 1969, United Kingdom
- **Education:** Manchester Metropolitan University
- **Previous Roles:**
  - CEO, Novartis Pharmaceuticals (2016-2019)
  - President, AstraZeneca US (2013-2016)
  - Various roles at AstraZeneca, Roche, Sandoz

### Tenure at Sanofi
- **Joined:** September 2019
- **Strategy:** Play to Win transformation
- **Achievements:**
  - Consumer healthcare divestiture
  - Dupixent growth to €13B+
  - Pipeline transformation
  - Pure-play biopharma positioning

### Compensation (2024)
- Base salary: €1.5M
- Variable compensation: Performance-based
- Long-term incentives: Stock-based
