# Chemicals Segment Reference

## Overview

The Chemicals segment is the foundation of BASF's Verbund, supplying basic chemicals and intermediates to other segments and external customers. It represents **16.9% of BASF Group sales** (2025).

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Share of sales | 16.9% |
| R&D expenses | €87 million |
| Investments | €2,108 million |
| Key differentiator | Verbund integration and feedstock flexibility |

## Intermediates Division

### Product Portfolio

**Acrylics Value Chain:**
```
Propylene → Acrylic Acid → Acrylates → Coatings, Adhesives
                    ↓
            Superabsorbent Polymers (SAP)
```

| Product | Applications | Key Customers |
|---------|--------------|---------------|
| Glacial Acrylic Acid (GAA) | Coatings, adhesives, SAP | Dow, Nippon Shokubai |
| Butyl Acrylate (BA) | Paints, coatings | AkzoNobel, PPG |
| Ethyl Acrylate | Coatings, textiles | Various |
| 2-Ethylhexyl Acrylate | Adhesives, coatings | Henkel, Bostik |

**Specialty Intermediates:**

| Product Category | Products | Applications |

| Glycols | Propylene glycol, butanediol | Polyesters, solvents |

### Capacity Expansion

**Ludwigshafen Modernization (2025):**
- 30% capacity increase for chloroformates and acid chlorides
- Three-digit million euro investment
- Completion: 2025
- Enhanced support for pharmaceutical and crop protection customers

**Zhanjiang Acrylics Complex:**
- 400,000 tonnes/year butyl acrylate capacity
- Mechanical completion: July 2025
- Serving Asia-Pacific demand growth

## Strategic Importance

**Verbund Integration:**
- Chemicals segment provides feedstock for 70%+ of BASF products
- Backward integration ensures supply security
- Forward integration enables value capture

**Competitive Position:**
- Top 3 global producer in most product categories
- Cost advantage through Verbund integration
- Feedstock flexibility mitigates price volatility

**Sustainability:**
- Renewable ammonia production (54 MW electrolyzer)
- Chemical recycling feedstock integration
- Carbon footprint reduction through process optimization
