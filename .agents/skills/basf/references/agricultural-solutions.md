# Agricultural Solutions Reference

## Overview

Agricultural Solutions is one of BASF's two **standalone businesses**, operating with greater flexibility to serve the global agriculture market. It represents **16.1% of BASF Group sales** (2025).

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Sales 2025 | €9.6 billion |
| EBITDA before special items | €2.9 billion |
| EBITDA margin | 30.0% |
| R&D investment | €990 million |
| Employees | ~12,000 |

**Strategic Status:** Standalone business with planned partial IPO in 2027
