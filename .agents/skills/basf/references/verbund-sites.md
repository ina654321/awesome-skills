# Verbund Sites Reference

## The Verbund Concept

The Verbund is BASF's unique competitive advantage—an intelligent system that links production plants, energy flows, and logistics for resource-efficient, CO2-optimized operations.

**Key Benefits:**
- By-products of one process become feedstock for another
- Shared utilities reduce capital intensity
- Energy integration lowers costs and emissions
- 6.1 million tonnes CO₂ emissions prevented annually vs. separate production

## Verbund Integration Metrics

**Global Impact:**
- **50%+** of BASF volumes produced at Verbund sites
- **6.1 million tonnes** CO₂ emissions prevented annually
- **2,850 km** of pipelines at Ludwigshafen alone
- **8,000+** products from a few raw materials

**Energy Efficiency:**
| Site | CHP Efficiency | Energy Savings |
|------|----------------|----------------|
| Ludwigshafen | >90% | 2.5 million tonnes CO₂/year |
| Antwerp | >90% | 1.2 million tonnes CO₂/year |
| All sites | Combined | 6.1 million tonnes CO₂/year |

**Material Flows:**
- By-product utilization: >95% at major sites
- Waste-to-energy: Remaining waste converted to steam/power
- Water recycling: Multiple use cycles before treatment
