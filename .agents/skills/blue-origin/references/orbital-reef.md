# Orbital Reef Space Station

## Overview
Orbital Reef is a planned commercial space station developed by Blue Origin in partnership with Sierra Space. It represents a key component of Blue Origin's vision for "millions of people living and working in space."

## Concept
> "Orbital Reef will be a mixed-use space station in low Earth orbit for commerce, research, and tourism by the end of this decade."
> — Blue Origin

Orbital Reef is designed as a "business park in space" — a commercial destination supporting diverse activities including research, manufacturing, tourism, and international collaboration.

## Station Architecture

### Core Modules (Blue Origin)
- **Function:** Utilities, power distribution, docking ports
- **Structure:** Rigid aluminum modules
- **Origin:** Derived from ISS heritage

### LIFE Habitats (Sierra Space)
| Feature | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Full Name** | Large Integrated Flexible Environment |
| **Type** | Inflatable/expandable habitat |
| **Launch Volume** | Fits in standard 5m fairing (compressed) |
| **Expanded Volume** | Up to 500 m³ (LIFE 500) / 285 m³ (LIFE 285) |
| **Material** | Vectran high-strength webbing/fabric |
| **Safety Factor** | 4:1 (NASA requirement) |

### LIFE Testing Milestones
| Test | Date | Result |
|------|------|--------|
| First Full-Scale UBP | Dec 2023 | 77 psi burst (exceeded 60.8 psi requirement) |
| Second Full-Scale UBP | Jun 2024 | 74 psi burst (confirmed design margin) |
| LIFE 500 Development | Q1 2025 | Planned burst test |

## NASA Commercial LEO Destinations Program

### Funding
- **NASA Award:** $130 million through Space Act Agreement
- **Purpose:** Develop commercial space station capability
- **Timeline:** 2021-2028 development

### NASA's LEO Strategy
- ISS transition to commercial platforms by 2030
- Multiple commercial destinations for redundancy
- Orbital Reef is one of two funded partners (along with Axiom Space)

## Key Technologies

### Inflatable Habitat Technology
- **Softgoods Construction:** Flexible fabric that becomes rigid when pressurized
- **Launch Efficiency:** 6:1 expansion ratio (volume launched vs. operational)
- **Micrometeoroid Protection:** Multiple layers with Nextel/Kevlar shielding
- **Radiation Protection:** Water shielding in shell structure

### Life Support Systems
- Closed-loop ECLSS (Environmental Control and Life Support)
- 93%+ water recycling (ISS heritage, targeting 98%)
- Carbon dioxide removal and oxygen generation
- Fire detection and suppression

### Power Systems
- Solar arrays (Redwire contribution)
- Energy storage systems
- Power distribution to all modules
