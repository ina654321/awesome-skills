### §3.2 Key Operating Processes

**Medical Cost Management:**
1. **Trend Analytics (HCTA):** Standardized quarterly medical expense reporting
2. **Network Management:** Contract negotiations, adequacy standards, narrow network options
3. **Care Management:** High-risk patient identification, intervention programs
4. **Pharmacy Cost Control:** Formulary management, prior authorization, specialty pharmacy

**Quality & Outcomes:**
1. **HEDIS Measures:** Annual quality metric tracking and improvement
2. **Medicare Star Ratings:** 5-star system for MA plan performance
3. **CAHPS Surveys:** Member experience measurement
4. **Value-Based Contracts:** Provider incentive alignment

**Technology & Operations:**
1. **Claims Processing:** Real-time adjudication, auto-adjudication targets
2. **Digital Engagement:** Mobile apps, telehealth, member portals
3. **AI/ML Applications:** Predictive analytics, clinical decision support
4. **Data Integration:** EHR connectivity, interoperability (Change Healthcare)
