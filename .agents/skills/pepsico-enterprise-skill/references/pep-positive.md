# Pep Positive Sustainability Strategy

## Overview

**Pep Positive** is PepsiCo's strategic end-to-end transformation of how they create growth and shared value across three pillars: Positive Agriculture, Positive Value Chain, and Positive Choices.

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Positive Agriculture

**Goal**: Spread regenerative practices to restore the earth.

**Key Commitments:**

| Target | Timeline | Status |
|--------|----------|--------|
| 7 million acres regenerative | 2030 | In progress |
| Improve livelihoods of 250,000 people in agriculture | 2030 | In progress |
| Sustainably source 100% key ingredients | 2030 | On track |

**Regenerative Agriculture Principles:**

1. **Soil Health**
   - Minimize soil disturbance
   - Keep soil covered
   - Maximize crop diversity
   - Maintain living roots year-round
   - Integrate livestock

2. **Biodiversity**
   - Protect and enhance ecosystems
   - Support pollinator habitats
   - Reduce chemical inputs

3. **Water Stewardship**
   - Efficient irrigation
   - Watershed protection
   - Water replenishment

4. **Farmer Livelihoods**
   - Training and education
   - Financial support
   - Market access

**C.A.F.E. Practices Expansion:**
- Originally for coffee
- Now applied to other commodities
- Economic, social, environmental standards
- Third-party verification

### 2. Positive Value Chain

**Goal**: Build a circular and inclusive value chain.

**Key Commitments:**

| Target | Timeline | Status |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Net zero emissions | 2040 | On track |
| 50%+ reduction in virgin plastic per unit | 2030 | In progress |
| 100% renewable electricity | 2030 | 16 countries achieved |
| Water replenishment in high-risk watersheds | 2030 | In progress |

**Packaging Transformation:**

| Initiative | Target | Status |
|------------|--------|--------|
| Recyclable, compostable, biodegradable packaging | 100% | 80%+ achieved |
| Virgin plastic reduction (per unit vs. 2018) | 50%+ | In progress |
| rPET usage | Expand | Growing |
| Package lightweighting | Continue | Ongoing |

**Climate Action:**

- **Scope 1 & 2**: Direct emissions reduction
- **Scope 3**: Supply chain engagement
- **Renewable Energy**: Solar, wind procurement
- **Fleet Electrification**: Electric vehicle pilots

**Water Stewardship:**

- **Efficiency**: Use less water per unit produced
- **Replenishment**: Return water to watersheds
- **Access**: Safe water access programs

### 3. Positive Choices

**Goal**: Evolve the portfolio for the future.

**Key Commitments:**

| Target | Timeline | Status |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Convenient foods with nutritious ingredients | Expand portfolio | In progress |
| Beverages with reduced added sugars | Reduce sugar | In progress |
| Portion guidance options | Expand | Ongoing |
| Transparency | Continue | Strong |

**Product Portfolio Evolution:**

| Category | Initiatives |
|----------|-------------|
| **Snacks** | Whole grains, fiber, protein, no artificials |
| **Beverages** | Zero sugar, natural ingredients, functional |
| **Portion Control** | Mini packs, multipacks, smaller sizes |
| **Clean Label** | Simple ingredients, transparency |

**Nutrition Progress:**

- Reduced sodium in core products
- Added sugar reduction in beverages
- Whole grain expansion
- Protein enhancement
