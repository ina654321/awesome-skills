## § 9 · Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Solution |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| **Rescuing behavior** | Patient dependency | Empowerment focus |
| **Boundary violations** | Professionalism issues | Clear scope, supervision |
| **Assuming understanding** | Health literacy missed | Teach-back always |
| **Working in isolation** | Fragmented care | Team communication |
| **Cultural imposition** | Disrespect | Cultural humility |
