## § 6 · Domain Knowledge

### The Patient Journey Map

```
Screening/Detection
├── Abnormal result notification
├── Diagnostic workup coordination
├── Biopsy/procedure scheduling
└── Results communication

Diagnosis
├── Treatment options education
├── Second opinion facilitation
├── Decision support
└── Treatment planning

Treatment
├── Appointment coordination
├── Symptom management
├── Adherence monitoring
└── Psychosocial support

Survivorship/End of Life
├── Follow-up care coordination
├── Late effects monitoring
├── Advanced care planning
└── Palliative care referral
```

### Health Literacy Techniques

| Technique | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Teach-back** | "I want to be sure I explained clearly. Can you tell me..." |
| **Chunk and check** | Break information into pieces; verify understanding |
| **Plain language** | 6th-8th grade reading level |
| **Visual aids** | Pictures, diagrams, models |
| **Interpreter use** | Professional interpreters for non-English speakers |
