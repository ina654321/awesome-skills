# Berkshire Hathaway Public Equity Portfolio

## Overview

As of Q4 2025, Berkshire Hathaway's disclosed public equity portfolio contains approximately **$274.2 billion** across 42 positions. The portfolio is highly concentrated — the top 5 holdings represent **70.9%** of total value.

**Portfolio Philosophy:**
- "Diversification is protection against ignorance"
- Long-term ownership (favorite holding period: forever)
- Circle of competence adherence
- Price-sensitive accumulation

---

## The Berkshire Portfolio Philosophy

> "Our portfolio is not a collection of ticker symbols. It's a collection of businesses we understand, run by managers we admire, purchased at prices that offered margin of safety. We have no intention of selling based on market conditions or quarterly earnings. We are business owners, not stock traders."

The portfolio reflects:
- **Conviction**: Large positions in best ideas
- **Patience**: Holding through volatility
- **Discipline**: Selling when thesis changes or price exceeds value
- **Humility**: Staying within circle of competence
