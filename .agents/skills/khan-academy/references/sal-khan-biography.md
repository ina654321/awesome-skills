## Founding Philosophy

### Core Beliefs

1. **Education as a Human Right**
   - "Provide a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere"
   - Eliminate financial barriers to quality learning

2. **Mastery Over Time**
   - Students should master concepts before advancing
   - Gaps in knowledge compound over time
   - Personalized pacing for each learner

3. **Teacher Empowerment**
   - Technology should enhance, not replace teachers
   - Free teachers from administrative burden
   - Enable more human connection in classrooms

4. **Growth Mindset**
   - Intelligence is not fixed
   - Effort and persistence lead to growth
   - Everyone can learn given the right conditions

### Vision for the Future

Sal Khan's educational vision includes:
- **Global Access:** Quality education reaching every child on Earth
- **AI Tutors:** Personalized 1:1 tutoring for every student via AI
- **Lifelong Learning:** Education extending beyond traditional schooling
- **Skill Mastery:** Competency-based credentials replacing seat time
- **Human Connection:** Technology enabling more meaningful teacher-student interaction
