# Intel Ohio Semiconductor Campus Reference

> **Document:** Intel Ohio Manufacturing Investment Deep Dive  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-03-21

## Overview

Intel's **Ohio One** campus represents the largest single private-sector investment in Ohio history and a cornerstone of Intel's IDM 2.0 strategy for U.S.-based semiconductor manufacturing.

## Project Timeline

### Key Milestones

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| January 2022 | Project announced ($20B initial) |
| September 2022 | Groundbreaking ceremony |
| 2022-2023 | Site preparation, infrastructure |
| 2023-2024 | Foundation, structural work |
| March 2024 | CHIPS Act funding announced ($8.5B) |
| November 2024 | CHIPS Act finalized ($7.86B) |
| 2024-2026 | Construction continues |
| February 2025 | Intel confirms production delay to late 2026 |
| Late 2026 | Target production start (Phase 1) |

### Schedule Changes

**Original Plan (2022):**
- Production start: 2025

**Revised Plan (2024-2025):**
- Production start: Late 2026
- Reason: Market conditions, funding timing, financial discipline under Lip-Bu Tan

### Supply Chain Ecosystem

Intel's Ohio investment aims to create a semiconductor ecosystem:
- **Materials suppliers**: Chemicals, gases, substrates
- **Equipment vendors**: Applied Materials, ASML, Lam Research
- **Services**: Engineering, logistics, facilities
- **Research**: Universities, national labs

## Challenges and Risks

### Current Challenges

| Challenge | Status | Mitigation |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| **Timeline Delay** | Production pushed to 2026 | Financial discipline, market alignment |
| **Cost Inflation** | Construction costs up | Phased approach, CHIPS funding |
| **Workforce Shortage** | Competing for talent | Education partnerships, competitive pay |
| **Market Demand** | PC/server market soft | Focus on AI, data center growth |

### Risk Factors

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood |
|------|--------|------------|
| Further delays | Reduced competitiveness | Medium |
| Cost overruns | Financial strain | Medium |
| Technology challenges | 18A yield issues | Low (de-risked elsewhere) |
| Competition | TSMC Arizona | Medium |
