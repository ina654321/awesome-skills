# Regulatory Submission Templates

> **Guidance:** ICH M4, ICH M8, FDA eCTD Technical Conformance Guide

## Module 2: Summary Templates

### 2.5 Clinical Overview Template

```
CLINICAL OVERVIEW

1. PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT RATIONALE
   1.1 Unmet Medical Need
       • Disease burden and current treatment limitations
       • Target population and prevalence
       
   1.2 Mechanism of Action
       • Drug target and pharmacological activity
       • Rationale for therapeutic approach

2. OVERVIEW OF BIOPHARMACEUTICS
   2.1 Formulation Development
   2.2 Bioavailability/Bioequivalence

3. OVERVIEW OF CLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY
   3.1 Pharmacokinetics
       • Absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion (ADME)
       • Special populations
       • Drug interactions
       
   3.2 Pharmacodynamics
       • Primary pharmacology
       • Secondary pharmacology
       • PK/PD relationships

4. OVERVIEW OF EFFICACY
   4.1 Dose-Response
   4.2 Pivotal Studies
       • Study design summary
       • Patient population
       • Efficacy results
       • Statistical analysis
   4.3 Supportive Studies
   4.4 Persistence of Efficacy

5. OVERVIEW OF SAFETY
   5.1 Safety Database
       • Exposure data
       • Demographics
   5.2 Adverse Events
       • Common AEs
       • Serious AEs
       • Deaths
   5.3 Laboratory Findings
   5.4 Vital Signs and ECG
   5.5 Special Safety Topics
   5.6 Immunogenicity (if applicable)

6. BENEFITS AND RISKS CONCLUSIONS
   6.1 Benefits
   6.2 Risks
   6.3 Benefit-Risk Assessment

7. REFERENCES
```
