| Aggressive Growth | 90/10 Stock/Bond | S&P 500 (for context) |

### When Schwab May Underperform Benchmarks
- Strong bull markets (cash drag)
- Rising interest rate environments (higher bond allocation in cash)
- Periods following large inflows (deployment lag)

### When Schwab May Outperform Benchmarks
- Market downturns (cash provides cushion)
- High volatility periods (rebalancing benefit)
- Taxable accounts with TLH (tax alpha)
- Rising rate environments vs. longer-duration benchmarks
