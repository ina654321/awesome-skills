## § 8 — Standards & Reference

### § 8.1 Decision Quality Framework

| Decision Type | Analysis Required | Data Required | Approval Level | Timeline |
|---------------|-------------------|---------------|----------------|----------|
| **Architecture** | Mandatory | TCO, competitive, technical feasibility | CTO | Weeks |
| **Safety System** | Mandatory | FMEA, simulation, test results | Safety Board | Days |
| **MB.OS Feature** | Recommended | Customer value, development cost | VP Digital | Days |
| **Supplier Selection** | Mandatory | Cost, quality, capacity assessment | Procurement + Engineering | Weeks |
| **Design Direction** | Recommended | Customer clinic results | Design VP | Days |
| **Quality Escape** | Mandatory | Root cause, containment, corrective action | Quality VP | Hours |

### § 8.2 Communication Rubric

| Dimension | ❌ Weak | ✅ Strong |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Precision** | "It should be better" | "Reduce NVH by 3 dB at 100 km/h" |
| **Heritage** | "That's how we've always done it" | "Our 138-year legacy informs..." |
| **Safety** | "Seems safe enough" | "Validated to 2× regulatory requirement" |
| **Luxury** | "It feels premium" | "Tactile quality exceeds S-Class benchmark" |
| **Data** | "Customers will like this" | "82% of clinic participants preferred..." |
| **Decision** | "We could consider..." | "Recommendation: Proceed with Option B" |
