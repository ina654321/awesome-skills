## § 3 — Risk Matrix

| # | Risk | Severity | Escalation Trigger | Consequence | Mitigation |
|---|------|----------|-------------------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | **Brand Promise Violation** | 🔴 Critical | Quality score <90% | Permanent brand damage | Chief Quality Officer review |
| 2 | **Safety System Failure** | 🔴 Critical | Any recall potential | Legal liability, fatalities | Immediate stop-ship |
| 3 | **Autonomous Driving Incident** | 🔴 Critical | Drive Pilot accident | Regulatory shutdown | Black box analysis, root cause |
| 4 | **Battery Safety** | 🔴 High | Thermal event | Fire, recall | Cell-level monitoring, isolation |
| 5 | **Supplier Quality Escape** | 🔴 High | PPM >100 | Warranty costs, reputation | Supplier development program |
| 6 | **Cybersecurity Breach** | 🔴 High | Vulnerability exposure | Data theft, vehicle control | Security patch within 24hrs |
| 7 | **China Market Decline** | 🟡 Medium | Sales -20% YoY | Revenue impact | Localized product, pricing |
| 8 | **EV Adoption Slowdown** | 🟡 Medium | BEV share stagnant | Stranded investment | Flexible powertrain strategy |

### Escalation Protocol

| Severity | Response Time | Escalate To | Action Required |
|----------|---------------|-------------|-----------------|
| 🔴 Critical | Immediate (<1hr) | CEO + Board Safety Committee | Stop work, crisis team activation |
| 🔴 High | <4 hours | VP Engineering + Quality + Legal | Root cause, containment plan |
| 🟡 Medium | <24 hours | Director + Department Head | Resolution plan, monitoring |
| 🟢 Low | Next review cycle | Manager | Document, continuous improvement |
