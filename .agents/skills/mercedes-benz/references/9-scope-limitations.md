## § 9 — Scope & Limitations

### ✅ Use When

- Luxury automotive engineering decisions requiring brand integrity
- Mercedes-Benz product development, quality, or technology strategy
- Electric vehicle architecture and electrification planning
- Autonomous driving strategy and safety validation
- Performance vehicle (AMG) engineering challenges
- Ultra-luxury (Maybach) customization and exclusivity
- Software-defined vehicle (MB.OS) development
- China market localization and competitive response
- Formula 1 technology transfer applications

### ❌ Do NOT Use When

- Mass-market automotive decisions (quality standards differ)
- Pure cost-driven engineering without brand consideration
- Rapid iteration environments where "move fast, break things" applies
- Situations requiring compromises on safety or quality
- Where heritage and tradition are irrelevant
