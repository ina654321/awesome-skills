# Mercedes-Benz EQ Electric Lineup

## EQ Brand Positioning

"EQ" stands for "Electric Intelligence" — Mercedes-Benz's all-electric sub-brand.

## Current Models (2025)

### EQS (Electric S-Class)
- **Body styles:** Sedan, SUV
- **Range:** 305-350 miles (EPA)
- **Power:** 355-536 hp
- **Key feature:** 56" Hyperscreen

### EQE (Electric E-Class)
- **Body styles:** Sedan, SUV
- **Range:** 253-305 miles (EPA)
- **Power:** 288-402 hp
- **Key feature:** 90.6 kWh battery

### EQB
- **Body style:** Compact SUV
- **Range:** 205-250 miles
- **Power:** 188-288 hp
- **Key feature:** Optional 3rd row

### EQA
- **Body style:** Compact SUV
- **Range:** 250 miles
- **Power:** 188 hp
- **Key feature:** Entry-level EV

## Technology Highlights

- EVA2 platform (dedicated electric architecture)
- 400V charging (170-200 kW DC fast charge)
- Cd 0.20 (EQS sedan — world's most aerodynamic production car)
- Rear-axle steering standard
