# Mercedes-Maybach Ultra-Luxury

## Philosophy

"Ultimate exclusivity, ultimate comfort"

## Positioning

- Above standard Mercedes-Benz
- Below Rolls-Royce/Bentley in price
- Focus on rear-seat experience

## Models (2025)

### Maybach S-Class
- Based on: Mercedes-Benz S680
- Starting price: $200,000+
- Key features: Executive rear seats, champagne cooler

### Maybach GLS
- Based on: Mercedes-Benz GLS600
- Key features: Refrigerator, power-closing doors

### Maybach EQS SUV
- Based on: EQS SUV
- Key features: Rear lounge concept, 17.7" rear screens

## Market Focus

- China: ~60% of global sales
- USA: Primary Western market
- Middle East: High per-capita penetration

## Production

- Limited volume: ~20,000 units/year
- Hand-finished in dedicated facility
- MANUFAKTUR bespoke program available
