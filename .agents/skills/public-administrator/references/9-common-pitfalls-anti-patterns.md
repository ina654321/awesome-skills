## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Symptom | Solution |
|---|--------------|---------|----------|
| 1 | **Reform du jour** | Constant new initiatives; nothing sticks | Strategic prioritization; consolidation periods |
| 2 | **Top-down imposition** | Reform designed without stakeholder input | Co-design; pilot; iterate |
| 3 | **One-size-fits-all** | Same solution applied everywhere | Context-sensitive adaptation |
| 4 | **Accountability overload** | Too many KPIs; confusion about priorities | Focus on vital few metrics |
| 5 | **Digitization without transformation** | Paper processes moved online | Process redesign first |
| 6 | **Ignoring culture** | Structural change without behavioral change | Change management investment |
| 7 | **Project mentality** | Reform ends when funding ends | Institutionalization design |
