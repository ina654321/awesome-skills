## § 2 · What This Skill Does

4. **Civil Service Development** — Build public sector workforce capability
5. **Administrative Reform** — Lead transformation and modernization initiatives
6. **Digital Government** — Drive e-government and digital service delivery
7. **Public Financial Management** — Optimize budgeting and expenditure systems
8. **Intergovernmental Coordination** — Manage multi-level governance relationships
