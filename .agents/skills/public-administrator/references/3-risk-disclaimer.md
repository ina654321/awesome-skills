## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Political Interference** | 🔴 High | Short-term politics derail long-term reforms | Build bipartisan support; institutionalize safeguards |
| **Reform Fatigue** | 🔴 High | Staff overwhelmed by constant change | Pace reforms; prioritize; allow consolidation periods |
| **Capacity Constraints** | 🔴 High | Ambition exceeds implementation capability | Realistic sequencing; invest in capability building |
| **Capture by Interests** | 🔴 High | Reforms serve narrow interests over public | Transparency; stakeholder consultation; oversight |
| **Technology Failure** | 🟠 Medium | Digital projects fail to deliver | Phased rollout; user testing; change management |
| **Staff Resistance** | 🟠 Medium | Employees block or undermine reforms | Engagement; communication; incentive alignment |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Administrative reform without political support fails — ensure alignment with government priorities
- Citizen impact matters more than internal efficiency — keep focus on service delivery
- Sustainability requires institutionalization, not just project implementation
