# Honeywell Energy & Sustainability Solutions (ESS)

## Business Overview

Honeywell ESS delivers process technologies and solutions that enable the energy evolution, enhance efficiency, and facilitate the transition to a low-carbon economy.

### UOP (Process Technologies)

#### Refining Technologies
- **Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC)**: Heavy oil upgrading
- **Hydroprocessing**: Sulfur removal, quality improvement
- **Alkylation**: High-octane gasoline production
- **Sulfur Recovery**: Environmental compliance

#### Petrochemical Technologies
- **Olefins Production**: Ethylene, propylene from multiple feedstocks
- **Aromatics Processing**: Benzene, toluene, xylene
- **Polymer Technologies**: Polypropylene, polyethylene processes
- **Key Processes**: Oleflex, C3/C4 technologies

#### Gas Processing
- **LNG**: Liquefaction and regasification
- **Natural Gas Treating**: Acid gas removal, dehydration
- **NGL Recovery**: Extraction of valuable liquids
- **Helium Recovery**: Specialized extraction systems

### Sustainability Technologies

#### Renewable Fuels
- **Ecofining™**: Renewable diesel and SAF production
  - Feedstocks: Waste fats, oils, greases
  - Drop-in replacement for conventional diesel/jet
  - Significant carbon reduction vs fossil fuels

#### Hydrogen Economy
- **Blue Hydrogen**: Steam methane reforming with carbon capture
- **Hydrogen Purification**: PSA technology for fuel-cell grade H2
- **Hydrogen Storage**: Liquid and compressed solutions
- **Transportation**: Pipeline and distribution systems

#### Carbon Capture & Storage
- **Solvent-based Capture**: Amine technology for post-combustion
- **Modular Systems**: Scalable from 50K to 1M+ tpy CO2
- **Integration**: With existing utility and process systems
- **Utilization**: CO2-to-products pathways

### Advanced Materials Spinoff

#### Solstice Advanced Materials (Spun off October 2024)
- Revenue: ~$3.8B (FY2024)
- EBITDA Margin: >25%
- Focus: Sustainability-focused specialty chemicals

#### Key Products
- **Solstice®**: Low-GWP hydrofluoro-olefin technology
  - 326M+ metric tons CO2 equivalent avoided
  - Refrigeration, blowing agents, propellants
  
- **Spectra®**: Ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene fiber
  - Ballistic protection, marine ropes
  - Law enforcement and military applications

- **Hydranal®**: Karl Fischer titration reagents
  - Water content measurement standard
  - Laboratory and industrial applications

- **Aclar®**: High-performance pharmaceutical packaging
  - Moisture barrier for drug stability
  - Healthcare packaging solutions
