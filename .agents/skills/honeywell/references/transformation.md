# Honeywell Three-Way Transformation

## Strategic Overview

In 2024-2025, Honeywell announced a comprehensive portfolio transformation to separate into three independent, industry-leading public companies, eliminating the conglomerate discount and unlocking shareholder value.

## The Three Entities

### Entity 1: Honeywell Aerospace Technologies

#### Profile
- **Revenue**: ~$16B (FY2024)
- **Margin**: ~26% segment margin
- **Focus**: Commercial aviation, defense, space

#### Spin-off Timeline
- **Announcement**: February 2025
- **Accelerated to**: Q3 2026 (from H2 2026)
- **Leadership**: Jim Currier (CEO of spun-off entity)
- **Structure**: Tax-free spin to Honeywell shareholders

#### Strategic Rationale
- Pure-play aerospace valuation multiple
- Focused capital allocation
- Agile strategic decision-making
- Direct access to aerospace investors

#### Key Strengths
- #1 position in business aviation
- CFM LEAP engine partnership
- Record backlog provides visibility
- Defense spending tailwinds

### Entity 2: Honeywell Automation

#### Profile
- **Revenue**: ~$22B (post-divestitures)
- **Margin**: ~21% segment margin (expansion opportunity)
- **Focus**: Building automation, industrial automation, ESS

#### Business Composition
- Building Automation (fire, security, controls)
- Industrial Automation (sensing, safety, core process)
- Energy & Sustainability Solutions (UOP, sustainability tech)

#### Strategic Position
- "Automation to Autonomy" leader
- Honeywell remains as this entity
- Simplified, coherent industrial technology focus
- Strong recurring revenue from connected assets

#### Divestiture Pipeline
- PSS and WWS under strategic review
- Process Automation exploring alternatives
- Post-divestiture: Cleaner, higher-margin profile

### Entity 3: Advanced Materials (Completed)

#### Solstice Advanced Materials
- **Spun Off**: October 2024
- **Revenue**: ~$3.8B
- **Margin**: >25% EBITDA
- **Focus**: Sustainability-driven specialty chemicals

#### Key Products
- Solstice® low-GWP technology
- Spectra® high-performance fiber
- Hydranal® analytical reagents
- Aclar® pharmaceutical packaging

### Entity 4: Quantinuum (IPO Planned)

#### Quantum Computing Pure-Play
- **Ownership**: 54% Honeywell (pre-IPO)
- **Valuation**: $10B
- **IPO Target**: 2026
- **Focus**: Commercial quantum computing

## Strategic Benefits

### For Aerospace
- Dedicated management focus
- Aerospace-specific investor base
- Flexible capital structure
- Competitive agility

### For Automation
- Coherent industrial technology story
- Cross-selling opportunities
- Unified "autonomy" vision
- Simplified operations

### For Shareholders
- Elimination of conglomerate discount
- Choice of exposure (aviation vs automation vs quantum)
- Potential multiple expansion
- Clearer investment thesis
