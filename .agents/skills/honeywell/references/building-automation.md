# Honeywell Building Automation

## Business Overview

Honeywell Building Automation transforms how buildings and infrastructure operate, serving millions of buildings worldwide with integrated solutions for fire, security, and building management.

### Core Solution Areas

#### Fire Safety
- **Detection**: Smoke, heat, and multi-criteria detectors
- **Alarm Systems**: Addressable notification and control panels
- **Suppression**: Clean agent, water mist, and foam systems
- **Key Brands**: NOTIFIER, Gent, ESSER, Fire-Lite

#### Security & Access
- **Access Control**: Card readers, biometrics, door controllers
- **Video Surveillance**: IP cameras, video management systems
- **Intrusion Detection**: Sensors, panels, monitoring
- **Key Brands**: Pro-Watch, MAXPRO, Galaxy

#### Building Management
- **HVAC Controls**: Thermostats, controllers, actuators
- **Energy Management**: Optimization software, demand response
- **Integration Platforms**: Enterprise Buildings Integrator (EBI)
- **Key Brands**: ComfortPoint, Spyder, TREND

#### Smart Buildings & IoT
- **Honeywell Forge for Buildings**: Cloud-based analytics platform
- **Digital Twins**: Virtual building models for optimization
- **Occupancy Analytics**: People-counting and space utilization
- **Predictive Maintenance**: AI-driven equipment monitoring
