# Honeywell Aerospace Technologies

## Business Overview

Honeywell Aerospace Technologies is the company's largest and most profitable segment, generating $15.5B in revenue (FY2024), representing 40% of total company sales.

### Financial Performance (FY2024)

| Metric | Value | Change |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Revenue | $15.5B | +13% YoY |
| Segment Profit | $4.0B | +6% YoY |
| Segment Margin | 25.8% | -180 bps (Bombardier agreement impact) |
| Organic Growth | 11% | Strong momentum |

### Key Product Lines

#### Engines & Power Systems
- **CFM LEAP**: Joint venture with GE; powers A320neo and 737 MAX families
- **HTF7000**: Business jet engine powering Challenger, Gulfstream, Falcon
- **TFE731**: Legendary business jet engine with 100M+ flight hours
- **T55**: Helicopter engine powering CH-47 Chinook

#### Avionics & Connectivity
- **Primus Epic**: Integrated avionics suite for business aviation
- **Honeywell Anthem**: Next-generation connected flight deck
- **JetWave**: In-flight connectivity solution for global broadband
- **SmartRunway/SmartLanding**: Safety-enhancing runway awareness systems

#### Mechanical Systems
- **Wheels & Brakes**: #1 in business aviation, carbon brake technology
- **Environmental Control Systems**: Cabin pressure, temperature management
- **Flight Controls**: Primary and secondary flight control systems

#### Defense & Space
- **Navigation/GPS**: Precise positioning for military applications
- **Guidance Systems**: Missile and munitions guidance
- **SATCOM**: Military satellite communications
- **Space Systems**: Components for launch vehicles and satellites

### Strategic Initiatives

#### Bombardier Partnership (2024)
- $17B lifetime value agreement
- Covers avionics, propulsion, and satellite communications
- Strengthens business aviation market position

#### Urban Air Mobility
- Electric and hybrid-electric propulsion systems
- Vertical lift technologies for eVTOL aircraft
- Autonomous flight systems development

#### Q3 2026 Spin-off
- Will become standalone publicly-traded company
- Jim Currier to serve as CEO of spun-off entity
- Enables focused capital allocation and strategic agility

### Competitive Position
- #1 in business aviation engines and avionics
- Major supplier to Boeing and Airbus (commercial)
- Key Pentagon supplier (defense)
- Leading installed base creates aftermarket annuity

### Market Outlook
- Commercial aerospace recovery continuing
- Defense spending growth driven by geopolitics
- Urban air mobility emerging opportunity
- Aftermarket services growth from aging fleets
