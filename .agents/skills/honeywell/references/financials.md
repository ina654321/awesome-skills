## Capital Allocation Strategy

### Priorities (in order)
1. **Organic Growth**: R&D, capex for growth initiatives
2. **Dividends**: Consistent, growing dividend (50+ years of increases)
3. **M&A**: Bolt-on acquisitions ($25B commitment through 2025)
4. **Share Buybacks**: Opportunistic repurchases
5. **Balance Sheet**: Maintain investment grade rating

### M&A Criteria
- Strategic fit with megatrends
- Accretive to growth and margins
- Cultural compatibility
- Clear integration plan
- ROIC > WACC
