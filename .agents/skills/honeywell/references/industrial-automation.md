# Honeywell Industrial Automation

## Business Overview

Honeywell Industrial Automation provides the fundamental building blocks of industrial automation, including sensing technologies, safety solutions, process control, and productivity systems.

| Metric | Value | Change |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Revenue | $10.1B | -7% YoY |

*Note: Performance impacted by short-cycle industrial softness*

### Business Segments

#### Sensing & Safety Technologies
- **Gas Detection**: Fixed and portable gas monitors
  - Brands: Sensepoint, BW Solo, BW Ultra
  - Applications: Industrial safety, environmental monitoring
  
- **Personal Protective Equipment**: Fall protection, respiratory
  - Brands: Miller, Sperian
  - Applications: Construction, oil & gas, utilities

- **Sensors**: Pressure, temperature, position, force
  - Applications: Automotive, medical, industrial
  - Growing healthcare sensor demand

#### Process Solutions
- **Distributed Control Systems (DCS)**: Experion PKS
- **Safety Systems**: Safety Manager, fail-safe controls
- **Advanced Process Control**: Profit Suite optimization
- **Instrumentation**: Field devices, analyzers

#### Warehouse & Productivity Solutions
- **Barcode Scanning**: Handheld and fixed scanners
- **Mobile Computers**: Rugged handhelds for field operations
- **Printers**: Industrial label and receipt printers
- **Software**: Workflow optimization, asset tracking
