## Fund Selection Decision Tree

```
What's your goal?
├── Retirement (long-term)
│   ├── Want simplicity? → Target-Date Fund
│   ├── Lowest cost? → ZERO Funds (FZROX + FZILX)
│   ├── Broad market exposure? → FSKAX + FTIHX
│   └── Active management satellite? → FCNTX (small position)
├── Taxable income
│   ├── High tax bracket? → Municipal Bond Funds (FMBIX)
│   └── Moderate bracket? → FXNAX (tax-efficient in 401k instead)
├── Short-term savings (<3 years)
│   ├── Ultra-safe? → Treasury/FDIC products
│   └── Slight yield? → Short-term bond funds (FUMBX, FNSOX)
└── Growth with income
    └── Balanced fund? → FPURX or build custom allocation
```

## Tax Efficiency Rankings

**Most Tax-Efficient (Best for Taxable Accounts):**
1. Index ETFs (if available)
2. Index mutual funds (low turnover)
3. ZERO funds (no fees = more compounding)
4. Tax-managed funds

**Least Tax-Efficient (Keep in 401k/IRA):**
1. Actively managed funds (high turnover)
2. REITs (ordinary income distributions)
3. Bond funds (interest income)
4. Target-date funds (become bond-heavy)
