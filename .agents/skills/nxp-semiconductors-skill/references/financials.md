## Investment Thesis Summary

### Bull Case
1. **Automotive semiconductor content growth:** EVs have 2-3x semiconductor content vs. ICE
2. **SDV transition:** NXP leading with CoreRide platform
3. **Market share:** #1/#2 position in automotive MCUs with strong moat
4. **Margin expansion:** Operating leverage as revenue recovers

### Bear Case
1. **Chinese competition:** Domestic suppliers taking share in largest auto market
2. **CEO transition:** Uncertainty around new leadership
3. **Auto cycle risk:** Potential recession affecting vehicle demand
4. **Valuation:** Premium multiple vulnerable to estimate cuts
