# Lam Research - Product Portfolio Reference

## Overview

Lam Research's product portfolio spans three core wafer fabrication processes: **Etch**, **Deposition**, and **Clean**. The company provides the industry's most comprehensive and differentiated solutions, with particular strength in high-aspect-ratio (HAR) etch and atomic layer deposition (ALD).

---

## Etch Systems

### 1. Sense.i® Platform - Dielectric Etch

**Description:** Lam's most advanced dielectric etch platform featuring Equipment Intelligence® for automated process control and predictive maintenance.

**Key Technologies:**
- **Lam Cryo™ 3.0**: Cryogenic etch for straighter profiles with 40% energy reduction
- **Vantex™**: High-aspect-ratio dielectric etch capability
- **Equipment Intelligence®**: AI/ML-driven process optimization

**Applications:**
- 3D NAND channel hole etch (200+ layers)
- DRAM capacitor etch
- Logic spacer etch
- Trench etch for isolation

**Specifications:**
- Process uniformity: <3% 1-sigma
- Etch rate: Up to 10 μm/min for HAR applications
- Aspect ratio capability: >80:1
- Wafer size: 300mm

**Market Position:**
- Leading share in 3D NAND HAR dielectric etch
- Growing adoption in advanced DRAM
- >10,000 chambers installed globally

### 2. Akara® - Conductor Etch

**Description:** Revolutionary conductor etch system with industry-first DirectDrive® solid-state plasma source. The most advanced conductor etch tool available.

**Key Technologies:**
- **DirectDrive®**: Solid-state RF generator with 100x faster plasma response
- **TEMPO™**: Plasma pulsing for etch selectivity and microloading control
- **SNAP™**: Ion energy control for atomic precision

**Applications:**
- GAA (Gate-All-Around) transistor nanosheet formation
- Advanced DRAM cell etch (4F2, 6F2)
- Logic contact and via etch
- Future: CFET (Complementary FET) structures

**Specifications:**
- Plasma response time: Milliseconds
- CD uniformity: Angstrom-level control
- Selectivity: >50:1 (SiGe:Si for GAA)
- Endpoint detection: Real-time optical emission

**Market Position:**
- Production tool of record at leading foundries
- Validated for 2nm GAA production
- Fastest growing new product introduction

## Deposition Systems

### 1. ALTUS® Halo - Molybdenum ALD

**Description:** World's first production molybdenum ALD system. Revolutionary metallization solution for advanced nodes.

**Key Technologies:**
- **Novel precursor chemistry**: Proprietary molybdenum delivery
- **Film purity**: <1% contamination
- **Low resistivity**: 50% lower than tungsten at scaled dimensions

**Applications:**
- 2nm GAA logic contact metallization
- Advanced DRAM word lines
- Future: 3D DRAM gate metallization

**Specifications:**
- Film thickness uniformity: <2% 1-sigma
- Resistivity: <15 μΩ-cm
- Step coverage: >95% on HAR structures

**Market Position:**
- First-to-market molybdenum ALD
- >70% share in logic contact metallization
- Critical for sub-20nm contact scaling

---

### 2. Striker® - Atomic Layer Deposition

**Key Technologies:**

- **QSM architecture**: Unique chamber design for multistep sequencing
- **Sub-atmospheric processing**: Enhanced step coverage

**Applications:**
- DRAM capacitor dielectric
- 3D NAND stack formation
- Spacer formation for patterning
- Gapfill for isolation structures

**Variants:**
- Striker FE: Front-end dielectric applications
- Striker SPARC: Low-k spacer applications

**Market Position:**
- Leading solution for DRAM gapfill
- Growing adoption in 3D NAND

---

### 3. VECTOR® - PECVD and Carbon Gapfill

**Description:** Plasma-enhanced CVD platform for dielectric films and carbon gapfill.

**Key Technologies:**
- **TEOS 3D**: Advanced TEOS deposition

**Applications:**
