# Lowe's Total Home Strategy (2025)

## Strategic Vision

"As we look ahead to the expected recovery in home improvement, we are making investments to position the company for long-term growth. We are evolving our Total Home strategy to help solve our customers' total home improvement needs with more value and exceptional service."

— Marvin R. Ellison, Chairman, President and CEO

## Five Growth Initiatives

### 1. Drive Pro Penetration

**Current State:** 30% of sales from Pro customers (up from 25%)

**Key Initiatives:**
- MyLowe's Pro Rewards relaunch (2025)
- Pro Extended Aisle for special orders
- Artisan Design Group acquisition ($1.33B)
- Foundation Building Materials acquisition ($8.8B)

**Target:** Increase Pro mix to 35%+

---

### 2. Accelerate Online Sales

**Key Initiatives:**
- First product marketplace in U.S. home improvement
- AI-powered search and recommendations (NVIDIA, OpenAI, Palantir partnerships)
- BOPIS optimization
- Same-day delivery expansion

**Target:** Double-digit online growth

---

### 3. Expand Home Services

**Key Initiatives:**
- Installation services across categories
- Protection plans and extended warranties
- Pro network for jobsite execution
- Design services through Artisan acquisition

**Target:** Services as percentage of sales growth

---

### 4. Create a Loyalty Ecosystem

**MyLowe's Rewards (DIY):**
- Three tiers: Bronze Key, Silver Key, Gold Key
- Points toward MyLowe's Money ($5 per 500 points)
- Free standard shipping at Silver Key+
- Member-only gifts and deals

**MyLowe's Pro Rewards:**
- Unified currency with DIY program
- Platinum Pro status with credit card
- Business tools and volume discounts
- Target: Drive 10-20 additional trips per year

**Digital Home Platform:**
- Personalized home maintenance
- Product registration and tracking
- How-to guides and support
- "Digital twin for your home"

---

### 5. Increase Space Productivity

**Key Initiatives:**
- Store-by-store localization
- Category space optimization
- 10-15 new stores annually in growth markets
- Rural assortment expansion (150 additional stores)

**Target:** $500+ sales per sq ft

---

## Technology Enablement
