# Seasonal Planning Calendar

## Annual Rhythm

### Q1: Spring Readiness (February - April)

**Key Events:**
- Spring Black Friday (April)
- Lawn & garden reset
- Outdoor power equipment launch

**Focus Categories:**
- Lawn & garden (mulch, soil, plants)
- Outdoor power (mowers, trimmers, blowers)
- Patio furniture (early sets)
- Paint (exterior)

**Operational Priorities:**
- Seasonal hiring (500+ associates)
- Garden center readiness
- Inventory pre-positioning
- Live goods supplier coordination

---

### Q2: Peak Outdoor (May - July)

**Key Events:**
- Memorial Day (grills, patio)

- July 4th (outdoor living)

**Focus Categories:**
- Grills & outdoor cooking
- Patio furniture
- Pools & recreation
- Lawn maintenance

**Operational Priorities:**
- Peak staffing
- Hot weather product flow
- Back-to-school early prep

---

### Q3: Transition & Prep (August - October)

**Focus Categories:**
- Storage & organization

**Operational Priorities:**

### Q4: Holiday & Indoor (November - January)

**Focus Categories:**
- Holiday decor

## Weather Contingencies

### Spring (Lawn & Garden)
- **Early Warm:** Accelerate live goods receipt
- **Late Freeze:** Delay plant shipments, pivot to hardgoods
- **Wet Season:** Emphasize indoor projects, storage solutions

### Summer (Outdoor)
- **Heat Waves:** Focus on cooling, pools, shade
- **Cool/Wet:** Pivot to indoor projects, early fall prep

### Fall (Weatherization)
- **Extended Warm:** Delay winter product set
- **Early Cold:** Accelerate heating, insulation sales

### Winter (Indoor)
- **Mild Winter:** Emphasize indoor projects, organization
- **Severe Winter:** Storm response, emergency supplies
