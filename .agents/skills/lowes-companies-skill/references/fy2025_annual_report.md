# Lowe's FY2025 Annual Report Summary

## Financial Highlights

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Net Sales | $86.29B | $83.67B | +3.1% |
| Net Earnings | $6.65B | $6.96B | -4.4% |
| Diluted EPS | $11.78 | $12.10 | -2.6% |
| Comparable Sales | +1.3% | -3.1% | Improvement |
| Gross Margin | 33.2% | 33.4% | -20 bps |
| Operating Margin | 12.3% | 12.5% | -20 bps |

## Fourth Quarter 2025 Performance

- Net Sales: $20.6B (vs. $18.6B prior year quarter)
- Comparable Sales: +1.3%
- Diluted EPS: $1.78 ($1.98 adjusted)
- Adjusted EPS increased 2.6% YoY

## Key Business Drivers

### Growth Initiatives
1. **Pro Penetration:** Now at 30% of sales, up from 25%
2. **Online Sales:** Continued double-digit growth
3. **Home Services:** Expanding installation capabilities
4. **Loyalty Ecosystem:** MyLowe's Rewards and MyLowe's Pro Rewards
5. **Space Productivity:** Store-by-store optimization

### Capital Allocation
- **Dividends:** $673M in Q4, $2.6B for the year
- **Share Repurchases:** Ongoing program
- **Acquisitions:** Artisan Design Group ($1.33B), Foundation Building Materials ($8.8B)

## 2026 Outlook

- Sales: $86B - $88B
- Comparable Sales: Flat to +2%
- Operating Margin: 12.3% - 12.5%
- Diluted EPS: $12.15 - $12.40

## CEO Commentary (Marvin R. Ellison)

"We delivered strong results this quarter, as our Total Home strategy is resonating with both our Pro and DIY customers. While the housing macro remains pressured, we are focused on directing what is within our control, which includes our ongoing productivity initiatives. We remain confident that we are well-positioned to take share regardless of the macro environment."

## Operational Metrics

- **Stores:** 1,759 (196M sq ft selling space)
- **Distribution Centers:** 130+
- **Employees:** 300,000+
- **Customer Transactions:** ~16 million per week
