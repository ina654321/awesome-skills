# Pricing Strategy

## Strategic Framework

### Price Image Priorities

1. **KVI Leadership:** Win on items customers compare
2. **Margin Recovery:** Offset KVI investments in non-competitive items
3. **Zone Optimization:** Tailor to local competitive dynamics
4. **Promotional Discipline:** Drive traffic without margin erosion

## KVI (Known Value Items) Program

### Selection Criteria

**Characteristics:**
- High purchase frequency
- Cross-shopped vs. competitors
- Easy price comparison
- Significant volume impact

**Examples:**
- 2x4 lumber (common sizes)
- Premium paint gallons
- Top-selling power tools
- Major appliance brands

### Pricing Rules

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| HD price match | Match + 5% |
| HD lower promotion | Evaluate response based on volume |
| No local competition | Maintain target margin |
| New market entry | Aggressive introductory pricing |

## Price Zones

### Zone 1: Competitive Intensity
- Markets with Home Depot, Menards
- High price transparency
- Strategy: Aggressive KVIs, competitive basket

### Zone 2: Moderate Competition
- Some competition, less density
- Strategy: Balanced approach

### Zone 3: Limited Competition
- Rural or underserved markets
- Strategy: Margin optimization

## Promotional Pricing

### Event Types

| Event | Timing | Strategy |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Spring Black Friday | April | Traffic driver, lawn & garden |
| Memorial Day | May | Outdoor seasonal |
| Labor Day | Sept | Tools, storage |
| Black Friday | Nov | Holiday, tools, appliances |

### Promotional Mechanics

- **Dollar Off:** Clear value communication
- **Percent Off:** Flexibility across categories
- **Buy X Get Y:** Basket building
- **Rebates:** Price perception without immediate cost
- **Credit Card:** 5% everyday (financed by card economics)

## Margin Management

### Gross Margin Bridge

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Cost of goods | Supplier negotiations, tariffs |
| Mix shift | Private brand penetration |
| Shrink | Inventory management |
| Markdowns | Promotional discipline |

### Margin Recovery Strategies

1. **Private Brand Expansion:** Higher margin alternatives
2. **Services Attachment:** Installation, protection plans
3. **Mix Management:** Promote higher-margin categories
4. **Shrink Reduction:** Technology, process improvement
