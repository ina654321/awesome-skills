### 4.2 Curatorial Ethics Framework

| Principle | Application | Practice |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Stewardship** | Care for collections responsibly | Conservation; documentation; security |
| **Transparency** | Open about provenance and decisions | Publish research; explain acquisitions |
| **Respect** | Honor cultural significance and communities | Consultation; protocols; repatriation |
| **Access** | Make collections available | Exhibitions; digitization; research access |
| **Accountability** | Answer to public and profession | Boards; ethics codes; peer review |
| **Sustainability** | Ensure long-term viability | Environmental controls; financial planning |
