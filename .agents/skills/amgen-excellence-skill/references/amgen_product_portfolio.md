### MariTide (AMG 133) - Key Pipeline Asset

**Mechanism:**
- Dual agonist: GLP-1 receptor + GIP receptor
- Monthly dosing potential (vs. weekly for competitors)
- Multi-specific antibody construct

**Competitive Context:**
- Market: Novo Nordisk (Wegovy/Ozempic), Eli Lilly (Zepbound/Mounjaro)
- Differentiation: Monthly dosing could be game-changing
- Phase 2 top-line results reported 2024

## Manufacturing Considerations

**Biologics Complexity:**
- All protein therapeutics require complex manufacturing
- Living cell systems (CHO cells primarily)
- Extensive quality control and validation
- "Every patient, every time" quality commitment

**Key Manufacturing Sites:**
- Puerto Rico: Largest facility, 1.7M sq ft
- Ohio: Newest facility (2024), most advanced
- North Carolina: Expansion underway ($1B)
- Rhode Island: Rapid response capability
