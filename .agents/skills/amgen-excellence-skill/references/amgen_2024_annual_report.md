# Amgen 2024 Annual Report Summary

**Document Date:** February 2025  
**Reporting Period:** Full Year 2024  
**Source:** Amgen Inc. SEC Filings and Earnings Release

---

## Executive Summary

Amgen delivered strong results in 2024, with total revenues growing 19% to a record **$33.4 billion**, driven by 23% volume growth. The company exited 2024 with **14 blockbuster products** (annualized run rate over $1 billion) and record sales across 21 products.

---

## Financial Highlights

### Revenue Performance

| Metric | 2024 | 2023 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| **Total Revenues** | $33.4B | $28.2B | +19% |
| **Product Sales** | $32.0B | $26.9B | +19% |
| **Volume Growth** | +23% | - | - |
| **Price Impact** | -2% | - | - |
| **Horizon Contribution** | $4.2B | $954M | +340% |

### Quarterly Breakdown

| Quarter | Revenue | YoY Growth |
|---------|---------|------------|
| Q1 2024 | $7.4B | +22% |
| Q2 2024 | $8.4B | +20% |
| Q3 2024 | $8.5B | +23% |
| Q4 2024 | $9.1B | +11% |

### Earnings Performance

| Metric | 2024 | 2023 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| **GAAP EPS** | $7.56 | $12.49 | -39% |
| **Non-GAAP EPS** | $19.84 | $18.65 | +6% |
| **Non-GAAP Operating Income** | $15.0B | $13.4B | +12% |
| **Non-GAAP Operating Margin** | 46.9% | 49.8% | -290 bps |

**Note:** GAAP EPS decline driven by Horizon acquisition-related amortization and mark-to-market investment losses.

### Cash Flow

| Metric | 2024 | 2023 |
|--------|------|------|

| **Dividend Per Share** | Increased 6% | 13th consecutive annual increase |

## Strategic Priorities

### 1. Pipeline Advancement
- **$6.0 billion** invested in R&D (up 25% from prior year)
- 5 Phase 3 data readouts in 2024
- Key approvals: BLINCYTO (B-ALL), IMDELLTRA (ES-SCLC)

### 2. Manufacturing Expansion
- **Ohio facility opened** (February 2024) - $950M investment
- **North Carolina expansion** announced - $1B investment
- State-of-the-art automation and digital capabilities

### 3. Horizon Integration
- $4.2B revenue contribution in 2024
- Integration ongoing with FTC consent compliance
- Rare disease capabilities enhancing portfolio

### 4. Obesity Pipeline (MariTide)
- Phase 2 top-line results reported 2024
- Phase 3 initiation planned
- Potential monthly dosing advantage
