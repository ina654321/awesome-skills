# MetLife International Operations

## Global Overview

MetLife operates in more than 40 markets worldwide, with leading positions in the United States, Latin America, Asia, Europe, and the Middle East. International operations are a core component of MetLife's diversification strategy and growth engine.

## Geographic Segments

### Asia Segment

**Market Position:**
- Leading life insurer in multiple markets
- Strong agency distribution network
- Focus on savings-linked protection products

**Key Markets:**

| Market | Position | Products |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Japan** | Major market presence | Life, health, annuities, group |
| **South Korea** | Strong market position | Life, health, retirement |
| **India** | Growing presence | Life insurance (via joint venture) |
| **China** | Strategic presence | Life insurance, accident & health |
| **Hong Kong** | Established market | Life, health, MPF (Mandatory Provident Fund) |
| **Vietnam** | Emerging market | Life insurance |

**Financial Performance (2024):**
- Adjusted Earnings: $443 million (Q4); up 50% YoY
- Adjusted Premiums, Fees & Other Revenues: $1.6 billion
- General Account AUM: $130 billion
- Sales: $513 million (Q4)

**Strategic Focus:**
- Middle-class wealth accumulation products
- Protection gap solutions
- Digital transformation
- Agency productivity enhancement

## Cultural Adaptation

### Local Market Considerations

**Product Customization:**
- Regulatory requirements by market
- Cultural preferences for savings vs. protection
- Local currency considerations
- Distribution channel preferences

**Examples:**
- **Japan:** Focus on savings-type products; aging population drives annuities
- **Korea:** High digital adoption; innovative product features
- **Chile:** AFP (pension fund) integration; encaje requirements
- **Mexico:** Growing middle class; protection gap focus
