# MetLife Agricultural Lending

## Overview

MetLife Investment Management (MIM) has been a leading provider of agricultural financing for over 100 years. The Agricultural Finance Group is the largest non-government sponsored enterprise (non-GSE) agricultural mortgage lender in the United States.

## Portfolio Scale

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Agricultural Mortgage Portfolio** | $22.8 billion |
| **Status** | Largest non-GSE ag lender in U.S. |

| **2019 Originations** | $4.3 billion globally |

**Key Considerations:**
| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|------------|

## Strategic Rationale

### Asset-Liability Matching

**Why Agricultural Lending Fits MetLife:**

| Insurance Liability Characteristic | Agricultural Loan Match |
|-----------------------------------|------------------------|
| Long duration (30+ years) | Long-term mortgages (up to 30 years) |
| Stable, predictable cash flows | Agricultural land income streams |
| Inflation sensitivity | Commodity-linked land values |
| Low correlation to financial markets | Real asset collateral |
| ESG-conscious policyholders | Sustainable farming practices |

**Portfolio Benefits:**
- Diversification from traditional fixed income
- Illiquidity premium
- Tangible asset backing
- Inflation hedging characteristics
- Relationship-based lending premium

### Competitive Advantages

**MetLife Differentiation:**
1. **Longevity:** 100+ years of agricultural lending experience
2. **Scale:** Largest non-GSE agricultural lender
3. **Relationships:** Multi-decade, multi-generational borrower relationships
4. **Flexibility:** Customized structures vs. standardized bank products
5. **Certainty of Execution:** Streamlined closing process
6. **Balance Sheet Strength:** Long-term capital availability
