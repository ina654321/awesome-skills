# Regulatory Framework

## FDA Regulations

### Drug Development Pathway

| Phase | Objective | Duration | Key Deliverables |
|-------|-----------|----------|------------------|
| **IND** | Safety in humans | 1-2 years | CMC, toxicology, protocol |
| **Phase I** | Safety, PK/PD | 1-2 years | MTD, dose-ranging |
| **Phase II** | Efficacy signal | 2-3 years | Proof of concept |
| **Phase III** | Confirmatory | 3-5 years | Pivotal efficacy |
| **NDA/BLA** | Approval | 6-12 months | Integrated dossier |

### Key FDA Guidance Documents

- **ICH E6(R2)**: Good Clinical Practice
- **ICH Q8-Q12**: Pharmaceutical Quality
- **FDA Guidance for Industry**: Expedited Programs (Breakthrough, Fast Track, Priority Review)
- **CGT-Specific**: Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) Information for Gene Therapy INDs

## EMA Regulations

### Centralized Procedure
- Mandatory for biotechnology products
- Single application for all EU member states
- CHMP opinion → European Commission decision

### Key EMA Guidelines
- **GMP Annex 1**: Manufacture of Sterile Medicinal Products
- **ATMP Regulation**: Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products (CGT)

| Principle | Definition | Application |

## Cell & Gene Therapy Specifics

### RMAT Designation (Regenerative Medicine Advanced Therapy)
- FDA program for serious conditions
- Benefits: Early FDA engagement, priority review, accelerated approval
- Eligibility: Preliminary clinical evidence

### EMA ATMP Classification
1. **Gene Therapy Medicinal Products (GTMP)**
2. **Somatic Cell Therapy Medicinal Products (sCTMP)**
3. **Tissue Engineered Products (TEP)**
4. **Combined ATMPs**

### Post-Marketing Requirements
- **Pharmacovigilance**: 15-day expedited reports (serious, unexpected)
- **Periodic Safety Update Reports (PSURs)**
- **Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategies (REMS)**
- **Long-term follow-up studies** (CGT: up to 15 years)

## Digital Health Regulatory

### Software as Medical Device (SaMD)
- **Class I**: Low risk
- **Class II**: Moderate risk (510(k) pathway)
- **Class III**: High risk (PMA pathway)

### Predetermined Change Control Plans (PCCP)
- Pre-approved AI/ML model updates
- Real-world performance monitoring
- Continual learning within guardrails
