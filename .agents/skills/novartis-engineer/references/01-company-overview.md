# Novartis Company Overview

## Corporate Profile

**Novartis AG** is a Swiss multinational pharmaceutical corporation headquartered in Basel, Switzerland. It is one of the largest pharmaceutical companies in the world by market capitalization and revenue.

### Key Statistics (2024)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Revenue** | $47.8 billion (continuing operations) |
| **Net Income** | $11.9 billion |
| **R&D Spending** | $9.9 billion (21% of net sales) |
| **Employees** | 76,000+ |
| **Countries** | 100+ |
| **Market Cap** | ~$200 billion |
| **Dividend** | CHF 3.50 per share (+6% YoY) |

### Historical Context

- **Founded**: 1996 (merger of Ciba-Geigy and Sandoz)
- **Sandoz Spin-off**: October 3, 2023 (completed)
- **Current Focus**: Innovative medicines only (post-Sandoz)

### CEO: Vas Narasimhan, M.D.

- **Position**: Chief Executive Officer
- **Since**: February 1, 2018
- **Background**: Former Global Head of Drug Development and Chief Medical Officer
- **Education**: M.D. from Harvard Medical School, MBA from Harvard Business School
- **Compensation**: CHF 14.2M total (2024)

### Strategic Priorities

1. **5 Technology Platforms**:
   - Chemistry (small molecules)
   - Biotherapeutics (proteins, antibodies)
   - Cell & Gene Therapy (Kymriah, Zolgensma)
   - Radioligand Therapy (Pluvicto, Lutathera)
   - xRNA (siRNA, mRNA)

2. **7 Therapeutic Areas**:
   - Cardiovascular-Renal-Metabolic
   - Immunology
   - Neuroscience
   - Oncology
   - Hematology
   - Rare Disease
   - GI

3. **Digital Transformation**:
   - AI/ML integration across R&D
   - Dataiku platform for citizen data science
   - $23B US manufacturing investment (2025-2030)
