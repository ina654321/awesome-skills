## Section Control ROI

### Planter Section Control

**Scenario:** 1,200 acre irregular fields with point rows

**Without Section Control:**
- 8% overlap on headlands and point rows
- 96 acres of double-planting
- 96 acres × $120/acre seed cost = $11,520 waste

**With Section Control ($3,500 investment):**
- 1% residual overlap
- 12 acres of double-planting
- Savings: $10,080/year

**Payback Period:** 0.35 years (4.2 months)

### Sprayer Section Control

**Scenario:** 2,500 acres, irregular field boundaries
