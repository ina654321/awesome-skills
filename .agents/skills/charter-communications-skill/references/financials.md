|----------|-------------|----------|
| YouTube TV | 8M+ | #1 vMVPD |
| Comcast | 13M | #1 traditional |
| Charter | 12.6M | #2 traditional |
| DIRECTV | 11M | Declining |

### Competitive Moats

1. **Network Scale**: 2nd largest cable footprint
2. **Passing Density**: Urban/suburban focus
3. **Bundle Economics**: Internet + Mobile synergy
4. **WiFi Hotspots**: 30M+ access points
5. **Rural Expansion**: Government-subsidized builds

### Threats

1. **Fiber Overbuild**: AT&T, Verizon expansion
2. **Fixed Wireless**: T-Mobile, Verizon 5G Home
3. **Satellite**: Starlink rural competition
4. **Cord Cutting**: Video revenue decline
5. **Regulation**: Net neutrality, franchise fees
