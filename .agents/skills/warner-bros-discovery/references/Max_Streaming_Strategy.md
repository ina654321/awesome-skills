# Max Streaming Strategy

## Overview

Max (formerly HBO Max) is Warner Bros. Discovery's flagship streaming platform, combining HBO's premium scripted content with Warner Bros.' film library and Discovery's unscripted programming. As of Q4 2025, Max has 131.6 million global subscribers.

## Subscriber Growth

| Quarter | Subscribers (M) | Growth |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Q2 2022 | 92.1 | - |
| Q4 2022 | 96.2 | +4% |
| Q4 2023 | 97.7 | +2% |
| Q4 2024 | 116.9 | +20% |
| Q4 2025 | 131.6 | +13% |

**Targets:**
- 150M subscribers by end of 2026
- Available in 100+ markets globally

## Product Features

### Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price (US) | Features |
|------|------------|----------|
| Max Ad-Lite | $9.99/mo | 1080p, 2 concurrent streams, ads |
| Max | $16.99/mo | 4K, 4 streams, no ads, download |
| Max Ultimate | $20.99/mo | 4K HDR, 100+ offline downloads |

### Key Features
- **4K UHD**: Available on select titles
- **Dolby Vision/Atmos**: Premium audio/visual
- **Offline Downloads**: Up to 100 titles (Ultimate tier)
- **Multiple Profiles**: 5 profiles per account
- **Kids Profile**: Curated safe content

## Technology Platform

### Streaming Infrastructure
- **CDN**: Multi-CDN strategy (Akamai, CloudFront, Fastly)
- **Encoding**: HEVC/H.265 for 4K, AVC/H.264 for HD
- **DRM**: Widevine, FairPlay, PlayReady

### Personalization
- ML-powered recommendation engine
- Viewing history across devices
- Content discovery: Editorial + algorithmic

## Competitive Positioning

### vs. Netflix
- **Content**: Premium quality over quantity
- **Price**: Premium positioning ($16.99 vs $15.49)
- **Differentiation**: HBO prestige + Warner Bros. day-and-date

### vs. Disney+
- **Content**: Adult-focused vs family-focused
- **IP**: DC vs Marvel competition
- **Bundle**: No ESPN integration vs Disney bundle

### vs. Amazon Prime Video
- **Content**: Curated vs voluminous
- **Model**: Subscription-only vs bundle with shipping
- **Quality**: Prestige focus vs broad appeal

## Strategic Priorities 2025-2026

1. **Global Scale**: 150M subscribers by end of 2026
2. **Profitability Growth**: Target $2B+ Adjusted EBITDA
3. **Content Efficiency**: ROI focus on every original
4. **Technology**: Enhanced personalization, reduced churn
5. **Ad-Supported**: Global expansion of ad tier

## Key Performance Indicators

- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- Churn Rate (target: <5% monthly)
- Content Engagement (hours viewed)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
