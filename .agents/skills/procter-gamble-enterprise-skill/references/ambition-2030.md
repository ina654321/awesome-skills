# P&G Ambition 2030 Sustainability Strategy

## Overview

**Ambition 2030** is P&G's comprehensive sustainability strategy with commitments across Climate, Water, Waste, and Nature, anchored by a **net zero greenhouse gas emissions goal by 2040**.

## Climate Commitments

### Net Zero by 2040

**Scope 1 & 2 (Operations):**
| Target | Timeline | Progress |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 50% reduction vs. 2010 | 2030 | 60% achieved |
| 100% renewable electricity | 2030 | 99% achieved globally |
| Net zero emissions | 2040 | On track |

**Scope 3 (Supply Chain):**
| Target | Timeline | Progress |
|--------|----------|----------|
| 40% reduction per unit vs. 2020 | 2030 | 9% achieved |
| Net zero | 2040 | Supplier engagement underway |

### Key Initiatives

**Renewable Energy:**
- 99% renewable electricity globally
- Solar and wind PPAs across operations
- On-site generation where feasible
- 16 countries at 100% renewable electricity

**Manufacturing Efficiency:**
- Energy optimization programs
- Process improvements
- Equipment upgrades
- Real-time monitoring

**Supplier Engagement:**
- Climate Unlock Program (2023)
- Supplier climate progress tracking
- Education and resources
- Manufacture 2030 partnership

## Water Stewardship

### 2030 Goals

| Target | Status |
|--------|--------|
| 35% improvement in production water efficiency vs. 2010 | 26% achieved |
| Water restoration in 18 water-stressed areas | In progress |
| 100% wastewater recycled or treated | On track |

### Key Initiatives

**Production Efficiency:**
- Closed-loop cooling systems
- Water recycling technologies
- Dry processing where possible
- Real-time water monitoring

**Water Restoration:**
- Watershed protection partnerships
- Community water access projects
- 3.49 billion liters recycled in FY2024

**Consumer Use:**
- Tide Cold Water: 90% energy reduction
- Fairy #SwitchtoShort: Shorter dishwasher cycles
- Cascade: Efficient dishwasher products

## Waste Reduction

### 2030 Goals

| Target | Status |
|--------|--------|
| 100% recyclable or reusable packaging | 80%+ achieved |

### Packaging Innovation

**Reduce:**
- Concentrated formulas (less packaging)
- Lightweighting
- Right-sizing

**Recycle:**
- Recyclable by design
- Recycled content (rPET expansion)
- Partnerships with recyclers

**Reuse:**
- Refill systems (Mr. Clean, etc.)
- Reusable packaging pilots
- Concentrate/refill models

**Replace:**
- Paper packaging (Tide evo)
- Alternative materials
- Plant-based plastics
