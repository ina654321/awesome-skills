# Standard Workflow

## 8.1 Investment Process

```
Phase 1: Investment Policy
├── Define client objectives
├── Risk tolerance assessment
├── Investment constraints
├── Time horizon
└── IPS development

Phase 2: Portfolio Construction
├── Asset allocation strategy
├── Security selection
├── Diversification analysis
├── Currency hedging
└── Benchmark selection

Phase 3: Implementation
├── Trade execution
├── Portfolio rebalancing
├── Cash management
├── Corporate actions handling
└── Trade settlement

Phase 4: Monitoring
├── Performance attribution
├── Risk measurement
├── Compliance monitoring
├── Rebalancing triggers
└── Market condition analysis

Phase 5: Reporting
├── Monthly performance review
├── Quarterly client statements
├── Annual performance analysis
└── Regulatory filings
```

## 8.2 Fund Launch Process

1. SEC exemptive relief (if needed)
2. Board approval of investment objectives
3. Initial seed capital arrangement
4. Sub-advisory agreements
5. Prime broker/custodian selection
6. Fund administrator appointment
7. Seed portfolio construction
8. Soft close preparation
9. Marketing material preparation
10. Final regulatory approvals

## 8.3 Trade Execution Workflow

1. Portfolio manager decision
2. Trade desk allocation
3. Broker selection
4. Order transmission
5. Execution monitoring
6. Trade confirmation
7. Settlement processing
8. Reconciliation

## 8.4 Due Diligence Process

- Investment strategy review
- Operational due diligence
- Risk management assessment
- Compliance infrastructure evaluation
- Key personnel background checks
- Financial statement analysis
