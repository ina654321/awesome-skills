# Sustainability: 100% EV by 2030

## The Commitment

### Announcement
- **Date**: June 2020
- **Goal**: 100% electric vehicles on Lyft platform by 2030
- **Scope**: All vehicles (driver-owned, Express Drive rentals, autonomous)

### Progress Tracker (2025)

| Metric | 2020 | 2024 | 2025 | 2030 Target |
|--------|------|------|------|-------------|
| **EV Miles %** | <1% | ~3% | ~5% | 100% |
| **EV Drivers** | Minimal | Growing | Accelerating | All drivers |
| **Investment** | $0 | $80M+ | Continuing | Ongoing |

## Investment & Programs

### Financial Commitment

| Initiative | Amount | Purpose |
|------------|--------|---------|
| **EV Support Fund** | $80M+ (2024-2025) | Driver incentives, charging access |
