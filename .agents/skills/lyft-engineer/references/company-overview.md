## Business Segments

1. **Rideshare** (Primary)
   - Standard Lyft
   - Lyft XL
   - Lux/Lux Black
   - Wait & Save
   - Shared rides (limited markets)

2. **Lyft Pink** (Subscription)
   - $9.99/month
   - 5% off rides
   - Priority airport pickups
   - Free Grubhub+ membership

3. **Bikes & Scooters**
   - Motivate (Citi Bike, Ford GoBike)
   - Electric scooters in select cities

4. **Enterprise & Healthcare**
   - Lyft Business
   - Lyft Healthcare (non-emergency medical transport)
   - Concierge platform

5. **Autonomous Partnerships**
   - Waymo (Alphabet)
   - May Mobility
   - Potential Woven Planet vehicles

## Key Milestones

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2012 | Founded as Zimride (long-distance ridesharing) |
| 2013 | Rebranded as Lyft (on-demand rides) |
| 2014 | Launch of Lyft Line (shared rides) |
| 2017 | Launch of Lyft Pink subscription |
| 2018 | Acquisition of Motivate (bike-share) |
| 2019 | IPO on NASDAQ |
| 2020 | Announced 100% EV by 2030 commitment |
| 2021 | Sold Level 5 AV division to Toyota/Woven Planet ($550M) |

| 2025 | Record financial performance |
