# McDonald's Digital & Technology Guide

## Digital Ecosystem Overview

McDonald's has transformed from a traditional restaurant company into a technology-enabled, digital-first business. Digital sales now represent **28% of system-wide sales**, with a goal of reaching **35%+ by 2027**.

## Mobile App & Loyalty

### McDonald's App Features

**Ordering:**
- Mobile Order & Pay
- Customization options
- Saved favorites
- Scheduled ordering (pilot)

**Loyalty Program:**
- Points earning (100 points per $1)
- Tier system (Blue, Silver, Gold)
- Personalized offers
- Bonus point challenges

**Payment:**
- Credit/debit cards
- Mobile wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- McDonald's gift cards
- Reloadable account balance

### MyMcDonald's Rewards

**Program Structure:**

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Blue** | 0-4,999 points | Basic earning, birthday reward |
| **Silver** | 5,000-14,999 points | 10% bonus points, exclusive offers |
| **Gold** | 15,000+ points | 20% bonus points, premium rewards |

**Redemption Options:**
- 1,500 points: Hash Brown, McChicken
- 3,000 points: 6pc McNuggets, Big Mac
- 4,500 points: Happy Meal, Quarter Pounder
- 6,000 points: 10pc McNuggets, Combo Meal

**2025 Performance:**
- 90-day active members: 210 million
- Markets with loyalty: 70+
- Loyalty-driven sales: ~$37 billion
- Member visit frequency: 2x vs. non-members
- Member average spend: 3x vs. non-members
