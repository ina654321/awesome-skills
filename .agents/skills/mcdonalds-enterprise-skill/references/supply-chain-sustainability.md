# McDonald's Supply Chain & Sustainability

## Supply Chain Overview

McDonald's supply chain is one of the most sophisticated in the world, supporting **43,000+ restaurants** across **100+ countries** with a focus on quality, consistency, and sustainability.

## The Three-Legged Stool

McDonald's supply chain philosophy is built on partnership between three equal stakeholders:

```
            ┌─────────────────┐
            │   McDONALD'S    │
            │    (Company)    │
            │  Brand, Menu,   │
            │    Standards    │
            └────────┬────────┘
                     │
           ┌─────────┴─────────┐
           │                   │
     ┌─────┴─────┐       ┌─────┴─────┐
     │ SUPPLIERS │       │FRANCHISEES│
     │  Quality  │       │  Restaurant│
     │  Materials│       │ Operations │
     └───────────┘       └───────────┘
```

## Sustainability Strategy

### Net Zero Commitment

McDonald's has committed to **net zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2050**, with interim targets aligned with climate science.

**2030 Goals:**
| Area | Target |
|------|--------|
| Climate | Reduce emissions 36% (2015 baseline) |
| Packaging | 100% renewable, recycled, or certified |
| Waste | Significant progress toward zero waste |
| Beef | Deforestation-free supply chain |

### Key Sustainability Initiatives

#### 1. Sustainable Packaging

**2025 Commitments:**
- 100% fiber-based packaging from recycled or certified sources
- Elimination of PFAS in food packaging
- 50%+ recycled content in plastic packaging
- Customer recycling available in 11,000+ restaurants

**Innovations:**
- Plant-based plastic alternatives
- Reduced packaging weight
- Reusable packaging pilots
- Elimination of single-use plastics (select markets)

#### 2. Responsible Beef Sourcing

**Commitments:**
- Zero deforestation by 2025
- Sustainable grazing practices
- Animal welfare standards
- Supporting farmer training programs

**Partners:**
- World Wildlife Fund (WWF)
- Global Roundtable for Sustainable Beef
- National Cattlemen's Beef Association

#### 3. Climate Action

**Emissions Reduction:**
- Restaurant energy efficiency
- Renewable energy procurement
- Supply chain engagement
- Electric vehicle pilots for delivery

**Progress (2025):**
- 60%+ reduction in restaurant emissions (vs. 2015)
- 100% renewable electricity in several markets
- EV charging stations at select locations

#### 4. Waste Reduction

**Programs:**
- Recycle by McDonald's (customer recycling)
- Food waste reduction in restaurants
- Composting pilots
- Sustainable packaging alternatives

**Circular Economy:**
- Designing for recyclability
- Partnering with waste management companies
- Exploring reusable packaging models
- Reducing food waste through donation

## Supplier Diversity

### Program Overview

McDonald's is committed to supplier diversity as a business imperative and social responsibility.

**2025 Goals:**
- 23% diverse supplier spend in U.S.
- Supporting minority, women, LGBTQ+, veteran, and disabled-owned businesses

### Categories

| Category | Definition | Examples |
|----------|------------|----------|
| **Minority-Owned** | 51%+ owned by ethnic minorities | Food suppliers, professional services |
| **Women-Owned** | 51%+ owned by women | Marketing agencies, facility services |
| **LGBTQ+-Owned** | Certified LGBTQ+ ownership | Technology, consulting |
| **Veteran-Owned** | Veteran ownership | Logistics, security |
| **Disabled-Owned** | Disabled ownership | Various categories |

### Programs

- **Supplier Diversity Summit**: Annual networking event
- **Mentorship Programs**: Capacity building for diverse suppliers
- **Tier 2 Reporting**: Encouraging prime suppliers to use diverse subcontractors
