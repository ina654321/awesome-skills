# Siemens Digital Industries Portfolio

## Overview

Digital Industries (DI) is Siemens' largest industrial business, providing automation, software, and services for discrete and process industries. DI helps customers optimize their entire value chain from product design through manufacturing to service.

**FY2025 Performance:**
- Revenue: €18.1 billion
- Profit: €3.2 billion
- Profit Margin: 17.6%
- Orders: €19.3 billion

## Business Units

### 1. Factory Automation

**Core Products:**

#### SIMATIC Controllers
| Series | Description | Applications |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **S7-1200** | Compact controller for small to medium applications | Basic automation, small machines |
| **S7-1500** | Advanced controller for complex applications | High-performance manufacturing |
| **S7-1500 TF** | Technology controller with integrated motion control | Packaging, converting, assembly |
| **ET 200SP** | Distributed I/O with CPU functionality | Modular machines, distributed systems |
| **SIPLUS** | Harsh environment variants (-40°C to +70°C) | Extreme conditions, outdoor use |

#### TIA Portal (Totally Integrated Automation)
- Unified engineering framework for all automation tasks
- Multiuser engineering for collaborative development
- Integrated safety, motion, and drive configuration
- Cloud-based engineering with TIA Portal Cloud Connector
- Version: TIA Portal V18 (latest)

#### Industrial Communication
- **PROFINET:** Real-time Ethernet (IEEE 802.3)
- **PROFIBUS:** Fieldbus standard (IEC 61158)
- **IO-Link:** Sensor/actuator communication
- ** Industrial Ethernet:** Standard IT protocols
- **5G:** Private 5G networks for industrial use
