## Strategic Framework: ONE Tech Company

### Vision
> "Siemens enters the next stage of growth with a highly synergistic core portfolio"

### Four Growth Levers

#### 1. Grow Digital
**Target:** Double digital business by 2030

| Metric | Current (2025) | Target (2030) | CAGR |
|--------|----------------|---------------|------|
| Digital Revenue | €9.4 billion | €18+ billion | 15% |

**Progress:**
- 12% average annual growth achieved (2021-2025)
- Exceeded original 10% target
- Software ARR: €5.3 billion (13% CAGR)

**Key Investments:**
- Altair acquisition (~$10B)
- Dotmatics acquisition
- AI development (€1B over 3 years)

#### 2. Grow Regions
**Focus Markets:**

| Region | Market Growth | Siemens Focus |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| United States | ~6% CAGR | Local-for-local production |
| China | ~4% CAGR | Technology leadership |
| India | >7% CAGR | Capacity expansion |
| Europe | Stable | Innovation and export |

**Strategy:**
- Invest where markets grow
- Build resilient local supply chains
- Leverage broad geographic footprint for tariff resilience

#### 3. Grow Verticals
**High-Growth Markets:**

| Vertical | Opportunity | Solutions |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Data Centers | €3.6B orders (FY2024) | Power distribution, cooling, BMS |
| Pharmaceuticals | Life sciences software | PLM, MES, LIMS |
| Food & Beverage | Track & trace, automation | MES, automation, traceability |
| Battery Manufacturing | EV growth | Automation, digital twin |

#### 4. Grow AI
**Investment:** €1 billion over 3 years

**Focus Areas:**
- Industrial AI applications
- Generative AI for engineering
- AI-powered automation
- Predictive maintenance

**Partnerships:**
- Intrinsic (Alphabet): AI robotics
- AWS/Azure: Cloud AI infrastructure
- Universities: AI research collaboration

## Capital Allocation Strategy

### Investment Priorities

1. **Organic Growth (R&D, Capex)**
   - R&D: €5+ billion annually
   - Capital expenditures: Expansion in high-growth markets

2. **Strategic Acquisitions**
   - Focus: Software, AI, digital capabilities
   - Recent: Altair, Dotmatics, Brightly
   - Criteria: Strategic fit, synergies, ROIC accretion

3. **Shareholder Returns**

**Progressive Dividend Policy:**
- FY2025 proposed: €5.35/share (+€0.15)
- Commitment maintained post-Healthineers deconsolidation
- Temporary higher payout ratio allowed if needed

**Share Buybacks:**
- Accelerated share buyback program ongoing
- Core pillar of capital return strategy
- Flexible execution based on market conditions

## Risk Factors

### Market Risks
- Economic slowdown in key markets
- Geopolitical tensions (tariffs, trade restrictions)
- Currency volatility (especially USD, CNY)

### Operational Risks
- Supply chain disruptions
- Cybersecurity threats
- Key personnel retention

### Strategic Risks
- Technology disruption
- Competition from new entrants
- Regulatory changes

### Mitigation
- Geographic diversification
- Local-for-local strategy
- Strong balance sheet
- Continuous innovation investment
