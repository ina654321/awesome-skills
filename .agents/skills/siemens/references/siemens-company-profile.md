# Siemens Company Profile

## Corporate Overview

**Siemens AG** is one of the world's largest technology companies, combining the real and digital worlds to create technology with purpose.

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Founded** | October 12, 1847 (Werner von Siemens & Johann Georg Halske) |
| **Headquarters** | Munich & Berlin, Germany |
| **CEO** | Roland Busch (since February 2021) |
| **CFO** | Ralf P. Thomas |
| **Employees** | ~318,000 (FY2025) |
| **Stock Listing** | Frankfurt Stock Exchange (SIE), Xetra, NYSE (SI) |
| **Market Cap** | ~€150-180 billion |

## Corporate Purpose

> "Technology to transform the everyday, for everyone."

Siemens empowers customers to accelerate their digital and sustainability transformations, making:
- Factories more efficient
- Cities more livable
- Transportation more sustainable

## Business Segments

### Digital Industries (DI)
- **Revenue:** €18.1 billion (FY2025)
- **Profit Margin:** 17.8% (Q1 2026)
- **Focus:** Factory automation, motion control, process automation, software
- **Key Brands:** SIMATIC, SINAMICS, SIRIUS, SIMOTICS, Teamcenter, NX, Tecnomatix

### Smart Infrastructure (SI)
- **Revenue:** €21.4 billion (FY2025)
- **Profit Margin:** 19.0% (Q1 2026)
- **Focus:** Electrification, building technologies, grid solutions
- **Key Brands:** SENTRON, 3VA, Desigo, Building X, Gridscale X, Electrification X

### Mobility
- **Revenue:** €11.5 billion (FY2025)
- **Profit Margin:** 8-10% target
- **Focus:** Rail vehicles, rail automation, electrification, turnkey systems
- **Key Brands:** Velaro, Mireo, Signaling X, Railigent X, ETCS, Trainguard

### Siemens Healthineers (Majority Stake)
- **Status:** Planned deconsolidation announced November 2025
- **Focus:** Medical imaging, laboratory diagnostics, advanced therapies
- **Ownership:** Siemens AG holds majority stake (to be distributed to shareholders)

### Siemens Financial Services (SFS)
- Leasing, equipment finance, project finance, advisory services

## ONE Tech Company Strategy

Announced November 2025, the ONE Tech Company program focuses on four growth levers:

### 1. Grow Digital
- **Target:** Double digital business by 2030 (15% CAGR)
- **Current:** €9.4 billion digital revenue (2025)
- **Acquisitions:** Altair (~$10B), Dotmatics

### 2. Grow Regions
- **Focus:** United States, China, India
- **Strategy:** Local-for-local production and R&D
- **Growth:** 6-7% CAGR in addressed markets

### 3. Grow Verticals
- **Target:** Data centers, pharmaceuticals, food & beverage
- **SI Data Center Orders:** €3.6 billion (+60% growth in FY2024)

### 4. Grow AI
- **Investment:** €1 billion over 3 years
- **Focus:** Industrial AI, generative AI for engineering
- **Partnership:** Intrinsic (Alphabet) for AI robotics
