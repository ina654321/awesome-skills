### 2. Rail Infrastructure

#### Signaling Systems
**ETCS (European Train Control System):**
- Level 1: Track-to-train communication
- Level 2: Radio Block Center (RBC) based
- Level 3: Moving block, train-based integrity

**CBTC (Communication-Based Train Control):**
- **Trainguard MT:** Metro signaling
  - GoA1 to GoA4 automation levels
  - 5G/ LTE-M communication
  - Up to 40% capacity increase

**Signaling X Platform (Launched 2024):**
- Cloud-based signaling data center
- Integrates mainline and mass transit systems
- DS3 (Distributed Smart Safe System) safety platform
- Standardized APIs for integration
- Benefits:
  - 20% improvement in operational efficiency
  - 30% energy savings with ATO over ETCS
  - Reduced lifecycle costs through virtualization

**Digital Interlocking:**
- **Simis W:** Electronic interlocking
- **Simis C:** Computer-based interlocking
- **Trackguard:** SSI replacement system

#### Train Control & Automation
**Automatic Train Operation (ATO):**
- ATO over ETCS for mainlines
- CBTC ATO for metro systems
- Grades of Automation (GoA):
  - GoA1: Driver controls doors, ATP protection
  - GoA2: Semi-automatic (driver in cab)
  - GoA3: Driverless (attendant on board)
  - GoA4: Fully unattended (UTO)

**First of its kind:** World's first refurbishment of existing GoA4 metro to new GoA4 CBTC system (Train2Cloud)

#### Rail Electrification
- AC and DC traction power supply
- Overhead contact lines
- Substations and switching stations
- Static Frequency Converters
- Energy management systems
