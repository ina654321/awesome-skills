# Siemens Smart Infrastructure Solutions

## Overview

Smart Infrastructure (SI) connects energy systems, buildings, and industries to address urbanization and climate change challenges. SI provides comprehensive solutions from power generation to consumption, with an increasingly digitalized ecosystem.

**FY2024 Performance:**
- Revenue: €21.4 billion
- Profit: €3.7 billion
- Profit Margin: 17.3%
- Orders: €24.0 billion
- Employees: ~78,500
- HQ: Zug, Switzerland

## Strategic Ambitions (2025+)

SI announced upgraded mid-term targets:
- **Revenue Growth:** 6-9% comparable annually
- **Profit Margin:** 16-20% over 3-5 year cycle
- **Digital Revenue:** 20%+ annual growth achieved

### 2. Electrification

**Grid Solutions & Automation:**

#### Gridscale X Platform
Siemens' cloud-native grid management platform launched 2024:

**Key Components:**
- **Protection Data Manager:** Centralized protection device management
  - Automated protection coordination
  - Reduced manual configuration errors
  - Streamlined maintenance workflows

- **Dynamic Line Rating (DLR):**
  - Real-time transmission capacity optimization
  - 10-15% increase in transmission efficiency
  - Weather-based ampacity calculations

- **Grid Planning & Analytics:**
  - Automated grid impact studies
  - Scenario modeling for renewables integration
  - Investment planning optimization

- **Grid Operation:**
  - Real-time grid monitoring
  - Automated switching and fault location
  - Self-healing grid capabilities

#### Substation Automation
- **SICAM:** Substation automation and protection
  - SICAM A8000: Remote terminal units
  - SICAM PAS: Power automation system
  - SICAM PQS: Power quality system

- **SIPROTEC:** Protection relays
  - Line protection (7SD, 7SA)
  - Transformer protection (7UT)
  - Busbar protection (7SS)
  - Motor/generator protection (7UM, 7VE)

#### Medium Voltage Switchgear
- **NXPLUS C:** Gas-insulated switchgear
- **NXAIR:** Air-insulated switchgear
- **blueGIS:** F-gas-free switchgear (SF6 alternative)
  - Vacuum interrupter technology
  - Clean Air insulation
  - Environmentally friendly

#### Low Voltage Power Distribution
- **SIVACON:** Low-voltage switchboards
- **ALPHA:** Distribution boards and terminal blocks
- **3VA/3WL:** Molded case and air circuit breakers
- **SENTRON:** Power monitoring and measurement
