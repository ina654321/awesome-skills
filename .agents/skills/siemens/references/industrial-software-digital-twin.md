# Siemens Industrial Software & Digital Twin

## Overview

Siemens Digital Industries Software provides comprehensive software solutions spanning the entire product lifecycle from design and engineering through manufacturing to operations and service. The portfolio enables customers to create comprehensive digital twins of their products, production, and performance.

**Strategic Importance:**
- Digital business revenue: €9.4 billion (FY2025)
- Growth target: 15% CAGR through 2030 (doubling by 2030)
- SaaS transformation: 24,000 SaaS customers, 70% new customers
- Altair acquisition: ~$10 billion (October 2024)

## Digital Twin Technology

### Comprehensive Digital Twin

Siemens enables three interconnected digital twins:

#### 1. Product Digital Twin
- Virtual representation of physical product
- Design and engineering simulation
- Performance prediction
- Requirements validation

**Technologies:**
- NX for design
- Simcenter for simulation
- Teamcenter for data management

#### 2. Production Digital Twin
- Virtual commissioning of production systems
- Process optimization before physical build
- Operator training in virtual environment
- Continuous improvement support

**Technologies:**
- Tecnomatix for manufacturing simulation
- TIA Portal for automation engineering
- PLCSIM Advanced for virtual controllers

#### 3. Performance Digital Twin
- Real-time monitoring of physical assets
- Predictive analytics and optimization
- Closed-loop feedback to design
- Service and maintenance optimization

**Technologies:**
- Insights Hub for IoT connectivity
- Industrial Edge for local processing
- Mendix for custom applications

### Digital Thread
Seamless data flow across the product lifecycle:
- Requirements → Design → Manufacturing → Service
- Bidirectional traceability
- Change impact analysis
- Continuous improvement loop
