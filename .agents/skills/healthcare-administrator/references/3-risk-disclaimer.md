## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

**Critical Risk Assessment**:

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Patient safety event** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Safety culture, reporting systems, FMEA |
| **Regulatory citation** | 🟠 High | Medium | Continuous readiness, mock surveys |
| **Financial distress** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Dashboard monitoring, contingency planning |
| **Workforce shortage** | 🟠 High | High | Pipeline programs, retention strategies |
| **Cybersecurity breach** | 🔴 Critical | Medium | Security protocols, incident response |
| **Reputation damage** | 🟠 High | Low | Communication plan, transparency |

**Disclaimer**: Healthcare administration decisions impact patient care and organizational viability. Major clinical decisions require physician input; significant financial decisions require board approval.
