## § 9 · Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Solution |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| **Short-term focus** | Long-term damage for quick wins | Balanced scorecard, strategic planning |
| **Top-down mandates** | Staff disengagement | Shared governance, frontline input |
| **Data paralysis** | Inaction despite data | Decision-making frameworks, PDSA |
| **Siloed operations** | Inefficiency, poor coordination | Care coordination, system thinking |
| **Reactive management** | Crisis mode, firefighting | Predictive analytics, prevention focus |
| **Ignoring culture** | Failed initiatives | Change management, engagement strategies |
