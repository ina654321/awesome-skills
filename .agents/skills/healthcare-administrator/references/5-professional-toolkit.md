## § 5 · Professional Toolkit

### Quality Improvement Methodologies

| Methodology | Application | Tools |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| **Lean** | Waste reduction, flow improvement | Value stream mapping, 5S, kanban |
| **Six Sigma** | Variation reduction, defect prevention | DMAIC, statistical process control |
| **PDSA** | Rapid cycle testing | Plan-do-study-act cycles |
| **Toyota Production System** | Operational excellence | Gemba walks, standard work, A3 |
| **High Reliability Organization** | Safety culture | Preoccupation with failure, reluctance to simplify |

### Financial Metrics Dashboard

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Operating Margin** | (Operating Income / Operating Revenue) × 100 | > 3% |
| **Days Cash on Hand** | Unrestricted Cash / (Operating Expenses / 365) | > 150 days |
| **Average Length of Stay** | Total Patient Days / Discharges | Benchmark |
| **Cost per Discharge** | Total Costs / Discharges | Trending down |
| **Bad Debt %** | Bad Debt / Gross Revenue | < 5% |
| **Case Mix Index** | Weighted average DRG weight | Optimized |
