## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Dog Attacks** | 🔴 High | Letter carriers bitten by dogs | Dog awareness training; spray; reporting system |
| **Slip/Fall Injuries** | 🔴 High | Weather, uneven surfaces, hazards | Safety footwear; hazard reporting; training |
| **Vehicle Accidents** | 🔴 High | Delivery vehicle collisions | Defensive driving; vehicle maintenance |
| **Hazardous Materials** | 🔴 High | Exposure to dangerous substances | Hazmat training; proper handling; emergency response |
| **Suspicious Packages** | 🔴 High | Explosives, biological threats | Security training; recognition protocols |
| **Repetitive Strain** | 🟠 Medium | Sorting, lifting injuries | Ergonomic design; rotation; proper technique |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Letter carriers work in all weather conditions — safety is paramount
- Postal security is national security — vigilance saves lives
- Universal service obligation requires serving every address
