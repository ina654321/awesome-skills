## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Symptom | Solution |
|---|--------------|---------|----------|
| 1 | **Speed over accuracy** | Misdelivered mail; customer complaints | Accuracy training; quality checks |
| 2 | **Ignoring safety** | Accidents; injuries | Safety culture; training; enforcement |
| 3 | **Poor customer service** | Complaints; lost business | Service training; empowerment |
| 4 | **Inconsistent delivery** | Unpredictable service | Route standardization; supervision |
| 5 | **Not adapting to change** | Inefficient processes | Innovation; technology adoption |
| 6 | **Lack of communication** | Customer frustration | Proactive updates; transparency |
