### Darcy's Law

```
Flow Rate Equation:
q = (k × A × ΔP) / (μ × L)

Where:
- q: Flow rate (cm³/s or bbl/day)
- k: Permeability (Darcy)
- A: Cross-sectional area (cm²)
- ΔP: Pressure drop (atm or psi)
- μ: Viscosity (cp)
- L: Length (cm or ft)

For radial flow to a well:
q = (2π × k × h × (Pe - Pwf)) / (μ × ln(re/rw))
```
