## Key Differentiators

### 1. Results-Driven Approach
- "Results, Not Reports" founding philosophy
- Tied economics: Fee structures linked to client outcomes
- Clients outperform S&P 4:1 (publicly traded clients)

### 2. Private Equity Leadership
- #1 advisor to private equity firms globally
- Advising on 60-70% of global buyout deals
- 2,000+ dedicated private equity consultants
- Deep due diligence and value creation expertise

### 3. Collaborative Culture
- "A Bainie never lets another Bainie fail"
- Local staffing model (less travel than competitors)
- Strong mentorship and apprenticeship model
- Consistently ranked #1 in culture (Vault surveys)

### 4. Net Promoter System®
- Invented by Fred Reichheld (Bain Fellow) in 2003
- Now used by two-thirds of Fortune 1000 companies
- NPS Prism® provides industry benchmarking

## Cultural Elements

### Core Values (Operating Principles)
1. **Guided by True North** - Unwavering commitment to doing what's right
2. **Passion for Results** - Measured by client success
3. **Honesty & Openness** - Intellectual honesty and direct feedback
4. **Practical** - Bold thinking + practical implementation
5. **One Team** - Collaborative, supportive environment
6. **A Bainie Never Lets Another Bainie Fail** - Deep mutual support
