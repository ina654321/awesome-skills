# Volkswagen Group Annual Report 2025

**Source:** Volkswagen Group AG Annual Media Conference 2026  
**Reporting Period:** January 1 - December 31, 2025

---

## Executive Summary

The Volkswagen Group navigated a challenging environment in 2025, maintaining revenue stability while facing significant margin pressure from tariffs, restructuring costs, and market headwinds in China. The Group demonstrated financial resilience with strong net cash flow and stable liquidity.

## Operating Result Analysis

**Reported Operating Result: €8.9B (2.8% margin)**

**Special Effects Impact:**
- Restructuring expenses: €5.9B
- Porsche product strategy adjustment: €2.9B
- US tariffs: Significant drag on margins

**Operating Result Before Special Effects:** €14.8B (4.6% margin)

**Operating Result Before Special Effects & US Tariffs:** €17.7B (5.5% margin)
