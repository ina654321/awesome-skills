# Volkswagen Group Brand Portfolio

**Overview:** 10+ brands across passenger cars, luxury, and commercial vehicles

---

## Brand Group Structure

### Core Brand Group

#### Volkswagen Passenger Cars
- **Positioning:** Mass market, volume leader
- **2025 Sales:** ~2.8M units (estimated)
- **Key Markets:** Global
- **Signature Models:** Golf, Passat, Tiguan, ID. series
- **Brand Promise:** People's car (Volkswagen = "people's car")
- **Price Range:** €20K - €80K

**Strategic Focus:**
- EV transition leader (ID. series)
- Cost leadership (€10B savings program)
- Global market presence

#### Škoda
- **Positioning:** Smart, value-oriented
- **2025 Sales:** ~920K units
- **Key Markets:** Europe (especially Eastern), India
- **Signature Models:** Octavia, Superb, Enyaq, Kodiaq
- **Brand Promise:** Simply Clever
- **Price Range:** €18K - €50K

**Strategic Focus:**
- Value engineering
- Eastern Europe strength
- EV entry (Elroq, Enyaq)

#### SEAT / CUPRA
- **Positioning:** Emotional, Mediterranean (SEAT); Performance (CUPRA)
- **2025 Sales:** ~470K units (combined)
- **Key Markets:** Europe
- **Signature Models:**
  - SEAT: Ibiza, Leon, Ateca
  - CUPRA: Born, Formentor, Leon VZ
- **Brand Promise:**
  - SEAT: Passionate design
  - CUPRA: Unconventional performance
- **Price Range:** €20K - €70K

**Strategic Focus:**
- CUPRA as growth driver
- Performance EVs
- Younger demographics

#### Volkswagen Commercial Vehicles
- **Positioning:** Professional transport
- **2025 Sales:** ~320K units
- **Key Markets:** Europe
- **Signature Models:** Transporter, Crafter, Amarok
- **Brand Promise:** Professional reliability
- **Price Range:** €25K - €70K

**Strategic Focus:**
- ID. Buzz commercial
- Electric vans
- Fleet solutions

---

### Progressive Brand Group

#### Audi
- **Positioning:** Premium, technology leader
- **2025 Sales:** ~1.7M units (including Lamborghini, Bentley)
- **Key Markets:** Global
- **Signature Models:** A4, A6, Q5, e-tron series, R8
- **Brand Promise:** Vorsprung durch Technik (Advancement through Technology)
- **Price Range:** €35K - €200K+

**Strategic Focus:**
- E-tron EV expansion
- Level 3 autonomy leader
- Premium EV dominance

**Technology Leadership:**
- PPE platform (with Porsche)
- Advanced ADAS
- Interior quality benchmark

---

### Sport Luxury Brand Group

#### Porsche
- **Positioning:** Sports car heritage, luxury performance
- **2025 Sales:** ~310K units
- **Key Markets:** Global (strong US, China)
- **Signature Models:** 911, Cayenne, Panamera, Taycan, Macan
- **Brand Promise:** There is no substitute
- **Price Range:** €60K - €300K+

**Strategic Focus:**
- Macan EV transition
- 911 electrification (hybrid)
- Maintaining margins

**Financial Profile (Pre-2025 Impairment):**
- Operating margin: 14-17%
- Highest profitability in Group
- Cash cow for transformation

#### Lamborghini
- **Positioning:** Extreme performance, Italian luxury
- **2025 Sales:** ~10K units
- **Key Markets:** US, Middle East, Europe
- **Signature Models:** Huracán, Urus, Revuelto
- **Brand Promise:** Expect the unexpected
- **Price Range:** €200K - €600K+

**Strategic Focus:**
- Urus success continuation
- Hybrid transition (Revuelto)
- Limited production exclusivity

#### Bentley
- **Positioning:** British ultra-luxury
- **2025 Sales:** ~10K units
- **Key Markets:** US, China, UK
- **Signature Models:** Continental GT, Flying Spur, Bentayga
- **Brand Promise:** Extraordinary journeys
- **Price Range:** €180K - €400K+

**Strategic Focus:**
- Beyond 100 strategy (EV future)
- Mulliner personalization
- Brand elevation

#### Ducati (Motorcycles)
- **Positioning:** Premium motorcycles
- **2025 Sales:** ~55K units
- **Key Markets:** Europe, US, Asia
- **Signature Models:** Panigale, Monster, Multistrada
- **Brand Promise:** Passion beyond reason

## Brand Synergies

### Platform Sharing
- MEB: VW, Audi, Škoda, CUPRA
- PPE: Audi, Porsche
- SSP: All brands (future)

### Technology Transfer
- Porsche engineering → VW performance models
- Audi tech → Škoda premium features
- VW scale → component costs for all

### Manufacturing Integration
- Shared plants (e.g., Bratislava: VW, Audi, Porsche)
- Flexible lines (MQB, MEB compatible)
- Component sharing (40%+ commonality)
