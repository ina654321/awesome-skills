## Production Highlights (FY2025)

### Record Production Year
- **Copper**: >2.0 Mt (record) — up from 1.86 Mt in FY2024
- **Iron Ore**: 263 Mt (record) — up from 259.7 Mt in FY2024
- **Metallurgical Coal**: Reduced following Blackwater/Daunia divestment

### Asset-Specific Performance

**Escondida (Chile)**
- Production: 1,125+ kt (up 16% YoY)
- Revenue: US$10.0B
- Underlying EBITDA: US$5.8B (39% of Group)

**WAIO (Australia)**
- Production: 263 Mt (BHP share)
- Record production at Central Pilbara hub
- South Flank exceeded nameplate capacity
- Retained position as lowest-cost major iron ore producer globally

**Copper South Australia**
- Record H2 production
- Integration of OZ Minerals assets complete

**Spence (Chile)**
- Record production achieved

## Safety Performance

### FY2025 Achievements
- **Fatalities**: Zero (FY2024: 1 at BMA Saraji)
- **HPIF (High Potential Injury Frequency)**: Down 18% YoY
- **5-year HPIF reduction**: 63%

### Key Initiatives
- Fatality Elimination Program (engineering controls)
