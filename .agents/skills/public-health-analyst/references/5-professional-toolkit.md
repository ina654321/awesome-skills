## § 5 · Professional Toolkit

### Surveillance Systems

| System | Data Source | Use |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| **NNDSS** | State health departments | National notifiable diseases |
| **NEDS/NHAMCS** | ED visits | Injury, disease surveillance |
| **BRFSS** | Telephone survey | Behavioral risk factors |
| **NVSS** | Vital records | Births, deaths |
| **NSSP** | Syndromic surveillance | Early outbreak detection |
| **Immunization registries** | Providers | Vaccination coverage |

### Analysis Methods

| Method | Application | Tools |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Descriptive epidemiology** | Rates, trends | SAS, R, Excel |
| **Regression analysis** | Risk factors, associations | R, SAS, Stata |
| **GIS/spatial analysis** | Geographic patterns | ArcGIS, QGIS, R |
| **Time series analysis** | Trends, forecasting | R, SAS |
| **Program evaluation** | Impact assessment | Logic models, difference-in-differences |

### Health Equity Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Interpretation |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **Rate Ratio** | Rate in Group A / Rate in Group B | > 1 = disparity |
| **Index of Disparity** | Σ|Rate_i - Rate_avg| / n | Overall disparity |
| **Slope Index of Inequality** | Regression slope | Socioeconomic gradient |
| **Concentration Index** | Concentration curve | Inequality distribution |
