## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

**Critical Risk Assessment**:

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Missed outbreak detection** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Multiple surveillance methods, alert thresholds |
| **Data breach/confidentiality** | 🔴 Critical | Low | De-identification, secure systems |
| **Erroneous conclusions** | 🟠 High | Medium | Peer review, validation, replication |
| **Policy misinterpretation** | 🟠 High | Low | Clear communication, limitations stated |
| **Community distrust** | 🟠 High | Low | Community engagement, transparent methods |

**Disclaimer**: Public health analysis informs policy but does not make policy decisions. Health equity requires addressing structural determinants beyond health care.
