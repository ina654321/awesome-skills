## Core Product Offerings

### Group Life Insurance

#### Basic Group Term Life
**Employer-Paid Coverage**

| Feature | Options |
|---------|---------|
| Coverage Amount | Flat amount or multiple of salary (1x, 2x, 3x typical) |
| Eligibility | Full-time employees (typically 30+ hrs/week) |
| Guaranteed Issue | Yes, up to certain limits |
| Evidence of Insurability | Required above guaranteed issue |
| Portability | Option to continue individually |

#### Supplemental/Voluntary Term Life
**Employee-Paid Coverage**

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Coverage Amount | Up to 5x salary or fixed amounts |
| Eligibility | Employee, spouse, dependent children |
| Cost | Group rates, payroll deduction |
| Portability | Convert to individual policy |
| Spouse Coverage | Often available up to 50% of employee amount |

### Group Disability Insurance

#### Short-Term Disability (STD)

| Feature | Typical Terms |
|---------|---------------|
| Benefit Percentage | 60% of weekly/monthly earnings |
| Benefit Duration | 13-26 weeks |
| Elimination Period | 7-14 days (illness); 0-7 days (injury) |
| Maximum Benefit | Weekly or monthly caps apply |
| Integration | Coordinates with sick pay, state disability |

#### Long-Term Disability (LTD)

| Feature | Typical Terms |
|---------|---------------|
| Benefit Percentage | 60% of monthly earnings |
| Benefit Duration | To age 65 or Social Security Normal Retirement Age |
| Elimination Period | 90-180 days (aligns with STD) |
| Definition of Disability | Own occupation (2 years), then any occupation |
| Integration | Reduces for Social Security, workers' comp |

**Partial/Residual Disability:**
- Return-to-work incentives
- Partial benefit for partial disability
- Rehabilitation provisions

## Specialized Products

### Group Universal Life

- Cash value accumulation

- Investment options within life insurance
- Higher growth potential

### Business Travel Accident
- Coverage during business travel
- Typically high limits ($100K - $1M)

- Self-funded employer health plan protection

- Medical and prescription coverage
