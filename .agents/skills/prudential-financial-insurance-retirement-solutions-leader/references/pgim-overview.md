# PGIM - Prudential Global Investment Management

## Overview

PGIM is the investment management business of Prudential Financial, Inc. (PFI), managing over $1.4 trillion in assets as of Q4 2025. Ranked as the 16th largest money manager globally by Pensions & Investments.
