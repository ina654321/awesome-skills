# Prudential Annuity Products

## Fixed Annuities

### Traditional Fixed Annuity
**Target:** Principal protection with guaranteed growth

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Minimum Investment | Typically $10,000 - $25,000 |
| Interest Rate | Declared rate, guaranteed for period (1-10 years) |
| Minimum Guarantee | Floor rate specified in contract |
| Tax Treatment | Tax-deferred growth |
| Withdrawals | Typically 10% annually without penalty |
| Surrender Charges | Declining over 5-10 years |

**Best For:**
- Conservative investors
- Principal preservation
- Tax-deferred growth
- Retirement income foundation

### Multi-Year Guaranteed Annuity (MYGA)
**Target:** Guaranteed rate for specific term

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Guarantee Period | 3, 5, 7, or 10 years |
| Interest Rate | Locked for guarantee period |
| Renewal | Rate declared at end of period |
| Liquidity | Limited during guarantee period |

## Fixed Index Annuities (FIA)

### Product Features
**Target:** Market participation with downside protection

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Index Options | S and P 500, Nasdaq, international indices |
| Crediting Methods | Point-to-point, monthly averaging, monthly sum |
| Cap Rate | Maximum return credited (varies by product) |
| Participation Rate | Percentage of index gain |
| Spread/Margin | Fee deducted from index return |
| Floor | 0% (no loss of principal or previous gains) |

**Best For:**
- Protection from market losses
- Potential for higher returns than fixed
- Retirement accumulation
- Long-term holding periods

## Immediate Annuities

### Single Premium Immediate Annuity (SPIA)
**Target:** Immediate guaranteed income

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Premium | Single payment |
| Income Start | Within 12 months of purchase |
| Payment Options | Life only, life with period certain, joint life |
| Payout | Fixed amount, guaranteed for life |

**Payment Options:**
- **Life Only:** Highest payment, no beneficiary benefit
- **Life with 10/20-Year Certain:** Guaranteed minimum years
- **Joint and Survivor:** Continues for spouse
- **Period Certain Only:** Fixed period, no life guarantee

**Best For:**
- Immediate retirement income needs
- Pension replacement
- Longevity risk transfer
- Guaranteed income floor

## Guaranteed Lifetime Withdrawal Benefits (GLWB)

### How They Work
1. **Income Base:** Protected amount that grows regardless of market performance
2. **Roll-Up Rate:** Guaranteed growth rate on income base (5-7% typical)
3. **Step-Up:** Automatic increases based on contract value on anniversaries
4. **Withdrawal Rate:** Percentage of income base available annually for life
5. **Lifetime Guarantee:** Payments continue even if account value reaches zero

### Withdrawal Rate Examples (typical)
| Age at First Withdrawal | Single Life | Joint Life |
|------------------------|-------------|------------|
| 59-64 | 4.0% | 3.5% |
| 65-69 | 4.5% | 4.0% |
| 70-74 | 5.0% | 4.5% |
| 75+ | 5.5% | 5.0% |

## Tax Considerations

### Non-Qualified Annuities
- **Growth:** Tax-deferred
- **Withdrawals:** Gain taxed as ordinary income (LIFO)
- **Before Age 59:** 10% penalty on gain
- **Annuitization:** Partly taxable based on exclusion ratio

### Qualified Annuities (IRA, 401k)
- **Growth:** Tax-deferred (already qualified)
- **Withdrawals:** Fully taxable as ordinary income
- **RMDs:** Required minimum distributions at age 73
- **No Additional Tax Deferral:** Benefits already tax-deferred

### Roth IRA Annuities
- **Growth:** Tax-free
- **Qualified Withdrawals:** Tax-free
- **No RMDs:** During owner's lifetime
- **Inheritance:** Tax-free to beneficiaries
