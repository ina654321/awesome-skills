# Business Segments

## Overview

Raymond James operates through four primary business segments that work together to serve clients across the financial spectrum.

---

## 1. Private Client Group (PCG)

**Contribution:** ~75% of firm revenue

The Private Client Group is Raymond James's largest segment, providing financial planning, investment advisory, and securities transaction services through a nationwide network of financial advisors.

### Services Offered

**Financial Planning:**
- Comprehensive financial planning
- Retirement planning
- Estate planning coordination
- Tax planning strategies
- Education funding
- Insurance review

**Investment Services:**
- Discretionary portfolio management
- Non-discretionary advisory services
- Brokerage services
- Alternative investments
- Managed accounts

**Lending & Banking:**
- Securities-based loans (SBLs)
- Residential mortgages
- Banking services through Raymond James Bank

### Key Metrics (Q1 FY2026)

| Metric | Value | Change (YoY) |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Quarterly Net Revenues | $2.77 billion | +9% |
| Assets Under Administration | $1.71 trillion | +15% |
| Fee-Based Account Assets | $1.04 trillion | +19% |
| Domestic Net New Assets | $30.8 billion | 8.0% annualized |
| Securities-Based Loans | $21.7 billion | +28% |

### Revenue Drivers

1. **Asset Management Fees** — Fee-based account revenues
2. **Commissions** — Transaction-based securities sales
3. **Account & Service Fees** — Account maintenance, mutual fund fees
4. **Net Interest Income** — From bank sweep and lending products

---

## 2. Capital Markets

**Contribution:** Variable; cyclical based on deal flow

The Capital Markets segment provides investment banking, institutional sales, trading, and equity research services to corporate and institutional clients.

### Services Offered

**Investment Banking:**
- Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) advisory
- Equity underwriting (IPOs, follow-ons)
- Debt underwriting
- Public finance (municipal bonds)

**Institutional Services:**
- Institutional sales & trading
- Equity research (1,200+ companies covered)
- Fixed income trading
- Syndicate operations

### Key Metrics (Q1 FY2026)

| Metric | Value | Change (YoY) |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Quarterly Net Revenues | $380 million | -21% |
| Investment Banking Revenues | $200 million | -37% |
| Quarterly Pre-Tax Income | $9 million | — |

**Note:** Capital Markets revenues are inherently cyclical and can fluctuate significantly quarter-to-quarter based on market conditions and deal timing.

### Strategic Focus

- Middle-market focus (Raymond James's traditional strength)
- Financial sponsor relationships
- Strong M&A pipeline with "pent-up demand"
- Fixed income and public finance capabilities (enhanced by Morgan Keegan acquisition)

---

## 3. Asset Management

**Contribution:** Growing segment with high margins

The Asset Management segment provides investment management services through Asset Management Services (AMS) and Raymond James Investment Management (RJIM).

### Services Offered

**Asset Management Services:**
- Discretionary portfolio management
- Internally sponsored mutual funds
- Separately managed accounts

**Raymond James Investment Management:**
- Multi-boutique model
- Sub-advisory relationships
- Proprietary investment strategies

### Key Metrics (Q1 FY2026)

| Metric | Value | Change (YoY) |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Quarterly Net Revenues | $326 million | +11% |
| Quarterly Pre-Tax Income | $143 million | +14% |
| Assets Under Management | $280.8 billion | +15% |

### Recent Developments

**Clark Capital Acquisition (January 2026):**
- Asset management firm specializing in wealth-focused solutions
- $46 billion+ in combined AUM and non-discretionary assets
- Cultural and strategic fit with Raymond James
- Will become part of multi-boutique offering within RJIM

### Growth Strategy

- Multi-boutique model allows acquired firms to maintain independence
- Focus on wealth-focused solutions
- Consistent strong inflows and high growth

## 4. Bank Segment

**Contribution:** ~13% of segment revenues; growing

The Bank segment includes Raymond James Bank and TriState Capital Bank, providing lending and deposit services that complement the wealth management business.

### Products & Services

**Lending:**
- Securities-based loans (SBLs)
- Residential mortgage loans
- Commercial & industrial (C&I) loans
- Commercial real estate loans

**Deposits:**
- Cash sweep accounts
- Enhanced Savings Program
- Certificate of deposits
- Money market accounts

### Key Metrics (Q1 FY2026)

| Metric | Value | Change (YoY) |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Quarterly Net Revenues | $487 million | +15% |
| Quarterly Pre-Tax Income | $173 million | +47% |
| Net Bank Loans | $53.4 billion | +13% |
| Net Interest Margin (NIM) | 2.81% | +21 bps |

### Strategic Value

- Keeps client cash deposits within Raymond James ecosystem
- Securities-based loans popular with advisory clients
- Strong credit quality maintained across portfolio
- NIM expansion driven by loan growth and favorable deposit mix
