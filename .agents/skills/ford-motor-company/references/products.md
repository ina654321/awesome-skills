## Powertrain Strategy

### ICE (Internal Combustion)
- Continued investment in efficient EcoBoost engines
- Focus on trucks and commercial vehicles
- Phase out in passenger cars (except Mustang)

### Hybrid
- **Strategy**: Expand across lineup to meet demand
- **Target**: 50% electrified by 2030
- **Offerings**: F-150 PowerBoost, Maverick Hybrid, Explorer Hybrid
- **Market Share**: 77% of US hybrid truck market

### Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV)
- Ranger PHEV in Europe
- Aviator PHEV
- Limited US focus (regulatory-driven)

### Electric (BEV)
- **Current**: Mach-E, F-150 Lightning, E-Transit
- **Future**: Universal EV Platform models
- **Strategy**: Focus on affordable, high-volume
- **Challenge**: Path to profitability by 2029
