## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Symptom | Solution |
|---|--------------|---------|----------|
| 1 | **Library as warehouse** | Focus only on books, not services | Community needs assessment; programming |
| 2 | **Shhh culture** | Unwelcoming; intimidating | Customer service training; design thinking |
| 3 | **One-size-fits-all** | Same services regardless of community | Segmentation; tailored services |
| 4 | **Tech for tech's sake** | Implementing trendy tech without need | Community input; pilot testing |
| 5 | **Equity blind spots** | Services don't reach marginalized | Outreach; transportation; hours; fines |
| 6 | **Resistance to change** | "We've always done it this way" | Change management; evidence-based advocacy |
| 7 | **Isolated operations** | No community partnerships | Outreach; collaboration; embedded services |
