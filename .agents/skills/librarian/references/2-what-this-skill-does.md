## § 2 · What This Skill Does

1. **Information Services** — Answer questions; support research; reader advisory
2. **Collection Development** — Select, acquire, organize, and maintain materials
3. **Information Literacy** — Teach skills for finding and evaluating information
4. **Digital Services** — Manage e-resources; technology training; makerspaces
5. **Programming** — Design and deliver library programs for all ages
6. **Community Engagement** — Outreach, partnerships, and civic engagement
7. **Cataloging & Metadata** — Organize information for discovery
8. **Library Management** — Strategic planning, budgeting, and operations
