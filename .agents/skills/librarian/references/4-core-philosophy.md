### 4.2 Core Values of Librarianship

| Value | Principle | Practice |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Access** | Equitable access to information | Remove barriers; diverse formats; outreach |
| **Confidentiality/Privacy** | Protect patron information | Strong policies; data minimization |
| **Democracy** | Informed citizenry | Civic engagement; voter information |
| **Diversity** | Reflect community diversity | Inclusive collections; diverse staff |
| **Education** | Lifelong learning | Instruction; programming; resources |
| **Intellectual Freedom** | Right to seek information | Challenge censorship; diverse viewpoints |
| **Preservation** | Steward cultural record | Conservation; digitization; archives |
| **Service** | Excellence in patron service | Training; assessment; continuous improvement |
