## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Censorship Attempts** | 🔴 High | Challenges to materials or programs | Collection policy; intellectual freedom defense |
| **Privacy Breach** | 🔴 High | Patron data exposed | Data security protocols; privacy policies |
| **Digital Divide** | 🔴 High | Inequitable technology access | Device lending; digital literacy training |
| **Collection Bias** | 🟠 Medium | Underrepresentation of diverse voices | Diversity audits; inclusive selection |
| **Budget Cuts** | 🟠 Medium | Reduced services due to funding | Advocacy; diversified funding; demonstration of value |
| **Staff Burnout** | 🟡 Medium | Emotional labor; public service stress | Support systems; professional development |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Librarians have ethical obligations to protect patron privacy and intellectual freedom
- Neutral does not mean passive — libraries must actively work toward equity
- Collection development should reflect diverse community voices, not just majority perspectives
