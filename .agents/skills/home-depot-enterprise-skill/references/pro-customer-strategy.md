# Home Depot Pro Customer Strategy

## The Pro Opportunity

### Market Size
- **Total Pro Market:** ~$450 billion annually (U.S. professional construction)
- **Home Depot Current Share:** ~15% of Pro market
- **Growth Potential:** Massive runway to capture 25%+ share

### Why Pro Matters
- **Revenue Concentration:** 45-50% of sales from ~10% of customers
- **Transaction Value:** $1,000+ average ticket (vs. $75 DIY)
- **Frequency:** High repeat purchase rate
- **Loyalty:** 92% annual retention rate
- **Lifetime Value:** Significantly higher than DIY

---

## Pro Customer Segmentation

### Simple Project Pros (70% of Pro customers)
**Profile:**
- Handymen and maintenance professionals
- Small repair specialists
- Property managers
- Part-time contractors

**Characteristics:**
- Transaction sizes: $200-$1,000
- Purchase frequency: Weekly
- Shopping behavior: Urgent needs, convenience-driven
- Primary channel: Stores (in-and-out quickly)

**Home Depot Strategy:**
- Store-based convenience
- Pro desk for fast checkout
- Will-call for pre-staged orders
- Tool rental for occasional needs

### Complex Project Pros (30% of Pros, 70% of market value)
**Profile:**
- General contractors (GCs)
- Remodelers and renovators
- Specialty trades (roofers, pool, landscape, electrical, plumbing)
- Medium-to-large home builders
- Building service contractors

**Characteristics:**
- Transaction sizes: $5,000-$50,000+
- Purchase frequency: Daily/ongoing projects
- Shopping behavior: Job lot orders, delivery requirements
- Primary channel: Mix of store, phone, online, direct sales

**Home Depot Strategy:**
- Dedicated sales teams
- Job site delivery
- Trade credit (Pro House Account)
- Volume pricing tiers
- SRS Distribution for specialty trades

## Pro Capabilities Stack

### 1. Pro Xtra Loyalty Program

**Program Overview:**
- **Members:** 15+ million enrolled
- **Value Proposition:** Points, perks, and exclusive benefits

**Key Benefits:**
- Volume rebates on qualifying purchases
- Exclusive Pro-only deals
- Tool rental perks (free days, discounts)
- Early access to sales events
- Free shipping thresholds
- Dedicated support line

**Tier Structure:**
| Tier | Qualification | Benefits |
|------|---------------|----------|
| **Member** | Enroll | Basic perks, track receipts |
| **VIP** | Volume thresholds | Enhanced rebates, priority support |
| **Elite** | Top spenders | Maximum benefits, dedicated rep |

### 2. Dedicated Sales Force

**Outside Sales Team:**
- Field-based sales representatives
- Assigned to large/key accounts
- Proactive account management
- Job site visits and consultations

**Inside Sales:**
- Phone-based support for orders
- Complex order management
- Quote generation

**Pro Desk:**
- Dedicated checkout area in stores
- Will-call order pickup
- Pro-trained associates
- Skip-the-line service

### 3. Delivery & Fulfillment

**Job Site Delivery:**
- Direct delivery from DCs to job sites
- Same-day and next-day options
- Flatbed trucks for bulk materials
- Scheduled delivery windows
- 17 top markets equipped (expanding)

**Relay Delivery:**
- Overnight trailer drops at stores
- Next-morning job site delivery
- Expands reach into adjacent markets
- Currently in Atlanta, expanding

**Delivery Types:**
- **Parcel:** Small items, standard shipping
- **Large Parcel:** Appliances, oversized
- **Flatbed:** Lumber, building materials, bulk
- **Specialized:** MRO, SRS branch delivery

### 4. Trade Credit & Financial Services

**Pro House Account:**
- Net 30 payment terms

- Online account management

**HD Commercial Card:**
- Accepted everywhere Visa is accepted
- Rewards on purchases
- Detailed reporting

**Financing Options:**
- Project financing for large jobs
- Equipment financing
- Lease-to-own programs

### 5. Digital Tools

**Pro App Features:**
- Quick reorder from history

**B2B Website:**

## SRS Distribution Acquisition (2024)

### Transaction Overview
- **Acquisition Price:** $18.3 billion
- **Closing Date:** June 2024
- **Strategic Rationale:** Accelerate Pro growth in specialty trades

### SRS Profile
- **Branches:** 400+ locations
- **Employees:** Thousands of associates
- **Revenue Contribution:** $6.4B in FY2024 (partial year)
- **Focus:** Roofing, landscaping, pool contractors

### Capabilities Added
1. **Specialty Trade Expertise:** Deep relationships with roofers, landscapers, pool pros
2. **Branch Network:** Local presence in key markets
3. **Delivery Fleet:** Specialized vehicles for trade-specific products
4. **Product Assortment:** Products not typically in Home Depot stores
5. **Credit Relationships:** Established trade credit with specialty contractors

### Integration Strategy
- Maintain SRS brand initially (regional recognition)
- Leverage Home Depot scale for purchasing
- Cross-sell capabilities (SRS customers → Home Depot, vice versa)
- Combine delivery networks for efficiency
- Shared technology platform over time

## Pro Growth Initiatives

### Priority Markets
Home Depot has identified top Pro markets for investment:
- 17 markets equipped with enhanced capabilities by end of 2024
- New distribution centers in Detroit, LA, San Antonio, Toronto (2024)
- Focus on markets with high contractor density

### Key Initiatives

**1. Share of Wallet Expansion**
- Target: Increase from ~15% to 25%+ of Pro spend
- Strategy: Capture complex projects, not just urgent needs
- Tactics: Job lot delivery, credit, dedicated sales

**2. Simple Project Pros**
- Tool rental expansion
- Pro desk enhancements
- Express checkout
- Digital convenience

**3. Complex Project Pros**
- SRS integration for specialty trades
- Showroom expansion (Construction Resources acquisition)
- Enhanced order management system
- Trade credit expansion

**4. B2B Platform Enhancements**
- New order management capabilities
- Tiered pricing visibility
- Integration with Pro project management tools
- Enhanced reporting and analytics
