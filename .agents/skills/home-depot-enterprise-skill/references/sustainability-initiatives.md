# Home Depot Sustainability Initiatives

## Sustainability Strategy Overview

Home Depot's sustainability strategy, "Eco Options," integrates environmental responsibility with business performance. The approach focuses on product innovation, operational efficiency, and community impact.

### Key Commitments
1. **Carbon Reduction:** Reduce carbon footprint 50% by 2030 (store operations)
2. **Renewable Energy:** 100% renewable electricity by 2030
3. **Sustainable Products:** Expand Eco Options portfolio
4. **Circular Economy:** Reduce waste, increase recycling
5. **Responsible Sourcing:** Sustainable wood, ethical supply chain

---

## Eco Options Program

### Program Overview
Launched in 2007, Eco Options identifies products with environmental benefits across all categories.

### Product Categories

| Category | Criteria | Examples |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Energy Efficiency** | Reduce energy consumption | LED bulbs, ENERGY STAR appliances, insulation |
| **Water Conservation** | Reduce water usage | WaterSense fixtures, drip irrigation, rain barrels |
| **Sustainable Forestry** | Certified responsible sourcing | FSC lumber, bamboo products |
| **Healthy Home** | Improve indoor air quality | Low-VOC paint, air purifiers, natural cleaning |
| **Clean Energy** | Enable renewable energy | Solar panels, EV chargers |

### Program Scale
- **Products:** 15,000+ Eco Options products
- **Categories:** All major departments
- **Growth:** Continuous expansion of portfolio

### Customer Communication
- **In-Store Signage:** Green Eco Options tags
- **Online Filtering:** Shop by Eco Options category
- **Product Badges:** Clear identification
- **Education:** How-to guides for sustainable choices

## Responsible Sourcing

### Wood Procurement Policy

**Requirements:**
- Preference for FSC-certified wood products
- No wood from endangered forests
- Supplier compliance verification

**Products Affected:**
- Lumber and building materials
- Paper products
- Furniture
- Flooring

### Chemical Policy

**Restricted Chemicals:**
- Phased elimination of certain chemicals
- Priority chemicals of concern
- Supplier notification requirements

**Focus Areas:**
- Paint (VOC reduction)
- Flooring (phthalate elimination)
- Insulation (formaldehyde-free options)

### Supplier Sustainability

**Expectations:**
- Environmental compliance
- Waste reduction
- Energy efficiency
- Packaging optimization

**Assessment:**
- Supplier scorecards
- Sustainability audits
- Continuous improvement requirements

## Circular Economy Programs

### Tool Rental

**Concept:** Rent tools instead of buying (use vs. ownership)

**Benefits:**
- Reduced consumption
- Access to professional tools
- Cost savings for customers

**Program Scale:**
- Available at most stores
- Wide variety of tools and equipment

### Battery Recycling

**Program:**
- In-store drop-off for rechargeable batteries
- Partnership with Call2Recycle
- Free for customers

**Impact:**
- Millions of pounds recycled annually
- Keeps toxic materials from landfills

### CFL and Bulb Recycling

**Program:**
- In-store CFL recycling
- Expanding to other bulb types

### Appliance Recycling

**Service:**
- Haul-away of old appliances with delivery
- Responsible recycling/disposal
- Compliant with EPA regulations

### Paint Recycling

**Programs:**
- Participating stores offer paint recycling
- Partnership with PaintCare (where available)
- Proper handling of hazardous waste
