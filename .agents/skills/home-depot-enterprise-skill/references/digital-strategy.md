# Home Depot Digital Strategy

## Digital Vision

Home Depot's digital strategy centers on delivering a "best-in-class interconnected experience" where customers seamlessly navigate between digital and physical channels based on their preferences and project needs.

### Core Principles
1. **Customer Choice:** Shop how, when, and where they want
2. **Speed:** Fastest delivery in home improvement
3. **Convenience:** BOPIS, curbside, job site delivery
4. **Knowledge:** Digital tools for project planning
5. **Integration:** Unified experience across touchpoints

## Omnichannel Integration

### The Interconnected Experience

```
Customer Journey Example:

Research Phase
├── Browse website for kitchen remodel ideas
├── Watch how-to videos on cabinet installation
├── Use AR to visualize new cabinets
└── Create project list and save products

Planning Phase
├── Check local store inventory
├── Calculate materials needed
├── Schedule in-home consultation (DIFM)
└── Get pre-approved for Project Loan

Purchase Phase
├── Order online for BOPIS (appliances)
├── Order online for delivery (cabinets)
├── Visit store for paint and hardware
└── Use app to locate items in store

Fulfillment Phase
├── Pick up appliances at store
├── Receive cabinet delivery
├── Track delivery in app
└── Schedule installation service

Post-Purchase
├── Leave product reviews
├── Book additional services
├── Reorder maintenance supplies
└── Start next project
```

### BOPIS (Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store)

**Process:**
1. Customer orders online
2. Store associate picks items
3. Customer receives "ready for pickup" notification
4. Pickup at customer service desk, Pro desk, or curbside

**Benefits:**
- **For Customer:** Speed, convenience, no shipping cost
- **For Home Depot:** Higher conversion, incremental sales (customers buy more in-store)
- **Inventory Efficiency:** Uses existing store stock

**Metrics:**
- High customer satisfaction
- Drives store traffic
- Higher basket size than pure digital

### Curbside Pickup
- Available at most stores
- Check in via app when arriving
- Associate loads vehicle
- Pro Express lanes for faster service

## Competitive Digital Position

### vs. Lowe's
- Similar digital capabilities
- Home Depot advantages: Pro app features, supply chain integration
- Ongoing feature parity battle

### vs. Amazon
- Home Depot advantage: Heavy/bulky items, project knowledge, services
- Amazon threat: Tools, basic hardware, convenience
- Differentiation: Services, installation, expertise

### vs. Wayfair
- Home Depot advantage: Store pickup, immediacy, services
- Wayfair threat: Décor, furniture selection
- Response: Expand décor assortment

### vs. Specialty Retailers
- Home Depot advantage: One-stop shop, convenience
- Threat: Category depth from specialists
- Response: Extended aisle, special order
