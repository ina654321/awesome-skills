# Home Depot Supply Chain & Fulfillment

## Supply Chain Strategy Overview

Home Depot's supply chain is designed to deliver the fastest, most efficient, and most reliable fulfillment capabilities in home improvement. The strategy supports both DIY convenience and Pro job site requirements.

### Strategic Objectives
1. **Product Availability:** Ensure the right product is available when and where needed
2. **Speed:** Same-day and next-day delivery capabilities
3. **Flexibility:** Multiple fulfillment options (BOPIS, delivery, will-call)
4. **Cost Efficiency:** Optimize inventory turns and logistics costs
5. **Reliability:** Consistent delivery windows and accurate fulfillment

## Fulfillment Capabilities

### Delivery Speed Evolution

| Era | Standard Delivery | Extended Aisle |
|-----|-------------------|----------------|
| **Historical** | Same-day (in-stock) | 5-9 days |
| **Current (2024)** | Same-day | 1-2 days (50%+ of products) |
| **Target** | Same-day | Next-day (majority) |

### Fulfillment Options

#### Same-Day Delivery
- **Coverage:** Most major metropolitan areas
- **Products:** In-store inventory
- **Cutoff:** Typically 2-3 PM for same-day
- **Cost:** Fee-based (free for Pro Xtra above threshold)

#### Next-Day Delivery
- **Coverage:** 90%+ of U.S. customers
- **Products:** Extended aisle (DFC inventory)
- **Mechanism:** Ship from DFC to MDO or customer
- **Advantage:** Speed without store inventory constraints

#### Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store (BOPIS)
- **Process:** Order online, pickup at customer service or Pro desk
- **Speed:** Ready in as little as 1 hour
- **Benefit:** Higher conversion, higher spend per visit
- **Curbside:** Available at most locations

#### Will-Call
- **Target:** Pro customers
- **Process:** Order ahead, pickup at Pro desk
- **Advantage:** Pre-staged, express pickup

#### Job Site Delivery
- **Target:** Pro customers
- **Origin:** FDC or SRS branches
- **Products:** Job lot quantities, bulk materials
- **Scheduling:** Advance booking, delivery windows

## Inventory Management

### Forecasting & Replenishment
- **Centralized Systems:** Sophisticated demand forecasting
- **Store-Level Optimization:** Tailored to local demand patterns
- **Seasonal Planning:** Weather-based adjustments
- **Promotional Planning:** Event-based inventory positioning

### Inventory Metrics
| Metric | Current | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Inventory Turns** | 4.7x | 5.0x+ |
| **In-Stock Rate** | >95% | >97% |
| **Safety Stock** | Optimized by velocity | Reduce while maintaining service |

### Inventory Positioning Strategy
```
High-Velocity Items (Top 20% of SKUs)
├── Store: High stock levels
├── RDC: Replenishment stock
└── DFC: Backup inventory

Medium-Velocity Items (Next 30% of SKUs)
├── Store: Moderate stock
├── RDC: Primary fulfillment
└── DFC: Regional backup

Long-Tail Items (Bottom 50% of SKUs)
├── Store: Minimal/no stock
├── DFC: Primary inventory
└── Vendor Direct: Special order
```
