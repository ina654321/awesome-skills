# Home Depot Executive Leadership

## C-Suite Leadership Team

### Ted Decker - Chairman, President & CEO

**Background:**
- **Joined Home Depot:** 2000 (25 years of service)
- **Became CEO:** March 1, 2022
- **Became Chairman:** August 2022
- **Previous Role:** President & COO (2020-2022)
- **Age:** ~60s
- **Education:** College of William & Mary (BA, Economics)
- **Net Worth:** ~$56M+ (primarily HD stock)

**Career Progression at Home Depot:**
1. **2000-2002:** Strategic Planning roles
2. **2002-2006:** VP - Strategic Business Development
3. **2006:** SVP - Strategic Business and Asset Development
4. **2006-2014:** SVP - Retail Finance, Pricing Analytics, Assortment Planning
5. **2014-2020:** EVP - Merchandising
6. **2020-2022:** President & COO
7. **2022-Present:** Chairman, President & CEO

**Prior Experience:**
- Kimberly-Clark Corp (strategic planning, business development, finance, treasury)
- Scott Paper Co.

**Leadership Philosophy:**
- "Servant Leadership" - supporting employees to boost motivation and performance
- "Science and Art" of retail - data-driven decisions with customer intuition
- Passion for customers, employees, and vendors
- Focus on Home Depot's culture and values
- Strategic vision with long-term perspective

**Key Priorities as CEO:**
1. Drive core business and culture
2. Deliver frictionless interconnected experience
3. Win the Pro customer
4. Digital transformation
5. Supply chain excellence

**Notable Quotes:**
- "We are focused on growing sales and delivering exceptional shareholder returns, supported by our culture and values."
- "The investments we've made over the last several years have further strengthened our distinct competitive advantages."
- On mortgage rates: "As rates head down towards 6%, we would expect to see [housing] activity."

## Leadership Culture

### Home Depot Values (Cultural Foundation)
1. **Excellent Customer Service** - Going the extra mile
2. **Building Strong Relationships** - With customers, associates, vendors
3. **Respect for All People** - Diversity, inclusion, equity
4. **Creating Shareholder Value** - Disciplined capital allocation
5. **Doing the Right Thing** - Ethics and integrity
6. **Entrepreneurial Spirit** - Taking risks, innovating
7. **Giving Back** - Community involvement, Team Depot

### Leadership Principles
- **Store-Centric:** Decisions start with impact on front-line associates
- **Customer-Focused:** Pro and DIY needs drive strategy
- **Results-Oriented:** Data-driven with retail instinct
- **Associate Development:** Promote from within (75%+ of management)
- **Community Engagement:** 1.5M+ volunteer hours since 2011

### Notable Leadership Statistics
- **Internal Promotion Rate:** 75%+ of management positions filled internally
- **Average Tenure:** Leadership team averages 15+ years at company
- **Diversity:** Commitment to DE&I in leadership ranks
- **Compensation:** Heavy emphasis on performance-based incentives

## Succession Planning

Home Depot has a strong track record of internal succession:

| CEO | Tenure | Background |
|-----|--------|------------|
| Bernie Marcus & Arthur Blank | 1978-2000 | Founders |
| Bob Nardelli | 2000-2007 | GE executive |
| Frank Blake | 2007-2014 | Lawyer, former GE |
| Craig Menear | 2014-2022 | Merchandising background |
| Ted Decker | 2022-present | Finance/Merchandising/Operations |

The company emphasizes developing leaders across multiple functional areas (stores, supply chain, merchandising, finance) before CEO consideration.
