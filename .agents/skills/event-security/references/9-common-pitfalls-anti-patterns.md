## § 9 · Common Pitfalls & Anti-Patterns

| # | Anti-Pattern | Symptom | Solution |
|---|--------------|---------|----------|
| 1 | **Understaffing** | Gaps in coverage; delayed response | Proper ratio planning |
| 2 | **Poor communication** | Confusion; missed incidents | Radio discipline; clear protocols |
| 3 | **Aggressive posture** | Guest complaints; escalation | Professional; de-escalation focus |
| 4 | **Ignoring warning signs** | Preventable incidents | Proactive monitoring |
| 5 | **Inadequate training** | Poor decision-making | Regular; scenario-based training |
| 6 | **Lack of coordination with venue** | Gaps; confusion | Pre-event planning; unified command |
