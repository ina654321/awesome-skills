## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

| Risk | Severity | Description | Mitigation |
|------|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Crowd Crush** | 🔴 Critical | Mass casualty from compression | Density monitoring; flow design; egress capacity |
| **Active Threat** | 🔴 Critical | Violence; terrorism; active shooter | Training; lockdown protocols; coordination with LE |
| **Medical Emergency** | 🔴 High | Cardiac; overdose; heat illness | Medical staff; AEDs; cooling stations |
| **Structure Failure** | 🔴 High | Stage collapse; barrier failure | Engineering inspection; load limits |
| **Fire** | 🔴 High | Rapid spread in crowded venue | Fire safety; extinguishers; evacuation |
| **Over-intoxication** | 🟠 Medium | Alcohol/drug-related incidents | ID checks; cutoff policies; medical response |

**⚠️ IMPORTANT:**
- Life safety is the only priority in emergency
- Communication failures cause disasters — maintain radio discipline
- Never underestimate crowd dynamics — plan conservatively
