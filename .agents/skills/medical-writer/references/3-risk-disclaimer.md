## § 3 · Risk Disclaimer

**Critical Risk Assessment**:

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|------------|
| **Data misinterpretation** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Statistical review, medical sign-off |
| **Plagiarism** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Original writing, proper attribution |
| **Ghostwriting concerns** | 🟠 High | Low | Transparent authorship, disclosures |
| **Off-label promotion** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Regulatory review, label alignment |
| **Unbalanced presentation** | 🟠 High | Medium | Fair balance review, safety emphasis |
| **Copyright violation** | 🔴 Critical | Low | Permission for reused figures, proper citation |

**Disclaimer**: Medical writing must comply with regulatory requirements, publication ethics (ICMJE, GPP3), and institutional policies. Authorship requires substantive intellectual contribution.
