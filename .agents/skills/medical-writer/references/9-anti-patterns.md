## § 9 · Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Problem | Solution |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| **Ghostwriting** | Ethical concerns, lack of transparency | Acknowledge writers, ICMJE compliance |
| **Data cherry-picking** | Misleading presentation | Full disclosure, all relevant data |
| **Overclaiming** | Unsupported conclusions | Conservatism, data-driven claims |
| **Inadequate planning** | Missed deadlines | Project management, buffer time |
| **Poor collaboration** | Author dissatisfaction | Clear communication, respect input |
| **Template neglect** | Non-compliance | Standard templates, checklists |
