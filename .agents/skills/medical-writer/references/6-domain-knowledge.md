## § 6 · Domain Knowledge

### CSR Structure (ICH E3)

```
Title Page
Synopsis (1-2 pages)
Table of Contents
List of Abbreviations
Ethics
  - IRB/IEC
  - Ethics conduct
Study Personnel and Structure
Introduction
Study Objectives
Investigational Plan
  - Study design
  - Selection of study population
  - Treatments
  - Efficacy and safety variables
  - Data quality assurance
  - Statistical methods
Study Patients
  - Disposition
  - Protocol deviations
Efficacy Evaluation
  - Data sets analyzed
  - Demographic and baseline characteristics
  - Efficacy results by endpoint
Safety Evaluation
  - Extent of exposure
  - Adverse events
  - Clinical laboratory
  - Vital signs
  - Other safety data
Discussion and Conclusions
References
Appendices
```

### Manuscript Structure (IMRAD)

```
Title (concise, informative)
Abstract (structured: Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion)
Introduction (context, gap, objective)
Methods (design, participants, interventions, outcomes, statistics)
Results (participant flow, baseline, primary, secondary, safety)
Discussion (summary, context, limitations, implications, conclusion)
Acknowledgments (funding, contributions)
References (journal-specific format)
Tables and Figures
Supplementary Materials
```

### Publication Ethics

**ICMJE Authorship Criteria** (all 4 required):
1. Substantial contributions to conception/design, data acquisition/analysis
2. Drafting or critically revising for intellectual content
3. Final approval of published version
4. Agreement to be accountable for accuracy/integrity

**Ghostwriting vs. Professional Writing**:
- Ghostwriting: Hidden sponsor involvement, misleading authorship
- Professional writing: Transparent, acknowledged, ICMJE-compliant
