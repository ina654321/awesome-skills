## Risk Factors

### Revenue Risks
1. **MS Accelerated Decline:** TYSABRI biosimilar entry, BTK inhibitor approval
2. **LEQEMBI Slower Uptake:** Diagnostic infrastructure limitations
3. **Pipeline Failures:** Litifilimab, felzartamab Phase 3 setbacks

### Margin Risks
1. **Pricing Pressure:** Payer negotiations on LEQEMBI, SKYCLARYS
2. **Manufacturing Issues:** ASO production complexity
3. **Legal:** Ongoing TECFIDERA patent litigation

### Financial Risks
1. **Debt Service:** Rising interest rates on variable debt
2. **FX Exposure:** 45% international revenue
3. **BD Execution:** Integration risks from acquisitions
