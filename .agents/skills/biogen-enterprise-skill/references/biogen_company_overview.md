# Biogen Company Overview

## Corporate History

### Founding & Early Years (1978-2003)
- **Founded:** 1978 in Geneva, Switzerland by Kenneth Murray, Phillip Sharp, Walter Gilbert, Heinz Schaller, and Charles Weissmann
- **Early Focus:** Molecular biology and genetic engineering
- **1996:** FDA approval of AVONEX (interferon beta-1a) for relapsing MS—established Biogen as neuroscience leader
- **IPO:** September 17, 1991 (NASDAQ: BIIB)

### Biogen Idec Merger (2003-2015)
- **2003:** Merged with IDEC Pharmaceuticals to form Biogen Idec
- **Strategic Rationale:** Combined Biogen's MS expertise with IDEC's oncology portfolio (Rituxan)
- **Key Products:** Rituxan (non-Hodgkin's lymphoma), TYSABRI (MS—approved 2004, withdrawn then reintroduced)
- **2013:** Company reverted to "Biogen" name, shedding Idec legacy

### Modern Era (2015-Present)
- **2016:** SPINRAZA approval—first treatment for spinal muscular atrophy
- **2021:** Aduhelm approval (controversial, withdrawn 2024)
- **2022:** Christopher Viehbacher appointed CEO (November 2022)
- **2023:** "Fit for Growth" restructuring announced; Aduhelm withdrawal
- **2024:** Four first-in-class launches (LEQEMBI, SKYCLARYS, ZURZUVAE, QALSODY)
- **2025:** Revenue growth return; pipeline advancement

## Corporate Culture & Values

### Mission Statement
"Discovering, developing, and delivering innovative therapies for people living with serious and complex diseases."

### Core Values
1. **Patient-Centricity:** Deep commitment to addressing unmet medical needs
2. **Scientific Excellence:** Mechanism-first approach to drug development
3. **Collaboration:** Strategic partnerships as core capability
4. **Integrity:** High ethical standards, especially after Aduhelm experience
5. **Financial Discipline:** Balancing innovation with shareholder returns

### Transformation Culture (2023-2025)
Under Viehbacher's leadership, Biogen has undergone significant cultural shifts:
- **Accountability:** Enhanced performance management
- **Speed:** Faster decision-making, reduced bureaucracy
- **Focus:** Clear prioritization of high-conviction programs
- **Transparency:** Improved investor communication
