## Risk Factors (Financial)

### Key Risks
1. **Medicare Margin Pressure**: MLR elevation may persist
2. **PBM Regulatory Risk**: Reform could impact profitability
3. **Interest Rate Exposure**: $33.6B debt subject to refinancing
4. **Goodwill Impairment**: $5.7B charge in 2024 related to Aetna
5. **Integration Execution**: Oak Street/Signify synergy realization

### Mitigation Strategies
- Cost reduction program ($2B target)
- Debt paydown prioritization
- Diversified revenue streams
- Value-based care growth
