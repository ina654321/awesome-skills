# CVS Health Business Segments

## Segment Overview

CVS Health operates through three primary business segments:

1. **Health Care Benefits** (Aetna insurance operations)
2. **Pharmacy Services** (Caremark PBM operations)
3. **Retail/Long-Term Care** (CVS Pharmacy, MinuteClinic, LTC pharmacy)

Additionally, through **CVS Healthspire**, the company integrates healthcare delivery assets:
- Oak Street Health (primary care)
- Signify Health (home-based care)
- MinuteClinic (retail clinics)
- Cordavis (biosimilars)

## 4. Healthcare Delivery (Healthspire)

### Healthspire Brand (Launched 2024)
Unifies all care delivery assets under single brand:
- **Caremark** (PBM services)
- **Cordavis** (biosimilars)
- **Oak Street Health** (primary care)
- **Signify Health** (home-based care)
- **MinuteClinic** (retail clinics)

### Oak Street Health

**Acquisition**: June 2023, $10.6 billion

**Model**: Value-based primary care for Medicare patients
- Capitated payments (per-member-per-month)
- Full-risk arrangements with Medicare Advantage plans
- Focus on complex, high-cost patients

**Metrics (Q2 2024)**:
| Metric | Value | YoY Change |
|--------|-------|------------|
| At-Risk Patients | 235,000 | +30% |
| Centers | 200+ | Expanding |
| Aetna Member Penetration | Tripled | Post-acquisition |

**Integration Progress**:
- Data integration with Aetna claims analytics
- Co-branded Aetna-Oak Street MA plans launching 2025
- Referral pathway: MinuteClinic → Oak Street → Specialist

### Signify Health

**Acquisition**: March 2023, ~$8 billion

**Services**:
- In-home health risk assessments
- Social determinants of health screening
- Provider enablement for value-based care

**Integration**:
- Serves nearly twice as many Aetna members post-acquisition
- Risk stratification data feeds Aetna care management
- Supports Medicare Advantage risk adjustment

### MinuteClinic

**Profile**:
- 1,100+ locations in CVS stores
- First retail clinic accredited by The Joint Commission
- Services: Acute care, chronic disease management, vaccinations, wellness

**Expansion**:
- Chronic care management (diabetes, hypertension, asthma)
- Behavioral health services (teletherapy integration)
- Health screenings (diabetes, cholesterol, depression)

**2024-2025 Initiatives**:
- Digital check-in (80% patient adoption)
- Integration with Oak Street for complex cases
- Virtual visit capabilities
