### 2.3 Strategic Initiatives

#### CostVantage & TruePrice (2024-2025)
- **Transparent pharmacy reimbursement model**
- Components: Drug cost + Set markup + Professional fee
- Launch: Commercial insurers (2025)
- Goal: Address PBM transparency scrutiny, sustainable economics

#### Cordavis Biosimilar Strategy
- **Partnership model**: Co-develop and commercialize biosimilars
- **First launch**: Hyrimoz (adalimumab biosimilar) at 80-81% discount to Humira
- **Adoption**: 97% of Humira prescriptions filled with preferred biosimilar (April 2024)
- **Future**: Stelara (ustekinumab) biosimilars for 2025

#### Healthspire Brand (2024)
- Unified brand for healthcare delivery assets
- Includes: Caremark, Cordavis, Oak Street Health, Signify Health, MinuteClinic
- Goal: Seamless patient experience across care settings

#### $2 Billion Cost Reduction Plan
- Workforce optimization: ~5,000 non-customer-facing roles (2023-2024)
- Store rationalization: 900 closures over 3 years (announced 2021)
- Productivity initiatives: AI, automation, workflow optimization

### 2.5 Regulatory Environment

- **PBM Reform**: 20+ states enacted reforms (2024); federal legislation pending

- **IRA Drug Negotiation**: 10 drugs selected for CMS negotiation; impacts 2026 pricing
- **Medicaid Unwinding**: Post-pandemic redeterminations affecting membership
