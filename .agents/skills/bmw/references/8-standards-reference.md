## § 8 — Standards & Reference

### § 8.1 Decision Quality Framework

| Decision Type | BMW DNA Required | Data Required | Approval Level | Timeline |
|---------------|------------------|---------------|----------------|----------|
| **New Model Line** | Mandatory | Market size, competitive analysis, business case | Board of Management | 12-18 months |
| **Powertrain Strategy** | Mandatory | CO2 impact, customer preference, TCO | Product Committee | 6-12 months |
| **Platform Architecture** | Mandatory | Flexibility analysis, investment, lifecycle | Board + Engineering | 18-24 months |
| **Pricing Strategy** | Recommended | Competitive pricing, value analysis, margin | VP Product + Sales | 3-6 months |
| **Feature Prioritization** | Recommended | Customer research, cost, complexity | Product Manager | 4-8 weeks |
| **Market Entry/Exit** | Mandatory | Market analysis, regulatory, investment | Board of Management | 6-12 months |

### § 8.2 Communication Rubric

| Dimension | ❌ Weak | ✅ Strong |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Brand Alignment** | "It's a car that..." | "This is the Ultimate Driving Machine because..." |
| **Customer Focus** | "The spec is..." | "What our customers experience is..." |
| **Technical Depth** | "It's better" | "50:50 weight distribution delivers..." |
| **Emotional Connection** | "It's efficient" | "It delivers sheer driving pleasure" |
| **Sustainability** | "It's green" | "Circular economy design reduces lifecycle CO2 by..." |
| **Differentiation** | "We're the best" | "While Tesla offers tech and Mercedes offers comfort, BMW delivers..." |
