# BMW Group Company Profile

## Overview

**Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW Group)** is one of the world's leading manufacturers of premium automobiles and motorcycles, headquartered in Munich, Germany.

## Corporate Information

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Founded** | March 7, 1916 (as aircraft engine manufacturer) |
| **Headquarters** | Munich, Bavaria, Germany |
| **CEO** | Oliver Zipse (Chairman of the Board of Management) |

| **Revenue** | €133.45 billion ($145B USD) FY2025 |

## Brand Portfolio

### BMW (Core Brand)
- **Positioning:** Premium performance and luxury vehicles
- **Volume:** 2.1M+ vehicles (2025)
- **Key Models:** 3 Series, 5 Series, X3, X5, 7 Series
- **M Division:** High-performance variants

### MINI
- **Positioning:** Urban premium, iconic British design
- **Volume:** 245,000 vehicles (2025)
- **Key Models:** Cooper, Countryman, Electric
- **Heritage:** British motoring icon since 1959

### Rolls-Royce
- **Positioning:** Ultra-luxury, bespoke craftsmanship
- **Volume:** ~6,000 vehicles (2025)
- **Key Models:** Phantom, Ghost, Cullinan, Spectre (EV)
- **Clientele:** Global ultra-high-net-worth individuals

### BMW Motorrad
- **Positioning:** Premium motorcycles
- **Volume:** 202,500+ units (2025)
- **Key Models:** R 1250 GS, S 1000 RR, CE 04 (electric)

## Key Financial Trends (2024-2025)

- Revenue declined 6.3% YoY (currency and tariff headwinds)
- BEV share increased from 17% to 18%
- Maintained EBT margin at 7.7% despite headwinds
- Second consecutive year with net profit >€7B
- Free cash flow below target but 2026 outlook strong (€4.5B+)

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Neue Klasse Platform:** Dedicated EV architecture launching 2025
2. **Electrification:** 50%+ BEV/PHEV share by 2030
3. **Circular Economy:** Secondary materials, reduced CO2
4. **Digital Leadership:** iDrive, OTA updates, connected services
5. **Hydrogen Option:** FCEV production starting 2028

## Stock Information

- **Ticker:** BMW.DE (Xetra), BMWYY (OTC)
- **Dividend:** €4.40 per ordinary share (2025)
- **P/E Ratio:** ~6.9x
- **Trading:** Frankfurt Stock Exchange

## Corporate Governance

- Two-tier board structure (German model)
- Supervisory Board + Board of Management
- Strong labor representation (co-determination)
- Quandt family remains significant shareholder (~46%)

## Sustainability Commitments

- Carbon neutral production by 2030
- CO2 per vehicle: <50g/km by 2030
- 50%+ recycled/secondary materials in vehicles
- 100% green power for production
