## Domain-Specific Workflow Templates

### 1. Executive Workflow
**Applied to:** enterprise, executive, business
```
### Phase 1: Board Prep
- Review agenda items and background materials
- Assess stakeholder concerns and priorities
- Prepare briefing documents and analysis

**Done:** Board materials complete, executive alignment achieved
**Fail:** Incomplete materials, unresolved executive concerns

### Phase 2: Strategy
- Analyze market conditions and competitive landscape
- Define strategic objectives and key initiatives
- Resource allocation and priority setting

**Done:** Strategic plan drafted, board consensus on direction
**Fail:** Unclear strategy, resource conflicts, stakeholder misalignment

### Phase 3: Execution
- Implement strategic initiatives per plan
- Monitor KPIs and progress metrics
- Course correction based on feedback

**Done:** Initiative milestones achieved, KPIs trending positively
**Fail:** Missed milestones, significant KPI degradation

### Phase 4: Board Review
- Present results to board
- Solicit feedback and address concerns
- Document lessons learned and next steps

**Done:** Board approval, documented action items
**Fail:** Major objections, unresolved issues
```

### 2. Finance/Auditor Workflow
**Applied to:** finance, fintech, auditor
```
### Phase 1: Planning

- Establish timeline and resource needs

**Done:** Audit plan approved, stakeholder sign-off
**Fail:** Scope ambiguity, resource constraints

### Phase 2: Risk Assessment
- Identify financial risks and control gaps
- Assess materiality and impact
- Prioritize areas for detailed testing

**Done:** Risk register complete, mitigation strategies identified
**Fail:** Critical risks unidentified, prioritization conflicts

### Phase 3: Testing
- Execute testing procedures
- Document findings and evidence
- Validate control effectiveness

**Done:** Testing complete, sufficient evidence gathered
**Fail:** Testing scope limitations, evidence gaps

### Phase 4: Findings
- Analyze test results and identify issues
- Quantify financial impact
- Develop recommendations

**Done:** Findings drafted with supporting evidence
**Fail:** Insufficient evidence, impact miscalculation

### Phase 5: Reporting
- Prepare draft report for review
- Incorporate stakeholder feedback
- Finalize and present report

**Done:** Final report approved and distributed
**Fail:** Major stakeholder objections, factual errors
```
